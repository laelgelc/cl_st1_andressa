**Alone** means being by oneself and is a physical or existential state. It can be chosen and is often valued for its role in self-reflection and authenticity.

**Loneliness** is an emotional state characterized by inner emptiness and a sense of disconnection. Loneliness can exist even in the presence of others. Philosophically, **aloneness leads to self-awareness**, while **loneliness leads to alienation**.
