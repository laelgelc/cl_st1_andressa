How life changes with time ;)
Almost 12 years ago I went to watch this movie with almost 10 people ig, all being my neighbours and their families.
But now, I went alone to watch this movie when it is re-released. Time flies, circles get smaller, your closed ones get busy, relationships mess up, but yeah at the end you survive.
Took a lot of courage to take this step with lot of hesitation from inside. But I did. When I sat in the theatre, I saw mostly couples and families but no one alone. Interacted with few peeps sitting on my side. I repeated all the dialogues from the movie, which made me a bit anxious if it disturbed others, but then they were also repeating so it was fine. I enjoyed the movie, like every time I rewatch on my laptop. My mom particularly stopping me always to rewatch the movies, some being very comfort films for me. But whenever I am alone and when my heart craves for comfort I just go and watch them. Chennai Express being the fun and happy mood movie.
Learnt a few things, experienced new things. Like SRK, says in the movie, “The best to way to escape your problems, is to solve them.” liked it very much hope, I just hope that I could implement the same
