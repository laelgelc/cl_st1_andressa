"Loner" does not mean "lonely". [This](https://theorion.com/76276/opinion/loners-arent-always-lonely/) article covers it pretty well.

"Loner" does not mean "introvert". [Here](https://solitarybeast.com/blog/are-you-a-loner-an-introvert-or-both) is a decent breakdown.

"Loner" does not mean "antisocial". [This](https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/blog/open-gently/201811/dont-say-youre-anti-social) is a great distinction.

"Loner" does not mean "misanthrope". This is left as an exercise to the reader.
