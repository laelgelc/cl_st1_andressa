I don’t even know how to put this into words anymore, but I’m so tired of being this alone.

My head feels like a war zone most days. My moods swing so hard and so fast it scares me. Sometimes there are voices in my head, and I can’t sleep, and then other times I just crash into this dark depression where everything feels pointless. I blackout when I get really angry and only remember pieces afterward. My body feels like it’s constantly on edge – stressed out, tense, and then on top of that there’s this random spike in sex drive that just makes me feel even more messed up and out of control.

I’ve tried to reach out to people, to make friends, online and in real life. I thought I had friends, but then they kick me out of voice chats, leave my messages on read for months, or just slowly disappear. Even people I looked up to, like YouTubers or artists I tried talking to, just end up ignoring me. It feels like nobody wants me around, like I’m some kind of contamination.

I don’t feel human most of the time. I feel like a skin walker trying to pretend to be a “normal” person. Growing up, people pitied me or talked down to me, called me autistic or the R-word like I was some kind of joke or burden. That stuff sticks. It makes it really hard to believe I could ever be liked for who I am.

Even my family feels like they don’t really like me. My mom’s boyfriend doesn’t like me, and honestly I don’t think my own family does either. I did open up to my mom about what’s going on in my head, but I still can’t get any real professional help, and it just makes me feel trapped in this broken brain.

I hate this. I hate being lonely. I hate my mind. I hate feeling like I’m fundamentally wrong and that everyone else can see it and wants to stay away from me.
