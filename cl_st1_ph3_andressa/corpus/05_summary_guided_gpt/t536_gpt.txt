I don’t even know how to put this into words, but I feel like I’m just… existing. I wake up, drag myself to work, come home, rot on my bed or couch, and then sleep. Repeat. Nothing feels fun anymore. It’s like the world is in grayscale and I’m just going through the motions.

I just got over a really heavy cold and these awful headaches, and now that I’m not physically miserable, I thought I’d feel better. But instead I feel kind of empty. Not terrible, not good, just… numb. And that scares me. I worry I’m turning into someone who doesn’t feel anything, who’s just useless and drifting.

I’ve always felt alone. I honestly believe I’ve always been alone and always will be. But at the same time, I want so badly to have someone. Just one mutual, loving connection. Even if it was only for a few hours, I think it would mean everything to me.

Sometimes I imagine you. The person I love, who loves me back. I picture us going on a random road trip, windows down, no plan, just us. I talk to you in my head and then wonder if you even exist. Life feels so meaningless without you, this imagined “you” I could care for. And I keep asking myself: if not for you, then what am I even here for?
