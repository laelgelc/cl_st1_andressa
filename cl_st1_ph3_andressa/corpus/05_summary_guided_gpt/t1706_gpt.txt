I’ve been working on this little project called OneSky, and I wanted to share it here in case it helps anyone else who’s up late feeling heavy or alone. The idea is simple: you write a thought on a virtual paper lantern and release it. At 9am or 9pm, that lantern gets delivered to a random person somewhere in the world.

There’s no chat, no profiles, no way to reply—just a single, anonymous connection between two strangers.

I wrote “tomorrow will be better” on mine, and weirdly, just sending it out made me feel a bit lighter. If you ever need to get something off your chest: https://thatonesky.com/
