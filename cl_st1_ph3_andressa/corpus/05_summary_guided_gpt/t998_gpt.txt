I’m 39 and I honestly feel like I’ve wasted my entire life.

I don’t have any friends. I haven’t had a girlfriend in seven years. People would probably say I’m “nice” or “polite,” but that’s about it. I’m boring, unremarkable. I’m the kind of person others will tolerate in a group, but nobody actually wants to be around. Nobody looks at me as attractive or desirable. I’m just… there. Background noise.

I’ve tried to “do the right things.” I work hard. I’ve traveled. I’ve made decent money at times. But it all feels pointless because I don’t have anyone to share anything with. What’s the point of seeing the world or grinding away at work when you come home to four walls and silence every single day?

I’ve tried dating. Tinder, Facebook Dating, trying to approach people in real life. Nothing works. I get ignored, ghosted, rejected. It’s like I’m invisible. I don’t have family to fall back on, no friends to talk to. I’m just completely on my own.

On top of that, I’m dealing with a chronic illness that I haven’t been able to treat properly. I’m about to lose my medical insurance, and I’m already barely keeping up with my bills. I feel like everything is slowly closing in on me. No support system, no health care, no relationship, no real joy in anything.

My days are basically: eat, work, sleep, repeat. That’s it. A meaningless loop. I wake up and I don’t look forward to anything. I go to bed and I don’t feel like I accomplished anything that matters. I’m just existing because my body hasn’t quit yet.

I feel hopeless, angry, and completely empty. It’s like I’ve been screaming into a void for years and nothing changes. I’ve started thinking more and more about just preparing my “exit,” because I genuinely don’t see things getting better from here. I don’t know why I’m even writing this, maybe just to put it somewhere outside my own head.
