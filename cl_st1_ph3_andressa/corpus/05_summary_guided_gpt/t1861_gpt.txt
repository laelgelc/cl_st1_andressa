Sometimes I look back and feel like I could’ve actually become “somebody” if I’d just tried harder, pushed more, made different choices. Instead it feels like I drifted through everything half-asleep. People who know me think I’m this chilled, laid-back person who doesn’t really care about much, but that’s not true at all. I do care. I’ve tried to change things, to improve myself, to build something better. Every time I start making a bit of progress, something happens and I end up right back where I started. It’s exhausting. Most days I just feel like a complete disappointment.
