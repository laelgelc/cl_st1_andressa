I feel like I’ve been lonely for as long as I can remember. In high school I mostly stayed in my room, barely talked to my family, and only had acquaintances and one real friend. I thought studying abroad in Sydney would be a fresh start, but it’s somehow made everything worse.

Now I live alone, eat alone, study alone. I go to class, sit by myself, and don’t know how to turn small talk into actual friendship. I feel extremely socially awkward, like everyone else got some manual on how to connect that I never received.

I’ve tried to change things. I’ve joined events, gone to different activities, pushed myself to show up. But I still don’t have any close connections or physical affection in my life. It feels like I already wasted my teenage years in loneliness, and now I’m just watching my 20s slip away the same way, like I’m doomed to always be on the outside looking in.
