Every time I open social media, it just hits me how alone I really am. I see all these people, especially women, posting about their close friends, trips, parties, group photos, people hyping them up in the comments. It looks so effortless for them to be wanted and included.

Meanwhile, I feel invisible. I’m barely noticed online, and it’s the same in real life. If I don’t put in constant effort to reach out, check in, suggest plans, everything just fades away. It’s like I’m the only one trying to keep any connection alive, and it still slips through my fingers.

It feels so unfair. Why do some people seem to naturally attract support and attention, while I’m stuck on the outside looking in? I keep wondering why I can’t have that kind of happy, meaningful life too.
