I’m 49 and somehow feel like my life just… evaporated. I spent everything I had on my family – time, energy, love, every decision – and now there’s no one left. No family, no friends. I’m in a domestic violence shelter, staring at four walls and wondering how I got here.

Everyone I loved or had history with is either dead, gone, or too unsafe to be around. All the shared jokes, the little rituals, the “remember when” moments – they died with those people or those relationships. I don’t have anyone who knows my stories anymore.

I keep trying to rebuild, but I honestly don’t know why. It feels impossible to imagine ever having people again to care for, to share stupid memes or deep talks with. I’d been looking forward to playing Rage and Bloom this summer with someone I loved. Now they’re gone, the apartment is gone, everything is gone. I feel like I’m just drifting.
