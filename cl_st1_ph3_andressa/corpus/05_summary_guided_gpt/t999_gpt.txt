I’m 17 and I feel so alone it’s starting to mess with my head. I’m not some super social, confident person, but I’m not a total outcast either. I’m awkward and nerdy, but so are most of my friends. The difference is… they all somehow have girlfriends, and I don’t.

I keep wondering what’s wrong with me. Is it how I talk? How I act? I try really hard to be respectful and considerate. I don’t push boundaries, I listen, I care. But it feels like whatever I’m doing just makes me invisible romantically.

It honestly annoys me watching my friends be all affectionate with their partners. I’m happy for them, but it stings. I want that too. I want a girlfriend to do simple things with—build LEGO sets together, watch movies, draw side by side, go for walks, watch sunsets. Just small, quiet moments where I don’t feel like I’m on the outside looking in.

I worry I sound needy or jealous, but I don’t know how else to say it: I just really want to be loved. I want to be someone’s “special someone,” not just the friend who tags along. I want that one person I can escape reality with for a while, where it’s just us and nothing else matters.

Right now it feels like that’s never going to happen for me, and I don’t understand why.
