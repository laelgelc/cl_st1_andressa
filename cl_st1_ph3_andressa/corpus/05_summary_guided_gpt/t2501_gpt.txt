I feel so lonely all the time, even though I’m technically not alone. I’m surrounded by people and I know there are a few I could message or call, but I stop myself because I’m scared I’ll just be bothering them or getting in the way. It feels like I’m always “too much” or an inconvenience.

I’ve never had many friends, and the ones I do have feel like they just tolerate me. I don’t feel genuinely liked or wanted. I keep trying to connect, to meet people, to be social, but nothing really touches this emptiness. It’s like there’s this constant shadow of loneliness following me everywhere, and I don’t know how to get rid of it.
