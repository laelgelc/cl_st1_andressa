I moved to a new country about a year ago and, at first, I really thought I was starting to build a friend group. I let myself relax a bit, started to trust them, and thought maybe I’d finally found “my people” here. Then I realized they acted differently behind my back, and it felt like the floor dropped out from under me.

Now I just feel hurt and tired. The dynamic with others from my home country here feels so fake and draining that I don’t even want to try with them anymore. But that leaves me with no one. I don’t feel like I have a single person I can rely on or be fully myself with.

It’s getting to the point where the loneliness is messing with my work and studies. I’m stuck in this mental loop of not belonging anywhere – not here, and not really back home either. I don’t even know where to start rebuilding. I just needed to say this somewhere instead of keeping it all inside.
