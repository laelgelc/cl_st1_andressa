I’m 21 now and it feels like I’ve basically gone eight years without a real friend. Since I was 13 I’ve eaten lunch alone, watched everyone else in groups while I sat by myself. Back then I was bullied and ignored, like I was invisible. It hurt a lot, but what hurt more was that no one seemed to care.

College is different in that people aren’t openly mean anymore, but the result is the same. I join clubs, try to talk in class, volunteer, make small talk. I put in effort, but it never goes anywhere. No one seems interested in actually being my friend or getting to know me.

My days just blur together: wake up, go through the motions, repeat. The sadness has been there so long it’s turned into this numb, empty void where love and attention were supposed to be.
