I’m 17, a junior, and I feel like I’m just drifting through life alone. I’ve been single for three years, but I still remember my ex so clearly it’s like she’s still here sometimes. Tiny details stick in my head: the way she laughed, the way she said certain words. It makes it so hard to move on.

I want a relationship so badly, but I honestly don’t believe it’ll ever happen for me. It feels like everyone else is moving forward and I’m stuck. Video games help distract me for a little while, but when I stop playing, the loneliness hits even harder.

Seeing couples at school, or out in public holding hands, just twists the knife. It feels like I’m meant to be on the outside looking in forever, and I don’t know how to change that.
