I used to hate being alone in public. I’d feel awkward, like everyone was watching and judging me. Lately that’s completely flipped. I actually enjoy going places by myself now. It gives me space to focus on self-knowledge, self-development, and my own idea of success.

When I look at people who always need to be in some social scene, it feels like they risk losing their sense of self and just blending in. I do have people I can hang out with and I know socializing matters sometimes, but most of the time I honestly prefer being on my own.
