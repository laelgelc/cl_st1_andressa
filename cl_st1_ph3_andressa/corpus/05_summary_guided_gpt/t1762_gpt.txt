I do online school and work a lot, and somehow I feel more alone than if I were doing nothing at all.

In my online classes, it feels like everyone already has their own friend groups. People joke around in the chat, talk to each other on Discord, and I’m just… there. When I try to message someone or join in, it’s like I’m invisible. No one responds. I’ll stare at the screen and watch the conversation move on without me.

Work isn’t much better. My coworkers are polite, but they only really talk to me if I start the conversation, and even then it never goes beyond surface-level small talk. It’s always about the weather, the shift, basic stuff. No one ever suggests hanging out after, and I don’t know how to ask without feeling weird or desperate.

I haven’t hung out with anyone outside of work or school in six years. Six years. Sometimes I’m literally standing in a group while everyone else is laughing and talking around me, and I might as well be a ghost. I’m there, but I’m not really part of anything.

I even catch myself pretending I have friends just to feel a little better, like making up stories in my head about “plans” I don’t actually have. Honestly, I don’t think I’ve had a real friend since elementary school, and realizing that makes me feel absolutely awful.
