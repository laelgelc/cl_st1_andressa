Sometimes I feel like my whole life has just been one long exercise in being invisible.

My parents were emotionally abusive and neglectful, so I learned early that my feelings didn’t matter. Being autistic on top of that meant I never really had friends growing up. Every hardship, every scary or painful thing, I just had to deal with alone. There was never anyone to lean on.

At 21 I finally made a genuinely good friend, someone who actually understood me a bit. But now they live in another city, and I’m back to handling everything by myself.

What really wears me down is that I can’t seem to ask for support without people acting like my life is easy or fun. I feel like I have to pretend everything’s great, because if I’m honest, I’m told I shouldn’t feel that way. It leaves me feeling so unseen, like I don’t even have the right to complain.
