My partner went to prison on December 16th for 5 months, and I feel like my entire world has just been ripped away.

We’ve been together for 22 months, living together almost the whole time, and we’d only just got our own flat. It was supposed to be the start of our life together, and now it just feels like a shell. I’ve only had one short reception call from him since he went in, and I have no idea when I’ll get to hear his voice again. That uncertainty is eating me alive.

I’m so depressed and lonely that even basic things feel impossible. I actually took a day off work because I just couldn’t get out of bed. I sleep surrounded by his clothes, clinging to his scent because it’s the closest thing I have to him now. The place that used to feel safe and warm now just feels painfully empty.

I miss the physical closeness so much it hurts. Lying on his chest, listening to his heartbeat, feeling his arms around me – that was my comfort, my calm. Now every morning I wake up and it’s like losing him all over again when I remember he’s not here.

I feel like I could just disappear and no one would really notice. My life feels like hell right now. The only thing keeping me going is knowing I have to keep working, keep paying the bills, so he has a home to come back to when he gets out. That’s the one thread I’m holding onto.

I don’t really know what I’m looking for by posting this. I just feel so alone, and I needed to put this somewhere.
