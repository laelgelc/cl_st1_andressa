I’m an elementary school janitor and I’m really struggling with anxiety over work right now.

A couple days ago I had a horrible headache but still went in because we’re short-staffed and I try to be reliable. Later I found out someone texted my boss saying I was asleep on the job. I wasn’t. I was just trying to push through feeling like garbage.

My boss texted me that we’d “discuss this matter Monday.” That one line has been stuck in my head. It really hurts that instead of asking if I was okay, a coworker apparently tried to get me in trouble. I show up, I do my job, and I thought that mattered. Now I feel like my reputation is on the line over something that isn’t even true.

The next day I got really sick. I even threw up in the school parking lot while dropping off my stepchild. At that point it was obvious I couldn’t work like that. I tried calling and texting my boss, no response. So I followed protocol and called the principal. She was kind and told me not to come in.

Now it’s early Saturday and my mind is racing. I keep wondering if I made the wrong call staying home, if it’s going to look like I’m faking or being lazy, and if Monday is going to be a disaster. I’m trying not to let it ruin my weekend but it’s hard to relax when I feel like I’m waiting for bad news.

I guess I just needed to let this out.
