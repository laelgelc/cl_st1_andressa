Lately it feels like people just… stopped texting me. If I don’t keep posting or commenting, it’s like I disappear from everyone’s mind. I’m in my 4th semester at a new college and I still don’t really know anyone here, even though my social media is full of “friends.” It makes me wonder if the online world is actually blocking real relationships, or if I’m just an unpleasant person and that’s the real reason I’m on the outside.

What’s weird is that I don’t *feel* lonely in the way I used to. There’s a certain peace in not constantly trying to fit in or please people just to belong. I’m not chasing anyone’s approval right now, and that feels… calm.

But there are consequences. Doing everything alone is exhausting. There’s no one to encourage me when I’m tired, no one to say “good job,” no one to offer guidance or reassure me that I’m not totally messing up my life. It’s all on me, all the time.

I’m not sure if this is the “right” way to live or just a phase. Maybe life is supposed to be uncertain and confusing, and the real challenge is just learning to exist in that confusion without losing yourself.
