I’ve felt lonely for a long time, even though I technically have people in my life. I can hang out, joke around, do small talk, but when it comes to really serious stuff, it feels like there’s no one I can actually open up to without worrying about judgment, drama, or it getting back to someone else.

That feeling is what pushed me to start building an anonymous chat app called Blurble. The idea is simple: you get matched with another person to talk, no profiles, no photos, no usernames to stalk later. Just two people and a conversation, without all the baggage of identity attached.

It’s not finished yet, but I’m collecting emails from anyone who might want to try it when it’s ready at blurble.net.

If you’re feeling lonely or just need to get something off your chest, feel free to vent in the comments too.
