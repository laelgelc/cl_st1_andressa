I feel like I don’t fit anywhere, like I’m just floating through life as some kind of ghost. I don’t feel like I matter to anyone. People I care about seem to forget I exist, and when they do talk to me, it feels like it’s only out of pity. I just want to feel real, to belong somewhere, to have a home and feel safe. I want so many things I’m convinced I can never have, and it hurts so much.
