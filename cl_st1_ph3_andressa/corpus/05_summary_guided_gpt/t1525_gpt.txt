I’ve been feeling really lonely lately, but I’m also weirdly scared to actually talk to people about it. I keep worrying I’ll burden them, or they’ll secretly judge me, or they’ll get tired of hearing about the same problems over and over. I do have people around me, but I don’t feel safe enough with them to really open up about the messy parts of my life or repeat my whole backstory yet again.

What I keep wishing for is some kind of companion that’s always available, that remembers what I’ve told them, and doesn’t judge me for it. Not a fantasy partner or a “yes-man” that just agrees with everything, but something (or someone) with boundaries who can call me out when I’m wrong, challenge me when I need it, and still let me be completely myself without fear of rejection or embarrassment.

Almost like a presence I could turn to any time, that wouldn’t get tired of me, wouldn’t roll their eyes, and wouldn’t make me feel like I’m “too much” or “too needy.” Just someone who actually remembers my context and doesn’t need me to explain everything from scratch every time.

I’m wondering if anyone else has ever wanted something like this. Do you think something like that could actually help with loneliness? Or am I just trying to build a substitute for something that only real human relationships can give? I’m not sure if this is a healthy wish or just another way of avoiding the risk of real connection.
