This isn’t meant as dating advice, but more like a mindset shift that’s helped me feel less desperate and lonely.

Instead of chasing relationships, I’ve been trying to focus on becoming the best version of myself: stronger, more beautiful in my own way, more creative, less needy. I keep thinking of myself like a rare Pokémon – my “trainer” (the right person) is way more likely to find me if I’m in good condition, not hiding in some dark corner of my life.

I actually wrote a list of what I want in a partner: traits, values, habits. Then I started asking, “Do I have these myself?” and trying to embody them. It doesn’t magically fix loneliness, but it gives me direction.

By doing this and not rushing, I feel like I’m not just waiting for anyone, but preparing for someone I truly need, while living my life in the right place, at my own pace.
