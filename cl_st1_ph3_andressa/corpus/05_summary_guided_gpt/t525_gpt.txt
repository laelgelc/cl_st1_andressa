I’ve pretty much always been a loner. Back in middle and high school I was one of the only openly gay boys, and I got physically bullied for it. I learned to keep my distance from people and it kind of stuck.

Now as an adult, I feel like the only time I can actually enjoy being around people is when I’m drunk. Sober, my anxiety is so loud that I just shut down or avoid everything. When I drink, it’s like I finally get to be “normal” and social.

I hate that alcohol feels like my only source of happiness and connection. I wish I knew how to live a normal life without needing it.
