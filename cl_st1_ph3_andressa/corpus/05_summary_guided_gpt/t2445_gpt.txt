I feel like I’m losing my mind watching what’s happening around me. I keep thinking about that quote from Anne Frank, about people being dragged out of their homes and families being torn apart, and it feels disturbingly close to what I’m seeing now. It’s like history is echoing and everyone’s pretending it’s fine.

What really messes with my head is seeing people actively trying to remove Anne Frank’s diary from schools and libraries. Of all things, *that* book. It’s like they want to erase the warnings and the humanity in it. And then the push to shut down the Department of Education on top of that… it feels like they’re trying to destroy education for an entire generation.

I keep thinking, this is insane, this is wrong. But when I look around, it seems like barely anyone cares, or they shrug it off as “politics.” I care so much it hurts, and it’s making me feel isolated and out of place. Am I really this alone in feeling this way?
