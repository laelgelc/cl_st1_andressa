I moved to California from another country about three years ago, and it feels like I’ve been stuck in the same loop ever since. I work two jobs, go home, do my chores, maybe scroll on my phone a bit, sleep, and then repeat. Days and weeks just blur together.  

What makes it harder is how difficult it’s been to actually connect with people here. It feels like everyone already has their own established friend groups, their own circles, and there’s no space for someone new. Sometimes people seem polite on the surface, but not really open to forming deeper connections. I end up feeling like I’m just orbiting other people’s lives without ever really being part of anything.  

I’m lonely. I really wish I had a woman in my life to share the small, everyday things with. Someone to cook a simple home meal with, sit down and eat together, talk about our day, and have real, meaningful conversations instead of just surface-level small talk.  

I’m pretty introverted and slow to open up, and I’m definitely not the outgoing type who can just walk up to strangers and make friends instantly. I love taking long walks on the beach alone, and going hiking around LA, but even when I’m surrounded by people, I still feel like I’m on my own.  

I just wish I could meet someone who enjoys the same quiet things and actually wants to build something real.
