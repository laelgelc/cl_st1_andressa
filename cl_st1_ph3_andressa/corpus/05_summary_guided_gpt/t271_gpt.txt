I’ve been thinking about what kind of “loner” I am. The closest comparison I can find is Tennouji Koutarou – not totally isolated, but with only one real friend and mostly surface-level interactions with everyone else.

Except I don’t even have that one close friend.

I talk to people, I function, but it all feels shallow and distant. My days don’t feel cheerful or youthful at all; it’s like I’m just watching other people live that kind of life from behind a glass wall. I’m there, but not really part of it.

I know we’re all strangers here, but I don’t want other loners to leave me right now. Even just knowing someone read this and stayed for a moment would mean something.
