My older brother is always going out, driving around to see different friend groups, constantly texting and laughing with people. I’m just…here. I mostly only text him, and outside of school I don’t really have any real friends. Watching him live this social life while I stay home makes me feel so depressed and lonely.

I keep trying to convince myself I’m better off alone, that I don’t need anyone, but I don’t actually believe it. I’m not happy just sitting at home with my parents all the time. I feel stuck and left behind, like I’m missing something everyone else has. I don’t know what to do or how to cope with these feelings anymore.
