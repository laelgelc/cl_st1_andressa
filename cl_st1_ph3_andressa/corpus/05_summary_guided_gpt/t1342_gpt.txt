I’ve known loneliness longer than I’ve known most people. I’ve feared it, fought it, and eventually sat down with it long enough that it became something like a friend. Somewhere along the way, it turned into a strange kind of strength, even a home.  

People tell me, “You can’t live alone forever.” But what is “forever,” really? I’ve lived alone for as long as I can remember, and at this point, I honestly believe I can keep doing it.
