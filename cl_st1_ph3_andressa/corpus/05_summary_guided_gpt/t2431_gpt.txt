I’m 46 and live with my 74-year-old dad. No partner, no kids, and honestly just one friend. I’ve had my share of “sick” people who seemed to enjoy putting me down, and after a while I just cut them all out. It was self‑protection, but now my trust is so damaged that meeting new people or even hanging out feels almost impossible.

I’m extremely lonely. I keep looking around and wondering how everyone else seems to build these lives with families, support systems, circles of friends. I feel like I missed some crucial class on how to be a normal social human being.

I’ve even thought about trying church just to meet people, but I find it boring and I can’t shake the feeling that some people there have their own agendas. I’m reaching out here because I’m trying to figure out how to socialize again without getting hurt all over.
