I’m 16, living with my parents, supposed to be focusing on my board exams, but I feel like I’m living in a war zone instead of a home. Every conversation turns into taunts, curses, or straight-up physical abuse. It’s like they’re just waiting for an excuse to explode. By the time I sit down to study, I’m mentally drained and shaking inside.

Recently they went on about how I have to be “perfect.” I tried to say that no one is perfect, and my dad slapped me. It wasn’t even the pain that hurt, it was the message: I’m not allowed to be human.

I feel like the family scapegoat. Anything that goes wrong is somehow my fault. My mom is sick and they even blame me for that, even though I’ve been the one making sure she takes her medicine and eats right. I know her disease isn’t caused by stress, but they still act like I’m the reason for everything bad.
