Today in class we had to form pairs for an activity. I naturally assumed I’d work with the person sitting next to me, but as soon as the teacher said “find a partner,” my seatmate immediately went over to two other people and formed a trio, leaving me sitting there alone.

The teacher even said trios weren’t allowed, but they just ignored it and stayed together. I could feel people looking at me while I just sat there with no one, and it was honestly humiliating. I felt this mix of embarrassment and anger and hurt, like I’d been very publicly rejected.

I ended up going to the teacher and saying I’d just work alone. After a while, another pair actually invited me to join them, so we ended up as a trio too. But even though it technically worked out, that initial moment just stung so much. It made me feel unwanted and out of place, and I can’t stop replaying it in my head.
