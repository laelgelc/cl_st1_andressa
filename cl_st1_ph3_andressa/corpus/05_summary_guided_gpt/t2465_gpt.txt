31M here with BPD, and I feel like I’ve finally accepted that I’m going to be alone forever… or at least that’s what I keep telling myself.

I’ve turned down casual sex because I don’t want something empty. I want a real relationship, something deep, but I honestly don’t think I can handle it. I love way too intensely, and the idea of giving someone that much power over me terrifies me. It feels like I’d be handing them the ability to completely destroy me.

I used to be a hopeless romantic. I loved romcoms, even Twilight. I really believed in that kind of love. Now I’ve spent years trying to make peace with this “fate,” trying to convince myself I’m better off alone because of my diagnosis.

But every week or two, it hits me again—this crushing heartbreak and grief. It can last 30 minutes, sometimes 3 hours. I just lie there in silence and cry. I’m so tired of feeling this over and over.
