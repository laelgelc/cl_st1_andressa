My birthday just passed and, like every year, it broke me. Only my immediate family said anything. None of my “friends” reached out unless I messaged first. I always go out of my way to make them feel special on their birthdays, but when it’s mine, it’s like I don’t exist.

I ended up crying again, like I do every birthday and most holidays. It feels so pathetic to admit, but it really hurts. I’ve basically stopped talking to my friends because I’m tired of being the only one who seems to care. It makes me feel invisible, like I don’t matter to anyone.

I’ve told people I’m depressed, that I’m not okay, and it’s like they just… ignore it or change the subject. I’m so exhausted. I don’t want to keep trying so hard to be seen or loved. I’m scared that when my family is gone, there will be literally no one left.
