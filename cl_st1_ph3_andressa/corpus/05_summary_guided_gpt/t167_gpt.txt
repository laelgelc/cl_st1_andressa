I’m 58 and retired from teaching English two years ago, and I’m honestly not sure who I am anymore.

When I was working, I didn’t realize how much of my identity and social life were wrapped up in school. I had colleagues to chat with, students to worry about, a routine that pulled me out of myself. Once I left, those work friendships faded faster than I expected. Now I don’t really have a close circle at all.

Most days I just drift. I sit in front of the TV or endlessly scroll through websites and local listings for clubs, groups, classes… and then I never actually go. The usual advice—join a gym, find a hobby group, volunteer—just feels impossible. The motivation isn’t there, and neither is the interest. Things I used to enjoy feel flat and empty.

Depression and anxiety are kind of my constant background noise now. I guess I’m posting because I just want to feel less alone for a moment. If anyone reads this and relates, I’d really like to hear from you.
