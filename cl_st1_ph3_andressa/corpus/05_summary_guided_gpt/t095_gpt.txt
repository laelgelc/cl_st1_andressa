I feel like I’m in this weird contradiction that I can’t get out of.

I’m not shy, I’m not socially awkward, and I can function fine in conversations. But the truth is, I dislike most interactions and I actively avoid people because, honestly, humanity just kind of disgusts me. I assume most people are like me deep down and would find each other boring, and if they’re not like me—if they’re actually excited about other people and want to connect—I just end up seeing them as idiots I don’t want to be around.

I haven’t dated in years. I don’t want to be judged, I don’t want to be evaluated, and I have zero interest in chasing anyone’s approval. The whole “performing” for someone else, trying to impress them, or even caring what they think just feels gross to me.

The messed up part is that I still feel lonely. I don’t want “friends” in the traditional sense, but I do want some kind of placeholder person—someone who’s just… there. A warm body, I guess. Someone who exists in my space so I’m not technically alone, but doesn’t demand anything from me emotionally or socially.

But when I actually spend time with potential placeholders, it never works. I end up feeling like I’d rather be alone. I start thinking about my personal projects, things I could be building or learning, and it feels like being with them is a waste of my time and energy. Even when I get what I thought I wanted—another human around—I still want to retreat back into my own world.

I keep imagining an AI “friend” with a customizable personality that I can turn on and off whenever I want. No judgment, no expectations, no pretending to care about stuff I don’t care about. Just something that gives me the emotional benefits of connection on my terms. But I don’t think anything like that is coming anytime soon in a way that actually feels real.

It feels like my loneliness is hard-wired, even though I don’t actually want to be around people. I want the benefits of human connection without the “awfulness” of other humans. Has anyone figured out how to kind of game the system here? Is there any workaround for this, or am I just stuck in this loop?
