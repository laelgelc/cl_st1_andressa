I’m 20, gay, in college, and I feel like I’m just drifting through life alone.

I have my family and I know that’s more than some people have, but I don’t have any real friends and I’ve never had a boyfriend. I want a boyfriend so badly it almost hurts, but I also feel like I “gave up on life” a while ago. After a bunch of disappointments, I just sort of stopped trying. Now I’m scared of failing at everything: dating, school, just… life in general.

Academically, I feel completely lost. I struggle to learn and retain information, no matter how much I study. It makes me feel stupid, like I’m not meant for college. I already switched majors once, from nutrition to accounting, hoping it would get better, but it hasn’t. I still feel like nothing is sticking in my brain, and I’m just going through the motions.

My parents pay for everything, and that makes it worse. I feel like I’m wasting their money and time. Part of me wants to just drop out, get a job, and stop pretending I’m something I’m not. Another part of me is terrified of doing that and disappointing them, especially when they’ve given me this opportunity. I’m stuck between going all in and risking failing classes, or quitting and feeling like a failure anyway.

This fear spills into dating too. Even if I met a guy, I’m scared he’d be disappointed in me once he sees how much I struggle with school and how little I have going on. I already feel behind everyone else my age.

I don’t really know what I’m asking for, but I guess… what would you do in my situation? Has anyone else felt like this about school and their future? How do you decide whether to push through or walk away? And how do you even think about dating when you feel like such a mess in every other part of your life?
