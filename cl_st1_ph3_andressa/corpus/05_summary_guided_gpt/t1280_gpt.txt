I’m 21 and I honestly feel like I’m going crazy from how lonely I am. I haven’t gotten a single message or had any real social contact in about three weeks. No calls, no “how are you?”, nothing. It’s like I’ve just fallen off the face of the earth and no one noticed.

I’ve been dealing with loneliness for around three years, but the last 2–3 months have been on a different level. It feels unhealthy, like it’s rotting my brain. I have no one to call, no one to ask to hang out, no one to just sit with and exist. I don’t even know what I’m supposed to do anymore.

What makes it worse is that I have no idea how people even meet others. Life doesn’t come with a tutorial. There’s no clear step-by-step on “how to make friends” once you’re out of school or just… stuck.

What should I actually do? How do you meet people when you’re starting from nothing?
