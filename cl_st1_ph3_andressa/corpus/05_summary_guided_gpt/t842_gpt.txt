I’m 28M and spent almost three years working on myself after a pretty rough period. Therapy, habits, trying to get my life together. I finally felt ready to open up to someone again, and I met this 30F. We talked for a while, met a few times, and the connection felt so natural and strong so quickly that it honestly scared me a little in a good way.

On our last date, everything shifted. She opened up about her past – a 12-year relationship, 3-year marriage, divorce – and it was like all that unresolved trauma just crashed over her at once. She ended up crying in my arms, saying she was still really messed up, that she wasn’t over it, and that it wasn’t my fault. I believed her, but it still hurt to see her in that much pain.

I suggested we take some time and maybe revisit things later, mostly because I didn’t want to push her into something she clearly wasn’t ready for. Since then, communication has basically stopped. It feels like my world went dark overnight. I keep thinking about her, wondering if she’s okay, wanting to check in… but I’m scared that reaching out would be pressure she doesn’t need.

I’m stuck in this place where I don’t know if wanting to reach out is me caring about her, or me being selfish because I miss the connection and hate this silence. This is honestly one of the hardest relationship situations I’ve dealt with. I thought I was ready, and for a moment it felt like the universe was finally throwing me a bone.

I guess I’m just writing this to get the noise out of my head. I don’t really know what to do from here.
