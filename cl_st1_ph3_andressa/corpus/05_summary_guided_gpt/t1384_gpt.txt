Over the past six months, I’ve started to realize how one-sided my relationships are. I’m always the one who shows up, listens, checks in, and tries to be there for people. But when it’s my turn to need support, it feels like no one really shows up for me in the same way.

When I do try to open up, I usually just get advice or “fixes” instead of empathy. It makes me feel like I’m being dramatic or like I’m a burden, so I end up shutting down and keeping everything to myself. The emotional load is getting really heavy.

The holidays are making it worse. Everyone talks about togetherness and family, but it just makes my grief, sadness, and sense of alienation feel so much louder. It’s confusing and painful to feel this distant from people who say they care.

I’m posting here hoping to connect with anyone who understands what it’s like to feel unseen, even when you’re surrounded by people.
