Holidays can feel especially heavy and lonely, and if that’s where you are right now, it doesn’t mean you’re broken or failing. It doesn’t mean you’re “doing life wrong.” A lot of people quietly feel this way too, even if it looks like everyone else is surrounded by love and joy.  

If you can, try to create even one small new experience this season: reach out to someone you trust, reconnect with an old friend, light a candle or cook a favorite dish to honor someone you’ve lost, or open yourself to new people and beginnings. You matter more than you know, and your presence in this world is important.
