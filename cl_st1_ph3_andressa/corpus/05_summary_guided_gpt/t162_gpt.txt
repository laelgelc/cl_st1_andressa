I’m so tired of feeling like this. I want people in my life, I want to just sit with someone, talk, relax, exist together. But whenever I’m actually around people, I feel this pressure building up inside me. It’s like I have to put on a version of myself that they’ll accept, constantly monitoring every word and reaction.

Even with my own family, I don’t feel like I’m really me. Conversations feel fake, like I’m acting in some play I never agreed to be in. It’s exhausting, and afterward I’m drained and even more aware of how alone I feel.

I don’t really see a way out of this. I’m just very tired and lonely, and I needed to let it out somewhere.
