I’m 24 and I feel like I’ve kind of lost the only version of myself I ever knew.

For a few years I had this small, close group of friends. We’d game together, play DND, just mess around online and hang out. Then my depression and anxiety got really bad. I got flaky, withdrawn, constantly negative. Eventually they stopped inviting me to things and the group just… moved on without me. I don’t blame them. I know I pushed them away, but it still hurts like hell.

What’s messing me up is realizing that all the things I thought were my “hobbies” were really just excuses to be with them. Gaming, DND, even playing guitar – they were fun because I was doing them with people I cared about. Now when I try to do any of it alone, it just feels empty. I keep thinking that my only real “interest” was making my friends laugh, and without them I don’t know who I am or what I like.

I want to rebuild some kind of life, find hobbies or interests that are actually mine, but my anxiety makes everything feel impossible. The idea of just joining a group or activity makes me feel sick. I don’t know where to start, or how to start small enough that I don’t freak out and give up.

If anyone’s been in a similar place, how did you begin again? How do you find new hobbies or people when you feel terrified and hollow?
