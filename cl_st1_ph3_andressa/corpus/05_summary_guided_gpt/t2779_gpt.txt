I’m really starting to feel used and invisible. There’s this woman in my life who is constantly talking about her parties, dates, clubbing, vacations, and all the attention she gets from guys. It’s like a running highlight reel of her social life. But I’m never actually invited to anything.

Whenever I say something like “I wish I could come too” or “I’d love to go out sometime,” she just ignores it or brushes past it. Yet when she needs a ride or some kind of favor, suddenly I matter again. I feel like I’m just there for her convenience, not as a real friend.

It honestly feels like she’s flaunting this fun, exciting life right in front of me, while I’m stuck on the outside looking in. The best way I can describe it is like someone eating candy in front of a child who doesn’t have any. It hurts more than I want to admit.
