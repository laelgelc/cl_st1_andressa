I have around 7–8 friends I talk to regularly, so on paper I shouldn’t feel lonely. But lately it feels like I’m just acting around them. I smile, laugh, joke, try to be the “normal” version of myself, but it all feels fake. When I’m alone, it’s like everything shuts off. I feel empty and kind of emotionless, like there’s just…nothing there.

I keep wondering if I’m only staying in these friendships because I’m scared of losing them, or because I’m trying to chase how things felt at the beginning when it all seemed more real and fun.

Recently I hung out with three of them and decided not to put on the mask. I stayed quiet and didn’t force myself to be cheerful. I was clearly not okay (at least it felt obvious to me), but no one asked if I was alright or if something was wrong. They just carried on.

Now I’m confused. Do I actually like being alone? Is something else going on with me? I just don’t know anymore.
