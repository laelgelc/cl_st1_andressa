Thank you, January, for bringing a new year and a fresh start. I really need it. Last year I was full of flaws I couldn’t control, and now I’m trying to fight them, to keep them from taking over again. You look so beautiful and glittering, with your cold air and snow, like a clean page. I’m watching the world shift from fallen leaves toward thriving nature, and I’m hoping that change can happen inside me too. A new beginning—for me, and for anyone who needs one.
