I don’t really know why I’m posting this, except that I’m so tired of holding it all in.

Lately it feels like I’m just… existing. Not living, not moving toward anything, just going through the motions. Everything feels so empty and pointless, like there’s this huge void inside me that nothing can touch.

I lost my mom in 2022. She wasn’t just my mom, she was my best friend, my safe person. Since she died, it’s like the world lost its color. I went no contact with my dad because he’s narcissistic and a bully, and being around him just made everything worse. So now it feels like I don’t really have parents at all.

I have a brother, and he’s a good person, but he has a young family and his own life. I live across the country, and I already feel like an imposition whenever I reach out. I don’t want to be this dark cloud hovering over their happiness.

On top of that, I’m still trying to heal from a broken engagement and betrayal. It shattered whatever trust I had left. The idea of dating again just feels impossible. I can’t imagine letting someone in like that, only to be hurt again.

I’ve tried to help myself. Therapy, fitness, hobbies, throwing myself into work, praying… nothing seems to touch the pain. Instead I just feel numb, like I’m watching my life from the outside.

The thoughts about not wanting to be here are getting more frequent. I keep thinking that people would get over my absence pretty quickly. I’m just so exhausted from the constant pain and loneliness.
