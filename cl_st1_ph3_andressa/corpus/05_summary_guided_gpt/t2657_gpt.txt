I’ve always been a bit of a loner and, most of the time, I genuinely enjoy doing things by myself. I like going out alone, doing my own thing, not having to coordinate with anyone. But every now and then, I wish I had more friends, or at least people I could occasionally share things with.

I used to get really jealous seeing groups of people together and assumed I was sad just because I was alone. Lately I’ve realized it’s not that simple. The deeper problem is that I feel weird and isolated for actually liking being a loner. It sometimes feels like I’m the only person who goes out and does stuff by themselves and prefers it that way.

I just wish I didn’t feel like the only “weirdo” who lives like this.
