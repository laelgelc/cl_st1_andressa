I walked through town today and it honestly felt like I didn’t exist.

There were people everywhere – laughing, talking, rushing past each other – but it was like I was walking through some kind of movie I wasn’t cast in. No one looked at me, no one moved aside, no one reacted. I tried to reach out, to just… connect somehow, and it felt like my hand went straight through them. Like they were solid and I was just a projection. A glitch. A ghost.

At one point I actually stumbled and fell, and the shock of it hit harder than the ground. Not because I was hurt physically, but because nothing changed. No one stopped. No one noticed. I just lay there for a moment feeling completely hollow, like there was nothing inside me anymore.

The best way I can describe this loneliness is being stranded on a tiny island in the middle of an endless ocean. I’m screaming for help, burning every signal fire I can, but no one ever sees the smoke, no one ever hears the sound. The world keeps moving, ships pass in the distance, but they never turn toward me.

I keep wondering if my life is already over in some way, and I just haven’t caught up to the fact yet. Like I died quietly somewhere along the line, and now I’m just drifting through society as a ghost that nobody can see.
