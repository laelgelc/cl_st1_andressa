I’m 21 and on paper everything looks fine. I’m finishing my bachelor’s, I’m close with my family, I travel with them, I’ve got a job and some side projects, and I’m working on applications for a master’s. From the outside it probably looks like I’m doing “well” or at least moving forward.

Inside, it doesn’t feel like that at all. Most days it feels like I’m just existing and killing time. I have a bunch of scattered goals in my head, but I don’t really move toward them. I don’t feel like I accomplish much day to day. I wouldn’t call myself hardworking; in school I just barely get by.

Socially, there’s not much going on. I have a few friends but we don’t really do anything meaningful together. I’m not involved in campus life, no clubs, no parties, no dating, no real social activities. At work, my colleagues are actually pretty cool and my boss is good, but I still don’t take initiative to connect with anyone.

Because of my background and upbringing, I’ve always had this feeling of not really belonging anywhere. It’s like there’s this gap between who I feel like I am inside and the person everyone else sees. Outwardly, I feel kind of empty, like I have no real personality, like I’m just some NPC going through preset motions.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for exactly, I just needed to put this into words somewhere.
