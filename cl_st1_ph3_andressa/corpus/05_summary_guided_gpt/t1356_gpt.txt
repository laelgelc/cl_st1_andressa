Someone in my family is thinking about starting a small paid chat/voice service for lonely people. The idea is basically casual, one-on-one conversations: someone to talk to, listen to you vent, share random thoughts with, or just keep you company for a while. It wouldn’t be therapy or anything professional like that, just friendly social interaction with clear boundaries.

I’m torn about it. On one hand, loneliness is obviously a huge issue and not everyone has friends or family they can call. On the other hand, I’m not sure how people feel about paying for that kind of connection, or if it comes across as exploitative, cringe, or just unnecessary when there are free communities.

Do you think there’s a real need for something like this? Would you ever use a service like that yourself? Also, if it’s not a terrible idea, where could something like this even be shared or advertised without it feeling spammy or inappropriate?

I’d really appreciate honest opinions, both positive and critical.
