I just found out my grandfather is going to die this week, and on top of that, I was told I have a brain cyst. My mom and sisters all have husbands, friends, pets… people around them. I’m just here in this empty house that feels so cold, with no one to hold me or even sit next to me.

During the day I go through the motions and seem normal, but underneath it I feel this constant sadness and heaviness. I cry a lot when I’m alone. I keep thinking I’m going to die alone, and sometimes that idea almost feels comforting, which scares me too. I just feel so completely alone.
