I matched with this guy on Tinder and we’d been having these really good conversations. It felt easy and fun in a way I haven’t had in a while. We decided to go see the new Chainsaw Man movie together. I was supposed to pick him up, so before I left I asked for his address.

About 30 minutes later he replied saying he was stuck at work, might be late, might even end up coming in halfway through the movie. I didn’t want to cancel, so I just went anyway. I sat there in a theater full of couples, watching the movie alone, checking my phone, texting, calling, hoping he’d show up.

He never did. At some point he just blocked me.

There was a line in the movie about love that really got to me, and on the way home I just broke down a bit. I kept thinking about all my past romantic experiences and how often I end up hurt or abandoned. It’s hard not to wonder if there’s something wrong with me. I’m so tired of loving people and getting treated like I don’t matter. It’s exhausting and it really, really hurts.
