I’m so tired of being “just a friend.”

What I want isn’t complicated or wild – I just want a monogamous romantic relationship. Someone to go on dates with, have sex with, spend holidays together. I want to be a girlfriend, and one day a wife. But no matter what I do, people only ever seem to see me as a friend. It feels like I live in this permanent friend-zone.

I really do care about my friends. I ask about their partners, their dates, their anniversaries. I try to be genuinely happy for them, and I don’t want to be the bitter single person who can’t handle other people’s joy.

But lately, it’s starting to hurt more and more. Every story about a cute date or a thoughtful gift or a weekend away just reminds me that I have none of that. It’s getting harder to stay supportive when I’m sitting there feeling invisible and unwanted in the way that matters most to me.

I don’t know how to balance being a good friend with this constant ache of being alone.
