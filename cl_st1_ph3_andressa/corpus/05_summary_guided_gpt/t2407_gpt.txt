I honestly feel like I’ve already ruined my life and I’m just waiting around to die alone and forgotten.

Dropping out of high school feels like the point where everything snapped. It wasn’t because I was lazy or didn’t care, it was because everything was just too much. But now that decision hangs over me like this permanent stamp of failure. School used to at least be a place where I was forced to be around people, where there was a chance to meet someone new. Now I’m just cut off. Most days I’m stuck at home, alone, in my own head.

I technically have a few friends, but I barely see them. Everyone is busy with their own lives, moving forward, doing “normal” things. I’m just here, isolated, watching time pass. I keep thinking I’m going to end up in some soul-sucking 9–5 job I hate, completely burnt out, maybe heavily addicted to something just to feel anything or get through the day. It feels like nobody really cares about my health or if I even exist, so sometimes I catch myself thinking, “Why should I care either?”

I feel like I’ve destroyed any chance I had at finding a partner. Who would want someone like me? Dropped out, no direction, depressed, barely functioning. I can’t imagine anyone looking at my life and thinking, “Yeah, I want to be part of that.” It feels like that door is just… closed forever.

I’ve lost almost all hope that things will ever get better. I don’t feel like my parents or anyone else would really care if I just disappeared. Maybe they’d be sad for a bit, but then life would go on, and I’d just be another person who faded away. That thought scares me and numbs me at the same time.

I’m so lost and scared of the future. Everything feels like it’s collapsing—my life, the world, especially the USA. Every time I look at the news or social media, it just confirms this feeling that everything is getting worse and there’s no escape. I feel like I’m going insane from never getting a break, from constantly being trapped with my own thoughts, from feeling like there’s nowhere to go and nothing to look forward to.
