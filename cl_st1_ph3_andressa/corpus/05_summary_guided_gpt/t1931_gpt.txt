I’m so stressed and exhausted by my social life at school. It’s a small boarding school, so there’s no real escape from anyone, and it feels like I’m constantly stuck in the middle of drama and people I don’t really connect with.

My roommate is generally nice, but sometimes she just takes her anger out on me. It’s like I’m her emotional punching bag when she’s in a bad mood. I never know what version of her I’m going to get, and it makes our room feel less like a safe place and more like another thing I have to manage.

The girls in my class are either chaotic and immature or shallow and mean. There’s one group that’s always loud, dramatic, and all over the place, and another that’s more “put together” but really judgmental and cruel underneath. The boys aren’t any better – most of them come off as arrogant and honestly kind of disgusting.

I don’t feel like I belong anywhere. I just drift between groups, trying to find a place where I fit, but I always feel like an outsider. Being surrounded by people all the time but still feeling this alone is really getting to me.
