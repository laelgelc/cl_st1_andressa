I’ve basically grown up using Reddit and other social media as my only outlet. I haven’t had friends for a long time, so since I was about 11, this has been where I put my thoughts and questions. It’s the closest thing I’ve had to talking to someone.

But lately it just feels like every time I post anything, I get torn apart. Almost every post attracts criticism, often really harsh. Strangers don’t care how I feel, they just want to argue, score points, or assume the worst about me. It’s like because there are billions of people in the world, individual feelings don’t matter unless you’re someone they personally know or care about.

It feels awful to be attacked by a bunch of people online when I’m just trying to be honest and vulnerable. Half the time I feel like I’m being completely misunderstood, and instead of anyone asking questions or trying to understand, they just jump straight to judging or mocking. Then I end up defending myself over and over, and it turns into this exhausting back-and-forth that leaves me feeling worse than before.

I’m so tired of constantly arguing with strangers just to justify my own feelings. I wish I had even one real person I could share my thoughts with, who would actually listen instead of immediately trying to pick everything apart. I just want to talk without having to be on guard all the time.
