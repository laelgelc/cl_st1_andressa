To anyone out there who feels alone and invisible right now: you matter more than you know.

I know people always say “focus on yourself,” but when you’re insecure, that feels almost impossible. Your mind keeps going back to what others think, to every embarrassing moment, to all the ways you feel like you’re not enough. But instead of trying to “fix” yourself, try loving yourself as you are. Embrace everything about you, even the parts you’re ashamed of. Stop giving so much power to other people’s opinions.

Look at what you do have, too. If you have your health, that’s a blessing. If you woke up today, that’s a blessing. It doesn’t erase the pain, but it means there’s still room for good things to come.

I truly believe God is putting you through this for a reason, because blessings are coming. These heavy feelings are shaping you, building your character. You have to love yourself first before you can really give and receive love.

You might feel alone right now, but you aren’t.
