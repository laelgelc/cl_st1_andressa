It’s like I was born to fail and to be a disappointment.

I’m just the loser, the failure, and that’s the whole story in their eyes. It makes me so bitter. I feel like my existence is a joke at my own expense.

I fantasize about a painful death, like maybe that’s the only thing I can really own, the only thing that’s truly mine.
