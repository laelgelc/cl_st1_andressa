I feel like I’m falling apart so fast I can’t keep up. I’ll just start crying out of nowhere, then suddenly I’m hyper and jittery, then crushed by this intense anxiety. My thoughts won’t stop racing, I can’t sit still, I get so angry over nothing, and sometimes I get these horrible intrusive visions of hurting myself that scare me.

It’s like I’m not in control of my own emotions anymore, and that terrifies me. I keep wishing I had just one loyal friend who would really stay. Someone who’d let me vent without judging, who’d reply quickly instead of disappearing, who wouldn’t ghost me when I get “too much.” I want someone who’d stick by me no matter how bad I get, who wouldn’t give up on me when I feel completely broken.
