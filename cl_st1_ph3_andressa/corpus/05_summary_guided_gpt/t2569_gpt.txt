I went to my church’s Christmas service and felt more alone than I have in a long time. It seemed like everyone else was surrounded by family, partners, or friends, laughing and talking, and I was just… there. The few people I usually talk to barely noticed me. One person made a bit of small talk that honestly felt more like pity than anything else. I left as soon as the service ended because I couldn’t take it anymore. The one bright spot was the choir—they were genuinely great.
