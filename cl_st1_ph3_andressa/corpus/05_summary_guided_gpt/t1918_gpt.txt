Lately I feel like I’m stuck in this loop of melancholy and nostalgia. My brain keeps telling me “the past was better,” even though I know, logically, a lot of it was actually worse. It’s like I’m romanticizing a time when I was also struggling, just in different ways.

Now I feel lonelier than ever, and I can’t seem to appreciate anything about the present. I’m always either worrying about the future or missing the past, and both feel awful. People keep telling me to “live in the moment,” but I honestly don’t know how. I hate that I think like this, but I don’t know how to stop.
