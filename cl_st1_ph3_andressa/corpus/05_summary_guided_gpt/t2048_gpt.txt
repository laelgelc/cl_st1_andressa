Second semester of college and I feel like I somehow missed the “make friends” window.

I’m not completely alone — I talk to people in my classes, clubs, and at work. Conversations are fine, but they never turn into “hey, want to hang out this weekend?” My only real close connection is my roommate, and I’m super grateful for them, but it also makes it obvious how little else I have.

I’m pretty introverted and I definitely didn’t put myself out there enough during orientation. Now it feels like everyone already has their friend groups and I’m just… extra. I feel “cooked,” like it’s too late to join anything.

Weekends are the worst. I see people going out, posting stories, making memories, and I’m just in my room. I feel awkward about asking to join in, like I’d be forcing myself on them.
