I’m 13 and I feel like I’m completely falling apart inside.

I’m so lonely and angry and full of self-hatred that it scares me. I’ve never had any romantic experience at all, but it feels like everyone around me is in some kind of relationship, or at least has someone who likes them back. I don’t want some random, shallow thing like I see everywhere. I want a loving wife one day. I want “my person,” someone I can really build a life with. I think about her a lot, even though I don’t know who she is or if she even exists for me.

Earlier this year I had a really bad episode. I saw a video of a lesbian couple getting engaged and it just broke something inside me. I almost overdosed on pain meds. I was that close. After that, things got a bit better for a while, like I had pulled myself back from the edge. But recently all those feelings have come back, only worse. Today they’re the strongest they’ve ever been and I’m honestly terrified of my own thoughts.

Part of me keeps saying I’m just being dramatic or attention-seeking, and that I should just shut up and deal with it. But there’s also this other part of me that feels like I’m silently screaming for help, like I really am in danger and nobody can hear me. I feel stuck in this endless loop: I want it all to stop, but I also want to live long enough to meet her, to marry her, to not feel this empty and broken forever.

Right now I’m lying here with a knife on my chest, and I know how bad that sounds. I think this post is my way of reaching out, because I don’t know what else to do. I just really, really want someone to understand what I’m feeling, and to tell me that maybe there’s still a chance that I’ll get to have the love I dream about one day.
