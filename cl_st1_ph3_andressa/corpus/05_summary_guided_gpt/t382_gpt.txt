I finally forced myself to book a salon appointment after avoiding them for years. I just wanted to fix the back of my hair and maybe have a normal, friendly chat like everyone else seems to have. Instead, the stylist basically refused to do what I asked. No real explanation, just this feeling of dismissal, like I wasn’t worth the time or effort.

It felt so disrespectful and humiliating. I walked out thinking, “Of course. I’m ugly. People see me and instantly hate me.” It’s like they took one look and decided I wasn’t worth bothering with.  

Now I’m back to thinking salon workers are rude and hateful, and this is exactly why I stopped going in the first place. I just wanted a simple, human interaction and ended up feeling worse about myself than ever.
