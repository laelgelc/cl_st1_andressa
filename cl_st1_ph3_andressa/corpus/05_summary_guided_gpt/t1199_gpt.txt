I feel broken in ways that can’t be fixed, like my heart’s been shattered too many times and glued back together wrong. I didn’t choose this path, but I’m stuck walking it, getting judged and hurt by people who don’t really see me.  

What’s strange is that, underneath all this damage, I still just want to protect someone. To lift them up, hold them, love them, and destroy anything that tries to hurt them. I’d give everything I have to take their pain away. If I had a soul, I’d give that too.
