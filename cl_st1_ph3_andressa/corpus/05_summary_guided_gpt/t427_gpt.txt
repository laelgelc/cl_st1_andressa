I’m 37 and I feel like my future just…ended. I lost my first pregnancy, then surgery turned into “you need a full hysterectomy.” Just like that, no more chance at biological kids. My only sister died of cancer a few years ago. Now it’s just my mom and stepdad, and they’re getting older too.

I keep trying to talk about how scared I am of ending up old and alone, and people just brush it off. When I say things like “my body will only be found because a neighbor smells it,” they act like I’m being dramatic. I’m not rich, I can’t just “do surrogacy” or buy my way into some other option. I feel so dismissed and invisible.
