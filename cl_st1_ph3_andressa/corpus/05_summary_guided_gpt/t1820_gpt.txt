I used to be so lonely that it felt like it would never end. I was stuck in my own head, feeling empty and sad all the time. But somewhere along the way, something changed. I started spending more time in my imagination, in my own mind, and instead of it being a prison, it slowly became a kind of refuge.

Now I don’t feel sad or lonely anymore. It honestly feels like “heaven” in my head, like I found a place that’s peaceful and full of light, and I found it while being completely alone. It’s strange to say, but I’ve become grateful that people left me alone, because without that, I don’t think I would have discovered this.

I guess I just want to say that loneliness can be a phase, not a life sentence. It can get better, and sometimes the time alone can lead you somewhere you never expected.
