I think I’m finally starting to accept that I’m just going to be alone. Women don’t like me romantically. I’m always “just a friend,” and not even a close one—more like the backup option when no one else is around or they’re bored. It feels like my role in life is to love people who will never love me back in the same way.

I’m trying to stop fighting that reality and just… live with it. I’ve got my cat, and honestly that’s the only creature that seems genuinely happy to see me. I’m hoping I can eventually find a new job, something to fill the time and distract me a bit.

I’m reminding myself of my place, lowering my expectations, and trying to focus on the basics: get through each day, keep breathing, and stay alive.
