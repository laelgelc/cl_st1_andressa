I’ve been feeling really lonely lately and I just want someone to talk to, even if it’s about something silly. I came across this simple two-person “creative response game” and thought it might be a fun way to connect with someone.

The idea is: Person A gives Person B a keyword, and Person B has to answer all of Person A’s questions using that keyword somehow. Person A wins if Person B ever forgets or fails to use the keyword in an answer; Person B wins if Person A runs out of questions.

For example, if the keyword is “cake”:
- “How was your day?” – “Honestly, it felt like a cake that didn’t rise.”
- “What’s your dream job?” – “Baking the perfect cake for people who need cheering up.”

You can ask any kind of questions—serious, random, or funny—as long as we stick to the keyword. If anyone’s up for playing this with me, please comment a keyword or ask me questions with one. I’d really appreciate the company.
