I just turned 20 and ended up spending my birthday alone again, but this time it was… different, in a better way.

Last year on my 19th, I just doomscrolled and gamed all day and felt absolutely miserable by the end of it. This year I forced myself to actually go out. I ate at a restaurant by myself (which felt super awkward at first), then visited a local museum, and later went on a small trek to a nearby waterfall.

The waterfall has kind of turned into a couples’ spot, so I definitely felt like the odd one out. I noticed a few looks and felt really self-conscious and lonely for a bit. I do miss having someone to share things like that with.

But the place itself was so peaceful that my anxiety slowly eased up, and I actually managed to enjoy being there alone. It wasn’t an amazing birthday, but compared to last year, it was at least a little better.
