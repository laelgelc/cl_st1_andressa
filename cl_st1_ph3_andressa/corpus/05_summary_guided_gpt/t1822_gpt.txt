I used to genuinely love being alone. My own company and my hobbies were enough, and I never really understood what people meant when they talked about feeling lonely. Now I’m in my 30s and it feels like everything flipped. The hobbies that used to make me happy barely interest me anymore. I have almost no friends, and when I do go on dates or hang out with people, I often walk away feeling like I wasted my time.

I hate how often I end up in situations where someone else talks about themselves nonstop. I sit there thinking, “Why am I here? What’s the point?” I crave a connection where I feel seen and understood, not just like a prop in someone else’s monologue.

I feel so empty and sad a lot of the time, but I don’t think it’s depression. It’s more like this constant ache for something real that I can’t seem to find. Sometimes I end up talking to several men at once just for the attention, but I get bored so quickly and then feel even more frustrated and lonely afterward.

I don’t know what to do. Writing this is coming from a place of pure desperation.
