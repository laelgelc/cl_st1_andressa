I’ve realized I actually prefer being a loner. I feel way more free and genuinely have more fun when I don’t have to worry about being around other people. Maintaining relationships, checking in, keeping up small talk – it all feels like a chore to me, not something I look forward to. On top of that, there are so many shitty people out there who judge, exploit, or mistreat others just to feel better about themselves. Avoiding all that drama just feels easier and safer.
