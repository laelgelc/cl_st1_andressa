My dad was injured in an accident when I was a kid and hasn’t been able to work since. We’ve lived off government money my whole life. I keep thinking my mom should have worked so we could’ve had a more dignified life, instead of always scraping by and feeling like a burden on the system.  

I feel miserable and guilty, like my entire existence has been funded by money that could have helped someone “more deserving.” It makes me feel ashamed of who I am and where I come from.
