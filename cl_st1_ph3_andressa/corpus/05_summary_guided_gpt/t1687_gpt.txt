It feels like the moment people find out I don’t have friends or a partner, they pull away and assume there’s something fundamentally wrong with me. It’s like a giant red flag to them. That reaction just makes me even more isolated and ashamed. I’ve started to feel like I have to hide who I really am, build some fake persona and pretend I have a normal social life just so people don’t immediately write me off. It makes me think my real self is inherently off-putting, and that any friendship I manage to make would be based on a lie instead of something genuine.
