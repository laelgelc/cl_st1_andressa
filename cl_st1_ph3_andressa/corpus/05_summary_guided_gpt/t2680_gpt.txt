I’m in Grade 12 now and I’ve had acne since Grade 8. It’s genetic, but it’s destroyed my confidence, especially during online classes when the camera has to be on. I can’t focus because I’m constantly thinking everyone is staring at my face and silently judging me.

What hurts even more is when people I consider friends say things like “just bathe more” or joke that I’m dirty or have bad hygiene. It makes me feel disgusting and ashamed, even though I know it isn’t because I’m not clean enough. Acne and skin conditions are not a joke. We don’t choose this.

If you have a friend with acne, please don’t tease them or make comments about their skin. It sticks with us. Support them instead. Friends are supposed to lift each other up, not drag each other down.
