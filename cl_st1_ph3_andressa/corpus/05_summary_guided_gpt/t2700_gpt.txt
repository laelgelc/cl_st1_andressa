I feel stuck in this weird in‑between place. On paper, I “have friends,” but I can’t shake the feeling that they don’t actually like me that much, or that they only tolerate me. Whenever I talk about my problems, I immediately feel like I’m being annoying or a burden, so I pull back and keep more to myself.

Because of that, I feel chronically lonely and misunderstood, even when I’m around people. It’s like no one really sees me or cares in the way I wish they would.

I keep going back and forth between two options: just accepting that I’m meant to be a loner and withdrawing, or forcing myself to keep trying to get close to people and hoping one day someone will genuinely appreciate me.
