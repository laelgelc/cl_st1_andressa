I’m 19 and I feel like my life is already over.

I’ve always loved cars and racing. It’s been the one constant thing I’ve dreamed about since I was a kid. But recently I’ve been seeing TikToks of other furries, some as young as 15, already out there racing in regional motorsport events. Actually racing. In real cars. And I’ve never even sat in a racecar once.

It hit me like a truck. I don’t have the money to start, I don’t have the experience, and every video I see just makes it feel more impossible. It feels like I’ve completely wasted my life so far, like those were the years I should’ve been doing something, anything, to get closer to that dream. Instead I did nothing, and now it feels like the window is just… gone.

Since Friday I’ve basically been locked in my room. I can’t stop thinking about how far behind I am and how stupid I was for not doing “just something” earlier. I’m actually considering giving up my love for cars altogether because now whenever I think about them, I don’t feel joy anymore, just this deep sadness and regret.

On top of that, I see myself as really unattractive and unhealthy, and it just feels like another thing working against me. Like even if somehow the money and opportunity magically appeared, I’d still be starting from such a huge disadvantage compared to all these younger, fitter, better-looking people who already have a head start.

I just feel like I’ve ruined everything before it even began. I wish I could go back and shake my younger self and tell them to do something, anything. Now I just sit here, scrolling, watching other people live the life I always wanted, and feeling like I’ve already missed my chance.
