I’m 22 and honestly feel like my life is just… stuck. I’m preparing for competitive exams and this is my fifth attempt with no success. Every failure just makes me feel more useless. I don’t earn, I stay at home, and I can feel my dad’s irritation like a constant background noise. It’s like he only sees what I’m not doing, not how hard I’m trying.

On top of that, I have vitiligo. It makes me so insecure about my appearance. I keep wondering if that’s why my ex cheated on me and lied to me after five years together. I know cheating says more about him than me, but in my head it always comes back to, “Maybe if I looked different, he wouldn’t have done that.”

I don’t have friends to talk to. I used to talk to my dad about how vitiligo made me feel, but now it feels like he only taunts me or uses my insecurities against me. I feel like a burden on this earth, like I don’t belong anywhere, and the loneliness is suffocating.

I can’t go to therapy because of money, lack of good therapists around, and the stigma where I live. I just wish someone would love me unconditionally, listen to me, and make me feel wanted. Right now, the only way I know to cope with all this mental stress is by thinking about physically hurting myself, and that scares me, but also feels like the only thing I control.
