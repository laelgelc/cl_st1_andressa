I keep having to relearn the same painful lesson: a lot of the people I call “friends” aren’t actually my friends. I overshare with the wrong people, hoping it’ll bring us closer, and then realize later they never really cared. I tolerate disrespect and crappy behavior because I’m afraid of losing whatever connection we have, even when it makes me feel small.

It hurts feeling like I’m only appreciated when I’m useful. I’ve been patient, caring, and forgiving with people who would never do the same for me. I open up, give them more chances, and every time I end up feeling stupid for believing it would be different.

I’m slowly accepting that not everyone is truly your friend, no matter what they call themselves or how badly you want them to be.
