I’ve always been the one who gives everything in relationships. I reach out, I initiate, I try so hard to keep connections alive. And somehow I still end up alone.

So I’ve decided I’m done chasing. I’m not going to keep messaging people who barely notice I exist, or force friendships that clearly aren’t mutual. If loneliness is what God has written for me, then I’ll accept it, even if it hurts.

From now on, I’ll stay quiet and wait for someone who genuinely sees me and chooses me without needing to be begged. Until then, I’ll live with the quiet ache and longing, and try to be gentle with my own heart, even when it feels like no one else will be.
