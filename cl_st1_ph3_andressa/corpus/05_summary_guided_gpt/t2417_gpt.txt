I keep looking at my friends’ lives and feeling like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me. They all seem to have “normal” lives – more friends, girlfriends, stuff to do, things they enjoy. I’m technically part of the group, but I don’t feel close to any of them. I don’t tell them anything important, and when I’m around them I feel like I have nothing meaningful to add.

In my head I’m just… bland. No real personality, not that smart, can’t talk properly, always saying the wrong thing or nothing at all. I do have a decent-paying job, so on paper I’m “fine,” but I’ve never had a girlfriend and it really feels like I’m missing out on a huge part of life. I’ve tried to improve, but this “loser” feeling just won’t go away.
