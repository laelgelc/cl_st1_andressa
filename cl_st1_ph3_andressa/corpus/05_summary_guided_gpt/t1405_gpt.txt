I’m 47, divorced, and it’s been 11 years since my ex-husband and I separated after a 15-year marriage and three sons. I always told myself I’d move on, that I’d eventually remarry, that this was just a chapter. But lately I’ve started to realize he might have been the love of my life, and it’s hitting me that I may never have that kind of relationship again.

We rarely talk now except about our boys. Recently we had this short text exchange about something totally mundane, and for a few minutes it felt like old times. Same jokes, same easy back-and-forth. When the conversation ended, I just sat there and cried. I miss my best friend. I haven’t felt that kind of connection with anyone since the divorce.

What hurts even more is that he left me for another woman. I thought by now I’d be in a new marriage, or at least in love again, but it just hasn’t happened. Meanwhile, all my friends are married, wrapped up in their own families. I don’t really have anyone I can open up to about this.

I feel scared, alone, and so isolated. It’s like everyone else’s life moved forward and mine just… stalled.
