I keep seeing posts here about wanting friends, partners, a loving family, and I can’t relate. I don’t understand other people and they don’t seem to understand me, so genuine connection feels impossible. I’ve tried in the past and it just left me exhausted and confused.

These days I keep my interactions to practical stuff: cooperation, information exchange, basic discussion. No emotional closeness, no “real” relationships.

What I’m wondering is: are there people here who have actually removed others from their life as much as possible and are genuinely okay with it? How do you live as an “actual loner”? How do you balance minimizing contact with people while still using all the things civilization provides, which obviously require other humans to exist?
