I feel like I’m stuck in this weird limbo where everything I want is both right in front of me and completely out of reach. I’m exhausted all the time, not just physically but mentally and emotionally. It’s like I’m constantly running on empty, but I keep forcing myself to move anyway.

I struggle so much with forming real, meaningful connections. I push people away without even fully understanding why, and then I end up hurt and confused when I look around and realize no one is there. It’s like I’m watching myself sabotage any chance at closeness, and I still don’t know how to stop it.

I keep myself insanely busy so I can feel like I have some kind of purpose. If I’m always doing something, I can pretend I’m not lonely. But underneath that, it still feels like I’m just wasting time, letting my life pass by while I become more and more disconnected from everyone and everything.

I’m tired of people slowly fading out of my life. I’m tired of feeling like I’m begging for attention or affection or just basic presence. I hate that feeling of wanting to share my day, my thoughts, my happiness with someone and realizing there’s no one I can actually turn to.

Romantically, I don’t let anyone in. I don’t even really know what I want, and on top of that, I often don’t feel respected. So I keep this wall up, telling myself it’s safer this way, but it just leaves me feeling even more alone.

Overall, I feel deeply, painfully alone. I don’t know how to reach out to anyone without feeling like a burden or an annoyance. I’m so tired of constantly trying to convince myself that being on my own is enough, that I don’t need anyone. It’s not working. I’m just exhausted from pretending it is.
