Lately I keep finding myself lying awake at night, crying and thinking about how my death would affect everyone around me. I replay it in my head, like some messed up movie. I don’t even know if I actually want to die or if I just want everything to stop hurting. I keep wondering if this is normal, if everyone around me is secretly struggling like this too.

On paper, I have a loving family. But I still feel like I can’t get ahead in life no matter what I do. I’m a single mom in my late 30s, and my 17-year-old son has been through so much because of my choices. My long-term partner (not his dad) has so many unresolved issues that ended up hurting both of us. My son never approved of the relationship, and now it’s over and I’m just left feeling heartbroken and like I failed them both.

I left a past life of fast, easy money because I wanted to be a “normal,” hardworking person. Now I’m broke, ashamed, and my parents still worry about me like I’m a kid. My self-worth has been trashed for years, starting when I was 17 with my son’s much older father. I feel like my empathy and low self-esteem have ruined me. I just want to make one person proud. I just want to look in the mirror and not hate who I see.
