I’m confused and kind of stuck. I can walk into a party or a meetup and talk to random people pretty easily. I can make small talk, crack a joke, and it usually goes fine. But it never turns into anything more. It’s like I’m good at one-off interactions and terrible at building something real.

I feel like actual friendships and relationships come from seeing the same people regularly—work, school, hobbies. But those groups are small, and if I don’t really click with anyone there, I’m just… out of luck. It feels like no matter what I do, I can’t win.
