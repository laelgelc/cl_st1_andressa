I feel like I don’t actually exist to anyone. My phone never lights up unless it’s some random notification. No one ever texts just to ask how I’m doing, or if I’m okay. It honestly feels like it wouldn’t make a difference to the world if I disappeared tomorrow.

Dating has been a dead end for about 7.5 years. Dating apps are useless for me – I get ignored, unmatched, or never even noticed. In real life, it’s like I’m invisible to women. I don’t get flirted with, I don’t get approached, I barely even get eye contact. I’m just… there, in the background.

I can’t seem to make real friends either. Even online, when I think I’m finally connecting with someone, eventually they just stop responding. I end up assuming it’s because I’m not interesting or entertaining enough to keep around.

I wish I had real friends. I wish I had a girlfriend who genuinely wanted me, who actually desired me. But it feels like that kind of life is something I only get to experience in my dreams, never in reality.

Every day feels the same now. Wake up, go through the motions, go to sleep. I’ve lost most of my motivation. I’m just tired of feeling this unseen and unwanted.
