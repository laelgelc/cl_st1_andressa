I’ve been thinking that maybe we’re never truly alone. Even when it feels like no one cares, we’re still surrounded by nature, the universe, God, ancestors, spirits—whatever you personally believe in. That thought helps me a bit.

I’m trying to put my energy into things I can control: reading more, getting into hobbies, learning new skills. There are so many like-minded people online if we’re willing to look and reach out, even if it feels awkward at first.

Instead of staying stuck in loneliness, I want to focus on what I want my life to look like by 2026. We don’t have unlimited time here. I’m trying to be brave and actually use the time I have, instead of just letting it slip by.
