I’m 19 and honestly starting to think I’m just fundamentally hard to be friends with.

One big thing is that I really hate social media, especially anything with likes/dislikes. It makes me feel weird and disconnected from people who use it all the time. I didn’t grow up with it, so when everyone’s talking in terms of posts, likes, DMs, I just feel like I’m from a different planet. It’s like I’m missing this whole language everyone else is fluent in.

On top of that, I don’t really have real hobbies. My interests change constantly. I’ll get into something for a few days or weeks and then just drop it. I mostly just scroll 4chan, but I’m too anxious and self‑conscious to actually post anything. So I just lurk and waste time, and it doesn’t make me feel any less alone.

The only thing I can point to as a “good trait” is that I’m a good listener. But even that feels fake, because I’m bad at actually expressing empathy or my own thoughts. When someone vents to me, I always feel like I say the wrong thing or nothing at all. I walk away thinking I probably made them feel worse. Then I spiral into hating myself, and it gets dark fast—like “what’s even the point of me” kind of thoughts, including suicidal ones.

I don’t really know why I’m writing this. I guess if anyone wants to be friends with me after reading all this, there’s probably something wrong with you too. But, yeah… that’s where I’m at.
