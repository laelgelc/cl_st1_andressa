I get these intense, almost life-defining obsessions with certain things, characters, or people. It feels like my whole sense of meaning gets wrapped around them. If I can’t have what I’m obsessed with, life starts to feel kind of pointless. I know I could try to work on myself, detach, move on… but I don’t want to. I just want the thing itself. Does anyone else feel like everything they’ve ever wanted is just an illusion and forever out of reach?
