I’m 22 and went out today planning to just enjoy my day, but it ended up hurting more than anything. Everywhere I looked, people were with friends or at least one other person, laughing, talking, just… belonging. And there I was, alone again.

I tried to join a couple of groups, just to see if maybe I could fit in somewhere. They told me they didn’t need a new member. I respect that, I really do, but it still stung.

What really gets to me is that this isn’t new. I’ve always been alone. I’ve never had real-life friends to go out with. Every outing is just me, by myself. It’s starting to feel like I’m not meant to have friends, like I’m somehow not allowed to.
