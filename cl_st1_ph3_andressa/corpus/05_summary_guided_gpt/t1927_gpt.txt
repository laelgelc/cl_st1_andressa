I’ve been on social media for about 20 years now, as an artist/creator and small business owner, and it feels like I’ve never actually… landed anywhere. I move platforms, or the platforms change, and every time I do, I lose whatever tiny network I had. Friends, connections, customers – gone. It’s like starting from zero over and over again.

Even when I manage to gain followers, no one talks to me. No comments, no DMs, barely any likes. When I try to engage in communities, I get ignored. I’ll reply thoughtfully to people, try to join conversations, show up consistently, and it’s like I’m shouting into a void. No one seems to notice or care.

It’s hard not to feel invisible, like I’m cursed or something, like the universe just hates me. I’m so lonely. I keep trying to build something meaningful, to matter to someone, to have a support network, but nothing sticks. Nothing changes, no matter how much effort I put in or how many times I cry out for help.

I honestly don’t know what to do anymore. I just feel very, very sad.
