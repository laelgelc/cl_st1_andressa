I’ve been thinking a lot about the whole “loneliness epidemic” and how many of us are struggling not just with connection, but with basic social skills and confidence. Ideally we’d all be making friends in real life, but that can feel like a huge leap when you’re already isolated.

One idea I’ve had is some kind of service where you can have online video conversations with strangers (like over Zoom) specifically to chat, practice social skills, and slowly build confidence. Kind of a low-stakes, short-term bridge toward real-life interactions.

Part of what pushed me to this was calling two different loneliness hotlines and getting no answer both times. It felt really vulnerable and honestly pretty rejecting.
