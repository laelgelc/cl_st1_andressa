I feel like I’ve spent my whole life being either a punching bag or invisible.

When I was a kid, nobody really taught me how to behave or connect with people, so I just… guessed. I lashed out, acted “weird,” did anything that got a reaction. I confused bullying and negative attention with being accepted. At least if people were laughing at me, they knew I existed.

I kept doing weird stuff for attention for years, and now I’m an adult living alone with this huge sense of regret. I don’t even know who I am. I don’t have real hobbies or interests because I always copied what other people liked, hoping they’d like me too. It never really worked, and now I’ve just pulled away from everyone.

The bullying, the fake versions of myself, all of it makes it so hard to talk to people now. I’m stuck between wanting to go out and try to connect, or just staying in my own head where it feels safer.

I guess I just hope someone out there can relate to this.
