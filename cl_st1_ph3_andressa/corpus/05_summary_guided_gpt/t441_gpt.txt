Christmas and New Year’s are hitting me like a truck this year. I used to spend them with my ex, and now it’s just… silence. I’m not in denial; I know it’s over. She’s already with someone else, already moved on. I keep telling myself that if she still loved me, she would have fought for us. But I still miss her so much it physically hurts.

The urge to text her is constant. It feels pathetic, wanting to reach out to someone who clearly doesn’t want me anymore. I hate that I still love her while she treats me like a stranger. It’s like I’ve been erased from her life, and I’m stuck replaying everything alone.

Staying in the apartment we shared makes it worse. She’s gone, but her absence is everywhere. It feels like we’re living in two different worlds now: mine, frozen in the past, and hers, moving forward without me. I can’t just “replace” her the way she replaced me, and that makes me feel so alone.
