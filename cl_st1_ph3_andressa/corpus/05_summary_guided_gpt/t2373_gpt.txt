It really hurts to open up to someone, show them the deepest, rawest parts of yourself, and then just get met with silence. Being ghosted after that kind of vulnerability feels like having your worst fears confirmed – that you’re invisible, that your feelings don’t matter, that you’re somehow “too much” or not worth the effort.

What makes it worse is that it even happens here, on a subreddit that’s supposed to be about loneliness and support. I don’t expect everyone to respond to every post, but sometimes the lack of compassion is really discouraging.

This should be a place where people can say what they truly feel, without shame or fear, and at least know someone has heard them. Not everyone wants or needs replies, but for those of us who do, just a small sign that someone is listening can make a huge difference.
