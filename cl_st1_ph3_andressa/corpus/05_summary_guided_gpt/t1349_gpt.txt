I’m 19, from Egypt, and I feel like my life is already over because of how I look. My face, my body, my physical traits – all the things I can’t change – feel like a curse that ruined any chance at relationships or sex. Every time I see couples holding hands or hear people talk casually about their sex lives, it breaks my heart. It’s like I’m being reminded that I’m excluded from something everyone else gets to have.

This pain has twisted me. I used to think of myself as a good person, but now I catch myself feeling drawn to other people’s suffering, like it somehow reflects my own. I cry almost every day. I feel traumatized and exhausted. I can’t attempt suicide because of my faith – I believe it would send me to Hell. I just want to know how to cope, how to get help, because I feel completely lost.
