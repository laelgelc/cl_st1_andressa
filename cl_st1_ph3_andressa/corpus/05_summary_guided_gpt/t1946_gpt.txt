I’m 20 and it feels like I’ve never had a real friend who actually liked *me*. People I thought were friends either shut me down, picked fights, or only liked this watered‑down, fake version of me. The second I’m honest or show more of myself, it’s like I become “too much” and they pull away.

I have BPD and autism, and it makes everything feel so intense and confusing. I constantly feel misunderstood, like we’re speaking different languages. I don’t know how to be “easy” to be around.

Lately I’ve been thinking maybe if I just changed everything about how I look—lost weight, different clothes, makeup, contacts, even taking out my septum ring—people might at least pretend to like me. I’m so tired of feeling unlikable and impossible to be around.
