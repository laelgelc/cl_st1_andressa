I’m 27 and I feel like I’ve been lonely for as long as I can remember. A year ago I moved to a new country hoping a fresh start would make me happier, but nothing really changed inside. I have a job here, and I’m grateful for it, but it doesn’t feel like enough to make life feel meaningful.

I don’t have family, friends, or a partner here. Most days I just go to work and come back to an empty place. I miss my mom so much it hurts, but I don’t want to go back home either. I feel stuck between two places and belonging to neither.

Sometimes it feels like life is happening somewhere else, to other people, and I’m just watching from the outside, not really living at all.
