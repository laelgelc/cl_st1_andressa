Instead, I was dragged back and forth between two far‑away continents, always having to start over, always trying to just survive.

I can’t shake the feeling that it should never have gotten this bad. That if someone had actually paid attention and cared enough to get me real help, I wouldn’t be here now feeling this way. I really do feel like my family failed me.
