I’ve been realizing how much my appearance affects my life, and it’s really getting to me. I’m not ugly, but I am overweight, and I’ve always noticed how much easier things seem for attractive people. Others just naturally make an effort to talk to them, include them, get to know them.  

I’ve started losing weight and, even though I’m still far from where I want to be, I can already see people treating me a bit more kindly. It just makes me sad how superficial people are.
