I feel like I’m going to be alone forever. I keep trying to make friends – I’ve gone to local groups, met people for coffee, hung out a few times – but it never turns into anything lasting. They stop contacting me, or I can tell their romantic relationships are their only real priority. It makes me feel like friendship is just optional background noise for most people.

On top of that, I still carry a lot from being bullied in the past. It’s hard to be vulnerable or honest about how lonely I am without feeling embarrassed or pathetic. I keep stopping myself from reaching out because I’m scared of being rejected again.

I’m so disillusioned. It feels like so many people just don’t care. I just want to meet genuinely kind, ethical people who actually have feelings, integrity, and want to do good. I’m tired of feeling like I’m asking for too much.
