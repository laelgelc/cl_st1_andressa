I wake up every day feeling like a piece of shit. First thing I see is my phone with no texts, no plans, nothing to look forward to. It’s like this empty confirmation that I’m not really important to anyone’s day.

I technically have friends, but it feels so messed up. With some of them, I feel fake because I don’t even like their personalities that much. I hang out, laugh, talk, and the whole time there’s this voice in my head going, “You don’t actually like them.” Then I feel guilty, like I’m a horrible person for thinking that.

With the ones I do genuinely like, I still feel incomplete and unloved. They all have closer friends than me, people they talk to more, hang out with more. We barely talk or meet up, and I feel like some side character in their lives. I need more consistency, but I never get it.

Sometimes it feels like I don’t truly like anyone and no one truly likes me as a friend. I even catch myself feeling this irrational hate toward my own friends, and then I spiral, wondering if they secretly feel the same way about me.

When I get home, it’s just silence. No messages, no “how was your day,” no plans. I try to do things I think I’ll enjoy, but they never feel as fun as I imagined in my head, which just ruins it and makes me feel even more empty.

I wish I had someone I could really talk to, someone who actually wants to be there. But at the same time, I don’t want to be texting constantly or on the phone all day. So I’m stuck in this weird place where I crave connection but can’t seem to handle it, and it just leaves me feeling incredibly lonely.
