I’ve always chosen to be alone. Even as a kid, I never really tried to make friends or get close to anyone. Now I’m in college and nothing’s changed—I spend all my time by myself, and it honestly feels normal to me. I do have social anxiety, but the weird part is that even if I didn’t, I don’t think I’d want to be around people anyway. I don’t feel any desire to connect. I just want to be left alone.
