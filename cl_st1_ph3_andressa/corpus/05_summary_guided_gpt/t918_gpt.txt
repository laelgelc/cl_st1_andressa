I’m 25 and lately I feel like I’ve hit this wall where I finally see how much effort absolutely everything takes, and it’s honestly crushing.

Nothing in my life feels like it just… fits, or flows, or is “for me.” If I want a genuine apology from a partner, it turns into this exhausting conversation where I feel like I’m begging to be understood. If I want to be treated fairly at work, I have to constantly advocate for myself and push and push just to get basic respect. Financially, it feels like I’m climbing a greased wall – every step forward takes so much energy, and I’m still nowhere near any kind of “success.”

Even “happiness” feels like another task on the to‑do list. I have to schedule it, make time for it, push myself to do the things that are “good for me” instead of just naturally enjoying anything. There’s no part of my life that feels easy or effortless or like it’s just happening in a good way without me forcing it.

I already struggle with mental health and I know I lean toward negative thinking, but this feels deeper than just a bad mood. I’m so tired. I’m tired of having to fight for every scrap of good, every bit of peace. I keep wishing that just one good thing would happen without me having to drag it into existence. Just one thing that would come naturally and remind me that life can actually be beautiful and not just a constant grind.

I used to use substances every day, and for a while it felt like that was my shortcut – like it was the one thing that made life bearable and took the edge off all this effort. But in the end it just made everything worse and piled on more problems, and I know it could have killed me if I’d kept going.

I don’t really know what I’m hoping for by writing this. I guess I just want to know if anyone else feels this way – like life is only effort and no grace, and you’re just so, so tired of forcing everything.
