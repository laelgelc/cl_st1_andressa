I feel so stuck between wanting to be around people and wanting to disappear. I crave connection, but the second I’m with others I feel like an outcast, like I don’t really belong there. I keep thinking I’m just a burden, that I’m “nobody,” and that my sadness ruins the mood for everyone else.

I’m scared to open up because I feel like if I say I’m really sad or depressed, people will just roll their eyes or tell me to get over it. So I stay quiet, but that just makes me feel more alone. I don’t know how I’m supposed to feel or what to do with any of this.
