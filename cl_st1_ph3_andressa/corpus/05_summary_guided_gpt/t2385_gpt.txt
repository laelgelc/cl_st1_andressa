People keep worrying about me being lonely, but honestly, I’m choosing this. Being alone just feels easier and more real. When it’s just me, I can deal with my own emotions without feeling like I’m dumping them on someone else or needing to twist myself into something I’m not.

That doesn’t mean I never want affection. Sometimes I do wish I had a hug, or someone to smile at me and mean it. But most of the time, I’d rather not have to navigate other people’s moods, expectations, or emotional baggage. It’s exhausting trying to manage my own stuff and theirs too.

I’m not suicidal. I know that in the big picture, my death wouldn’t really change much, but I still don’t want to die. I just want to live my life my way and “do me,” without pressure to be more social or more connected than I actually want to be.

If I do end up genuinely caring about someone, that’s rare. And if that happens, it should mean something and be appreciated.
