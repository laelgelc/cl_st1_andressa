It feels like everyone else’s life turns into some kind of story, and mine is just… static. When I look around, people either seem to “make it” – careers, marriages, kids, adventures – or they crash and burn with drugs, jail, homelessness, all that. I’m not in either category. I’m just stuck in this weird middle, like a purgatory.

I don’t have a good career. I’ve never dated. I don’t travel, I don’t do anything exciting or impressive. But I also haven’t done anything terrible, I’m not out committing crimes or ruining my life. I’m just there. Existing.

It makes me feel like a grey blob: not successful, not a disaster, just… nothing worth mentioning. No big wins, no dramatic failures. Sometimes I wonder if this “in between” is even a real life, or if I’m just drifting until something finally changes, if it ever does.
