I’m 21, a guy, still a virgin with barely any dating experience, and I feel like I’ve completely derailed my life over the past year.

Last summer actually felt… good for once. I was on track for a promotion at work, doing well in school, going to the gym regularly, and I had this coworker who I really liked. She was shy, really attractive to me, into the same stuff I was, and she was genuinely friendly. It felt like the first time someone like that actually saw me.

Then she moved away for college. I was disappointed but I tried to accept it and move on. Later she came back and things changed. She started acting flirty, we sexted, we talked about meeting up. It felt surreal, like all the times I’d been lonely and invisible were finally paying off. But every time we tried to make plans, something came up and it fell through.

I got impatient and anxious. It felt like this was my one and only shot at something real. I ended up confessing my feelings to her. She replied with something like “I don’t catch feelings.” That hurt way more than I expected. I felt stupid, like I’d misread everything.

I made it worse. I got drunk and sent her nasty, forceful messages. Stuff I’m honestly ashamed of. She blocked me, and I don’t blame her at all. I ruined whatever we had, and I confirmed every fear I had about myself.

Since then, everything’s gone downhill. I quit my job because the emotional toll was too much. I’m still in school but I have no idea what I’m doing with my future anymore. I stopped working out. I barely talk to anyone. I just isolate and replay everything in my head.

It feels like whenever my life starts to come together, it just falls apart. I feel hopeless and lost, like I messed up the only chance I’ll ever get at love or even basic connection. I don’t really know how to move on from this or believe that things can get better.
