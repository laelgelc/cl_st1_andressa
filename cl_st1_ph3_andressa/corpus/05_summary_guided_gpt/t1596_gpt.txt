I’ve been ghosted three times now. And once, a guy dumped me for another girl. My most recent boyfriend suddenly blocked or removed me with no explanation. I guess that’s being ghosted too. One day he’s saying all these sweet things, and the next it’s like I never existed.

I don’t understand how they can do that so easily. How can someone say “I love you” and then act like my feelings mean nothing? I keep wondering what’s wrong with me, why they can just throw me away like that and not seem to care at all.
