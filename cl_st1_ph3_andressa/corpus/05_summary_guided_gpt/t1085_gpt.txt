I’ve spent years keeping my distance from people, telling myself it was because I’m very selective and most people just aren’t a good fit for me. Now I’m stuck wondering if I should try to let new people in or just keep living the way I have been.

What holds me back is this constant fear of being abandoned or hurt again. It’s happened before, and it’s hard not to expect it to happen again. On top of that, almost everyone I know feels so similar to each other that it makes me hesitate even more.

I’m not completely hopeless, but I’m really conflicted and tired of feeling this way.
