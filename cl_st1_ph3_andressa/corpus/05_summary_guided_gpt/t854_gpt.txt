I feel so painfully lonely most days. I just want someone to actually choose me, to come up to me and really want to know who I am. I keep imagining what it would be like to have a partner, to share little moments, to not always be the one watching from the outside.

Everywhere I look, people are in relationships, holding hands, laughing together. It makes the emptiness hit even harder. I’m scared I’m always going to be that friend who never finds love, the one everyone assumes will just “get there eventually,” but never does.

I think about marriage, kids, a home filled with warmth and love, and it feels like some distant fantasy that was never meant for me. I wish I knew what I was missing, what everyone else seems to have. Underneath all of it, I just want to feel wanted.
