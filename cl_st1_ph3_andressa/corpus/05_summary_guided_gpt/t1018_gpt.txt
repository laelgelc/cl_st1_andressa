I feel like everything has kind of fallen apart at once and I don’t really have anyone to talk to about it.

I’ve always struggled to make and keep friends, but I had this one best friend, “J,” for about seven years. She was the one person I really trusted, the one constant. Then a while back a new coworker came along and, for a while, it felt like I’d finally made another close friend. I let my guard down. I actually thought, “Maybe things are finally changing for me.”

Then out of nowhere she turned on me and made a false accusation at work. I had a completely clean record before that, and because of what she said I ended up on a final warning. It was like getting the rug pulled out from under me. I felt betrayed and humiliated. But I kept telling myself, “At least I still have J.”

In December, J and I went through a rough patch. I honestly thought we’d worked through it and were okay again. But over the last month she’s been so distant. She barely responds to my messages, and when she does, it’s short and delayed. She says there are family health issues going on, and I don’t doubt that, but I still feel like I’ve lost her too.

Meanwhile, I just lost a family friend I really loved to cancer, work is a constant source of stress now, and I have this big opportunity coming up that could really affect my future. I feel like I’m carrying all of this alone and I just want to talk to my best friend about it, the way I used to.

I do understand that J has her own life and problems, and I don’t want to be selfish or demanding. I just feel so alone. I don’t even know what I’m looking for posting this. I just needed to get it out somewhere, because right now it feels like I don’t really have anyone left.
