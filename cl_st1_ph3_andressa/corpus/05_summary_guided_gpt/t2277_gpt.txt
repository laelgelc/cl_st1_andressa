I’m almost 19 and I feel like I’ve already messed up my whole “social life” storyline before it even started.

In high school I only ever had one or two acquaintances. Not real friends, just people I’d occasionally talk to. Now I’m in college and it somehow feels even worse. I literally have nobody. I go to class, go home, repeat. No one texts me, no one asks me to hang out. I’m just… there.

What makes it worse is that I feel completely socially inept. I’m so uncomfortable meeting new people. I don’t know what to say, how to act, how to keep conversations going. And the honest truth is that I don’t even want to put in the effort to build friendships from scratch. It sounds exhausting. I wish people would just magically appear in my life and care about me, but obviously that’s not how it works.

At the same time, I’m terrified of what that means for my future. I’m scared of living and dying alone, of going through life with nobody who really knows me. In my head, that equals a sad, unsatisfying life. I see couples in shows and movies and I can’t help but want that too – a girlfriend who actually wants to be with me, who I can share emotional and physical intimacy with. But then I look at myself and think, “If I stay like this, I’ll never have friends, let alone a relationship.”

I don’t feel naturally suited to social success at all. Some people just seem built for it, and I’m not one of them. But the fear of being lonely forever is always there, and I don’t know what to do with it.
