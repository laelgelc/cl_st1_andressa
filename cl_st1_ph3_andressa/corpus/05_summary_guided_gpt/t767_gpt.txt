I feel like I’ve been craving a real, emotional, loving relationship since I was 19, and it just never happens. I’ve never felt loved, chosen, or truly seen by anyone in that way. It feels like I’m standing outside a window watching everyone else get what I’ve been begging the universe for, and it’s breaking me down.

I’m so tired. I’m angry at myself, at life, at whatever force keeps dangling hope in front of me and then yanking it away. People say things like “love yourself and love will find you” and it just feels so empty and unrealistic to me now. I’ve tried. I really have. It hasn’t changed anything.

I keep having this thought that maybe I’ll never be loved, never be married, and I’m trying to make peace with that, but I know how harmful that belief is. Still, it feels more and more like a reality. The loneliness is so overwhelming that sometimes I think, if it gets any worse, maybe it’d be okay to just… stop. Stop existing. Stop feeling this constant ache.

The distress is so intense it feels physical—like a tightness in my chest, a weight on my body, a constant knot in my stomach. I’m exhausted from hoping, from waiting, from feeling like this day after day. I don’t know how much longer I can keep going like this.
