I’ve been in such a dark place lately that it scares me how close I feel to actually breaking. It’s like I’m right on the edge of doing something irreversible. I wrote something the other day that’s been stuck in my head: I keep imagining that when I finally give up, my pain will be so huge and violent that it’ll leave a mark on everything around me. Like the only way anyone will ever really see me is if my suffering explodes into something they can’t ignore.

I feel like an invisible man most days. I move through life and no one really notices or cares. I just want to be seen, to be loved, to matter to someone. And it hurts that it feels like the only way I’ll ever be visible is through the aftermath of my own destruction.
