I’ve always been a loner, but not by choice. It’s just how my life ended up. There are a lot of downsides to that, but one strange “pro” really hit me last year. When everything shut down, my life and friendships didn’t really change. I was already used to being alone, so while others were suddenly isolated, I was just… living my normal. In a weird way, that actually made me feel better for once.

If everything shut down again, missing social hangouts wouldn’t be my biggest concern, and I know other loners can relate. I really feel for people who struggle with being alone. Loners get overlooked, but we have some amazing, rare traits and we’re very unique.
