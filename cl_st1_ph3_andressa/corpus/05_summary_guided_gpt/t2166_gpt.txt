I don’t even know why I’m writing this, except that I want someone to know I existed and what I’m going through. My father hates me, my wife betrayed my trust, and I’m drowning in debt I can’t possibly pay off with what I earn. On top of that, I got dengue—breakbone fever—and now I’ve got complications that I’m told could be fatal if they’re not treated. I feel like I’ve run out of strength. I can’t fight anymore. I just didn’t want to disappear completely unnoticed.
