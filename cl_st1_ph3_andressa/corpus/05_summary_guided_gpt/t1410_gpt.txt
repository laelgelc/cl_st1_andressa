I’m 47, single, and honestly don’t have any real friends. Most of my “social life” is just me trying to make small talk with women around my age when I’m out running errands. It usually goes nowhere, just polite conversation and that’s it.

Recently I went to a new Trader Joe’s and ended up chatting with a cashier who was clearly much younger than me. I thought it was just a normal interaction, but when I went back another time she greeted me really warmly, like she was actually happy to see me. It kind of caught me off guard because I’m not used to that reaction from anyone, let alone a younger woman.

Same thing with my beard trimmer. She’s 23, and I get along really well with her and the other women who work there. They’ve complimented my beard and just seem genuinely friendly and comfortable around me.

All of this has me thinking: I seem to connect better with women who are significantly younger than me. I don’t know why, but that’s how it feels. Part of me wonders if it would really be such a bad thing if I ended up in a successful relationship with a much younger woman. I’m not talking about anything creepy or forced, just… if it happened naturally. Is that wrong?
