I don’t even know where to start anymore. I’ve been lonely for so long that it just feels like my default state now. I’m an adult, not in school anymore, and work doesn’t feel like a safe place to try to make friends. Everyone there has their own lives, and it feels risky mixing “real” connection with a professional environment.

I’ve tried going out, joining clubs, trying to be proactive. But the clubs I find are mostly full of older people, and while they’re usually nice enough, I never feel like I actually fit in. When I do meet people my age, they just… don’t stick around. We talk once or twice, maybe hang out, and then they drift away, stop replying, and that’s it. Every “maybe this time” turns into another dead end.

It’s been like this for about six years now. No real friends, no sense of belonging, no community. I’m honestly sick of it. Sometimes I wonder if I just don’t belong in this country at all. I keep asking myself: when did life stop happening for me? And what am I supposed to do now?
