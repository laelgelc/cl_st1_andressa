I’m an 18-year-old woman and I feel like such an outcast. I don’t really have any friends, and what makes it feel worse is that I almost never see other girls like me. When I think of “loners,” I mostly see guys, online and in real life. It makes me feel like there’s something especially wrong with me for being a girl and still ending up alone.

In high school I was that girl who always sat alone at lunch. I could go weeks without speaking to anyone besides teachers. From grade 10 until graduation, I literally had no friends. It was like everyone else had their groups and I was just… there.

Now I’m in my first year of university and I really want it to be different. I want to actually make these years count and have friends and memories, not just drift through like I did in high school. But I’m scared it’s going to be the exact same thing all over again.

Being alone is easier in some ways, but I’m embarrassed by it. I just want to know if there are other girls who feel like this too.
