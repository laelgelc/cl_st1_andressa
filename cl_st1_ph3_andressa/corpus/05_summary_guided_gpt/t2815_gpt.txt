I’m midway through Yale’s free online course on happiness and well-being (found it through a CNN article that linked to Coursera), and I wanted to share because it’s actually been pretty eye-opening.

I’ve learned a lot so far, especially about how many of my “intuitive” ideas about what would make me feel better are just… wrong. It’s weird realizing how often I’ve chased things I thought would help, only to find out the science says otherwise. The way the course breaks it all down is really captivating, and it’s made me reflect a lot on how I’ve been living and what I focus on.

I honestly recommend it to anyone, even if you feel like you don’t have the time. I went in a bit skeptical, but I really think it’s the kind of thing that could change who I am in the long run.
