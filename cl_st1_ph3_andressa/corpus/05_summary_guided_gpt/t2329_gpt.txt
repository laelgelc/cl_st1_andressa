I’m 18 and I feel like my shyness is finally catching up with me in a really painful way.

I’ve always been the quiet one, but it used to be easier because I had a close childhood friend group. Over time they changed a lot and started getting into bad influences and constant drama. They’d bring around guys who made me uncomfortable and didn’t respect my boundary about wanting some distance from men. I felt like I was always on edge, stressed, and not really seen or respected, so I eventually cut them off.

It did make my life less stressful, but now I’m just… lonely. I have some mutuals I’m comfortable around, but we’re not actually close. Sometimes I miss my old friends and the feeling of belonging, even though I know exactly why I left and that it was the right choice.

I’m trying to be more social and meet new people. I’ve actually met some genuinely kind people, but my shyness gets in the way. I struggle with eye contact, I don’t know what to say, and I overthink everything. I end up looking uninterested or distant, even when I really want to connect. I also tend to avoid attention, even though part of me wishes I could just relax and be seen.

I don’t really know how to break out of this. I’m tired of feeling so alone but I also feel stuck inside myself.
