About once a month I get hit with this really heavy wave of loneliness and panic that I’m going to be alone forever. It feels awful, but it always passes after a while, and then I just go back to doing nothing about it.  

Part of it is that changing my situation feels like it would require changing who I am at a really fundamental level. And I honestly don’t think those parts of me can change. I’m convinced that 99% of people dislike my personality, and if I’m honest, I dislike 99% of people too.  

I feel lonely for a person who doesn’t exist, or is so rare that it might as well be impossible. And even if I did somehow find someone I truly clicked with, I’m pretty sure I’d still cause problems and things would just become difficult in a different way.  

I don’t even want romantic love. I just want companionship.
