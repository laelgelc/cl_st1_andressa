I’m so tired of this same pattern repeating over and over. At first people act like I’m important, like they’re genuinely interested in me. They message a lot, seem excited to talk, make me feel like I actually matter. Then, out of nowhere, it just… stops. They pull away, ignore me, act like I was never there. 

It leaves me feeling abandoned and confused every time. I keep wondering: do people really like others at all, or is it all just a façade? Was the eventual ignoring always there underneath, or does it grow over time? 

I don’t understand why someone would bother getting close, letting me care and get attached, only to emotionally distance themselves and hurt me like this.
