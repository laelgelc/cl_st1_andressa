To anyone who feels numb, lost, or in pain right now: I really, genuinely understand where you are.

I’ve had so many moments where I thought I’d finally figured it out, finally found the answer that would fix everything. Every time, it felt like I was standing on solid ground for a second… and then it crumbled, and I was back to feeling lost again. That cycle is exhausting in a way that’s hard to explain.

There have been times where it felt like I had to choose between “life and feelings” – like either I keep going and shut everything down inside, or I let myself feel and risk completely falling apart. I know what it’s like to live with a constant dull ache in your chest, like something heavy that never moves. I know what it’s like to cry until there’s nothing left, until your body is tired and your mind is empty, but the pain is still there underneath it all.

I don’t have solutions. I’m not here to pretend I know how to fix anyone’s life, including my own. But I do know how to survive. And if there’s one thing I want, it’s to use my own pain for something other than just hurting me. If I can make even one person feel a tiny bit less alone, then some part of all this will mean something.

You are not truly alone, even if it feels that way. I see you, I care, and I’m here in solidarity with you. If I can help you in any way, even just by listening, I want to. I’m sending love to anyone who needs it.
