Every year feels like a copy‑paste of the last. I don’t have any real friends, my phone is basically silent, and I almost never get calls or texts that mean anything. On the rare occasions someone seems interested in me, they disappear after a few weeks. The only people who stick around are the ones who treat me badly or bully me. Everything just feels awful. The only things I look forward to are junk food and random impulse buys, like they’re the closest thing I have to hanging out with a friend.
