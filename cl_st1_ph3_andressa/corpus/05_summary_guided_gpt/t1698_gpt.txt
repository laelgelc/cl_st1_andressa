I feel like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me and I can’t tell if it’s in my head or if the universe is just confirming it over and over.

I’m so tired of feeling unwanted. I keep wondering if I’m just unlovable, boring, or if this is some kind of punishment for all the mistakes I’ve made. Other people talk about these “miracles” in love – random chances, being in the right place at the right time, someone suddenly seeing them and choosing them. I watch it happen for everyone else and it just… never seems to be my turn.

I just want someone who truly chooses me. Not because I’m convenient, not because they’re lonely, but because they look at me and decide I’m worth it. Instead I feel stuck in my own head, looping through the same questions: what’s wrong with me, why not me, what am I missing?

There’s this girl I can’t stop thinking about. I love so many things about her – the way she looks, her personality, how much she loves music. The way her nervous British accent slips out sometimes, her smile, just her presence in a room. Her hugs feel like safety, and her voice is so soothing it calms me down without even trying. 

But all of that just makes it hurt more. The more I care, the more I feel this sharp reminder that she’s not really mine, that maybe no one ever will be. It makes me question whether I’m even meant to be happy at all, or if I’m just here to watch everyone else find what I can’t seem to have.
