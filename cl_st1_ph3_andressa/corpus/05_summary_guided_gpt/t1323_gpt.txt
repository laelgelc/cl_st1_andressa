I’m a senior in high school and lately it feels like everything is piling up at once. My mental health has been getting worse and I don’t really have anyone I feel close enough to talk to about it. I go to school, pretend I’m fine, then come home and just feel this heavy sadness. It’s like everyone else is out having fun, hanging out with their friends, living some kind of “normal” teenage life, and I’m just stuck in my room, alone.

On top of that, schoolwork is overwhelming. There’s always another assignment, another test, another thing to stress about. Sometimes it hurts so much emotionally that it actually feels physical, like my heart is cracking open or something. I don’t really know how to describe it better than that.

There’s this girl who lives near me that I’m really interested in. She’s incredibly smart, really focused on her future, and she actually loves learning the way I do. I’ve wanted that kind of connection for a long time, someone who gets that side of me. But I don’t think she likes me very much. She’s really reserved and hard to read, and I’m scared I’m just imagining something that isn’t there.

I don’t know what to do about her, or about any of this. Should I try to talk to her more, or just leave it alone?
