I ended a 7‑month relationship at the end of summer. At first he seemed so sweet, then he basically told me I wasn’t enough for him. It broke something in me.  

After a few months I tried to move on, started dating again after Christmas, and I actually had high hopes for this new guy. Now he’s slowly ghosting me. No explanation, just less and less contact. It hurts more than I want to admit.  

I keep asking myself why I’m so easy to let go of, why I’m never enough for anyone. I know I’m supposed to “focus on myself,” but I feel this constant pressure because I want to get married before my parents die. That thought is always there. It’s gotten so bad I’ve started thinking about just settling for someone, and that scares me.
