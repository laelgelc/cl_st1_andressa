Every day feels like the same loop: work, go home, cook, eat, do whatever needs doing, sleep. Repeat. All of it alone.  

I used to actually enjoy cooking. Now it just feels like this harsh reminder that there’s nobody to share the food with, no conversation across the table, no “how was your day?” Just me, eating in silence.  

When I try to talk about it, people brush it off as “normal” or “that’s just adulthood,” but they all seem to have someone to go home to. It makes me feel like my feelings don’t matter, like nobody really cares.  

Lately it’s hard not to wonder what the point of any of this is.
