Summer just makes everything hurt more. It’s like the whole world is out celebrating – parties, trips, festivals, barbecues – and I’m always on the outside looking in. I’m never the one people choose or invite. When I try to make plans, they get rejected, or people just ghost me and leave me hanging, waiting for a “maybe” that turns into a silent “no” when they find someone more “shiny.”

After going through this over and over, I’ve started to hate summer. For everyone else it’s joy and connection; for me it’s just a season where my loneliness feels louder than ever.
