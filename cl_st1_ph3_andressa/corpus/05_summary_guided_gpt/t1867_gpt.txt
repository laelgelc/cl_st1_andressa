Most days feel like I’m just existing, not really living. I don’t have many hobbies. I listen to music, I sleep, and sometimes I’ll play games or watch something, but it’s just to kill time. None of it actually makes me feel better, it just makes the hours pass.

I work full-time. The pay is decent, but I don’t like the job. The only part I really appreciate is being alone on my breaks, where I don’t have to pretend I’m okay or try to be social.

I only have one friend, and they’re usually busy, so we barely see each other. I’ve tried to make new friends through their friend group and at social events, but it never goes anywhere. I feel like I have nothing to talk about, like my interests and knowledge are so limited that I just end up quiet and awkward.

It often feels like I’m only still here for my family. I don’t really believe anything in my life is going to change, but the idea of just “disappearing” feels selfish because I know it would hurt them and make them worry. I’m stuck between not wanting this life and not wanting to cause them pain.
