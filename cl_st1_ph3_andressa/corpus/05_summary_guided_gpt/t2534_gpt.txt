I feel like I’ve just ripped my own heart out.

I chose to walk away from someone I love more than anything, because staying in their life hurt too much. Being around them, talking to them, knowing I could never really have what I wanted with them… it was like slowly drowning. So I made the decision to leave, thinking it would protect me. But now it just feels like I’ve lost everything.

They were my only real daily connection. The only person who made me feel seen, wanted, and like I mattered. My routine revolved around them – messages, calls, little check-ins. Now it’s just silence. The loneliness is crushing. I cry constantly, and it actually hurts in my chest and stomach, like grief you can physically feel.

I keep fighting the urge to reach out, to just send one message, hear one word back. But I know I can’t. I know it would just drag everything out and hurt more in the long run. Still, it’s so hard when I have no one else. No support system, no close friends to lean on. Just this emptiness.

To anyone who feels this way too: I’m so sorry. I wouldn’t wish this on anyone. And if you read all of this, thank you for giving me a tiny moment of feeling heard.
