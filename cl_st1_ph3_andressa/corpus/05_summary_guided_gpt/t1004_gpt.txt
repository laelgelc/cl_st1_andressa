I feel like I’m living two different lives. Online, I can talk to strangers, make friends, and feel like I belong somewhere. But in real life, it’s like I don’t exist. I used to think my husband’s friends were my friends too, but now I see I was wrong. They all hang out and game together, and I only find out after the fact, or when I ask to be included. It hurts that I have to ask, and that they never think to invite me on their own.

What makes it worse is my husband doesn’t really see the problem. I feel unwanted in my own social circle, like I’m just an extra. Sometimes I catch myself thinking that if I disappeared, no one would really notice or care.
