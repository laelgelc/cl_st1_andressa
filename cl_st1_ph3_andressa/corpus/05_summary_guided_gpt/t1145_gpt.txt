I’m 17, Indian, and I feel like I’m just drifting through life completely disconnected from everyone around me.

I technically have “friends,” but I don’t really trust them. I’ve seen how they talk about each other’s personal stuff behind their backs and make jokes out of it. So I keep everything to myself. I’m always scared that if I open up, I’ll just become the next joke. It’s easier to stay quiet and pretend I’m fine.

Most days I feel like I’m just acting. I overthink every word before I say it, trying to be what I think people expect. I don’t even know who I actually am anymore. It’s like I’ve lost the real me somewhere along the way.

I go on morning runs just to be alone. My parents don’t really get me either; it feels like we live in different worlds. I feel like a burden in my own house, like I’m just… there. No one to love, no one who loves me. I’ve never been in a relationship, and a part of me keeps thinking that if I had someone, at least I’d have one person to talk to, one person who might understand.

I don’t fit in where I am. People around me don’t share my interests, and when I see others connecting so easily, I feel like there’s something seriously wrong with me. I try “self-improvement,” but it just feels fake, like I’m forcing myself into some routine that doesn’t belong to me.

I’m scared I’ll go my whole life without ever finding someone who truly understands me.
