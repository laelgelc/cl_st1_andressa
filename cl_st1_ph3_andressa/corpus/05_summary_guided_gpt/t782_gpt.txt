I’ve always done most things alone – cinema, library, shopping, concerts, museums. At this point I even prefer going to the movies by myself. But one thing really scared me: eating alone at a restaurant. The idea of sitting there, facing other people, feeling judged or awkward, kept me from trying it for a long time.

Recently I finally did it. I went to a restaurant, ordered a tarte flambée and a glass of Pinot Gris… and it actually went well. No one stared, nothing weird happened. It felt strangely freeing.
