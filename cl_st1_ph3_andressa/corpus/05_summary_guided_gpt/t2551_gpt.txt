I don’t know what I’m supposed to be aiming for anymore. I have a few friends, but I feel like I take them for granted and I still feel lonely even when I’m with them. What I really want is a boyfriend. It feels like having one would make life feel complete, or at least less empty. I’m so touch-starved it hurts sometimes – I rarely even get hugs. I just want to feel like someone’s first choice, like I’m really loved, like I actually have something worth living for.

I keep going in circles: should I focus on making more friends, on trying to find a boyfriend, or just on learning to accept being alone? People tell me I have time, that I’ll find someone eventually, but I don’t understand why I have to wait. My social anxiety makes being around people so hard that it all feels hopeless. I don’t know what to do with myself or what direction to take.
