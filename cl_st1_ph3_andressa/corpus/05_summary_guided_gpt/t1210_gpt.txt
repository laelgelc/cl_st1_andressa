I’m so tired of hearing “you’ll find someone one day” from people whose lives are clearly going fine. It just feels like empty noise at this point. They say it, then go back to their partners, their friends, their plans. Meanwhile I’m still here, stuck in the same place I’ve been since I was 15.

I’ve basically been lonely and miserable for as long as I can remember. Every day is the same spiral: wake up alone, go through the motions, try to distract myself, then end the day feeling even more alone. It’s exhausting.

I don’t want anything crazy. I just want a good life with someone. Someone to come home to, to share stupid little moments with, to not feel like I’m just existing on the sidelines while everyone else actually lives.

I don’t believe it’s going to “get better” anymore. I don’t even know if it can. I’m just tired and frustrated and so, so lonely.
