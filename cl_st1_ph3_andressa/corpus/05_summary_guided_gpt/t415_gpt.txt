I’m 19, French, a student, and objectively my life is… fine. I have friends, my days are pretty stable and calm, nothing dramatic is going on. From the outside, I probably look okay. But inside I feel incredibly lonely and weirdly overwhelmed by everything.

The idea of actually saying how I feel makes me cringe at myself, like they’d think I’m being ridiculous or overdramatic. So I keep everything to myself, and it just keeps piling up.

For a while I thought maybe being in a relationship would fix this, like having “that one person” I could talk to about anything. But I know that’s not some magic solution, and it’s not like partners just fall from the sky anyway.

Lately this loneliness is twisting me into someone I don’t like. I’ve been having darker, more disturbing thoughts, and I’m starting to feel a kind of disgust toward other people and toward myself. I feel more on the sidelines than ever, like everyone else got some manual on how to be a normal human and I missed it. It really feels like there’s something wrong with me.

This is my first Reddit post. I’m kind of hoping that talking to a stranger might help, even though I’m not great at chatting. Still, if anyone relates or just wants to talk, I’d actually be glad to get to know someone.
