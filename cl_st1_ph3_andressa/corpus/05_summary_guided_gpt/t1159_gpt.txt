It feels like every time my life has blown up, my entire social world has gone with it.

After I left an abusive partner, my friends just… evaporated. Then came a period of drug abuse that ended really badly. I’m clean now, but that version of me destroyed what was left of my friendships. Then another nasty breakup, and again, everyone disappeared. It’s like major life events hit “reset” on my relationships every time.

Now I’m in a new town and technically part of a community, but I don’t have any close friends. There’s one person I’m slowly getting closer to, someone who actually seems comfortable around me, and instead of leaning into that I’m pulling back. I keep telling myself I should “wait until I’m a better person” before I let anyone get close.

I deleted Facebook because it stressed me out. I kept a few numbers, but I rarely reach out. I tell myself they’re better off without me.

Lately I’ve been thinking maybe I’m just a bad person who doesn’t deserve relationships or friends. That maybe I’m meant to be alone, and that staying isolated is safer for everyone. If I don’t let anyone close, I can’t hurt them. And a part of me feels like that’s what I deserve.
