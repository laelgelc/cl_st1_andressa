Every time I reach out or try to do something for myself, it turns into conflict or guilt. The only people who have really made me feel cared for lately are his family. They’ve been kind and supportive in a way I’m not used to.

I want to move closer to them because I feel less alone around them, but my mom reacted so negatively. It just makes me feel like I’m selfish or broken for wanting to be somewhere that doesn’t hurt all the time.

I have BPD and I feel like it makes everything worse. I’m always scared I’m too much, too emotional, too unstable. I feel like a problem to everyone around me, like I’m hard to love or even be around. On top of that I was just diagnosed with PCOS, and I already have arthritis that threatens the one thing I’ve always wanted to do: be an artist. Even my dreams feel like they’re slipping away.
