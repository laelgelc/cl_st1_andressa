I’m 30 and it feels like I somehow fell through the cracks of life.

I don’t have siblings, cousins, or really any extended family. It’s just me. I’ve felt isolated since I was a kid, and instead of getting better with age, it feels like the loneliness is just getting heavier.

Friends tend to vanish as soon as they get into relationships, or they turn out to be unreliable. I try to put myself out there, make plans, show up for people, but it rarely seems to be mutual. It’s exhausting always being the one reaching out, and then being the one left behind.

On top of that, I’m an adult child of abusive parents, and the one serious relationship I had was also abusive. I’ve been in therapy for years, but it’s hard not to feel worn down. Dating feels dangerous, like I’m just setting myself up to be hurt again, so I keep my distance. But that just adds to the loneliness.

My dog is the one consistent source of comfort I have. I’m grateful for them, but I still wish I had a human who really understood what this feels like.

I’m mostly posting to ask: does anyone else relate to this kind of isolation?
