There’s this episode of Star Trek: Voyager where they have to cross a starless stretch of space. That’s what my life feels like right now.

I’m technically “around” people. I’m polite, I smile, I make small talk. From the outside I probably look friendly enough. But inside it feels like no one really sees me, or who I’ve become. It’s like I’m just a projection going through the motions while the real me drifts somewhere far away, unseen.

Right now it’s like I’m floating in a dark, empty void and all I can do is keep going. I’m trying to hold on to the belief that this is just a phase, that there’s something beyond this – a place with more “stars,” more people who actually connect with me.

I don’t want this lonely stretch to define me or defeat me. I just hope I can make it through.
