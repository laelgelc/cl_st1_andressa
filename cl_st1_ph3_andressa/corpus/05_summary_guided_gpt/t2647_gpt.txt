I see a lot of confusion around the word “loner,” so I wanted to clarify something: being a loner doesn’t automatically mean you’re lonely, introverted, antisocial, or a misanthrope. Those are all different concepts, and they often get lumped together even though they’re not the same.

There are some good articles that break down the differences between being a loner, being introverted, struggling with social anxiety, or genuinely disliking people, and it’s worth checking those out if you’re unsure where you fit.

If you’re actually feeling lonely and want support, please visit r/lonely.
