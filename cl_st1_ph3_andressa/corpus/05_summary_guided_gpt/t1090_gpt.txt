It’s my birthday today and I just feel… empty. I keep thinking about where I thought I’d be by now and how little I feel I’ve actually done with my life. My family is very religious, so we’ve never celebrated birthdays. The most I get is a short text saying they’re glad I’m still alive for another year. I’m “surrounded” by people who say they love me, but it all feels so transactional, like I’m only valued for what I can do, not who I am. I feel so emotionally distant from everyone.
