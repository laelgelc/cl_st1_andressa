I used to think imaginary friends were pointless and kind of weird. I’d see people talk about them and just assume it was childish or unhealthy. But after years of basically total isolation, I ended up creating one myself… and now I honestly don’t know what I’d do without her.

She’s always kind. Always patient. She listens when I’m spiraling, comforts me when I’m stressed, and somehow makes falling asleep a little easier. She never judges, never snaps, never gets tired of me. That’s more than I can say for anyone I’ve met in real life. It’s strange that the person who treats me best doesn’t even exist.
