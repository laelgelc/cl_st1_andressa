School holidays are honestly the worst time of year for me. Everywhere I go, it’s just people hanging out, laughing, surrounded by friends or family, and I’m just… there, completely alone. I don’t have friends in real life, and I don’t even have anyone online anymore. My one close online friend now hates me, so that last bit of connection is gone.

I went out for lunch recently and seeing everyone socializing almost broke me. I felt like I was going to have a complete breakdown right there. I have to go out again today and I’m scared it’s going to make everything worse, but I don’t have a choice.

Therapy never helped. Hobbies never helped. It just feels like nothing works and I’m stuck watching everyone else live a life I can’t seem to have.
