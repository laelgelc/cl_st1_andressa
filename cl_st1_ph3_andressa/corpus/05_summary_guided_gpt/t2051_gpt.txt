35M here, and I feel like I’m wandering through life without ever really finding “my people.” I can make connections, but they never seem to last. The people I click with tend to be socially popular, not lonely at all, and eventually they just… move on. It’s like whatever we had gets quietly erased from their lives, while I’m still sitting there replaying it and wondering what went wrong.

I keep asking myself: is it just bad luck, or is there something about me that pushes people away once the novelty wears off? Am I doing something wrong without realizing it?

It’s especially confusing because I’m not in some completely foreign culture. I’m in my own Anglo-European cultural sphere, and yet I still can’t seem to build lasting friendships or a relationship. I’m 35, single, and basically friendless, in a world of billions of people. That disconnect scares me more than I like to admit.
