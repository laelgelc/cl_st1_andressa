For a little while I actually felt happy and alive. My new boyfriend was staying with me and a friend was crashing here too, so I had people around, people to do things with. The apartment felt full and I didn’t feel so stuck in my own head.

Then by mid-January everything changed. My boyfriend started pulling away and eventually stopped coming over, and my friend went to rehab. Now it’s just me again and the silence is so loud.

I have really severe depression and my life feels like the same empty loop: go to work, come home, watch TV, eat, shower, sleep, repeat. It feels like I’m just existing, not living. I didn’t realize how much I needed friends until I suddenly had some around and then lost them.

Does anyone else barely have any friends and only now realize how important friendships actually are? How do you cope with this?
