I’m a second-year in college and somehow I feel more lonely now than I did as a freshman. My “only” friends are my roommate and a suitemate from last year, and even calling them friends feels weird sometimes because they both have their own social circles and are always busy. I’m happy they have that, but it just makes me feel even more like the extra person no one really needs around.

I really regret moving to an off-campus apartment. I thought it would be nice to have my own space and be more “grown up,” but it just feels like I cut myself off from everything. I go to campus, go to class, and then come back here alone. I feel like I’m watching college happen from the outside instead of actually living it.

I do have a long-distance girlfriend at another university, and I’m grateful for her, but I feel this huge need for people here, in person. I want friends I can grab dinner with or study with or just sit around and do nothing with. Phone calls and texts don’t fill that gap.

I’m rushing a sorority right now and I am so anxious I feel sick. The idea of not getting a bid makes me nauseous. It feels like this is my “last chance” to find a group, and if it doesn’t work out, I’m just going to be stuck like this. I’ve even thought about transferring, but I’m terrified I’d just end up even more isolated somewhere new.

Social media makes everything worse. I see people from my school posting pictures at parties, game days, brunches, whatever, and it makes me so jealous. It feels like everyone else is having the college experience I’m supposed to be having. My mental health is tanking. I cry almost every day. I say I hate it here all the time. I feel like I’m drowning and no one in my real life really knows.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just needed to tell someone how I feel.
