I’ve always been a dreamer. When I was 20, I decided I wanted to learn music. A lot of people doubted me, told me it would be too hard, that I’d started too late. It took a long time just to get my hands on any instruments, and even longer to figure out where to begin.

It’s been about five years of really trying, and only recently do I feel like I’ve actually started to find myself through it. I still see myself as a beginner, but for the first time I feel like I’m finally walking the path I always wanted.

I made a promise to my younger self that I wouldn’t abandon this dream, that I’d do everything I could to make it real. That promise is what keeps me going when I feel behind or discouraged.

The picture I mentioned isn’t an ad or anything like that. It’s just a small piece of my journey, something I hope might resonate with someone else who’s still trying to follow their own dream.
