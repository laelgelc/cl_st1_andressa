I feel like my whole world is crumbling and I don’t really know where else to put this.

I’ve always struggled with friendships. I’m shy, and for whatever reason I’ve only ever managed to have male friends. I’ve always wanted close female friends so badly, but it just never seems to work out. So the few friendships I do have mean everything to me. I’m the kind of person who gives 100%, who’s loyal and caring even when it’s not really reciprocated. I stick around, I show up, I try.

Now my main friend group is basically falling apart, and it’s happening in the worst way. One of the guys, while he was drunk, accused me of saying something I didn’t say. It was completely false. At first I thought it would blow over once he sobered up, but instead he somehow convinced himself it was true. He’s clinging to this version of events that never happened.

There were people there who heard everything and know I’m innocent. They’ve said as much. But it doesn’t matter. He’s still absolutely convinced, and he’s turned others in the group against me. They’ve started acting cold, distant… like I’ve suddenly become this awful person overnight.

The part that hurts the most is my best friend. He’s also close to the guy who accused me. I sent him a message asking if we could talk, just to clear the air, and he hasn’t even opened it. It’s been three weeks. Three weeks of silence from someone I thought would at least hear me out.

I feel abandoned and cornered. I keep cycling between panic and numbness. This group was pretty much my entire social life, and now it feels like it’s being ripped away over something I didn’t even do.

I’m trying to hide how much this is affecting me from my partner, because I don’t want to seem dramatic or needy, but inside I feel so, so lonely. It feels like withdrawing is my only option now, like I should just disappear quietly instead of fighting to stay somewhere I’m clearly not wanted.

I don’t know how to handle this or where to go from here.
