I feel like I’ve changed so much since my teens and early 20s. Back then, connecting with people felt natural. Now my anxiety gets in the way of everything. I get nervous, my eye contact is weird and awkward, and I can literally see people getting uncomfortable. It’s like I ruin every potential friendship. I know I have chances to make friends, but I keep self-sabotaging, and I’m starting to lose hope that I’ll ever fix this.
