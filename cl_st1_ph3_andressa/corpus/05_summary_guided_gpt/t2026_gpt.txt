I’m 20 and moved to another country for university about a year ago. I live alone and my life feels like it’s on a loop: go to class, come home, go to my fast-food job, come home again. The job drains me, and every time I finish a shift I wish there was someone waiting for me, someone to talk to or just sit with.

I’ve made a few friends here, but I’m really shy and scared to reach out more. I worry I’m too clingy, and I’m also really selective about who I actually like, so it feels even harder. Most of the time I just end up doing everything by myself.

Lately I’ve been wondering if this is just what life is going to be like: quiet, repetitive, and lonely. Is this normal? Does it get better, or is this just how adulthood is supposed to feel?
