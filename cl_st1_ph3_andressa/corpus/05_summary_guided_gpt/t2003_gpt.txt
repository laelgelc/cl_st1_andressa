About a year ago I was feeling really isolated, so I decided to try Meetup. I was terrified at first, but I forced myself to actually show up to events and talk to people instead of hiding in the corner. Eventually I joined a gym group through there, and that’s where things really changed. 

Little by little, I started building real friendships. Now I’ve got a whole new group of close friends I never would’ve met otherwise. I know Meetup isn’t super active everywhere, but it’s free and it honestly helped me a lot. If you’re lonely and don’t know where to start, it might be worth giving it a shot.
