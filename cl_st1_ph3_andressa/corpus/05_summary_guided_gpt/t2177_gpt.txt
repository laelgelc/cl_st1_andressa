Last night I woke up suddenly because my dog did something weird and for a second I was terrified. The first thing that hit me wasn’t even the fear, it was the realization that there’s no one I can message or call. No one I can rely on. It felt like this heavy emptiness.

The worst part is I did this to myself. I pulled away from people I once thought would be in my life forever. I stopped talking to them, hoping they’d be the ones to reach out first. When they didn’t, I convinced myself they didn’t care, so I tried to stop caring too. Now it just feels like I’m completely alone.
