My birthday was a few days ago and I can’t shake this heavy, disappointed feeling. I have a decent-sized family, so I kind of expected to feel… I don’t know, more seen? Aside from my mom, my partner, my best friend, and a few immediate family members, it felt like everyone else just did the bare minimum.

Most of my relatives just wrote a generic “happy birthday” on Facebook. No personal messages, no texts, no calls. It all felt so impersonal, like they were just ticking a box because the notification popped up. My best friend actually called me right after midnight, and that meant a lot. But once that moment passed, the rest of the day just felt flat and empty.

Now I’m stuck wondering if I’m being selfish or dramatic for feeling hurt. Part of me thinks I should just be grateful for the people who did show up. But another part of me feels kind of forgotten, and I don’t know if that’s fair or not.
