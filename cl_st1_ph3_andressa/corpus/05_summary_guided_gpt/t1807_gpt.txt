Most days I just sit here, staring at a screen, letting time blur. It’s like I keep choosing comfort and avoidance over actually facing anything real. I know I’m numbing myself, but it feels easier than dealing with the pain and uncertainty that’s waiting if I stop.

Every now and then, though, something cuts through all the noise. It’s like a brief moonlight shining on everything I’ve been trying not to see: the flaws I keep running from, the regrets, the version of my life that might have existed if I’d made different choices.

In those moments I feel so weak and anxious, like I’m fundamentally unreliable, even to myself. I don’t even know what my heart really wants anymore. But underneath all of that, I still want to be loved. I still wish someone could see all of this mess and not turn away.
