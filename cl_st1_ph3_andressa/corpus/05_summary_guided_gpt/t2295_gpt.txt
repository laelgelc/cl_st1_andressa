My spouse said “happy birthday” a few times before going to work, and a couple of old family friends messaged me, but that was it. I felt like an afterthought to everyone who claims to care about me.

I’m not upset about not getting gifts or anything material. I just feel invisible and so deeply hurt. I don’t know how much longer I can keep going like this. I just want this sadness to stop.
