It’s strange how many of us walk around looking “fine” while secretly feeling lonely, out of place, or like total outcasts. I’m one of those people. On the outside, I function, but inside it often feels heavy and overwhelming, like I’m carrying something I can’t put down or explain.

I keep craving this simple sense of peace, calm, and happiness, but it feels so far away most days. The world is so loud and chaotic that it’s hard to even hear myself think, let alone feel okay.

If you’re reading this and you feel the same, it doesn’t mean you’re strange or broken. It doesn’t mean there’s something wrong with you. A lot of us are struggling quietly. You’re not the only one feeling this way, even if it really feels like you are.
