I’m a 22-year-old guy in Cuba and lately I just feel incredibly alone. I’m not really looking for advice, I just need to put this somewhere.

I’ve tried reaching out to the few people around me, mostly through social media, but it feels like I’ve hit a dead end. Every interaction feels like walking through a minefield. People seem so hypervigilant and defensive, like they’re always waiting to catch you saying the wrong thing or having some hidden motive. It makes every conversation feel like a test I’m destined to fail.

On top of that, it feels like society only sees you as part of some group or label. Like you’re not an individual anymore, just a representative of whatever category they put you in. Those inherited labels erase who you actually are. It’s getting harder and harder to build any real bridge with others, and I just end up withdrawing more into myself.
