I don’t think I’ve ever been truly wanted by anyone. Not in that “you’re my person, I’d notice if you were gone” kind of way. I know my friends care about me, but they don’t really reach out unless I do first. It’s like I’m always on the edge of people’s lives, never the center of anyone’s world.

My one close friend has a partner and a kid now, and I get it, they’re busy. But it still hurts to feel myself slipping further down their priority list.

Romantically, it’s even worse. I’ve never been anyone’s “type.” No one has ever really considered me that way. Seeing people pair off, talk about their partners, plan futures together… it just highlights how invisible I feel.

I don’t buy into incel stuff or that mindset, but I still wish I could feel worth loving, even once. Just to know what it’s like to be chosen.
