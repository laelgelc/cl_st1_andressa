I’m 23 and lately I just feel invisible, like I’m walking around as a ghost. I work retail and every day it’s people talking over me, ignoring me, acting like I’m just part of the scenery. It sounds small, but after a while it really gets to me. I go home feeling like I don’t exist.

My family doesn’t really get it. I’ve grown so distant from my sister, and that hurts a lot because she used to be my best friend. Now we barely talk. Most of the people I still “have” are online, or distant relatives who never actually reach out unless there’s some obligation. It’s like I’m surrounded by people and still completely alone.

I’m also still grieving my dad. He had an addiction and died of an overdose. I miss the dad I had when I was a kid, the loving version of him, but I’m also so angry and hurt about how it all ended. I wish I could talk to him one more time, just hear him say he loves me and that I mattered to him.

I’ve never had a girlfriend. I’m not super social and I do like being alone, but I wish there was someone to share life with, someone who actually sees me. I’m not suicidal, but I’m tired of feeling dead inside. I just want to feel like I matter to someone, like I’m actually seen and understood in all of this.
