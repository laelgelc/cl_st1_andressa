Lately my weeks feel like they’re split in two different lives.

I’ll get maybe one or two days where I feel genuinely happy and energetic. I can laugh, talk to people, even joke around. On those days I feel like “myself” and I start thinking maybe things are getting better.

But the rest of the week I’m just… drained. Things I usually love, like coding, suddenly feel like a chore. Even talking to my own family feels heavy, so I just give really short answers because I don’t have the energy for more.

I’ve been isolated and kinda lonely for about two years now, and it’s really messed up my social life. Making new friends feels almost impossible. I’m at this weird point where I really want to meet new people and “live my age,” but actually socializing feels exhausting. I’m scared people will just see me as boring.

I even tried going out alone because I heard it can be fun, but I just got bored and went home. It felt like I was bored of myself, which is a pretty awful feeling.

Has anyone else gone through something like this, or is it just me?
