I’m 23 and on June 29, 2025, my partner and I separated after almost seven years together. Writing that out feels surreal. It’s like my whole adult life so far has been tied to this one person, and now I’m trying to figure out who I am without them.

The last few months have changed me a lot, in good and bad ways. I’ve learned things about myself I probably wouldn’t have otherwise, but at the same time I feel like I’ve lost a part of myself. I keep thinking about everything I could have done differently, all the moments I wish I could go back and fix.

I’ve tried to move on when I felt ready, but I still find myself reminiscing. I don’t feel fully healed at all, and there’s still this small hope in me that maybe we’ll get back together someday.

I don’t really talk to many people about this, so just writing it out here is oddly relieving.
