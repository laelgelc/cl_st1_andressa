I feel so unbearably lonely lately. I’m stuck aching for someone I know I can’t have, someone I know I can’t even trust. My brain understands that the relationship is gone and it will never be like it used to be, but my feelings just won’t catch up. Instead of fading, the pain and the longing keep getting worse with time.

It honestly feels like the most painful loss I’ve ever gone through. What makes it harder is that there’s still this tiny piece of me that keeps hoping things will somehow go back to the way they were, even though I know that’s not realistic. I don’t know how to let go of that hope, and it’s exhausting.
