I don’t even know why I keep looking for a therapist anymore. I’ve seen so many over the years, and it feels like therapy has done more harm than good. It’s left me with what I can only describe as “therapy trauma” — heartbreak, disappointment, and this deep anger that no one has really understood what I’ve been through.

I grew up surrounded by therapists, counselors, social workers, caseworkers. So my brain automatically goes, “You’re struggling? Find a professional.” But what I actually need isn’t another intake form or treatment plan. I need genuine human kindness. I need someone who will really listen and actually care, not just nod and take notes.

I don’t have those kinds of real connections in my life. I feel horrible all the time. I cry every single day and I can’t make it stop, and it feels like there’s nowhere to put all this pain. Medications aren’t a long-term solution for me either. I just feel stuck, alone, and completely unseen.
