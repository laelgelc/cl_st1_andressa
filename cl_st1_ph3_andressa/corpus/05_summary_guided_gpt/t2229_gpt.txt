My sister organized a family group trip for the Fourth of July and told everyone to check their email for details. I waited and never got anything, so I finally asked her about it. That’s when she told me the trip was “for couples only.” 

I can’t shake how hurtful that feels. It’s not just missing a trip, it’s the message that I don’t really count because I’m single. It just reinforces this resentment I already have toward my family, like I’m always the extra, the one left out.
