You don’t have to turn yourself into the person everyone else wanted just to deserve care or kindness. You owe yourself more than what others have claimed, demanded, or taken from you. Their conditional validation is not the measure of your worth. You’re allowed to claim your own “privilege” and freedom, to say no, to step away, to define yourself. No matter what’s been taken, your spirit is still yours. In the end, you’re the one who has to live with yourself—so stand by yourself, fight for your own pride, and refuse to let anyone own your story.
