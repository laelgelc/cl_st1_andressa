I finally ended a friendship that’s basically been my whole life. We grew up together, and for a long time I thought we’d always be in each other’s lives. But lately it’s just been them dodging paying back money they owe me, making excuses, acting like it’s no big deal. It wasn’t just about the money, it was the feeling that I didn’t matter as much as they did to me.

My mental state is already pretty fragile, and I realized I can’t keep people around who take advantage of me or treat me like some kind of court jester. I’m not there to entertain someone who doesn’t respect me.

Still, it hurts so much. I miss the simplicity of our childhood, when things felt easy and real. I feel intensely lonely and empty right now, but somehow it still feels less painful than staying in that role of being used.
