I’m 27 and I honestly feel like I might have missed my chance at love.

I’ve had a few “relationships” when I was younger, but they were mostly online and never really involved much, so I don’t even feel like they count. I’ve always been fixated on love, on having someone I can care for and spoil. It’s like my brain is stuck on this idea of being a boyfriend, a partner, and I don’t know how to shut it off.

Logically I know I should focus on being stable first, getting my life in order, but I can’t stop thinking about how badly I want someone. Lately it feels like it might just never happen for me, and my depression has gotten worse than it’s been in years because of that.

Without love, life just feels kind of meaningless. I’m terrified of getting older, of watching the years go by while it only gets harder, maybe even impossible, to find a relationship.
