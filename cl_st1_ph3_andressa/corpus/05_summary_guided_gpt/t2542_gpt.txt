I think I’ve reached the point where I’m just… accepting that I’m going to be lonely, and trying to frame it as a choice so it hurts less.

I’ve tried a lot of things over the years: online stuff, apps, meeting people in real life, putting myself out there more than I was comfortable with. None of it really stuck. I still only have two long-term friends, both from preschool, and that’s basically my entire social world.

I don’t blame anyone for this. I don’t think people are bad or that the world is out to get me. I just feel like, for whatever reason, I’m not someone who fits easily into other people’s lives, and I don’t want that to be anyone’s problem but mine.

So I’m trying to figure out: how do you actually be okay with this? How do you build some kind of happiness or contentment while being lonely? If this is just how my life is going to be, I want to at least find a way to live with it.
