I just turned 18 and I feel like I’ve been done with life for years already. Since I was around 15 I’ve had suicidal thoughts on and off. It’s weird because my life hasn’t been “bad” in any obvious way. My parents love me, I know that logically, but I can’t feel it. When people say nice things about me, it never sinks in. I just don’t believe them.

I’ve never been happy with anything I do. No matter what it is, I always find a way to hate it or hate myself for not doing it better. I’ve never had a girlfriend, and the few times someone showed interest in me I turned them down. I don’t love myself, so I don’t understand why anyone else would.

I’m so tired of everything. Sometimes I just want it all to stop. The only thing that keeps me going is thinking that if I can’t be happy, maybe I can at least make other people’s lives a bit better.
