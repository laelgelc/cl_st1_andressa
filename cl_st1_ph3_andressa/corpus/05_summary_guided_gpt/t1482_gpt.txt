Last night I had this really intense dream that’s still stuck in my head. I heard a woman calling my name, over and over. I kept running toward the voice, but every time I got close she would disappear and start calling from somewhere else, like I was always one step behind.

When I finally found her, she was having a really strong panic attack. I just stood there at first, not knowing what to do. Then I went closer and hugged her, even though she was a complete stranger to me. I stayed by her side like that.

When I woke up I had this really strange feeling I can’t quite explain. I often dream about strangers, but never with this intensity. I’m writing this in Spanish using voice dictation, so I hope the translation makes sense.
