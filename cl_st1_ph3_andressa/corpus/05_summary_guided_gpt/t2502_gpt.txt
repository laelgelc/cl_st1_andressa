I really hate how fast I catch feelings for any woman who shows me a bit of kindness or attention. It’s like the second someone is nice to me, a switch flips in my head. Even when I’m telling myself, “She’s just being friendly, don’t read into it,” there’s still this stubborn part of me that starts hoping for more.

It’s happened so many times now that I can almost see it happening in slow motion. I start getting attached, then I notice myself getting weird and overthinking everything. Eventually I pull away because I don’t want to make it awkward, or I’m scared of getting hurt when I inevitably find out she’s already with someone else.

I’m tired of this pattern. It makes me feel pathetic and lonely, like I’m desperate for any scrap of affection.
