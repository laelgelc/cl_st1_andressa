I feel so painfully lonely and touch-starved most days. It’s like I just move through life without anyone really there. The other day at church, this man I know gave me a hug. It wasn’t weird or flirty or anything, just a strong, enveloping, genuine hug. Not cautious, not hesitant. For a few seconds I felt…whole, like all my broken pieces were held together. I didn’t realize how much I craved that kind of physical affection until it was gone again.
