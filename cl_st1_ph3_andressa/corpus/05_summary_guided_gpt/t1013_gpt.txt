I live in a big city, surrounded by people, but somehow I still feel completely alone. Most social stuff here seems to revolve around drinking, and I don’t drink anymore. I used to self-medicate a lot with alcohol and drugs just to get through the days and to feel like I could connect with people.

I’ve been sober from alcohol for almost three years now, and I quit cannabis a few weeks ago. Without those crutches, it feels like everything I pushed down for years is rushing to the surface. I cry a lot, I barely sleep, and I keep thinking about how much time I spent just coping instead of actually building a life or real connections.

I’m not sure what I’m looking for, but I guess I’m wondering if anyone else has gone through something like this. Thanks for reading. Peace and love to all of you.
