Lately I’ve been stuck in this vague, lingering sadness I can’t quite shake. I’m 30 now, and the few people who used to be in my life just aren’t around in the same way. Most of them have families, kids, responsibilities. I’m not even sure I want a family myself—maybe just one person who feels special. But I feel so lonely. I miss when I could just go to someone’s house, hang out, and feel like I belonged somewhere.
