I don’t see a future where I’m ever loved or wanted. It feels like I’m destined to be alone forever. I honestly believe I can’t give anyone what they need, that anyone I care about could easily find someone better, someone actually worth choosing.

I hate myself so much it’s exhausting. It feels like I deserve every bad thing that happens to me, that happiness just isn’t meant for me. I feel like I’m supposed to be punished, constantly reminded that I’m nothing, that I don’t matter and never will.

I can’t imagine anyone ever genuinely wanting me, finding me desirable, or choosing me for real. Not as a backup, not as a pity option, but actually wanting me. That idea feels like a cruel joke.

So I just keep coming back to the same conclusion: I’m no one, and I’ll always have no one.
