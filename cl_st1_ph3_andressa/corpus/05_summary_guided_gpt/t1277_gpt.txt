Another New Year’s Eve alone. I’m 23, from a tiny village in rural Italy, and I haven’t had real friends for about nine years now. Tonight I’m sitting here by myself again and it just hits me how completely alone I feel in every part of my life.

University, work, the gym, running, even just watching movies… I do everything alone. I’m so tired of it. It’s not just “being introverted” or “liking my own company.” I feel like I’m living next to life instead of in it. I wake up, do my stuff, go through the motions, but there’s no one to share anything with. No one to text, no one to call, no one to say “happy new year” to in a real way.

I keep wondering if I messed up somewhere. Was I wrong not to “adapt” more? To want friendships to form naturally instead of forcing myself into things that didn’t feel right? To avoid certain experiences because I wanted to stay true to my principles? Sometimes it feels like friendship is just luck, and I just never had it.

I wouldn’t wish this level of loneliness on anyone. If you’re celebrating tonight, I genuinely hope you’re having a good New Year’s Eve and that you’re surrounded by people who care about you.

If anyone has a movie recommendation I can watch alone tonight, I’d really appreciate it.
