I spent almost a year showing up to this one community, putting in time and emotional energy, really trying to be present. I honestly believed I had a place there, that I mattered to people. It felt like somewhere I could finally belong.

Then I had a conflict with one person. It left me feeling disrespected and unsafe, like the ground had just dropped out from under me. I didn’t make a big scene or anything; I just decided to step back for my own sanity.

What hurts is what happened after: nothing. Weeks went by, then months, and not a single person reached out. No “hey, haven’t seen you in a while,” no “are you okay?” Just…silence. It’s like I vanished and the space I thought I filled there was never real.

I’m not even angry at them. I just feel this heavy sadness, like I was only ever background noise. It makes me feel deeply lonely and unimportant, like my leaving only mattered to me and no one else noticed or cared.

It’s hard not to see this as proof that I don’t really matter anywhere, that I’m always more invested than everyone around me. I don’t know how to shake this feeling that I’m invisible.
