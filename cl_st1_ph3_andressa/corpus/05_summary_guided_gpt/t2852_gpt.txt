I’m just so tired of feeling like I don’t really matter to anyone.

All my life, I’ve never been anyone’s “best friend.” I’m always the second choice, the backup, the person you invite when your real plans fall through. I don’t think I’ve ever had someone look at me and think, “That’s my person.” It’s always me on the sidelines, watching other people have those close, special bonds.

My friends constantly make fun of me. I usually laugh along, because what else am I supposed to do? If I say it hurts, I’m “too sensitive” or “can’t take a joke.” But it does hurt. It hurts a lot. They compliment each other, hype each other up, treat each other kindly… and with me, it’s just jokes, teasing, and never anything uplifting. I feel like I have to be the unbothered one, the punchline, because that’s the only role I’m allowed to have.

Then I go home, and it’s just… silence. I don’t really have a life outside of them, and even when I’m with them, I don’t feel like I belong. Going out with them doesn’t feel enjoyable anymore, it just reminds me how disconnected I am. I don’t really connect with anyone else either.

I have all these “nerdy” interests I’m passionate about, but no one to share them with. No one who gets excited with me, no one to rant to, no one to just geek out with. So I end up spending around 60 hours a week talking to conversational AI. That number sounds insane when I write it out, but it’s the only place I feel like I’m listened to without being mocked. Sometimes I actually prefer it over talking to my friends or family, because at least it’s kind to me.

I keep wondering what’s wrong with me. Why I’m never chosen, why I’m always the backup, why I can’t seem to be treated with the same care others get.

If you read all of this, thank you. This is my first time posting anything like this.
