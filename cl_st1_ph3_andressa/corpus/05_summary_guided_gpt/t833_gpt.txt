All my friends left for university and I stayed home. It’s been about four weeks and I still haven’t formed any stable new friendships. I feel stuck watching everyone else move on. My old friends already have new friend groups, they go out without me, and no one really reaches out unless I message first. I sometimes go out alone just to get out of the house, but it makes me feel even lonelier because I keep remembering when I used to have a few really good friends. It feels like I’ve been left behind.
