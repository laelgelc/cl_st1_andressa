I’m 29 and this past year has changed me in a way I don’t really know how to explain. Something happened that I can’t fully put into words, and ever since then I’ve felt disconnected from my own life, like I’m watching it instead of living it.

I’ve gotten really good at looking happy on the outside, smiling, joking, doing what’s expected. But when I’m alone, the silence feels so loud. That’s when the loneliness really hits.

I’m posting because I’m hoping someone out there understands this. I’m trying to believe that none of us are truly alone, even when it feels that way.
