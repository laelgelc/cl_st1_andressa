I’m 25 and I feel like I’ve basically started life over with nothing. I lost almost all my friends while I was in prison; only one stuck around. I’ve been out for four months now, working almost full-time, and still living with my grandma. I’m grateful to have a roof over my head, but it makes me feel like dating is impossible. Who wants to go out with a guy who still lives with his grandmother and has a record?

I don’t even know how to make new friends anymore. I got ghosted recently and it hit me way harder than I expected. The loneliness is crushing. I knew life after prison wouldn’t be easy, but I didn’t think it would feel this miserable.
