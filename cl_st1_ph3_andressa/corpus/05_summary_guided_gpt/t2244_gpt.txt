I got medically retired from the military at 35 and it felt like my whole life got ripped away overnight. Friends, family, social life, job, even transportation – all gone. Years later, nothing has really changed for me socially.

I live with my wife, her family, and our two dogs. I like her family, but we’re not close. My wife has built a life here, made friends, found her place in the community. I’m just…on the sidelines.

I feel broken and alone, like I’m watching life happen through a window. The worst part is I’m not even sure I want friends or a community anymore.
