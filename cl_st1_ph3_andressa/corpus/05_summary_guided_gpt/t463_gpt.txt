Spent all of Christmas at Waffle House, working all day. By the time I got home I was exhausted and my voice was shot, so I didn’t really have it in me to talk much on the phone anyway.  

What stung, though, was that no one in my family called. Not even a quick “Merry Christmas.” I kind of expected it, but it still hurt. A few friends did reach out, and one of them even gave me $100, which honestly meant a lot.  

I do like living alone. I like the freedom, the quiet, doing what I want when I want. But at the same time, it really highlights how few people actually reach out to me, and how much I crave real companionship, connection, and intimacy.  

Anyway, I hope everyone here had a great Christmas, or at least a peaceful one.
