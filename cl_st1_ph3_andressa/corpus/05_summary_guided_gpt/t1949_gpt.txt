I’m 23 and I feel completely numb and alone most of the time. The only people I really talk to are my parents. I don’t go out, I don’t have friends my age, and most days are just me existing in the same rooms as them.

Recently I overheard them saying they’re tired of me always being around. I can’t even blame them. At my age I should have my own life, my own friends, but I don’t. It just made me feel even more like a burden.

I don’t think I’m interesting at all. I can’t hold a conversation, I’m really awkward, and I speak so quietly that people can barely hear me. Being around others feels like this impossible task I keep failing at.

It’s starting to feel like I’m just meant to be alone, not because I want to be, but because I genuinely don’t know how to connect with anyone.
