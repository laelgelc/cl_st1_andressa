I don’t even know how to explain this properly, but I constantly feel like everyone secretly hates me. I technically have friends and family, so I’m not “alone” on paper, but in my head it feels like I am. After every interaction I replay everything I said, picking it apart and regretting it. It’s exhausting. I’m so sensitive to every little word or silence.

I’m introverted and anxious about talking to new people, and even with people I know, I keep asking for reassurance that they don’t hate me. Then I worry that I’m annoying them and actually making them dislike me. It’s like I’m creating the thing I’m most afraid of.

If someone isn’t actively talking to me, my brain jumps straight to “they don’t like you.” I feel stuck in this loop and I’m starting to hate my life because I feel lonely every single day.
