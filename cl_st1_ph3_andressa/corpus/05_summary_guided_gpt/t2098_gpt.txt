I feel so lonely even when I’m surrounded by people and doing everything “right.” I did bar service at this queer venue recently, really tried to look good, put effort into my appearance, smiled at everyone, tried to be friendly and involved. But no one really talked to me unless I started the conversation first.  

I just felt like this awkward fifth wheel, like I was intruding on everyone else’s good time. I don’t have any real friends where I live, so when I had the chance to take a break and hang out, I just… kept working instead. It felt less painful than standing around obviously alone.  

I’ve been told so many times not to be “so whiny,” so I’m sorry if this comes off that way. I’m hoping this is the one place where it’s okay to say it out loud.  

I wish I could be that cool butch girl, but I just feel like a clingy, lonely weirdo who’s trying way too hard.
