Weekdays aren’t so bad for me. I stay busy with work and hobbies, and it keeps my mind occupied. But weekends hit me like a brick every time. That’s when it feels like I’m “supposed” to be out having fun with people, and instead I’m just…here.

My roommates and acquaintances are always heading out to parties, dinners, or some kind of social event. I hear them getting ready, making plans, laughing, and I’m just sitting in my room with no one to go out with, no one to chat with, not even an online hang.

I really don’t want to go to events alone, but the contrast between their lives and mine makes the loneliness feel so much sharper. It honestly hurts.
