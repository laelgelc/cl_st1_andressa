I really wish I could talk to people from other countries just to connect, but the language barrier always gets in the way. I try using translation tools, but it’s so tiring and clunky. Copying and pasting every message, switching apps all the time… by the time I send something, the moment feels gone.

And the translations themselves feel so stiff and emotionless. It’s like my words lose their personality. I might be trying to sound warm or casual, but what comes out looks like a robot wrote it. It ruins the flow and makes me self-conscious.

I keep wondering: would it feel better if translation just happened automatically in the background, keeping the tone and emotion, even if it wasn’t perfectly accurate? Like, something that lets us actually “talk” without thinking about the tool so much.

Do you think that would make conversations feel more natural and less stressful, or would it still end up feeling awkward and fake?
