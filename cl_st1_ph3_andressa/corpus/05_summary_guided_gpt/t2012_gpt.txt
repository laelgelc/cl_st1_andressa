Lately I’ve been wondering if what I do counts as “daydreaming” or something else. When I’m alone, I constantly escape into these really elaborate imagined realities. It’s not just random thoughts, it’s like full-on alternate lives and scenarios that I sink into for long stretches.  

A quick Google search made it sound like this can be common with loneliness or depression, which honestly worries me a bit. When I’m around people or busy with work, I barely do it. But otherwise, it’s basically my main way of getting by.
