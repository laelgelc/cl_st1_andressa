I feel like there’s something seriously wrong with me in social situations. Every time my cousin messages me, my heart just sinks. I don’t hate them or anything, I just feel this heavy dread about having to respond and keep a conversation going.

Even tiny things feel impossible. When people take group photos and someone needs to do a countdown, I literally can’t say it out loud. I just count with my fingers and hope no one notices. And when I’m out with my mom, I ask her to stand far away when I talk to cashiers or clerks because I can’t handle being watched or heard. It all feels so exhausting and embarrassing.
