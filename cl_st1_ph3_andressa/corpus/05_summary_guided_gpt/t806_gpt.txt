I’m Eli, I’m 14, and I feel like everything is slowly falling apart around me and I’m just pretending it’s fine.

At school I used to spend breaks with a group, but lately they’ve started doing stuff without me and one other friend. They’ll plan things and we just… don’t get invited. We still hang around sometimes, but it’s obvious we’re not really part of it anymore. Every day I force myself to get up, go there, smile, laugh at jokes, act “normal,” while inside I feel like I’m right on the edge of breaking down.

I already struggle with depression and these weird existential fears about the future and life in general, and this whole situation is making it worse. My parents are divorced, and I only see each of them every other weekend. My dad works late, so I’m alone a lot. The house feels way too quiet and empty.

I’ve tried finding people online through games and social media, but most people don’t want to talk to someone my age, or they already have their friend groups. I just end up feeling like an outsider everywhere, both in real life and online.

I’m scared about how I’m supposed to handle the next few years, or adult life at all. Sometimes I feel stupid for even posting this when other people have “bigger” problems, but I just needed to say it somewhere.
