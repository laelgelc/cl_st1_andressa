I feel so strange even writing this, because on paper my life is “fine.” I have a husband, two teenage kids, a stable home. But I feel so emotionally alone inside it all.

Most of what we talk about at home is logistics—who’s going where, what needs to be done, schedules. The kids mostly stay in their rooms, and my husband and I rarely talk about anything deeper than what’s on the calendar. My friends are all busy with their own families and problems, and I don’t want to burden anyone.

Some days I feel like an outsider in my own life, like I’m just managing everything but not really living it. I don’t even know how I’m really doing emotionally anymore, because there’s no one I actually talk to about it.
