I feel like an empty, broken person most days. Like I’m just a body without a soul, or with one that’s slowly decaying. I can be in a room full of people and still feel completely alone, like there’s an invisible wall between me and everyone else. When I’m actually alone, my mind just fills with loud, desperate, hopeless thoughts that I can’t quiet down.

It feels like there’s nowhere left to run or hide from this. I can’t even clearly remember my own reflection anymore. I’m so tired of putting on a happy face while inside I feel hollow, worn out, and like I’m fading away.
