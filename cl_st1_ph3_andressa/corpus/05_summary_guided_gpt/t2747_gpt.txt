Saw the new Terminator movie the other night, completely alone in the theater. Small town of maybe 20,000 people, midweek showing, so I pretty much knew it’d be empty. Honestly, I considered it an epic win. No crowds, no noise, just me and the screen.

The movie was great. Not T2-level great, but still really good. Funny thing is, I saw T2 in a packed theater when it first came out, shoulder to shoulder with people. Now I can’t even imagine doing that again. I deliberately pick times when I know nobody will be there.
