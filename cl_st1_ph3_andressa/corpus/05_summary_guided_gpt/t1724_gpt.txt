I had one of my worst seizures recently. I fell and smashed my nose, then kept seizing so badly I had to crawl to my bedroom just to get to my meds and some water. I lay there, still seizing, waiting for the medication to kick in. It felt like it went on forever.

When it finally stopped, I just lay there thinking about how no one really knows what this is like, and honestly, it feels like no one cares either. I’m in my mid-30s and instead of imagining a future with possibilities, all I can see is more illness and more loneliness. 

Most days I manage to hold it together, but episodes like this make me wonder what my life is going to look like in 10 or 20 years, and it scares me.
