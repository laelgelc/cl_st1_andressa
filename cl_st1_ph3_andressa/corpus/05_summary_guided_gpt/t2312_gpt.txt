My family still treats me like I’m constantly on the verge of doing something terrible to myself. If I go even a short distance from home, they panic. I get why—they’re scared—but it feels like I’m on a very short leash, like I can’t be trusted to exist on my own.

On top of that, I feel like society looks at men like me—quiet, awkward, timid—and sees potential violent offenders. I cling to fictional characters because real people mostly dislike me, and that just seems to prove every negative stereotype people already have.

Today everything feels too loud, too sharp. I don’t feel safe in my own head or in the world. I just want to disappear, live alone in the woods somewhere, where maybe my soul would finally work the way it’s supposed to.
