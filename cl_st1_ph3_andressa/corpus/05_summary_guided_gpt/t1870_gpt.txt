Mid-40s woman here, and I feel like I’ve somehow slipped through the cracks of everyone’s lives. I work, I do my routines, I keep things together. But when the day quiets down, there’s no one to text, no one waiting for me, no group chat buzzing in the background. 

Everyone around me seems to have “their people”—partners, kids, long-term friend groups—while I’m just orbiting around other people’s lives, never really part of a solid circle. I don’t think that means anyone, including me, is broken. It just feels very human and very painful at the same time.  

I’m curious how others in this stage of life experience this kind of loneliness, regardless of gender. How does it show up for you?
