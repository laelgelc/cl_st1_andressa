I’m Linus, 25, from Belgium. I’m very shy and I have a stutter, and it feels like both of those things are walls between me and other people. Making friends is already hard, but when it comes to finding a girlfriend it feels almost impossible. I constantly worry people will lose patience or think I’m weird.

A few years ago I was diagnosed with chronic kidney disease. Since then, I’ve kind of shut down. I felt like I was the only one my age dealing with something like this, and that just made me withdraw more. I work in a nursing home, and there I somehow manage to talk to people in this “different talking voice.” It’s like I can switch something on for work. But when I go home, it switches off and I’m just alone again.

Outside of work I go to the gym sometimes, read, and play video games, but I still struggle to connect. I really hope this changes someday.
