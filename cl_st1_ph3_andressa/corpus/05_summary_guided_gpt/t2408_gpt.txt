Sitting alone in this coffee shop, it feels like I’m watching life happen from the outside. Everyone who’s talking seems to already know each other so well. They’re laughing, leaning in, totally wrapped up in their own little worlds. I keep catching snippets of conversations and thinking how badly I want that kind of connection, just… to belong somewhere like that.

I thought coming here might help, that being around people and having a coffee would make it easier to talk to someone. But I’m not talking to anyone. I’m just staring at my drink and pretending to be busy.

There are a few other people here who are alone too, but none of them feel approachable. They’re on their phones, in their laptops, or just radiating “don’t bother me.” So I’m stuck in my own head again, feeling invisible. It’s really cold outside, so I guess I’ll just go home.
