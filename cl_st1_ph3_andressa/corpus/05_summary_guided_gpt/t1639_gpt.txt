I’m a 51-year-old man and I feel like I have zero friends.

I have colleagues, I have family, but I don’t have anyone I’d honestly call a friend. I haven’t made a new friend in something like 25 years. The friendships I did have just faded away, and at some point I just… stopped trying. I’ve basically given up on the idea that I’ll ever have friends again. I keep thinking I have nothing to offer anyone as a friend anyway.

I’m also stuck in a bad relationship. I’m not staying because it’s good – I’m staying because I’m terrified of being completely alone and I don’t have any support to actually leave. I don’t think I’m a bad person, but I feel like a failure for ending up here.

I’m exhausted from pretending I have friends when I don’t. I’ve hidden this for years and I’m ashamed of it. I guess I’m posting this mostly to finally admit it to myself instead of just quietly carrying it around.
