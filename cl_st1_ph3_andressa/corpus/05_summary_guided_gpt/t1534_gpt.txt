I moved to another country hoping to finally live more freely as a gay person, but it hasn’t turned out how I imagined. I still don’t have real friends, and dating feels impossible. I feel uncool, unattractive, like I’m always on the outside looking in. People are polite and call me the “kind foreigner,” but no one seems to want me as their close person, their actual partner or best friend.

I feel very lonely. What keeps me going is a sense of duty and attachment to my family and my heritage, even though my parents are disappointed in me for being “not normal.” Sometimes I just wish I could be reborn into a better situation in the next life.
