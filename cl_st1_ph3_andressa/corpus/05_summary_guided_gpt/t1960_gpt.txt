I’ve been thinking a lot lately about how alone I feel with certain parts of my life. I have pretty bad anxiety and it makes me isolate a lot, so I don’t really have many friends. Most of the time I’m kind of okay with it – I like being alone, and I spend a lot of time with my brother. He’s great, but he doesn’t really “get” some of the stuff I’d like to talk about.

I have one best friend, but we barely see each other now. We mostly just send each other reels here and there, and she’s busy moving out and dealing with her own life, so I don’t want to bother her too much.

Recently an old online hookup messaged me out of nowhere and it made me feel all giddy and stupid in a fun way. It reminded me of high school, when I had friends to gossip with about crushes and silly drama. Now I don’t really have anyone to share those feelings with, or even just talk about random light stuff like TikTok influencer drama.

I really wish I had a close female friend to talk to, someone who understands that my anxiety makes me go quiet for a few days sometimes, and doesn’t take it personally. It feels like people just don’t understand that part of me, and it makes me pull back even more.
