Sometimes I just wish there was someone who would simply sit with me in whatever I’m feeling and not try to fix it. When I make small mistakes or I’m feeling down or doubting myself, I don’t want advice, or “look on the bright side,” or “others have it worse.” I don’t want to be analyzed, compared, or “motivated.”

I just want to hear something like, “Yeah, I understand,” or even “That’s just like you,” in a warm way. Just a simple acknowledgment that I’m seen and accepted as I am in that moment.

It makes me wonder if there’s something wrong with me for wanting this so badly. Does this mean I might have some kind of mental illness? Or is this a normal thing? Does anyone else feel this way, or am I alone in wanting this kind of quiet understanding?
