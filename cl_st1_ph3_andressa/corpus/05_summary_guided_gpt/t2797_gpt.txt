I’ve been turning this dark idea over in my head: a morbid board game using my real-life friends (and me) as the pieces. Each of us would have stats on our card—age, height, weight, substance abuse, income, family situation. Then we’d place these “character tiles” on a big bingo-like board where one axis is age and the other is cause of death, ranging from the obvious to the really weird and unlikely.

The “game” would be to predict the order in which each of us dies and how it happens. It sounds messed up, but I can’t stop thinking about it. Maybe it’s my way of trying to make sense of how fragile everything feels.
