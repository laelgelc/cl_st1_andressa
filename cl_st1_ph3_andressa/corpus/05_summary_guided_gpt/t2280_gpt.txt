I’ve never had a big friend group or a relationship. At this point it feels like I basically have no one. My former best friend literally refused to be seen with me because they were scared of being bullied, so I cut them off. That hurt more than I can really explain. It made me feel like I’m some kind of embarrassment people need to hide.

Now I only text two people. One is a long‑distance friend, so obviously we can’t just hang out. The other lives nearby, but they never actually want to meet up. I’ve also had a friend since elementary school who just stopped responding months ago. No explanation, just silence.

I have a part‑time job that keeps me busy on weekends, which helps distract me, but the rest of the time I feel like I’m going insane from the emptiness. My dad basically blames me for all of this, like it’s my fault people don’t want me around. My therapist doesn’t really seem to help either; I leave sessions feeling the same or worse.

There aren’t any local clubs or groups I can join, and I’m not in school physically anymore, so I don’t even have that environment to maybe meet someone. I just sit with my thoughts and end up spiraling into self‑hatred, telling myself there must be something fundamentally wrong with me.

I don’t know what to do. I’m so tired of feeling this alone all the time.
