I’m 23 and it feels like I was never meant to make it this far. I honestly never thought I’d live past high school, definitely not past 20, so I never really made any long-term plans. Now I’m here and I feel completely lost and aimless.  

When I try to imagine myself in five years, my mind just goes blank or jumps straight to not existing. I want friends, I want a girlfriend, I want some kind of life, but I feel too mentally messed up and insecure for anyone to actually want to be close to me.  

Every day just feels like I’m drifting, living day by day with nothing changing. It’s this weird mix of numb and painful, like I’m just occupying space instead of actually living.
