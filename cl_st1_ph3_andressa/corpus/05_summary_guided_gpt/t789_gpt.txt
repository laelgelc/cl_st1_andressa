Five months ago I walked away from my abusive ex with almost nothing and moved four states away. Since then I’ve basically rebuilt my life from scratch alone. I got a full-time job, managed to rent a two-bedroom house, even furnished it. I cut contact with everyone from my old life, including my family.

On paper it sounds like progress, and I have met some genuinely good people here. But I feel so incredibly lonely. I miss simple, quiet intimacy more than anything: cuddling on the couch, watching Netflix with someone, sharing food, cooking for and caring about a person, celebrating tiny wins together, just… not waking up alone every single day.

Most nights I come home after a long day, sit in this house I built for myself, and feel this heavy sadness. Sometimes I wonder if I really exist to anyone, like I’m Schrödinger’s cat—there, but not really seen.
