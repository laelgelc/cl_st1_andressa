I don’t understand why this keeps happening. I meet someone nice, we start texting or hanging out, and for a little while it actually feels good. We talk often, share things, maybe even send each other dumb memes or videos. Then, out of nowhere, they start replying slower and slower… until they’re basically gone.

It hurts every time. I’m left staring at my phone, wondering what I did wrong, why I suddenly became a low priority or a “second choice.” I replay conversations in my head, trying to find the moment I messed up, but I never can. They just fade away, like I wasn’t worth sticking around for.

I don’t need constant attention, but I wish I had someone who actually wanted to stay. Someone who checks in every day, asks how my day was, if I’m okay. Someone who doesn’t lose interest after a few weeks, who enjoys the little things like sharing silly videos and random thoughts, and actually cares enough to keep showing up. I’m tired of feeling like no one ever really chooses me.
