I’m so tired of feeling like this. I don’t want surface-level small talk or “how are you?” followed by silence. I just want real human connection, but it feels like it’s never been meant for me. I’ve felt lonely my whole life. I tried so hard growing up to make friends and somehow still ended up with none.

As an adult, it’s the same pattern: I put effort into friendships, then life changes and they disappear. I’m exhausted from starting over again and again. It feels like no one really likes me, like I’m just… forgettable. Even my family barely seems to care, and when I reach out to people, my messages are often ignored.

It’s getting harder not to feel completely hopeless. I keep wondering what the point of existing is if it’s just going to be like this—alone, unwanted, and invisible.
