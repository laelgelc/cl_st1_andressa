I feel so desperately lonely that I literally pay someone to act like they care. Not even for advice, just to have someone listen and pretend I matter for an hour. It makes me feel pathetic, but it’s the only time I don’t feel completely invisible.

Everyone around me seems indifferent. They have their own problems, so I bite my tongue and keep mine to myself. I don’t want to be a burden. But inside it feels like I’m walking around with a black dog chewing on my chest and juggling a live grenade, just waiting for it to go off. I feel constantly on the edge of breaking.

I’m not planning to kill myself, but if someone told me there was a suicide mission that might help others, I’d probably go. That’s how little my own life feels worth. I don’t see a way out, but I’m writing this so if anyone else feels like this, you know you’re not completely alone.
