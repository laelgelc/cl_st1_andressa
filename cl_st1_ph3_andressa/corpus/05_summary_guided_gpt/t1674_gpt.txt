I’m 16 and I honestly don’t think I’ve had a real friend in about six years. I was homeschooled most of my life, so I never really learned how to be around people. I feel like everyone else got a head start on social skills and I’m just… stuck at the starting line.

I have social anxiety, though it’s a bit better than it used to be. But even when I’m around people, I never know what to say or how to react. I don’t relate to what they’re talking about, and my mind just goes blank. I end up sitting there, quiet and awkward, and then I hate myself for it later.

I play volleyball and I’m actually pretty experienced, just like the more social and popular players. But even there, no one really talks to me. I’m just “that quiet kid” in the corner. It’s so frustrating because I’m trying, but it feels like nothing changes.

My mental health had started to get a bit better, but now it feels like it’s slipping again. I’m so tired of this. I’m crying at midnight and I just don’t want to feel this alone anymore.
