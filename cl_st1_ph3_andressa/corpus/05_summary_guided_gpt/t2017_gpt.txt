I’m 17 and it feels like I’ve been socially rejected since first grade. Everyone around me seems to have friends and people who actually want them around—classmates, random people at school, even my own family members. Meanwhile, I have no one.

At school, people ignore me, avoid eye contact, and never want me in their group projects. It honestly feels like even the teachers don’t really tolerate me. I go home and there are no plans, no messages, nothing. Just me and my thoughts.

I really, really want someone to talk to, to hang out with, to do anything with. But I feel so consistently unwanted that I’m starting to think I shouldn’t even bother trying anymore. It’s like I’m invisible and unnecessary.
