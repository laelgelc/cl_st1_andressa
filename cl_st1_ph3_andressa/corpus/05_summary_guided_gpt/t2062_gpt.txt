My birthday is coming up and I can already feel that familiar heaviness settling in. Birthdays have just gotten harder and harder for me as I’ve gotten older, and I’m pretty sure it’s tied to childhood stuff and CPTSD. I feel so lonely and empty lately, and I honestly don’t know what I’m doing with my life. Having a day that’s supposed to “celebrate” me just makes all of that feel louder.

They’re also extra loaded because I’m a recovered suicidal person. There’s this strange pressure, like I should be grateful I’m still here (and I am), but at the same time the day often ends with me crying and not really understanding why. It’s confusing and kind of scary.

I did try to reach out this year. I invited some friends, but a few haven’t replied at all and the ones who did can only make it in the evening. So I’ll be alone most of the day anyway. Now I just feel guilty and silly for having asked people to come, like I’m being needy or making a fuss over nothing.

This will also be my first birthday single after having a partner for the last two. That makes it feel even emptier for some reason.

Does anyone else struggle with birthdays like this? Why do they hurt so much for some of us? And if you spend your birthday mostly alone, how do you get through the day or even make it feel okay? I’d really appreciate any thoughts or experiences.
