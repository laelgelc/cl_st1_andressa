I’ve been thinking a lot about how Gen Z seems lonelier than ever, even though we technically have access to everything and everyone online. It feels like we’re drowning in content but starving for real connection. I don’t actually think making connections is that hard in itself — we just don’t have many spaces built specifically for genuine, low-pressure conversation.

That’s why I made something called Vooz. It’s a video and text chat site where you put in your interests and get matched with people who are into similar things. You can also add location and gender filters if you want to meet locals or talk to a specific gender, but that’s optional.

To keep it safe, the site is AI-moderated, and anyone who does NSFW stuff gets permanently banned.

If you’re feeling lonely and want to try meeting new people in a more intentional way, you can search “Vooz” on Google and give it a shot.
