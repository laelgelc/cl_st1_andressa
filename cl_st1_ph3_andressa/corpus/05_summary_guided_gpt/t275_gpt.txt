I feel like I’ve been alone since I was a kid, and now it’s like there’s this wall between me and everyone else that I can’t see but can definitely feel. I obsess over what’s wrong with me—am I ugly, does my voice sound weird, are my interests too strange? I keep replaying interactions in my head, trying to find the exact thing that makes people not connect with me, but I never figure it out.

Over time it’s made me bitter and resentful. I catch myself blaming everyone else for shutting me out, but at the same time I know it’s somehow my problem too. It’s like I’ve been dehumanized, like I’m not really part of the same world everyone else is in. I honestly feel like it’s too late to “join” people now. I don’t want to grow old like this. I’d rather die young than end up as some lonely, forgotten person.
