For as long as I can remember, I’ve been the person everyone forgets exists. In groups, I’m always the one people talk over or forget to include. I’ll say something and it’s like my words just vanish into the air. Then someone else says basically the same thing a minute later and suddenly it’s a great idea.

It’s not just in real life either. Online, in group chats or comment threads, people skip past me like I’m not even there. It feels like there’s some invisible sign on me that says “not worth interacting with.” It’s not about reputation, because this happens even with people who don’t know me at all.

I’m friendly and polite, not standoffish or rude. I honestly try to engage. I keep wondering if there’s some sociological or psychological explanation for why some people just get consistently ignored like this.
