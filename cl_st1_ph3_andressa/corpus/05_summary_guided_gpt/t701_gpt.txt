I’m 22 (almost 23) and I feel like I’m already done with life. I’m just sitting here stewing in bitterness while everyone else seems to be moving forward.

People from my school who were absolute assholes – bullies, troublemakers, people who treated others like garbage – now have relationships, kids, even houses. Meanwhile I have nothing. No friends, no girlfriend, no kids, no job, no money, no place of my own. I did everything “right” in school. I was sensible, respectful, tried to be a decent person. And it feels like I got punished for it.

It makes me so angry that people who were horrible get to be happy and start families, while I’m stuck here alone. I know it sounds petty, but I can’t help thinking I *deserve* a girlfriend and some kind of success more than they do. Even my younger cousin has a hot girlfriend and a life that looks so much better than mine. I’m older and I’ve got nothing to show for it.

I feel like I have no future. The only thing keeping me afloat is my dad, and he’s supportive, but I can’t rely on him forever. I’m terrified of what happens when he retires or dies. I picture myself jobless, broke, and homeless, and it feels almost inevitable.

I’m just so full of anger and resentment. I don’t know how to stop comparing myself to everyone else or how to believe things could ever get better from here.
