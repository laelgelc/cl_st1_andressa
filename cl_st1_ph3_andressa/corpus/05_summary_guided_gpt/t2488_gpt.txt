I moved to a new city for my studies about seven months ago, and I still haven’t managed to make any friends here. It’s such a weird contrast, because before I moved, life actually felt pretty good. I had friends, I did well in school, and I had a girlfriend. I wasn’t exactly a social butterfly, but I felt like I had a place.

After I moved, I kept in touch with my old friends and my girlfriend mostly through texting and being online together. I ended up spending most of my free time with her that way. Then things fell apart and we broke up in October. Since then, the loneliness has just hit way harder. It feels like I lost the main person I was connected to, and the days suddenly got a lot emptier.

I still have my old friends, but they’re in another city, and I don’t want to bother them or seem clingy, so I’ve been reaching out less and less. Now it’s been about three months of feeling really lonely, and it’s starting to feel overwhelming. I’m not sure how to cope with this or how to change anything.
