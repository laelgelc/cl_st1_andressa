I’m Ammar, a concept artist (I go by “Untamed Draws” online), and lately the loneliness has been hitting harder than usual.

From the outside, my life probably looks pretty good. I have what many would call a dream job in a creative field, and I’m genuinely passionate about art. I get lost in drawing, music, games, cooking, thinking about self-care, even daydreaming about building a home or tending a garden someday. My inner world feels full and alive.

But when it comes to actual human connection, it’s just… quiet.

I’m an empath, and I feel other people very deeply when I’m around them. The problem is, there just aren’t many people around. I don’t really have anyone I can message randomly about some strange thought at 2 a.m., or share a song with, or talk to about why a certain painting hits so hard, or even just complain about a bad day.

I’m not looking for instant best friends or constant chatting. I’d be happy with slow, gentle conversations—about life, art, existence, or even mundane everyday stuff. Long pauses are okay. Quiet is okay. I just miss feeling understood by another human being.

If any of this resonates with you, I’d be open to talking.
