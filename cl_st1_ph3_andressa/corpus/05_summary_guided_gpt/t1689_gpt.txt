I’m 17 days sober and right now all I can think about is drinking again. I used alcohol hard for a long time to cope, and everything got worse when my dog died. He was my best friend for years. Around the same time, a friend who had actually given me some hope just… left. Losing them both at once really broke something in me.

Today I suddenly remembered the taste of my favorite mead and it hit me so hard. The cravings, the memories, that feeling of being abandoned all over again. I feel like my hope is gone. I tried a suicide hotline before and it didn’t really help, just made me feel more alone.

Right now I really want to drink, but instead I’m just sitting here crying.
