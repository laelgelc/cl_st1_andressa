I constantly feel like I’m not meant to be here, like I somehow ended up on the wrong planet. No matter where I am, it feels like I’m out of place, like I don’t belong anywhere. I keep thinking about all the things I could change—move somewhere “better,” make more friends, get a job I like—but even when I imagine those things going well, this heavy feeling in my chest doesn’t go away.

It’s like this deep, constant loneliness that nothing can touch. I feel trapped in it, like there’s no version of my life where I could actually feel okay, let alone happy. I just can’t picture any future where this emptiness isn’t there.
