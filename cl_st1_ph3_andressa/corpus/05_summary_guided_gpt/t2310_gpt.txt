I’m a 40-year-old blind gay man and I feel like I’ve already lost the game before I even start. I’ve been called ugly more times than I can count. On dating apps, as soon as I mention I’m blind, people just stop replying. A few have even said it wouldn’t work because I “can’t appreciate their looks.” After enough of that, it’s hard not to give up. I’ve basically resigned myself to a life alone with my dog and cats, convinced love just isn’t for me.
