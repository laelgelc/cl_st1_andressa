I don’t really know what it feels like to be loved or wanted. I’ve always been on the outside of everything – no close family bonds, no friends, no relationships. I try so hard to connect with people, but somehow I’m always the extra person, the one people talk around instead of to. It’s like I’m physically there but not really a person in their eyes.

One time someone literally asked my mom what my name was while I was standing right there. That moment just stuck with me. It summed up how invisible I feel. Even my parents don’t feel like they ever truly loved me – more like they tolerated me or just did what they were supposed to do.

I’m starting to accept that I might never experience love, marriage, or any deep connection. I can’t picture anyone ever choosing me, or even caring if I’m gone. I honestly don’t think anyone would show up to my funeral besides maybe out of obligation.

I’m finishing school soon and instead of feeling excited, I just feel like I’m stepping into a lifetime of being alone. It’s like I’m already grieving a life I’ll never have.
