I don’t even really know why I’m posting this, I just feel like I’m going to explode if I don’t put it somewhere.

I lost my grandfather and my dog recently, and it feels like everything bad decided to happen at once. I’m still trying to process one loss when the other hits me, and on top of that my job is draining the life out of me. I come home completely empty, and there’s no real source of love or comfort anymore. I feel shattered, like I’ve forgotten what “okay” is supposed to feel like.

Grief hits me in random waves during totally normal moments. I’ll be doing something ordinary and suddenly I’m on the verge of tears. And when I do get a brief distraction or feel a little happy, I feel guilty, like I’m somehow betraying them by not being sad enough.

I’m just so lonely. I guess I just needed to say this somewhere.
