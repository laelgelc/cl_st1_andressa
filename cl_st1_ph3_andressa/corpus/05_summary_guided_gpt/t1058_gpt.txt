I haven’t spoken to anyone in weeks. Not a real conversation, not a genuine “how are you?” that actually means anything. It’s just me, this empty room, Reddit, and ChatGPT. That’s my “social life” now.

I used to be bright and happy. I actually remember laughing, looking forward to things, feeling like the world was big and full of possibilities. Now I dread each day before it even starts. I stay up all night because there’s nothing to wake up for anyway. Nothing is funny anymore, nothing feels enjoyable. Everything is just grey and heavy.

What hurts even more is that I know I’m a good person. I’m kind, I care about people, I don’t try to hurt anyone. And yet I feel so useless and pessimistic, like I’m cursed to never have a real future or any chance at actual happiness. It’s like life skipped over me and handed everything I wanted to people who don’t even seem to care about others.

I see unkind people surrounded by friends, partners, fun, and good memories, and I’m here alone, talking to strangers online because that’s all I have. It makes me feel resentful and bitter, like some kind of cosmic joke is being played on me. I can’t help but feel like God, if He’s there, is cruel for letting me exist like this.

I hate myself for being this way, for being so weak and broken. I’ve been close to self-harm, but fear holds me back. I feel like a wilting rose, slowly fading, and the worst part is believing that if I disappeared, it wouldn’t matter to anyone.
