I’m 21 and recently moved out of my parents’ place for a new job, and honestly the last couple of months have felt like hell.

I live alone now and the loneliness is hitting me harder than I ever imagined. I cry almost every night. I feel like I’m going crazy. I don’t have anyone to talk to in person, and the few online friends I used to have have basically disappeared from my life. It feels like I went from having some kind of support to having absolutely nothing.

My motivation at work is vanishing. Getting out of bed in the morning feels like dragging myself through mud. I keep thinking about just giving up, quitting, and going back to my family, even though I know I moved for a reason.

I miss love and care so much it physically hurts. I miss having someone there to give me a hug, to tell me “it’s going to be okay,” to just sit with me when I’m not okay. Instead I’m just…alone with my thoughts.

The stress is getting so bad that I’ve started having panic attacks and trouble breathing, especially at night. Being alone during those moments feels unbearable. I lie there thinking I can’t do this, that each day is getting worse, and I honestly don’t know how to cope anymore.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I guess I just needed to put this somewhere because keeping it all inside is starting to feel impossible.
