I miss the feeling of another person so much it’s like a physical ache. It’s been a year since I last held someone’s hand, felt their warmth against me, and I can still remember every detail of what that was like. I honestly don’t think I’ll ever experience that again. 

I’m surrounded by billions of people on this planet and yet I feel like I’m on a different one. I’m convinced everyone would rather do anything else than spend time with me. I feel fundamentally unlovable, like there’s something rotten at the core of who I am.

I see myself as worthless and awful. I keep thinking that if the people who “care” about me could see what’s really going on in my head, they’d hate me, maybe even wish me dead. 

I want to die so badly, but I’m scared I’d even fail at that, just like everything else.
