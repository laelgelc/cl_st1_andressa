I’m starting to feel like, besides my mom, no one has ever actually cared about me in a real way. Not romantically, not in my family, not even in friendships. People seem to like having me around when it’s convenient, when I’m useful or comforting, but that’s it.  

I’m a really sensitive person, I feel things deeply, and I have a big heart. I have so much love to give and I give it freely. But it’s starting to feel like my love is worthless to everyone else. Like it doesn’t matter how much I care, it never really lands with anyone.

My boyfriend is the latest reminder of this. I honestly feel like he’s only with me so he doesn’t have to be alone. I feel like a placeholder, a convenience. He always has time and energy for his family, his friends, his video games, but when it comes to me, I’m an afterthought. Lately he’s been ignoring me more, zoning out when I talk, not really listening. It hurts more than I know how to explain.

It’s the same pattern with friends. I drop everything for people. I’ll stay up all night to comfort someone, even when I’m barely holding it together myself. But when I finally reach out and say I’m not okay, suddenly everyone is “busy” or disappears, even the ones who promised they’d be there for me.

I don’t need grand gestures or dramatic declarations. I just want someone to genuinely care. To listen. To show up. To see me as more than a backup option or emotional support tool. I just want to matter to someone.
