I’m so exhausted from trying and failing over and over again to make friends. I keep putting myself out there in these tiny, painful ways, and it never goes anywhere. Nobody ever tells me what I’m doing wrong, so I’m stuck in this loop of guessing and blaming myself. It feels like I’m broken in some way I can’t even see.

I’m in my 30s and it feels like everyone else my age just…knows how to connect. They meet people, click, form groups, make plans. Meanwhile I’m here with severe social anxiety, feeling like every interaction is a minefield. I’m lonely and isolated to a point where it honestly feels like it’s killing me from the inside.

I feel so worthless and I keep thinking I wish I’d never been born. On top of that, years of depression and cycling through antidepressants make me feel like whatever personality I used to have has been stripped away. I don’t even know who I am anymore.

My interests are kind of spooky, bizarre, unusual, and instead of feeling unique it just makes me feel like even more of an outsider. I look at people around me building friendships and I can’t imagine ever getting there. I’m starting to think I should just give up on trying, because I don’t know how many more rejections and silent failures I can take.
