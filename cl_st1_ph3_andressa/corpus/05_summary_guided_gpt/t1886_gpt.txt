I’m on this week-long work trip that’s supposed to be “great for networking and growth,” and on paper I can see the value. The meetings and training are actually interesting. But the second it shifts to socializing, it’s like I hit a wall.

We had a team dinner and everyone else was talking, laughing, making connections. I just sat there in silence, completely frozen. My chest hurt, my brain went blank, and all I could think was how out of place I felt. It’s not even shyness at this point, it feels like actual emotional pain. This has been a pattern my whole life.

I keep thinking this is exactly why I have no friends and never go out in my free time. I just can’t handle these situations, so I avoid them, and then I feel even more alone.

There’s an awards dinner with a dance floor coming up and I’m already dreading it. I wish I knew how to get past this.
