I’m a senior in high school and lately I just feel so incredibly alone and empty. I feel like I’m always the “backup friend” – the one who gets invited last, the one who doesn’t get the references or inside jokes because I wasn’t there when they were made. Everyone’s laughing about some memory and I’m just standing there trying to piece together what happened, pretending I’m in on it.

When my friends hang out with other people and have fun, I get way more jealous and attached than I should. It feels like everyone else has that one close friend or a partner, someone they really matter to, and I’m just… extra. Like if I disappeared, the group would move on just fine.

Recently I’ve started to hate everything about how I come across. My voice, my laugh, the way I talk. I’ll say something I think is funny and then it just dies in the air, and there’s this awkward silence. It makes me want to stop talking altogether. I keep replaying conversations in my head and cringing at myself.

I went through something similar over a year ago and I started self-harming back then. Sometimes I did it in ways that people could maybe notice, and now I’m scared that part of me did it for attention. That thought makes me feel disgusting. I haven’t self-harmed in a while, but lately the urge has been coming back stronger and I’m honestly scared I’m going to relapse. I don’t want to go back there.

I feel so much self-hatred and at the same time I feel guilty for even wanting people to treat me better or notice me more. Like I’m selfish or dramatic for feeling this way. I’m sorry if this sounds like rambling or attention-seeking. I just don’t really know where else to put all of this.
