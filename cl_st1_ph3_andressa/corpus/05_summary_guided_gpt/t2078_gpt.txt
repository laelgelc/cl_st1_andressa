I feel like I give off this deeply unsettling vibe that makes people uncomfortable, even when I’m just existing. It’s like I’m “too weird” even for people who are usually considered creepy. Men don’t see me as attractive or interesting, just as someone to steer clear of. 

I’ll walk into a room or just refill my water bottle and I can feel the tension, like my presence alone is wrong. It’s exhausting.  

I honestly think this started when I was really young, like four, when caregivers already treated me as “bad” or “off.” Now it feels like I’m stuck playing this role of the villain or creep, even though I don’t want to be that person at all.
