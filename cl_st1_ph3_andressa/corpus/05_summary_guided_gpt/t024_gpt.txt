I’m a trans woman and I’m still trying to process something that happened recently.

There’s this girl at work who always seemed kind of into me. Coworkers commented on it, and I picked up on the vibes too. She once mentioned she had a crush on someone, and my friend and I both thought she was just teasing, maybe hinting at me.

She came over to visit and we ended up cuddling on the couch, being really affectionate. At one point she even suggested that we kiss. It felt so real and intimate that I started thinking maybe this was actually going somewhere.

But then she pulled back and said we were “just really good friends.” She repeated it again before she left, like she wanted to make sure I understood.
