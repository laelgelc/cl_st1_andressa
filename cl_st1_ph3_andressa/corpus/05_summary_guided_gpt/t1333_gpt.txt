I’m 40 and lately it feels like my life just…shrunk.

When I got divorced, I didn’t just lose my wife and stepkids, I lost every bit of family that came with them. The friends I had were “our” friends, and when the marriage ended, so did those connections. I went from a full house and busy weekends to silence. Now, outside of my parents and siblings, I don’t really have anyone to talk to.

I’m introverted, I don’t drink, and that combination makes it really hard to connect with people. I’ve tried getting more involved at church, but it’s mostly surface-level small talk. No one really engages beyond a quick “hi” and “how are you?” and then they move on to their own groups. I walk out of there feeling more alone than when I arrived.

I run my own online LEGO retail business, which is going well financially, but it means I have no coworkers, no office, no built-in social life. I can go days without any real conversation that isn’t with family or a customer over email.

I’ve tried multiple dating sites with no luck. I’m not just looking for a girlfriend; I really miss family life. I miss the chaos of kids, the shared routines, planning trips together. Ideally I’d love to meet someone who already has kids and wants to share that kind of life again—traveling around the US, going to beaches, swimming, playing board games, going to comedy shows or movies, bowling, mini golf, all that simple, fun stuff that feels better when you’re not doing it alone.

I’m not posting this for advice. I just feel very isolated and wanted to put this out there in case someone else feels the same way. If you’re in a similar spot—lonely, missing connection, wanting more than just online acquaintances—feel free to send me a message. I’d like to start with talking online, but my hope is to eventually build real-life friendships too. I’m tired of my world being so small.
