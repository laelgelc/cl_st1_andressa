I’ve been feeling really isolated lately and don’t know what to do about it.

For five years my dad and I lived in an RV park in Maysville, MI. It wasn’t perfect, but people were always around. You could step outside and instantly run into someone to talk to. Then a new owner bought the place and decided he only wanted short-term “weekend warriors,” so all of us long-term residents got kicked out.

Now we’re in a house. The only person I really have is my dad, and he’s gone a lot for work. I have a disability that makes things like handling money hard, I can’t drive, and getting a job feels almost impossible. In the RV park, everything was within walking distance and it felt natural to socialize. Here in a regular neighborhood, everyone just disappears into their homes.

I don’t need a relationship or people over all the time. I just wish there was a casual hangout spot where I could go, sit, and chat. The only place nearby is a bar and grill, and it’s not really that kind of place. I just feel stuck and alone.
