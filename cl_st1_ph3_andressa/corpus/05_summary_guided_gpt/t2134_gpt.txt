I’ve always liked being alone, but lately it’s started to feel less like “me time” and more like this heavy, constant loneliness. I keep finding myself wishing I had one person who really gets me — a genuine companion or “soulmate,” but not necessarily in a romantic way. Just someone I could be fully myself with.

Right now I feel like I don’t belong anywhere. My family is in another state, and my close friends — who I never fully trusted enough to open up to anyway — are moving to another country. Soon I’ll be starting work in healthcare, and instead of feeling excited, I mostly feel… untethered.

My schedule already makes everything harder. I’m up at 4:30 a.m. for my internship, back home around 4 p.m., and by then I’m exhausted and still have assignments to do. I can barely keep up with online chats, let alone build any real connections. It feels like life is pushing me forward, but emotionally I’m stuck in the same place I’ve been for a long time: lost and lonely.

I don’t really know what I’m looking for by writing this. Maybe just to not feel so alone in feeling this way.
