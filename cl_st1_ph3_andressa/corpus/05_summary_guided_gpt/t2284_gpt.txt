I feel like I’ve lived an incredibly lonely life and now I’m just… numb. I don’t even know what to do anymore. I only ever have casual interactions with people—small talk, surface-level stuff—but there’s no one I’m actually close to, no one I see regularly or can really rely on.

It hits me the hardest when I’m out and see people together. Even homeless couples, just sitting side by side, or groups of friends laughing, make me feel like I’m on the outside of something everyone else seems to have figured out. It makes me wonder what I’m doing wrong, or what’s wrong with me specifically that I can’t seem to form those kinds of connections.

I’m stuck between wanting more and feeling too worn down to even try.
