I feel like I’m drowning lately. The holidays just make everything worse. Everyone talks about warmth and togetherness and I just feel this huge, empty ache inside me. Thanksgiving is coming up and I’m dreading it. I don’t want to see my extended family. I don’t want to socialize, pretend I’m okay, make small talk about my life when I honestly have nothing good to say.

I know exactly how it’ll go: I’ll be quiet, withdrawn, and people will think I’m miserable or ungrateful. I’ll be “the miserable daughter” again. But I also feel like I can’t say no without hurting my parents, and that guilt is suffocating. So I’ll go, sit there, feel completely alone in a crowded room, and then beat myself up afterward for not being able to just fake it better.

I just want someone to hold me and tell me it’s going to be okay. I want comfort and love so badly it physically hurts. I’m so tired of feeling like this, so tired of crying over a life that never happened.

I hate living at home. I want to move out, start over somehow, but I have no job, no real interest in any career, no idea what I’m doing. I feel completely stuck. The little savings I do have are going to end up spent on holiday gifts because I feel obligated, and then I’ll be back to nothing again.

I’m still mourning the future I thought I’d have with my ex. It feels like I lost an entire life, not just a person. I keep thinking about the version of me that might’ve been happy, and it just makes me feel even more broken. I don’t want to keep going like this, but I don’t see a way out.
