I don’t know what’s happening to me lately. I used to be okay being alone, it was just… life. But now, every time I meet someone and we have a good conversation or a nice moment, my brain instantly jumps 10 steps ahead. I start imagining this perfect friendship or relationship, all the things we could do together, future plans, how they might “fit” into my life.

And then reality doesn’t match any of that. The connection stalls, or they don’t seem as interested, or it just fades away. When that happens, I feel completely crushed, like I’m losing my mind over someone I barely know.

This cycle of getting hopeful and then disappointed is exhausting. It’s tearing me apart emotionally, and I honestly don’t know how to stop doing this to myself.
