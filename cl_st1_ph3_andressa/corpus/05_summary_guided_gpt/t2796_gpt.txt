Sometimes I think about how my whole high school experience basically hinged on one person’s decisions and personality, and it kind of freaks me out.

I was totally fine being a loner. I didn’t really seek out friends, didn’t feel like I needed to. Then this extroverted acquaintance from middle school transferred to my school. She was the opposite of me socially, just… naturally making friends everywhere.

I sort of just followed her around, and through her I ended up with the friend group I still have now. When I look back, I keep wondering: what if she never switched schools? What if she’d been shy instead? Would I have gone through all of high school completely alone?
