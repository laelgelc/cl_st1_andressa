I’m so tired of being told to “just put yourself out there” like I’m not already doing that.

I go to dance studios, I tried in college, I start conversations, I give people genuine compliments, I try to be friendly and open. I’m bubbly, I’m nice, I listen, I ask questions. And somehow people still seem weirded out, cut the conversation short, or just ghost me afterward. It makes me feel like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me even though I’m trying so hard.

I’m in Southern California and honestly it feels like a lot of women here are just… rude or closed off. I don’t drink, smoke, or go clubbing, and my interests are gaming, snowboarding, movies, and cars. That already puts me out of sync with most people around me, so it feels like I’m starting ten steps behind.

I’m lonely and frustrated. I keep doing what everyone says you’re “supposed” to do, and it still goes nowhere. I don’t know what else I’m supposed to change.
