I’ve been thinking a lot about how isolating life can feel, and I keep coming back to this idea of an “unalone” app. Something that isn’t just another social feed, but a tool to actually help people feel less alone.

I imagine features like:
- a buddy system to connect 1:1 with someone else who’s lonely  
- a meetup/events calendar for local or online hangouts  
- daily progress tracking so you can see how you’re doing over time  

For the tracking part, the open-source project behind Habitica seems like a great technical base (they have public code and an explanatory video).

I’m curious what you all think. Would you use something like this? What would make it actually helpful?
