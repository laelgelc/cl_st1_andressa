I’m extremely reclusive and I don’t really talk to people unless I have to. I do have a few friends, but that’s about it. Most of the time I just avoid people altogether. A big part of it is that I have this pretty cynical view that most people are driven by insecurity, and they enjoy pointing out others’ flaws. I feel like if I make a mistake, people will either humiliate me for it or just ignore me like I don’t matter.

On top of that, I don’t really feel much in general. I’ve never experienced romantic love or even any real desire to be in a relationship. Even back when I felt more than I do now, that part was still just… blank. It’s not like I hate the idea, it just doesn’t appeal to me. I don’t daydream about it, I don’t miss it, I don’t feel lonely in that specific way.

But sometimes I wonder if something is missing from my life. I look decent, I’m in good shape, and I’m pretty sure I could find a partner if I made an effort, but I’ve never even tried. I just don’t see the point, and at the same time I’m not fully confident that this is “normal” either.

I’m curious what other people think. Is this just how some people are, or is it something I should be worried about? And if I should do something about it, what exactly should I do?
