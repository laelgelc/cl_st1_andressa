I was an unplanned kid born to older parents. My brothers were already way older and living their own lives, so we were never really close. Growing up, I was always “mature for my age,” soaking up everything from my parents. I was social, always around people, and usually the youngest in every group. Weirdly, I used to feel a bit envied for that.

Now it’s like everything flipped overnight. I’m suddenly the oldest in my circles, and instead of feeling grounded or wise, I feel like the lost old guy tagging along. I genuinely want to help, to be supportive, but I keep feeling like I’m just… in the way. It’s a lonely, disorienting place to be.
