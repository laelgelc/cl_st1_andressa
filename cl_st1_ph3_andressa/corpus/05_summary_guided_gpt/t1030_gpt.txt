I feel like I constantly have to justify why I’m around, even with people I’ve known for years. When I’m in a group, there’s this voice in my head asking, “Do they actually want you here, or are you just… tolerated?”

It really hits me when I think about invitations. There seems to be this invisible line between people who get invited and people who do the inviting. I’ve never been to a social gathering I didn’t organize myself. If I don’t put something together, my social life just… doesn’t happen.

In my friend group, I feel like the only one who doesn’t get invited to things. Everyone else seems so much more charismatic and easy to be around. My self-image is pretty awful, so I automatically assume I’m the expendable one.

When I try to talk about this, my friends just brush it off, tell me I’m overthinking and make me feel kind of “insane” for feeling this way. I don’t blame them, I care about them a lot, but I feel really misunderstood.

Does anyone else experience this?
