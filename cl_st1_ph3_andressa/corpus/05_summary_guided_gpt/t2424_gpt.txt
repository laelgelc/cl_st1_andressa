I feel like I’m nobody’s “number one.” Honestly, I don’t even feel like I’m on anyone’s list at all.

I’ve tried. I reach out, I invite people, I ask to hang out, I try to start conversations and keep them going. But it never seems to go anywhere. No one really reciprocates. I watch other people in their groups, with their plans and inside jokes and shared history, and it just makes me feel even more out of place. Like there’s this whole world of connection that I’m somehow not allowed into.

I’m always on the outskirts. Not invited, not included, not really thought of. I’ve had to turn myself into this “loner” who looks like they enjoy doing everything alone, because what else am I supposed to do? But the truth is, I don’t want to do everything alone. I just don’t know where I fit.

I feel like I’m nobody’s close friend. I’m often the last to hear about news from people I thought I was getting closer to. It’s like I’m always an afterthought, if I’m a thought at all.

I keep asking myself: what’s so wrong with me? What is it about me that makes people not want to get closer? I’m willing to change, to grow, to work on myself—but I don’t even know what it is that’s so off-putting. It hurts not knowing, and it hurts feeling this invisible all the time.
