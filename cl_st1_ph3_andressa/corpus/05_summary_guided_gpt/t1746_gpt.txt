I’m 19 and I feel like I’m living on a completely different planet from everyone my age. I can only really talk to my parents. With coworkers, I freeze up, overthink every word, and feel like I’m one wrong sentence away from being judged or laughed at. I don’t drive, I don’t go out, and it just makes me feel even more behind than I already am socially. “Alien” is honestly the best word for it.

I have these really intense nerdy interests that I hyperfixate on, but I’m embarrassed to talk about them because I’m scared they’ll just make me seem even weirder. So I keep them to myself and then wonder why I can’t connect with anyone.

On top of that, my body is covered in severe acne scars and dark spots. I wear long sleeves and pants even in summer because I’m terrified of anyone seeing my skin. I keep thinking that if people already don’t seem to like me much, they’d like me even less if they saw what I actually look like.

Most days I just feel embarrassed to exist. I wish I could be “normal,” whatever that even means.
