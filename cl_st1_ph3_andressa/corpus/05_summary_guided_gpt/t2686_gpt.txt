I tried again to make friends at school after kind of becoming the “new loner” a while back. About three weeks ago I joined this tight-knit group. They were friendly enough on the surface, but most of the time it felt like I was just… there.

They mostly talked in inside jokes and stories I wasn’t part of, and I never really knew how to jump in. It’s like watching a show that’s already in the middle of season 5. No one was rude or anything, but I felt like a background character more than a friend.

So I decided to go back to my usual spot and just be on my own again. I don’t feel ashamed about it. Honestly, I doubt anyone will even notice I stopped hanging around them.

It kind of feels like this fits me better anyway. I’m pretty introverted at heart, and being alone feels more natural than forcing myself into conversations where I don’t really belong.
