I’m so tired of living like this. Since my parents died in 2018, I’ve been paying off everything they left behind, on top of what I owe to loan sharks. It feels like my whole life is just debt and fear.

Every day is a battle just to afford one meal. I’m constantly anxious, scared of what will happen if I can’t keep up with the payments. I feel exhausted down to my bones, like I’m running on empty but still forced to keep going.

I’m ashamed of my situation, so I don’t really talk about it. My depression is quiet and internal. On the outside I’m just “getting by,” but inside I’m falling apart, just trying to survive one day at a time.
