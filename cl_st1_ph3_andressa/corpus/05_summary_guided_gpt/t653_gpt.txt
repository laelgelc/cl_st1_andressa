I feel so intensely lonely lately that it’s starting to scare me. My friends have all moved away, and I feel like an outsider at work. I come home to an empty house, and even on my days off I just stay inside, staring at the walls, feeling like I’ve completely lost control of my life. I honestly don’t know what I’m doing anymore.

I’ve tried therapy, I’ve tried antidepressants, but I still feel like I have no one to really talk to. I keep questioning my own worth. I catch myself thinking I don’t deserve nice things, or any kind of happiness, and then I wonder what the point of all of this even is.

My partner moved in this year, and I thought that would make things feel less lonely. But our work schedules are opposite, so most of the time the house still feels empty. It’s like I’m living next to someone instead of with them.

I feel this constant pressure to “do something” with my life, but I just see myself as a failure. I don’t know how to convince myself that I’m worth it, or that I’m not just wasting space. I’m tired of feeling this way.
