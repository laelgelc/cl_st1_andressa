I feel like every time I open my mouth I’m just weighing people down. Like I’m this drain on conversations, on moods, on space itself. Most of the time I’m basically invisible, unless I’m having some kind of emotional meltdown — then suddenly I exist, but only as a problem to be managed.

It honestly feels like I “fell out” of my own life at some point, and there’s no way back. Like once you slip off the track everyone else is on, that’s it. You just watch them move forward while you’re stuck on the side of the road.

Being a misfit, disabled, or a minority feels like being branded a net negative. People might tolerate you for a while, but eventually they drift away, like it’s inevitable. I’ve gone from alone to lonesome to reclusive to estranged, and now I just feel desolated — like I’ve lost the ability to really connect with anyone at all.
