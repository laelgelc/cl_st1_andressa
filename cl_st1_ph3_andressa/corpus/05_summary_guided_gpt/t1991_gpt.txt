I’m 22 and I’ve never been in a relationship. Not even something short or half-serious. It’s like I’m watching life through a window while everyone else is out there actually living it.

I feel like an outcast in a lot of ways, but it hits the hardest when it comes to love. I see people my age going through crushes, first loves, messy breakups, getting back together, whatever. They get stories, memories, scars. And I’m just… here. It feels like no one has ever even considered me as an option, like I don’t exist in that part of life.

Sometimes I dwell on it so much that I can literally feel it in my body. My chest gets heavy, my stomach tightens, and there’s this weird shiver that runs through me. It’s painful but in a strange way it also feels… good? Like scratching at a wound just to feel something. I’ll lie there and sink into my loneliness on purpose, replaying everything I don’t have.

The messed up part is I know I’m not really trying to “fix” it. I’m not putting in much effort to meet people or work on whatever might be holding me back. I just kind of sit in it and watch time go by. And then I get hurt when other people get chances that I don’t, even when those chances end badly for them. At least they had something.

Everyone always says “22 is still so young, you have time,” and logically I understand that. But it doesn’t feel young to me. It feels like I’ve already missed something important that most people got years ago.

I’m mostly writing this to see if anyone else feels like this too. Do any of you sink into your loneliness like this and almost cling to it? I just want to know I’m not the only one.
