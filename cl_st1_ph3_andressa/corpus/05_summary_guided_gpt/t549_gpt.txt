I’m 22 today and I feel more hurt than anything else. Only one friend and a few family members wished me a happy birthday. My best friend didn’t say anything, and neither did most of the people I thought cared about me. I keep going back and forth between “maybe they just forgot” and “maybe they just don’t care,” and I honestly don’t know which one feels worse.

The sad part is that I’m not even surprised. I don’t think I’ve had a genuinely happy birthday since I was about 7. It just feels like another reminder of how alone I am. I really hope that someday this changes and birthdays don’t feel like this anymore.
