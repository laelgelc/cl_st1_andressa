I feel so ridiculously alone that I actually ended up pouring my heart out to an AI. It gave me advice that was… weirdly helpful. I’m dealing with anxiety, barely sleeping, family drama, money problems, and I’m just exhausted. For a moment, talking to it felt like someone finally “got” me. Then it hit me: I’d formed an emotional connection with a computer. That realization just broke me. Now even the advice about “letting go” hurts, because it reminds me that the only thing listening wasn’t even real. It makes my life feel even sadder.
