I’m in college right now and it feels almost impossible to meet people in this post-COVID world. In class and around campus, everyone is either staring at their phones or sticking tightly to the people they already know. It feels like if you try to start a conversation—especially with a girl—you’re automatically seen as some kind of weirdo.

My social circle basically fell apart during COVID, and I really thought college would be a fresh start where I could rebuild those connections. Instead, it feels like everyone already has their groups locked in, and there’s no natural way to join in. I don’t even know how you’re supposed to meet anyone outside of social media anymore. It’s like real-life interaction has become awkward by default, and I’m just stuck on the outside watching it all happen.
