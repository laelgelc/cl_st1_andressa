It feels like I’m back in 7th grade, except this time I’m stuck here for four years.

The social environment at my college is honestly exhausting. A lot of the guys here constantly put each other down, trying to provoke reactions just to get laughs and attention. It’s like bullying disguised as “banter,” and if you don’t play along, you’re automatically on the outside.

There are a few guys who seem to have made me their favorite target. The second I walk into a room, they start in with the insults and little jabs just to entertain everyone else. But when no one else is around, they suddenly act nice, like we’re cool. It’s so fake and confusing. I don’t even know how I’m supposed to respond anymore.

What hurts more is that even people I thought were “good” or decent get sucked into it. They start copying the same behavior, calling me an asshole too, like they’re just going along with the group narrative about me. It’s like my reputation is being decided for me by people who enjoy tearing others down.

In 7th grade I dealt with something similar and I escaped by just leaving that friend group. Now I feel trapped in this environment, like there’s nowhere else to go and I’m stuck with this for the rest of college. I don’t know what to do, and it’s making me feel really alone.
