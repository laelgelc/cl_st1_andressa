I don’t think I’ll ever get to be a dad, and it hurts more than I can put into words. I feel like I have so much love to give, so much I want to share and teach, but it’s just… not going to happen for me. People say stuff like “we’re all here for a reason,” but I don’t buy that. It all feels like random accidents that we desperately try to give meaning to.

I’m scared of being alone forever. On top of that, it feels like artificial beings are slowly replacing what used to be normal human connection, and it makes everything feel even more distant.
