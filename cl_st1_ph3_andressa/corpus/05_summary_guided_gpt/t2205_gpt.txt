Every day feels like the same empty loop. I wake up late, stay up way too late, and in between I just play bass and guitar. That’s it. No real structure, no real purpose. Part of me keeps saying I “should” be working, like that’s what a normal adult is supposed to do, but when I actually think about it, I just can’t see the point.

The idea of clocking into some dead-end job feels like giving up a piece of my dignity for almost nothing in return. It doesn’t feel that different from doing nothing all day, just more exhausting and soul-draining. I’m pretty much resigned to the fact that I’ll probably end up taking one of those jobs anyway, just to survive, but it feels like walking into a cage I can already see the inside of.

Underneath all of this, what I really want is to be someone’s someone. To matter to one person in a real way. I honestly believe I could be that for someone else, that I have it in me to care and show up. But when I look at my life and how things are going, the chance of actually finding that kind of connection feels really bleak.
