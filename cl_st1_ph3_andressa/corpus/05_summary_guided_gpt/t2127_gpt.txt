Some days I actually feel okay being on my own. I go to work, hit the gym, distract myself with hobbies, and it almost feels like this is just my normal life. But other days it hits me really hard how alone I am, and I just want someone to talk to, especially a girl I connect with.

Social anxiety makes that feel impossible. I freeze up, overthink everything, and dating apps haven’t helped at all. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if this empty feeling is just how my future is going to be.
