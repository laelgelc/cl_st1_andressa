I feel lonely even when I’m surrounded by people. It’s like I’m physically there but emotionally invisible. I’m introverted and anxious, and it feels like that just makes it easier for others to ignore me. Girls don’t really notice me, or I’m the one they joke about. In groups I’m just… background noise. It slowly eats away at me and makes me feel small, ashamed, and kind of pointless.

People always say “change your mindset,” “just be confident,” “fake it till you make it,” but it doesn’t work for me. It just makes me feel like I’m failing at even pretending to be okay. I don’t have some big solution or motivational message. I just know that forcing myself to be someone else isn’t it.

If anyone else feels this quiet, invisible kind of loneliness: I see you. You’re not as alone in it as you think.
