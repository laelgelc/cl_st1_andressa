I feel so loved, yet I’m still deeply lonely and tormented inside. I miss my friends, my parents, the food I grew up with. On paper I “have everything,” but somewhere along the way I lost myself.

I gave up my career and now I feel completely empty. I can’t relate to people my age whose whole focus is earning money, but I also feel childish for still longing for my working life and that sense of purpose. Everyone acts like it’s a good thing for a woman to have nothing to do, to just be taken care of. Honestly, it’s not. You slowly lose yourself and forget what your own values even are.

I’ve come to think love and marriage are not necessary in life. Please, be careful with your choices and stay aware of who you are.
