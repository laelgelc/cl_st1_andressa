I’m 17 and over this past year it feels like my whole world has slowly emptied out.

People I used to call classmates and friends have started ignoring me, avoiding me in the halls, acting like I’m not even there. Sometimes they’ll make jokes about me in front of others, and everyone laughs like it’s normal. I pretend it doesn’t bother me, but then I go home and just cry where no one can see. It’s like I don’t matter to anyone.

I feel like no one really understands me or even wants to. No one listens when I try to talk, and I’ve started to believe that I can’t trust anyone with how I actually feel. I’ve tried to talk to my parents, but I never seem to find a good time, or the words get stuck in my throat. So I just keep it all to myself.

Even being around my goldfish, which used to make me feel a little calmer, doesn’t help anymore. The loneliness is just there, all the time. My motivation is disappearing. Things I used to enjoy don’t interest me, and my studies are getting worse because I can’t focus. It’s like everything is slowly fading.

I pray for just one real friend. Just one person who would actually understand me, listen to me, and stay when things get hard instead of walking away or making fun of me. I keep asking for that, but a part of me doubts I’ll ever find that kind of friend.

I don’t really know why I’m writing this here, maybe just to let this painful part of my life out somewhere, since I can’t seem to say it to anyone around me.
