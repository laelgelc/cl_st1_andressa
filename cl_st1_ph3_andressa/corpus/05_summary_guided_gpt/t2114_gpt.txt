I’m 22 and I feel like I’m the only person in my friend group who’s never had any romantic experience at all. No first kiss, no relationship, nothing. It makes me feel like I stick out in this really pathetic way. Everyone else seems to have these stories and connections and I’m just… there.

It also makes me question my friendships. I feel like I’m the least connected one, like what I have with people is kind of artificial, like I’m fundamentally unable to form a real bond with anyone. It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like something broken in me.

I know some of this is probably on me, but the thing is, I don’t really want to “change my ways.” What I want is to somehow get rid of this basic need to be loved and appreciated. That need is what’s making me feel so intensely lonely and misunderstood. I just want that desire, and the pain that comes with it, to stop.
