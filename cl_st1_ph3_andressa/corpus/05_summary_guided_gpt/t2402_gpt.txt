I feel like I’m living in this weird paradox where I’m “the cool friend” on the surface, but I’m actually lonely as hell underneath it.

People always tell me I’m confident, that I don’t care what others think, that I’m different in a good way. I’m the one who can say the uncomfortable truths, have the deep 3am conversations, question everything, not follow trends, not care about being “polite” if it means being fake. I’ve never been interested in fitting into some neat little box. I don’t mind being the odd one out.

But the older I get (late 20s now), the more it just feels like I’m on my own island.

Most people I meet seem so insecure, terrified of being seen as weird, constantly managing their image. Or they’re intimidated by me, like my just-existing is some kind of threat to their comfort zone. I’ve gotten comments like “you’re intense” or “you’re too much” or “you scare people” when all I’m doing is being honest and not dumbing myself down.

I’ve tried different online spaces: punk, goth, rock, alternative subcultures, etc. You’d think those would be full of people who don’t care about norms, but it still feels off. Either people are way more timid than their aesthetic suggests, or they’re doing their own thing in such a separate, disconnected way that it never goes deeper than surface-level interests.

What I’m looking for is real friends who are also rebels, outcasts, artists, strong individuals. People who can stand on their own two feet emotionally and mentally, who aren’t trying to be carbon copies of whatever’s trending, who actually want to question stuff and be themselves fully. Not “I wear black so I’m edgy,” but people who actually live outside the script.

I’m a bit of a shut-in now, and honestly I feel out of place in this era. It seems like everyone is either chasing some curated, sanitized version of life or too scared to step outside of it.

So where do people like me actually find each other, especially online? Are there spaces that aren’t just about an aesthetic, but about mindset and personality? I’m tired of being the “interesting friend” and still feeling completely alone.
