It’s my birthday and, like every year, I’m alone. I don’t even know why this particular time of year hits so hard, but it does. The early darkness, the holidays coming up, everyone posting about their “perfect” relationships and cozy plans… it just makes the loneliness feel heavier.

I just got out of a toxic two‑month relationship with a bipolar partner, and I can’t shake the feeling that I wasted those months. I really tried, but it was draining and unhealthy. Now that it’s over, I’m back to being alone, and it feels like I’m starting from zero again.

I keep wishing I could have just one year of genuine happiness. Just one real friendship that lasts, or a loving, unconditional relationship where someone accepts my flaws the way I would accept theirs. But it never seems to happen for me, and I’m so tired of feeling like this.
