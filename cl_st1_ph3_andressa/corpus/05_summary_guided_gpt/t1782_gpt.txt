I’m 28 and I feel like I don’t have any real friends, even though I’m surrounded by “friendly” people. Coworkers, neighbors, acquaintances are all kind enough, but everything stays on this shallow, surface level. No one seems interested in actually knowing me or putting effort into a mutual, genuine friendship.

Most of the time I end up being the listener while they talk about themselves, their problems, their lives. When it comes to me, it’s like the conversation just… stops. I feel more like a sounding board than a person they want to connect with.

What really messes with my head is that so many people say they’re lonely, but when it comes to being vulnerable or intentional, they pull back. I’m angry and confused by this gap between “friendly” and “real.” It makes me wonder if true friendship is even something people want anymore.
