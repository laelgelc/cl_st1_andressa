I feel like I’ve just… given up on people. Whenever I’m around others I feel like an outsider, like some kind of alien trying to copy human behavior. My autism makes it so hard to move past surface-level stuff, and the depression just kills whatever motivation I have to keep trying. I never know what to say, I can’t keep conversations going, and I get so anxious that I feel like I need a script for every possible response.

I get really jealous and upset when I see girls my age hanging out with their friends, laughing and being close. I can’t imagine that ever being me. I haven’t had a real friend since I was about 10, and I’ve never been anyone’s “best friend.” It hurts more than I like to admit.

Living in a small city in New Zealand makes it feel even more impossible. It feels like everyone already has their groups and it’s too late for me to find mine, especially being autistic. I just wish I had one friend I could feel comfortable with. Right now, the only person I have is my grandmother.
