I’ve been alone for so long that talking to people doesn’t feel natural anymore. I never join a conversation unless someone directly asks me something, and even then I’m hesitant. When I finally do start talking, it feels like people just interrupt, talk over me, or change the subject. It makes me shut down and retreat back into my own little bubble.

Then I get labeled as “so quiet,” like that’s all there is to me. It bothers me, because I wasn’t always like this. I used to be more talkative, more willing to jump in. Over time I just learned that my personality or “vibe” isn’t for everyone, and now I’m stuck in this loop of silence and feeling misunderstood.
