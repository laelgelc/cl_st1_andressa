I’m 16 and I feel like I’m doing all the “right” things on paper, but I still feel completely alone and kind of hopeless.

I’ve been going to the gym, I’m on track to graduate early, and I’m working on getting my driver’s license. Those are the goals I set for myself and I’m actually following through with them. But none of it has changed how my life feels day to day. School is still the same socially. I don’t really talk to anyone. It’s like I just exist there and then go home.

I technically have one friend, but we mostly just game together. I know he has other friends and a whole social life outside of me, and I don’t. When he’s busy, it just reminds me how empty my own life is. Most of my time is spent alone in my room, either doomscrolling or sleeping, just trying to make the day pass faster.

I feel like I’m incapable of making friends or ever getting a girlfriend. I don’t know how to hold conversations. I never know what to say, and when I do talk, it feels forced or awkward. I honestly feel like an NPC sometimes, like I’m just there in the background while everyone else is actually living.

I keep thinking this might just be my future: being alone, watching other people have connections I can’t seem to make. Part of me is starting to think I should just learn to live with being lonely, because maybe that’s all there is for me. But I really don’t want that. I don’t want to spend my whole life feeling like this, but I don’t know how to change it or if I even can.
