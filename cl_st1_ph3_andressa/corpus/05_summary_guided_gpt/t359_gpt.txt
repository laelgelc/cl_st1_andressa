I feel like I’m just… there. At home, family gatherings happen around me, not with me. People talk over me, around me, never to me. I’ll sit in a room full of relatives and feel like a ghost. At school it’s the same. If I don’t show up, no one notices unless they need someone to fill the empty space in their own life for a bit. Then I go back to being invisible.

I’m just this quiet emo lesbian who sits alone and ends up talking to herself out loud because there’s no one else. I actually had something good once, a relationship that made me feel seen, but I ruined it because I didn’t know how to express anything. Now I’m back in that dark, lonely place again.

I can’t seem to feel happy unless I’m smoking, just to shut my brain up and keep the negative thoughts away. Most of the time I just feel like a sad nobody.
