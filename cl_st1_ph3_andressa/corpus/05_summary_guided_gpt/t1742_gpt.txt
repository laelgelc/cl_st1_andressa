I’m 32, a guy, and new here. I’ve been married, had relationships, had different connections, but nothing has really lasted as long as I hoped. Lately I keep thinking about how different things felt when I was growing up. Adults would just… talk to strangers. At the bus stop, in line at the store, outside their houses. Real conversations, not small talk to fill the silence. Now it feels like everyone’s in their own bubble, and that simple, genuine human connection is just gone.
