I can be in the middle of a loud, busy crowd and still feel like I’m on an island. Small talk, surface-level conversations, all the “how’s the weather?” stuff just leaves me feeling emptier. I crave deep thinkers, real connection, someone who’d actually notice if I disappeared and genuinely miss me if I died. Honestly, I’d rather have one reliable, caring friend like that than a hundred casual acquaintances.

It doesn’t help that I’ve had a chronic illness my whole life, so getting out and meeting people is hard. I live with a kind, honest man who has Asperger’s. I care about him, but he struggles with verbal and emotional communication. He’s often lost in his books, and I end up feeling invisible, neglected, and bored, even though I know he doesn’t mean to make me feel that way.

I know everyone’s situation is different. How does loneliness look for you?
