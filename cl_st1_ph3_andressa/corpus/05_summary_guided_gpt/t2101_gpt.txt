I feel like I’m stuck in this weird in‑between state. I’m a teenager, I have some online friends, and I can talk to people pretty easily on the internet. But in real life I feel completely alone. Whenever I’m around people my age (and even a lot of adults), all I see are shallow conversations, no real thinking, no curiosity. It feels like they can’t or don’t want to think deeply, and it makes me not want to be around them at all.

At the same time, I’m lonely. Part of me wants to be totally alone forever, and another part of me is desperate for someone who actually thinks. My rational side tells me I should reach out, be social, compromise, whatever. But my feelings just don’t line up with that. I like philosophy, I think a lot about life and existence, and sometimes I even imagine being immortal and not having emotions, just pure thought.

I’m also really timid when it comes to actually doing anything about this. I hesitate, overthink, and end up doing nothing. I’m not sure what I should do or where to even look. Are there any online spaces or communities where people are generally above‑average in intelligence and actually like thinking deeply?
