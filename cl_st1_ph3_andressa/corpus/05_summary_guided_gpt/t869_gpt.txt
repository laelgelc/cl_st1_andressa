I’m 26 and I’ve never experienced mutual romantic love. Not even once. I’ve liked people, caught feelings, tried to put myself out there in my own way, but it’s never been returned. It’s like I’m permanently stuck in the background, watching everyone else get what I want so badly and can’t seem to reach.

On paper, my life looks fine. I play football, I go to the gym, I have a decent career as a software developer. From the outside, it probably looks like I’m doing “well.” But emotionally, I just feel empty. None of that fills the hole that comes from never being chosen, never being seen in that way.

I keep going back and forth in my head: is it just bad luck? Is there something fundamentally wrong with me that I can’t see? Am I missing something obvious that everyone else seems to have figured out? I don’t know if it’s my personality, my looks, the way I come across, or if I just somehow slip through the cracks of people’s attention.

What hurts the most is that I haven’t even had the chance to experience heartbreak. I’m so far on the outside that I don’t even get close enough for things to go wrong. I’m just… not picked. Ever.

I’m scared that this is just how it’s always going to be. That I’ll always be the one who’s around but never really noticed, never loved, never the person someone is excited about. It feels like I’m destined to be the forgotten one, and I don’t know how to live with that.
