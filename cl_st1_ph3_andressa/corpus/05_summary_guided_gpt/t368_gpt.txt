I just got back from another gathering where everyone seemed so happy and settled. Engagements, weddings, buying homes, pregnancy announcements… it feels like every time I see them, they’ve moved one step further into this stable, secure life.

And I’m just… stuck.

I care about them, I really do. I smile, I hug, I say “congratulations” and try to sound excited. But then I come home to silence, to an empty place where no one is waiting for me, and it makes all those words feel fake. It’s like I’m just a spectator to everyone else’s life.

I don’t have a partner to love, no shared plans, no sense that my own life is really going anywhere. I feel like I have no life of my own, just this weird in-between existence where I watch other people build futures I’m terrified I’ll never have.
