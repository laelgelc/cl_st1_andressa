I’ve basically had one close online friend since around 2013/2014. That’s it. No other friends online, none in real life. A big part of it is my social anxiety and how hard it is for me to talk to people. I freeze up, can’t think of what to say, and end up avoiding conversations altogether. So this one friend has meant a lot to me for years.

Lately, though, it feels like they’re slowly drifting away. I’m always the one who has to message first. If I don’t, we just don’t talk. They can go weeks without initiating anything. When they do reply, it’s usually just one-word answers like “yup” or something similar. It wasn’t always like this. We used to actually talk, have proper conversations, share things, joke around.

Now I feel like I’m bothering them every time I say something. I keep overthinking everything I send, wondering if I’m not saying enough, or not saying the “right” thing, to deserve a real response. Then when they only reply with a single word, it just makes me feel stupid for trying.

It really hurts, because this is basically my only friendship. I’m scared that I’m losing them, and I don’t know what I’ll have left if that happens. I care about them a lot, but I don’t think they feel the same way about me anymore. I feel so lonely and unwanted, but I also don’t know how to talk to them about it without sounding clingy or making things even more awkward.
