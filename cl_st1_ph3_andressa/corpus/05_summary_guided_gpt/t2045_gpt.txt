Since COVID lockdown my social anxiety has gotten so bad. I was about 11 when it started, and I never went back to school afterward because I was too scared. I feel like I missed everything you’re “supposed” to do as a teenager—parties, hanging out, just having friends. I started college in September and I still have no friends. I literally haven’t had a friend since I was 11. I feel so alone and I honestly don’t know what to do anymore.
