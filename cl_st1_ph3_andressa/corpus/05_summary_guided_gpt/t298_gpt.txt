I think I’ve basically given up on finding a partner. Dating apps just make me feel worse – so many women seem fake or only interested in money, and it’s hard not to take that personally. People tell me I’m decent looking, but it doesn’t matter when my self-esteem is trash and social anxiety makes going to bars or social events feel impossible.

In the relationships I’ve had, I never felt like I could be myself. But when I actually am myself, I just end up alone. It feels like I can’t win either way. I wish relationships could be “reset,” with no scripts or expectations about who’s supposed to do what or how things are supposed to go. Just two people figuring it out without all the pressure.
