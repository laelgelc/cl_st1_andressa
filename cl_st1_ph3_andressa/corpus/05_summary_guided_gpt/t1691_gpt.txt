I’m 24, from India, and working in another state because there just aren’t many decent corporate jobs back home. My parents are both retired now and keep hinting (sometimes outright saying) that I should move back, get a job there, be “with them.” But I really don’t want to.

Career-wise, it would be a dead end. There’s barely any growth where they live, and I’ve worked hard to get where I am. On top of that, I honestly don’t picture them in my future the way they seem to imagine it. That sounds harsh even to me, but it’s how I feel.

Our relationship has always been distant. Growing up, they were always busy with work. In high school I had a big conflict with my father, and I’ve never really gotten over it. I still see him as kind of a douchebag, and my mother always sides with him. When I visited home for a week recently, I mostly stayed in my room, barely spoke to them, and just pretended things were normal with my dad.

There was a time I thought maybe I’d bring them to live with me in the future, to help them with the whole “empty nest” thing. But after this visit, I really doubt that’s possible. They don’t fit my lifestyle at all, and my mom is still extremely controlling. She actually insists I should be home by 9 p.m., even if it’s for work-related stuff.

I feel guilty for not wanting them in my future, but also suffocated when I’m around them. I don’t really know what to do with these feelings.
