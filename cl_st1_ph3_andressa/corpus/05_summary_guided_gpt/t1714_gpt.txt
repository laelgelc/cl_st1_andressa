I’m 24 and I feel like I’m stuck in the worst place possible: the middle of everything. I’m not especially good or bad at anything, not popular but not a complete loner either. I’m just… there. Background character energy. It feels like people look past me, like I’m forgettable, and that hurts more than outright rejection.

I don’t have any close friends. No one who really knows me, checks in on me, or could honestly say I’m “their person.” I see everyone else having at least one close friend and I keep asking myself why I don’t. It’s not like I think I’m awful to be around. I’m humorous, easygoing, empathetic, emotional, and I’m not super insecure about my looks either. I genuinely try to make other people happy, to be kind and supportive, but it never seems to be enough for anyone to actually stay and get to know me deeply.

Because of all this, I feel so unmotivated and unhappy. Life feels mechanical: wake up, go through the motions, go to sleep, repeat. I don’t feel excited about anything. Even things I know would probably help, like going to the gym or working on myself, feel pointless when I don’t see the bigger picture or any real reason to try.

I just feel stuck in this gray area where I’m not miserable enough to completely fall apart, but not happy enough to feel alive. I don’t know how to move forward from here, or how to break this pattern of being “in the middle” of everything and everyone.

What should I do?
