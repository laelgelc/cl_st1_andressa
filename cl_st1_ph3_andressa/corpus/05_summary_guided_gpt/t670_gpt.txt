I’m 30 and I feel like I’ve hit a wall with nothing to show for it. No partner, no real friends, no one I can actually talk to about this. It’s like my life just… never started, and now I’m already behind everyone else.

I live in this crappy, deteriorating house that I’ve been trying to swap out of, but nothing works out. I’ve tried and tried, but it’s like I’m stuck here, literally and metaphorically. I even tried to buy my own place at one point, thinking maybe that would be a fresh start, some kind of step forward. That failed too. Every time I think I see a way out, it just collapses.

My job is just a dead-end cleaning job. No prospects, no ladder to climb, nothing. I go in, do the same repetitive stuff, and leave feeling exactly the same as when I arrived: empty and replaceable. My grades were always poor, and that just adds to this feeling that I’m too “dumb” to do anything better. I’ve tried online courses, but I either don’t understand them or can’t keep up, and it just reinforces this belief that I’m not cut out for more.

I’ve tried to use my spare time to do something different—selling things, making videos—just trying to create some kind of opportunity for myself. Nobody cares. No interest, no views, no sales. It’s like I’m shouting into a void.

I see myself as a bottom feeder in society. There are “main characters” and people in the middle who actually contribute something, and then there’s me. No real purpose, no value. I just want one “win” in life. Just one thing that works. I want to be part of something, to feel like I matter, to have someone love me. But it feels almost impossible, like it’s not meant for people like me.

On the outside I pretend not to care, act like I’m fine with it, but that’s a lie. I care a lot. It hurts. I feel stuck in this life I don’t want, and I don’t know how to escape it.
