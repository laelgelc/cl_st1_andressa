I feel like I’m destined to always be alone. The last time I celebrated a birthday with someone special was four years ago, and even then I had to practically beg them to spend time with me. It’s like there are only two kinds of people in my life: those who hate me and those who just tolerate me out of pity. I don’t believe anyone is capable of loving me. The only person who’s ever shown me even a hint of love is myself.
