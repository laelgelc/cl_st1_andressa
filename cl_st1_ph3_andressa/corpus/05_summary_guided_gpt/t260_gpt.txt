Sometimes I catch myself daydreaming about just disappearing to a remote island and starting over from nothing. I picture building some kind of tree structure to live in, something simple but sturdy, and just… living off the land as much as possible. I’d bring books, art supplies, my piano and guitar, and over time I’d figure out how to get electricity and a proper water system going.

I imagine growing all sorts of crops, raising chickens, goats, maybe even cows. Making my own food, using what I grow, and having sushi all the time, maybe with a monthly shipment of things I can’t get on the island.

I grew up in a small crop house, so the idea of being surrounded by plants and animals feels familiar and comforting. Honestly, I feel like I think more clearly when I’m alone. I feel safer too, like I’m not constantly being pulled in different directions by other people’s expectations or bad influences.

Sometimes I wonder if life really could be that simple—just me, my little island setup, and no one else around. Away from all the temptations, the noise, and the pressure. Just quiet, work, and the things I actually care about.
