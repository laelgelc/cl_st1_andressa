I’m 20 and I’ve never dated before. No real romantic experiences, no “almosts,” nothing. For a long time I’ve watched other people have relationships, hookups, heartbreaks, whatever, and I’ve just been on the sidelines wondering if that kind of connection would ever happen for me.

A few days ago I matched with a girl on Hinge. She’s 18. We moved to texting pretty quickly and we’ve been talking for three days now. I know that’s not long at all, but it feels… different.

She’s funny, sweet, and actually really thoughtful. She asks questions back, remembers little things I say, and the effort feels mutual instead of me trying to drag the conversation along. When my phone lights up with her name, I don’t get that anxious, panicky feeling I usually do. I just feel kind of… calm and genuinely happy.

We’ll be joking about something, or she’ll say something kind, and I catch myself smiling like an idiot at my screen. I’m not overthinking every word I send, I’m not rereading messages ten times. It just feels natural. That’s completely new to me.

I’m very aware it’s early. Maybe it fizzles out, maybe it doesn’t become anything serious. But right now I’m allowing myself to enjoy this hopeful, giddy feeling instead of shutting it down out of fear.

I mostly wanted to share this because if you’re lonely and feel like it’s never going to happen, I’ve been there for a long time. And then, out of nowhere, this little spark showed up. It can happen. Your moment can come too.
