I’m 33 and have been living in Valencia for almost two years now, and I feel more depressed and lonely than I ever have in my life. The last few years have just been a slow downhill slide. Somewhere along the way I drifted away from my friends, and now it feels like I’ve ended up here almost completely alone.

I do have a few acquaintances – people I see for board games or a beer sometimes – but it never goes beyond that. It’s all small talk and surface-level stuff. There’s no one I can really open up to, no one who actually knows me.

What I want feels so simple: at least one person I genuinely enjoy spending time with, someone I can talk to about real things. Right now I feel like I barely exist, like I’m just going through the motions. I just want to feel alive again.
