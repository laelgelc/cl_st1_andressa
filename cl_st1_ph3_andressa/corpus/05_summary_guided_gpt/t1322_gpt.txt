I don’t even know where to start. I feel like I’m walking around with this giant open wound that nobody can see, or worse, they see it and just turn away.

I’ve been through some shit – jail, health problems, people dying around me – and still, I’ve tried to show up for everyone. I’ve tried to be present, to care, to love. Especially for those kids. Those kids are my anchor. They’re the only thing that makes me feel like I still matter in this world. And now even that feels like it’s being ripped away.

The person who’s hurting me the most is someone I still love. That’s what makes this so messed up. I don’t understand what I did wrong. Nobody will tell me. I’m just… being treated like I don’t exist. Ignored, avoided, like I’m some kind of ghost. I show up and it’s like I’m not even there.

The other day, that little girl – the one who used to run to me, talk to me, light up when she saw me – looked right through me. She actually looked past me, like I was invisible, and wouldn’t even speak. Do you know what that feels like? To have a child you love more than anything act like you’re nothing? It broke something in me. I keep replaying it in my head, trying to figure out what I did to deserve that.

I feel like my daughter was stolen from me twice. That’s the only way I can describe it. Once wasn’t enough, apparently. And I’m left here, angry and hurt and so damn confused. I’m being punished but I don’t even know what the crime is.

I still love the person who’s doing this to me. That’s the worst part. I don’t want to hate them. I don’t want to lose them. But I’m reaching my limit. I’ve told myself: if this isn’t resolved by the end of the year, I’m done. I’ll cut them off for my own sanity. I can’t keep begging to exist in someone’s life.

But the kids? I will never reject them. No matter what happens between the adults, I won’t turn my back on those children. They’re innocent in all this. I just wish someone would see how much this is killing me.
