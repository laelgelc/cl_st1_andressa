I feel like I’ve become the permanent third wheel in my own life.

My best and only friend is also my roommate, and for a while we were really close. We talked about everything, spent time together, and I honestly thought we were each other’s person. Lately, though, I feel like I’ve been pushed to the bottom of their priority list.

They rarely make any effort to spend time with me. If I don’t initiate, nothing happens. They always seem to prefer hanging out with other people, making plans with them, laughing with them. When something emotional happened recently, they went to a new acquaintance for support instead of me. That hurt more than I can explain.

It makes me feel used and unappreciated, like I’m just background noise in their life. I’m physically around them all the time but I feel constantly alone. I keep asking myself if I’m being selfish for wanting the same level of care and effort I give to others. Is it too much to want to be chosen sometimes?

I’m starting to wonder if I should just stop trying so hard, but the thought of letting go of the one person I have terrifies me.
