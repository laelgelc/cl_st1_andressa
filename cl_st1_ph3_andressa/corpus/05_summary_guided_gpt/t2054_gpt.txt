I’m 21 and every year February feels like some kind of punishment, especially Valentine’s Day. It’s like the whole month exists just to rub in my face how alone I am. Since elementary school it’s been the same pattern: I like someone, I try, I get rejected or humiliated. Over and over. 

Recently I really thought I’d finally found someone different, that maybe this time it wouldn’t end the same way. But it did. Same rejection, same empty feeling afterward, just deeper this time because I actually let myself hope.

Now Valentine’s Day is coming and my parents will be together, all my friends will be with their partners, and I’ll be the odd one out again with no one and no real support. I’m so worn down by this. I honestly don’t know how much longer I can keep going like this.
