Lately I’ve been stuck on this idea that we’re fundamentally alone. We’re born alone, we die alone, and even when we’re surrounded by people, there’s still this core part of us that no one else can fully reach. I don’t mean we’re doomed to feel lonely all the time, but there’s this basic aloneness that never really goes away. Because of that, I’m starting to feel like self-respect is everything. How can I respect myself if my happiness is always dependent on other people’s opinions or feelings about me?
