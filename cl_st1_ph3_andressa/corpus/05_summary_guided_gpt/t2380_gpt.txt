I’m 18 and I feel so incredibly lonely. I don’t have any close in‑person friends, just two online friends I talk to sometimes. I miss having real-life friends to go out with, to sit around at each other’s places, to just exist together. 

After graduation it really hit me. The people I thought were my friends took graduation photos without me. They never invited me, never even mentioned it. Now they still watch my stories, but they never reach out. Meanwhile, I see their posts of them all hanging out, smiling, doing exactly the things I wish I could do with people.

It makes me feel like I’m someone who can be easily discarded, like I never really mattered to them at all. So I just stay inside all day, imagining what it would be like to have a friend group. I feel hopeless about it, like I’m already “cooked” at 18.
