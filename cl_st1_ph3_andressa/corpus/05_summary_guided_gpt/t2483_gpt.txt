I found this old plushie today, the one I hid when I was 13 so no one would see it and make fun of me. As soon as I picked it up, it was like something broke open inside me. I’m more than 10 years older now, and all I could think was how that kid never grew into anything he hoped he’d be.

Now I just feel like some old, ugly man who’s never even had a girlfriend, like I missed every chance I was supposed to have. It honestly feels like there’s no saving me anymore. I feel so unloved and abandoned, like I don’t belong in this world at all. It seems like everything is made for selfish, abusive people who somehow always get what they want, while people like me just sink.

I keep wanting to apologize to my mom for turning out like this, and to that younger version of me who thought things would be different. I feel like I’m drowning and everyone can see it but just looks away. I can’t stop crying. I just want someone to hold me and tell me I’m not completely worthless.
