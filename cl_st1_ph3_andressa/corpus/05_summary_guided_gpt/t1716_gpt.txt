Went out with some old high school classmates tonight and it just reminded me how alone I feel. They’re not really my friends, more like familiar strangers. The bar was loud, bad music, not my vibe at all, and I spent most of the time wondering why I was even there.

I keep thinking I still haven’t found “my people.” I’ve never even kissed anyone. The only time my phone lights up is for work stuff. I’m bored, can’t seem to enjoy anything, and feel like I’m just watching my life happen from the passenger seat.

I honestly believe I’ll never have a partner. People always seem to drift away from me eventually. And I’m tired of hearing “go to therapy” like it’s a magic fix. It doesn’t feel that simple.
