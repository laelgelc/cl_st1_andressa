Woke up this morning feeling awful – sore throat, pounding headache, just that heavy, sick feeling. I decided to stay in bed and asked my roommate if they could bring me something back from brunch. They said yes, so I actually felt a bit cared for in that moment.

Then they came back… already eating, with nothing for me. Not even an apology or “they were out of stuff” or anything. Just… nothing.

Now my choices are either waiting until dinner or eating instant ramen, which I really don’t feel like. It’s such a small thing, but it hit me hard. It just makes me feel like nobody really cares about me or thinks of me, and that hurts more than the sickness.
