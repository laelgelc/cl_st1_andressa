I feel so alone lately that it’s like I don’t really exist outside my own head. I’m always thinking, overthinking, replaying conversations, imagining ones that never happen. Sometimes I catch myself talking out loud and I don’t even realize I’ve been doing it.

When I see other people laughing together or just being comfortable with each other, I don’t even know what I’m supposed to feel. Jealous? Sad? Numb? I just feel like some kind of alien watching a different species live their lives.

I can usually recognize what people are feeling and maybe even why, but I still don’t actually understand people. I don’t understand myself either. I feel completely lost in my own mind.

Most of the time I fake my reactions so no one notices how different I am. The only real emotions I have left are anger at myself for existing and this constant frustration. I just wish there was someone out there who really understood this.
