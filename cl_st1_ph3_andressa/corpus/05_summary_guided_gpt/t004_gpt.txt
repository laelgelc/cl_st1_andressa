They think teenagers only go out to do dangerous or “dumb” things, so I’m basically never allowed to go anywhere. Because of that, I don’t really get invited to things anymore. I feel awkward even asking people to hang out, because I’m always worried my parents will say no and it’ll be embarrassing.

It’s starting to really hurt. I feel left out and like I don’t belong anywhere. I’m scared this is just how my life is going to be, like I’ll always be the person who’s “there” but never really included.
