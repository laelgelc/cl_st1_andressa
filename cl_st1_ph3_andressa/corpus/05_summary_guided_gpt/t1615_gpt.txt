I’ve noticed something weird about myself. When I’m in a big crowd, I feel incredibly lonely and unhappy, like I’m just another anonymous face while everyone else is laughing and connecting. It almost makes the loneliness louder. But if I’m in a small, quiet place—like a bookstore with only a few people around—I feel calm and content, even if I’m still alone. It’s such a stark contrast, and I don’t really understand why I’m like this.
