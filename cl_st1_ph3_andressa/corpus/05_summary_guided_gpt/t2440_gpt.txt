I don’t really know where else to put this, so here it goes.

On paper, my life looks fine. I have a job I actually love and I’m good at it. I pay my bills on time, my place is clean and in good shape. I do sports and I’m decent at them. I play online games and I’m in therapy. I love my dogs more than anything and I take really good care of them.

But I feel so intensely, crushingly lonely.

I’ve got some ugly stuff too – I’m addicted to vaping and gambling. The gambling has put me in debt, but my job is good enough that I can realistically climb out of it if I don’t screw it up. Even with all that, the thing that hurts the most is this feeling that none of it really matters because I’m doing it all alone.

I don’t have a girlfriend, and it feels like that’s a big part of why I feel this way. I have so much love to give and nowhere for it to go. My achievements, my struggles, my small wins… it all feels unseen, or like I can’t fully share it with anyone. Life just doesn’t feel worth living alone. It feels like I could disappear and it wouldn’t change much.

What really messes with my head is that even when I was in long relationships before, the loneliness only got quieter. It never really went away. So I don’t even know if a relationship would fix this, but I still ache for one.

I’m a man, and it honestly feels like that means I don’t matter much these days. The weird thing is I know I’m not some hopeless case – I’m handsome, smart, with a normal body. I’ve prayed to God about this specific loneliness and it just… hasn’t lifted.

Has anyone else felt like this? Like you’re doing “everything right” and still feel completely alone?
