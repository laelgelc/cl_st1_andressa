I’m 38, introverted, shy, and quiet, and it feels like I’ve never really had a proper friendship or relationship. The few people I hung out with in high school slowly drifted away as their lives got better, and I stayed the same. I was always the “weirdo,” the one people whispered about and gossiped over. It never felt like anyone actually wanted me around; I just got pity and sympathy, never a real connection.

I was recently diagnosed with avoidant personality disorder, and it kind of confirmed what I’ve always feared: that I’m going to be alone for the rest of my life. I’ve tried therapy, dating apps, hobbies—everything just ends up failing. I honestly don’t think anyone will ever truly care about me.
