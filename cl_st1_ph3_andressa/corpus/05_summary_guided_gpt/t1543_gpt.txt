I’m just so tired of all of this.

I’ve tried doing the “right” things. New hobbies, new places, trying to meet new people. Nothing sticks. Nothing feels interesting for long. It all feels like I’m going through the motions because I’m supposed to, not because I actually care.

I recently ended things with someone because I knew they weren’t right for me. It wasn’t dramatic, just… wrong in that quiet way where you know you’d be settling. And now I’m back where I was before: alone. I know I did the “healthy” thing, but it doesn’t feel rewarding. It just feels like more empty space.

I’m exhausted by the whole dating “trial and error” process. Swiping, small talk, the awkward first meetings, the hope, the disappointment. I keep wondering if I’m just not cut out for this, like maybe some people are meant to find someone and some just aren’t.

The worst part is I still want it so badly. I want connection, intimacy, someone to come home to, someone to share the boring parts of life with. If I’m meant to be alone, I wish the desire would just go away. It would be easier if I didn’t care.

Most days blur together: work, distractions, scrolling, sleep. Underneath it all, there’s this constant ache and this fear that this is just… it. That I’ll be alone forever. I feel like I’ve been alone most of my life already.

People say “love yourself first” and “do the self-work,” but it’s starting to feel like a loop that never ends. In the end, it feels like it all just comes down to luck, and I’m scared I’m never going to get any.
