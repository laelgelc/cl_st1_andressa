I had an argument with my sisters, and they kept repeating, “You don’t know what it’s like to be a parent.” They probably didn’t mean it the way it landed, but it cut really deep. The one thing I wanted most in life was a family, and it never happened. My marriage failed, I never had kids, and that’s my biggest regret. They don’t seem to understand how painful that reminder is. I came back to my empty apartment and it just echoed in my head.
