I’m 22 and it feels like my life is just… empty space.

I barely talk to my family, and the few relatives I have I rarely see. Even when they reach out, I struggle to reply. I don’t have any friends, never had a relationship, and I spend most of my time alone. I work as a delivery driver, but even there I don’t really interact with anyone – just drop things off and move on.

At home I’m supposed to be caring for my mom, who has MS, but a lot of the time I can’t even find the strength to leave my room. This has basically been my life since around 2019. I just feel tired and hollow, like I’m stuck in some loop.

I keep wondering if it’s my personality, my lack of effort, or something else that’s made things this way. It honestly feels like I might be damned to this kind of existence.

Has anyone else been here and actually found a way to navigate this kind of meaningless (or maybe meaningful?) conscious existence?
