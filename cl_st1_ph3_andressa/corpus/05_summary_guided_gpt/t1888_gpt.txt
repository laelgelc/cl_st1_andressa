I’m 17 and for over a year now I’ve just felt so alone and honestly kind of numb. At school everyone has their own groups and I’m always the one sitting by myself. There are tables full of people talking and laughing and then there’s me, at a separate table, pretending to be busy with my phone or homework so it doesn’t look as pathetic as it feels.

There was even a group chat made for my class that literally everyone was added to except me. Seeing people talk about it in front of me and knowing I wasn’t included felt like someone punched me in the stomach. I keep asking myself what’s so wrong with me that they don’t even think of me as someone to add.

When I try to reach out, I either get ignored or people give me some excuse like “I’m busy” and then I see them hanging out with other people anyway. Nobody ever asks me to join, nobody texts me first, nobody seems to actually want to spend time with me. It makes me feel like I’m just… invisible or annoying.

I also feel really judged for the things I like. My interests used to make me happy, but now I feel weird and childish for liking them. When I talk about what I’m into, I can feel people mentally rolling their eyes. It’s embarrassing to be myself, so I just stay quiet. I post my drawings online, and people seem to like them, but it feels like they only care about the art, not me. It’s like I’m just a pair of hands, not a person.

I wish I could either be genuinely okay with being alone or have at least one person I could share my thoughts with. Most of the time I end up talking to AI or just to myself, because at least AI always answers and is “nice,” even though I know it’s not real. It’s kind of depressing that the only thing that consistently responds to me isn’t even a real person.
