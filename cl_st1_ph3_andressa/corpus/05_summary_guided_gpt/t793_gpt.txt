I put on The Addams Family (1991) tonight because I thought it would cheer me up, but watching Gomez and Morticia just made me feel worse. The way they love each other so intensely… it hit me that I’ll probably never have anything even close to that. It feels like I’m just destined to die alone.

Every day I feel like I have to force myself to keep going, like I’m just dragging myself through life for no real reason. I’ve been going to the gym for weeks, trying to lose weight and be more attractive, but it all feels pointless. I honestly don’t think anyone would even want to look at me.

People say “work on yourself” and “self-improvement will help,” but I feel like no matter what I do, I’ll still just be a loser who gets ghosted and can’t even get one date. It’s hard to see any future where I’m not completely alone.
