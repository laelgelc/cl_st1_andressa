For a short while I thought things were getting better at work. It seemed like my coworkers were talking to me more, and I actually felt a bit lighter. Now I’m convinced it was all fake, like they were just humoring me, and underneath it everyone hates me or is constantly judging me.

I feel like I can’t live with this constant sense of being watched and evaluated. I almost wish no one would talk to me at all, but I don’t want to be rude or hurt anyone. Is it selfish to tell people, even someone close to me, that I just want to be alone, especially when I’m already judged for being alone so much?
