I’m in my 30s and I’ve only ever had one relationship, a really bad six-year one that left me pretty messed up. Now I have no family, no friends, no social life. It feels like I’m just drifting through life completely alone.

What hurts the most is missing romantic connection and simple physical affection. I miss being touched, hugged, held. I can’t remember the last time someone hugged me just because they cared. It makes my skin almost ache sometimes, like my body remembers what it’s like but can’t have it.

I get so frustrated when I see men online saying women can easily find partners. That’s just not my reality. When I go out, no one seems interested in me. I honestly don’t think I’m very interesting, and it feels like people pick up on that and overlook me.

Nighttime is the only time I get any relief. When I sleep, I’m not lonely. I dream about my childhood, when I still had family around, and for a little while it feels like I belong somewhere again.
