I’m a 26-year-old woman and I’m really confused and hurt by how casually people say “I have no friends.” I see people my age post that, then the next day they’re out drinking, going to concerts, having game nights with the same group of people. When I say I have no friends, I mean it literally. I don’t have a single person to text, hang out with, or confide in. It feels like a slap in the face seeing others call themselves “friendless” when they clearly aren’t as alone as I am. It makes my loneliness feel invisible and invalid.
