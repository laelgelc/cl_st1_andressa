I’m 18 and I feel like my BPD has been getting worse. My episodes are more intense and I’ve been isolating more and more. Even when I’m physically around people, I feel completely alone. I watch my friends laugh with each other and I can’t shake the feeling that they’re all closer to each other than to me, like I’m just… there. Extra.

I want “my person” so badly it hurts. A boyfriend or even just a friend who genuinely likes me, understands me, chooses me. Someone I don’t feel like I’m bothering or forcing to be around me. But I’m starting to worry that maybe that kind of love just isn’t going to happen for me.

Whenever I try to talk about this, people dismiss it with “you’re still young, you have time” or “don’t worry, it’ll happen when you least expect it.” It makes me feel like I’m not allowed to be honest about how much I crave connection, like I’m being dramatic or needy for wanting it this much.

I keep wondering what’s wrong with me. Why does everyone else seem to find their person, or at least feel like they belong somewhere, and I’m stuck feeling like an outsider everywhere I go? I don’t want to be alone. I just want to feel normal for once, like I’m not some broken person destined to always be on the outside looking in.
