Every time I go to the mall and see people my age who are fit, well-dressed, laughing with friends or holding hands with their partners, I feel this wave of anxiety that’s almost like a panic attack. It’s like I’m being shown everything I’m not. I feel so stuck and nonfunctional, like a complete failure in life. 

I haven’t had a loving hug in a year. Seeing girls my age just makes me feel this deep sadness and emptiness. I miss human love so much. It honestly feels like no one cares about me, like my suffering doesn’t matter and my death wouldn’t matter to anyone either.
