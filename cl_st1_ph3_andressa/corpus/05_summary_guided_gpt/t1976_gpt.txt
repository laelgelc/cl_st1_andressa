I’ve been on my own for about a year now, and it honestly feels like this is just how it’s going to be from here on out. I’m worn down in a way I don’t really know how to put into words. Life has never really been easy, and after so many heartbreaks I feel like I’ve hit some kind of emotional rock bottom.

My ex used to say they wanted to see me happy with someone else one day. They’ve moved on, and I’m genuinely glad they’re okay. But every time I try to move on myself, it just… doesn’t work. Every attempt at something new ends in disappointment, and I come out of it even more mentally exhausted than before. Seeing other people happy together makes me start wondering if I can really keep going alone like this.

Recently I’ve started to catch feelings for someone. They answer my compliments, they actually go out with me, and they’re also recovering from a breakup. I can’t tell if I’m just a friend they’re leaning on or if there’s something more there. I don’t want to read too much into it and end up hurting myself again.

I’m just so tired of only surviving. It feels like that’s all I’ve been doing since I was a kid—just getting through, not really living. I don’t even know what I’m looking for by writing this. Maybe I just needed to say it somewhere.
