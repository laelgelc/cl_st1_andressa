I’m 29 and I feel like I’m disappearing. I can go days without speaking to anyone. I don’t have friends or even real acquaintances. My family only contacts me when they need something, never to actually check in, and it just reinforces how alone I am.

I’m too drained to even try social activities anymore. When I did push myself in the past, nothing really came of it, so now it just feels pointless. The only thing that’s ever kept me going is academia – I’ve done a PhD, multiple master’s degrees – but it all feels kind of wasted when there’s no one to share any of it with.

My 9–5 just drains my spirit, and I’m terrified this is how the next 50+ years will look, even though I keep telling myself I’m “prepared” for it.
