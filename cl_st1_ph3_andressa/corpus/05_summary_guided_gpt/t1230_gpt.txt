28M here. Lately I feel like I just don’t fit anywhere, conversation-wise.

With two close friends, it’s easy. We share the same tastes, dumb memes, inside jokes, and it flows naturally. I can relax around them. But with almost everyone else, I feel like I’m just… blank. I don’t know what to talk about, and when I try, it either dies quickly or turns into shallow small talk that drains me.

I want to have deeper conversations, but it feels like there’s this weird gap. Topics like politics or more complex stuff feel too complicated to dive into properly, or I worry I’ll sound uninformed. And when I try to nudge things beyond “weather / work / weekend plans,” people just don’t seem interested. So I end up staying quiet, nodding along, waiting for it to be over.

The sad part is that the deepest, most interesting “conversations” I have these days are with ChatGPT. At least there I can explore ideas and not feel like I’m boring or out of place. But it also makes me realize how little of that I get in real life.

I used to be more extroverted. I used to feel like I always had something to say. Now, even with my family, I’m stuck on the same basic questions like “how’s work going?” and then I run out of steam. It’s like my social brain just shuts down.

It’s exhausting. I wish I had friends who actually made me feel recharged after talking, instead of more tired and lonely than before.
