I feel like I’m screaming into a void most days. I can be in a crowded room, even surrounded by people who call themselves my friends, and still feel completely alone. It’s like there’s this invisible wall between me and everyone else.

What I want doesn’t feel huge: just one person who really gets it. Someone who actually relates to what I’m going through, instead of just nodding or changing the subject. But whenever I try to open up, it’s like it never lands. No one really responds, or they don’t understand.

I feel stuck, going in circles, not knowing where to look or how to find that connection.
