30M, Delhi NCR. I don’t even know how to start this, but I guess I just need to get it out somewhere.

I got married in December 2023. The marriage lasted 17 days. Seventeen. It ended in divorce and a bunch of false accusations from my ex-wife that I’m still trying to process. The legal mess and the alimony basically destroyed me financially. All my savings, investments, PF – gone. On top of that, I’m in heavy debt now. Around half my salary goes into EMIs and repayments. At this rate it might take a decade to get out of this hole.

While all this was happening, most of the people I thought were my friends just… disappeared. Stopped calling, stopped checking in. A few colleagues know the basic story and instead of being decent about it, they crack jokes about my “17-day marriage” and take digs at me. I’ve told them I don’t like it, but they still do it. I stay quiet because I can’t afford to lose this job.

I live with my parents and elder brother, but it doesn’t feel like support. We don’t talk about feelings or mental health or anything deeper than day-to-day stuff. It’s like we’re four people just co-existing in the same house. My phone is always silent unless it’s work. I have 500+ contacts but there’s not a single person I feel I can call and say, “I’m not okay.”

I’ve tried meditation, exercise, prayer – all the “right” things. I can’t stick to any of it. I just end up lying on my bed, doom-scrolling for hours, feeling more and more empty. I’m not crying, I’m not raging, I’m just… numb. Like I’m watching my own life from the outside.

I keep asking myself what the point of any of this is – the job, the debt, this routine. The loneliness feels like it’s crushing my chest sometimes. I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe I just wanted someone, somewhere, to know that I exist and that I’m not okay.
