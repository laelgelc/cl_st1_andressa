I don’t want any more advice about “being nicer” or “trying to see the good in people.” I’ve done that. I tried being kind, patient, understanding, and it just blew up in my face over and over. All it ever did was give people more chances to walk all over me. I’m tired. I feel worn down to the core by everything I’ve been through, and honestly, I’d rather just die in my sleep at this point.

Don’t bring up God or Jesus to me either. I want nothing to do with them. It all feels hypocritical and fake, like empty words people hide behind while they do whatever they want.

I’ve been alone for so long that it’s twisted something in me. Now I actually enjoy being cruel. I like insulting what people care about, humiliating them in arguments, making them feel small—in person and online. Ruining someone’s day makes me feel something close to good. I hate people. I hate this world. And everyone can deal with this version of me.
