I’m 21 and I feel like I’ve been lonely for as long as I can remember. I don’t have any in-person friends. I only have a few online ones, and the only family I really have is my dad. Every “friend group” I’ve ever had has been short-lived. It’s like I’m always the person people talk to when they’re bored or lonely, and then once they have other options, I just fade into the background.

I’ve always preferred online friendships because they’re less draining and I don’t feel like I have the energy or social skills for real-life ones. Recently I thought I’d finally found someone who really understood me. He was also lonely, and he told me he’d prioritize me and that I mattered to him. For a little while I actually believed it. But once he reconnected with his other friends, he slowly pulled away. Now we barely talk, and I’m back to feeling like I was just a temporary filler until something better came along.

I just want one person who genuinely cares about me as much as I care about them. I always seem to care more, try harder, and stay longer. I like being supportive and listening to people’s problems, but it hurts when they eventually ditch me.

I’m shy, I don’t have many interests besides playing random Roblox games, and I honestly don’t know how to make friends or keep them. I feel so sad and lonely most of the time, and I don’t really have anyone I can say this to. I guess I just needed to get this off my chest somewhere.
