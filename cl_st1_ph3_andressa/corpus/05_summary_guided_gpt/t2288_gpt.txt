I’m at this point where trying to find new people just feels pointless. Every time I’ve had “friends,” it’s ended up being me listening to their problems, supporting them, giving advice… and when it’s my turn, they either change the subject, minimize it, or just don’t get it. It’s like I’m only useful as an emotional dumping ground.

Because of that, I’ve basically stopped talking to the few people I did have. They’re so wrapped up in themselves that I don’t see the point anymore. My therapist keeps telling me I can’t make connections if I stay at home, which I get logically, but when I try to meet new people it feels like the same pattern: they’re not really interested in *me*, just in what they can get.

I keep wondering where the genuine people are. Are they even out there? Being alone at least means I’m not being used, but it still feels awful and empty. I’m stuck between dealing with selfish people or just… being by myself. Neither option feels good.
