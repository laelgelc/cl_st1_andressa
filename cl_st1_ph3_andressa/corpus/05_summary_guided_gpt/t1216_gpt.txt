I’m a sophomore in college and somehow I feel more alone now than I ever have, which is saying a lot because I feel like I’ve always been this way. On paper, I’m not completely isolated – I’m in film school and I’ve got people I work with on projects, group chats, all that. But none of it feels real. It’s like everyone else has actual friends and I’m just… there, filling a spot.

I don’t really have anyone I can honestly call a friend anymore. I broke up with my girlfriend and I regret it so much now. It feels like I pushed away the one person who actually cared, and I can’t undo it. My “best friend” did something that made me lose trust in them, and now I can’t look at them the same way. My family barely talks to me and I don’t even fully understand why. It’s like I’m on the outside of my own life.

I’ve been trying to make friends for as long as I can remember, but nothing sticks. People are nice enough in the moment, then it just fades. When I try asking girls out, it’s usually a no, and that just adds to this feeling that there’s something wrong with me.

I don’t think I’m weird or anything, but I’m starting to feel like maybe I’m just meant to be alone and I should start getting used to it.
