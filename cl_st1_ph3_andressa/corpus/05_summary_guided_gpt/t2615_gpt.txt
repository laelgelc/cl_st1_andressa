My place is a disaster and I honestly need help, but I feel really uncomfortable with the idea of hiring a maid. I keep thinking about how domestic workers are often treated like they’re beneath the people they work for, almost like “slaves” to rich people, and it makes the whole thing feel morally wrong to me. It feels like there must be some hidden catch in the whole arrangement. So I’m stuck: I need my home cleaned, but I don’t want to be part of that dynamic. On top of that, I’ve learned that being alone can feel great for a few years, but after a while it just stops being sustainable.
