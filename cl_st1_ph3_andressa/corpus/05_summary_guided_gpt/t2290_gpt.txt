I just realized it’s been a year since anyone has reached out to me first. Not a single “hey, how are you?” unless I’m the one who starts it. I stopped texting first to see what would happen, and the answer was… nothing. Silence.

Meanwhile, my sister still has people visiting her, checking in, making plans. I watch her get messages and calls, and it just drives home how forgotten I am. It makes me feel like I’m invisible, like I don’t matter to anyone.

I know people are busy, but it just feels so unfair that in an entire year, not one person thought of me enough to send a simple hi. I keep wondering what’s wrong with me, why I’m so easy to forget. It hurts more than I can really explain.
