I’m 18 with AuDHD and severe social anxiety, and I haven’t had a real friend since I was 12. I literally don’t know how to talk to people anymore. It feels impossible, like there’s a wall between me and everyone else. People keep saying “it’ll get better” or “you’ll find your people,” but I just can’t believe that. Because of my ADHD, I feel subhuman, fundamentally incompatible with others. My mind is made up. Nothing can change it.
