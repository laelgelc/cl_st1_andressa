I’m confused about myself socially. I honestly enjoy hanging out with friends and being around people, but I still prefer being alone most of the time. Whenever I’m with others, it feels like I have to “turn on a switch” and suddenly be this nice, pleasant version of myself. It’s not that I’m faking it, but it feels like work, and it gets exhausting really fast.

On top of that, I notice I get mad or irritated over small things when I’m around people. Little comments, habits, or noises start to bother me more than they should. That just pushes me to choose being alone instead. It feels easier, even if it’s a bit lonely sometimes.
