It feels like there’s literally nothing to live for except work, and even that just stretches out the misery. I’m completely alone and alienated. People either ignore me or treat me like garbage—bad-mouthing, scorning, judging, ridiculing. It’s like I’m some kind of joke they pass around. Honestly, I don’t think anyone would even notice if I died until my body started to smell. That’s how invisible I feel. Sometimes I catch myself wondering how long I’ll actually last like this. Anyone want to place their bets?
