I’m starting to think I’m just the “funny one with a good personality” and nothing more. People seem to like having me around, but not in a “let’s date” or “let’s build a life together” way. I haven’t even been on a date in 10 years. Ten years. It’s like I’m invisible romantically.

I always pictured myself with kids, a partner, a home that felt warm and full. Instead, I’m here alone. No parents anymore. One friend, and even that friendship feels… mediocre, like we’re just hanging on out of habit. My sibling has the life I wanted so badly – kids, a partner, all of it – and I’m genuinely happy for them, but it also hurts in this deep, ugly way I can’t admit out loud.

I feel this heavy grief for a life that never happened, like I’m mourning something that only existed in my head. And I keep thinking: maybe this is it. Maybe this is all I’ll ever get.

What makes it worse is knowing that tomorrow I’ll put the mask back on, crack jokes, be “the funny one” again, and no one will have a clue how lonely I really am.
