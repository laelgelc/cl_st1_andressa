I feel like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me, like I just wasn’t built to be cared about or chosen. It’s not even that people are cruel to me – it’s more like they’re right not to care. I look at myself and think, “Why would anyone want me around?” and I honestly can’t come up with a good answer.

It feels like this deep, hollow emptiness inside me, like I’m just taking up space. I keep thinking that no one will ever pick me, that I’ll always be the one people forget, the one they tolerate but don’t actually want. It makes me want to push everyone away preemptively, just so I don’t have to feel that inevitable rejection again.

I don’t know how to stop hating myself or believing that I’m inherently unlovable. But if anyone reads this, even if I can’t believe it for myself, I want you to treat yourself kindly. Take care of yourself. You deserve care and gentleness, even if I can’t see why I would.
