Sometimes it feels like “follow your dreams” is a cruel joke, because no achievement feels worth it if it costs you being known and seen by someone. Nothing I’ve done, or could do, feels better than the idea of really being understood. I wish I’d realized that sooner.

But I also have to admit: chasing your dreams does teach you a lot about life and about yourself. At least it kills the “what if” that can haunt you forever.

I’m extremely lonely some days. Other days I feel more like myself than I ever have. If you’re on the fence, I’d still say: take a chance on life.
