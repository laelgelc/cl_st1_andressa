24M here. Single, no kids, haven’t been in a serious relationship in about 2.5 years.

In that time I’ve actually grown a lot. I’ve gotten way better at being on my own. I don’t just “cope” with being alone anymore – I genuinely enjoy going to the gym, running my errands, watching shows, just doing my own thing. I like my routines and the quiet. For the most part, I’m okay.

But every now and then I get hit with these waves of loneliness that catch me off guard. It’s not that I miss my ex – I really don’t want that relationship back – but I miss feeling emotionally safe with someone. I miss having that person I could come home to and just lie next to, talk about nothing, fall asleep next to. That feeling of being known and still accepted. I haven’t had that in a long time.

I only really have the occasional hookup or short fling, maybe once or twice a year. It scratches the physical itch for a night, but it doesn’t touch the emotional side at all. Sometimes I catch myself thinking maybe I’m just not meant to end up with someone, even though deep down I still quietly want to fall in love again and build something real.

Most of my friends are getting married or having kids now, so the dynamic has changed. They’re busy, their priorities are different, and I get it, but it makes me feel even more like I’m on the outside looking in at a life I’m not part of.

I’m in the military, spend most of my free time at the gym, and honestly feel kind of boring. The funny part is my friends tell me I’m good-looking and act confused about how I’m still single. I don’t really know what to say to that.

I’m not even sure why I’m posting this. Maybe I just needed to put it somewhere. If you read this far, sorry if it feels like a waste of time.
