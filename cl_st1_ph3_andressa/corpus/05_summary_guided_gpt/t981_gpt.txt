I’m 20 and I’ve never had a best friend or any kind of romantic relationship. Whenever it comes up, people act shocked, like it’s some huge anomaly, and that reaction just makes me feel like I must be doing something wrong without knowing what it is.

I always feel like the third or fourth choice. People seem to have “their person” already, someone they’re excited to see, someone they call first. I’m never that person for anyone. I’m just… there, an option if others aren’t available.

There are times when I really am okay being alone and I tell myself I’m just waiting for the right person, friend or partner. But after so many years, that mindset is getting harder to hold onto. The wait has worn me down, and I’m starting to feel like maybe no one will ever truly choose me first.
