Lately I feel like I’m stuck in this weird in‑between state where I can’t even tell what’s wrong with me. I don’t know if I’m just exhausted, misunderstood, or actually lonely. It’s like I’m emotionally numb most of the time.  

I really struggle to make genuine, deep connections. Almost every interaction feels surface‑level, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. Even with the two close friends I do have, there’s still this distance I can’t seem to cross. I care about them, but sometimes supporting them feels draining, like I’m running on empty and still trying to pour into other people.  

The numbness is confusing too. Sometimes it’s a relief not to feel everything so intensely. But other times I just wish I could feel anything at all, because this emptiness makes me feel even more alone.
