Lately I’ve been feeling really jaded and worn down by how lonely I am. I just want someone to talk to, to message throughout the day, to say good morning to. I keep imagining how nice it would be to just hold someone’s hand and enjoy the weather together, and it makes the emptiness feel even sharper.

I’ve been homebound and working remotely for about two years now, and it’s like my world has shrunk to four walls and a screen. I know people say “go out and meet people,” but I honestly don’t know how much that would help. I feel like no one would want to talk to someone who’s clearly a loner.

Dating apps haven’t helped either. I don’t think I’m photogenic at all, and it’s hard to put myself out there when I already assume I’ll be ignored or rejected.

It feels like I’m stuck in this loop of loneliness that I can’t escape from, and I keep blaming myself for not doing better. Is anyone else in a similar place right now?
