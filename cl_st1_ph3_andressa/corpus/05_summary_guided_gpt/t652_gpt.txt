My birthday was recently and I can’t shake how much it hurt this year.

None of my close friends said anything. Not even a “happy birthday.” There’s this girl I’ve been talking to for four years. We’ve shared so much, and it just feels like something as basic as my birthday would be in her mind by now. But the day came and went. Nothing.

Then there’s a friend I’ve known for nine years. He definitely knows my birthday. Still, silence. And what really stung was that not long after, he messaged me asking for help figuring out a gift for his girlfriend. So he can remember her, think about what she’d like, but not even send me a two-word text on my birthday.

It makes me feel like I’ve been quietly replaced in people’s lives, like I’m just a background character now that they’re in relationships and moving on. I keep telling myself it’s not about presents or big celebrations. I just wanted some sign that I still matter, that I haven’t completely faded from their hearts.

Part of me wonders if I care too much about this, or if I’m expecting too much from people. But the pain feels very real. It’s like this clear reminder that I don’t hold the same place in their lives anymore, and I don’t really know what to do with that feeling.
