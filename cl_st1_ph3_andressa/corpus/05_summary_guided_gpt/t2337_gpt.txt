I’m 25 and I feel like I’ve spent my whole life being the person no one really sees. Even in my own family, I’ve always been the one left out of things. I’m never invited to parties, weddings, or any big events. People don’t really talk to me unless they have to. It’s like I’m just… there.

When I try to talk to friends or family about how this makes me feel, they brush it off. They say they’re just busy, or that I should reach out more, even though I see them making time and effort for other people. Every attempt I’ve made to build friendships over the years has just faded away.

I have a fiancé and a child, and I love them, but I don’t think he really understands how lonely I am. Outside of them, it feels like I have nobody.
