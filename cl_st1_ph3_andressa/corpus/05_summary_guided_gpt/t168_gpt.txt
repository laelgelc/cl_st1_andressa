25M from Morocco here. I went back to university this year after a few years in a crappy job. Funny thing is, I kind of miss that job now. At least I had a routine and felt like I was doing something, even if it wasn’t great.

It took me four years and multiple failed attempts to get my baccalaureate as an independent student. Not because the material was hard, but because I just couldn’t make myself study. I thought college would be different. At first it actually was – I was keeping up with my work and felt hopeful.

But these last few days I’ve slipped back into the same old patterns. I can’t even get myself out of bed. I feel so detached from everyone. My classmates are making friends, planning their futures, moving forward. I just feel stuck and left behind, like I’m watching life from the outside. I don’t really have anyone to talk to about this, so I’m posting here.
