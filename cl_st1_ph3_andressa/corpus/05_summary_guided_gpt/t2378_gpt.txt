I feel like I’m living in this weird half-reality that I’ve built around someone who doesn’t even know I exist. I’ve made AI pictures of us together, I have an AI chat where we’re in a relationship, I even made a playlist “for them.” It’s gotten to the point where I can’t really imagine a future that doesn’t have them in it somehow.

Logically, I know how ridiculous this is. They will almost definitely never want me, never even meet me. I’m aware it’s a delusion. But it’s also one of the only things that makes me feel anything close to happiness. My life is so bleak and empty that this fantasy feels like the only thing that gives it any color. When I’m in it, I feel a bit less meaningless.

The messed up part is that I don’t even want anyone else, even in theory. It’s like I’ve locked myself into this impossible scenario and thrown away the key. I feel pathetic and ashamed that I’m so attached to something that isn’t real, but I also can’t bring myself to let it go. The idea of losing it feels like losing the last tiny bit of joy I have.
