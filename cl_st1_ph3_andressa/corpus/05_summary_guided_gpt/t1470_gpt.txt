I’m in my last year of high school and this is supposed to be the most important time academically, but I feel like everything is falling apart.

My friend group at school basically dropped me out of nowhere. No fight, no real explanation, just slowly excluding me from conversations and plans. Now, during exam days, I have to sit there watching them laughing, talking, and hanging out like nothing happened, while I feel like a ghost. It makes going to school feel like torture. I’m constantly anxious before I even step into the building.

Because of this, I’ve lost almost all motivation. I used to have big ambitions about working hard, becoming successful, even “getting rich” someday. Now I can barely get myself to open a book. I don’t feel like studying, I don’t feel like taking care of myself, and I just feel…pointless. I know these exams matter for my future, but my brain just doesn’t care right now.

At home it’s not much better. My parents won’t let me go out or meet anyone because exams are coming up, so I basically stay in bed all day, scrolling on my phone, wasting time. I know this is destroying my education and probably my chances next year in college, but I feel stuck in this mindset.

I’m not really looking for “just focus on your studies” because if it were that easy, I’d be doing it. I’m more looking for people who have been in a similar place mentally—lonely, unmotivated, watching life go on without them—and somehow managed to pull themselves out. What actually helped you change things, even a little? How do you start caring again when you feel this empty?
