I’m 16 and I feel like I’ve been lonely forever. It really started when I was around 11. I got bullied a lot at school, called names, made to feel like I didn’t matter. Over time it turned into this heavy depression that just never really left.

There was a point where I honestly wanted to end everything. The only reason I didn’t was because of my friend who also has depression. I felt like I had to be there for them, and I promised myself I’d never leave them alone like that.

But even with that, I still feel so alone. It’s like I want to cry but nothing comes out. I just wish someone would hug me and tell me it’s going to be okay, and actually mean it.
