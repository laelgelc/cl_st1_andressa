I’m 16 and I feel like I’ve already messed everything up.

I missed three months of school and I’m so far behind on my assignments that it feels impossible to catch up. Every time I look at the list of things I owe, it just makes me feel more and more worthless. It’s like everyone else is moving forward and I’m stuck in the same place, watching from the outside.

I don’t really have any talents except music and art, and those don’t exactly help when you’re drowning in schoolwork. I can’t really connect with anyone at school either. I just kind of float around, alone. There is one teacher who knows what’s going on and actually cares, and I’m grateful for that, but it still feels like I’m on my own most of the time.

I don’t hate school or the education system. In a weird way, school and the library are the only places I have to go. Home doesn’t feel like much of a refuge. My mom is 57 and there’s a language barrier between us, so even when I want to talk, I don’t really know how. I’ve tried running away before and now it just feels like one more stupid decision on a long list.

I’m basically an internet-addicted loner who hates how they look and wishes appearances didn’t matter so much. I want simple things like new glasses and a cheap laptop so I can actually work and do things, but right now I just have my phone.

There’s this guy online who expects me to be someone I’m not. I’m a hopeless romantic, but I’m also the kind of person who suffers in silence and helps others instead of myself. I feel sorry for myself a lot, but I’m still open to talking to people and trying to help them.

I just really, really hope life gets better from here.
