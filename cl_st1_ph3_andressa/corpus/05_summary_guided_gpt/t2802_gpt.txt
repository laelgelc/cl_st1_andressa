I’m not afraid of people and I wouldn’t call myself antisocial, but I genuinely enjoy being alone and being left alone. It probably sounds selfish, and honestly, I love it.

I’ve been a loner since high school back in the early 2000s. I’m the oldest, and there was a time when it was just me before my siblings came along. I remember that period as wonderful—quiet, simple, just me and my thoughts. 

Sometimes I think I’m trying to get back to that feeling: carefree, alone, living in my own mind without anyone needing anything from me. I don’t really have a point here, just wanted to share this.
