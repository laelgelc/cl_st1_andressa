I’m sitting alone in this busy café right now, and I feel weirdly out of place. It’s mostly older people here, chatting and laughing in their little groups, and I feel like I accidentally time-traveled into the wrong era. I keep catching myself imagining I’m some person from the future who doesn’t quite belong in this scene.

They all look so comfortable, so settled. Couples sharing food, friends joking around, people who clearly have years of shared history between them. It makes me think about what it would be like to grow old with someone, to have that kind of warmth and familiarity. I hope I get there someday.

Meanwhile I’m just here alone, staring at my phone, and I can’t stop worrying about how I look to everyone else. Like I’m some sketchy kid who might dine and dash, or just another person wasting time scrolling. I feel both invisible and judged at the same time.
