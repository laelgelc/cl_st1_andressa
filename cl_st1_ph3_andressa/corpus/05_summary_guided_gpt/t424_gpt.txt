I feel like I don’t know how to connect with people on equal footing. Deep down I always feel like I’m below everyone, like there’s some invisible hierarchy and I’m stuck at the bottom. It messes with how I think about relationships.

Sometimes I catch myself thinking I don’t actually want a relationship, I want… ownership? Control? Like if I was somehow “above” the other person, I wouldn’t be as vulnerable and couldn’t be hurt as badly. I know how messed up and unrealistic that sounds. I don’t want to be that person, but the thought keeps creeping in and it makes me feel like a horrible loser.

This applies mostly to romantic relationships, but also a bit to friendships. I’m scared of being vulnerable, scared of being rejected or abandoned, and my brain twists it into this weird power dynamic fantasy instead of a normal, mutual connection.

I don’t really know what to do with these thoughts. I just needed to get it out.
