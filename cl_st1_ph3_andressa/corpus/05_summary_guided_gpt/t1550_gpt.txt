Lately I feel like I’m standing in some weird in‑between place, like a hallway between the life I’ve lived so far and… whatever comes next. I keep feeling this pull back toward faith, but I honestly don’t know what it is I’m reaching for. Is it God? Is it just that I’m lonely? Or am I missing the structure, the community, and the certainty I had when I was younger and religion was just “the way things are”?

It’s confusing because I feel things so intensely and I do have a sense of myself, but at the same time I’m not sure who I really am underneath it all. I don’t know if this searching is going to bring me closer to something eternal, or if I’m just chasing a more polished reflection of myself.

I’m moving forward in this contradiction: craving certainty but somehow finding comfort in doubt, wanting answers but still clinging to my questions, stepping into the unknown with my eyes open and yet not really seeing where I’m going.
