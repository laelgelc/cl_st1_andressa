I feel invisible most days. Ignored, alone, like I’m just moving through life without really being here. I don’t have happy memories to look back on, no real friends, no family that feels like family. I’m not writing this because I expect help or answers. I just want there to be some record that I existed. I’m not even sure if I want to be saved or just finally rest. If you read this, thank you for seeing me, even for a moment.
