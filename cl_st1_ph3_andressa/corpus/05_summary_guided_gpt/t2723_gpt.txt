They basically ignored all of those ideas and kept only inviting me to the same late-night drinking plans.

Now they’re mad at me for not going out at night with them, and it feels like my individuality and needs don’t matter to them at all. I don’t want to change my personality just to fit in, especially when I’ve been clear about what I’m comfortable with.
