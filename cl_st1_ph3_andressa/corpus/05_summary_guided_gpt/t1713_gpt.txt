It hurts so much seeing people with long-term friends, group photos, regular hangouts, birthdays and holidays full of people who actually show up. I don’t have anyone like that. No one to text, no one who’d think to invite me. I keep wondering what’s so wrong with me that no one ever wanted to stay, that no one misses me when I’m not around. I’m scared that when I’m gone, I’ll just be forgotten, like I never mattered at all.
