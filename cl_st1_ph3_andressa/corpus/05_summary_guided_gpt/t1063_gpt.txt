I’ve never eaten alone in the dining hall before, and I’m thinking about trying it for the first time. Honestly, I’m terrified.

People here don’t really talk to me and I get the feeling they don’t like me. I’m convinced I’m physically unattractive – I literally think of myself as an ugly loser. The idea of walking in there alone and sitting by myself makes me feel sick.

I keep imagining everyone staring at me, thinking, “Wow, they have no friends.” When I look around, it feels like only “normal” people eat alone – like athletes grabbing a quick meal after practice, or people doing work on their laptops. They look busy, confident, like they have real lives. I feel like I’d just look sad and pathetic in comparison.

It makes me feel so uncomfortable and weird that I’ve avoided it completely. But I’m also tired of being scared of something as simple as eating a meal.
