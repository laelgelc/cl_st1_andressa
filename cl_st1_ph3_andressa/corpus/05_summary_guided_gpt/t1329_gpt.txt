I’m a student and I recently cut off my whole friend group, and now I don’t really know what to do with myself.

For a long time I felt like I was just… there. Invisible. They’d make plans without me, talk over me, and when I was going through a really rough time with my mental health and school, it felt like nobody cared. I used to come home after school and just cry from how lonely I felt, even though technically I “had friends.”

Eventually I couldn’t take it anymore and I cut them off. Now I think they’re mad at me, but I don’t think they’re actually sorry for how they treated me. It’s like I had to choose between two kinds of pain: being hurt while I was with them, or being hurt now that I’m on my own.

I have some classmates I talk to during school, but we don’t hang out or text outside of it. So once I get home, it’s just me and this draining feeling, like there’s a hole in my chest. I feel exhausted by it.

How do you cope with this kind of loneliness? How do you live with that empty feeling without it swallowing you?
