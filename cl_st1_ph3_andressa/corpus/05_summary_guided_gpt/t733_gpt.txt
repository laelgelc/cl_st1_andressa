I don’t really know where else to put this, or what I’m even looking for, but I just need to say it somewhere.

I’m living in Japan with no friends or family anywhere nearby. I’m going through a divorce I never wanted. I still love my wife, and I tried so hard to fix things between us, but it’s all just falling apart in front of me and I can’t stop it.

We have a four-month-old son. There’s an investigation going on because the authorities think my wife might have hurt him by shaking him. Just typing that makes me feel sick. I don’t even know what I’m supposed to feel – anger, fear, guilt, sadness – it’s all just a blur. On top of that, I’m terrified she’ll still somehow manage to take him away from me, even after all of this.

For a while, being with her made me feel less alone. I really thought I had finally found my place, my family. Now that it’s all collapsing, the loneliness is somehow worse than before I met her. It’s like I got a glimpse of the life I always wanted and now it’s being ripped away.

I always wanted a family. I never wanted my child to grow up without a parent, and now that’s exactly what’s happening. I feel like I’ve failed him before he’s even had a chance to live his life. I feel like I’ve lost everything in such a short time.

Honestly, I keep thinking I wish I’d never been born. If I didn’t exist, none of this mess would exist either. I know that’s a dark thought, but that’s where my head is right now.
