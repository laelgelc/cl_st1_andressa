It’s hitting me lately how much of my life I’ve spent chasing hobbies and even abusing substances just to feel like I belonged somewhere. I thought if I joined enough scenes, picked up enough interests, I’d finally have a solid group of friends. Now I’m sober, and it feels like all that’s left are memories—some really good, some that hurt to think about. One by one, everyone drifted away. I feel completely alone now, but the worst part is I’m scared to even try to make new friends again.
