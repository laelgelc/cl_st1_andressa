It hit me during a snowstorm, after crying all night, that I’ve been lonely for a long time and just refused to see it. I kept telling myself I was fine, burying everything under work and random side projects so I wouldn’t have to sit with that feeling.

Making friends feels impossible. People say it’s hard for them too, but they never really open up. When I try to connect or be honest, it’s like they slowly pull away, and I’m left feeling like I’ve been quietly pushed aside again.

I usually hate seeing myself as a victim, but admitting out loud that I’m lonely actually feels… good. Honest. Like I’m finally not lying to myself anymore.
