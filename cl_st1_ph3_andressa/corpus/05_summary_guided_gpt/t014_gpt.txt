I feel like something’s wrong with me. Listening to people talk about their lives is unbearably boring, almost physically painful. Their conversations feel so generic and fake that I start to feel this intense loathing and frustration, like I want to crawl out of my skin. Sometimes I honestly feel like I want to die during every interaction. But I’m still lonely. I want connection, yet I hate almost every social interaction I have. I don’t understand how both can be true.
