With everything going on with COVID-19, I keep seeing people talk about how lonely they feel staying home, not being able to see friends and family, and how hard the social distancing is for them. I totally understand that, but it’s made me wonder if anyone else feels kind of… okay in this situation?

I’ve grown really used to being alone over the years, to the point where I actually enjoy my solitude. Being by myself feels normal and even comfortable. Is anyone else experiencing something similar during all of this?
