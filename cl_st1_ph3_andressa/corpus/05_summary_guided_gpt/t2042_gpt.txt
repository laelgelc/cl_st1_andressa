I feel so overwhelmingly lonely all the time that it’s like a constant ache I can’t escape. I don’t even know what to do with myself anymore. Sometimes I just curl up and hug myself, pretending there’s someone there who cares, pretending I matter to someone. It’s pathetic, but it’s the only thing that brings even a tiny bit of comfort.

Deep down I don’t feel special at all. I feel like I’m just existing, not really living, like I’m not even real. I don’t want to be alone anymore. I just want to be okay, to feel like I’m a real person who matters to someone, anyone.
