I’m 19 and I feel like I’m completely alone in the world. I don’t even care if I never find romantic love at this point, I just want a best friend and a couple of people to actually hang out with. Someone I can message when something funny happens, or when I’m hurting, or when I do something small I’m proud of. Right now I have no one for any of that, and it hurts more than I can explain.

I’ve never really had a true friend. The people I thought were friends, or the people I went on dates with, always ended up using me or just discarding me when they were done. I feel like I’m only ever temporary to people.

My loneliness gets so intense that sometimes I don’t want to be here anymore, or I just wish I could disappear. I’ve been trying to change things for almost two years now, but nothing works. The last time I genuinely had friends was when I was about 17.
