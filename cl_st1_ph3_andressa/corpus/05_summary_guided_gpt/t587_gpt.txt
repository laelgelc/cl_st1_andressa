I don’t really know why I’m writing this here, maybe just because it feels like the only place I can say it out loud: I’m planning to end my life tonight.

My life has just been one long stretch of misery. My dad died of a drug overdose when I was a baby, so I never got to know him. My mom is an addict too. Instead of giving me up to someone who might’ve actually cared, she kept me and took everything out on me. She beat me constantly growing up. It was just normal to get hit for anything and everything. That went on until I was 16.

At 16 I started working, paying for my own tiny apartment and my schooling, and that’s basically the only reason the physical abuse stopped – I just wasn’t home enough to be her punching bag anymore. But it’s not like things got better. I’m 18 now and I’m in debt I never even chose. My mom used my name to get drugs, and now there are dangerous people after me for money I don’t have. They’ve already found me and beaten me up, and I’m constantly looking over my shoulder.

I don’t feel safe anywhere. I don’t really have anyone left. No friends, no family I can turn to. I feel like I’m just existing to suffer and pay for things I never did. I’m tired. I’m so tired.

All I ever wanted was something simple: a stable education, a safe home, a place where I could sleep without fear. It doesn’t sound like much when I write it out, but it’s always been completely out of reach for me.

If anyone reading this ever has children, please, please be responsible. Don’t bring a kid into the world just to neglect them or use them or hurt them. They don’t deserve that.

If you read this all the way through, thank you. This is the first time it’s felt like someone might actually be listening to me.
