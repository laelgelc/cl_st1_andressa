When I was a kid and teachers asked “What do you want to be when you grow up?”, everyone else seemed to have an answer. I never did. I figured it would come to me eventually, but it never really has.

Now I’m an adult and I still don’t know who I am or what I want. I don’t have a job, no real relationships, and I don’t feel like I have any place in this world. It’s like I’m just drifting, watching everyone else live real lives.

I feel so alone, confused, and disconnected. Does anyone else feel like they just don’t belong anywhere, like there’s no role or spot meant for them?
