I’m almost 70 and I don’t think I’ve ever felt popular or particularly liked. I’ve always genuinely liked people and tried to connect, but it feels like I somehow miss whatever “it” is that makes others gravitate toward you.

I do have a couple of friends, but they often criticize me. One in particular is pretty clearly narcissistic, and I’m aware of that, but I’ve kept the friendship anyway. It feels like part of a lifelong pattern of being the one who doesn’t quite fit, who’s tolerated more than wanted.

I’m kind, open, and fairly chatty. But afterward I replay conversations in my head and feel anxious, wondering what I said wrong. I think it’s because I’ve gotten so used to criticism over the years that I expect it.

I’m not in crisis and I’m not really looking for advice. I just wonder if anyone else has spent most of their life feeling generally unpopular or like they never quite fit in.
