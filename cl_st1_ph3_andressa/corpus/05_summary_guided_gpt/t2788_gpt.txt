I actually really like being alone. Walking around campus by myself or going to events solo is one of the few things that helps me relax, especially when my anxiety is bad. But it feels like every time I sit alone at an event, some staff member comes over and starts talking to me like I’m a problem to be fixed.

They’ll ask if I’m okay, or try to “include” me, and it just makes me more uncomfortable. I’m not antisocial, I’m not trying to get attention, and I’m not secretly hoping someone will come rescue me. I just genuinely enjoy being by myself. I wish people understood that choosing to be alone isn’t the same as being lonely.
