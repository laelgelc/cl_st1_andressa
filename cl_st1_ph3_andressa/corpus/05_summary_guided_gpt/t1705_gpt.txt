I’m 17 and a junior, and on paper it probably looks like I’m fine. I have people to talk to at school, I don’t sit alone at lunch, I can join a group in class and not feel totally out of place. But it all feels really surface-level.

Once the school day ends, everything just…stops. My phone is basically silent unless it’s about homework. I almost never get texts just to talk, or invites to hang out. I end up spending most of my time alone in my room, doomscrolling and feeling like life is happening somewhere else without me.

What gets to me most is feeling like I don’t have anyone I can really open up to or just exist with. I’m not sure if this is a normal thing people go through, or if I’m just complaining and should suck it up. Does anyone else feel like this?
