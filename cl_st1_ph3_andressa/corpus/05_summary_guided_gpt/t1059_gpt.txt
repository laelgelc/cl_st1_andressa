Last December my whole world flipped. One day we were talking about marriage, the next he just… vanished. No fight, no explanation, just silence. He blocked me everywhere and became completely unreachable. I still sent him a birthday present and tried to act like things were normal, but of course there was no response.

He had problems with alcohol and really needed therapy, and part of me keeps wondering if I should have done more, even though I know that’s not fair to myself.

What hurts most is this pattern: I love with everything I have. I’m loyal, patient, genuine. I give so much, and people only seem to realize my value after they’ve already left. Exes try to come back, but by then I’m just exhausted and alone again.
