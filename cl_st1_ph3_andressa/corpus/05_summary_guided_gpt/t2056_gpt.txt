It’s strange how my experience keeps swinging between two extremes. There are days when I feel totally fine on my own, absorbed in my own plans and projects. I’m content, almost peaceful, like solitude is exactly what I need and I don’t really care about having people around.

Then there are other days where it suddenly hits me how utterly alone I am. I’ll be surrounded by people, noise, life everywhere, and still feel like I have no one. In those moments, everything I’m working toward feels pointless, because there’s no one to share any of it with.
