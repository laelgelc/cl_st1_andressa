Five years ago I had to flee my home country for political reasons, change my identity, and start from zero. It was terrifying, but I adapted. I learned the language, built a life, became financially and psychologically stable. I even genuinely love this new country.

But now, at 38, I feel like I don’t really exist anywhere. My old identity is gone, my new one still feels like a costume. I’m stuck in between, disconnected from any clear sense of who I am.

On paper I’m positive, ambitious, intelligent, I speak five languages… yet I feel like I’ve forgotten how to make friends or meet someone. I don’t know the “rules” anymore. I feel invisible, like I’m watching life from the outside, and I don’t know how to step back in.
