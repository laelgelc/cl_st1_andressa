I don’t really know what to make of my friendships anymore. These are people I grew up with, people who say we’re close and call me “one of the homies,” but it doesn’t feel that way in reality. They don’t invite me when they hang out, they don’t call, they don’t message unless I reach out first.

I finally asked one of them why I wasn’t invited to something, and he just said he assumed I wouldn’t come. That really messed with my head. I never said I didn’t want to be there. It felt like they had already decided my answer for me, and used that as an excuse to just leave me out.

The worst part was when my dad had cancer and had to go through surgery. I was going through one of the hardest times in my life, and when I met up with this friend, he didn’t ask about my dad at all. He just talked about his new girlfriend, like nothing else in the world existed. After he got into that relationship, it was like I disappeared from his radar completely.

Now, even when I’m physically with them, I feel alone. It’s like I’m just there by myself, watching everyone else connect. I keep wondering if I’m just dumb for feeling this way, or if I’m being too sensitive. I don’t really have anyone to talk to about this. I just needed to put it somewhere and maybe have someone say something back.
