I’m so tired of being ghosted or immediately friend-zoned. The few people who actually show interest usually turn out to be fake profiles, scammers, or someone looking for financial help. On the rare occasion they’re real, they’re just not my type at all. 

It makes me feel like a joke for being open about how broken I am and how much I just want mutual love. I’m not chasing anyone’s money. I’m retired, financially stable, my home is paid off. I just want to share my life with someone genuine who isn’t shallow, someone who sees me as a person, not a wallet or an easy target. 

I’m starting to feel ridiculous for even hoping that exists.
