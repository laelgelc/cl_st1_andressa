I drink way too much and I know it, but it’s the only thing that makes the emptiness fade a little. I don’t have real friends, no family that actually cares, no partner. I honestly don’t think anyone has ever really loved me. I feel ugly, boring, like I’m just there for people to use as a joke or a prop to make themselves look better, even for their religious image.

I’m so tired of doing everything alone. Every day is just me, by myself, going through the motions. No purpose, no meaning, just one long struggle from the start. I feel hollow, like there’s nothing inside but self-hatred and regret.

Everyone else is celebrating and pretending things are hopeful, and I’m just here, stuck in this same miserable place. Happy new year, I guess.
