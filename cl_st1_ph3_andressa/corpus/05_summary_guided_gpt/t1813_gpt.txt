I live a pretty solitary life. Most days it’s just work, then coming home to movies and video games. It keeps me busy, but it doesn’t change the fact that I feel like I don’t have anyone I can really talk to.

I technically have friends, but it feels like they only reach out when they need something or when it’s absolutely necessary. When I get lonely, I feel this heavy, painful weight in my chest that’s hard to describe.

I’ve never dated. I struggle to hold or even start conversations, so I usually end up going places alone. Sometimes that solitude is peaceful, but it also hurts when I see people sharing moments together and I wish I had that too.

I’m not sure my story is worth anyone’s time, but I just needed to write it out somewhere.
