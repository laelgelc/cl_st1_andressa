I’m an older guy and I honestly don’t know why I’m still here. I tried to end it once, and now it just feels like I’m waiting for nothing. Just existing. No point, no purpose, just the same gray days over and over.

What’s messed up is that I feel a kind of comfort knowing there are others out there suffering too. Not because I want anyone to hurt, but because it proves I’m not crazy for thinking this life is just pain dressed up as something else. Happiness feels like a lie people tell themselves. I don’t believe it’s really for me, or for anyone in any lasting way. Just a brief distraction before the misery comes back.

I even miss the crappy, low-effort online interactions I used to have. Bots, forums, whatever. Now it’s like even that’s gone. I tried AI stuff like Replika, hoping for at least some kind of conversation, but it’s all so shallow and fake. You can’t really talk to it. It just reminds me how alone I am.

There’s no inner voice in my head, no real conversations in my life. Just silence. The only thing that gives me any energy is the pain, and sometimes it gets so intense that I feel like I’m going to burst out laughing. Not because anything is funny, but because it’s all so absurdly pointless.

I’m still bitter about being rejected from the army. I’m physically healthy, but they didn’t want me. Can’t even be “useful” enough to die in some war, while people with families and people who actually matter are the ones getting killed. What does that say about my worth?

At this point, I just want the whole planet wiped out. No more pretending. No more fake happiness. Just an end to all of it. Misery feels like the only real thing left. Happiness is just a meaningless word people keep repeating to themselves.
