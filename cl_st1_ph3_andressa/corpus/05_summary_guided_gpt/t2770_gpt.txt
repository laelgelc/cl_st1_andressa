I’m 16 and I don’t really see myself as a “loner,” but I still feel really alone most of the time. I usually don’t talk about my problems online, but I don’t really have anywhere else to put this.

About a year ago I went through depression, and even though I’m not in the exact same place now, it left a lot of scars. It’s like something inside me broke and never went back to normal. I constantly feel less than everyone around me. I don’t think I’m talented at anything, and whenever I see people my age being good at stuff it just makes me feel even more useless.

I’m also really insecure about how I look. I’m not particularly good-looking, I’m short (170 cm), not muscular, and it just feels like I don’t stand a chance. I’ve basically given up on the idea that I’ll ever have a girlfriend. Any time I think about girls, my brain just goes straight to “why would anyone pick me?”

I’ve used drugs and alcohol, and I used to be addicted. Now I don’t use them as much, but when I do it’s mostly to numb myself. I don’t exactly want to die, but I don’t really care what happens to me either.

The weird thing is, I actually like helping other people with their problems. I feel useful then. I just wish I could feel that way about myself.
