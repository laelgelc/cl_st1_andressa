I don’t even know where to start, but I just need to get this out somewhere.

From around 9–10 years old, school was hell for me. I was bullied constantly for my dark skin. People threw racist slurs at me, mocked the way I looked, even made fun of my father’s name. It went on for years, right up until Class 12. I started to hate school so much. Everyone else seemed to have their groups, their fun, their memories. For me it was just humiliation and trying to survive the day.

In Class 11 I changed schools, hoping it would be a fresh start. Instead, I felt even more out of place. I was singled out by teachers, never really understood why. I never fit into any friend group. I was always there, but never really part of anything. Just… on the outside, watching.

Since Class 12 (around 2020) until now, I basically haven’t had any friends. No one I can call, no one to text, no one who actually knows what’s going on in my head. I live with my family, but even then I feel completely alone. It’s like I’m physically present, but emotionally invisible.

In 2022 I went to Kota for NEET prep, stayed in a hostel, went to coaching classes. I thought maybe this would finally be a chance to connect with people. But I couldn’t. I didn’t make any friends there either. Whenever someone tried to approach me, I’d end up avoiding them, pulling back before anything could start. I don’t even fully understand why I do that anymore.

Now it’s 2026 and it feels like nothing has changed. No friends, no real conversations, just this constant feeling of being alone in every place I go.

That’s basically the story of my life.
