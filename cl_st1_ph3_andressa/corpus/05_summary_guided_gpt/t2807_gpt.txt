Just graduated and I feel like I’m supposed to be excited, but mostly I’m just… depressed and stuck. I’m on the East Coast, and everything here feels gray and heavy. Now that school is over and I actually have free time, my brain keeps drifting to California like it’s some kind of escape hatch.

I keep thinking about this “West Coast magic” I’ve built up in my head. I’m a huge Childish Gambino fan and his Pharos concert has been living rent-free in my mind for years. That whole vibe—surreal, warm, trippy, communal—makes me think maybe I could find something like that out there. Like maybe there’s a place where I don’t feel so out of sync with everyone.

I’m a loner by nature, but I’m tired of feeling alone. I picture myself in the sun, maybe smoking weed with people who get it, not even needing to talk much—just sharing a peaceful, hippie-like energy with others who are also kind of drifting. It sounds cheesy, but I want to reconnect with people without forcing it.

I don’t know if I’m chasing a fantasy or if I should actually go. I just know I don’t want to keep feeling like this.
