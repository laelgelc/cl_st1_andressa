I feel like an outsider everywhere I go. I’m always on the edges, just watching other people talk and laugh while I stand there, not really part of it. I want to join in, but my mind goes completely blank and I can’t think of anything to say. It’s like there’s a wall between me and everyone else. Sometimes I have brief moments where I feel okay, but they never last. I always end up back in this constant, heavy sadness and loneliness.
