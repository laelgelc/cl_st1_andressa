It’s hard to put into words how empty I feel most of the time. It’s not just being alone, it’s like there’s this hollow space where a sense of “me” should be. I always feel like I quietly fade out of people’s lives, like I’m forgettable background noise. There’s nothing special or important about me that anyone seems to notice or care about.

What hurts is that even when people I care about hurt me, I still love them. I keep taking it, because I don’t want them to feel this kind of loneliness. It feels like my role is to absorb the pain.

To anyone else feeling this way: I’m sending you love and strength.
