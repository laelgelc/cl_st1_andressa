Some nights hit harder than others. Tonight is one of those nights where I just want to cry, to be held, to be spoiled a little, to feel genuinely loved. Instead I’m lying here alone in this big dark room, and the silence feels heavier than usual.

I’m a man, and I’ve been sad and fighting this loneliness for a long time. Some nights I feel so weak, like I’m right on the edge of breaking. My mind even mocks me with this song that keeps looping in my head, like it’s rubbing it in.

But I keep telling myself I’m strong. I won’t give up. I’ll keep moving forward and keep dreaming, until I find love or die trying. I’m still thankful for what I do have… I just wish it didn’t hurt this much.
