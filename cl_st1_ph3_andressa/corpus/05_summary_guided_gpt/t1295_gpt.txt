New Year’s is coming up and I just feel miserable. My grandparents are out of town, my only close friend is in the province, and I’m stuck here alone with way too much time to think.

I keep replaying all the dumb choices I made. I left my old friends, distanced myself from people who actually cared, and pushed away a girlfriend who was really supportive because I was confused about my own feelings. Now she’s happy with someone else, and I’m just… here. Stuck. I only have a few people I really trust and none of them are around to actually go out or do anything with.

I don’t even understand my own emotions half the time, and when I try to express them, it comes out wrong or not at all. It feels like that’s the pattern: I ruin good things because I can’t handle what I feel or say what I mean.

The gym used to be my escape, the one place where I could forget everything for a while. But I completely fell off this December and didn’t go at all. Now I just feel even worse.

I keep telling myself I’ll “lock in” next year and leave all this behind in 2025. I want to believe that. But right now, everything just feels really heavy and I don’t know what to do with myself.
