I’m in college and I feel like I’ve hit this weird point where I just don’t enjoy being around people anymore. I’ve always valued being alone, but it used to feel peaceful. Now it just feels empty. When I’m with others, I struggle to make small talk, I never know what to say, and I’m pretty sure I come off as unapproachable. It’s like there’s this wall around me that I don’t know how to take down.

I keep asking myself what the point of any of this is. I feel unhappy and kind of hollow most of the time. I don’t really see a purpose in my life right now. I know that sounds dramatic, but it’s the only way I can describe it.

The thing is, I don’t see myself as a bad person. I know I’m caring, and I like helping people when I can. I honestly believe that if anyone actually gave me a chance, I could relate to them and connect. But my past experiences have been so negative that I’ve started to see other people as the “bad guys.” I’ve stopped giving people opportunities to know me because it feels like every time I try, it just ends in disappointment or hurt.

On top of that, I have intense facial rosacea. It’s bad enough that I feel like I look like some kind of monster or alien. People stare, make comments, laugh, call me names. I get ridiculed for something I can’t control. It feels like I’m constantly being judged only by my appearance, like no one even cares to look past my skin and see who I actually am.

All of this has made me want to just give up on trying to form any kind of relationship. It feels pointless when everyone only seems to see the surface and not the person underneath. I’m tired of being treated like a joke or a freak. I’m tired of feeling like no one cares about who I am inside. I don’t know how to keep going like this or how to change anything.
