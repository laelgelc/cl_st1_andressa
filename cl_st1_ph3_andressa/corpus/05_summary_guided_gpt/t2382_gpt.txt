I’m 33 and I feel like I’ve always been on the outside looking in. I’ve never had a real friend group, never had a girlfriend, never really felt like I belonged anywhere. Growing up I was bullied a lot, and it’s like that never really left me. Now I barely leave the house. 

I have really bad social anxiety and even simple interactions feel terrifying. I keep expecting people to reject me or make fun of me for being different, so I avoid them altogether. But then the loneliness just gets worse. It’s like I’m stuck in this loop: I’m alone because I’m scared of people, and I’m scared of people because I’ve been alone and bullied for so long. 

I’m just exhausted by feeling this lonely all the time.
