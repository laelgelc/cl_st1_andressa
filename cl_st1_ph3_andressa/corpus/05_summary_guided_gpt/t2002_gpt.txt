Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m not a real person, just some kind of bundle of processes and reflexes pretending to be human. I watch other people do normal human things so naturally, and it’s like I’m on the outside looking in. I worry people can tell I’m different, or worse, they don’t see me at all. In relationships I feel like I’m only ever a “snack,” never the “real meal.” I keep wondering if I just never learned how to be human growing up.
