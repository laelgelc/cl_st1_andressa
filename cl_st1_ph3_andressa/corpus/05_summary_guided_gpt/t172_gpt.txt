I’m 18 and moved to a new city about a month ago to start university, and I feel like I’ve been dropped on a different planet.

Most of the time I’m either in lectures, in my room, or just walking around campus pretending to be busy so I don’t feel like such an outsider.

Another thing that makes it weird is that in my program I’m almost the only guy. It just makes me feel even more out of place, like I don’t fit the “vibe” at all. Everyone seems to already have their own little groups and inside jokes, and I’m on the outside looking in.

On top of the loneliness, I keep thinking about how an 18-year-old is “supposed” to live. Parties, relationships, sex, all that. I’m still a virgin, and it makes me feel like I’m behind everyone else, like I’ve somehow messed up already. I know logically there’s no rule about when things should happen, but it still gets to me. It’s like I’m watching everyone else live this “normal youth experience” while I’m stuck in my head.

I’ve thought about talking to a psychologist, but I honestly don’t know what they could do. I’m not sure if what I’m feeling is serious enough, or if they’d just tell me things I already know. At the same time, this loneliness and feeling of being behind is starting to really bother me.

I guess I’m just looking for advice or support from people who’ve gone through something similar. How did you deal with feeling this isolated and “behind” at this age?
