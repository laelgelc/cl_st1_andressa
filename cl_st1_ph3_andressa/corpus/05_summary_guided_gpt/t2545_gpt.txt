I feel like loneliness has been following me since childhood, and lately it’s getting heavier. I grew up in a small village in a very intelligent but overprotective family. Because of that, I didn’t have many chances to meet other children, and when I did, I never really knew how to connect. I was always the “weird” one. People my age bullied me and made me feel like there was something wrong with me, and even now I still rarely see anyone my age.

I have hobbies like drawing, and I spend a lot of time on the internet, but even there I feel like I don’t belong. I scroll through places where others seem to find their people, but I always end up feeling invisible, like I’m standing just outside the window looking in.

What hurts the most is the feeling that no one is interested in my inner world. People seem to only see my outward appearance, their first impression, and that’s it. No one asks about what I think, what I dream about, or the books and movies that mean so much to me. It makes me feel like a stranger in this world, like I was born in the wrong place or time.

I’m scared that this is how my life will always be. Not just being single, but being alone in a deeper way, without anyone who really understands me. I keep wishing for just one sincere companion, someone who also feels deeply alone and would want to share that inner world together.

Right now I feel lost and I don’t know what to do or where to look for this person. I wonder if there is any way to make this dream real, or if it’s just something I’ll always be hoping for.
