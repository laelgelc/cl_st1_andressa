I don’t even know where to start anymore. I feel like my whole life has been one long reminder that I don’t belong anywhere.

I’m ADHD and autistic, and I’ve always been the “loud” one. I really do try to treat people with respect, to be kind, to smile even when I feel like crap inside. But it never seems to matter. Any girl I like either calls me “like a brother” or laughs at me behind my back. I’ve been bullied by people who called themselves my friends, and even by teachers. One teacher almost got me a felony over something tiny. It’s like I’m always the one who’s “too much,” “too weird,” or just… wrong.

I never fit in, even in places where I should, like with people who share my music taste. I lost everything from my childhood in a hurricane—every physical reminder of who I used to be—but I helped save people during that storm. I did what I thought was right. I try so hard to do good, but it feels like the universe just spits in my face. Things don’t get better; they just get heavier.

Getting denied from the army after five brutal months of dealing with useless recruiters was another punch in the gut. I pinned so much hope on that, and they just shut the door in my face.

Now I’m scared to even leave my house. I’m terrified of being laughed at again. I used to be able to talk to anyone, and now I can barely look my own family in the eye. My dreams feel pointless. Life is just grey. I even graduated a year early, and that only made me happy for like two weeks before everything went back to feeling empty.

I barely talk to my family. I have one online friend. No one I can actually call. I feel exhausted all the time, like I’m stuck in this endless abyss. I keep hoping someone will pull me out, or that I’ll somehow pull myself out, but it just… never happens.
