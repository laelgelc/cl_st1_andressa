Lately it feels like I’ve hit some new level of loneliness I didn’t even know existed. It’s come on really suddenly in the last few weeks and it’s honestly scaring me. I feel this desperate need to be seen and to matter to someone, but whenever I meet new people it already feels… too late somehow. Like the chance to be important to them has already passed, and that hurts more than I expected.

I do have friends, but being around them doesn’t really help. I still feel just as alone, just as empty, even when I’m technically “not alone.” It’s starting to mess with my ability to focus in class because my mind keeps looping on how isolated I feel.

I keep wondering if the problem is just me. I’m socially awkward and talking to new people is hard, but I still feel like I should be able to be “somebody” in someone’s life. It feels like the good parts of life rarely show up for me. I also feel kind of young to even be posting this here, but I don’t know where else to say it.
