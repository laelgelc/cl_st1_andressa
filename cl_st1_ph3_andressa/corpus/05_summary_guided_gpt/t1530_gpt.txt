I’m 26 and I feel like I’m just… existing, not really living. My days are on repeat: college, gym, work, football, home. On paper it looks normal, but it all feels empty, like I’m just going through the motions because that’s what I’m supposed to do.

I talk to people at college, at work, and at football, but I don’t feel like I actually have friends. It’s more like they talk to me when I’m there, but no one really talks *with* me. I always feel like the third wheel, or the extra person in the group who could vanish and nothing would change.

Even in group settings, surrounded by people, I feel weirdly alone and disconnected. Everyone else seems to naturally fit in, while I’m just kind of… there. I can’t shake the feeling that if I disappeared for a while, the only ones who would really notice are my immediate family. It honestly feels like the world would just keep spinning without me, and I’m not sure what to do with that thought.
