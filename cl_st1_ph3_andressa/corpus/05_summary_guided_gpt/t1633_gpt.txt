I’m hosting a free online support circle on grief this Thursday, December 12 at 7:30 pm (scheduled to avoid work and weekend conflicts).  

We’ll start with some simple group guidelines: things like confidentiality, respect, and no interruptions while someone is sharing. After that, we’ll take turns sharing our struggles, thoughts, and experiences with grief—whatever feels right for each person.  

The goal is to create a space where people can feel less alone, connect with others who’ve gone through similar things, and maybe open up in ways that aren’t always possible in everyday life. I also want to gently push back against the taboo around talking about mental health and grief.
