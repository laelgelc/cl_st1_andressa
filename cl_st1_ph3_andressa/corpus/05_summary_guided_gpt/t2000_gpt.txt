Is anyone else in this weird middle space?

I’m not introverted, I actually like people and I’m interested in meeting new ones. But I don’t have those long-term high school or college friendships everyone seems to have. Between being busy, moving a lot, and dealing with personal stuff through my teens and 20s, I never built anything that lasted.

I’m not talking about people who don’t want friends, and I’m not really looking for romance. I’m looking for others like me: social, open, but somehow starting from scratch.

Where do people like us even exist? Are you all hiding in online games with chat turned off or something? And if you don’t currently go out, where *would* you actually want to go socially, if you had the option?
