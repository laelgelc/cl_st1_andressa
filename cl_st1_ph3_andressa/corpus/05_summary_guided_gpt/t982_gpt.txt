I’m 22, in the US, and it feels like my life got fast‑forwarded into some nightmare I never asked for. My parents divorced years ago and somewhere along the way they just… stopped being parents to me and my little sister.

I’m working three jobs. I had to quit college to keep the lights on and food in the fridge. I feel so tired all the time, like I’m already burnt out before my life even started. I love my sister more than anything; she’s honestly the only reason I keep pushing. She’s all I need, and I’d do anything for her.

What kills me is that both of our parents have new families now. New kids, new houses, new everything. And still, when I ask for even a little help—especially for my sister—they say no. Every time. It’s like we’re disposable.

I hate them for abandoning us. I know that’s a strong word, but I do. I just want someone to hear this and tell me I’m not crazy for feeling this way.
