I finally worked up the courage to ask a girl out for drinks and she actually said yes. The catch is she’s only free next Saturday, so now I’ve got this whole week to sit with my thoughts. Part of me is really excited, but a bigger part is convinced she’ll cancel at the last minute or show up and decide she’s not into me.

The past six months have been full of rejections, so it’s hard not to expect the same pattern again. I’m trying to stay positive and just look forward to it, but my brain keeps preparing for the worst.

I’ve already decided that if she bails or rejects me, I’m going to go buy three lottery tickets. It’s kind of my way of joking with myself that maybe another rejection will somehow turn into something good, even if it’s just a silly superstition to soften the blow.
