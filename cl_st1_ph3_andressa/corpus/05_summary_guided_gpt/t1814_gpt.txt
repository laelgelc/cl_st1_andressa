I hit this point recently where it just clicked: no one is coming to save me. The people I thought would be there either left or never showed up in the first place. So I’ve been trying to build my own happiness from scratch.

I’m working on loving myself, taking care of my body, improving my skills, trying to push my career forward, and doing what I can for my family. I keep telling myself that if I don’t do it, no one else will.

The weird part is, I’m actually starting to see some worth in myself and in my life. But there’s still this empty space where other people should be. No one to share it with, no one to celebrate anything with.

So I just keep going, picking myself up over and over. Everything’s “great” on my own, I guess.
