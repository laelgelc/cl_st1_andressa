Long weekend alone and my brain is just… loud. Heavy, destructive thoughts circling nonstop. I keep catching myself wishing there was at least one person I could really talk to, someone who’d actually listen instead of just hearing words and moving on.

It feels like I don’t have anyone to turn to. People drift in and out of my life, but it never goes deep. They see some version of me, but not the real me. I don’t even know if I’m letting them see it, or if they just never stick around long enough to notice.

Today I didn’t even have the energy to go out. Just stayed in my head, stuck. I do have some plans for tomorrow that might distract me a bit, but it feels more like delaying the thoughts than actually changing anything.

Lately it feels like I’m not really living. I’m just surviving, dragging myself through each day, each week, each year. Existing instead of actually being alive.
