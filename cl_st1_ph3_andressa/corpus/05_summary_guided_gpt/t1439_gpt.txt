I’m 21 and for as long as I can remember I’ve had this feeling that I don’t want to live past 30. It used to be more of a vague thought in the background, but since my ex died it’s become so much stronger and scarier. I still thought of him as my boyfriend. I loved him so much, even though our relationship really messed me up and left me with a lot of emotional damage and self-esteem issues that I still carry.

His family was really supportive at first, but now they’re upset with me and somehow I’ve become this person in the middle of their conflicts. They come to me to vent or expect me to help fix things between them, but no one seems to think, “Maybe I should ask how she’s doing.” It feels like I’m just here to absorb everyone else’s pain and drama.

My friends don’t really reach out unless I text first. If I stop talking, everything just goes quiet. I don’t have a car, a job, money, or a place of my own. I feel like I have nothing going for me and no real future to look forward to.

On top of that, my body feels like it’s falling apart. I’m exhausted all the time, my physical health is a mess, and emotionally I’m all over the place. I have BPD and sometimes I can manage it okay, but other times it completely runs my life. My relationship with my family is strained too, so I don’t really feel like I have a safe place anywhere.

I can’t eat properly, I can’t sleep, I can’t relax or enjoy the things I used to like. I’m trying to cope, but I keep wondering what the point of any of this is. I feel so alone, and it honestly scares me how alone I am.
