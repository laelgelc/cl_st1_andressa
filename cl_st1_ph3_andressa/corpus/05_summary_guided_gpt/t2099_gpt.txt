I’m 24 and I feel like my life is already over in all the ways that matter.

My close friendships have faded. My family has basically fallen apart. The only relationship I’ve ever had was with a narcissist, and that completely broke something in me. For a while I really believed I was loved and special, and then it all turned into this nightmare that made me question my worth and my judgment. Now I don’t even know what being loved in a healthy way is supposed to feel like.

I feel like an outsider everywhere. I don’t fit into any group, no matter how hard I try. It seems like other people just… slide into friendships, relationships, communities. Their lives fall into place naturally. Meanwhile I’m forcing everything, putting myself out there, even using apps to find friends, and nothing sticks. It’s like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me that people can sense.

I’m stuck in regret. I keep wishing I’d been stronger, more confident, more loving toward myself earlier in life. I feel like I missed all these milestones everyone else seems to have: the solid friend group, the fun young-adult years, the supportive family, the “first love” that isn’t traumatic.

Seeing happy couples, groups of friends laughing together, or students hanging out on campus hits me like a punch in the stomach. I picture the life I wish I had and it’s almost physically painful. I don’t know how to move forward when I feel like I’ve already failed at the basic parts of being human.
