I know a few sociopaths and sometimes I honestly envy them. They don’t seem to crave human connection, they’re not devastated when people leave, and they can stay emotionally detached when others are hurting. Meanwhile I feel everything so intensely. I get attached, I miss people, I hurt when they’re gone. Sometimes I wish I could just flip a switch and turn off my empathy and need for relationships. I keep wondering if being alone would feel easier that way.
