I’m just so tired of all the “soft rejections.” The people who never actually say no, they just… stop replying, or keep things vague, or let plans die slowly. I try really hard to be upfront about how I feel, and I even give people an easy way to decline so they don’t have to feel bad. And still, I get met with silence. It feels like being slowly pushed out of a room without anyone ever looking me in the eye.

It’s making me wary of trying at all. Every time I think about opening up or asking someone to hang out, I remember all the times I’ve been left on read or gently ghosted. I end up feeling awkward, like there’s something fundamentally off about me that I can’t see or fix.

I wish I had some reason to improve that wasn’t just “for myself.” Some sort of proof that if I worked on myself, there would be someone there who actually wanted me around. Right now it just feels like I’m shouting into a void.

My best friend’s wedding really drove it home. Sitting at a table surrounded by friends and their partners, watching them touch each other casually, lean in close, share little looks… and I just sat there, painfully aware of how alone and touch-starved I am.

I don’t really need comments. I just needed to put this somewhere.
