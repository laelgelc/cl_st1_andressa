I feel like I’m stuck in this loop where every time I try to connect with people online, it ends the same way: they eventually ghost me. No drama, no explanation, just silence. After a while it’s hard not to see a pattern and wonder if this is just how my life is going to be.

I’ve tried going back to old hobbies and building new habits, trying to fill the emptiness with something productive. But my health issues keep getting in the way, and I can’t do things as consistently as I want. I keep telling myself that maybe when my health improves, I’ll have a better shot at things feeling different.

It really hurts seeing others having real conversations, meaningful interactions, while I stay on the outside looking in. Lately I’m thinking it might be better to stop seeking connection altogether and just accept that I’m going to be lonely. This is going to be my last post here. I’ll try not to post or even think about loneliness anymore.
