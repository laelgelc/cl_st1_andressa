I feel like I’m disappearing in slow motion and no one even blinks.

I don’t have anyone I can actually talk to when I’m at my lowest. Not even my twin. My mom doesn’t call anymore. My “best friend” has over 30 unread messages from me, including one where I literally said “I love you,” and… nothing. It’s like I don’t exist.

I’ve tried so hard to be someone people would want around. I became the one who cooks for everyone, who makes these really good meals and bakes and hosts. People say they love my food, they show up for that. But no one sees that I’m struggling with anorexia underneath it all. I can feed everyone but myself, and it feels like no one notices or cares to look deeper.

On paper I have a wide social circle, but everyone is wrapped up in their partners, kids, families. I’m just… extra. Living in this remote village makes it worse. It genuinely feels like I could move away, or just vanish, and it wouldn’t really matter to anyone.
