I’ve been thinking a lot about loneliness and how much of it might actually be within our control. For me, what’s helped most hasn’t been “finding the right people,” but becoming more valuable to others by improving both my technical skills and my social skills. As I’ve done that, my own loneliness has eased a bit.

Now I want to try something with others who are willing to focus on self-improvement first, and only then worry about social validation. My idea is to work one-on-one with people here to:

- Figure out one high-impact goal (professional or personal)  
- Break it down into a realistic plan and actually execute on it  
- In parallel, explore what kinds of social situations might fit you, and how you present yourself in them  

In return for helping, I’m hoping to learn what kind of product or system I should eventually build to support more people like us.

If this resonates with you and you’re willing to put in effort, send me a message and we can talk about whether this might be useful for you.
