I’m 29, but I don’t feel 29 at all. Physically I’m disabled and I can’t drive, so even basic “adult” stuff like getting a job or just going places on my own feels almost impossible. I’ve missed out on so many normal experiences that people my age seem to have by default. 

Emotionally and socially I feel more like a young teenager. I struggle with social cues and I keep replaying awkward moments in my head. Like with my friend and her husband – I’ll say or do something that feels fine to me in the moment, and then later I realize how weird or out of place it probably seemed. I don’t always know how to act “appropriately” for my age.

I can’t really be friends with actual teenagers, because obviously that age gap is not okay, but when I look at people my own age they have jobs, homes, kids, bills, responsibilities… a whole life I just don’t relate to. I feel stuck in this strange in-between where I don’t fit anywhere.

It’s starting to feel like I’m dead inside, just watching life happen to everyone else. I’m so lonely and I don’t know how to bridge this gap between who I am and who I’m “supposed” to be by now.
