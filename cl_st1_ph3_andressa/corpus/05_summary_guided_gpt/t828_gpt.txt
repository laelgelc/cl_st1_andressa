I’m 21, non-binary and queer, stuck in Ohio for now but hoping to move to upstate NY. I feel like a walking contradiction: “goth nature spirit” with “mega autism,” anxiety, and depression. The mental health stuff is mostly managed, but I’m still incredibly socially awkward and rarely interact with people offline.

Most of my life revolves around animals and being outside. I’m obsessed with basically everything non-human: exotic pets, reptiles, birds, fish, insects, wildlife rehab, random animal facts. I love foraging, fishing, and collecting bones and skulls. Vulture culture, little art/craft projects, jewelry, fursuit work, and messing with clothes are my main “creative” things, but I go through phases with them.

I don’t really fit into typical social spaces. I don’t go to concerts, conventions, bars, or clubs. I’m not much of a gamer either. I like metal and rock, and a lot of music from the 1930s–1980s, but I just listen alone.

I’m lonely and unsure how someone like me is supposed to find friends or community when I barely know how to talk to people in the first place.
