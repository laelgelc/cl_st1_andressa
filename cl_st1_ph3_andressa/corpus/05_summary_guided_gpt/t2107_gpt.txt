My mom died of cancer last September, and since then I’ve felt lonelier than I ever imagined. I feel like I have to be the strong one for my dad and my sister, the one who holds everything together. At work and in life, it feels like every battle is mine alone. I’m always the one people lean on, the one who listens and supports, but I don’t feel like anyone is really there for me. I knew loneliness would come someday and thought I was preparing myself, but I never realized it would hurt this much.
