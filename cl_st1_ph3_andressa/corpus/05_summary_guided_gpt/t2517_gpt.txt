I’m 23 today and I honestly wish it was just another random day on the calendar. When I was a kid I used to get so excited about my birthday, counting down the days, imagining people caring, celebrating. Now it’s just this huge reminder that no one really gives a damn that I exist.

No one remembered. Not my parents, not my siblings, not even random acquaintances. Not a text, not a “hey, how are you doing today?” Just silence. I’ve spent most of the day with this heavy feeling in my chest, trying not to cry, and I know I’ll probably end up crying myself to sleep again.

I didn’t think I’d still be alive at 23, and somehow that doesn’t feel like an achievement. It just feels like more time to be disappointed. My only “goal” for the rest of the year is to stay alive and find distractions. I feel so alone and hopeless.
