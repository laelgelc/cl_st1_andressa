I’ve been a loner for as long as I can remember. I only ever had one truly good friend, and when they ended the friendship it felt like the last real connection I had was gone. Since then, I don’t really remember having many other friends, and now I spend most of my time alone.

I’m trying to embrace this loneliness and accept myself as I am. I just want to say: every one of us is special, a flower waiting to blossom, even if it doesn’t feel that way yet.
