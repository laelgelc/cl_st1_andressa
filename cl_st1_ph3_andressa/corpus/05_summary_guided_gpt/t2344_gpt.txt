I feel so alone in a house full of people who say they love me. I do everything for my partner and kids, I take care of the house, the bills, everyone’s needs. I stay in shape, other women compliment me, but I still feel ugly and invisible where it matters most. I know my partner loves me, but I don’t feel desired. I miss simple things like a hug when I come home. It feels like my only purpose is to provide, not to be wanted.
