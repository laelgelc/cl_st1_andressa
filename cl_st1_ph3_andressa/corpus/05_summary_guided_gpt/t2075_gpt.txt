I’m 21 and it feels like I’ve lived several lives already, none of them good. My parents were deep into drugs, my brother is dead, my sister’s in prison because she unknowingly laced our cousin. I bounced around foster homes until I ended up with a family friend. I’m grateful to have a roof over my head, but it doesn’t feel like I have real parents or a real family.

All the friends I used to have are gone because of moving so much. The one person I really loved turned out not to care the way I thought she did, and that completely shattered whatever belief I had in love. I’m fit, I guess I look okay, but I just feel more and more indifferent to meeting people.

I don’t know if love is even real anymore. I feel nihilistic, like nothing matters and I’m not built for love anyway. I have no one I can really pour my heart out to, so I’m just dumping it here.
