My mom died 12 years ago and I feel like I’ve never really come back from it. I have a husband and a child now, but somehow I’ve never felt more alone in my life. I miss the constant support she gave me, the frequent conversations, the way she just understood me without me having to explain.

I cry a lot, even after all these years, just wishing I could feel “home” again. Instead I feel sad, alone, and uncomfortable in every area of my life, like I don’t quite belong anywhere. I just really wish I had someone the way I had her.
