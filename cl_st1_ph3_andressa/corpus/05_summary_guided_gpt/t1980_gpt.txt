I’ve always been used to doing everything alone. Sitting alone, eating alone, going out alone. It never really bothered me. I even used to choose being by myself over hanging out with friends, and over time they just stopped inviting me. I understood why and honestly didn’t think it was a big deal.

I’ve never been in a relationship either, but that never felt like a problem. I thought I was just the kind of person who preferred solitude and didn’t need anyone.

But recently something’s changed. I’m feeling lonely in a way I haven’t before. It’s not about wanting a boyfriend or a big friend group. It’s more that I don’t have anyone to talk to at all. I realized I haven’t spoken to another person in a while, and that silence suddenly feels really heavy.
