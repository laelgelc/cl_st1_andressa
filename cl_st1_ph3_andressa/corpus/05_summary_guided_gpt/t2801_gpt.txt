I’ve never really gone out of my way to make friends, but somehow I still ended up with a few good ones. I’m generally more comfortable being on my own. As a kid I was shy and mostly attached to family friends, and no one really pushed me to go out, socialize, or build my own circle, so I stayed pretty quiet. I just want to say that not all loners are angry or bitter. Some of us are genuinely content with our own company.
