I graduated recently with a CS degree and now I’m self‑employed, working from home. On paper it sounds kind of ideal, but in reality it just means I’m alone with my thoughts most of the time. I’m neurodivergent and making new connections feels terrifying and confusing, like everyone else got a rulebook I never saw.

I still have a few high school friends I see maybe once a week and I know I’m lucky to have them, but after my breakup things feel different. My ex was my best friend, even though the relationship wasn’t healthy. Losing that constant presence made everything feel so much emptier, like the background noise in my life just stopped.

I post a lot of NSFW stuff online, but honestly, I think I just want to feel close to people in any way I can. Even small, random moments—someone saying hi on the street or asking me for directions—can make my whole day. It just feels like making real friends as an adult is so much harder, and I don’t really know where to start.
