Most days I honestly feel okay. I chose this life, this path, and I still believe in not settling just because I’m scared of being alone. I can usually laugh things off, stay lighthearted, and not take everything so seriously.

But every now and then it just hits me like a truck: I don’t actually have anyone. No close friends, no one who really knows me, no one to share the small, stupid daily stuff with. I’ll be going about my day and suddenly it’s like a curtain lifts and I see how empty my social life really is.

People used to tell me I’d always find “my people,” that I’d never really be alone. It’s weird and a bit painful to realize my life has gone in the opposite direction. I usually just push these thoughts down and move on, but today it’s really getting to me.

I guess I just need someone to tell me that it’s not too late, that things can still change, and that I’m not completely broken for ending up like this.
