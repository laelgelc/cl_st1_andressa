I matched with this guy on a dating app where we both said we were just looking for friends. We’ve been talking for about a month now. I feel really safe with him, we have similar interests like fitness, and I catch myself thinking about him a lot.

The problem is, I’m terrified of getting attached. Every time I start to like someone, it eventually turns into emotional pain and endless overthinking. I imagine little moments with him, like working out together or just hanging out, and then I feel this weird hurt because it’s all in my head. I keep worrying he’ll lose interest or just leave one day.

The worst part is that my life is actually pretty full. I have hobbies, I work out, I go to school, I cook, I watch YouTube. But even when I’m busy, my mind goes back to him. I wish I could just be content and happy alone, without this constant anxiety about attachment hanging over me.
