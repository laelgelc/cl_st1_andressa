Eventually I get treated like I’m some kind of pest. I’ve been called ugly, a “rat,” and other things I don’t even want to repeat. Sometimes it’s been from people I thought were my friends, sometimes from complete strangers. It’s gotten to the point where just thinking about social interaction makes me feel physically sick.

Now I’m scared to leave the house, scared to be seen. I switched to online school thinking it would help, but I’m too afraid to turn on my camera or talk in class. I just sit there silently, trying to be invisible.

It’s hard not to believe that I really am unworthy of any kind of relationship, friendship, or anything. So I avoid people, both offline and online, because I’m terrified of being rejected or hated again.

Even something as simple as posting a nice comment online scares me, because I’m always expecting someone to snap at me or mock me.
