Sometimes I look back and feel like I just… missed all the “normal” milestones. I spent my teens and early 20s buried in my interests, schoolwork, and TV. I had crushes, but I never did anything about them. Even during my gap years, when I supposedly had more time, I still never managed to find a girlfriend.

The closest I’ve had to a “date” was going to senior prom with a friend, and I’m not even sure that really counts. I’m just not a social person, and it feels like that’s the whole explanation for why I’m still alone.

What hurts is seeing other shy, quiet people somehow end up in relationships. It makes me feel like there’s something uniquely wrong with me. I honestly don’t know what “miracle” would have to happen for me to ever experience a first love.
