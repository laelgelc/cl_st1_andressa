I’ve started to feel like I’m some kind of ghost. People don’t hear me when I talk, or they talk right over me. I get bumped into a lot, like I’m not even there. I’m really quiet and calm, never the person who stands out in a crowd, and it’s gotten to the point where people actually call me “ghosted.”  

I’ve kind of leaned into it: I can slip into lectures without anyone noticing, and sometimes I joke that I could probably steal from a store and no one would see. But it still feels weirdly lonely.
