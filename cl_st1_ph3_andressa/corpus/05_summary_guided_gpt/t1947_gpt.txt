I don’t really know what to do with myself anymore. I feel so lonely and it’s turning into this constant frustration that’s slowly becoming anger at everyone, including myself. I technically “have friends,” but most of the time they just feel like acquaintances I happen to hang out with sometimes, not people who actually care about me.

There’s one friend group that really gets to me. When I message them, they either don’t respond at all or they give these super short, bare-minimum replies. It makes me feel like I’m annoying them just by existing. I start thinking, “If they cared, wouldn’t they at least try?” Being ignored like that makes me feel small and stupid, like I’m forcing my way into a conversation no one wants me in.

My other friends aren’t much better in a different way. I feel like I have to drag them into doing anything, like I’m always the one pushing for plans or trying to keep things going. It’s exhausting. And one of them sometimes makes me uncomfortable, though it can also be funny at times, which is confusing. I don’t know how I’m “supposed” to feel about that.

I keep turning the blame back on myself. I tell myself I’m overreacting, that I’m too sensitive, that maybe I’m just not that important. I wonder if I talk too quietly, or if I’m just boring, or if I’m actually a terrible person and this is why people act like this around me. It’s like I can’t trust my own feelings.

I feel stuck. I don’t want to cut these people off because then I’d have almost no one. But staying with them like this makes me miserable. I always thought friends were supposed to make you happy, not make you cry or feel like you’re begging for scraps of attention. I don’t know if I’m just being too emotional or if this is actually as bad as it feels.
