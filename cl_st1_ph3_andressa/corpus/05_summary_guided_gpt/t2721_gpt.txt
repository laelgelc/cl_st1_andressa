I used to be somewhat social when I was younger. I had friends in school, hung out with them, did the usual stuff. But somewhere in my teens, through high school and then college, I just… pulled back.

It wasn’t like some dramatic event happened. I just slowly stopped wanting to socialize. Now, most people in my life feel more like acquaintances. I’ll occasionally keep in touch with old friends, but it’s rare and usually pretty surface-level.

What’s strange is that I’m not even sure why I became quieter. It just kind of happened over time. And now I’ve grown used to it. I actually prefer being alone. Solitude feels more comfortable and freeing than being around people, like I can finally breathe.

I’m not sure if that’s something I should be worried about or just accept as who I am now.
