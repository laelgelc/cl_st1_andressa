I’m 24 and lately I’ve been feeling this heavy mix of loneliness and… I guess “unaccomplished” is the best word. From the outside, my life probably looks fine. I work, I get through my days, I’m not in total crisis or anything. But inside, it feels like I’m just stuck in place while everyone else is moving forward.

People around me seem to be building careers, relationships, confidence, like they all got some memo about how to be an adult that I somehow missed. I don’t really feel proud of anything I’ve done. I’m not even sure if I’m actually building a life or just distracting myself while time quietly passes.

I’m not looking for pity, I just wanted to be honest somewhere. If anyone else feels this way, you’re not the only one. And for those who’ve been through something similar and come out the other side, how did you get through it? What actually helped, even in small ways?
