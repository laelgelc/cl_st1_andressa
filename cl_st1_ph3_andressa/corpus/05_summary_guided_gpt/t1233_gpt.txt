I feel like I’ve been lonely my entire life. Not just “alone sometimes,” but completely unwanted. It’s like I’ve never been chosen, by anyone, for anything.

As a kid and a teenager, I didn’t socialize. I felt strange, out of place, like there was some invisible sign on me that said “stay away.” I went through school barely talking to anyone. In PE, when they picked teams, I’d be standing there, ready and willing to play, and they still wouldn’t pick me. I’d end up left over, like an object no one wanted. That feeling never really left.

It’s not just people. It feels like it’s everything. Animals, romantic interests, anyone I try to connect with — there’s this same pattern of rejection. It’s like others see something in me that I can’t see, something that makes them despise me. I’ve started to believe they must be right, because the pattern is so constant.

I feel useless and rejected, like my existence doesn’t matter. I’m convinced that when I die, no one will care. That thought doesn’t even scare me anymore; it just feels like a fact.

All of this has turned into this intense hatred toward everyone and everything. I’m disgusted with life, with people, with myself. I can’t even stand my family. I honestly believe there is nothing in this world for me, and I’m just so tired. I just want it all to end.
