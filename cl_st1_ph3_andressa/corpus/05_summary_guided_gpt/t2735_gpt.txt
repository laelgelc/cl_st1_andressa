I have this meeting coming up at an acting school, and it honestly feels like everything is riding on it. I want to be a film/TV actor so badly, but I keep wondering if I even have that “it factor” people talk about. I know I’m hardworking and diligent, I’ve always been pretty independent, and I’ve held down a job at a bakery, but this feels completely different and way more personal.

I’ve always felt kind of different, like an outcast. Even now I only really have a couple of close friends. Acting and music are the only things that make me forget that feeling for a while. I don’t like being alone, so I throw myself into creative stuff, trying to make people feel something, anything.

It scares me that this dream feels like all I have. I’m afraid of failing and ending up back in that lonely place with nothing to hold onto.
