I feel like I’ve been lonely for as long as I can remember. I’ve never had a girlfriend, and I’ve only ever had a few friends, but none of them were really close. I finished college and now I’m back at home, mostly just sitting around with my laptop and gaming. That’s basically my life right now.

I keep trying to make friends or find a girlfriend online, but nothing ever lasts more than a week or two. It feels like as soon as people see my picture, they lose interest and ghost me. I honestly think it’s because I’m ugly. I have no jawline and this “potato” face that I blame on breathing issues and not getting braces when I should have. On top of that, I feel like I can’t lose weight no matter what I do, like my metabolism is just messed up.

I’ve kind of come to the conclusion that I’ll probably never get a girlfriend. But my mind and my heart won’t let it go. I still crave love and connection so badly. I want someone who doesn’t care about looks, someone I can laugh with, hold hands with, and actually share a life with. I want that more than anything.

I keep trying, I keep putting myself out there, and I keep getting disappointed. Every time it happens, it just reinforces this belief that I’m going to be forever single and never loved. Deep down, I really do believe that’s my future. And yet, I still can’t fully give up on the idea of love, even though it hurts so much to keep hoping.
