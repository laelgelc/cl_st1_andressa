I’m 21 and this year I’ve basically watched my whole social life fall apart. I lost four close friends in what feels like no time, and now my world is pretty much just my relationship of four years. I love my boyfriend and I’m grateful for him, but it’s scary realizing he’s basically my only real source of companionship.

I’m introverted and it’s always been hard for me to make friends, but lately it feels impossible. My self-esteem is really low, and I get so anxious talking to new people. I feel embarrassed about who I am and what I’ve (not) achieved. When people my age talk about their lives, it’s jobs, internships, school, money, moving out, traveling… and I just feel like I have nothing to add.

I know logically a lot of people in my generation are struggling with jobs and money, but I still feel behind. I just got hired as a server and start on Tuesday, and I’m already stressed out of my mind about money. My $200 credit card is maxed out and even though that’s not a huge amount, it feels like a mountain to me. I’m worried people my age would think I’m pathetic if they knew how much I stress over something so small.

I also feel like I don’t look or talk like other people my age. Like there’s some unspoken “normal 21-year-old” way of being that I just don’t have. It makes it really hard to connect. But when I talk to the friends I still have or my boyfriend, everything feels natural and easy. That just leaves me wondering what’s wrong with me, and why I can’t seem to be that person around anyone new.
