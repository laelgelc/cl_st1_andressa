It feels like my life is stuck in this awful loop. I’m introverted and don’t have many chances to meet people, so it’s rare that I find someone I connect with easily. But every few years, someone shows up who I grow really attached to or even fall in love with. And every single time, there’s some kind of obstacle that makes anything close or meaningful impossible.

Sometimes it’s circumstances, sometimes it’s my own morals telling me I have to back off and let the connection fade. I keep doing what feels like the “right” thing, and I end up alone again.

This has been happening for about a decade now. It honestly feels like I’m being sabotaged by design. I’m so tired of this pattern and I don’t know how to break out of it.
