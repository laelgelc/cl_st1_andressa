Lately I’ve been wondering if I’m actually depressed, because nothing feels enjoyable anymore. Everything just kind of sucks. Even when I want to do something nice for myself, money stuff like taxes and insurance keeps getting in the way, and it feels like I can’t ever really get ahead or buy the things I want.

Socially, it’s a mess too. Every few years it’s like I hit a reset button and suddenly I have no friends again. I’m actually an extrovert, I need people to talk to, but I grew up without siblings and I’m used to being alone in a way that doesn’t feel healthy.

So I just disappear into video games, art, and music. They’re the only things that feel even slightly like an escape from all of this.
