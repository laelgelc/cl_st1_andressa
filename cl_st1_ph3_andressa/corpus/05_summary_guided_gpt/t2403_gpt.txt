I’m 20 and honestly I feel like my life is going nowhere. I’ve got this crappy once-a-week job that barely pays anything, I have no friends, no social life, no relationship, and every time I try to look for other work it just goes nowhere. Most days I just feel lonely, depressed, and kind of hopeless about the future.

The only thing that really keeps me going is GTA V. Specifically director mode. I’ll just load in, turn off wanted levels, mess with the weather and time of day, and drive around for hours or go on these insane rampages. It’s weird how immersive it still feels after all this time, like I can disappear into that world and forget how empty mine is.

I know some people would say I should “face my problems” or that I’m wasting my life, but honestly I don’t care. Right now GTA V is the only thing that makes me feel anything close to okay. It’s crazy how good the game still looks after like 10–11 years. I just hope GTA 6 ends up being just as immersive and successful, maybe even bigger, because I don’t know what I’d do without something like this to escape into.
