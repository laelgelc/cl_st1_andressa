I’m starting to really believe that nobody actually cares about me. My family puts on this whole act in front of other people, like they’re so loving and supportive, but once the doors are closed it’s a completely different story. They call me a failure, pick apart everything about how I act, and somehow twist everything so that I’m always the problem. It’s like they’re constantly gaslighting me into thinking I’m wrong for just existing the way I do.

Outside of them, there’s just…nothing. I’ve never had real friends, no matter how hard I try. I genuinely listen to people, remember what they tell me, try to be kind and interested in their lives, but it never turns into anything. People drift away, or keep me at arm’s length. No one ever seems to choose me.

I’m so tired of being nobody to everyone. I just want something so basic: someone who remembers me without me reminding them I exist. Someone who actually wants to spend time with me, who’s happy to see me instead of just tolerating me. I feel like I’m missing all these “normal” experiences everyone else seems to get by default: having friends on social media, going to a movie with someone, getting a “good morning” text, having a person to sit with at lunch. It’s like I’m watching life from behind a glass wall.

It makes me wonder if there’s something fundamentally wrong with me, like I’m inherently terrible or unlikable and I just can’t see it. Friendship looks so effortless for other people, like they just breathe and it happens, and for me it feels impossible no matter what I do.

Lately I just feel more and more empty and lonely. I don’t know how much longer I can keep pretending it doesn’t bother me. It does. It hurts all the time.
