I struggle with loneliness too, and one of the few things that’s actually helped me is volunteering. It gets me out of the house and around different kinds of people, so even if I don’t walk away with instant best friends, I’m not alone and I feel like I’m doing something that matters.

Places like animal shelters, food pantries (even the ones run by churches, even if you’re not religious), or hospitals usually need extra hands. A lot of them let you apply online or just call and ask.

I live in a different state from where I’m originally from, and volunteering has really helped take the edge off the loneliness.
