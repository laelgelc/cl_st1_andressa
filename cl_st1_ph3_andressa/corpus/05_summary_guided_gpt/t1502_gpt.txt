I don’t even know what I’m looking for by writing this. I just feel like I’m screaming into a void and no one hears me.

I technically have “friends,” but every time I try to talk about what’s going on with me, they get annoyed or brush it off. I’m always there for them, always listening to their problems, trying to support them, but the second I open my mouth about how I feel, it’s like I’m too much. It makes me wonder if I’m just a bad friend, or if there’s something fundamentally wrong with me. I feel boring, like there’s nothing about me that makes people want to stick around. Making and keeping friends feels impossible.

My family might as well be strangers. I feel no connection to them at all. I’m pretty sure they hate me because I’m gay. I end up calling myself a fag in my head before they can, like I’m beating them to the punch. It’s easier to hate myself first than wait for them to do it.

Lately I’ve been having more and more suicidal thoughts. I’ve started drinking too, not a lot yet, but it’s creeping in as this way to numb everything for a while. I keep thinking I just want one person who actually loves me, listens to me, and supports me. But then I hate myself for wanting that so badly, like I’m pathetic for needing anyone.

Sometimes I catch myself wondering why I’m not “depressed enough” to just go through with it, and then I feel even more disgusted with myself. I don’t know. I just feel so alone.
