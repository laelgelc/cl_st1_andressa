I don’t really have anyone in my life I can say this to, so I’m dumping it here.

I’m under 25 and I feel like my life is just… shitty. I’m stuck in this low-paying job with long hours, and every day feels the same. I’m constantly sad because it doesn’t feel like I’m building anything. I don’t even know what I want to do, I just know this can’t be it. It feels like I’m wasting my time and my life, and I don’t see a clear way out.

What makes it worse is when I compare myself to people I used to know. Old classmates, people I used to talk to a bit – they’re in university, or they’ve got better jobs, or they just seem to be moving forward somehow. Meanwhile I’m here, stuck, tired, and empty. It makes me feel like I already failed at life before it even really started.

The other day I saw an old school friend on the train. He still looked happy, like life was going well for him. It hit me harder than I expected. It brought back all these memories of how I used to be: shy, closed off, never forming real relationships. I think I hurt people without meaning to, just because I was naive and apathetic and didn’t know how to connect.

I’ve tried to improve myself since then, but somehow I ended up completely alone. No real friends, no one to talk to about any of this. I just want someone I can talk to and smile with again, like I used to imagine things could be. Right now it feels like I’m invisible and left behind.
