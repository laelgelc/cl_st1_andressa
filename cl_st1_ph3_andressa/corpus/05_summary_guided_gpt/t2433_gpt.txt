My therapist recently told me that I don’t actually have any real, external proof that I’m unlovable or ugly—that those beliefs are coming from my own thoughts, not from anything other people have said or done. It kind of shook me. I realized I might be one of those people whose inner struggles are basically invisible to everyone else. On one hand, that’s a little comforting. On the other hand, it really bothers me that I look “normal” from the outside while feeling so completely different on the inside.
