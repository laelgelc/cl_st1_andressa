It’s strange living in a time where we’re supposedly more connected than ever, yet so many of us feel completely alone. I keep thinking of it as a kind of “pandemic of loneliness” that quietly affects billions of people, but most of the world just shrugs it off or pretends it isn’t there.

I’ve been through my own stretches of loneliness and what I can only describe as mental hell, and I didn’t really have anyone to lean on. That experience is what pushed me to create this subreddit.

If you’re dealing with loneliness in any form, you’re welcome here. You don’t have to sugarcoat anything. Use this space to reach out, ask for help, or just vent and get things off your chest.
