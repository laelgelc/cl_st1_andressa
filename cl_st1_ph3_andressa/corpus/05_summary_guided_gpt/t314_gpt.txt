Lately I’ve been thinking that some of us walk alone not because we’re empty, but because we see too much. The more I notice the layers in people, intentions, moments, pain, and beauty, the harder it is to stay in the usual noise. It’s like awareness has a cost: you quietly step away from what no longer feels real. So if you’re alone, maybe you’re not behind. Maybe you’re ahead in some strange way, just standing still and really looking. That’s how it feels to me, anyway.
