I don’t even know how to explain this without feeling dramatic, but I feel so intensely lonely even though I’m not technically “alone.” I talk to people every day, I’m around others, I do the normal life stuff. But it feels like I’m moving through everything as a ghost.

It’s not one big trauma, it’s a thousand tiny cuts. Being talked over, not really listened to. Being forgotten about unless someone needs something. Feeling like my needs are inconvenient, like my feelings are “too much” or an overreaction. People say they care, but I feel more tolerated than chosen, like I’m just… there.

On the outside I probably look fine, functional. Inside, I feel hollow and exhausted from carrying this quietly. It scares me how easy it is to imagine just disappearing into a life where no one ever really knows me, and no one even notices.

Has anyone else ever felt fundamentally unseen like this? How did you find your way back to feeling real again, like you actually exist in other people’s minds and hearts?
