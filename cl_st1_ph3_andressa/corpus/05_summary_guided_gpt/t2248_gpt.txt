I feel like my existence is harmful to everyone around me. I honestly believe I’m a bad person who only ever hurts people, even when I don’t mean to. It’s like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me, and just by being here I make things worse.

I haven’t had any contact with anyone for three years. I still go to university, but I’m completely alone there too. My classmates seem to hate me for being silent and boring and unable to communicate. I can feel how uncomfortable my presence makes people. No one likes me, no one tolerates me. I don’t fit anywhere.

I don’t even know what to do with myself anymore. Writing this feels like I’m betraying myself, like I don’t deserve even this small attempt to reach out. More than anything, I just want to be erased. I wish every trace of me could be deleted, and that even my parents could forget I ever existed. I just want to disappear from everyone’s memory.
