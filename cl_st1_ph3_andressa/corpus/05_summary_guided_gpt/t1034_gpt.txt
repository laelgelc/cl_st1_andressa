Lately I’ve been thinking about how strange it is that I can have tons of “friends,” likes, and views online, and still feel like I have almost no one I could really lean on if something truly bad happened. On paper I look “social” and “connected,” but in reality I feel lonelier and more isolated than ever.

I even made a video about the hidden cost of having a lot of followers: you get attention, but not necessarily support. People will watch, comment, and scroll on, but who would actually show up if you needed them?

It’s made me question what connection even means anymore.

Does anyone else feel this way? How many people could you realistically call at 3 AM if you were in crisis—and be sure they’d actually pick up and care?
