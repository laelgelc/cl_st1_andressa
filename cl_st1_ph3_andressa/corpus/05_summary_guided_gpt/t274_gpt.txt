Woke up feeling really off today and it just hit me again how out of place I feel most of the time. I’ve always been kind of eccentric and I don’t get along easily with others. I’m starting to wonder if I might be autistic. I’m super sensitive to noise, and in conversations I can barely hear my own voice unless I practically shout. I crave silence. I don’t like being seen, don’t like trusting people. My favorite part of any social interaction is the end—saying a quick, cheerful goodbye and escaping outside to the open air.
