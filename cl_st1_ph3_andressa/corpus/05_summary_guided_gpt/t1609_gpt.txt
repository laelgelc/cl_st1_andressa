I’ve reached a point where I honestly don’t care about finding a girlfriend or romance anymore. I’ve saved money, I look after my health, and I can still travel and have little adventures when I want. But emotionally, animals fill the void way better than humans ever did. I respect them as individuals in a way I can’t seem to respect people. I feel like I’m meant to stay in the shadows, just quietly observing life and waiting to die, patiently.
