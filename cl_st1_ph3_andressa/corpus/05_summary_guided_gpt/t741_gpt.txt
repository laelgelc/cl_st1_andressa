I feel completely and literally alone. I’m not exaggerating when I say I have no one. No friends, no family, no partner, no coworkers I’m close to. There is nobody who really knows me, nobody I could put down as a next of kin, nobody who would even notice if I disappeared.

I don’t know how to meet people or where to even start. It feels like I somehow missed all the normal ways people build connections, and now I’m just… outside of everything. The idea of trying to reach out is terrifying, and honestly, it took everything in me just to write this post.

On top of that, I’m struggling financially and barely keeping myself from becoming homeless. It all feels like walking on a very thin edge with no safety net.

If you’re reading this and you have even one person, or some stability in your life, please be grateful. Some of us really do have nothing at all.
