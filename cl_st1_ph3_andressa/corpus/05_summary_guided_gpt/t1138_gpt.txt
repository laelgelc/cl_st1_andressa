Tonight I heard the married couple who live in the same house as me having sex. Not just the sounds, but the way they talked and laughed with each other afterward. It hit me how genuinely happy and loving their relationship seems. They have this whole world together that I’ve never even come close to.

Meanwhile I’m sitting here struggling with a job application I’m pretty sure will get rejected. I can barely get through the questions without feeling like a complete failure. It feels like I’m stuck watching life happen for other people while I just…exist on the sidelines.

I’ve never had a partner. Never had sex. At this point I honestly believe I probably never will. It’s like that part of life is for other people, not for someone like me. All I have is my plushie to hold at night, which makes me feel even more pathetic sometimes, even though it’s the only thing that brings me any comfort.

After hearing them tonight, I just lay in bed and cried. I felt so small and unwanted and left behind. I don’t know how to stop feeling this way.
