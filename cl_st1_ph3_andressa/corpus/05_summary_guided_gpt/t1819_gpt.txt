I’m so tired of feeling like this. I’m scared that this is just how my life is going to be forever – lonely, invisible, easy to ignore. I do technically have friends, but whenever I try to open up and say I’m not okay, their responses feel so shallow. A quick “that sucks” or a joke to change the subject, and I just shut down again. It makes me feel like I have to deal with everything alone.

I’m always the one who has to reach out first. If I don’t text or call, it’s just… silence. It makes me feel like I only exist when I initiate contact, like I disappear the moment I stop putting in the effort. I wish, just once, someone would check in on me without me having to ask for it.

The second half of last year felt relatively stable, but since the start of 2026 everything has crashed. I wake up feeling heavy, go to sleep feeling heavy, and the quiet in between feels suffocating. I keep asking myself what’s wrong with me, what makes me so easy to overlook. I can feel myself spiraling, and I’m honestly afraid that this loneliness is just going to swallow me whole if something doesn’t change.
