I keep circling around this same longing that I can’t seem to shake: I want that one deep, mutual bond where we’re each other’s “person.” The kind of connection where you know each other better than anyone else does, where you naturally turn to each other first in every high and low, and you both quietly prioritize each other without having to ask.

I don’t know if I’m asking for too much or if this is a normal, reasonable thing to want. It just feels so far away from my reality that it almost seems like a fantasy. Every time I’m reminded I don’t have it, it makes everything else in life feel a little heavier and more pointless.

I’m aware I might be putting too much of my mental well-being on something I ultimately can’t control. But knowing that doesn’t make the ache go away. I just want to be someone to someone, in that deep, unquestioned way.
