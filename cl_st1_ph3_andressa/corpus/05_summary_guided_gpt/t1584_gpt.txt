I’m turning 29 soon and I’m honestly dreading it.

For the last four birthdays I was away from my family because of the military. I always told myself, “It’ll be different once I’m out. I’ll finally get to celebrate with people I care about.” But I’m out now, and it still feels like no one wants to do anything with me.

Last year was my first birthday as a civilian and I spent it completely alone. My (now ex) wife had left me for someone she met while I was deployed, just a few weeks before my birthday. I had planned this really nice birthday for her earlier in the year, put a lot of thought into it, and then when it was my turn I just… sat in an empty place, trying not to think about it.

At the time I was so focused on the breakup and the divorce that I kind of numbed myself to the fact that I was spending my birthday alone. Now that the divorce is behind me, that loneliness is hitting a lot harder. It feels like this date on the calendar is just a reminder of how replaceable and forgettable I am.

I deleted all my old posts and comments because I don’t want anyone I know to recognize me. I probably won’t respond to replies because that makes me anxious. I mostly just needed to put this somewhere instead of letting it bounce around in my head.
