Thirteen years ago, around my birthday, I brought him home as a present to myself. I was in a really dark place back then—depressed, lonely, stuck in addiction, having thoughts I don’t even like to admit. He gave me something to wake up for, a reason to come home. He was my friend when I didn’t have any, my comfort when everything felt like it was falling apart.

He was there for everything: traveling, getting married, losing close friends, losing my son, and eventually getting divorced. No matter what happened, he was just… there. Steady. Loving. My little anchor.

Eight months ago, I had to say goodbye to him. Now my birthday is a week away, and I feel like I’m right back where I started—alone, empty, drifting. I miss his “smile” so much it physically hurts.

Sorry if this is all over the place. I just needed to let it out somewhere.
