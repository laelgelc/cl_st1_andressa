I don’t really know where else to put this, but I feel like I’m reaching my limit.

I have no friends. I’ve never been in a relationship. For a long time I told myself I liked being alone, that I preferred it this way, that I didn’t need anyone. Lately that just feels like a lie I used to survive. Most nights I end up crying myself to sleep because the loneliness feels so heavy.

My family moves a lot, so every time I start to get close to someone at school, we move and I have to start over. By the time I get to a new place, it feels like everyone already has their own friend groups and they’re not interested in adding anyone new. I’m always the outsider who came too late.

I honestly don’t think anyone has ever really liked me. I feel completely invisible and uninteresting. People call me “weird,” and I know there are jokes and laughs behind my back. It hurts more than I can explain. It makes me feel like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me that I can’t fix.

I’m so tired of pretending it doesn’t bother me, of acting like I’m fine with being on my own when I’m not. The pain of all this is getting really hard to carry, and I don’t know how much longer I can keep going like this. I just needed to say it somewhere.
