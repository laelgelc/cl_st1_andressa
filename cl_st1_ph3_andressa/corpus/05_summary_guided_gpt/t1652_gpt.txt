This is my first time posting on here, and honestly I’m just exhausted. Exhausted with life in general and with living where I am. I feel like I’m just drifting through days without any real purpose. People who used to care about me seem to have faded away, and I don’t know when that happened or why.

In every relationship or friendship I’ve had, I’m always the one putting in all the effort. I’m the one reaching out, replying quickly, trying to make plans, investing my time and energy. It feels like I’m constantly pouring into other people, and no one ever really pours back into me.

What I want feels so simple but completely out of reach: someone who genuinely cares. Someone who actually messages me good morning and good night because they’re thinking of me. Someone who calls me just to hear my voice, who invites me to hang out without me having to beg for their time. Someone who wants to hug me, cuddle with me, just be close.

I feel so attention-, affection-, and touch-deprived that it hurts. Because of my trauma, it’s hard for me to even feel or believe love when it’s there, and that makes everything even more confusing. Seeing other people get the affection I’m desperate for feels like a punch in the chest every time. I don’t know how much longer I can keep going like this.
