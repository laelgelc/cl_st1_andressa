I’m 20 and I feel like I’ve been stuck in the same dark place for 3–4 years now. The depression just never really went away. I still have all these negative thoughts, I feel emotionally numb most of the time, and even my physical health has gotten worse. I’m tired all the time, and it feels like my body is just as exhausted as my mind.

What hurts the most is how alone I feel in all of this. My family has never really given me the care or support I needed. It’s like my feelings don’t matter, like I’m just… there. My best friends, the people I thought would always be there, abandoned me when I was at my lowest. Now I don’t really have any good friends left. It feels like everyone just slowly drifted away when I needed them the most.

I’m in a relationship, but even that makes me feel lonely. My boyfriend always seems to prioritize his friends over me. I feel taken for granted, like I’m just an option. He keeps disappointing me, even on days that should matter, like my birthday. He doesn’t really understand me, especially when it comes to how I feel about his friendships and how they make me feel so unimportant.

I don’t know how I’m supposed to respond to his behavior anymore. Part of me wants to leave, but another part is scared of being even more alone. I just wish I had someone who truly understood me and actually cared, someone who wouldn’t make me feel like I’m asking for too much just by wanting to be loved and prioritized.
