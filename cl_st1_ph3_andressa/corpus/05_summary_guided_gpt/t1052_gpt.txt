I’m 20 and I feel like my life is already stuck in some kind of loop I can’t get out of. It feels like things are either standing still or slowly getting worse, and I’m just watching it happen from the backseat.

I have a really hard time forming new bonds. I don’t know if it’s me, my situation, or both, but it feels like I only ever lose people. The few connections I’ve had just kind of fade or fall apart, and I don’t seem to gain anything new to replace them. Every time it happens, it just reinforces this feeling that I’m going to end up alone.

I’m still heavily dependent on my parents for pretty much everything: money, transport, and just general day-to-day stuff. The economy is terrible, jobs are hard to get, and public transport where I live is awful, so it’s not like I can easily just go out, be independent, or even meet people. College is eating up most of my early twenties, and instead of feeling like I’m building something, it feels like I’m just watching my youth get consumed by stress, classes, and commuting.

On top of that, my mom is extremely overprotective. She makes relationships even harder. She has this mindset that no one is good enough for me, that anyone I get close to will hurt me or hold me back. It’s like every potential friendship or relationship is already judged and rejected before it even starts. I feel like I’m constantly being told that the world is dangerous and people are bad for me, and it just makes me feel more trapped.

I feel powerless, like I’m just a passenger in my own life, being driven along a path I didn’t choose. I keep imagining myself at 40, completely alone, wondering where my youth went and why I never actually lived it. I’m scared and frustrated because I honestly don’t see a clear way out of this. I don’t know how to change anything when everything feels so out of my control.
