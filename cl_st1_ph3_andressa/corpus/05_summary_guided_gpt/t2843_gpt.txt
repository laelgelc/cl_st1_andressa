Two years ago I moved back home after uni. My old school friends all went off in different directions – some had kids, some moved away, one joined the navy. My uni friends scattered too, or we just slowly stopped talking. It wasn’t like any big falling out, they just… disappeared from my life.

Since then I’ve basically spent my weekends at home with my parents. Recently I started going to bars alone, thinking maybe I’d meet people that way, but it honestly just makes me feel worse. I sit there watching groups of friends laughing, couples on dates, and I’m reminded of everything I’m missing.

My coworkers are all a lot older, with families and totally different lives, so I don’t really see them as potential friends. I’ve tried Tinder and similar apps but nothing has come of it.

I feel really stuck and don’t know what I’m supposed to do next.
