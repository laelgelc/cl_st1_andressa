Lately it feels like something in me just switched off. Life’s become this empty, repetitive loop. I don’t get excited about new experiences, meeting people, or even watching shows anymore. Sometimes it feels like I’ve lost the will to live, like I’m just going through the motions.

I try the usual stuff – gym, self‑improvement, “working on myself” – and it helps for a moment, but the loneliness always comes back. I feel stuck constantly chasing money, health, friends, relationships… and I can’t imagine a whole future that looks like this.

I don’t really feel safe with anyone or genuinely cared for. It always feels like people are talking to me because they want something. And whenever I finally start to feel comfortable with someone, something happens and the bond just breaks.

I don’t know if I’m not being true to myself, or if I’m just completely exhausted from pretending all the time.
