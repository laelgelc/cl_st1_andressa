Sometimes I feel like talking to people online just makes everything worse. It’s like a reminder that I don’t have anyone here, in real life, to actually sit with, talk to, spend time with, or even hug. I miss that kind of closeness that seems to only exist when you’re a teenager, hanging out after school or on weekends. At my age it feels like that kind of friendship doesn’t exist anymore.

So I keep thinking the only way to get that now is to find a boyfriend. But I’ve never dated, I don’t know what to expect, and yet it feels like there’s no other option. Deep down I have some idea how it might go, but it feels so hopeless that I just try not to think about it.
