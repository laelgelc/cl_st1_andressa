To anyone out there hurting right now, feeling lost, stuck in regret or haunted by painful memories – I see you. I’m there too.

I know what it’s like to replay the past over and over, to feel like you’re drowning in what-ifs and should-haves. It can feel endless and so isolating. But please, try to hold on to the good memories too, even if they feel small or far away. They’re proof that not everything has been pain.

Be gentle with yourself. You did the best you could with what you knew then, just like you’re doing your best now. Self-compassion isn’t weakness; it’s survival.

Even if it doesn’t feel like it, you are loved by someone. Maybe you can’t see them right now, maybe you don’t believe it, but your existence matters more than you realize.

Please stay. Stay strong. You’re not alone in this.
