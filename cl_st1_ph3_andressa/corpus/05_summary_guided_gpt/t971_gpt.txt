I’ve been feeling really alone lately and realized a lot of us here probably feel the same way, with no one to talk to about everyday stuff or how we’re really doing. So I’m making a small Discord server for people from this subreddit who just want a quiet, kind place to exist together.

It’s meant to be low-pressure: you don’t have to be super active, and even if you only ever connect with one person, that’s already a win. The focus is on respect, openness, and support. Hate speech, political propaganda, and insults won’t be tolerated at all. This is not a place for drama or arguments, just somewhere to be honest and be heard.

If you’re interested, send me a DM and I’ll share the invite link. I hope some of us can find a bit of comfort, maybe a few new connections, and someone to talk to about daily life.
