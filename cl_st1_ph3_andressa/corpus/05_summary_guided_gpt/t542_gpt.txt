I’m 26 and lately I feel like nobody takes me seriously, not even my own family. People only seem to come to me when they need something, then disappear once they get it. My “friends” literally make plans in front of me and don’t invite me, like I’m just background noise or the circus joker there to be laughed at.

For the past year I’ve been struggling to socialize. It feels like I’m slowly becoming invisible. To cope, I’ve fallen into drinking and smoking way more than I should, and now those feel like addictions I can’t shake.

All of this has messed up my ability to trust. I’m constantly suspicious of everyone’s intentions, always waiting for the moment they’ll use me or ignore me again. I keep wondering if the problem is actually me. Am I the one who’s wrong here?
