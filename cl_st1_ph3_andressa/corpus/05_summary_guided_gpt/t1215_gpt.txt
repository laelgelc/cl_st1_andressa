I’m 19M and I feel like I’ve been trying to connect with people for almost a decade now, and I’m still just… alone. I keep hearing that so many young adults are lonely, but it doesn’t feel like anyone actually wants to help each other out of it. Everyone says “put yourself out there,” but I honestly feel like I already do. I try to talk to people, I try to be friendly, I try to show up.

Yet I still feel left out, ignored, like I’m always on the outside looking in. I don’t even know what else I’m supposed to do anymore. I don’t know what groups or clubs to join, or if it would even matter. Sometimes I wonder if people even want real connection at all.
