It’s my birthday today and I’ve spent most of it crying.

I’m an immigrant, so my family is far away. I don’t have close friends nearby, and I feel this huge emptiness today more than usual. Some people did celebrate with me and gave me gifts, but it all felt so forced and obligatory, like they were doing it because they had to, not because they really wanted to. Instead of feeling happy, it just made me feel like my existence is a burden they’re tolerating.

A lot of my close friends forgot to wish me at all. I know people are busy and we’re all getting older, but it still hurts. It feels like everyone is slowly drifting away and I’m left behind, dealing with depression, breakups, and homesickness on my own. I keep thinking about how different it would be if my family were here, if I could just be around them and not feel so alone on a day that’s supposed to be special.

I’ve been crying so much that my body actually hurts. There’s this physical ache in my chest and head. At the same time, I do feel genuinely grateful to the few people who did care in a real way. But even then, I start wondering if I’m exaggerating everything, if I’m being dramatic.

I’m scared to tell my friends how I really feel because I worry they’ll think I’m too sensitive or needy and just pull away even more. The fear of being abandoned for being honest about my feelings makes me stay quiet, and then I just end up alone with all of this inside my head.

I just really miss having my family around.
