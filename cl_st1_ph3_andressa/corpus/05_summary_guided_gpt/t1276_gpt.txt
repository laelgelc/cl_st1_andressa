New Year’s Eve was supposed to be nice this year. We were meant to host my in‑laws, I’d already cooked a lot of food because NYE is a big deal in my Eastern European culture. Instead, a small argument with my partner spiraled into a full-blown fight. We’re in the same house but it feels like we’re in different worlds. They don’t want to resolve anything, just keep fighting, and I’m exhausted.

I don’t have any friends or family to turn to, so it’s just me, the food, and this heavy silence. This is my first New Year’s Eve completely alone, and it really hurts. If you’re reading this, I hope your night is kinder than mine.
