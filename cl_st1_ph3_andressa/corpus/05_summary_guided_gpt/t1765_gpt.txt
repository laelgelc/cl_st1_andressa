I’m 27 and I feel like I’ve been lonely my entire life. I’ve never really “fit” anywhere or been anyone’s first choice. I’ve worked hard to learn how to talk to people, how to be social, and I can sometimes make friends, but it never lasts. People always drift away, and I’m left wondering what I did wrong.

Lately it’s been really getting to me. I feel like I’m always on the outside looking in. I’m not thought of, not invited, not included. It hurts seeing other people make plans together and realizing no one even considered asking me. It makes me question what’s so off-putting about me that people don’t want me around.

I even tried to be direct with my current friend group. I asked if I’d done something wrong or if there was a reason they don’t seem to like hanging out with me. All I got were vague “we’re just busy” answers. But I know that’s not true, because I see them making plans and texting each other without me, sometimes right in front of me.

I’m so frustrated and confused. I feel hurt and honestly a bit desperate to understand why this keeps happening. Why does no one seem to want me? What is so wrong with me that people always leave?
