I feel completely alone. There’s no one I can hug, no one to love, no one I can honestly say is “mine” in that mutual, safe way. It’s like I was given this deep ability to feel, but most of what I feel is pain, emptiness, and all the bad emotions.

I keep imagining one loyal, caring person who would actually stay. Someone who’d notice when my mood shifts, who’d look at me and ask, “What’s wrong?” and really mean it. Someone who’d tell me I’m not alone and make me believe it.

Right now, having no one feels like having nothing. I miss things I’ve barely even had: the warmth of another body next to mine, the simple comfort of touch, the scent of someone close, shared laughter filling the silence. I just want that connection so badly it hurts.
