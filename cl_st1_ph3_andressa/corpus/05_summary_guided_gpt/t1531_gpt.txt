I’m a girl from India with an anxiety disorder, and it makes socializing feel almost impossible. I feel lonely and empty most of the time. On the rare occasions I do make friends, I become really clingy and desperate because I’m so scared of losing them.  

In college, I put on this fake, cheerful version of myself. I laugh, smile, and try to look normal, but inside I feel so awkward and out of place. I see other people in groups going out, talking, making plans, and I usually just come and go alone.  

I’ve tried finding online friends too, but that hasn’t worked either. Everyone sees the “happy” me, but when I’m alone, I just cry.
