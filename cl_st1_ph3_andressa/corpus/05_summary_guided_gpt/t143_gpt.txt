I’m 42, an introverted autistic woman with ADHD, and I feel like I’m wired completely differently from everyone around me. I’m sensitive, have a pretty dark sense of humor, and I’ve made this conscious decision to actually feel my emotions instead of numbing them, which means I cry. A lot.

I really miss my ex. He was also my best friend, and I honestly doubt I’ll ever find someone like him again. I’ve been working on myself, but he wants nothing to do with me now. I’m trying to distract myself, but it’s hard not to think I’ll never get married. I feel “too different,” like I just can’t connect or feel truly seen.

At this point I half-joke that the only warm body I’ll ever sleep next to is a cat. Which isn’t the worst… but it feels so lonely.
