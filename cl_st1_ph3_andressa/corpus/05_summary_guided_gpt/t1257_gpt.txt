I wasn’t trying to ditch anyone, I was just busy and tired.

Now at work, my coworkers often go out together and somehow I’m never included. I see the plans being made, hear about the nights out afterwards, and I’m just… not part of it. I honestly don’t know what I did wrong or what vibe I’m giving off.

I feel like I have no friends left and I’m tired of doing everything alone. How do people even make friends nowadays as an adult? Any advice would be appreciated.
