I feel like I’m always the one putting in the effort with my friends. I’m the one who texts first, suggests plans, checks in. Recently I decided to stop texting first for two weeks just to see what would happen, and out of about seven people, only one actually reached out to me. It made me feel like no one really cares whether I’m around or not, and it’s hard not to question if these friendships are even real.

What’s confusing is that I’m actually pretty outgoing. I can meet people easily and get along with them, but when I look back, I feel like I’ve only ever had one or two genuine friendships in my whole life. I’ve gotten somewhat used to being on my own, and sometimes I even prefer it, but I still wish there was someone who would actually miss me.

I’m stuck between wanting to cut off these one-sided friendships and being scared of ending up completely alone.
