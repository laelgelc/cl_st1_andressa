I haven’t really had friends for about a year now. I cut off my old friend group for personal reasons, and it’s weird because all of them were people I’d known since elementary school, before I developed social anxiety and started becoming self-aware in like 6th grade.

Now I’m a senior and I’m basically trying to learn how to make friends for the first time. I recently met someone and we’ve been talking, but it just feels so forced and awkward. We run out of things to say really fast, and then there are these long pauses where I’m scrambling in my head for something, anything, to keep the conversation going.

I don’t know if I should keep trying to talk to them every day, even though it feels this difficult and uncomfortable, or if that’s a sign we’re just not a good match. I’m scared I’ll stay alone if I don’t push through, but I also feel drained and fake.
