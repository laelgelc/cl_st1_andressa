I’m so tired of being ghosted. I’m on friendship and dating apps, I put in the effort, we chat, we make plans, it feels like it’s going well… and then they just disappear. No explanation, nothing. It keeps happening over and over, to the point where I’m starting to think there’s something deeply wrong with me.

I have ASD, and I try so hard to be kind and loving. I’m not perfect, but I don’t understand what I’m doing that makes people just drop me like that. My therapist keeps telling me there’s nothing wrong with me, that it’s about them and not me, but I honestly don’t know how to believe that anymore.

What hurts even more is that two autistic friends I recently made have now ghosted me too, right after we made plans. It makes me feel like I don’t deserve to be alive if everyone keeps leaving.
