I’m an immigrant living in the UK, married to a British husband who isn’t very social. Honestly, I feel quite happy and content most of the time. My parents, my husband, and even my cat are my best friends. I don’t really feel a huge void where “friends” should be, the way people often describe.

But at the same time, I sometimes wish I had someone to do things with that I can’t really do with my husband, my cat, or my family. Like going for a casual coffee, wandering around a market, or taking a clay course together. Just someone to share small, everyday experiences with.

It makes me wonder: is it weird to feel mostly content, but still want more connection? Do I “have to” have friends in the traditional sense, or is it okay if my life doesn’t quite look like that?
