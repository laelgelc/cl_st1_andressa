I’m so tired of hearing “just be yourself” like it’s some magical solution.

When I was myself as a kid, I got bullied, excluded, called weird. Teachers, classmates, even other parents treated me like I was some kind of problem. People say they like “different” until it goes past some invisible line, and then you’re just the outcast everyone whispers about when you leave the room.

So I learned to shut everything down. I started watching how everyone else acted and tried to copy it, because being me clearly wasn’t acceptable. Now I feel like I don’t even know who I am anymore. I don’t know how to act around people, and underneath it all I’m just… ashamed of myself.
