It’s weird how I’ve just… gone numb to all of this. In high school I had no friends and I used to cry about it almost every day. Now I don’t even cry anymore. It’s like I’ve gotten so used to being alone that it doesn’t register the same way, even though nothing has actually changed.

I still think about what I’m missing. Having someone to sit next to in lectures, a group to eat lunch with, people to message about plans or look forward to seeing. I watch other people casually meeting up with friends, talking about parties or hangouts like it’s the most normal thing in the world, and it honestly feels unreal to me.

I can’t imagine my life being any different from this constant isolation. It’s like the idea of actually having a social life belongs to some other universe I’ll never get to live in.
