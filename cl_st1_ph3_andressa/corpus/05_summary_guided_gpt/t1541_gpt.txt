I’m 16 and on paper my life looks fine. I eat well, play sports, keep up with school. But inside I feel completely empty and alone.

I’ve never had a girlfriend, never even really talked to a girl. I was bullied about my looks before, and it messed me up. Now I avoid people because I’m scared of being judged again. My social anxiety just keeps getting worse and I feel like I’m the weird one in every room.

My parents love me, I know that, but we don’t really talk about feelings. There’s no deep connection, no real conversations. I have friends and we hang out, but I don’t feel like I can tell them how lonely I am. It feels like they wouldn’t really care or understand.

So I just play video games for hours to escape. I just want someone who truly cares, someone who understands me, and honestly… I just want a hug.
