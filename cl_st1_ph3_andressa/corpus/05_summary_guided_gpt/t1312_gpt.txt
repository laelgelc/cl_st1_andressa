I don’t even know if this is going to make sense, but I just need to ask: is there anyone out there who isn’t messed up on drugs and actually wants a real friendship?

I’m so tired. I feel like I have maybe two real friends, and that’s it. Everyone else just seems to want something from me. Money. Help finding drugs. A ride somewhere sketchy. They act like they care, like we’re close, but the second the money is gone or I say no, they disappear.

It makes me feel like a walking piggy bank instead of a person. Like my only value is what I can give them, not who I am. It’s hard not to look back and wonder if everything has been fake this whole time, if any of the good moments meant anything at all.

I don’t even know what to do anymore. If I keep letting people in, I get used. If I shut everyone out, I’m alone. Either way, I feel stuck and hopeless. I just want someone who actually cares, who isn’t just waiting for their next chance to take.
