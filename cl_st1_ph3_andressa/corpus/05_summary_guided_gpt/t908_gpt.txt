I don’t even know how to explain what this feels like. The closest I can get is cold and empty, like there’s a hollow space where something important used to be. I wake up every day with this heavy regret, and the only thing I really want is to go back to sleep and stay there. It’s not that I want to die, I just don’t want to be awake for this.

What makes it worse is that, on paper, I’m not alone. I have family, I have friends. But they don’t actually know me. They know the version of me I put on for them – the social mask that smiles, jokes, acts “fine.” Underneath that, I feel so hurt and “damaged” that I don’t even know how to let anyone see it.

I keep thinking back to the one person who really did see me and love me deeply. That relationship ended before I could fully give that love back, and losing it feels like losing the only real chance I had. Nothing since has come close to that depth. It makes me feel like I’ll never love like that again, and honestly, I don’t know why I should even bother trying.
