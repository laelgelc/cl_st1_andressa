I’m 18, just graduated high school a few months ago, and I feel like my social life has basically collapsed without me noticing.

Back in high school I used to hang out with friends multiple times a week. It wasn’t anything crazy, just walking around, grabbing food, playing games, whatever. I always kind of thought of myself as the “social one” between me and my girlfriend. She only had a few friends and I had what felt like a lot.

Now it’s completely flipped. I see my friends maybe once a month, if that. Most of my time is just me, my girlfriend, and studying. I’m somewhat introverted so it’s not like I need constant social stuff, but it still kind of hurts that I rarely get invited to anything unless I’m the one to start the conversation or make plans. I’ve mostly stopped initiating because I got tired of feeling like I was forcing myself into people’s schedules.

Meanwhile, my girlfriend is always out with her friends. She’s hanging out, going places, doing things, and I’m spending most nights and weekends alone. I’m happy she has people and a social life, but it’s hard not to compare it to my situation and feel like I’ve somehow become the boring one who just sits at home.

I keep thinking that at 18 I’m supposed to be going out more, partying, making memories, doing “young people” stuff. Instead I’m just… not. I’m scared I’m missing out on experiences I won’t get back, that I’ll look back at this time and regret how isolated I was.

I don’t even know if I want a crazy social life, but I want to feel wanted. I want to feel like people actually want me around, not like I’m an afterthought or someone they only see when I push for it.
