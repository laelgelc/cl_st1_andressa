I’ve been thinking a lot about how different my life feels now compared to a few years ago. I used to feel so isolated, like I could go days without anyone really noticing how I was doing. It was a really dark and lonely time, and it still scares me a bit to remember how invisible I felt.

These days, I’m incredibly grateful to have a few people in my life who genuinely care and check in on me. That contrast made me realize how much it matters to simply feel seen and supported on a daily basis. I honestly believe everyone deserves that, not just by chance, but as something they can count on.

Because of that, I’m starting a project aimed at providing daily connection and support to people who feel lonely. Before I build it out fully, I really want to understand where help is most needed.

I’ve made a short survey (about 3 minutes) to gather experiences and ideas. At the end, there’s an option to join a free trial of the project if it resonates with you. There’s also an Instagram account where I’ll share the progress and what I’m learning along the way.

If you’re willing, it would mean a lot if you could fill out the survey and share it with anyone who might relate.
