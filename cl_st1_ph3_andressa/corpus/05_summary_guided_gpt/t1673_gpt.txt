I’m a young mum in the UK with an 8‑month‑old baby girl, and I feel completely alone.

My daughter is honestly my whole world, the only thing that makes me feel like I’m worth anything. But since she was born, my relationship with my partner has been falling apart. He’s become so angry and emotionally cruel, and I feel like neither of us is coping. I’m constantly walking on eggshells, exhausted, and somehow still feeling like I’m failing at everything.

I’ve also basically lost all my friends. People my age just don’t seem to want anything to do with someone who has a baby. It feels like I became invisible the second I became a mum. I scroll through social media and see everyone living their “normal” young adult lives and I’m here, covered in baby sick, trying not to cry. I’ve always been the “loner” anyway – at school I was that person who was kind of tolerated but never really included. I thought adulthood might be different, but it isn’t.

I’m posting here because I just want some friends. I’d really love to talk to other young parents who get what this is like, but honestly I’m open to anyone, anywhere in the world, who’s kind and actually wants to talk.

I’m into art and I’d love to become a tattoo artist one day. I play League of Legends when I can grab a bit of time for myself. I really enjoy silly, low‑stakes conversations where we can just chat about random stuff and not be so serious all the time. I’d love to use VRChat someday but I can’t afford the gear, so Discord or anything similar would be fine.

I just want to feel appreciated and seen as more than some worn out, dead‑beat young mum.
