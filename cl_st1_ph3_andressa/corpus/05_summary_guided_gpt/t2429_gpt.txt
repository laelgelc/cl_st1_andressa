At the end of this summer everything kind of imploded for me. I was part of a friend group of around 18 people, and then this one guy – who used to be my friend – spread this really vile, sexual rumor about me. I’m pretty sure he did it to push me out so he could date a mutual friend. And it worked. Almost everyone just… quietly dropped me. No conversation, no “hey, is this true?” Just distance and silence.

Out of that whole group, only two people actually stayed close to me. I clung to that, like, “okay, at least these two know me, at least I’m not completely alone and maybe I’m not as disposable as I feel.” That was kind of the thin thread holding my trust together.

Then recently, one of those two started pulling away. She stopped initiating anything, barely replied when I reached out, and the vibe just completely shifted. I finally asked her what was going on, and she told me she’d heard the rumor and believed it, and that she didn’t feel comfortable being friends anymore.

So that’s another person gone without even asking me what happened. Another person who chose a disgusting lie over the actual experience of knowing me. It hurts in this really deep, almost humiliating way that people I cared about could just accept something like that and let me go so easily.

I already struggle with this fear that everyone will leave me, and it feels like life keeps handing me “proof” that I’m right. Every time I start to think maybe I can trust someone, something like this happens. It’s messing with my head so much. I keep thinking, “Why would anyone ever actually stay? Why would anyone really have my back?”

I don’t know how I’m supposed to ever feel secure with people again or believe that someone genuinely has my best interests at heart. I feel like I’m watching my ability to trust just erode, and I don’t know how to stop it.
