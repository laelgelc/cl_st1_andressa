Lately I’ve been writing these lines that keep looping in my head, and they’re basically about how far away I feel from everyone. There’s this one person I used to care about a lot, but the more time passes, the more indifferent I feel. It’s like I’ve gone numb around them, and I don’t even know if that’s good or bad anymore.

Everyone else seems to be out there making friends, going to parties, getting close to each other. Meanwhile, I’m realizing I actually feel safer and freer when I’m alone. I like being in my own space, in my own head, where I don’t have to explain myself or pretend I’m feeling something I’m not.

It’s strange, because it almost feels like there’s a “party” going on inside my mind that no one else can attend. No one really gets how I feel, and honestly, I don’t think anyone could match it even if they tried. So I just stay here, with myself.
