I keep finding myself wanting to go on holidays, try new things, just enjoy life a bit… but I don’t want to do any of it alone. I barely have any family, no partner, no kids. The few friends I do have are wrapped up in their own families and lives, which I understand, but it leaves me on the outside looking in.

Most days feel dull and empty. I can feel myself slipping into this depressed, numb state where I have no passion, no hope, no motivation to change anything. I rarely get invited anywhere. It honestly feels like I don’t matter to anyone, like I could disappear and nothing would really change. I hate that this is my reality, but I don’t know how to break out of it.
