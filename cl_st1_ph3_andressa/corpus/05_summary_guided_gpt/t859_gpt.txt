I’m in a relationship, but I still feel completely alone. It sounds weird even to type that. I have someone, technically, but I can’t shake the feeling that no one really cares about me in a deep way. I keep wondering if anyone’s life would actually change if I just disappeared. I’m not suicidal, but the thought keeps circling in my head: would it really matter if I was gone?

On paper, I’ve done the “right” things. I finished high school, got not just one but two college degrees, I work, I have my own place. All the stuff that’s supposed to make you feel like you’re doing okay in life. But I feel like a failure anyway. I’ve failed at relationships, at jobs, at friendships, at family. Everywhere that actually feels like it should matter, I feel like I’m coming up short.

Emotionally, it’s like my whole life is just sadness and anger on repeat. Happiness feels like this rare, almost foreign thing that I barely recognize when it shows up. Most days I just feel heavy and tired, and I keep asking myself why I even bother trying anymore.

I always dreamed of having my own family one day, something stable and loving. Lately I’m scared that’s just never going to happen for me, that I’m somehow not meant for that kind of life. Some dark part of me keeps whispering that maybe God is punishing me just for existing, and I don’t know how to argue with that voice anymore.
