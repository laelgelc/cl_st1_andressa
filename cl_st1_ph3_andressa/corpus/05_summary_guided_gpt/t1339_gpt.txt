I feel like I’ve hit a wall emotionally. No matter what I do, no woman has ever really wanted to get to know me beyond a couple of dates. It’s always the same pattern: a bit of interest, some messages, maybe one or two meetups, and then silence. On the apps it’s even worse – just endless ghosting. I’m so burned out from it that I don’t even want to try anymore.

But the idea of being single forever is eating me alive too. It’s like I’m stuck between two kinds of pain: the pain of trying and getting rejected, and the pain of not trying and feeling completely alone.

I try to keep my life together. I have hobbies, I do sports, I go to the gym and run, I make music. I recently rejoined a local football team and those weekly matches actually made me feel something again – a bit of purpose, adrenaline, being part of something. Now there’s a winter break and it’s just back to work, workout, sleep. Rinse and repeat. Weekends are the worst; I feel like I’m just existing, not living.

I just want to be loved by a woman, to feel chosen for once. Right now I don’t see any hope, I’m just trying to stay strong and survive. Needed to get this off my chest.
