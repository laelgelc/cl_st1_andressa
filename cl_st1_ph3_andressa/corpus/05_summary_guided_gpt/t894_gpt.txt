I feel like I live in a world that makes no sense to me. Everywhere I look, people seem shallow, irrational, and selfish, like they care more about protecting their feelings and egos than about truth or consistency. Socializing feels horrible because I end up watching people say incoherent things, contradict themselves, and still act like they’re in the right. It’s like I’m the alien for noticing.

I genuinely wanted to work with others, to build healthier environments and systems. Instead, I’m treated like some kind of criminal or threat for questioning the way things are. Now I’ve basically given up on “putting myself out there.” I don’t see any point, and I don’t really have any goals left.
