I’m 25 and already dreading Valentine’s Day. Last year, it passed like any other day—no messages, no flowers, no one even hinting they cared. I’m expecting the same this year. People sometimes tell me I’m attractive, but it never seems to mean anything. I just see all the flaws in my face and my personality. I’m terrible at flirting, I barely go out, and I was really isolated for several years growing up, so I never learned how to connect. Valentine’s Day just feels like a giant reminder that I’m getting older and still completely alone.
