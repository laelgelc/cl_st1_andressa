On paper, my life looks fine. I have routines, goals I’m working toward, people I talk to regularly. From the outside, it probably seems like I’m doing okay. But there’s this quiet, persistent loneliness that doesn’t really go away, it just gets drowned out when I’m busy.

It sneaks in at night, or when things finally slow down. I don’t always want company, exactly. I just want to feel understood without having to put on the “I’m fine” version of myself. I catch myself replaying conversations and wondering if I matter to people as much as they matter to me.

Some days I’m productive and it feels like progress. Other days it feels like I’m just stacking distractions on top of this empty spot I don’t know how to fill.

I’m not really looking for advice. I just needed to say this somewhere people might get it. And if any of this sounds familiar: you’re not the only one.
