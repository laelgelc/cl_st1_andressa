I used to tell myself a very simple story: I was always hated, always ignored, never had a normal life or any intimacy. Lately I’m realizing that wasn’t completely true. There were probably people who were interested in me, and I did have at least one friend. But my mind keeps insisting I was never really close to anyone, like it’s trying to erase any exception.

I can see the cycle now: I got rejected, so I closed myself off. I stopped expressing myself, stopped trying, and over time I became emotionally stunted. It feels like there’s a difference between circumstantial loneliness and what I did to myself. Circumstances could have changed, but I made a decision to shut down, and that turned into my “personality.”

Now I’m stuck in this severe alienation that feels like my own fault. I think about my life every day and wonder if there’s any way back.
