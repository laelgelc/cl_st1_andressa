I’m so tired of being the one who’s always there for other people, only to realize that no one really shows up for me. People are usually nice enough on the surface, but there’s no real concern, no real care or affection. Maybe my parents care, but outside of them, it feels like I could disappear and nothing would really change for anyone.

I do have a couple of friends and some acquaintances, so it’s not like I’m completely alone. But even then, I still feel like nobody truly cares. It’s like my existence is just… taken for granted. I’m there when they need something, but when I’m struggling, it’s silent.

For years I’ve hoped things would change, that someone would love me the way I want to be loved. Instead, my expectations from people keep shrinking, and with that, I feel myself getting more distant from the world. I don’t know if it ever gets better. Is it even possible to live with zero expectations from anyone and not feel completely empty?
