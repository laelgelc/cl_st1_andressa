I’m in my 40s and I’ve always been a loner. I have a small circle of friends I might see once or twice a year, but we don’t really keep in touch in between. I’m happily married, and most of my time is either spent alone with my hobbies or with my family. I don’t use social media besides Reddit, and I basically have no social life outside of that.

What bothers me isn’t loneliness in the day-to-day, but this thought that when I die, almost no one will come to my funeral. I worry it’ll be embarrassing or sad for my family.
