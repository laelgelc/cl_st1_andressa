I’m turning 29 today and I’m sitting alone in a hotel room on a “spa trip” I booked for myself, crying. Not a single message from anyone. No family, no friends. Nothing.

I used to tell myself I liked solo travel, that it was empowering and independent. Tonight it just feels like I do everything alone because nobody actually wants to be there with me. It’s like I’ve hit this age and have nothing to show for it – no partner, no close friends, no one to share any of this with.

I’m autistic and I’m scared this is just how my life is going to be: me, doing things alone, pretending I’m okay with it while I feel completely hollow inside. I don’t even know what to do with this feeling anymore.
