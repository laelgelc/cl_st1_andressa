I’m so tired of feeling this way all the time. Every day feels the same: wake up, exist, go to sleep. No friends, no plans, nothing to look forward to. I’ve got this mix of social anxiety, insecurity about my looks, and just feeling like my personality is… mid. Not interesting, not fun, just average. It makes me feel like an “average girl” that no one really notices or wants to get to know.

I never built real friendships or any kind of relationship. I spent my teenage years at home doing nothing while everyone else was out living. Now I see people my age and older actually enjoying life and it makes me question what I’m even doing here.
