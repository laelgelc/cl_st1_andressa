I don’t even know what I’m feeling anymore.

I was with this guy I loved and trusted so much. In the beginning he was so caring, always there, calling, checking on me. I really believed he was “my person.” But slowly he changed. He got agitated all the time, stopped calling, blocked me, threatened to remove me from his life, said awful things to me. He would disappear and never tell me where he was or what he was doing. I felt like I was going crazy, constantly in the dark, begging for scraps of attention.

It hurt so much that my body just broke down. Panic attacks, shaking, not being able to breathe, and I did things to myself I’m not proud of just to feel something I could understand.

Then one day he finally left. He sent me this last text and… I just turned my phone off. No arguing, no begging, nothing. I shed one single tear and that was it. Since then I’ve been weirdly “normal.” No crying, no screaming, just this empty, blank feeling. I don’t understand myself. I don’t know if I’m numb, in denial, or if something inside me just died.

We live across from each other, so I saw him recently in the neighborhood. He looked so happy, smiling like nothing ever happened. I actually smiled too, then hid so he wouldn’t see me. I still want him back, even after everything he did. But I’m terrified to turn my phone back on in case there’s no message from him. I don’t know which would hurt more: seeing nothing, or seeing him move on completely.

I don’t really have anyone in my life I can talk to about this. I feel like I’m just floating alone in this weird emptiness. I don’t even know if I’ll ever come back online after posting this. I just needed to put it somewhere.
