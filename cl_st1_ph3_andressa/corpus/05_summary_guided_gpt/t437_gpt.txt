I’m 25 and I feel like I’m watching life happen from the outside. Every time I see people my age partying, traveling together, or just hanging out with their close friends, it hits me how alone I am. I do everything by myself. In the last few years I haven’t made a single real friend, and it feels like I’m wasting my life with no fun shared memories, just existing.

People have told me I’m bubbly and easy to talk to, so it’s confusing that no one seems to actually want to get to know me. I’ve tried to make girl friends, I’ve asked people to hang out, but nothing ever really goes anywhere. It’s like I’m always the extra, never someone’s person.

I graduated college four years ago without making any friends there, and now I feel like I literally have no one. It’s starting to feel like this is just how my life is going to be.
