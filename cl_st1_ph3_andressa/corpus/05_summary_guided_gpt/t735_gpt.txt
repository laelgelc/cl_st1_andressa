I don’t really know where else to put this, so I’m just dumping it here.

I feel so alone it’s starting to feel like this is just going to be my life.

My partner of 7 years left almost 3 years ago, and it’s like that breakup ripped the curtain down on everything else. I realized the people I thought were my friends weren’t really there for me at all. When I needed them most, they just… weren’t there. No one checked in, no one really cared beyond a “that sucks” and then disappearing.

I technically still have one best friend. We’ve known each other for 13 years. On paper that sounds like something solid, but in reality it feels so one-sided. I’m always the one reaching out, trying to make plans, checking in. She’s never invited me to anything. Not once. Lately it feels like her boundaries are tightening, like she’s slowly pulling away, and I’m trying to respect that but it just hurts. It makes me think that once she finds a partner, I’ll be pushed completely out of her life. I already feel like I’m on the edge.

I’ve tried dating, but I honestly hate it. Most of the time it feels like I’m just a free meal or someone to take advantage of. The few times I tried to keep things equal, like splitting the bill or taking turns, I ended up getting ghosted. It feels like no one actually wants something real with me. I’m just… convenient until I’m not.

I’ve tried making new friends too, but it’s always the same pattern: I put in effort, they don’t. The worst was when I organized this beach party for over 20 coworkers. I planned it all, hyped it up, tried to make it fun. In the end, only 5 people showed up. Standing there, pretending it didn’t bother me, I just felt like a complete joke.

I’m 24, my birthday is coming up, and instead of being excited I’m just dreading it. It feels like my loneliness is only going to get worse from here. I don’t have much hope left that I’ll find love again, or even just people who genuinely want me around. I feel depressed, unwanted, and like there’s something fundamentally wrong with me.

Sorry if this was long or pointless. I’m sorry if I wasted your time.
