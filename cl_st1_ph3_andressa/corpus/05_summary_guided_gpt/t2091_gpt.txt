I’m 25 and I honestly feel like I’ve ruined my life.

From 18 to 23 I completely hated myself. I was convinced I was ugly, boring, not worth anyone’s time. I avoided going out, turned down invitations, and basically just worked and went home. No hobbies, no social life, nothing. Just hiding.

The only times I “let someone in” were two relationships with guys who treated me badly. I stayed way too long in both because I thought that was all I deserved. Now they’re gone and I’m left with no one.

I keep thinking about those years and how different things could’ve been if I hadn’t been so insecure. I cry at night wishing I could go back and force myself to live instead of just existing in the background.

Now at 25, it feels like I missed the window. Everyone my age seems to be getting married, or buried in their careers, or already settled with their friend groups. I feel like I wasted my formative years and that making real friends now is almost impossible.
