Most people on here talk about how much they hate being lonely, but I’ve kind of made peace with it. I’ve learned I can’t really form deep connections, and honestly, I don’t want to anymore. People either laugh with me or at me, and they never stick around long. I’m always “too much” for them, so I’ve decided they don’t get my presence at all.

Everyone acts like affection and intimacy are some magical things, but if it’s really just physical, there are sex workers for that. No need to pretend it’s deeper than it is.

My family loves to say communication is a “two way street,” yet I’m always the one expected to call, text, visit. After a lifetime of this, I’m done. I finally love who I am, I’m okay with being alone, and I’m finished bending over backwards to please people. Now it’s just me, focusing on myself.
