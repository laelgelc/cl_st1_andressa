I feel like somewhere in the last couple of years I just… forgot how to be a friend.

I don’t know how to text people anymore, or how to keep in touch without it feeling forced or weird. I haven’t made any meaningful new friends. Everyone in my life feels like an acquaintance, and no one ever reaches out first. I’m always the one trying, and even then I end up pulling back. I have this nagging feeling that I’m unconsciously pushing people away, and that all of this is my fault.

The only close relationship I’ve managed to hold onto is my girlfriend, and I’m incredibly grateful for her. It honestly feels like she’s the only person I have. Home isn’t a refuge either – my family is miserable and they get angry at me a lot, so being there just makes me feel worse, not safe.

Sometimes I feel like I could disappear and it wouldn’t really matter to anyone outside of my girlfriend. When I try to get close to people, I get really anxious and overthink everything. I’m constantly worried I’ll say something wrong or be misunderstood, and that fear makes me act distant or awkward, which just pushes people away anyway.

I don’t even know if I want advice. I just needed to get this out somewhere.
