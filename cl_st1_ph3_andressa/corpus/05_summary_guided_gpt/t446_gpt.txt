I’m sitting here on yet another “special” day, drunk and alone, and I honestly don’t know what the point of any of this is anymore.

Every big occasion that’s supposed to mean something – Valentine’s Day, my birthday, Pride, Thanksgiving, Halloween, Christmas, and now New Year’s – I’ve spent completely alone. No one to text, no one to go out with, nothing. I’ve never even had someone genuinely wish me a happy birthday or had any kind of birthday celebration. Not once. It’s like I don’t exist.

I feel like I’ve done absolutely nothing with my life, but it’s not like I haven’t wanted to. I just have no one to do anything with, I can’t afford most activities anyway, and when things do happen, I’m never told about them or included. I’m always on the outside looking in.

When I try to talk about how I feel, my parents just respond with religious crap, like I’m supposed to pray it away or be grateful or whatever. Other people tell me to “just be happy alone,” as if that’s some magic switch I can flip. Meanwhile my dad keeps pressuring me about my life, my future, what I’m doing, while I’m just trying to get through the day without killing myself.

On top of all that, I’m furious at everything: politics, religion, pollution, rich people, celebrities, influencers, the insane cost of living. It all feels rigged and pointless. Everything just sucks. Even basic daily tasks feel like climbing a mountain. I’m so tired of being this alone and this angry and this empty all the time.
