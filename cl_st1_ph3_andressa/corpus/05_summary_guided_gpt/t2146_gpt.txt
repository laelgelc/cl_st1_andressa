I feel like I’ve been on the outside looking in for most of my life. I haven’t really had friends since elementary school, and somehow it got even worse after COVID. It feels like everyone either disappeared or moved on, and I’m just…stuck.

I tell myself I should try to be more outgoing, but it honestly feels pointless. There’s nobody around me who seems to share my interests, so even if I did put myself out there, I don’t know who I’d connect with.

I’ve been single my whole life, and being closeted makes everything more complicated. Flirting with guys feels dangerous, like I’m risking being seen as a creep or running into homophobia. So I just don’t try, and stay in my head instead.

The worst part is I did have two really good friends, but both moved away after a few months. They found new people, new lives, and I was left feeling like I was just a temporary phase. I still think highly of them, but it hurts.
