I’m sitting here in this quiet house in eastern North Carolina, and it feels like the walls still think we’re married. Every room has some memory baked into it, and now it’s just me walking through them alone.

We were together a long time. No kids, no big legacy, just a lot of years that I stayed committed even when the love faded and both of us changed. Now that it’s over, I keep wondering what I have to show for all of it.

Coming home to an empty house is the hardest part. I miss having someone to share the small, boring moments with. I just want to meet a kind, loving woman who will really love me, stay through the hard times, and help make this place feel like a home again.
