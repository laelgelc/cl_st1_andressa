I’m a 31-year-old guy and I feel like I’ve been romantically lonely my entire life. I’ve really been trying to change that, especially since last year – putting myself out there more, trying to meet people – but it just never works out. 

With Christmas coming up, it hits harder than usual. The decorations, the snow, couples cuddling by the fireplace… it all just makes me feel the emptiness more. I’m not even dreaming of anything crazy, I just wish I could experience something as simple as a hug with a girl under a Christmas tree at least once in my life. 

Sometimes I wonder if that’s asking for too much. Maybe I really do need Santa Claus for this.
