Lately it feels like my life is just copy-paste days. I wake up, go to work, exchange a few words with the same 2–3 people, then head to the gym where I don’t talk to anyone. That’s it. I’ve only got about two friends left and I barely see even one of them. When we do meet, we just get drunk and wander around our rundown town at night, and somehow that now counts as a “fulfilling” weekend. A year ago I actually had more going on. Since I turned 18, it’s like I’m stuck reliving the same empty day over and over.
