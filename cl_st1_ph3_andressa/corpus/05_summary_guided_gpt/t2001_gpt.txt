I keep realizing how shallow my friendships feel and it’s starting to really hurt. I do like my friends – we hang out, play games, joke around. On the surface it looks fine. But I don’t feel like I have anyone I can actually talk to, about my day or what’s going on in my head.  

I want that one person I can call just to say “hey, I saw this thing today” without needing an excuse. Someone who actually knows me, not just the version of me that shows up to play.  

I feel like I had a chance once and I ruined it. Now it’s hard not to believe I’m just broken and that kind of connection isn’t meant for me.
