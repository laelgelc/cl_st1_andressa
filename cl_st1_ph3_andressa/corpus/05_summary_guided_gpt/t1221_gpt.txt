I deleted all my dating apps and, weirdly, most of my loneliness went with them. Swiping just made me feel like a tiny, forgettable speck in this endless sea of people, constantly comparing myself and wondering why I wasn’t enough.  

Without them, I suddenly have time and headspace. I’m reading more, drinking tea, walking my dogs, rock climbing, going to the gym, actually enjoying my own routines. It makes me think social media might be doing something similar to our sense of loneliness too.
