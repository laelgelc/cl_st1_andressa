It really hurts when people ghost me and then suddenly pop back up only when they’re bored or want company. I always act like it doesn’t matter, like I’m cool with it, but it actually gets to me a lot. Some of these people had become a real part of my life, part of my routine, and then they just vanish for months like I never existed.

I do have good childhood friends who care about me; they’re just busy with their own lives, so I’m not completely alone. But I still feel lonely sometimes, in a way that’s hard to explain.

I’m not even sure why I’m writing this. Maybe I just needed to get it out and hope I feel a little lighter afterward.
