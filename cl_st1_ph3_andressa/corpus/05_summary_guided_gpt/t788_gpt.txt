I feel like I ruined my life by trusting the people who were supposed to protect me.

A few years ago I told my family how alone and like a failure I felt. Instead of just letting me stay close, they pushed me to move away, get a Master’s, “start fresh,” become “independent.” I listened. Now I’m drowning in student debt, working a job that’s nothing like what I wanted, and stuck in a place I hate.

I told them I want to move back home, that I’m miserable here, and they basically said no unless I first find a better job where I am. That feels impossible. It’s like they want the image of me being successful and independent more than they want me to be okay.

I used to have friends, crushes, my whole family nearby. Now I only go home twice a year. My nephews are growing up without really knowing me. I feel like a stranger in my own life.

I don’t want this “independence.” I just want to go home.
