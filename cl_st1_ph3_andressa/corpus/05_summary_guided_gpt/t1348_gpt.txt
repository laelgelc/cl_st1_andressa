My whole life has been lies, bullying, discrimination, rejection, and pain coming from other people. At some point I just decided I’d had enough and chose isolation. Honestly, it felt like the best choice. Alone, I make my own rules. No one can hurt me, lie to me, or turn into another “beast” I have to defend myself from.

But being away from everyone for so long has made me emotionally numb. I feel indifferent toward people now. I really do believe it’s better for me – and for everyone else – if I stay separate, only interacting when absolutely necessary, with no friends at all.
