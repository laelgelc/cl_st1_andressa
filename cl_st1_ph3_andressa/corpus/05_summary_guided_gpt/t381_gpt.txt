I keep picturing myself as something I could just deposit somewhere safe. Like a bank, a vault. I want to take the next years of my life, all my feelings and fears, and lock them away inside someone I can trust. Just slide everything across the counter and know it’ll be guarded, that I won’t be exposed to how harsh and hostile the world can be.

But I don’t know if this “vault” is really safe. I don’t know who’s been let in before, or how they were treated. Were they protected or picked apart? Were they treasured or just…stored? I’m scared that if I hand myself over, I’ll just become another thing on a shelf, something to be counted, not cared for.

I wonder if, behind what looks like thick steel doors, it’s actually hollow. Maybe the locks are just for show, or maybe there are secret keys I don’t know about. I’m terrified that I’ll pour myself in, all this trust and vulnerability, and one day they’ll just forget I’m there. That I’ll sit in the dark, untouched, while life goes on outside.

I want safety so badly. I want to rest inside someone who won’t use what I give them as leverage, or entertainment, or something to trade. But I can’t tell if I’m seeing a sanctuary or just another place where I’ll be kept, not loved.
