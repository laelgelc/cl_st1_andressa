I feel like I’m screaming into a void and no one even turns their head. Whenever I talk, it’s like my words just fall flat and disappear. I watch people my age laughing, going out, being close to each other, and I honestly can’t imagine what that feels like. I cry myself to sleep and it just feels so pathetic, but I can’t stop.

Living in this body feels like dragging around a weight I never asked for. It’s like I’m “special” in the worst possible way, marked for being left out. I don’t even see this as something that can change anymore. It feels like this is just how my life is meant to be: alone, ignored, and lonely until the day I die.
