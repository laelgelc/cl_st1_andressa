Hey everyone,

We’ve made some important changes to what’s allowed here, and I want to lay it out clearly so there’s no confusion about what this subreddit is for going forward.

From now on, emotional/venting posts are **not** allowed here and will be removed. That includes posts centered on:

- Loneliness and sadness  
- Hating society or general rants about “people”  
- Life problems, crises, or “my life is spiraling out of control”  
- Seeking friends, support, or emotional comfort  
- Hopelessness, despair, or “I’m lonely and…” type posts  

If you’re looking to talk about those things, please use subs like **r/lonely** or **r/loneliness**, or, if you’re in serious distress, check out **RedditCareResources**. Those spaces are set up specifically to offer emotional support and crisis-related help. This one is not.

The goal here is to keep this subreddit focused on higher-quality, on-topic content about **loners** as a concept and experience, not as a place to vent or look for personal support. We’re trying to build a space for discussion, exploration, and ideas around being a loner, not a general support group.

Acceptable themes now include things like:

- Loner activities and hobbies  
- Famous, fictional, or modern loners  
- Loners and solitude (philosophical, practical, cultural angles)  
- Loners and relationships (friendship, romance, family, boundaries)  
- Loners and religion or spirituality  
- Loners and technology or the internet  
- Loners in literature, movies, games, and media  
- Travel and how loners experience the world  
- Related memes that clearly fit the “loner” theme  

Some examples of posts that **will be removed**:

- Self-promo YouTube links or channels disguised as “loner content”  
- “Why does everyone hate me?”  
- “How can I make friends?”  
- “My life is spiraling out of control, I don’t know what to do”  
- “I’m lonely and…” followed by a vent or cry for help  

If your post is mainly about personal emotional distress, it does not belong here. Please redirect that energy to the subs and resources made for it. This change is to keep this place focused, readable, and genuinely about **loners** rather than general loneliness support.
