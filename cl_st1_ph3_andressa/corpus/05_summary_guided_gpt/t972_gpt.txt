Outside of work, everything just feels empty. I pour myself into my job because as long as I’m busy, I don’t have to sit with all the thoughts about how alone I am. The second I’m off the clock, it’s like the silence rushes in and I’m left with this ache I don’t know what to do with.

I spend a lot of time in my head, reading, thinking, fantasizing about a life that feels warmer and more loving than the one I have. I keep imagining some parallel version of me who has a big friend group, a relationship, maybe even a chance to start over somewhere new. I cry about it more than I’d like to admit.

One of the weirdest things I do is search my own name online, over and over, like I’m hoping to stumble across proof that someone, somewhere, is thinking about me. Of course I never really find anything, but I keep doing it anyway.

Lately I feel like I’m emotionally dissolving, like I’m changing in ways I can’t clearly see or explain. I’ve thought about turning more to God, but I get stuck on the idea of worshipping someone who doesn’t talk back. It feels like more one-way talking into a void, and I already do enough of that.

More than anything, I just want someone who genuinely wants to know me as deeply as I want to know other people. I’m so tired of feeling like that kind of connection is something that happens to other people, but not to me.
