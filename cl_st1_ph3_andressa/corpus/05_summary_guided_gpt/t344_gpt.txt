Nothing has really changed in my life. I’m still alone, still stuck in the same place. I’ve basically given up on finding “the one.” I’m surrounded by people who don’t seem to understand me at all, and even talking to a psychologist didn’t help me figure out what’s wrong. I just feel burned out.

Every girl who shows interest in me disappears after about a month. I don’t push anyone, I don’t play games, I feel like all I offer is pure love… and yet they vanish. I’ve been obsessed with the idea of being deeply loved since I was young. Now I’m honestly wondering: is love really that hard to find, or am I just some kind of freak?
