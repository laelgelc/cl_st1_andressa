I live completely alone in a tiny one-room apartment in a city where I’m basically invisible. My days are all the same: wake up, work, eat, sleep, repeat. The only people I talk to in person are colleagues about work, store workers at the checkout, and drivers in passing. That’s it.

I don’t date. I don’t even really want a relationship anymore in the way I used to, but I still feel the emptiness where that kind of connection should be. I’m touch-starved, love-starved, friendless. I work like a drone and my life feels like it’s just moving along a conveyor belt in some giant factory.

I feel like I’m becoming less human. I used to feel sad; now it’s more like I’m just hollow. And please, I don’t want to hear that I should “learn to love myself.” That just feels like another empty slogan.
