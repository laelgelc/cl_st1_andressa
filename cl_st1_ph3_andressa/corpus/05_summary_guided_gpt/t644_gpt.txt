I live alone and lately it feels like there’s this constant hollow space inside me. I keep myself busy with personal projects, but even when I’m “productive,” the emptiness is still there, like I’m just killing time instead of actually living.

I don’t have social media, and I only talk to maybe 2–3 acquaintances every now and then. Nothing deep, nothing that really makes me feel known. I’m conflicted about relationships too. Part of me believes having a partner isn’t for me, like I’m not built for that kind of connection. But then I feel this aching loneliness without one and it just… hurts.

Work is draining me and I’m burning out, but my free time doesn’t feel rewarding either. I don’t see any financial progress, my projects don’t feel like they’re going anywhere, and I don’t have supportive friends to lean on.

I miss laughing with someone, physical affection, and real, stimulating conversation. Right now my life feels like it’s just me, YouTube, and my thoughts echoing in an empty room.
