I feel so lonely even though, on paper, I’m “not alone.” I have a boyfriend and siblings, and I get along fine with his friends and people at work, but they’re not *my* people. No one texts me to hang out, no one invites me to things just because they want me there. I don’t have that person I can message randomly or grab coffee with on a whim.

Most of my free time is just…empty. When I see others posting pictures with their close friends, it really stings. People say I’m a good, funny person, so I keep wondering what I’m doing wrong. Is this just how my life is going to be?
