I feel like I’m stuck in this loop where I can make friends easily, but nothing ever seems to last or get deeper. I’m outgoing, I can talk to people, I click with them at first… and then it just fizzles out. I don’t know what I’m doing wrong. I keep wondering if I’m actually just annoying and people are being polite, or if I’m just a “friend of convenience” until something better comes along.

There are also some old rumors about me floating around, and I can’t help but wonder if that’s still affecting how people see me, even if no one says it to my face.

I recently stopped taking Citalopram and honestly, it hasn’t helped. If anything, it’s made the loneliness feel sharper. One example that’s really getting to me is a friend who used to always want to hang out. We’d talk all the time, and now they just leave my messages on read with no explanation. It hurts more than I want to admit.

I don’t want to fall into using a romantic relationship to fill this void again. I want real, stable friendships. I just have no idea how to actually maintain them or what I’m doing to push people away.
