Another Saturday morning and it feels like they just keep getting heavier. I’m sitting here already knowing how the day will go: alone, like so many before it, and probably so many after. It’s not just the silence or the empty schedule that hurts, it’s the absence of *her*—not a specific person, but the idea of someone real.

Someone who actually likes me and wants me, physically and emotionally. Someone who feels safe with me, who knows I care, who lets me be there for her… and actually wants to be there for me too. That mutual wanting just feels impossibly far away.
