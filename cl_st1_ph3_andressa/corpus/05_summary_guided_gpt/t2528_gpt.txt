I used to feel so much for girls whose dads left them. I thought I was lucky, that my father was one of the good ones. Now I’ve found out he spent years wanting another woman and would have walked out on us if he’d had the chance.

After my mom died, some married woman contacted him online. Now they’re having this emotional affair and he talks shit about my mom and her family to her. My mom, who’s not even here to defend herself. It makes me sick.

I feel like I’m swinging between hatred and total numbness. Part of me wants to cut him off completely, just tell him to leave me alone. But he acts like I’m the ungrateful one, like I’m somehow the problem for not accepting this.

It hurts so much I can barely put it into words. I just wish my mom could come and take me somewhere quiet and safe, where none of this exists. Somewhere I can finally rest and not feel this pain anymore.
