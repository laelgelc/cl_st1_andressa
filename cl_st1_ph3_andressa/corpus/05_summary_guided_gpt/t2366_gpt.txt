I don’t even know why I’m writing this, but I guess I just need to put it somewhere.

I feel so low and so alone all the time. I ended a three-year on-and-off relationship about a year ago, and I’m still not over my ex at all. Before New Year’s I sent them this long apology message, poured my heart out, and they didn’t even open it. Not even a “seen,” nothing. It’s like I’m completely invisible to them now. It makes me feel like I’m just a waste of space, like my life is pointless.

I don’t really have friends who are there for me. I’m always the one who shows up, who listens, who tries to help everyone else, but when it comes to me, it’s like nobody is really there. Most days I just sit alone in my dark room, doing nothing, feeling like I’m rotting away. I feel hated, or at least completely unwanted.

My dad keeps telling me to leave the house, like he’s just waiting for me to go, and I’m only 20. Hearing that from your own parent really messes with your head. It just adds to this feeling that I don’t belong anywhere.

Sometimes I think if something happened to me, I don’t even know if anyone would really care. Maybe for a moment, but then life would just go on and I’d be forgotten. I’m just so tired of feeling this empty and alone.
