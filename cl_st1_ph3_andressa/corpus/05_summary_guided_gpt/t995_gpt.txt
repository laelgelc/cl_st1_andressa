My life has always felt like terror and pain with no real break, just this constant mental starvation. When I try to talk about it, my relatives either minimize it or look so uncomfortable that I end up shutting down. I don’t think they’re bad people; I honestly believe they acted out of love in their own way. But it still leaves me feeling like nobody really understands, and most people just don’t know how to handle someone like me.

I feel ashamed even putting this into words. It feels like my whole existence is a shit show, and the world itself is some kind of hell. I’m not angry at my family, but I am furious at whoever or whatever created a world where so much suffering is even possible. If there could have been peace and kindness, why wasn’t that the default?

I keep circling back to self-harm and suicidal thoughts. If even the creator doesn’t seem to care, why shouldn’t I hurt myself? Why keep going in a place that feels designed for pain? I don’t know what to do with any of this.
