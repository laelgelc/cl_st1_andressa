I feel so stupid for feeling lonely when I actually have a lot of caring friends. They check in on me, they’re kind, they’re there if I need them. And yet, especially at night, this heavy loneliness creeps in. I think it’s because what I’m really missing is a real, mutual romantic relationship. I’m tired of wondering if my “other half” even exists. In the past, love has almost always been one-sided – I care deeply, they don’t. I’m exhausted from giving so much and never feeling truly chosen back.
