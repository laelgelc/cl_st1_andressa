I’m so tired of feeling like lonely people are treated as if we’re some kind of monsters. We’re not. Everyone deserves someone. I know I’m kind and caring, just… a little neurodivergent, and I’ve accepted that relationships will probably always be different and harder for me. But it still hurts. It makes me sad that it has to be this way, and sometimes I just wish I could be “normal” so it wouldn’t feel so impossible.
