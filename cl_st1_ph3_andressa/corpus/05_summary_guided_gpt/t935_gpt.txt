I finally booked a trip to Europe this fall, something I’ve wanted for years. I thought I’d be buzzing with excitement, but instead I just feel… blank. It’s strange to have something I’ve dreamed about actually happening and not feel much at all.

My birthday will fall during the trip, and that’s the part I keep clinging to. I usually feel painfully sad and invisible on my birthday. I’m hoping that being in a completely new place, far away from my usual surroundings, might help me feel different for once. Maybe even a little happy.
