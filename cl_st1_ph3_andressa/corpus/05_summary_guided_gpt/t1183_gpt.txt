I’m almost 50 and I feel like everyone around me assumes I’m doing fine. On the outside I probably look stable enough, but inside I just feel empty and worn down. I don’t even know how to start talking about it or what words to use without sounding dramatic or needy.

I do long drives for work and I just listen to music to stay awake. But what I really want is someone to actually talk to, to share the time with. Friends and family are always “busy,” barely reply, never really engage. So it’s just me, the same playlist, and this heavy, quiet loneliness.
