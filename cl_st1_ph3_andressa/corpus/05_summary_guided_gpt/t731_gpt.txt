2025 has honestly just been a shit year for me. It’s been one thing after another: a bad breakup that really messed with my head, a job that was mentally draining me, my dog dying, and constant fights with my parents. It felt like every part of my life was falling apart at the same time.

I thought things were finally looking up when I got a new job in the same field as my best friend and moved in with him and his girlfriend. Before that, he and I used to go out a lot, hang out with his friend group, and actually have fun. I really believed moving in together would bring us closer, that I’d finally have a solid group of people around me.

At first it kind of worked that way. The three of us did a lot together. But now it’s like I barely exist. I almost never spend time with him without his girlfriend there, and the wider friend group has basically stopped inviting me to anything. It’s like I got phased out without anyone telling me.

I even talked to him about it, told him I’d like more one-on-one time like we used to have. For a short while things got a bit better, but then everything slipped back to how it was. Whenever I ask him to hang out, he’s “never free.” It’s hard not to feel like the message is that I just don’t matter that much anymore.

So now I’m living with people who don’t really feel like friends, with no other local friends to turn to. My days are just: go to work, then run or go to the gym, then come home and sit in my room alone. That’s my whole life right now. It’s starting to feel really heavy, and I don’t know how much longer I can keep pretending it’s fine.
