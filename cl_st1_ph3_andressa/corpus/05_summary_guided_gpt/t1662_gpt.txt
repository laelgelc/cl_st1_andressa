I’m a few years out of university, working a corporate job, and I feel like I’ve lived two different lives. Before COVID I was overweight, unhealthy, and constantly depressed. Since then I’ve worked hard, got in shape, built some confidence, and on paper I’m “doing better.” But inside, not much has changed. The loneliness is brutal.

I don’t really know how to make friends as an adult, and I have no idea how to connect with women. It feels like nobody actually needs or wants me around. When I try going to events or social things, I end up feeling even more alone because I’m always going by myself and leaving by myself.

My job isn’t helping either – it just drains me and makes me feel like I’m wasting my life. I was once very close to ending it all, and I know a big part of that was not having any real social life.

I have loving parents, but no close friends, no girlfriend, and being an only child just amplifies everything. I’m not sure what to do anymore. How do I actually build connections from this place?
