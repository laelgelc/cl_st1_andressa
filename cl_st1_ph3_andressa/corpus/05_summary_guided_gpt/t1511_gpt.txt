I’m 17 and I honestly feel like my life is already over.

I tried doing all the stuff people say you should do to feel better. I tried being more positive, taking better care of myself, trying to change my mindset. But nothing feels any different. I still feel just as depressed and alone as before, maybe even worse.

I don’t have any real friends. The few people I thought I could trust ended up talking behind my back. They make fun of my humor, the way I look, the way I act. I hear about the jokes, or catch little comments, and it just confirms everything I already think about myself.

I think I’m really ugly. I hate seeing my reflection. Every time I do, I just feel disgusted and embarrassed. It feels like just existing in this body is humiliating. I always imagine people laughing at me when they see me.

I can’t communicate with people properly. I don’t really understand them, or how I’m supposed to act. I think I might have autism or something, but I don’t even know. I just know I always feel out of place and wrong. I argue with my parents a lot because I’m scared to go outside in case someone from school sees me and makes fun of me again.

I’m scared of liking anyone, even just having a crush. I feel like a creep automatically, like if anyone knew I liked them they’d just laugh and tell everyone. I feel like a walking joke.

My grades are terrible. I might have to repeat 10th grade again. So I’m not only ugly and weird, I’m also stupid on top of it.

I just want one person I can care about and be myself around. Not even romantically, just someone who actually cares about me. But people always find reasons to make fun of me. I feel completely pathetic. I don’t see this getting better. I really think I’m going to kill myself.
