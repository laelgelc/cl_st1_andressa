I don’t really spend time with anyone outside of work, so I’ve started trying to hang out with coworkers after hours. But then I keep hearing, “Don’t be friends with coworkers, keep work and personal life separate.” Okay… then who exactly am I supposed to be friends with?

Am I just supposed to walk up to random people on the street and start talking? Sit alone in a coffee shop like some kind of NPC and hope someone approaches me? I don’t go to bars, I don’t drink, and at my age it feels like everyone already has their established circles.

Modern socializing just feels backwards. You’re told not to mix work and personal life, but also told to “put yourself out there” without any real explanation of what that even means in practice. Dating feels the same way—just cross your fingers and hope something magically works out.
