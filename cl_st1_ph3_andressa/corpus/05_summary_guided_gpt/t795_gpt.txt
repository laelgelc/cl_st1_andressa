I’m lying here in a hospital bed in New Zealand with a serious blood clot in my lung. The doctors keep telling me I’m lucky to be alive, but honestly I just feel disappointed it didn’t kill me.

I don’t have friends. It’s basically just my dad and my brother. My brother stopped in briefly but he’s busy with his own life, and I don’t blame him. Still, the room feels so empty.

I’m just here, hooked up to monitors, feeling incredibly lonely and wishing I wasn’t alive at all.
