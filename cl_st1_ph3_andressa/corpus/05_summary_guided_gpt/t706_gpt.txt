Listening to “Heroes” by David Bowie and I keep imagining dancing to it with someone. It still makes me feel lonely, but it’s different now. The wish for togetherness feels more warm and gentle, not like a sharp reminder of what I don’t have. I lean on my imagination a lot to cope, building little scenes in my head. When I stay really busy, it’s almost like I can forget what a hug feels like, what physical affection is like. It’s easier that way, but also kind of sad.
