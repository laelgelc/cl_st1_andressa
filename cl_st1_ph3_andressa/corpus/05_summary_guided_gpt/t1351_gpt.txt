Lately I’ve been feeling so lonely and left out, and I don’t really know what to do with it. My close friends all have partners, and whenever they’re going through something, they immediately go to their boyfriend/girlfriend and kind of shut the rest of us out. I get that it’s normal to lean on your partner, but it still stings.

I really value one-on-one time and I only have a small friend group, so when my friends are busy or emotionally checked out because they’re wrapped up in their relationships, it feels like I’m just…left with no one. Then I get jealous of what they have, and I hate that. I know it’s coming from my own insecurities, but I still feel mad and sad and incredibly lonely.

On top of that, I feel embarrassed about even wanting a relationship this badly. It feels like if you admit you’re single and lonely, people either make jokes or act like you’re pathetic. It’s like being lonely is something to laugh at, not something people actually take seriously, and that really hurts.
