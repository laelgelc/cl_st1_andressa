I’m 31 and I feel like I’m checking all the boxes I’m “supposed” to. I work out, I have hobbies, I try to take care of myself, I put effort into meeting new people and making friends. From the outside, it probably looks like I’m doing everything right. But inside, it all feels…flat. Like I’m going through the motions on mute.

The truth is, none of it feels truly fulfilling without being in love. It’s like a part of me is asleep and only really wakes up when I have a romantic connection. I know that sounds dramatic, but that’s how it feels. I refuse to lower my standards just to have someone, though. I don’t want just “any” relationship. I want something real, mutual, and healthy.

When I like someone, I give 100%. I have so much love to give, and it feels like it’s just sitting there, unused. People always say “love yourself first,” and I do try, but loving myself doesn’t scratch the same itch. It doesn’t fill the same space. It leaves me feeling like I’m somehow both too much and not enough at the same time.

I do believe I’ll find someone eventually, that my love won’t go to waste. But this in-between—this empty waiting—is so painful.

If you’re spending tonight alone too, I hope you feel some warmth and comfort, even if it’s just knowing someone else out here feels the same.
