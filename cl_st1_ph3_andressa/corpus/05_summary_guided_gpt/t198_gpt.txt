I feel like my whole “friend group” has been built on this one toxic person, and I’m finally at the point where I can’t keep doing it anymore.

I’ve only ever had two best friends in my life, plus my sister who’s always been included in the group. For about six years now, one of those friends has been hurting me, my sister, and even our other friend. Little comments, manipulation, drama, making people feel guilty… just constant negativity. It’s been slowly draining me.

I finally decided I was done and planned to end the friendship. The idea was that we’d all confront her together, because she’s hurt all of us. But my other best friend backed out. She didn’t want things to be “awkward” and basically chose to stay on the toxic friend’s side, or at least not stand up to her. She even skipped the confrontation completely.

Now I just feel like she can’t stand up for herself or for me, and it makes me not want to be in this friend group at all anymore. I’m only still hanging around because of my sister.

I feel awful and mean, but I also feel like I have to protect myself. It really does feel like “either me or her,” and I know that once I walk away, I’ll probably have no friends left. I’m bracing myself for that loneliness, but I don’t see another way.
