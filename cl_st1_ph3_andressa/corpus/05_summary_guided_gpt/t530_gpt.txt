It’s that time of year again and I feel like I’m falling apart all over. A year ago my boyfriend killed himself in front of me online. I still see it when I close my eyes. I keep thinking I should be “over it” by now, but I’m not. If anything, it feels heavier right now.

I’m close to graduating, and everyone around me seems to have some kind of plan, some direction, something they’re excited about. I don’t. I just lie in bed most days, scrolling or staring at the ceiling. I don’t really have hobbies anymore. I don’t know what I’m supposed to want from life. It’s like the future is just this blank wall.

My family doesn’t really know me. They shame me for being bi, so there’s no way I could ever tell them I’m also trans. I already know how that would go. So I just stay quiet and let them think whatever they want. It makes me feel like I’m living someone else’s life, not mine.

I feel so unwanted. I don’t feel attractive to women or men. I barely have any friends. The one person I was talking to recently just ghosted me, and it hit way harder than it should have. It just confirmed this feeling that I’m easy to walk away from.

The worst part is this person who used to be like a mother to me. I thought she was the best person in my life, the one who really cared… and then she stole from me. Now I have to see her every day, pretending everything is normal while I’m stuck wondering if she ever actually gave a shit about me at all.

I’ve tried to kill myself so many times. I honestly don’t see a way out that isn’t death. It really feels like no one cares whether I’m here or not.

Maybe I’m just yelling into the void. But I wanted to leave something behind, in case one day I actually go through with it. At least then there’ll be proof I was here, that I felt all of this.
