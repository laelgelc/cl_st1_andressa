I feel like I don’t really connect with anyone, at least not in the way I actually need. I’m always the one making sure everyone else is comfortable, entertained, having a good time. I play the listener, the planner, the “it’s fine, don’t worry about me” person. And I’m tired.

What I want feels so simple but impossible: a mutual, equal friendship. Someone who sees me as their “number one” best friend, and I see them the same way. Someone who actually understands this heavy, quiet loneliness and the pain that comes with it, and doesn’t just brush it off or change the subject.

I’m exhausted from doing everything alone, from hyping myself up just to get through another day. I want someone who “gets it” without me having to over-explain, who matches my energy and actually reciprocates.

I don’t really want another introvert. I’d love someone more ambiverted—who can enjoy going out and having fun, but is also okay with staying in and just existing together.
