I feel like my whole life has been about trying to survive alone. I grew up in an abusive, loveless, broken home and had to figure everything out by myself. Now, looking back, I’ve had three failed marriages and no children. The people I thought were my true friends are gone—some passed away, and others turned out not to be real friends at all.

Because of all this, I have serious trust issues and awful social skills. I avoid going out, I’m terrified of rejection, and that just makes me withdraw even more. The loneliness hits the hardest at night, right before bed. That’s when it feels like I’m just crying on the inside, and I’m so tired of feeling this way.
