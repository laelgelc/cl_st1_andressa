My phone has been dead silent lately. No messages, no calls, nothing. I’ll pick it up out of habit, hoping for some kind of notification, and it’s just… blank. Then I open social media and see people hanging out with their friends, going to parties, posting stories, and it just hits me how invisible I feel. It’s like my presence or absence doesn’t matter to anyone.

Most of my time is spent alone in my room. I watch movies by myself, sometimes even go to the cinema alone. Recently I got into F1 and I actually enjoy it a lot, but there’s no one I can talk to about it. Same with Star Wars or Marvel/DC – all these things I’m passionate about just stay in my head because I don’t have anyone who cares enough to listen or share it with me.

I keep jumping from one interest to another just to distract myself, but the second I’m not actively doing something, the loneliness comes rushing back. I really just want to feel important to someone. I want someone who genuinely wants to talk to me, who gets excited to hear from me, who actually asks me about the things I like.

I know part of this is my fault. I don’t reach out to people. I don’t talk to anyone in college – I just go to class and then go straight back to my room. It was kind of the same in school too; my friends didn’t really talk much to me back then either. So I’ve started having long conversations in my head instead, usually with this girl I liked but can’t bring myself to talk to in real life. It’s getting to the point where I feel like I’m losing my mind.

I see myself as boring. I’m not into parties or loud music. I don’t know how to be that “interesting” person people want to be around. I just feel like I’m here, alone, and no one would really notice if I disappeared.
