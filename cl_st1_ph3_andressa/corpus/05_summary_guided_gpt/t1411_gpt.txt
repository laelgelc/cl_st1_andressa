I’m 30, a single dad, and I feel like I’m already at the end of the road when it comes to finding “the one.” Everywhere I look, people are paired up—at church, my siblings with their partners, friends posting online—and I’m just…alone. I don’t even really have friends. Family only shows up if I’m the one constantly reaching out and planning things.

My job is lousy, and I never got an education. Honestly, I never thought I’d live this long. I was suicidal for a long time and didn’t plan for any kind of future, so now I feel stuck and behind everyone else.

People tell me I’m attractive, but it doesn’t matter. I’m miserable. I don’t want hookups or casual sex. I just want real companionship, affection, someone to come home to, and a chance to actually rest.
