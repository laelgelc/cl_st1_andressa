I’ve been feeling completely alone for over a year now and I honestly don’t know what to do anymore. I’m on this “gap year” after grad school, but it doesn’t feel like a break – it feels like being stuck in limbo. No job, no real group or community, nothing that feels like it’s mine. My family situation is really bad, so I can’t lean on them for support at all.

I can still meet up with a few friends for lunch or whatever, but it’s all surface-level. We talk about normal things and then go home. I can’t bring myself to be honest about how dark my thoughts actually are. It feels like if I said it out loud, it would scare them off or make everything weird.

On top of that, no jobs sound even remotely interesting to me. I don’t understand how people can enjoy work environments or find meaning there. I just feel this heavy dread when I think about it. I cry multiple times a day when I’m alone. I swing between feeling severely depressed and then having these short, almost random periods where I feel okay, and then it crashes again. It’s exhausting.

I keep thinking if I just had someone around me all the time, it would help, but I don’t have any close peers like that. I don’t know how to actually improve things in a real, lasting way. How do you even start?
