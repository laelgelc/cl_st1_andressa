Lately it’s been hitting me how intensely lonely I feel. My phone is just… quiet. No messages, no one checking in, no plans coming up. It’s like I’ve fallen off the map and no one noticed.

My brain keeps replaying every rejection, every awkward interaction, every time someone didn’t respond. I take those moments as “proof” that I’m not worth reaching out to, and then it gets even harder to try again. It feels like I’m slowly disappearing in real time.

Weirdly, admitting to myself how much this actually hurts has been the first small step in breaking the cycle a bit. Just saying “this is painful” instead of pretending I’m fine.
