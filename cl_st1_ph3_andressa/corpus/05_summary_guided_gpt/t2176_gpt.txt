Lately I’ve been replaying a lot of my big life choices in my head and wondering if I messed everything up by just… taking what came along. I got married, took the job that was offered, made these huge decisions without really stopping to ask if they were what I truly wanted or if I was just afraid to wait.

Now I feel kind of hollow and restless, like I’m living a life that just “happened” to me instead of one I chose. I’m discontent but also scared to change anything, because what if I make it worse? It’s like I’m standing in the middle of my own life and I don’t recognize it.

I don’t know where I stand, or if this is just a phase that will pass, or if things will ever actually feel okay.
