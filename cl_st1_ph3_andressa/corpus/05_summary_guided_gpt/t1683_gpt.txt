I’m 24 and I feel like my whole life has been lived in a bubble I never chose. My parents were so overprotective that I basically grew up without friends. No sleepovers, no hanging out after school, barely any contact with people my age. Now I’m an adult with no real-life friendship experience and almost no social skills, and it feels like I was never allowed to even try.

I still live at home because my parents are old and constantly sick. The house feels more like a hospital than a home. My mom unloads all her worries and problems on me, and every time I try to think about leaving or changing something, she shuts it down. I’m starting to resent her for it, but then I feel guilty because they did give me food, shelter, all the “basics.”

My hobbies, my ambitions, everything I used to care about has just…faded. I’m ashamed of where I live, ashamed of how behind I am, and I’m so, so lonely. I don’t want advice, I just needed to say this somewhere.
