I’m so exhausted by all of this. I come home to my empty, silent apartment and it’s like the quiet is screaming at me. It feels like I’ve been forgotten, like I’m just this unwanted presence people tolerate at best. Most of the time I feel like an eyesore, something people look past or look at with a bit of disgust. I hate feeling like an inconvenience just for existing.

It’s not just in my head either; my body feels it. I barely sleep, I clench my teeth all night, and every morning I have to drag myself out of bed and put on this act like everything is fine. I’m so tired of my life, tired of being me. The whole thing feels surreal, like I’m watching someone else’s miserable life from the outside.
