I spend pretty much every day taking care of everyone else – my parents, extended family, work. I’m the one who picks up the slack, carries the emotional and practical burdens, and it feels like I don’t really have a choice. It’s just expected of me.

Now I’m in my late 30s, no house, no kids, and my partner of two years wants those things. I don’t blame them, but it makes me feel even more stuck, like I’m failing at both my own life and everyone else’s expectations.

Any time I show weakness or say I’m struggling, I get told that I’m the problem, that I’m the reason for my own misery. After hearing that enough, I’ve just started bottling everything up. I don’t really talk about how burned out I am anymore, because it never seems safe.

I feel really alone with all of this. If you read this, I hope your day is at least a little productive and gentle on you.
