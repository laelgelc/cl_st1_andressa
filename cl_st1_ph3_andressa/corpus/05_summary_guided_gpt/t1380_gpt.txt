I’m 20 and technically I’ve had friends, but I’ve never felt that real, deep love or connection people talk about. I can be in a group, laughing and joking, and still feel completely alone, like I’m just playing a role.

I’ve got depression, anxiety, I’m introverted, and my social skills aren’t great, so I already feel behind. But even if I magically fixed all of that, I’m not sure I’d be satisfied. What I want feels… too much? I crave this intense, almost self-sacrificial bond where you’d both really show up for each other no matter what. Not just “we hang out sometimes” but something truly deep.

What really messes with my head is how people act when they’re angry. I’ve seen people, including someone I started to love, suddenly belittle and attack just to “win” an argument. It’s like all the care and respect disappears the second they’re upset. That makes me feel like all these relationships are thinner than I thought, like they can just flip.

It leaves me confused and resentful, wondering if I’m just naive about human nature, or if I’m doomed to never find the kind of connection I’m looking for.
