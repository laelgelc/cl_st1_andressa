I’m starting to feel a little weird about how “okay” I am with being alone.

I live by myself in a small town in North Dakota, decent salary, comfy two-bedroom apartment. My routine is pretty predictable: 9–5 job, gym after work, then I cook dinner and watch movies or TV. Weekends I bike on a local trail and catch new movies in theaters. It’s simple, but I genuinely like it.

Outside of my family and coworkers, I don’t really talk to anyone. No real friends here, no dating. My parents are pretty worried and keep telling me I should “get out there,” make friends, maybe try dating apps. But honestly, I’m happy. I enjoy my own space, and as an introvert, socializing and dating just feel exhausting.

Is it actually a problem if I’m content living like this?
