I’m 21 and a senior in college, and I feel like I somehow ended up completely alone again.

I transferred here junior year and started with literally zero friends. It sucked, but over time I actually built a really close friend group. Then I studied abroad and made even more friends there. For a while I felt like I finally had a social life.

When I came back, everything kind of fell apart. I couldn’t dorm with my old friends, and now I rarely see or talk to them. My main friend from abroad barely responds to my messages. My current roommates are polite but distant, like we’re just strangers sharing a space. In class, people will literally choose to sit anywhere but next to me.

I take care of myself, I try to look approachable and be friendly, but it feels like no one wants to know me. I keep wondering if I’m just too boring, or unattractive, or there’s something else wrong with me that I can’t see.

I haven’t had a girlfriend since I was 15. I haven’t even hugged anyone in years. It feels like no matter what I do, I end up back in the same place: alone.
