I feel like I built my entire life around one man and now that he’s gone, there’s nothing left of me.

I gave up everything for him, and now I’m looking around at my life and it’s just…empty. No friends. No hobbies. No job. No dreams or plans. I don’t even know what I’m good at anymore, or if I’m good at anything at all. It’s like I erased myself to fit into his life, and when he left, he took everything with him.

He still has friends, family, goals, things to look forward to. I have no marriage, no kids, no education, no family to fall back on—not even a mom I can call and cry to. It’s just me, and this huge, suffocating silence.

I don’t know what to do next or where to even start.
