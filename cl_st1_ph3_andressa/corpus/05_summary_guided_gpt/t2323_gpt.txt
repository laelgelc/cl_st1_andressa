I feel like that quiet person in the corner of the classroom who’s too scared to even look at their crush, not because of shyness exactly, but because I don’t want them to realize I have no friends. Between classes I just sit in the university library all day, pretending to study, but really I’m just killing time and overthinking.  

I keep wondering how people actually see someone like me. Do classmates and strangers think I’m weird? Pathetic? Invisible? I wish I could know honestly, even if the truth is harsh. Not knowing feels worse.
