My work announced it was closing back in 2019, and it’s just been this slow-motion car crash ever since. I’ve applied for close to 300 jobs. Ten interviews. Ten rejections. Got the latest one last night. At this point I’m pretty much just waiting for the axe to fall by the end of the year, and it feels like the clock is ticking down on my life, not just my job.

Tonight I bought three bottles of liquor. I’ve already gone through one and a half. I’m very drunk right now and, honestly, this is the best I’ve felt since I found out I’m losing my job. That probably says everything.

I can’t shake the feeling that there’s something fundamentally wrong with me. Other people manage to find work. I send out application after application and it’s just silence or “we went with another candidate.” It makes me feel completely worthless and broken. I hate myself. I hate my life. I hate waking up in the morning because it means I didn’t die in my sleep.

I still have more alcohol sitting here and I don’t know if I’m going to stop before it’s all gone tonight. I’m not even sure I care. I just feel done.
