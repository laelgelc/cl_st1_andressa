I’m 16 and my relationship with my parents is awful. We scream, we say horrible things, sometimes it even gets physical. After our last fight I locked myself in the bathroom, sat in the tub and just cried. I scrolled through my contacts and realized there was literally no one I could call or text. In those moments I want my parents, but I also think they’re not good people. That emptiness, that loneliness, feels worse than the fights themselves.
