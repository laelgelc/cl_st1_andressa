I just watched “Les Olympiades” and it really messed me up emotionally. Romance is such a raw topic for me, and I probably shouldn’t have picked a romantic movie, but I did anyway. One of the actresses reminded me so much of someone I had a “situationship” with years ago. It took me years to get over that whole thing, and even now, more than five years later, it still feels like this open wound I never really healed.

To make it worse, I found out that actress has the exact same birth date as me. It’s such a weird, almost cruel coincidence, and it just pulled me even deeper into those old feelings. Normally my meds kind of blunt this stuff, but tonight it all cut through. I suddenly feel so lonely, like painfully aware that I have no one to talk to about any of this.

I keep thinking how nice it would be to talk to a therapist, or a really close friend, or, in some ideal world, a girlfriend who actually cared and listened. But the thought of just chatting with strangers, small talk or surface-level stuff, feels pointless. I don’t want to paste on a fake smile and pretend I’m okay.

It’s late, I’ve got a candle burning, and all I can really think to do is journal for a bit and then try to sleep. Today already felt like a string of failures, one after another, and this just kind of capped it off. I’m hoping that getting some of this out on paper, and here, might help even a little.
