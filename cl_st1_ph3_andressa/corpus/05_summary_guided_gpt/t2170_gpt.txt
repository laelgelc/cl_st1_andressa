I’ve been feeling lonely for about 4–5 years now, and it just doesn’t seem to get better. In big crowds I feel like an outcast, like I’m standing on the outside of a glass wall watching everyone else connect and belong. I want so badly to fit in, to have someone just come up and say hi, to feel noticed. I wish I had at least one friend I could lean on when my thoughts and the weight of life get too overwhelming.
