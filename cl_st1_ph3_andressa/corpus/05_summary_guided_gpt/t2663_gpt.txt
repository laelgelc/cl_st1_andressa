I’ve always felt like a loner, even when I technically had friends. In school I was part of small friend groups, but I never truly felt like I fit in anywhere. Being around people for too long just drains me. After a while I start getting irritated or finding everyone annoying, even when they haven’t really done anything wrong.

I remember turning 16 and my friends organized a birthday party for me. I was supposed to feel happy and grateful, but all I could think was that I’d rather be at home alone watching a movie. That moment really cemented this feeling that I’m just a loner at my core.
