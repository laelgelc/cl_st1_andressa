I feel like my face is a curse. Every time I catch my reflection, all I feel is rage and disgust. It honestly feels like my appearance pushes people away. Even my friends seem uncomfortable, like they’re forcing themselves to look at me or talk to me, and I can feel it in every interaction.

I’m a big person physically, but somehow I’m still invisible. People look past me, around me, through me. It’s like I take up space but don’t actually exist.

I wish I could just tear my face off, start over, be someone else. I just want someone to really see me, not just my ugliness, and treat me like I matter.
