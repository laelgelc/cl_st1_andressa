I’ve been feeling like this for years and I’ve never really talked to anyone about it. It’s not just loneliness, it’s this deep sense of being useless, like I’m just taking up space. I procrastinate on everything, even simple tasks, and nothing feels exciting anymore. After seeing so many things and people, it’s like the small stuff that’s supposed to bring joy just… doesn’t.

I’m tired all the time, mentally and physically. I’m underweight for my age and height, and that just makes me feel weaker and more drained. Inspirational books, videos, quotes – none of that helps. I might feel a tiny spark for a moment, but it disappears so fast.

I keep trying to understand myself and believe in myself, but I always lose that feeling. I feel stuck, sad, forgetful, and honestly just bad at everything. I don’t know what I like, what I’m good at, or what I want to do. I feel like I’m just alive, not really living.
