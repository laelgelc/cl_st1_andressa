I’m 22 and I honestly just feel like a complete loser most days. I can’t seem to make friends, and when it comes to women I just freeze. The second one shows even a bit of interest, I start overthinking everything and end up sabotaging it in my head before anything can even happen. I already feel like I look older than I am, and I keep thinking I’ll never “find the one,” even though there’s this tiny voice in the back of my mind saying maybe I will someday.

My life is basically: college, work, home, video games. That’s it. The only time I really go out is to see a movie or walk around the park. It feels like everyone else my age is living some kind of actual life and I’m just stuck in this loop.

The few in-person friends I had are drifting away—moving, getting serious about their careers, getting into relationships. I don’t blame them, but it hurts. I thought I’d at least have my online friends, but even there I feel like I’m not really seen as a “real” friend, even though we’ve known each other for years and I’ve actually visited some of them. It’s like I’m always the extra, the one people talk to when no one else is around.

What really gets to me is watching people who are honestly kind of assholes have no problem finding friends, relationships, attention. Meanwhile I’m sitting here wondering what’s so wrong with me that I can’t find a single person who genuinely cares.

Sometimes I just wish I could turn my feelings off. Feeling nothing seems like it would be easier than feeling this lonely and miserable every single day.
