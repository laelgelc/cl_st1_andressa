It feels like my friends are slowly drifting away and I don’t even know when it started. I’ve been lonely for so long now that staying inside has become my default, and I rarely leave the house anymore. I used to have this “spark,” some kind of energy or light, and I’ve been trying for years to get it back, but it just won’t come. It makes me so angry and upset.
