If you feel like you don’t belong anywhere or have nowhere to go, I really suggest just… going out into the world around you, especially your own city. Walk to places you’ve never been, or even familiar spots, but actually be there. Try not to hide behind your phone or headphones the whole time; I’ve noticed that just makes the loneliness louder. When you’re present, you notice people, and they notice you. If you’re intentional and consistent about going out and engaging with your environment, you really can end up meeting a lot of new people.
