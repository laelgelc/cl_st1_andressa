Lately it feels like I’m disappearing while still being physically here. I go through my day doing all the normal things—work, errands, small talk—but none of it feels connected to anyone. People know my name, but it’s like they only know the version of me that smiles and says “I’m good, you?” on autopilot.

I scroll through chats and realize most conversations are just logistics or memes. No one really asks how I am, and when they do, I panic and say “fine” because I don’t know how to explain this heavy emptiness without sounding dramatic.

I’m surrounded by people who seem to already have their circles, their “person,” their group chats buzzing nonstop. I’m always the one on the outside, invited sometimes but never essential. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like a ghost standing in the corner, watching everyone else live.
