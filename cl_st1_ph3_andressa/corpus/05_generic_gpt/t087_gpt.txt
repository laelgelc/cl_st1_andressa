I don’t really know how to talk about this in real life, so I’m dumping it here.

Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it. I go to work, I’m polite, I crack small jokes, people seem to like me “enough,” but when I come home and close the door it’s like I stop existing for everyone else. My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from an app. Group chats keep going whether I say anything or not. If I disappear for a few days, nobody seems to notice.

The weirdest part is I’m not technically “alone.” I’m surrounded by people at work, in public, online. I can hold a conversation, I can be social when I need to be. But I don’t feel known by anyone. The things that actually matter to me just stay in my head because I don’t know who would care enough to hear them.

I’ve tried joining clubs, talking more, saying yes to invitations. I always end up feeling like an extra in other people’s stories, the person who’s there to fill a seat or listen, but not someone anyone would choose as their first call.

I’m starting to wonder if this is just how my life is going to feel: present but not really connected, visible but not really seen.
