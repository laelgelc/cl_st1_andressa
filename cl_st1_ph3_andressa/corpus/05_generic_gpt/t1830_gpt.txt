I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my family group chats, but it all feels like background noise. I can talk about surface stuff just fine, but the second I try to say anything real, it’s like I freeze.  

I watch other people form friendships so easily, making plans, inside jokes, group photos. I’m always the person just outside the frame, the “nice” one, the “reliable” one, but never the one anyone chooses first.  

What messes with me most is how normal I look from the outside. No one would guess how heavy the silence feels when I finally get home and close the door.
