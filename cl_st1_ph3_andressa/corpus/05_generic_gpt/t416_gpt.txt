I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I make small talk with, but no one I’d actually call if I was falling apart. Most of my conversations feel like I’m playing a role: “I’m fine, work is busy, yeah the weather’s weird.” Then I go home, sit in my room, and it’s like the silence gets heavier every night.

I scroll through my phone hoping someone will message first, but it almost never happens. When I try to reach out, I overthink every word and usually delete the message. It’s embarrassing how needy I feel for something as simple as a genuine “How are you?” that isn’t just politeness.
