I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. Friends, coworkers, family—I can talk to them, joke around, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role. I keep everything important to myself because I’m scared of being misunderstood or dismissed. Nights are the worst; I scroll endlessly, hoping someone will notice me online, but conversations fade quickly. It’s like I’m visible enough to interact with, but not enough to actually know.
