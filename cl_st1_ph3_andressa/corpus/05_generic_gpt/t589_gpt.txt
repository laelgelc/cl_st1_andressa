I’m starting to feel like I don’t exist outside of other people’s convenience. Friends text when they’re bored, not because they’re thinking of me. If I stop reaching out, everything just goes quiet. Days blur together: work, home, scrolling, sleep. I’m surrounded by people but somehow I’m never really “with” anyone. 

I try to be funny, helpful, easygoing—anything to make myself worth keeping around—but it never seems to stick. People move on, get busy, pair off, and I’m left hovering on the edge of their lives. It’s embarrassing how much I crave a simple, unprompted “How are you really?” and how rarely it comes.
