I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly fading out of everyone’s life and no one noticed when it started.

I’m in this weird place where I’m “known” by a lot of people but not really known by anyone. I have coworkers, classmates, old friends on social media. People say hi, we crack jokes, sometimes we even hang out. But when something actually hurts, I open my phone and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging. Or I type something out and delete it because I don’t want to be “too much.”

What makes it worse is that from the outside my life probably looks fine. I function. I show up. I smile. So when I try to hint that I’m lonely, people brush it off like I’m exaggerating. It makes me question if I’m the problem, like maybe I’m just fundamentally hard to care about.

Most days I just go home, scroll, eat, sleep, repeat. I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be genuinely seen.
