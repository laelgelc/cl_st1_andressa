I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. I watch everyone else move through life in these tight little circles, and I’m just orbiting around them. I try to initiate plans and it fizzles out, or I overthink every message and end up sending nothing. It feels like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s lives, including my own.
