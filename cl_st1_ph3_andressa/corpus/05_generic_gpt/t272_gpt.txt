I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely. My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. I’ll scroll through chats and see conversations that died months ago and feel too awkward to revive them. 

I go to work, make small talk, come home, and then it’s just…silence. I try to fill it with YouTube, podcasts, anything, but as soon as I pause them the emptiness feels loud again. 

What messes with me most is that from the outside I probably look fine. I have a job, I’m not totally isolated, but I don’t feel like I’m important to anyone in particular. More like background noise in other people’s lives.
