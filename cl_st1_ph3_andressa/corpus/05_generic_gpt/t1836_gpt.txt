I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I joke with or update about work and random stuff, but nobody I can text when I’m falling apart at 2 a.m. I watch everyone else make plans, move in together, get married, have kids, and I’m just… orbiting around their lives, never really part of anything. I’m scared this is just how it’s going to be: always “nice to see you,” never “I’m glad you’re here.”
