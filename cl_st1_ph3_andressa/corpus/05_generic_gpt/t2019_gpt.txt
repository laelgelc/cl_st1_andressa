I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s a bit terrifying. My days look full on paper—work, errands, small talk—but nobody really knows me. I have people to message, but I hesitate because I feel like an intrusion, like I’m always “too much” or “not enough.” Even when I’m with others, it feels like I’m watching life through glass. I miss a version of connection I’ve never actually had, and I don’t know how to bridge that gap.
