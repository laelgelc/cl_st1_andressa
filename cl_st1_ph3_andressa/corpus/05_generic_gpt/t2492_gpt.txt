I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I message sometimes, but it all feels thin, like I’m play-acting a normal life. I overthink every interaction: “Did I say too much? Not enough?” Then I retreat and wait for others to reach out, which they rarely do. I’m surrounded by people yet feel weirdly invisible, like I could disappear and only a few notifications would slow down.
