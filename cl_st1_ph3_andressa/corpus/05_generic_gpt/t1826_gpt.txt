I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family—but I don’t feel genuinely known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, random memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and actually wanted the full answer.  

I’ve tried joining groups, talking more, even forcing myself to be “interesting,” but it all feels like an act. When I go home, it’s just silence and the sense that if I disappeared, my absence would be more of an inconvenience than a loss.
