I don’t know when “being alone a lot” quietly turned into “being lonely all the time,” but it has. I’m surrounded by people at work, I make small talk, I laugh at the right moments, and then I go home and it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move forward. I don’t really have anyone I can message without feeling like I’m bothering them. It’s like I’m a background character in my own life, and I’m starting to get used to it.
