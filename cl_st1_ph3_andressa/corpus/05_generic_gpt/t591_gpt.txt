I’m starting to think I’ve forgotten how to be close to people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I go entire weekends without speaking out loud, except to cashiers. When something good or bad happens, I open my phone to share it and then realize there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging. I keep wondering if I missed some crucial “how to make friends” stage in life. It’s like I’m present, but not really part of anything.
