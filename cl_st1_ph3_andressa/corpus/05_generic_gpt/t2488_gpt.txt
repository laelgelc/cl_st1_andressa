I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I’m surrounded by people on paper—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but none of it feels like real connection. I go through the motions: small talk about the weekend, jokes about work, sending “lol” in group chats. Then I go home, shut the door, and it’s like everything goes silent at once.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels. From the outside I probably look “fine.” I show up, meet deadlines, remember birthdays. Inside, it feels like nobody actually knows me beyond the surface. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and actually wanted the honest answer.

I’ve tried to reach out more, but I always end up feeling like I’m intruding or forcing it. I overthink every message: Am I being annoying? Too needy? Not interesting enough? So I pull back, then feel even more isolated, and the cycle just keeps repeating.

Sometimes I wonder if this is just how my life is going to be: physically present, socially functional, but emotionally alone. I don’t even know what I’m asking for here. Maybe I just needed to say it somewhere.
