I’ve been feeling like I’m stuck behind glass lately. I’m technically “around people” a lot—work, family chats, random group messages—but it all feels weirdly distant, like I’m just playing a role and not actually there.

I keep trying to open up in small ways, but conversations slide back to surface stuff: work, shows, memes. I leave feeling more drained and somehow more alone than before. It’s like everyone else is plugged into something I can’t access.

I miss having someone who actually knows me, not just the version I present. Someone I can message without overthinking every word, or sit next to in silence without it feeling awkward.

Most days I just distract myself with screens until I’m tired enough to sleep. Then I wake up and repeat it, hoping something will shift, but not really expecting it to.
