I’m starting to realize I’m not sure what “having friends” even feels like anymore. I have people I talk to sometimes, coworkers I joke with, family I text, but no one who actually knows me. I scroll through my phone at night and there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging without overthinking it. I keep replaying old conversations, wondering if I was too quiet, too awkward, too much. 

Weekends are the worst. Everyone seems busy with their own circles and I’m just… extra. I’m tired of being the person people only remember when they need something, never the one they choose first.
