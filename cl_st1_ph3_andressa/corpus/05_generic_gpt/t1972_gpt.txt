I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go to work, come home, scroll through my phone, and that’s basically it. I have acquaintances, but not the kind of friends you can text at 2 a.m. when everything feels too heavy. When I do talk to people, I feel like I’m performing a version of myself that’s easier to like, and it just makes me feel more disconnected. It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely unseen.
