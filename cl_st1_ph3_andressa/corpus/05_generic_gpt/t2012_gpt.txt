I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing while still being physically here. I talk to people at work, I reply in group chats, I send memes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who’s loosely based on me. Nobody really knows what my days look like when I go home, or how quiet my apartment is. I used to think I just hadn’t met “my people” yet, but now I’m wondering if I’ve forgotten how to actually connect, or if I ever knew in the first place.
