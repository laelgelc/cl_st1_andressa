I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people every day, but it feels like I’m behind glass. I laugh at the right times, say the right things, and no one seems to notice that it’s all kind of scripted.

What gets me is how long it’s been since I’ve had a genuinely vulnerable conversation with someone offline. Not small talk, not “how’s work,” but the kind where you say something ugly and the other person doesn’t flinch. I miss that, even though I’m not sure I’ve ever really had it.

I keep thinking I must be the common denominator. Everyone else seems to eventually find their people. I’m just stuck in this loop of being “fine” on the outside and hollow on the inside.
