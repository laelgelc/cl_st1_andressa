I’m starting to realize I’m not “busy and independent,” I’m just alone all the time.

My phone barely buzzes unless it’s spam or a delivery notification. Group chats move on without me; I’ll say something and it just dies there. When I try to reach out one-on-one, people are “swamped” or “we should totally hang sometime” and then nothing. I can’t tell if I’m boring, awkward, or just easy to forget.

Nights are the worst. I finish work, eat, scroll, and there’s no one who’d notice if I disappeared from every app tomorrow. I’ve tried hobbies, meetups, even online communities, but everything feels surface-level, like I’m just passing through other people’s lives as background noise.
