I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. In groups, I’m there, I talk, I laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that everyone likes just enough to forget about once they go home.

What gets to me most is how my phone stays silent unless I’m the one starting the conversation. People answer, they’re not rude, but no one seems to think of me first. I’m always the “oh yeah, you can come too” person, never the reason plans are made.

I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong. Am I too quiet? Too boring? Too needy? I try to be easy to be around, not complain, not overshare, but then I end up feeling like no one actually knows me. When I do try to open up, it feels like I’ve made things awkward, like I broke some unspoken rule.

Nights are the worst. That’s when it hits me that if I disappeared from social media and stopped reaching out, it might take a long time for anyone to notice in a real way, not just a “hey, you good?” afterthought.
