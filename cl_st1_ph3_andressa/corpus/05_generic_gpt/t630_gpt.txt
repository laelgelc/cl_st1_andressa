I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to be around them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I message sometimes, but no one who really knows me. I keep conversations light because I’m scared if I show more, they’ll disappear, but then I feel invisible anyway. Nights are the worst—phone in hand, no one to text without feeling like I’m bothering them. It’s like I’m permanently on the outside looking in.
