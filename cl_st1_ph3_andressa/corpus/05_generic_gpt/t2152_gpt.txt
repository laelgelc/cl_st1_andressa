I’m starting to feel like I’ve aged out of belonging anywhere. Friends drifted into marriages, kids, careers, and I’m stuck in this weird limbo where no one has time, but I somehow have too much. I scroll through chats I’m barely part of, hovering over the text box, then closing it. I’m not isolated enough to be a complete hermit, but not connected enough to feel wanted. It’s like living in the margins of everyone else’s lives.
