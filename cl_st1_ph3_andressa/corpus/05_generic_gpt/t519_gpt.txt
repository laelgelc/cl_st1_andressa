I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, even when I’m physically around them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with about nothing, but I don’t feel known by anyone. When I try to open up, it feels awkward and forced, like I’m performing “being vulnerable” instead of actually being it. 

Nights are the worst. My phone is silent unless I start the conversation, and even then, replies feel delayed or polite. I replay old interactions in my head, wondering if I was too much, too quiet, too weird. I’m tired of being the one who reaches out first, but if I stop, everything just goes still. 

It’s a strange feeling: surrounded by people, yet somehow completely outside of everyone’s life.
