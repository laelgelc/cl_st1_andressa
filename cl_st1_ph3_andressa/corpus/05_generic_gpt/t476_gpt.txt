I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but it all feels like acting. I go home and there’s no one who really knows me, no one to message just because I saw something funny. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen. I’m not exactly unhappy, just… hollow and disconnected, like I’m watching my own life from the outside.
