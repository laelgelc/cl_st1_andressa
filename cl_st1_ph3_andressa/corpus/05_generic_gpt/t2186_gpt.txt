I’ve hit this weird point where I’m constantly surrounded by people and still feel like I’m on mute. I have coworkers, acquaintances, family chats buzzing, but no one I can actually text, “hey, I’m not okay,” without feeling like I’m burdening them or overstepping.

When I’m in a group, I feel like an extra. I laugh, I nod, I say the right things, but it’s like I’m playing a role instead of being a person. When I get home, the silence feels heavy, not peaceful.

I’ve tried hobbies, social apps, even forcing myself to say yes to invitations. I come back feeling more disconnected, like everyone else got a script for life that I somehow missed. I’m starting to feel less “alone” and more “unreachable,” even to myself.
