I’ve been feeling like I’m stuck in this weird in‑between place where I’m not exactly isolated, but I still feel completely alone.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about work, shows, news, whatever. I can send memes and get laughs, answer “how are you?” with “I’m fine” and move on. But I don’t feel like I exist in anyone’s inner circle. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to bridge that gap without feeling needy or awkward.

When I’m home, the silence feels heavy. I scroll through my phone hoping someone will message first, then feel stupid for caring so much. I try to initiate plans sometimes, but when people are “busy” a few times in a row, I start assuming I’m just not that important to them.

It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re on the outside of everything, watching life happen through a window you can’t open.
