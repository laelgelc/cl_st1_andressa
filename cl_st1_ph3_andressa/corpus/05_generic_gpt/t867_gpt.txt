I feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life. I go through the motions—work, commute, groceries—but it’s like I’m just passing by other people’s stories, never really in one. I have acquaintances, people I chat with, but no one who actually knows me in a way that feels solid or safe.

What bothers me most is how loneliness changes the way I see myself. I used to think I was interesting, or at least worth getting to know. Now I second-guess everything I say, convinced I’m just background noise. I scroll through group photos and inside jokes online and it feels like I’m staring at a world that decided I wasn’t needed.

I’m tired of talking “around” how I feel in real life, pretending I’m fine because I don’t want to make anyone uncomfortable. Here, at least, I can say it plainly: I’m really, really lonely.
