I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about surface-level stuff, but it all feels like acting. When I’m around others I feel invisible, and when I’m alone I feel like I’m disappearing. I watch people my age building lives, relationships, inside jokes, shared histories, and I’m just… not in anyone’s story. I keep telling myself I’ll try harder, be more open, but when the moment comes I freeze or make excuses. It’s like I’m stuck behind glass, watching life happen to everyone but me.
