I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I don’t actually exist to anyone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me beyond the surface. I go through entire days without a single genuine conversation. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate before reaching for my phone because I don’t know who I’d even text. I keep telling myself I’m “used to it,” but it still hits hard at night, when everything is quiet and there’s nothing left to distract me.
