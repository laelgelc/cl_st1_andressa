I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep, repeat. I talk to people every day, but it’s all surface-level: “How was your weekend?” “Good, yours?” Then silence. No one really knows what’s going on with me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.

What hurts most is when I try to reach out and it fizzles. Messages left on read, plans that never happen. I start wondering if I’m just… forgettable. Like if I stopped trying, nothing in anyone’s life would actually change.
