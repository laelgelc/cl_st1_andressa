I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I have friends on paper, group chats that light up, coworkers who say “we should hang out sometime,” but when I’m actually around them I feel like I’m watching myself from the outside, improvising a version of me that they might like.

I go home afterward and replay every sentence I said, every pause, every awkward laugh. It’s exhausting. I’m terrified that if I stop performing, there’ll be nothing underneath that anyone would want to stay for.

Most days I’m not even sure what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I was. I’m lonely, but also weirdly afraid of being seen.
