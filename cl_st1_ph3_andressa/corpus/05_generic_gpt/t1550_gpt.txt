I’m starting to realize that I’m not just “between friends” or “in a busy phase.” I’m actually lonely, in a way that feels like it’s baked into my life now.

I have people around me—coworkers, family chats, the occasional group outing—but it all feels like I’m acting a part. I can make small talk, crack jokes, seem “fine,” but no one really knows what my days feel like when I go home and the door closes.

The silence at night is the worst. I keep the TV or podcasts on just to hear other people’s voices, but it just highlights that none of them are talking to me. I scroll through social media and see people casually hanging out, texting in group chats, having inside jokes. I can’t remember the last time someone texted me first just to talk.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just wanted to put this somewhere instead of letting it echo around in my head.
