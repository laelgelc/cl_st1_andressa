I feel like I’m living in spectator mode. I go to work, I chat with coworkers, I even crack jokes sometimes, and yet the second I’m alone again it’s like none of it counted. It all feels like acting, like I’m playing the role of a person who’s “fine.”  

I scroll through my phone wanting to reach out, but I stare at the names and realize I don’t actually know who I’m close enough to message without it feeling weird. I miss having someone who really knows me, not the curated version.  

Mostly I’m just tired of feeling this invisible gap between me and everyone else.
