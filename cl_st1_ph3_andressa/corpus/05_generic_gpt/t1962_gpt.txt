I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. Conversations feel scripted: work, weather, “how was your weekend?” I answer, they answer, but nothing actually lands. I don’t think anyone really knows me, and I’m not sure I’ve given them the chance.

I cancel plans because I’m tired, then feel even worse sitting alone at home. I scroll through chats that are mostly dead, wondering when I quietly became the “optional” friend. When people do include me, I feel like a guest star in their lives, not someone essential.

What scares me most is how normal this has started to feel. I go through the motions—work, chores, small talk—but underneath is this constant dull ache, like I’m fading out of focus while everyone else keeps moving forward.
