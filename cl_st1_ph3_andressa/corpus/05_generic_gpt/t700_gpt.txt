I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life without anyone really noticing.

I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work, but no one who actually knows me. When something good or bad happens, I have to decide whether it’s “big enough” to bother anyone with, and usually I just don’t. Then days go by where my phone doesn’t light up unless it’s a notification from an app.

I keep thinking it’s my fault: maybe I’m too quiet, too boring, too… something. I try to initiate plans, but people are always busy, or they cancel last minute. After a while you stop asking.

It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely separate from all of them.
