I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work, shows, memes. But it all feels like I’m acting out a script I memorized years ago. When conversations end, there’s this weird emptiness, like nothing real happened. I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live.

I miss having someone who actually knows me, not just the version I keep polished for public use. I censor my thoughts because I’m scared they’ll sound too intense, too needy, too weird. So I stay on the surface, then get upset that nobody sees what’s underneath.

Nights are the worst. That’s when it hits that nobody is going to text first, nobody is wondering how my day went. It’s like I’ve slowly faded out of everyone’s “important” list and just… stayed faded.
