I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside lately. I go to work, say the right polite things, look busy, come home, and then it’s just silence. Not peaceful silence, more like the kind that makes you suddenly aware of how loud the fridge is.

I have people I “know,” but no one who really knows me. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, TV. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I was and actually wanted a real answer.

I keep thinking maybe I missed some critical stage where people learned how to form close friendships. Everyone else seems to have group chats, plans, people to text when something small or stupid happens. I notice I reach for my phone, then put it down because there’s no one I’d message who wouldn’t find it weird.
