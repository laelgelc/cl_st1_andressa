I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but nothing feels connected to anyone. Friends drifted away slowly, no big fights, just fewer messages until it was silence. I keep wanting to reach out, then convincing myself I’d be bothering them. Even when I’m around people, I feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else know how to belong while I’m stuck on the outside.
