I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. I can go days without anyone asking me how I really am, and if they did, I’m not even sure I’d know what to say beyond “I’m fine.” I’m not fine. I’m just… there.

What bothers me most is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped reaching out, conversations just die. No one checks in. It’s like I’m only part of others’ lives when I’m the one doing the work to keep the connection alive.

I miss feeling chosen. I miss the comfort of knowing someone is genuinely glad I exist. Lately I’ve started to wonder if I’m just not the kind of person people ever truly attach to.
