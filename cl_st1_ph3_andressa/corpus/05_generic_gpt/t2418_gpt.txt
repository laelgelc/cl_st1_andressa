I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work or small stuff, but it all feels like acting. When the conversation ends, it’s like I disappear again.

I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging without overthinking it. If I do reach out, I spend the next hour rereading what I wrote, convinced I sounded weird or annoying. So I usually just don’t.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels from the outside. I show up, I smile, I make jokes, and everyone assumes I’m fine. But the second I’m alone, it’s like the silence is screaming at me. Even when I’m around people, I feel like I’m watching life through glass.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just needed to say this somewhere.
