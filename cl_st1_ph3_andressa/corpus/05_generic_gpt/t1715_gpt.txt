I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people in a way that lasts.

On paper, my life doesn’t look that empty. I have coworkers, a few acquaintances I’ll grab a drink with, a family group chat that pings sometimes. But everything feels…surface-level. I can talk about work, memes, the weather, but the moment it gets deeper I freeze up or make a joke and change the subject.

I keep thinking how no one really knows me. Not the stuff that actually matters, like what I’m afraid of, what I want, or how often I feel like I’m just watching my own life from the outside. I’m “easygoing” to most people, but it’s because I rarely say what I actually think or need.

Nights are the worst. I’ll scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, then convincing myself I’d just be bothering them. So I put it down, stare at the ceiling, and tell myself I’ll try again “another time,” even though I never do.
