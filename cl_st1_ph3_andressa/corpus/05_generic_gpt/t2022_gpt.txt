I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something truly went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of them are old numbers I’m too embarrassed to message now. I keep telling myself I should “put myself out there,” but I’m tired, and the idea of being the one to initiate every conversation just makes me feel even more unwanted.
