I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people most days—work, public transport, neighbors—but it all feels like walking through a movie set where everyone else has a script and I’m just improvising badly.

Conversations feel shallow. I can talk about tasks, news, weather, but the moment it inches toward anything real, I freeze up or make a joke. I keep thinking, “If they really knew me, they’d pull away,” so I never let anyone get close enough to test that.

Nights are the worst. Phone in hand, scrolling, hoping someone will message first. I keep wanting to say, “I’m lonely,” but I don’t know who I’m even saying it to.
