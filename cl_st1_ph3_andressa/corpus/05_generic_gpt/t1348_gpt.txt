I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like talking through glass. I can be funny, helpful, “easy to be around,” but no one really knows me and I don’t know how to let them. When I try to open up, it feels forced and awkward, like I’m reading lines from a script. Then I retreat, tell myself I’m better off alone, and immediately feel the emptiness again. It’s like being homesick for a place that might not exist.
