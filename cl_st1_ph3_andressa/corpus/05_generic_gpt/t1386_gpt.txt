Sometimes I feel like I’m living in a parallel universe where everyone else got the memo on how to be human and I didn’t.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I still feel like a ghost hovering at the edge of every interaction. I can talk about work, the weather, random small talk, but when it comes to actually connecting, my mind just… blanks. It’s like there’s a thick glass wall between me and everyone else.

I scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one who stops replying first because I’m terrified of being annoying. Then I feel hurt when no one checks back in. I decline invitations because I’m anxious, then sit alone wishing I’d gone.

What makes it worse is that I’m starting to believe this is just who I am: someone people forget about once they walk out of the room.
