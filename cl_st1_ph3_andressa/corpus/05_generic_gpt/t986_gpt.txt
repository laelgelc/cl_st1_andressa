I’m starting to realize that I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels baked into who I am now. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel close enough to bother. I keep conversations shallow because I’m scared of being too much, then feel invisible because no one really knows me. It’s like I built my own cage.
