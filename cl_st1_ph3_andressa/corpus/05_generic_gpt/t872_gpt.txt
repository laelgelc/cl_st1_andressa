I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I have people around me—coworkers, family, a few acquaintances—but I don’t feel actually known by any of them. Most of my conversations are surface-level: work, weather, random memes. As soon as it starts to feel even slightly real, I pull back, or they do.

I scroll through old chats and notice how many of them just… faded. No big fight, no drama. Just slower replies, more silence, and then nothing. It makes me wonder if I’m just forgettable, or if I somehow drain people without realizing it.

I try to be there for others, listen, give advice, remember details about their lives. But when I need someone, I end up staring at my contacts list and realizing there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging. Or I type something out and delete it because it feels “too needy.”

Most days I feel like I’m watching life from behind glass. There, but not really in it.
