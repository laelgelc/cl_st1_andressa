Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, come home, scroll on my phone, repeat. I talk to people every day but it all feels like small talk on top of a huge empty space. I don’t really have “my people” anywhere. Friends drifted away, relationships ended, and I never seemed to replace them with anything real. I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be genuinely known by someone, not just recognized. It’s like I’m fading into the background of my own story.
