I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people most days—at work, on the train, in stores—but it all feels like walking through a movie set where everyone else has a script and I’m just improvising badly.

Conversations stay on the surface. I crack jokes, I nod in the right places, but I can’t remember the last time I said something real and felt like someone actually caught it, held it, and responded from the same place. When I try to be honest, I either feel like I’m oversharing or like I’m talking into a void.

What scares me is how normal it’s starting to feel. The silence after a long day, the phone that doesn’t light up unless I’m needed for something practical. I’m not sure when “fine” became my default lie.
