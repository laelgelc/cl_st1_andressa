I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I go through the motions—work, small talk, errands—but it all feels like acting. I have people around me, yet no one really knows what my days actually feel like. I overthink every message I send, then convince myself I’m annoying and delete it. Nights are the worst; it’s just me, my thoughts, and this heavy sense that I’m fundamentally forgettable.
