I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, I can be “friendly,” I can make small jokes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character I don’t believe in.

Most of my days are just me, my phone, and background noise from YouTube or music so the silence doesn’t feel so loud. I scroll through chats where I used to talk to people regularly and now it’s just dead conversations, last messages left on “seen.” I keep replaying them and wondering what exactly I did wrong, or if I was just never that important in the first place.

I’m at that stage where I’m surrounded by people in public but feel like a ghost. At work, I smile and nod, but no one knows anything real about me. I don’t even try to share much anymore because I’ve convinced myself it won’t matter.

It’s like I’m waiting for something to change, but I don’t actually believe it will.
