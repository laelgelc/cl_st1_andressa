I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I talk to coworkers, reply in group chats, even go out sometimes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who knows the right lines. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling needy or awkward.

I miss having someone who actually checks in, not out of politeness, but because they genuinely care. I scroll through old conversations with people I used to be close to and wonder when exactly the distance started. It’s like I slowly faded out of everyone’s priority list, and now I don’t know how to make myself matter to anyone again.
