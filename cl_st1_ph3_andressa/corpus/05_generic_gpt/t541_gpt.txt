I feel like I’m stuck in this weird in‑between place where I’m not completely alone, but I still feel lonely all the time. I have acquaintances, people I can chat with about work, memes, or random stuff, but no one I can actually be vulnerable with. I scroll through my contacts and realize there isn’t a single person I’d feel comfortable calling if I really needed someone.

What makes it worse is that I used to have close friends. Over time, people moved, got partners, got busy, and I just… didn’t keep up. Now I hesitate to reach out because it’s been so long that it feels awkward, like I’ve missed my chance.
