I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have people around me—coworkers, family, a few old friends—but I don’t feel actually known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and had the time or interest to hear the real answer.

I’ve tried reaching out, suggesting plans, opening up a little, but it always feels one-sided or awkward. Group chats go silent when I talk. Calls get postponed indefinitely. I end up scrolling, gaming, or just lying in bed, half-hoping someone will message first, fully expecting nobody will.
