I’m starting to feel like I exist in the gaps between other people’s lives. Everyone around me seems to have a group chat buzzing, weekend plans, someone who checks in. I have acquaintances, but no one who’d notice if I went quiet for a week. I overthink every message I send and usually end up deleting it. The silence becomes proof that I’m forgettable. I’m tired of my own company, but I don’t know how to be anything else.
