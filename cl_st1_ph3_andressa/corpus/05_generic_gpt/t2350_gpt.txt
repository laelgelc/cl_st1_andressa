I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family who check in, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being known. I overthink every message I send, then end up not sending anything, and the silence just keeps growing. I see other people forming these easy, natural bonds and I can’t figure out what’s wrong with me that I can’t seem to do the same.
