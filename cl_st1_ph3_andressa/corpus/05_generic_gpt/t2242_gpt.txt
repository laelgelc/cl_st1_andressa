I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I watch other people slip into friendships like it’s the most natural thing in the world, and I feel like I’m always standing just outside the circle, close enough to see but not to belong.

I overthink every interaction—did I talk too much, too little, sound weird?—and then I pull back to avoid the imagined rejection. Then I’m lonely again and hate myself for withdrawing. It’s like I’m both the person who’s starving and the one guarding the locked door to the kitchen.
