I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really match my life on paper. I have coworkers, a couple of “friends,” family chats on WhatsApp, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. Conversations stay on the surface: work, memes, random small talk. No one really asks how I am in a way that feels like they actually want to know.

I’m starting to worry that I’ve forgotten how to connect. When I try to open up, I feel awkward and oversharing, so I pull back and make a joke. Then I go home, lie in bed scrolling, and feel this ache in my chest that I can’t explain to anyone in my life. It’s like I’m invisible even when people are looking right at me.
