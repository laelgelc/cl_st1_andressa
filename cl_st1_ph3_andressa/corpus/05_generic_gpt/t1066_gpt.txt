I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I see people my age forming tight friend groups, moving in together, planning trips, and I’m just… there. I talk to coworkers, I reply to group chats, but it all feels like I’m orbiting other people’s lives instead of being part of them.

What messes with my head is that on paper I’m “fine.” I have a job, hobbies, I’m not completely socially inept. But when the day ends, no one really checks in. If I disappeared from social media for a week, I’m not sure anyone would notice.

I miss feeling known. Not just talked to, but understood. I don’t even know how to fix it anymore; I just keep going through the motions and hoping it feels less empty at some point.
