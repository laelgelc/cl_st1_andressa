I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “around” people. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what my days look like, what I’m afraid of, or what I’m hoping for. I don’t even know who I’d call in an actual emergency.

I keep thinking it’s my fault. I’m awkward, I overthink every message, I convince myself I’m bothering people just by existing in their notifications. So I stay quiet, and then feel hurt that no one reaches out. It’s like I’m building my own cage and then complaining about the bars.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. I go through whole weekends barely speaking out loud. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move forward. Mine feels like it’s stuck in pause, and I’m not sure how to unstick it.
