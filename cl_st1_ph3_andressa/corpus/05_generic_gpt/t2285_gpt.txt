I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but everything feels like background noise. I go through the motions: “How’s it going?” “Not bad, you?” and then it just dies. I can’t tell if I’m boring or if I’m just too guarded.

Nights are the worst. I scroll endlessly, hoping something will distract me from the feeling that everyone else has a life happening somewhere I’m not invited. I replay old conversations in my head, wondering where I pushed people away or where they quietly gave up on me.

I’m scared that if I tell anyone how lonely I feel, they’ll either pity me or slowly back away. So I just pretend I’m fine and keep drifting further out, hoping someone will notice without me having to say anything.
