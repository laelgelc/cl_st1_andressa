Lately I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I go to work, make small talk, come home, scroll, sleep. I’m surrounded by people but no one really knows me beyond the surface-level stuff. I have “friends,” but the kind where if I stopped reaching out, I’m not sure anyone would notice for a long time. I miss having someone I can message about nothing and everything. I’m tired of pretending I’m fine, but I don’t know how to say “I’m lonely” without sounding pathetic.
