I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in that way where it feels like I’m fading out of other people’s lives.

I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers. I can make people laugh in short bursts. But no one really knows me. I’m always the one who reaches out first, and when I stop, everything just goes quiet. It’s like I’m optional background noise.

I scroll through my phone sometimes trying to figure out who I could text and say, “I’m not okay,” and the honest answer is: there isn’t anyone I’d feel safe bothering with that. I’m either “too much” or “not important enough.”

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. Like I’m slowly getting used to the idea that this might just be it.
