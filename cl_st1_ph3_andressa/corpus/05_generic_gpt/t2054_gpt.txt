I don’t even know how to explain this feeling anymore. I’m surrounded by people most days – coworkers, neighbors, random small talk – but it all feels like I’m acting in a play where everyone else knows their lines except me. I have “contacts” but not friends. People I can text about logistics, not about how my chest feels heavy for no obvious reason.

Nights are the worst. I scroll and scroll, half hoping someone will message me, half dreading having to pretend I’m fine. I overthink every interaction: Did I talk too much? Too little? Did I sound weird? It’s like I’m permanently on the outside of a glass wall, watching other people’s lives move forward.

I tell myself I’m used to it, but I’m not. It still hurts, just in a quieter way now.
