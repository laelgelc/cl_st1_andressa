I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people every day, but it feels like I’m just passing through scenes in someone else’s life. I talk, I laugh at the right moments, I do the small talk, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that nobody really cares to know.

I keep wanting deeper connections, but I’m terrified of being “too much” if I open up. So I stay quiet, then hate myself for being distant. It’s like I’m stuck behind glass, watching everyone else belong to each other. Nights are the worst; the silence gets so loud. I scroll through my phone, hoping someone will message first, and when no one does, I pretend it doesn’t hurt as much as it actually does.
