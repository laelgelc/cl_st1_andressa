I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m kind of invisible. Days blur into each other: work, commute, phone, sleep. I talk to people, but it’s all surface-level—small jokes, “how’s it going,” nothing that feels like anyone really knows me. I’ll scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one reaching out, or the last message is from months ago. 

It’s weird how you can be surrounded by noise and still feel like a background character. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually asked how I’m doing and meant it.
