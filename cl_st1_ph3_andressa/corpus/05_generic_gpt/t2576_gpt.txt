I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really fell apart. I scroll through my contacts and it’s all old numbers, people I let drift away because I never knew how to keep them close. 

Most days I’m surrounded by others, but I still feel like I’m behind glass. I watch everyone else slide into friendships and relationships like it’s natural, and I’m stuck analyzing every word I say, afraid I’m too much or not enough, and ending up saying nothing.
