I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.”

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with. Online I can be talkative. But when it comes to anything deeper, it’s like I hit an invisible wall. I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I say nothing and walk away feeling like a ghost.

Most days are just routines: work, home, scrolling, sleep. I might exchange a few texts, but if I stopped messaging first, I’m not sure who would notice. I keep thinking I’m just in a “phase,” but years are going by like this.

What really gets to me is the small stuff: seeing friends in group photos I’m not part of, watching couples argue in public and realizing even that kind of messy closeness is something I don’t have. I miss being expected somewhere, by someone.

I don’t know if I’m lonely because I’m bad at relationships, or bad at relationships because I’m lonely. It feels like I’m stuck at a distance from my own life, watching it happen to someone else.
