I’m starting to wonder if I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one who actually knows me. Conversations feel like small talk on repeat: weather, work, “How was your weekend?” and I give the same safe answers every time. I can’t remember the last time someone asked a deeper question and actually waited for the real answer.

When I scroll through my phone, there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging without overthinking it. I’ll type “Hey, how’s it going?” and then delete it because I don’t want to be annoying or needy. It’s like I’m standing behind glass watching everyone else live in color while I’m stuck in grayscale.

What bothers me most is how normalized it’s become. I go days without speaking to anyone outside of work, and it doesn’t even feel unusual anymore. Just… the way things are.

I’m not even sure what I’m hoping for by writing this. Maybe I just needed to put it into words somewhere, because keeping it all in my head is starting to feel heavier than the silence itself.
