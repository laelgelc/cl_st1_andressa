I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like small talk stacked on small talk. No one really knows me, and I don’t really let them, because I’m scared they’ll think I’m too much or not enough.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, watch videos, stare at my phone hoping someone will message first. When they don’t, I tell myself it’s because I’m forgettable. I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I’m tired of being the one who tries. It’s starting to feel like I could disappear and it wouldn’t change anything for anyone.
