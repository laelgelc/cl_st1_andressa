I’m starting to realize how rarely I’m actually known by anyone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. I go home, sit in my room, and it’s like the world forgets I exist until I show up again. I try to reach out, but conversations die quickly. It makes me wonder if I’m just fundamentally forgettable, or if I somehow missed the part where everyone learned how to connect.
