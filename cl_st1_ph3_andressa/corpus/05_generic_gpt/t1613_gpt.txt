I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like surface-level noise. I go home and there’s just this heavy silence that feels louder than any conversation. I’m tired of being “the quiet one” who’s always on the edge of groups, never really in them. It’s like everyone else got a manual for living and I’m faking every page.
