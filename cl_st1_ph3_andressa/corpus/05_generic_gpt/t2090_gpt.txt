I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, I make small talk, I even laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like I’m acting a part I never auditioned for. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s my fault or just how life is now.

When I get home, the silence is so loud it almost has a weight. I scroll through my phone, see everyone else’s group photos, inside jokes, relationships, and I feel like I’m pressed up against the glass of other people’s lives.

I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually asked, “How are you really?” I’m not sure I remember how to open up without feeling like I’m being too much, or not enough, all at once.
