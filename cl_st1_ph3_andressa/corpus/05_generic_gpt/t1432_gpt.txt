I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, I can be polite, I can even joke around, but it all feels like I’m playing a character I don’t really like. When conversations end, nobody seems curious about me, and I don’t know if that’s because I’m boring or because I never learned how to open up properly. 

Most days I just scroll, lurk, watch other people’s lives. I’m surrounded by noise but feel like I’m under glass, watching everyone else figure out something I keep missing.
