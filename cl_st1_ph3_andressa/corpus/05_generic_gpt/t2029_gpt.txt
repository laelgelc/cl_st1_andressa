I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m disappearing in slow motion.

On paper my life looks “fine.” I have a job, I pay my bills, I answer emails, I occasionally show up when someone has a birthday dinner. But I move through all of it like I’m behind glass. People can see the outline of me, but no one really gets close enough to tap and ask, “Are you actually okay?”

I have “friends,” but they’re mostly group chat names and people I see maybe once every couple of months. We talk about TV shows, memes, work drama. No one asks anything deeper than “How’s work?” and even when they do, I just say “busy” and change the subject. Part of me is terrified that if I actually said how empty I feel, it would either make things weird or they’d just… not know what to do with it.

I miss having someone who knows the little details. Someone who notices that I’ve been quiet for a few days and checks in. Someone who remembers what I said last week and asks how it went. I’m tired of being the one who always reaches out first, who suggests plans, who keeps the conversation alive. It’s exhausting constantly feeling like you’re auditioning for a place in other people’s lives.

Nights are the worst. I come home, drop my stuff, and there’s just silence. I scroll, I watch videos, I “like” things, but it all feels like I’m pressing my face up against other people’s lives without actually being in any of them. I’ll see groups of friends on Instagram or couples doing nothing special together and it hits me how much of my life is spent in my own head, with no witness.

I don’t even know what I’m looking for by posting this. I’m not expecting advice or some magical solution. I think I just needed to put into words that I feel incredibly alone, even when I’m technically not. It’s like I exist in the background of my own life, and I’m starting to worry that this is just how it’s going to be.
