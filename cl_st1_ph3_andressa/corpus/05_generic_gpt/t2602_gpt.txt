I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but nobody I’d call at 2 a.m. if things fell apart. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing and scaring people away, so I pull back and act casual again. Then I feel fake and even more distant. It’s like I’m always slightly outside the glass, watching everyone else have the kind of closeness I can’t seem to reach.
