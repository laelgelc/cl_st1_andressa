I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I go through the motions—work, gym, errands—but there’s this constant sense that I’m just orbiting around everyone else’s lives instead of actually being in one. I have acquaintances, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me. I can go days without a real conversation that isn’t about tasks or logistics.

What gets to me is how invisible it feels. I can be in a crowded place and still feel like I’m behind glass. I try to reach out sometimes, but I second-guess myself: “Am I bothering them? Do they actually want to talk to me?” So I end up pulling back, then feeling even more disconnected.

Social media makes it worse. Everyone seems to have their group, their person, their go-to friend. I’m just… there. A background character in my own story, watching everyone else have the kind of connections I can’t seem to hold onto.
