I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people who’ll chat about weather or TV shows, but no one who really knows me. I’ll get invited to things occasionally, but I always feel like the extra chair at the table, the person they don’t mind having around but wouldn’t miss if I didn’t show up.

I’ve tried joining groups, talking more, saying yes to plans. I still end up standing on the edge of conversations, waiting for an opening that never comes. When I do talk, I overthink everything I say and replay it later, convinced I sounded weird or boring.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. I go days without a real conversation and it barely surprises me now. It’s like I’m slowly fading into the background of my own life and I don’t know what to do with that.
