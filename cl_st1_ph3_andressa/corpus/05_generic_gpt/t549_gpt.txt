I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people most days – coworkers, classmates, neighbors – but it all feels like background noise. Conversations stay stuck on small talk, and I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and actually wanted to know.

I try to reach out sometimes, but I always feel like I’m interrupting other people’s real lives. Like I’m the extra, not part of the main cast. Even when I’m invited somewhere, I feel like a placeholder until someone better shows up.

Nights are the worst. Phone in hand, scrolling, hoping someone will message first. Usually, it just stays quiet.
