I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m standing behind glass. I say the right things, make the right jokes, but nobody really knows me. And when I think about letting them in, I freeze up. I don’t even know what “letting them in” would look like anymore.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through chats I’m too scared to start, stare at my phone hoping someone will message first. They usually don’t. When they do, I overthink every response until the conversation dies.

I’m not completely isolated, but I feel invisible even when I’m with others. Like I’m always “extra,” never the person someone thinks of first. I keep wondering if I missed some crucial stage in life where everyone else learned how to form real bonds and I just…didn’t.
