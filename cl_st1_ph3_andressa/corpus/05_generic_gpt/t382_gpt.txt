I don’t know when “being alone a lot” quietly turned into “feeling lonely all the time,” but it has. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s my fault or if I’m just not the kind of person people want to know.

I catch myself rehearsing conversations in my head, then saying nothing when I actually get the chance. I want connection so badly that it makes me awkward, like I’m trying to hide how desperate I feel. Then I go home, scroll endlessly, and feel even more disconnected watching everyone else’s “normal” lives.

It’s this weird mix of wanting to be seen and being terrified that, if I am, people won’t like what they see. So I stay stuck somewhere in between, and it’s exhausting.
