I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I’m surrounded by people on paper—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats that light up my phone—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. I can make small talk, crack a joke, even seem “fine,” but it all feels like I’m playing a character that people tolerate rather than someone they genuinely want around.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through social media and see people hanging out, having inside jokes, building histories together. Meanwhile, I don’t have anyone I’d feel comfortable texting just to say, “Hey, I’m having a rough day.” I worry that if I disappeared from everything, it would take a while for anyone to really notice.

What scares me most is how normal this has started to feel. Like I’m slowly adjusting to a life where I’m just…background noise.
