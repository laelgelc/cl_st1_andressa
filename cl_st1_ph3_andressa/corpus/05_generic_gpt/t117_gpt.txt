Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, I make small talk, people laugh at my jokes, but it all feels like acting. When the day’s over, everyone goes home to someone, and I go back to my apartment and the silence hits like a wall.

I have friends, technically, but I don’t feel close to anyone. Conversations stay on the surface. I’m scared that if I show how lonely I really am, I’ll push people away, so I keep it light and pretend I’m fine.

What scares me most is how normal this has started to feel.
