I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really make sense on paper. I have coworkers I chat with, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family who’d probably pick up if I called. But most days I feel like I’m just kind of…floating around people instead of with them.

I miss having “my person,” or even “my people.” The ones you can message stupid memes to at 2 a.m., or call when something small but important happens. Now I hesitate before sending anyone anything because I’m scared I’m bothering them, or that I’m way more invested than they are.

What really gets to me is how replaceable I feel. Like, if I stopped reaching out, the group chats would go on, the work days would look the same, and maybe it’d take a while before anyone noticed I’d gone quiet. I’m not dramatic enough to think no one cares at all, but it feels like I’m always on the outer ring of everyone’s life.

I try to be “fine” in front of people, but it’s exhausting. I don’t even know what I’m asking for by writing this. Maybe I just wanted to admit, somewhere, that I’m tired of pretending this doesn’t hurt as much as it does.
