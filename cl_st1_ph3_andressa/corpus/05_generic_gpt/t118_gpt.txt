I’ve been feeling like I’m fading into the background of my own life.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but it all feels surface-level. No one really knows me beyond the “I’m fine, busy with work” script. I’m not even sure I remember how to let someone in deeper than that without feeling stupid or needy.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, watch random videos, pretend I’m choosing to be alone, but it feels more like I’ve just quietly fallen out of everyone’s orbit. I see people making plans, posting group photos, and I can’t remember the last time someone texted first to ask how I’m doing.

I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I’m tired of being the one who initiates everything and still going to bed feeling like I don’t really matter to anyone.
