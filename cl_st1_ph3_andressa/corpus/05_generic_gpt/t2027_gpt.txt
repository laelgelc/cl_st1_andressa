I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I can go weeks without a real conversation that isn’t about work, logistics, or some shared hobby. When something good or bad happens, I open my phone to tell someone…and then I just put it back down because there’s no one it feels natural to message.

What makes it worse is that I *used* to have close friends. Life changes, people moved, some drifted, some I pushed away because I was scared of needing them too much. Now I’m stuck in this loop where I’m lonely, but also exhausted by the thought of trying to start again from scratch.

Most days I function fine. I get things done, smile when I’m supposed to. But it feels like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it.
