Lately I feel like I’m living in a glass box. People can see me and I can see them, but there’s this invisible layer that keeps everything from actually touching. I have friends, technically. Group chats, occasional meetups, coworkers who joke around. But I always feel like the extra piece in a puzzle that’s already complete.  

I keep trying to open up, but the conversations stay on the surface. I leave social situations feeling more drained and somehow more alone than before. Nights are the worst. I lie there scrolling, watching everyone else’s lives move forward while mine feels like it’s stuck on pause.
