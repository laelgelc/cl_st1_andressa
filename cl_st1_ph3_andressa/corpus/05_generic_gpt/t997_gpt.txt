I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one who really knows me. I scroll, I lurk, I watch everyone else’s lives happen. When something good or bad happens, I literally don’t know who to message. Even when I’m in a room full of people, I’m quiet, half-present, like I’m behind glass. It’s making me wonder if this is just how my life is going to be.
