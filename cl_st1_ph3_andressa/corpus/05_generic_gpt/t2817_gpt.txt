I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even some old friends—but I don’t feel truly connected to anyone. Conversations stay on the surface. I’m either “too much” when I open up or “too quiet” when I don’t.  

Most evenings I just scroll or watch something until I’m tired enough to sleep. The silence hits hardest when I turn everything off and realize there’s no one who’d really notice if I disappeared for a while.  

I miss feeling known. Not liked, not tolerated—actually known. I’m starting to wonder if maybe that just isn’t in the cards for me.
