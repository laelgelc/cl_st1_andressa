I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people—at work, on social media, even in my own family—but I still feel completely disconnected. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being myself. When I try to open up, I worry I’m oversharing or being “too much,” so I pull back and keep things surface-level again.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone just to feel less alone, but seeing everyone else seemingly living full lives makes the emptiness louder. I don’t really have anyone I’d feel comfortable calling if something genuinely bad happened. It’s like I’m always “almost” close to people, but never actually close.

I’m not even sure what I’m looking for by posting this. Maybe I just wanted to say it somewhere.
