I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but no one I feel safe being fully honest with. Every interaction feels like I’m performing a version of myself that’s easier to tolerate. I go home, scroll, lurk, maybe type something and delete it.
