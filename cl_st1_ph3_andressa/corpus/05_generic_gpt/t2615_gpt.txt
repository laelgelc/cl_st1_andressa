I’ve reached a point where I’m not even sure what “connection” is supposed to feel like anymore. I talk to people at work, I respond to messages, I say the right things, and somehow it still feels like I’m behind glass watching myself interact. 

When I’m alone, it hits harder. I scroll through chats I never start, numbers I never call, and convince myself they’re all just being polite anyway. I want to be known in that deep, unfiltered way, but I’m terrified that if I show that side, people will quietly back away. 

So I stay half-visible and then wonder why I’m lonely.
