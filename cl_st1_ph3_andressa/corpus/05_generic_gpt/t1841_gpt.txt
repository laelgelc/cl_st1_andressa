I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like a personality trait. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I feel like I’m behind glass. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to open up, I either get talked over or I regret saying anything and shut down again.  

Most nights it’s just me, my phone, and this heavy silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen. I keep wondering if I’m the problem, or if I’m just fundamentally hard to connect with.
