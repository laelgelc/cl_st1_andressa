I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. When I scroll through my messages, it’s just old group chats and one-word replies that fizzled out months ago.

I keep thinking it’s my fault: I cancel plans, I overthink every text, I assume people are just being polite. Sometimes I’ll rehearse a message for 20 minutes and then delete it because it feels “too needy.”

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. Days blur together, and the silence doesn’t even feel loud anymore. It just feels expected.
