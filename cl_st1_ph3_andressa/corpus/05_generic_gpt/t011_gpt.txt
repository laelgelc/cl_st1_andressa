I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call when something really good or really awful happens. I scroll through my phone and there’s no name that feels “safe.”

What bothers me is how invisible this feels. From the outside I look fine: I show up, smile, make small talk. Inside, it feels like I’m behind glass watching everyone else have lives that overlap and tangle together, while mine just runs parallel to theirs and never touches.

I’ve tried things people suggest—hobbies, meetups, reaching out first—but conversations stay surface-level and fade quickly. I worry that I’m just… forgettable. Like I don’t leave any kind of impression strong enough for someone to think, “I want this person in my life.”

It’s exhausting pretending I’m okay with it when I’m really not.
