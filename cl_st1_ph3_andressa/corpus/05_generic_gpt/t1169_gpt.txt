I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, family chats, a couple of old friends I text sometimes—but I still feel completely disconnected. Conversations feel scripted. I say what I’m supposed to say, they say what they’re supposed to say, and then we both go back to scrolling our phones.

I miss feeling known. Not just “how was your weekend?” but someone actually remembering what I care about, asking follow-up questions, noticing when I’m off. I can’t remember the last time anyone did that without me prompting it.

I’ve tried initiating more, suggesting calls or meetups, but it feels one-sided. When plans fall through, I act like it’s fine, but it just confirms this quiet fear that I’m optional in everyone’s life.
