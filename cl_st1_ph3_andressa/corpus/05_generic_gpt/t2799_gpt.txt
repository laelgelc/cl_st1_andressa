I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family I text occasionally—but it all feels thin, like cellophane between me and everyone else. Conversations never seem to move past small talk. I try to open up a little and it either gets brushed off, joked away, or I instantly feel like I’ve overshared and retreat.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives unfold in photos and comments, and it makes me feel both connected and completely outside of everything at the same time. I want someone I can message without overthinking every word, someone who actually notices if I disappear for a few days.

What messes with my head is that from the outside I probably look fine—functional, busy, “doing okay.” But it just feels like I’m quietly drifting further out, and no one sees it happening.
