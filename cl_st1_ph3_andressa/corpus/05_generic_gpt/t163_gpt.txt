I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives while still being physically present in mine. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but almost no one really checks in, and I don’t know how to break that pattern without seeming desperate. I have acquaintances, people to joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. 

What bothers me most is how quiet everything feels when my phone stays dark. I keep wondering if I somehow missed the part where everyone else learned how to form real connections, and now I’m too late to catch up.
