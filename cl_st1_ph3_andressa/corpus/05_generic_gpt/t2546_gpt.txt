I feel like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own. Days blur together: work, commute, phone, sleep. I have acquaintances, but no one who actually knows what’s going on in my head. Conversations stay on the surface—weather, TV, “how’s work?”—and I can’t seem to push past that wall. I’m scared that if I vanished tomorrow, people would just assume I got busy, not that something was wrong.
