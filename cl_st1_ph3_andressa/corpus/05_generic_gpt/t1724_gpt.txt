I’ve started to realize I’m not sure I remember what “close” feels like anymore. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but everything feels like I’m talking through glass. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m watching my life from the outside.

I miss being known in the small ways: someone remembering my favorite snack, or sending me something because it “reminded them of me.” I don’t really get messages unless I send them first, and even then it feels like I’m intruding.

It’s weird how you can function, go to work, make small talk, and still feel like no one would really notice if you quietly drifted out of their lives. I’m not falling apart, but I’m not really okay either.
