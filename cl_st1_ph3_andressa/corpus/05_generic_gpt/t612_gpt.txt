I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can joke, make small talk, even seem confident, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. When the conversation ends, there’s this heavy silence that feels louder than anything. I watch other people fall into friendships so easily and I can’t figure out what I’m missing. I’m tired of being “the person who’s always there for others” but never really known. It’s like I’m visible but not actually seen.
