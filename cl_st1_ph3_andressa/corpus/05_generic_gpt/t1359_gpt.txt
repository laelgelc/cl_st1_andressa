I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people at work and online, but it all feels like noise instead of connection. I have acquaintances, not friends. No one I’d text first when something good or bad happens. I overthink every interaction and usually end up staying quiet because it feels safer than risking rejection or awkwardness. Even when I’m with others, I feel like I’m watching through glass. I’m starting to wonder if this is just how my life is going to be.
