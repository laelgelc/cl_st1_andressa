I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really fit into the usual boxes. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. I can talk, joke, make small talk just fine, but it’s like I’m playing a character of myself and nobody knows the actual person underneath.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels. From the outside, I probably look “fine” or even “busy.” On the inside, I feel like I could disappear and it would just be a minor schedule adjustment for everyone else. I keep thinking I should reach out, say something real, but when I try, the words feel too heavy and I end up changing the subject.
