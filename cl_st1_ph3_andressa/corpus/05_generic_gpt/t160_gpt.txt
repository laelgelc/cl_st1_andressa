I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in slow motion. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I’m not sure anyone wants to. I try to initiate plans and conversations, but they fade quickly, like I’m forgettable. Even when I’m in a room full of people, I feel like a ghost standing just outside the glass.
