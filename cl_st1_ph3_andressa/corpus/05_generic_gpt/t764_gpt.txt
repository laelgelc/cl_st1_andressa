I don’t think I ever learned how to turn “acquaintances” into “friends.” I’m surrounded by people all the time—at work, on the train, in group chats that never stop pinging—but somehow none of it feels like it includes me. I make small talk, I laugh at the right moments, I go home, and then it’s just silence. No one really checks in, and I don’t really know how to ask to be included without sounding desperate.

It’s weird how you can be “known” by people (they know my name, my role, random facts about me) and still feel like no one actually sees you. Most days it feels like I’m just passing through other people’s lives as background noise.
