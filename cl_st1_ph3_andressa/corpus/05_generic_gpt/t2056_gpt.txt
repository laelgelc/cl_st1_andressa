I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep, repeat. I talk to people every day, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m just performing “normal human interaction” instead of actually connecting. Friends have slowly drifted away—no big fights, just unanswered messages and “we should hang out sometime” that never happens. I’m never anyone’s first choice to text or invite. It messes with my head; I start wondering what’s wrong with me, but there’s no clear answer—just this constant dull ache of being unnecessary.
