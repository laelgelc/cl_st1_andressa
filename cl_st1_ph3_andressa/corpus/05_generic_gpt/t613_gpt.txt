I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives, but I’m still very present in my own head. Days blur together: work, commute, scrolling, sleep. I talk to people, but it’s all surface-level—little jokes, updates, nothing that feels like being known. My phone almost never lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. I miss having someone who actually checks in, who remembers small details about me. It’s strange how heavy silence can feel when it’s everywhere.
