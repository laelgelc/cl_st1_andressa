I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside lately. I go to work, talk to people, make small jokes, but it all feels like I’m acting out a role I don’t remember auditioning for. When the day ends, no one really checks in. My phone is mostly silent unless I’m the one reaching out, and even then conversations fade fast.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel in group settings. I’m there, but I’m not really there. I listen more than I speak because it feels like my words land flat. I keep wondering if I somehow missed the point where everyone else learned how to actually connect.
