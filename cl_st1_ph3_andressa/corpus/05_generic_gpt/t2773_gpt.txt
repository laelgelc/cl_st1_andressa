I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay” around them. I have friends, technically. Group chats, occasional meetups, inside jokes. But it all feels like I’m watching myself from a distance, playing the role of someone who belongs there.

The worst part is how invisible this feels. No one sees anything wrong because I laugh at the right times and ask the right questions. Then I go home and it’s like I disappear. No one really checks in. I don’t even know what I’d say if they did.

It’s a weird kind of loneliness: being surrounded by people and still feeling completely unreachable.
