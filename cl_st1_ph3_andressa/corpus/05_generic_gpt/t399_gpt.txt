Lately I feel like I’m fading out of my own life. I go through the motions—work, home, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels like watching someone else’s routine. I have acquaintances, people who’d say hi if they saw me, but no one who’d really notice if I disappeared for a while. I miss being known, having someone remember small details about me. I’m starting to forget what it’s like to feel genuinely excited to see another person.
