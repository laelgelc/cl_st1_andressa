I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels paper-thin, like I’m just playing a role. When conversations end, it’s like I was never really there.

I miss feeling known. Not just “how was your weekend?” known, but the kind of known where someone can tell when you’re off and actually cares enough to ask. Lately I catch myself rehearsing what I’ll say before I speak, then deciding it’s not worth it and staying quiet. Then I feel lonely for not having said anything.

Social media makes it worse. Everyone looks busy, loved, plugged into something. I scroll hoping to feel connected and end up feeling like I’m watching life from the other side of thick glass.

I’m not even sure what I’m looking for posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist outside my own head for a minute.
