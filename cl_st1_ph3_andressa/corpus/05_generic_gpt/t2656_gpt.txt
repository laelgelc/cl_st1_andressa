I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, I reply to group chats, I even go out sometimes, but it all feels like I’m acting out a role instead of actually being there. Conversations stay on the surface—small talk, jokes, memes—and I can’t seem to bridge the gap to anything real.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels from the outside. People think I’m “doing fine” because I’m functional. Inside, it feels like I’m slowly drifting away from everyone. I miss feeling genuinely known, like someone actually sees me and remembers small details about me without being prompted.

I don’t even know where to start changing this.
