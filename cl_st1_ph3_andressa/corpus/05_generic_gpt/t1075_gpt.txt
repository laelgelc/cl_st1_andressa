I don’t really know how to talk about this in my “real” life, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. At work I can make small talk, crack jokes, even seem confident. Then I go home and it’s just silence. The weird part is I’m not technically “alone” — group chats ping, social media scrolls on forever — but none of it feels like it actually involves me.

I’ve noticed I edit myself constantly. I stop mid-text because I’m sure I’m being annoying. I don’t invite people to hang out because I’m convinced they’re just being polite when they say we should. I overthink every interaction afterward, replaying sentences like I’m reviewing evidence that I don’t really belong.

Nights are the worst. That’s when it hits that no one really knows me in the way I wish they did, and I’m not sure if that’s because they don’t want to, or because I’ve forgotten how to let them.
