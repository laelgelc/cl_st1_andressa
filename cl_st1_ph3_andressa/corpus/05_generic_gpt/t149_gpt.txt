I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really match my life on paper. I have coworkers, acquaintances, group chats that light up all day. But I can go an entire week without feeling like I’ve actually *connected* with anyone.

Conversations feel surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. I censor what I say because I’m afraid of being “too much” or boring or awkward. Then I go home and replay every interaction, convinced I sounded strange or annoying.

What makes it worse is that I don’t even know what I’m longing for exactly. A best friend? A relationship? Just someone who actually asks follow-up questions and remembers small details? I keep thinking I’m the common denominator in all of this, and that thought is starting to stick.
