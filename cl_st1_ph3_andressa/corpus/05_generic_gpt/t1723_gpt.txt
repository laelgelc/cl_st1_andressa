I’m surrounded by people but feel like a ghost. I have coworkers, family, acquaintances, but no one I can text just to say “I’m lonely, can we talk?” Every interaction feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. When I try to open up, I end up making a joke or changing the subject because I’m scared of being “too much.” It’s weird how my phone is always in my hand, yet there’s never anyone I actually feel comfortable messaging.
