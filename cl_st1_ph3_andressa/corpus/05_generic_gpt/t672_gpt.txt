I don’t really know how to say this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m slowly disappearing from my own life.

On paper, I’m “fine.” I have a job, a place to live, people I can technically text if I need something. But I don’t feel like I actually exist in anyone’s mind when I’m not physically in front of them. It’s like I’m only a background character in other people’s stories, and when the scene ends, I just…stop.

Most days I go to work, make small talk, come home, scroll, eat something, and go to bed. I’m surrounded by people but I don’t feel connected to any of them. Even when I’m in a group, I feel like I’m on the outside looking in, watching everyone else have these easy, natural conversations while I’m trying to figure out when it’s my turn to speak or if anyone even wants me to.

I’ve tried to “put myself out there” like everyone says. Joined a couple of hobby groups, forced myself to go to social events. I show up, people are polite, we talk about surface-level stuff, and then it just ends there. No one follows up, no one says “hey, want to hang out again?” I start wondering if I’m just forgettable, or if I’m doing something wrong that I can’t see.

The worst part is how it messes with my sense of reality. When days go by and no one messages me unless I message first, I start thinking maybe I really don’t matter that much. I know logically that people are busy, that everyone has their own lives, but it’s hard not to take the silence personally.

I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by writing this. Maybe I just needed to say out loud that I’m lonely, instead of pretending I’m fine all the time.
