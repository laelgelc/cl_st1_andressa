Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m fading out of my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but none of it feels connected to anyone. My friends are more like old contacts I’m too embarrassed to message after so much silence. Family asks how I am and I automatically say “fine” because explaining feels exhausting. I’m surrounded by people every day and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live. I keep thinking I must be the common denominator, but I don’t even know what I’m doing wrong.
