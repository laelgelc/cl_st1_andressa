I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but everything feels like background noise. I’ll be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching instead of participating. When conversations happen, I overthink every word I say and replay it later, convinced I sounded weird or boring. So I pull back, which just makes the distance worse. I miss feeling known by someone, not just recognized. Lately it feels like I could disappear and life would just continue, uninterrupted.
