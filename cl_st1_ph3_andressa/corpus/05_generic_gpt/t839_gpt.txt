I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even some old friends I still message occasionally, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being myself.

I miss having someone who really knows me. Not the “how was your weekend?” small talk, but the kind of connection where silence isn’t awkward and you don’t have to translate every thought. Lately I’ve started to question if I’m just fundamentally hard to connect with, or if I’ve somehow missed the window where people form those deep bonds.

Most days I just go through the motions and try not to think about it too much.
