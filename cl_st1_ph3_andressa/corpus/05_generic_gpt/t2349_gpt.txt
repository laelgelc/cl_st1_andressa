I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I feel truly close to. I keep conversations shallow because I’m scared if I open up, they’ll see how empty I feel and quietly back away.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, watch something, sit in silence. I want someone who messages me first, who notices if I disappear for a few days. I’m tired of always being the one reaching out, of wondering if anyone ever thinks of me unprompted. It’s like I exist on the edge of everyone’s life, but never in the center of anyone’s.
