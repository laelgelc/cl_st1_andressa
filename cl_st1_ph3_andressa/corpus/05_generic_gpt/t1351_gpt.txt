I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. But there’s no one I’d call if something actually went wrong at 2 a.m. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of the names are people I haven’t spoken to in months, or years, and it’s not because of one big falling out. It’s more like I just slowly stopped being part of their “real life.”

I try to reach out sometimes, but conversations die fast. People are “so busy,” they answer hours or days later with short replies, and I can feel myself becoming a background character in their story. I’m not angry at them; I get it, everyone has their own stuff. It just hurts that I never seem to be anyone’s priority.

I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if I’m just fundamentally forgettable. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect while I stand there, invisible.
