I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” lately. Like I’m somehow both forgettable and overwhelming at the same time. I have people around me, technically—coworkers, acquaintances, family texts—but it all feels like background noise instead of connection.

I keep catching myself editing what I say so I don’t sound needy or dramatic. Then I end up saying nothing, and of course no one notices anything is wrong. It’s like I’m building my own isolation and hating myself for it at the same time.

Nights are the worst. I’ll scroll through chats and see conversations I used to be part of that just… moved on without me. I want to reach out, but I stare at the message box until it feels embarrassing to even try. I tell myself, “If they wanted to talk to you, they would.”

I don’t think I’ve forgotten how to be around people, but I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be genuinely wanted.
