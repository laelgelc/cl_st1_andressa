I don’t really know how to describe this feeling except that it’s like being a ghost in my own life. I’m surrounded by people at work, I reply to messages, I even crack jokes, but there’s this constant sense that none of it “lands” anywhere. Like I could disappear and it would just be a minor scheduling inconvenience.

I’ve tried to be more open, to hint that I’m not doing great, but it always feels like I’m forcing vulnerability into conversations that weren’t built to hold it. People say, “You can always talk to me,” but when I actually do, I can see their eyes drift, or the responses get shorter after a while.

What scares me most is how normal this has started to feel. The silence when I put my phone down. Eating alone and not even bothering with music anymore. It’s like I’m slowly adjusting to the idea that this might just be it.
