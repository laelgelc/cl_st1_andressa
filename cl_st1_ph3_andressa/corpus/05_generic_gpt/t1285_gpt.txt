It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have friends, a job, family chats on WhatsApp, but everything feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. Conversations skim the surface: work, memes, small talk. I can’t remember the last time I said what I was honestly thinking without editing it to sound “normal.”

I scroll through social media and see people going out, traveling, starting families. I’m happy for them in theory, but it also feels like I’m watching life from behind glass. I want to reach out more, but I overthink every message, every invitation, until I convince myself I’m just bothering people.

Most days I just come home, lie down, and let the hours pass. I’m not miserable, exactly. Just empty, like I’m quietly fading into the background of my own life.
