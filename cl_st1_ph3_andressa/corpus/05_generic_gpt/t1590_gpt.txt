I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about weather, TV, deadlines. But when conversations get anywhere near “real,” I freeze up. I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or stay on the surface and walk away feeling invisible.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through chats, see people “online,” but can’t think of a single person I feel comfortable messaging without worrying I’m annoying them. When my phone lights up, it’s usually a notification, not a person.

What gets me is that from the outside I probably look fine: I show up, make jokes, get work done. Inside, it feels like I’m watching my own life from behind glass. I’m there, but not really in it.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I guess I just wanted to put it into words somewhere, instead of just letting it echo around in my head.
