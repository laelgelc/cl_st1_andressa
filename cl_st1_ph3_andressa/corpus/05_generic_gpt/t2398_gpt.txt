I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, be “pleasant,” but it all feels like I’m acting out a script I only half remember. When the conversation ends, there’s this heavy silence that follows me home. I scroll through my phone, stare at chats that died months ago, and can’t bring myself to type anything because I’m convinced I’m just bothering people. I’m surrounded by others all the time, but it feels like I’m watching life from behind glass.
