I’ve been feeling like I’m fading out of everyone’s peripheral vision.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. We talk about schedules, memes, random small stuff, but nothing that makes me feel seen. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and actually waited for the answer.

I try to reach out, but I always feel like I’m interrupting someone else’s life. Messages get left on read, plans get postponed “when things calm down,” and eventually I stop asking. Then I start wondering if I’m the problem, if I’m just fundamentally… uninteresting.

It’s a strange mix of craving connection and wanting to disappear so I don’t have to keep noticing how replaceable I seem.
