I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m sealed behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a few people I’d technically call friends, but everything feels surface-level. We talk about work, shows, memes, but never anything real. When I try to open up, I can see people get uncomfortable or change the subject, so I stop halfway and laugh it off.

Nights are the worst. My phone’s full of conversations that just died out. I scroll, type something, delete it, and end up saying nothing. It feels like I’ve become “that person” who’s always around but never really chosen. I’m starting to wonder if I’m just fundamentally hard to connect with.
