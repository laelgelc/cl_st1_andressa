I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, and it’s getting harder to pretend I’m okay with that.

On paper, my life looks fine: I go to work, I answer emails, I make small talk about the weather and whatever show everyone’s watching. People would probably describe me as “nice” or “quiet.” But when I come home, it’s like I step into a vacuum. No messages, no calls, no one who really expects me to show up anywhere.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel in group settings. I’m there physically, but conversations just seem to flow around me. I rehearse things to say in my head, then by the time I’m ready, the moment has passed. Afterward I replay everything and wonder if it’s my personality, my face, my energy—something must be off.

I’ve tried hobbies, meetups, online communities. I talk to people, but it never moves past surface-level. Everyone else seems to already have their people. I keep asking myself if this is just how my life is going to feel from now on.
