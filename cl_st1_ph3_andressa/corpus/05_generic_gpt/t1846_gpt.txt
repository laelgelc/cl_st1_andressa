I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, even seem “normal” for a bit, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. As soon as the conversation ends, I’m back in my head replaying everything I said, convinced I sounded weird or boring.  

Everyone around me seems to have these effortless friendships, group chats, weekend plans. I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. It’s like I exist on the edge of everyone else’s lives, watching but never really in it.
