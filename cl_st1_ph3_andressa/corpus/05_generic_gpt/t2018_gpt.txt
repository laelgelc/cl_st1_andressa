I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but no one who really knows me or checks in first. I overthink every interaction, worried I’m being too much or not enough, so I pull back and then feel even more isolated. Nights are the worst—scrolling, lurking, wanting to reach out, but not wanting to be an inconvenience.
