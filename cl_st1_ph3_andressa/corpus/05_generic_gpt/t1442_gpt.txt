I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I don’t know how to let them. Whenever conversations get close to anything real, I pull back or make a joke, then later I’m frustrated that nobody “gets” me.

Nights are the worst. I’ll scroll, watch something, sit in silence, anything to avoid noticing how quiet it is. I keep thinking I’m too old to still be this socially lost, like I missed some critical window where everyone else learned how to connect and I just…didn’t.
