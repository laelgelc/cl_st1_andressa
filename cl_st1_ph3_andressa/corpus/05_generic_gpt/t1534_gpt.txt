I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

On paper, I’m “fine”: job, small apartment, a couple of people I text sometimes. But most days, I go from waking up to going to bed without having a real conversation with anyone. Meetings at work are all surface-level. Afterward, I walk home, cook, scroll, and it’s like I’m just filling time between sleeps.

What bothers me most is how quiet my phone is. I’m always the one who reaches out first, and if I stop, everything just…stops. It makes me wonder if I actually matter to anyone, or if I’m just convenient background noise when people are bored.

I don’t know how to fix it. I’m tired of my own company.
