I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to mimic it.

I have friends on paper: a group chat, coworkers who joke with me, family who’d say they love me. But I feel like I’m behind glass all the time. I say the right things, laugh at the right moments, but inside it’s like I’m watching someone else live my life.

I’ve tried being more “open,” but when I talk about anything real, the conversation either dies or gets redirected back to something light. It makes me feel like my feelings are too heavy or annoying, so I go back to being the “easy” version of myself.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I half-type messages and delete them. I want someone to notice I’m not okay without me having to make a big dramatic announcement about it.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just needed to put this somewhere.
