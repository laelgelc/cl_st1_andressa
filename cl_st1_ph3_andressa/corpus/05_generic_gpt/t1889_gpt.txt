I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. Like no one really sees me, but I’m constantly aware of how alone I am. I have acquaintances, people I talk to at work and online, but it all feels surface-level. When the day ends, the silence in my room feels way louder than anything that happened.

I keep thinking maybe I’m the common denominator. I’m not great at starting conversations, and when I do, I overthink every word. I replay interactions for hours, wondering if I sounded weird or needy. So I end up pulling back, then feel even more isolated.

What makes it worse is seeing everyone else’s “normal” lives—group chats, inside jokes, plans for the weekend. I don’t really have anyone I’d feel comfortable texting just to say, “Hey, today sucked. Can we talk?”
