I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people most days – coworkers, classmates, random small talk – but it all feels like background noise. I can go a whole week without anyone asking how I really am, and honestly I’m not even sure what I’d say if they did.

I keep thinking it’s my fault. I’m not the “group chat” person, I’m bad at initiating plans, and when I do finally see people I feel like I’m watching myself from the outside, trying to act normal. Then I go home and replay every interaction, convinced I was awkward or boring.

I miss feeling known. Not just recognized, but understood. I don’t even know where to start anymore – making new friends as an adult feels like trying to join a movie halfway through when everyone else already knows the plot.
