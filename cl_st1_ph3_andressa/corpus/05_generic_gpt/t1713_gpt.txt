I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I watch other people slip so easily into friendships and relationships while I overthink every interaction. I’m tired of being “the quiet one” who’s always on the outside looking in. It’s like I’m present, but never really *there* to anyone.
