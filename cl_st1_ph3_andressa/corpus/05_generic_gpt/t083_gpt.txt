I’ve realized lately that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely separate from everyone. I go to work, make small talk, laugh at the right moments, and then come home to silence. It’s like I’m playing a role all day and the second I’m alone, everything just drops. I scroll through my phone hoping someone will text first, then feel stupid for caring so much. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually reached out. I just feel… invisible and unnecessary most of the time.
