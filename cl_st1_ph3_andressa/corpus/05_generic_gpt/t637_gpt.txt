I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my family group chats, but it’s like I’m acting a role instead of actually being there. I keep conversations light because I’m scared if I’m honest about how lonely I feel, I’ll make things awkward or be seen as needy.  

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch people joke and share memories, and I feel like I’m behind a glass wall. I’m not sure when I forgot how to connect, but it feels like I did.
