I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

On paper, I’m not isolated. I go to work, I answer messages, I show up to things when invited. But it all feels like I’m behind glass. I can see everyone else laughing and bonding, and I’m there physically, but emotionally it’s like I’m on mute.

I keep conversations light and safe because I’m terrified that if I show what’s really going on, people will pull away. At the same time, I resent that no one seems to notice how lonely I am. It’s like I’ve trained everyone to believe I’m fine and now I’m trapped in that role.

Sometimes I scroll through my contacts wanting to reach out, and then I just…don’t. I stare at my phone until the feeling passes and go back to pretending again.
