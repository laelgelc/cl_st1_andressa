I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time.

I have people in my life—coworkers, family, a couple of old friends—but I still feel like I’m watching everything through glass. I’m there, I participate, I laugh at the right times, but it never feels like anyone actually knows me. When I start to open up, I can see people get uncomfortable or change the subject, so I pull it back and go back to the surface-level stuff.

Most nights I just scroll on my phone until I’m tired enough to sleep. I tell myself I’ll reach out to someone, but I don’t know what to say that doesn’t sound needy or awkward. Then I feel even lonelier because I realize no one is really reaching out to me either, unless I initiate.

It’s like I’m constantly “almost connected” but never actually there. I’m starting to wonder if this is just how my life is going to feel.
