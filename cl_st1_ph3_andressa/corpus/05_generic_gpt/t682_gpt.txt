I’ve been feeling like a ghost lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling through my phone—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else’s life from behind glass.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers, some acquaintances I could text if I really wanted to. But there’s this gap between “knowing people” and actually feeling known that I can’t seem to cross. Conversations stay on the surface: weather, shows, small jokes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually waited for the answer.

What’s weird is that I’m tired of my own company but also exhausted by the idea of trying to connect. I overthink every message, every silence, every time I’m the one who reaches out first. It starts to feel like evidence that I’m just…optional.

Sometimes I wonder if this is just how adulthood is for some people, or if I somehow missed a step everyone else figured out.
