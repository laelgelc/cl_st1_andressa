I’m starting to feel like I exist in the gaps between other people’s lives.

I have acquaintances, people I can joke with at work or message occasionally, but no one I feel truly close to. I don’t get invited to things. When I try to initiate plans, people are “busy” or we reschedule endlessly until it just dies. It makes me wonder if I’m just… optional.

What hurts most is the silence. My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. I scroll through social media and see everyone else in group photos, inside jokes, trips, weddings. I’m always the person who “likes” the post, never the one in it.

I’ve started talking less because I feel like I’m bothering people when I open up. Then I feel even more disconnected. It’s like I’m slowly fading out of my own life.
