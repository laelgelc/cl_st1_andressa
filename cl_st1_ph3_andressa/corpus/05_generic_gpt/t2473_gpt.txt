I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I still feel completely disconnected. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. 

I scroll through my phone hoping someone will message first, then feel pathetic when no one does. When I do reach out, I overthink every word and usually convince myself I’m being annoying. 

Nights are the worst. The silence feels heavy, like it’s pressing on my chest. I don’t even know what I’m missing anymore, just that something’s gone.
