I don’t even know if I’m lonely “enough” to post here, but lately it feels like my life is just…background noise.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. But no one who really knows me. I’ll be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else actually participate in life while I just imitate it.

I’m tired of being the one who listens to everyone’s problems and then goes home to silence. My phone only lights up for work emails or group chats where I could disappear and no one would notice. I try to initiate plans and it’s always “we should sometime” and then nothing.

I keep wondering if I’m somehow fundamentally uninteresting, or if I missed some crucial step where everyone else learned how to turn casual connections into real ones. It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like my default setting.
