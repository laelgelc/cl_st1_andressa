I’m starting to feel like I don’t actually exist outside of other people’s convenience. Friends text when they’re bored, not because they want me specifically. I’m always the one asking to hang out, and when I stop initiating, everything just goes silent. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect. I keep wondering if I’m just fundamentally unlikeable, or if I somehow missed the class where everyone learned how to be close to each other.
