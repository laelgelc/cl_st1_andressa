I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, I can be polite, I can even be “funny” in that surface-level way, but it never goes deeper. It feels like I’m always playing a role and then going home alone to the real version of me that no one sees.

Most of my “friendships” are just group chats that have gone quiet or people I only hear from when they need something. I’ll see photos of people I used to be close to, out together, and it hits me that they just… moved on. No big fight, no drama. I just slowly stopped being someone they thought of.

I keep thinking it must be my fault. I overthink every interaction: Did I say something weird? Was I too quiet? Too needy? I end up withdrawing because it feels safer than risking another slow fade-out.

The worst part is how normalized it’s become. Days pass where I don’t speak to anyone outside of work or basic errands, and it barely even surprises me anymore.
