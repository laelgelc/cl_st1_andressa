I’m starting to feel like my life is just a series of quiet rooms. I go to work, I do what I’m supposed to do, I make small talk that feels like reading from a script, and then I come home to four walls and a screen. People would probably say I’m “not alone” because I have coworkers, acquaintances, group chats. But none of it feels like it actually touches me.

I don’t know how to bridge the gap between “friendly” and “close.” I can be funny, helpful, even comforting to others, but I never feel like someone’s first choice. I’m always the backup plan, the extra chair at the table, the person you invite so it doesn’t look empty.

I’m not sure what scares me more: that this is just a phase, or that this is simply what my life is going to be like.
