Lately I feel like I’m watching my own life from behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. I’m always the “nice, reliable one,” never the person someone chooses first. I overthink every message I send, worried I’m being annoying, so I stay quiet and then feel invisible. It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely unnecessary.
