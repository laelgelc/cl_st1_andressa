I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family nearby, but everything feels surface-level, like we’re all reading from a script. I’m scared to say “I’m lonely” out loud because it sounds pathetic, like I’ve failed at something basic. I scroll through chats I’m too afraid to start, stare at my phone hoping someone will message first, and then feel stupid for caring so much.
