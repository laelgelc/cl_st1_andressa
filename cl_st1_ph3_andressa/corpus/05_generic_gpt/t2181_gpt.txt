Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside, like a background character in everyone else’s story. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. I talk to people every day, but it all feels like small talk on top of a huge empty space.

I have “friends” but they’re more like people I used to be close to. We send memes and “we should hang out soon” messages that never turn into plans. When I do see people, I feel like I’m performing a version of myself that’s easier to be around, and then I come home drained and weirdly more lonely than before.

What messes with my head is that from the outside, my life probably looks fine. I have a job, a place to live, hobbies. But there’s no one I’d feel comfortable calling at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. Sometimes even at 2 p.m. I’m not sure who I could text without feeling like I’m bothering them.

It’s a strange feeling to be this surrounded and still feel this alone.
