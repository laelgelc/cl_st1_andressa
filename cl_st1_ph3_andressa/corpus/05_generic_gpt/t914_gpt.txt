I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay” around them.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. But no one I’d call if something really bad happened. I scroll through my contacts and there isn’t a single name that feels safe. It’s like I’m surrounded by people and still completely untouchable.

I keep thinking it’s my fault: I cancel plans, I overthink messages, I convince myself they’re just being polite. Then I feel hurt when no one insists.

The worst part is how quiet everything feels when I get home. It’s not dramatic, just this low hum of emptiness that never really shuts off.
