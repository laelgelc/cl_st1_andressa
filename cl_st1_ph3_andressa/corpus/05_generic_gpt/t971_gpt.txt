I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing.

I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, people who’d say “we should hang out sometime,” but no one who actually reaches out unless I initiate. And I’m getting tired of being the one who keeps conversations alive. When I stop messaging, everything just…stops.

I scroll through social media and see people tagging each other in inside jokes, casual photos, random “thinking of you” messages. I can’t remember the last time someone thought of me without a reason or obligation. Birthdays feel like a performance where people post on my wall but don’t really know me.

What scares me is how normal the loneliness is starting to feel. I go entire weekends without speaking out loud, except to a cashier. Then Monday comes, and I put on the “I’m fine, just busy” mask again.

I don’t know how people build real connections from where I am now.
