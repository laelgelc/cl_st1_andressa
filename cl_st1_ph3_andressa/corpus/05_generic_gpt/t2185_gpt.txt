Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone without anything dramatic actually happening. My friends are “busy,” which I understand, but the invitations just stopped coming and I don’t know when that shift even happened. I scroll through group chats where I used to be active and now I mostly just react with an emoji so I don’t feel like I’ve vanished completely.  

I talk to coworkers, cashiers, neighbors, but it’s all surface-level. I miss feeling known. I miss having someone text me first for no reason. Nights feel the worst: phone in hand, no one to message who wouldn’t see it as weird or needy. It’s like I’m surrounded by people, but somehow not really there.
