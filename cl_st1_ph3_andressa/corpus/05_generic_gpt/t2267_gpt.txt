I feel like I’m stuck in this weird in‑between where I’m not completely isolated, but I never really feel *known* by anyone. I have people to talk to, but it’s all surface-level: work, memes, small talk. When conversations end, it’s like I disappear again. I overthink every interaction, replaying what I said and convincing myself I sounded awkward or boring, which makes me pull back even more. I crave connection so badly, but when I get the chance, I freeze up and default to “I’m fine.” Then I go home and wonder why nobody sees me.
