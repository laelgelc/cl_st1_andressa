I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, joke, even seem outgoing, but it’s like I’m acting out a script I only half remember. When conversations end, there’s this heavy silence that feels louder than anything I said.

I scroll through chats and see long gaps, unanswered messages, or ones I never sent because I didn’t want to “bother” anyone. I keep telling myself I’m just busy or tired, but the truth is I feel quietly invisible. I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m pressed up against the glass, watching everyone else live.
