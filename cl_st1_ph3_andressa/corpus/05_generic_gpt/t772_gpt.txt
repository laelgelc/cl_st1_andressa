I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but everything feels like acting. I can be funny, helpful, “easy to talk to,” and still go home feeling like no one actually knows me. 

I keep trying to open up a little, but I either overshare and feel embarrassed or stay surface-level and feel invisible. It’s like there’s a glass wall between me and everyone else. They see a reflection, not what’s behind it. 

I’m not completely isolated, but the loneliness feels worse when I’m around others. Sometimes I wonder if I missed some crucial step where everyone else learned how to be real with each other and I just…didn’t.
