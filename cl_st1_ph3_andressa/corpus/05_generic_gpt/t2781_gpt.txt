I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but it all feels like background noise. No one really checks in unless I reach out first, and even then it feels like I’m interrupting something more important.

I miss feeling chosen. Not as a backup plan, not as the “oh yeah, we should invite them too,” but as someone’s first thought. I overanalyze every message I send, terrified I’m being annoying, so I send fewer and fewer. The silence that follows feels like confirmation that I was never that important to begin with.
