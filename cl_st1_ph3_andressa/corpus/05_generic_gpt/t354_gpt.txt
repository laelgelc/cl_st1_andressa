I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can make small talk with. I can even be funny sometimes. But it all feels like I’m performing a version of myself that people find acceptable, and then I go home and just…disappear. No one checks in, no one really knows what’s going on with me, and I’m not sure anyone would notice if I quietly drifted out of their lives.

What makes it worse is that I don’t even trust my own perception. I’ll replay conversations in my head, trying to figure out if I said something weird, if that pause was awkward, if they were just being polite. It’s exhausting.

I’m not completely isolated, but I feel emotionally exiled. Surrounded by people and still somehow on the outside, watching everyone else know how to be human while I keep fumbling the instructions.
