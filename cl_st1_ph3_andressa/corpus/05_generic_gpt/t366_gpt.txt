I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I keep trying to open up a bit and it either gets brushed off or turned back to them.

What hurts is that I used to have close friends. Late-night talks, stupid inside jokes, that feeling of being someone’s “person.” Somehow, over the years, people moved, got partners, got busy. I guess I did too, but I didn’t find anything to replace what I lost.

Now I spend most evenings scrolling, lurking, half-hoping someone will message first. I replay old memories like a highlight reel and wonder if that was the peak. It’s like I’m stuck watching life from behind glass, present but not really participating.
