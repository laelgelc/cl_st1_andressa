I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. I scroll through my phone and there’s no name that feels “safe.” 

What bothers me is how invisible this feels. From the outside I probably look fine, maybe even social. Inside it’s like I’m always on the outside of the glass, watching everyone else have real lives and real friendships, and I’m just… there, filling space.
