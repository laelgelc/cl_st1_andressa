I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m just drifting through my own life as a background character.

I have people around me—coworkers, a couple of old friends I text occasionally, family chats—but I don’t feel actually *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, memes, “how’s it going?” followed by the automatic “fine.” I can’t remember the last time someone asked a follow-up question and really wanted the answer.

Nights are the worst. I’ll be scrolling, half-watching something, and suddenly it hits me how quiet it is. Not just the room, but the fact that no one is thinking, “I wonder how they’re doing right now.” I’m not sure what’s more painful: the loneliness itself, or the fear that this is just how things will be for me, and that I’m somehow the common denominator.
