I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there isn’t a single person I feel comfortable messaging without overthinking it.

I’m tired of being the “quiet one” in every group and then going home and wishing someone would notice I’m not just quiet, I’m lonely. I see people effortlessly making plans, forming inside jokes, becoming “regulars” in each other’s lives. I feel like I’m always orbiting other people’s worlds, never really part of them. I’m not even sure what I’m asking for here. I just needed to say it somewhere.
