I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I talk to people every day—coworkers, neighbors, even family—but it all feels like small talk on top of a huge empty space. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live. I don’t really have anyone I can message at 2am when my brain won’t shut up. I scroll, I lurk, I “like” things, but it never turns into real connection. Mostly I just feel tired of my own company.
