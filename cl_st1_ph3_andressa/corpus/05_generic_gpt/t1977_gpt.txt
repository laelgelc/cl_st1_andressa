I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is just…background noise.

I have people around me. Coworkers, some acquaintances, family chats that light up my phone. But none of it feels like it’s meant for me. Conversations skim the surface, jokes and small talk, and I play my part, laugh at the right times, but I’m not actually *there*. It’s like I’m standing behind glass watching everyone else live.

I keep thinking how weird it is that no one really knows me. Not the version I keep polished and acceptable, but the actual mess underneath. The thoughts I don’t say out loud because I’m scared they’ll sound needy or weird or “too much.” So I keep quiet, and then I resent that no one sees me.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I see people talking about their people—friends, partners, “my person.” I can’t even imagine what it feels like to be someone’s first choice. Not the backup plan, not the person they text when everyone else is busy.

Just…someone’s person.
