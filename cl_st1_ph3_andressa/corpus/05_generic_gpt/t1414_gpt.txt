I’ve been feeling like I’m living behind glass lately. I go to work, talk to people, crack jokes, join meetings, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who’s better at being human than I actually am. When the day ends and I come home, it’s like someone hits mute on my life.

My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from some app. I scroll through chats where I used to talk to people regularly and see how every conversation just slowly faded out. No big fight, no dramatic ending—just… less and less, until nothing.

I keep thinking there must be something wrong with me that I can’t seem to maintain close friendships or find anyone who really wants to know me beyond surface level. I’ve tried joining groups, hobbies, online communities. I show up, participate, then somehow stay on the outside.

It’s a weird mix of wanting to be seen and being terrified that if someone actually sees me, they’ll just confirm my worst fears and walk away.
