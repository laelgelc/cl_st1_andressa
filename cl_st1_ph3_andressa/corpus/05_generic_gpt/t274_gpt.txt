I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I see others making plans, having group chats, sharing inside jokes, and I feel like I’m watching life from behind glass. I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. When I try to open up, I either overshare or shut down, then replay every word later and cringe. I’m tired of pretending I’m fine with being alone all the time. It feels less like solitude and more like being forgotten.
