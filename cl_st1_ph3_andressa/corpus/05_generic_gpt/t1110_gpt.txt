I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.”

On paper I’m not isolated: I have coworkers, acquaintances, family chats, the occasional group outing. But everything feels like I’m watching my own life through a window. I talk, I laugh at the right moments, I send memes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character whose lines I only half remember.

I keep wanting someone to really see me, but when I get close to that point I shut down or make a joke and change the subject. Then I go home and feel angry that no one understands me, even though I’m the one hiding. It’s like I’m terrified of being known and equally terrified of never being known.

Nights are the worst. That’s when the silence gets heavy and I start wondering if anyone would actually notice if I slowly drifted out of their lives, or if I’ve already started to.
