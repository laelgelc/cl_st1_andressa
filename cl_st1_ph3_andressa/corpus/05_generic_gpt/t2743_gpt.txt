I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too noticeable at the same time. In groups, I’m the quiet one people forget to include, but when I try to speak up, it feels like I’m interrupting something important. I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if things fell apart. 

Most of my “conversations” are scrolling through chats I’m not part of, or typing messages and deleting them because I don’t want to seem needy. I’m always the one who reaches out first, and when I stop, everything just…stops. 

I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong socially, or if this is just how adulthood works now—everyone busy, everyone tired, and some of us quietly drifting to the edges.
