I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too visible at the same time. Invisible in the sense that no one really checks in, no one really knows what’s going on with me. Too visible because in every group setting I’m painfully aware of how out of place I feel, like everyone can see that I don’t belong there.

My days are mostly quiet. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. I have people I talk to, but it’s all surface level: work updates, memes, “how are you?” followed by “I’m fine.” I can’t remember the last time I was truly honest with someone in real life.

What scares me is how normal this has started to feel. The silence, the empty weekends, eating alone, pretending I’m okay with it. I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe just proof that I’m not the only one drifting like this.
