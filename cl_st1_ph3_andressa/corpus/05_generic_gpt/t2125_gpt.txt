I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly fading out of everyone’s lives and no one noticed when it started.

My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from some app I don’t care about. I scroll through chats and see long stretches of blue from me, short replies from others, and then silence. I keep telling myself people are just busy, but it’s hard not to read it as “you’re not worth the effort.”

I’m surrounded by people at work and still feel like I’m behind glass. I can do small talk, I can joke around, but as soon as things get even slightly personal, I freeze up or make it about them instead.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. Like I’m getting used to being invisible, and I don’t know if that’s better or worse.
