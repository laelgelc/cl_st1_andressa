I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people almost every day—at work, on the bus, online—but it all feels like background noise. I talk to coworkers, I crack small jokes, I nod along, yet I walk home and it’s like none of it actually touched me.

I’m not sure when it shifted from “I like my own space” to “I don’t know how to be close to anyone anymore.” I overthink every message I send, every silence in a conversation, and then I usually just… don’t reach out. It’s like I’m waiting for someone to notice I’m lonely without having to say it out loud.
