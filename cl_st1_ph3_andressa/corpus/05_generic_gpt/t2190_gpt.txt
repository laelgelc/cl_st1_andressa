I’m starting to realize I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I feel safe being fully myself around. Group chats go silent when I speak. Invitations happen around me, not to me. I overthink every interaction afterward, replaying sentences like evidence in a trial. I keep wondering if I’m actually hard to love, or if I’ve just convinced myself that I am.
