I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing. I have acquaintances, a few group chats, coworkers I joke with, but no one who actually checks in on me. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if this is just how adulthood quietly isolates you until you stop expecting anything different.
