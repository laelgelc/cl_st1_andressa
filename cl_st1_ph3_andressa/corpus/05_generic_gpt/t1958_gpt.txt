It’s weird how I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I can talk, joke, even seem confident, but it’s all surface-level. No one really knows me, partly because I don’t know how to let them. I overthink every message, every silence, and then end up withdrawing. Nights are the worst—phone in hand, scrolling, wanting to reach out to someone and realizing I don’t actually have anyone to text.
