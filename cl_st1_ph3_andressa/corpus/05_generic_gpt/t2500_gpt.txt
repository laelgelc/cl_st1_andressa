Lately I feel like I’m drifting through my own life as a background character. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I’m craving the kind of connection where you can say, “I’m not okay,” and someone genuinely wants to hear the rest.

What makes it worse is that I used to have that. Over the years, people moved away, got partners, had kids, and I somehow stayed stuck in the in-between. I try to reach out, but I always feel like I’m interrupting lives that are more important than mine.

Most days I just scroll, lurk, and pretend I’m fine. It’s starting to feel permanent.
