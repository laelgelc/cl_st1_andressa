I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people most days – coworkers, neighbors, random small talk – but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s my fault or just how things are now.

I’ve tried joining groups, talking more, saying yes to invitations. It always ends the same way: surface-level conversations, then everyone goes back to their own lives and I’m left overthinking every word I said. I can’t tell if I’m boring, awkward, or just not worth the effort.

What scares me is how used to this I’m becoming. Nights blur together: phone in hand, scrolling, half-hoping someone will message first, half-expecting no one will. I miss feeling important to someone, like my existence actually changes their day, even a little.
