I’m starting to think I’m one of those people who just slips through the cracks of everyone’s life.

I have acquaintances, people who’ll chat in group settings, but no one who’d notice if I disappeared for a week. My phone only lights up for work, spam calls, and the occasional “sorry, just saw this!” reply to a message I sent days ago. I keep trying to initiate plans, and people either cancel last minute or say “we should definitely hang out sometime” and then never follow up.

What’s messing with me is how invisible it feels. I’ll be in a room full of people laughing and still feel like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I overthink everything I say, wondering if I’m boring or just fundamentally unlikeable.

I’m tired of pretending I’m fine with “low-maintenance friendships” when it just feels like no one actually needs me around.
