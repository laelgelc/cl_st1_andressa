I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can do small talk at work, I can crack a joke in a group chat, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that goes home and powers down.

Most days are just… quiet. I wake up, scroll, go to work, come back, stare at my phone or laptop, and then go to sleep. I can go an entire weekend without using my voice for anything other than “thank you” at a checkout. It’s like my life is technically full of “contacts” but empty of any real contact.

What messes with my head is that I’m not a total recluse. People say I’m “nice,” “easy to talk to.” But no one really knows me, and I don’t know how to let them. Any time I think about opening up, I feel stupid, dramatic, or like I’m about to scare them off.

Sometimes I wonder if this is just how it’s going to be: surrounded by people, but basically on my own.
