I’ve been feeling like I’m living behind glass. I go to work, say the right things, laugh at the right moments, and then come home to a silence that feels heavier every day. I have acquaintances, not friends. People I can message about a show, but not about how scared I am of being alone forever. I keep trying to “put myself out there,” but it feels like everyone else already has their people. I’m starting to wonder if something about me is just fundamentally unrelatable, and I can’t figure out how to fix that.
