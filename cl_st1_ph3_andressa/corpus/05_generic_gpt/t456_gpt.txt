I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me in the technical sense—coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family chats that light up on holidays—but it all feels like I’m just “there,” not really part of anything. Conversations stay on the surface: work, memes, random small talk. No one really asks how I am in a way that suggests they actually want the real answer, and I’m not sure I’d know how to give it if they did.

I miss feeling known. I miss when someone could tell from a single word or a look that something was off. Now I can go days without anyone noticing if I go quiet, and honestly, I’m getting good at disappearing.

I keep telling myself I should reach out more, join things, initiate plans. But there’s this heavy, tired part of me that’s convinced I’m just forcing myself into spaces where no one is actually waiting for me.
