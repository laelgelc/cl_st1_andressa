I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. When I’m around others I feel like I’m acting out a version of myself that’s easier to digest, and then I go home and crash into this heavy silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives. It’s like I’m always almost included, but never really chosen.
