I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I don’t exist to any of them.

I go to work, talk to coworkers, answer emails, sit in meetings, but it’s all surface-level. Nobody really knows anything about me beyond what I’m paid to do. When I come home, it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen through a screen and wonder how they all seem to find “their people” so naturally.

I’ve tried joining groups, being more “out there,” but it always feels like I’m hovering at the edge of everyone else’s circle. I’m invited sometimes, but more as a filler than someone who’d be missed. I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. Like I’m getting used to the emptiness, and I don’t know if that’s better or worse.
