I’m starting to think I’ve forgotten how to be close to people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. When something good or bad happens, I open my phone and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging first. I’m always “the listener,” the one others vent to, but when I try to share, conversations get shallow or drift away. I go days without hearing my name spoken out loud. It’s strange how busy life can look from the outside while feeling completely hollow inside.
