I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, family chats buzzing on my phone, but I don’t feel like I can be honest with any of them. Every interaction feels scripted: small talk, jokes, “I’m fine, you?” on repeat.  

What I’m really thinking is that I feel invisible and replaceable. I overthink every message I send, then delete it and stay quiet. I’m scared that if I show how lonely I actually am, it’ll just push people further away.
