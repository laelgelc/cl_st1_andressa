I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one I can text when something small happens, good or bad. My phone is either silent or full of group chats where I’m basically invisible.

I’m tired of being “the quiet one” who listens to everyone else’s problems but never gets asked how I’m doing. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I retreat again and tell myself it’s safer this way.

Weekends are the worst. I scroll through social media and see everyone else out together, and it just highlights how replaceable I seem to be. I’m not sure if people don’t care, or if I’ve just made myself too small to notice.
