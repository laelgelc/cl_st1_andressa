I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

I have people around me—coworkers, neighbors, some old friends online—but I don’t feel actually known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I laugh, I respond, I play the role, and then I go home and it’s like the silence just swallows everything.

I don’t really know how to bridge the gap between “friendly” and “close.” Every time I think about opening up more, I imagine the other person feeling awkward or burdened, so I just don’t. Then I watch other people form these tight bonds and it makes me wonder what’s wrong with me that I can’t seem to do the same.

It’s a weird combination of craving connection and wanting to hide. I want someone to ask how I really am and actually want to hear the answer, but I’m not sure I’d know how to respond if they did.
