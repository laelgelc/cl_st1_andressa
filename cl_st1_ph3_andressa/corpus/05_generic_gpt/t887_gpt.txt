I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I only know how to perform like I’m connecting.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people who’d say “we should hang out sometime,” but somehow it never turns into anything real. When I’m with others I can be funny, attentive, even “social,” but I walk home feeling like a background character in my own life. Nobody really knows me, and I’m not sure I’ve given anyone the chance.

What scares me is how routine it’s become. I wake up, work, scroll, distract myself, sleep. Days pass without a single genuine conversation. I keep thinking I’ll “fix” it when I’m less busy, more interesting, more something. But time keeps moving and nothing changes.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just needed to say out loud that being this alone all the time is starting to feel permanent.
