I’m starting to worry that I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. I go whole weekends without speaking out loud, except to say “thank you” at a checkout or “have a good one” at work.  

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like an outsider watching everyone else who already has shared stories and inside jokes. I leave feeling more alone than before.  

I scroll through my contacts sometimes, trying to figure out who I could message just to talk, and I close it again because I can’t think of anyone who wouldn’t find it weird.
