I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I have people around me—coworkers, neighbors, even some acquaintances—but it all feels shallow, like I’m just playing a role in conversations. I’m always the one listening, nodding, making jokes, but no one really knows me beyond the surface. When I try to open up, I feel awkward and exposed, like I’ve broken some unspoken rule.  

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, but I don’t know who wouldn’t see it as weird or needy. It’s like I’m surrounded by noise but starved for actual connection.
