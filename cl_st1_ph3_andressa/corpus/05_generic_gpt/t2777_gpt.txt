I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I joke with or work with, but no one I could call at 2 a.m., or even 8 p.m., honestly. I watch everyone else’s lives move in these predictable patterns—relationships, group chats, weekend plans—while mine is just… silence punctuated by scrolling. I second-guess every message I send, then usually delete it. When someone cancels plans, I act relieved, then feel pathetic later. It’s like I’m both desperate for company and exhausted by the idea of trying.
