I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I’ll get messages, but they’re surface-level, and I don’t feel safe going deeper because I’m scared they’ll pull away. Then I resent that no one “sees” me, even though I’m the one hiding. It’s like I’m trapped behind glass, watching life happen around me, but never quite in it.
