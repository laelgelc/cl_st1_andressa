Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, make small talk, answer emails, and everyone probably thinks I’m functioning fine. But I don’t feel actually connected to anyone. It’s like I’m constantly “on stage,” playing the role of a normal person, then going home to complete silence.

I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call in a crisis or even text something random to. Group chats move without me. If I disappeared, I’m honestly not sure how long it would take anyone to notice beyond missed obligations.

I try to initiate conversations, but they fizzle out or stay surface-level. I can’t tell if I’m boring, awkward, or just forgettable. I’m tired of pretending I’m okay with this, but I also don’t know what to do differently anymore.
