I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone without anything dramatic actually happening.

On paper, I’m “fine.” I go to work, I answer emails, I make small talk about weekends and TV shows. I laugh in the right places. But the second I’m alone again, it feels like the sound just cuts out and I’m left in this heavy silence that I don’t know what to do with.

I have people in my life, but I don’t feel *known* by any of them. Conversations never seem to move past the surface. I’ll think about saying something real, like “I’m lonely” or “I feel disconnected from everything,” and then I swallow it because I don’t want to be the weird one who kills the vibe.

What makes it worse is how invisible this feels. If I say I’m busy or tired, people accept it. If I said, “I’m lonely,” I don’t even know how they’d respond, or if they’d just feel awkward and pull away.

Most nights I end up scrolling or watching something until I’m tired enough to sleep, just trying to not think too hard. Then I wake up and do the same loop again. It’s like living around people instead of with them.
