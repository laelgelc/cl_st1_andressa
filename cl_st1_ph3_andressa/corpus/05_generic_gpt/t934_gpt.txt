I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to connect with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one I can text without overthinking every word. When I’m around others I feel like I’m acting, mirroring what they say, laughing at the right times, then going home drained and weirdly empty.  

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, watch other people’s lives happen, and it just highlights how quiet mine is. I’m not isolated physically, but I still feel completely unseen.
