Lately I feel like I’m drifting through my own life as a background character. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances—but no one who really knows me. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or being weird. Nights are the worst; I scroll endlessly, hoping for some connection that never really comes. It’s strange to be surrounded by noise and still feel this painfully unseen.
