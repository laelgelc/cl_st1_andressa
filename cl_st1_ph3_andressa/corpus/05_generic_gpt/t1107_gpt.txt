I’ve started to notice how quiet my life actually is. I go entire weekends without speaking out loud, except to say “thanks” to a delivery driver. My phone lights up only with notifications from apps, never from people. I scroll through group photos of others and feel like I’m watching a world I somehow missed the entrance to. When coworkers talk about their plans, I nod along and pretend I’m busy too. I’m not. I just go home, sit in my room, and try to make the silence feel less heavy. It never really works.
