I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

On paper I’m “fine”: job, roof over my head, a couple of people I text sometimes. But I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface—work, memes, small talk—and the moment I try to go even a little deeper, it feels like the air changes and people quietly back away.

I watch other people form these easy connections, inside jokes, group chats, weekend plans. I’m always the “extra” person, the one you invite if there’s room, never the one the plans are built around. I can go days without hearing my name spoken out loud by someone else.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. I eat alone, walk alone, scroll alone, and it’s starting to feel normal, but in a numb, hollow way. When something happens—good or bad—my first thought is still, “Who do I tell?” and the answer is usually no one.

I don’t know if I’m the problem or just forgettable. Maybe both.
