I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely alone. I have coworkers, a few acquaintances, family I text sometimes, but it all feels like I’m acting in a play instead of actually living my life. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I don’t know how to cross that invisible line into something real.

I watch other people make plans, send each other memes, talk about their day like it matters to someone. I don’t really have anyone I can message just to say “today sucked” without feeling like I’m being a burden or too much.

I’ve also gotten so used to my own company that I’ve forgotten how to let people in. When someone shows a bit of interest, I freeze or joke my way out of it. Then I go home, sit with the silence, and wonder if this is just how it’s going to be.
