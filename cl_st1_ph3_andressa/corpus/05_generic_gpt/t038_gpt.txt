I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d actually call if something genuinely good or bad happened. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of them are basically strangers I once knew a little better.

The weirdest part is how invisible it all feels. From the outside I probably look fine—busy, functioning, posting memes, making small talk. Inside I’m just constantly aware that no one really knows me, and I don’t really know anyone either.

I’m not even sure what I’m hoping for writing this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
