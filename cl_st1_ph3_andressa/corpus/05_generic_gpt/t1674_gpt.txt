I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I technically have “people” around me—coworkers, some acquaintances, family I text on holidays—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. If I try to say something real, it feels like I’ve broken some unspoken rule and everyone quickly steers away.

Most days I go to work, come home, scroll on my phone, and then lie in bed thinking about how quiet it is. I’ll open chat apps, stare at the list of names, and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging without worrying I’m being annoying or intrusive. So I just put the phone down and let the silence sit.

What bothers me is how invisible this all is. From the outside, I probably look fine—show up, smile, make small talk. Inside, I feel like I’m slowly drifting further away from everyone, like I’m behind glass watching life happen to other people.

I’m not even sure what I want from posting this. I don’t have some big story or turning point. I just needed to put into words that I’m lonely in a way that feels less like “I’m alone” and more like “I’m missing from my own connections,” if that makes sense.
