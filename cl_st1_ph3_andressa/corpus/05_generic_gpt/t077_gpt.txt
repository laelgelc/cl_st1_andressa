I’m starting to feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life. I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep, repeat. Group chats are muted, but even if they weren’t, no one really messages me directly. I’m always “included,” but never actually wanted. I miss having someone who checks in unprompted, who notices when I disappear for a few days. Lately it feels like if I vanished, it would mostly just be quieter.
