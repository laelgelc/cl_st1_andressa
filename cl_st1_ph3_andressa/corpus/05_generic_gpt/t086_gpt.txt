I’m starting to wonder if I’ve somehow aged out of being “reachable.” I have coworkers, neighbors, people I chat with, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me beyond surface-level facts, and I’m not even sure I remember how to let them. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, would anyone’s daily routine actually change?” I try to initiate plans, but they fizzle, or I get polite excuses. It’s like I’m always hovering at the edge of everyone else’s life, never quite important enough to be pulled into the center.
