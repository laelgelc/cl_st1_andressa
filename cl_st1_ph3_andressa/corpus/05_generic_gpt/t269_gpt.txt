I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m lonely. I go days without speaking out loud beyond “thank you” to a cashier. Group chats are muted, invitations stopped coming, and I’m not sure when that shift happened. I watch people my age getting married, traveling with friends, posting about “found family,” and I feel like I missed some secret class on how to be wanted. Mostly I just feel invisible, even to myself.
