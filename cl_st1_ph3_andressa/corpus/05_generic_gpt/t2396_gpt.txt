I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, groceries, scrolling on my phone—without really connecting with anyone. I have “people” around me, but most interactions feel shallow, like we’re all acting out some script of small talk and memes.

I don’t know how to cross that invisible line between acquaintance and actual friend. Whenever I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I retreat and then hate myself for it. It’s this loop of wanting closeness but fearing rejection more.

Nights are the worst. I keep thinking that if I disappeared, my routines would change more than anyone’s life would. I’m not sure how to fix this or even where to start.
