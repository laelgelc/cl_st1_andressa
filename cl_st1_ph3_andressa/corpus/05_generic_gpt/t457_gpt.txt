I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too visible at the same time.

On paper, I’m “not alone” – I have coworkers, some old friends I text every now and then, family chats that light up my phone. But none of it feels like it actually reaches me. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else participate in something I can’t quite access.

I’ve also noticed I’ve started editing myself down. I share less, keep things short, avoid saying what I really think or feel because I’m tired of the awkward silence that follows. Then I feel even more disconnected, which makes me withdraw further. It’s like I’m slowly erasing myself from my own life.

Nights are the worst. That’s when the weight of it hits—this sense that there’s no one who really knows me right now, not fully, not in the way I’m craving. And I’m not sure what to do with that.
