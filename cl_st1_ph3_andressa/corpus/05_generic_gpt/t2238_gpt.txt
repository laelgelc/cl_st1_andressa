I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, I answer messages, I crack jokes, and then I go home and it’s like I disappear. No one really knows me beyond the surface-level version I keep performing.

When something good happens, I have no one I feel comfortable texting first. When something bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and realize I’m mostly just background characters in other people’s lives. I’m there when they need something, but when I try to open up, the conversation somehow drifts back to them.

I’ve tried joining groups, going to events, talking more. It all feels like I’m pushing against glass: I can see connection happening for other people, but I can’t quite reach it. I’m not sure if I’m doing something wrong socially, or if this is just how life is going to be.
