I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to be around them.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people who’d say hi if they saw me in public. But no one really knows me. I keep conversations shallow because I’m terrified that if I show more, they’ll quietly drift away, and I won’t even get the surface-level stuff.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, maybe send a message and delete it before hitting send. I tell myself it’s fine, that I’m “independent,” but it feels more like I’ve built a life no one else needs to be part of.

I don’t know how people go from “we talk sometimes” to “you can call me at 2 a.m.” It feels like a language I never learned.
