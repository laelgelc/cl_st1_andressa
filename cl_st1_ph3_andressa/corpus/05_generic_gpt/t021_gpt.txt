I’m at that weird point where I’m technically “not alone,” but I feel completely disconnected anyway.

I have coworkers I talk to, neighbors I nod at, family group chats that light up my phone. But none of it feels like it’s actually for me. I can go days without anyone asking how I really am, and when they do, I don’t even know how to answer without sounding dramatic or needy.

I miss having someone who knows my little details. The kind of person who remembers what I’m anxious about next week, or sends a random meme because it’s “so you.” I scroll through old conversations with people I used to be close to and wonder how I became this… background character in my own life.

What makes it worse is that I’m starting to feel out of practice with people. Like I’ve forgotten how to be a friend, how to open up without oversharing, how to be interesting. When I do get social invitations, I feel like an impostor the whole time, then go home and replay every word I said.

It’s like I’m stuck between wanting to be seen and wanting to disappear, and I don’t know how to move from here.
