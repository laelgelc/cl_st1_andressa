I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, home—but it’s all on mute. People know me, but I don’t feel known. Conversations stay on the surface: weather, deadlines, “how was your weekend?” with no real interest in the answer.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel even when I’m around others. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I try to reach out sometimes, but I second-guess every message, worry I’m being annoying, and then delete it.

Nights are the worst. That’s when the silence feels loud and I start wondering if this is just how it’s going to be.
