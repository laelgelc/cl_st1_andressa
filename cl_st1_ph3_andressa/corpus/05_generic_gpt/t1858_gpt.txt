Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, I answer messages, I show up when I’m supposed to, but it all feels like acting. People think I’m “busy” or “independent,” but the truth is no one really knows me. 

I have friends, technically, but everything stays on the surface: memes, small talk, “we should hang out sometime” that never happens. I don’t know how to cross that invisible line into something real. I’m scared of being too much, so I stay quiet. Then I’m lonely because no one reaches out. 

Nights are the worst. The silence makes me realize how replaceable I feel.
