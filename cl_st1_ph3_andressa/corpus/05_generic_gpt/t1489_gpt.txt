I’m starting to think I forgot how to connect with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me. The silence after I get home feels heavier lately. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move forward—relationships, group photos, inside jokes—and I can’t figure out where I’m supposed to fit.  

I second-guess every message I send, worry I’m annoying or oversharing, so I end up saying nothing. Then the isolation just confirms the fear that I’m not someone people would choose to be close to.
