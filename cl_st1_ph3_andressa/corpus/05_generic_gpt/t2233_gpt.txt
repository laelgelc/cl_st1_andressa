I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, even when they’re right in front of me.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about work, shows, random stuff. But it all feels like I’m acting out a script I memorized years ago. I laugh at the right moments, ask how their weekend was, nod along. Then I go home and it’s just silence, and this heavy sense that none of those interactions meant anything.

I used to have close friends. Somehow they drifted away, or I did. I’m not even sure which. I scroll through old messages and see all these conversations that just… stopped. No big fight, no drama. Just slower replies, then nothing.

What scares me is how normal this has begun to feel. I’m getting used to the emptiness. And I don’t know if that’s acceptance or if I’m just shutting down.
