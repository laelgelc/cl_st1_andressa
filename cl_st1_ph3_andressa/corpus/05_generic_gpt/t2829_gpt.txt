Lately I feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s lives in slow motion. Friends are “busy,” texts get left on read, plans never quite materialize. I go days without speaking out loud to anyone except cashiers. I’m not exactly unhappy, just weirdly hollow, like I exist slightly to the side of the real world. I scroll and lurk and watch everyone else interact, but I can’t seem to insert myself anywhere. I’m tired of my own company, but it’s all I seem to have.
