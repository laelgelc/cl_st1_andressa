I’m starting to think I’ve forgotten how to connect with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one who actually knows me. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s nobody I feel comfortable messaging. I keep conversations surface-level because I’m scared of seeming needy, then feel invisible when nobody reaches out. It’s like I’m standing in a crowded room behind glass, watching everyone else live.
