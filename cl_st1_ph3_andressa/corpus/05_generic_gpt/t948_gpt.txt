I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing while still being physically here. My phone lights up, but it’s all notifications that don’t really involve me—ads, group chats where I’m just… there, unread messages I don’t know how to answer. I have people around me, technically, but I don’t feel known by any of them. When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel stupid afterward or stay quiet and feel fake. I miss feeling naturally included instead of like an afterthought someone remembers out of obligation.
