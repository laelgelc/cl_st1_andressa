I’ve started to realize that I can go days without having a real conversation with anyone. I talk to coworkers, answer emails, nod in meetings, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who knows the right lines. When I get home, the silence feels heavy, like the walls are absorbing whatever’s left of my voice.

I used to think loneliness meant having no one at all, but now it feels more like being surrounded by people who don’t actually see you. I’m not sure if it’s that I’ve pulled away or that no one really tries, maybe both. I scroll, lurk, watch other people’s lives happen, and somehow it makes me feel even more separate, like I’m pressing my face against a window I can’t open.
