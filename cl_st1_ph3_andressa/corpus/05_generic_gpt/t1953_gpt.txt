Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone without anything dramatic actually happening. My friends still exist, my family still messages me, but it all feels like I’m watching their lives through a window instead of being part of them. I reply to messages hours late because I don’t know what to say that doesn’t sound fake or forced. When I’m around people, I feel oddly more alone than when I’m by myself, like there’s this invisible layer between me and everyone else. I keep wondering if this is just who I am now, or if I somehow missed the point where I could’ve changed it.
