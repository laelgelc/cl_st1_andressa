I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, and it scares me.

On paper I’m “fine”: job, roof, no huge crisis. But my life feels like I’m watching it through a window. I talk to coworkers, make small jokes, pretend I’m part of things, but as soon as I get home it’s just silence. Hours of scrolling, lurking, half-writing messages I never send because I convince myself no one really wants to hear from me.

I’ve had friends before, but somehow every connection fades. People move on, get busy, find partners, new circles. I stay in this weird orbit around everyone else’s lives. When I try to reach out after a long time, the gap feels too big and I end up deleting the message.

What really gets to me is how invisible I feel. I can disappear for days and nothing changes for anyone but me. The world keeps going; I just sit here, wondering if this is all there is.
