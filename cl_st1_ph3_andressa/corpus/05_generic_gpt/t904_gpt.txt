I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of old friends I message sometimes, but it all feels…thin. Surface-level. I’m always “fine” when people ask, because I don’t know how to say “I feel invisible” without sounding dramatic.

What makes it worse is that I used to have close connections. Late-night conversations, people who actually knew my history. Now it’s small talk, memes, and unread messages. I scroll through my phone looking for someone I could actually call, and end up putting it down. Then I just sit there, in the quiet, feeling like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s lives, including my own.
