I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel like a ghost. I go to work, I make small talk, I even joke around sometimes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that everyone else believes in more than I do.

I have “friends,” but they’re more like acquaintances I orbit. We don’t talk about anything real. If I disappeared from our group chats, I’m not sure anyone would actually notice, just eventually assume I’m “busy.”

What really messes with me is how hard it is to admit I’m lonely without sounding pathetic or dramatic. I keep everything surface-level because I’m scared that if I show the real me, people will just quietly back away.

So I stay in this half-life of being seen but not really known.
