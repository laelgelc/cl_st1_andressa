I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s a weird difference. My days are full on paper: work, messages, scrolling, background noise. But I can go a whole week without a genuinely honest conversation. I have people around me, but I’m always the side character in their lives, never the person someone thinks of first.

I miss feeling known. Not just “how was your day?” but someone who actually remembers the answer from last time. I’m tired of performing “I’m fine” and then coming home to silence that feels heavier every night.
