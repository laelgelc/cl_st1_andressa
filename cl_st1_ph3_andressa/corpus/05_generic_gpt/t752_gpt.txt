I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever really did. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m acting a role instead of being a real person. I say the “right” things, laugh at the “right” times, and then go home feeling like I somehow tricked everyone into thinking I’m normal.

The worst part is how quiet it gets when I’m alone. Not just the physical quiet, but the mental one. No one is expecting a message from me. My phone only lights up with notifications from apps, not from people. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives unfold, and it just makes me more aware of how empty mine feels.

I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
