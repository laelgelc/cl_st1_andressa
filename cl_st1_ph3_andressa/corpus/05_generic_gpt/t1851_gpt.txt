I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is made up of small, disconnected scenes with no real story tying them together.

I go to work, talk to coworkers about projects and deadlines, maybe crack a joke, and then come home to silence. My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from some app. When it does light up, I get this tiny rush of hope, and then it’s just spam or a random notification. It’s embarrassing how disappointed I feel over something so small.

I’ve tried to be more social. I say yes to invitations when they come, but I always feel like the extra person, the one who could leave and nothing would really change. People are kind enough on the surface, but I can’t shake the feeling that no one actually thinks of me when I’m not physically there.

What really gets to me is the lack of emotional “witnesses” to my life. No one really knows what my day looked like, what I worried about, what I was proud of. I could have a horrible day or a great one, and it just disappears into the void.

It’s like I exist, but only in a very thin way, like a background character in everyone else’s story.
