I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore. I’m surrounded by coworkers, neighbors, random faces online, but it all feels like I’m watching life through a window. I say the right things in small talk, laugh at the right moments, and then go home and feel like I don’t exist to anyone once the conversation ends.

What scares me is how used to it I’m getting. I wake up, go through my routine, scroll through feeds, maybe send a meme to someone I barely know, and that’s it. Days pass and no one really checks in, and I’m not sure who I’d even call if something went wrong.

I keep thinking I must be the common denominator. Other people seem to just… form friendships. For me, everything feels forced or temporary, like I’m always the extra piece in someone else’s puzzle, never one of the main ones.
