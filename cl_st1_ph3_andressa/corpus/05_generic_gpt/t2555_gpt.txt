I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family—but almost no one I feel truly close to. Conversations stay on the surface: weather, work, “how was your weekend?” I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and had the time or interest to hear an honest answer.  

I scroll through chats and realize I’m usually the one reaching out, and even then it’s mostly dry small talk. When I’m alone, the silence feels heavy, but being with others doesn’t really make it lighter.
