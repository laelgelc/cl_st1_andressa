I don’t really know how to talk about this in real life, so I’m dumping it here.

I’m surrounded by people but feel weirdly invisible. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I’ll have a “busy” day and then come home and realize not a single interaction went deeper than small talk. It’s like I’m playing a role everywhere I go.

What bothers me most is how long it’s been this way. Years. I’ve tried joining clubs, going to events, talking more, talking less, being “easygoing.” It always stalls at the surface. I start to feel like there’s something fundamentally off about me that people can sense but never say.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, listen to podcasts, anything to fill the silence. The thought that this might just be how my life is going to look long term is starting to feel less like a fear and more like a possibility I should start getting used to.
