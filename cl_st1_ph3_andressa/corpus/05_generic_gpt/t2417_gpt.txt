I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, a few acquaintances—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, “how was your weekend?” I answer, but it’s like I’m playing a character that vaguely resembles me. No one sees the parts that are scared, tired, or just quietly desperate for connection.

I try to reach out sometimes, but I overthink every message. If they don’t reply quickly, I assume I’m annoying them. Then I pull back, which makes everything worse. It’s a loop I can’t seem to break.

Most days I just go home, put on something in the background, scroll endlessly, and realize hours passed without really existing for anyone.
