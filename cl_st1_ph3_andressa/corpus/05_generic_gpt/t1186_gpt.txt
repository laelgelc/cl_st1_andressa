I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. Friends, coworkers, family—I can talk about work, weather, memes, but nothing that feels like “me.” When I try, the words get stuck because I’m convinced they’ll find me boring or too much. I watch other people form easy connections and wonder what’s fundamentally off about me. I crave someone who genuinely wants to know me, not just the version I perform. Lately it feels like I’m fading into the background of my own life.
