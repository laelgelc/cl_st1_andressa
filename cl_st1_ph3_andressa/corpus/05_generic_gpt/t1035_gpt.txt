Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, make small talk, answer emails, but it all feels like acting. No one really knows me beyond the surface-level stuff. I have “friends,” but they’re more like people I occasionally share memes with. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate to message anyone because I’m not sure who would genuinely care, or if I’m just bothering them. I miss having someone who checks in first, unprompted. I scroll through chats and see long gaps, dead conversations, people who slowly drifted away. It makes me wonder if I’m the common denominator.
