I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people most days – coworkers, acquaintances, family chats – but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. I say the right things, laugh at the right moments, and then go home and feel like I never actually existed in anyone’s mind.

I don’t really have “a person.” No one I can message without overthinking it, no one who knows what my day-to-day life actually looks like. I scroll through my phone and realize most of my conversations are just logistics or memes, nothing real. When something hurts or something good happens, I catch myself thinking, “Who would I even tell?”

What makes it worse is feeling like I used to be better at this. I used to have close friends. Now it’s like the older I get, the harder it is to break past the surface. I’m tired of pretending this doesn’t bother me.
