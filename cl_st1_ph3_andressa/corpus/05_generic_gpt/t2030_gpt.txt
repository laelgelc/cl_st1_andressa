I’m starting to realize I’m not “busy and independent,” I’m just alone all the time. I go through entire days where my phone doesn’t light up once unless it’s a notification from some app. When something small happens—like a weird interaction at the store—my first instinct is to tell someone, and then I remember there isn’t really anyone who’d care enough to hear it.  

I have people I talk to, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m., and I’m not anyone’s first choice either. It’s this constant sense of being extra, like my presence is optional in every situation.
