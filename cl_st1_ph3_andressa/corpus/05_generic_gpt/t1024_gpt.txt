I’ve reached this weird point where I’m constantly surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d actually call if something really bad or really good happened. I keep conversations light because I’m scared of seeming needy, then feel invisible when no one really knows me. It’s like I’m watching my own life from the outside, and I don’t know how to step back in.
