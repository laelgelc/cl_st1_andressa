I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, old classmates, coworkers, but no one I can text without overthinking it first. I scroll through chats and see long gaps where I tried to keep conversations alive and they just… stopped.  

I keep telling myself to “put myself out there,” but I’m tired of being the one who initiates everything. When I stop trying, everything goes quiet, and the silence feels like proof that I don’t actually matter much to anyone.
