I’m starting to realize I don’t actually talk to anyone unless they need something from me. My phone lights up for work, favors, or logistics, but never just “hey, how are you?” I try to reach out, but conversations die quickly, like people are doing me a courtesy. I’m surrounded by others every day, yet feel like a background character in their lives. It’s getting harder to remember the last time I felt genuinely wanted, not just tolerated.
