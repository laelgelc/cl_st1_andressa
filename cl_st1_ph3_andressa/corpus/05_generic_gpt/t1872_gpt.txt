I keep realizing how replaceable I am to people. I’m “nice to have around,” but never the person anyone thinks of first. Friends hang out without me and say they “forgot to text.” Group chats die when I speak. I try to initiate plans and get vague “sometime soon” answers that never become actual dates. Online, conversations fade once I stop asking questions. It feels like I only exist in the gaps of other people’s lives, never as part of the core.
