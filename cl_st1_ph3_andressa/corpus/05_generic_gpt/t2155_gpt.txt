I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is happening behind glass.

I’m “busy” on paper. I go to work, make small talk, send memes in group chats, answer “how are you?” with “good, you?” and everyone accepts that. But I can’t remember the last time I felt genuinely known by anyone. Conversations feel like we’re all reading from a script, and the second I try to go off-script and say something real, people get uncomfortable or change the subject.

I’ve drifted from most of my old friends. No big fights, just slow fading. They have partners, kids, new cities, new priorities. I scroll through their photos and feel like a ghost who used to exist in that world. I don’t even know when I became this… extra person no one really needs.

I try to reach out, but it always feels like I’m imposing. I overthink every message, every pause in a conversation, every “seen” with no reply. Then I pull back to avoid feeling rejected, which just makes everything worse.

Nights are the hardest. The silence is loud. I keep the TV on just to have voices in the background, but it doesn’t change the fact that no one is actually here, and no one is expecting me anywhere.

I’m not sure what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe I just needed to say it somewhere.
