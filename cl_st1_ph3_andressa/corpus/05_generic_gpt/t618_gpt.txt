I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time.

On paper, I’m not “alone.” I have coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family group chats that light up with memes. But it all feels like static. I go through entire days where I don’t say anything real to anyone, and no one says anything real to me. Just “how’s it going,” “lol,” “nice,” and then silence.

The part that messes with my head is how performative everything feels. I can hang out, make jokes, seem fine. People tell me I’m “easy to talk to.” But I’m never actually saying what’s in my head. I feel like I’m playing a character that everyone likes more than the actual me. And then when I’m home, it’s just…quiet. The kind that makes you aware of every little sound in your apartment.

I’ve tried reaching out more, suggesting plans, being honest in small ways. Sometimes it works for a bit, but then people get busy, or they don’t respond, or the conversation dies and I’m left overanalyzing every message I sent. It makes me want to retreat even more, because it’s easier to say “I’m just tired” than “I feel disconnected from everything and I don’t know how to fix it.”

I don’t even know what I’m looking for writing this. Maybe just a place to admit that I’m lonely without having to immediately follow it up with a joke or a silver lining.
