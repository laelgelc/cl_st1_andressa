I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, only how to perform being “fine” around them.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. But nobody I can text when something small but important happens. I scroll through my contacts and it’s just names I’m too embarrassed to message because it’s been “too long.”

I overthink every interaction. If someone takes a bit longer to reply, I assume I’ve annoyed them. If I try to open up, I feel like I’m dumping. So I keep conversations light and then complain to myself that nothing feels real.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. Like I’m slowly becoming a background character in my own life and I don’t know how to step forward again.
