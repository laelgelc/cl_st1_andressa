I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life in slow motion.

People still text me sometimes, we still send memes, but it all feels like maintenance, not connection. Conversations never go deep. I’m always the one asking questions, listening, reacting. When I stop initiating, everything just goes quiet.

I’ve tried joining groups, going to events, talking to coworkers, but it’s like there’s an invisible wall between “friendly” and “actually close” that I can’t cross. I leave interactions feeling more alone than when I arrived.

What scares me most is how normal it’s starting to feel. I go through my days, do my job, eat, sleep, repeat. No one really knows what my life is like, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.
