I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family nearby, but no one I can text without overthinking it first. I replay every interaction, convinced I was awkward or annoying. I want real connection so badly, but when chances appear I freeze or make excuses. Nights are the worst—scrolling, lurking, watching everyone else’s lives move forward while mine feels stuck on mute. I’m tired of feeling invisible in my own life.
