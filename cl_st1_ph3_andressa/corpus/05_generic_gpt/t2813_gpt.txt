I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about shows or work or random stuff. But nobody I can text “hey, I’m not okay” without feeling like I’m crossing some invisible line. I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel close enough to bother.

Most of my time is spent in my room, on my phone or computer, cycling through apps just to feel less alone, but it usually makes it worse. Everyone looks like they’re busy living, while I’m just… existing on the sidelines, watching.

I keep thinking it’s my fault: I cancel plans, I’m awkward, I overthink every message. Then I get upset that no one pushes past my walls, even though I know that’s not fair.

I don’t even know what I want anymore—friends, connection, or just proof I’m not invisible.
