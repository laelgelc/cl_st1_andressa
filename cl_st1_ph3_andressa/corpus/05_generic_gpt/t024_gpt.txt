I’ve been feeling this weird combination of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people most days — coworkers, classmates, random small talk — but it all feels like background noise. Nobody really knows me, and I don’t know how to let them.

When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I stay surface-level and walk away feeling like I missed another chance. I keep replaying conversations in my head, analyzing every word, wondering if I sounded boring or annoying. Then I pull back even more the next time.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, but I have no idea who wouldn’t see it as an obligation or a bother. It feels like everyone else has these built-in circles of people, and I’m just orbiting outside, watching.
