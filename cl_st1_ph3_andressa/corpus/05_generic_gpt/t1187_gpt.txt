I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m around people every day—coworkers, neighbors, even family—but I don’t feel like I actually exist to anyone. Conversations never go past small talk, and when they do, I feel like I’m oversharing or making it awkward.

What scares me is how used to it I’m getting. I come home, scroll, watch something, go to bed. Days blur together. I tell myself I should reach out, but I don’t know who to message without feeling like I’m bothering them. The few times I have, the replies are slow or short, and I instantly regret it.

It’s not that I want a huge social circle. I just want one person who genuinely gets it when I say, “I’m not okay,” and doesn’t treat it like an inconvenience or a puzzle to fix.
