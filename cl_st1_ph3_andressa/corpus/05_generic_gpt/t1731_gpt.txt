Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m living in a parallel universe next to everyone else’s life instead of in it. I go through the motions—work, commute, scrolling on my phone—but I don’t really feel connected to anyone. I have “people” in my life, technically: coworkers, family chats, a couple of old friends I message every few weeks. But it all feels surface-level, like we’re actors reading lines instead of actually talking.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel. I can disappear for days and no one really notices unless I’m needed for something. I’m always the listener, the “reliable” one, but I don’t know where to put my own feelings. When I do try to open up, conversations get steered back to lighter topics so fast that I feel stupid for even trying.

It’s a strange kind of emptiness, being surrounded by people and still feeling completely alone.
