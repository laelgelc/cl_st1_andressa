I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but it’s like I’m not really there for any of it. I have acquaintances, people I can chat with about shows or memes, but nobody I can text when I’m falling apart at 2 a.m. I keep wondering if I missed some crucial stage where everyone else learned how to connect. I’m tired of pretending I’m “busy” instead of just…unwanted.
