I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. Like I take up space but somehow don’t really exist in anyone’s mind once I’m out of the room.

I have people I talk to, but it’s all surface-level: work, memes, “how’s it going?” followed by “good, you?” I don’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and actually wanted the messy, unfiltered answer. I keep censoring myself because I’m scared if I show the real stuff—neediness, anxiety, the constant fear of being a burden—people will quietly drift away.

Most days I’m surrounded by noise but feel like I’m in a soundproof box. I scroll, I reply, I “like,” but it doesn’t feel like connection. It feels like I’m mimicking what connected people do, hoping one day it’ll stop feeling so hollow.
