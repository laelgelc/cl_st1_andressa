I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

I go through my days doing everything I’m “supposed” to do—work, errands, small talk—but none of it feels connected to anything or anyone. I have acquaintances, people I joke with, but no one who actually knows what my life looks like when I go home and shut the door.

I keep wanting to reach out, then I overthink every message until I talk myself out of sending it. I worry I’m too much when I open up, and too boring when I don’t. So I hover in this weird in‑between where I’m lonely but also scared of being seen. It’s exhausting.
