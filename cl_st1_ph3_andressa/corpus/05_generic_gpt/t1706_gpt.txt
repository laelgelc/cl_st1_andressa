I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting through life without really existing to anyone. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m., no one who’d notice if I disappeared for a while. I try to initiate plans, but they fall through or feel forced, like I’m the extra piece in a puzzle that doesn’t need me.  

What hurts most is how invisible it feels. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I scroll through social media and see everyone else forming memories together while I’m just…here. It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like my default state.
