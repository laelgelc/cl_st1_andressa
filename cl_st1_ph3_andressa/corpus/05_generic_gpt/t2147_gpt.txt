I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family I talk to regularly, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being myself. I keep thinking I’m “too much” when I open up, and “too boring” when I don’t, so I end up saying nothing real at all. Nights are the worst—scrolling, lurking, watching everyone else’s lives move forward while mine feels stuck in this quiet, invisible place no one notices.
