I’m starting to think I’ve forgotten how to be close to people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one who really knows me. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging. It’s like I’m always “too much” or “not enough,” so I keep everything surface-level and then complain to myself that no one understands me. I watch other people make plans, share inside jokes, talk about “their people,” and I feel like I’m permanently stuck outside a window, looking in.
