Lately I feel like I’m drifting through everyone else’s lives as background noise. I have people around me, but I don’t feel genuinely known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface, and I’m too scared that if I open up, I’ll just make things awkward. I scroll through my phone wanting to message someone, then convince myself they’re too busy. It’s strange how exhausting it is to feel this alone while technically not being “alone” at all.
