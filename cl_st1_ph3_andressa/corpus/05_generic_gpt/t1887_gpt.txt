I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to connect with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one I can text when something small happens. I scroll through old chats where conversations just faded out and I can’t tell if it was them or me. I keep thinking I’m too boring, too quiet, too late to learn how to be close to anyone. It’s like I’m here, but not really in anyone’s life.
