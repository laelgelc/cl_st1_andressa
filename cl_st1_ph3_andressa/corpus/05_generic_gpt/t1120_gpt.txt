I’ve started to realize that most of my “social life” exists in my head. I replay old conversations, imagine future ones, and somehow that feels more real than what I actually have. I talk to coworkers, neighbors, cashiers, but it all feels like background noise. Nobody really knows me, and I’m not sure I remember how to let anyone in anymore.

I keep my phone close, pretending I’m waiting for a message, but I’m not. There’s no one to text. I scroll, lurk, comment sometimes, then delete. It’s like I’m haunting my own life, watching it instead of living it.
