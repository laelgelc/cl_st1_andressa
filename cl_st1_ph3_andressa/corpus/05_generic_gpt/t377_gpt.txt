It’s weird how I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with, but no one I’d actually call if something really bad happened. I scroll through chats and it’s all dead threads, half-started conversations I didn’t know how to keep alive.

I’m tired of being the “quiet one” everyone assumes is fine because I smile and get my work done. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone asked how I really am. It feels like I’ve forgotten how to connect beyond surface level, like I missed a class everyone else took on “how to be close to people.”

Nights are the worst. Everything slows down and there’s nothing to distract me from how empty my life feels when the noise stops.
