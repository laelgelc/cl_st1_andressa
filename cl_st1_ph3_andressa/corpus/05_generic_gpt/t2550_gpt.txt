I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, chores—but almost no one really knows me. I have acquaintances, people I joke with, but when I go home it’s just silence and the hum of my computer. I keep wanting to reach out, but I get stuck wondering if I’m just bothering people, so I don’t. Then I scroll through social media and see everyone else hanging out, getting married, having kids, and it feels like I’ve somehow missed some crucial step everyone else understood. It’s weird how you can technically “have people” in your life and still feel completely disconnected from all of them.
