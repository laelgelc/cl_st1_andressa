I’m starting to realize I’m not sure I know how to be close to anyone anymore. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family I text occasionally—but everything feels surface level, like I’m playing a role. I listen to other people, make them laugh, but I never feel safe enough to actually say what’s going on in my head.  

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, watch other people’s lives, and feel like I’m behind glass. I keep thinking there must’ve been a moment where I drifted too far from everyone, and now I don’t know how to swim back.
