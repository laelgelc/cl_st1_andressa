I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but it all feels like background noise. Nobody really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to. I try to join conversations and I can literally see people’s eyes drift past me, like they’re waiting for someone more interesting to show up. 

The worst part is how tired I am of my own company. I’m sick of my thoughts, my habits, my routines. But I don’t know how to break out of this version of myself that no one seems to choose.
