I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I have people around me—coworkers, family, acquaintances—but it never feels like anyone actually knows me. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to go deeper, it feels like I’m oversharing or making things awkward.

I scroll through chats and realize I’m usually the one who reaches out first. When I stop, things just…go silent. It makes me wonder if anyone thinks of me when I’m not right in front of them. I miss having someone who checks in unprompted, someone I can message without rehearsing what to say.

Most days I act fine, crack jokes, do my work. Then I go home, shut the door, and it’s just this heavy, echoing quiet.
