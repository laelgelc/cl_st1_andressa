I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I talk, I laugh at the right times, but it all feels like I’m playing a role that doesn’t belong to me. When I’m finally alone, it’s like the silence is louder than anything that happened during the day.

What gets to me most is that I don’t even know how to fix it. I try to reach out, but my messages feel forced, like I’m bothering people. I overthink every word, then usually delete it. I miss having someone I can just text without planning the conversation in my head first.

It’s exhausting feeling this disconnected and still having to function like everything is fine.
