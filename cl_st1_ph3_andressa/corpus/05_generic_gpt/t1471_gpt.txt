I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives. Friends are “busy,” messages get left on read, plans keep getting postponed until they just disappear. I go through entire days where my phone doesn’t light up unless it’s some app notification. I’m around people at work, but it’s all surface-level, nothing that feels like being known. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if I’ve just become easy to forget.
