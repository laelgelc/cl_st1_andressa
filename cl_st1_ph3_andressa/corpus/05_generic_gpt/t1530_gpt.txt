I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I technically “have people” around me—coworkers, a couple of old friends I talk to every few weeks, family chats that are mostly memes—but I still go entire days without feeling genuinely seen.

I notice it most at night. I’ll be lying in bed scrolling, wanting to message someone, but I can’t think of anyone I feel close enough to just say, “Hey, I’m lonely, can we talk?” It’s like every connection I have is stuck at this shallow, “How’s work?” level, and I don’t know how to push it any deeper without seeming weird or needy.

I’ve tried joining groups, hobbies, even going out alone to places where people socialize, but I always end up on the edge of things, watching conversations instead of being part of them. I feel like I’m missing some invisible social skill everyone else learned a long time ago.

What makes it worse is the sense that time is passing and nothing is changing. I keep thinking, “This can’t be it,” but most days it just…is.
