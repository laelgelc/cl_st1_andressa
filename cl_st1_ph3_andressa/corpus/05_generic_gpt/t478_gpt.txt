I’m starting to feel like I’ve slipped out of everyone’s peripheral vision. People still text me, I still show up to work, I still smile and nod in conversations, but it all feels like I’m playing a background character in my own life. When I try to open up, I either downplay everything or feel like I’m oversharing and scaring people away. I miss feeling genuinely known, like someone could look at me and understand what’s going on without me having to translate it. Lately, it just feels like I’m slowly fading into static.
