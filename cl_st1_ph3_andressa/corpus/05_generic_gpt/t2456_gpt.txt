I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. Like I take up space but somehow don’t really exist to anyone.

I have acquaintances, people I can chat with about work, shows, whatever. But nobody I feel safe being fully honest with. Every time I start to open up, I hear this voice in my head saying, “You’re being needy, they’re going to pull away,” and I end up changing the subject or making a joke.

What makes it worse is that on paper my life looks fine. Job, roof over my head, I’m not completely isolated. So when I try to explain the loneliness, I feel dramatic. I keep telling myself, “Other people have it worse,” and then I shut down and keep it to myself.

I miss feeling chosen. Not just tolerated or included by default, but actually wanted. I can’t remember the last time someone texted me first just to ask how I was doing, without needing something. I’m always the one reaching out, and when I stop, everything just…goes quiet.

I don’t really know how to fix it. I’m tired of advice that boils down to “join clubs” and “put yourself out there,” because I’ve tried, and it feels like I’m always stuck on the outside of everyone else’s already-formed lives.
