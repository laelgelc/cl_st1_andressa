I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but there’s this constant heaviness that comes from realizing no one really knows me. Not the “how was your weekend?” small talk, but the real stuff: what I’m afraid of, what I care about, what I’m actually thinking.

I have people around me, but it never seems to go deeper. I try to open up and the conversation drifts back to surface-level topics. Then I feel stupid for even trying.

Nights are the worst. I keep checking my phone, hoping someone will reach out, even though I know they won’t. And I don’t reach out either, because I’m starting to believe my absence wouldn’t matter.
