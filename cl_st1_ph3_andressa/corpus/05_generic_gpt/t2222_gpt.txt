I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to be close to people.

I have acquaintances, people I joke with, coworkers I get along with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts sometimes and it hits me that there isn’t a single person I’d feel comfortable bothering.

I keep thinking it’s my fault. I cancel plans, I second-guess every message I send, I assume people are just being polite when they invite me. I’m terrified of being “too much,” so I end up being nothing at all. Then I sit at home wondering why nobody really knows me.

The worst part is that I’m not technically alone. I’m surrounded by people every day, but it still feels like I’m living behind glass, watching everyone else connect while I just… exist nearby.

It’s exhausting pretending this doesn’t bother me as much as it does.
