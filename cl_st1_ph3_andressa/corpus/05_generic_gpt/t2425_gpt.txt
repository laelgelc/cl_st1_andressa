I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about surface stuff just fine—work, weather, some show I watched—but as soon as it gets personal, I freeze up. I either overshare and feel ashamed after, or I shut down and seem cold. Then I replay every interaction in my head for days, convinced I was weird or annoying.  

Most people think I’m “doing fine” because I function: job, errands, small talk. But I go home to silence, eat alone, and scroll until I’m too tired to think. It feels like I’m disappearing in plain sight.
