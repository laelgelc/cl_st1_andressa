I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of old friends I still text sometimes, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. I go home, close the door, and it’s like I disappear from the world. No one really checks in unless I initiate, and even then the conversations stay shallow. I miss being known. I miss feeling like my presence actually matters to someone in a real, tangible way.
