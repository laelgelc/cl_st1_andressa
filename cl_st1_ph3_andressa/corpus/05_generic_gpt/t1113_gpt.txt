I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold small talk, crack a joke, pretend I’m fine, but as soon as it gets even slightly real I freeze up or change the subject. I watch friends from a distance on social media moving in together, getting married, having kids, and I feel like I’m stuck behind glass, just observing. I keep telling myself I prefer being alone, but then nights feel like they last forever. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing.
