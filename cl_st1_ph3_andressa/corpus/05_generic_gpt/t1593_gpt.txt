I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I go through the motions—work, chores, scrolling—but it all feels like I’m just watching someone else’s boring routine. I have acquaintances, people I can joke with, but no one I can actually say, “I’m not okay,” to without feeling like I’m burdening them or being weird.

I keep thinking about how long it’s been since someone texted first just to ask how I am, not because they needed something. I don’t even know what I’d say if they did. I’ve gotten so used to being “fine” that I’m not sure I remember how to be honest anymore.
