I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “around people.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I message sometimes, but it all feels like I’m acting a role instead of actually being myself. When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel embarrassed afterward, or I stay surface-level and leave feeling empty.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, watch videos, lurk here, but the silence in my room feels so loud. I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally wrong with me that makes real connection impossible, like I missed some crucial step everyone else learned as teenagers. It’s exhausting pretending this is fine when it doesn’t feel fine at all.
