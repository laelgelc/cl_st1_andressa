I’m at that point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family nearby, but every interaction feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. I scroll through my phone wanting to message someone, then realize I don’t know who wouldn’t find it weird. I miss having someone who actually checks in, who knows my routines and moods. Lately it feels like if I disappeared, nothing in my day-to-day world would really change.
