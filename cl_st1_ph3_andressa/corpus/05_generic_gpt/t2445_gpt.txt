I don’t really know how to talk about this in real life, so I’m dumping it here.

I’m at this weird point where my life looks “fine” from the outside. I have a job, coworkers I can chat with, a few group chats that light up occasionally. But it all feels like background noise. I go home and it’s just silence. Not peaceful silence, more like the sound of nothing happening.

What bothers me most isn’t that I’m alone, it’s that I feel completely disconnected even when I’m with people. I can be in a room full of conversation and still feel like I’m on the other side of a glass wall. Everyone seems to already have their people, their history, their inside jokes. I’m always the extra.

I try to initiate plans, but I’m constantly second-guessing if I’m being annoying or forcing myself into places I’m not really wanted. So I pull back, then feel even more isolated. It’s like I’m slowly fading out of other people’s lives, and I’m not sure anyone’s really noticing.
