I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life without anyone really noticing. I have people around me, technically—coworkers, family, a couple of old friends—but everything feels surface-level, like we’re all just acting out our roles. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted to hear the messy, unfiltered answer. I try to reach out, but conversations die quickly, or I feel like I’m forcing myself into other people’s lives. It’s strange how loud the silence feels when the day ends and there’s no one to message.
