I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected from everyone.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can make small talk with. I can joke around, ask about their weekend, even hang out occasionally. But it all feels like I’m acting out a role instead of actually being there. When I go home, it’s like the whole day didn’t really “count” because no one actually knows me.

I don’t think anyone in my life knows what I’m afraid of, what I care about most, or what keeps me up at night. I’ve tried opening up a few times, but the conversation either gets brushed off with a joke or turns into them talking about themselves. After a while I just stopped trying.

What messes with my head is that, from the outside, my life probably looks fine. Stable job, decent health, not totally socially isolated. But I feel like a background character in my own life, watching everyone else connect and wondering why it never quite works for me.
