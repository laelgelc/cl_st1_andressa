I’m starting to realize how much of my life is just… background noise in other people’s stories. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people who’ll chat about weekend plans, but no one who actually checks in on me. If I didn’t initiate, I don’t think anyone would notice I’d gone quiet. I scroll through group photos I’m not in, watch people joke about “their people,” and I can’t even picture what that would feel like. I’m tired of being the person everyone likes “well enough” but never chooses first. It’s like existing in low resolution.
