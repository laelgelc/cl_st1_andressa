I’m starting to feel like I exist in the negative space of other people’s lives. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but nobody I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really broke inside me. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of them are people I’d feel weird messaging without a specific reason. 

I try to be friendly, to show up, to say yes when invited, but it never seems to move past the surface. I’m the “nice” person, the “reliable” one, but not the one anyone chooses first. 

What scares me is how normal it’s starting to feel—coming home, sitting in silence, realizing nobody noticed I was gone all day. It’s like I’m slowly fading into the background of my own life.
