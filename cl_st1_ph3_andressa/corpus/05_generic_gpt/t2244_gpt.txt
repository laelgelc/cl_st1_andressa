I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s my fault or just how things are now. I try to reach out, but conversations die quickly or stay shallow. I scroll social media and see everyone else in groups, relationships, families, and I can’t remember the last time someone texted first just to ask how I am. It’s like I’m here, but not really part of anything.
