I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s a weird thing to admit. My life looks fine on paper: job, small apartment, a couple of acquaintances I see occasionally. But most nights it’s just me, scrolling, lurking, pretending I’m too tired to go out when really there’s nowhere to go and no one expecting me.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel. People like me well enough in group settings, but no one really knows me. I don’t get texts just to check in. If I disappeared from social media, I’m not sure anyone would notice for a while.

I’ve forgotten how to start conversations that go beyond small talk. I’m not even sure what I’d say if someone actually wanted to get close.
