I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I text sometimes, but no one I feel I can actually be honest with. Any time I try to open up, I either downplay it mid-sentence or they change the subject, and I end up feeling stupid for even trying. So I default to “I’m fine” and small talk, then go home and overthink every interaction. It’s like I’m invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m starting to forget what real closeness even feels like.
