I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, in public, online, but it all feels like background noise. Conversations stay on the surface—weather, tasks, small jokes—and then everyone goes back to their lives, and I go back to my room that’s too quiet.

What gets to me most is how long it’s been since someone really knew me. Not the “how was your weekend” version, but the messy, unfiltered parts. I catch myself rehearsing things I’d say if I had someone to say them to, then realizing no one’s actually asking. It makes time feel heavy, like I’m just waiting for days to pass rather than actually living them.
