I’m starting to realize I don’t really have “people,” just acquaintances who pass through. I can go days without a personal message unless I’m the one reaching out first. When I finally do see someone, I feel like I’m performing the “normal, fine” version of myself instead of actually being there. I miss being known, but I’m also terrified that if I show the real me—needy, quiet, awkward—they’ll slowly back away, the way others already have.
