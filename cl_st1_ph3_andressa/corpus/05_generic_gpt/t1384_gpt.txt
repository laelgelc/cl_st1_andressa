I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online. I even go out sometimes. But it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. I crack jokes, ask questions, nod at the right moments, and then go home and realize nobody actually knows me. Not really.

What I miss is that feeling of being someone’s “person.” The one they text first, the one who doesn’t have to perform or worry about being too much or too little. I’ve had glimpses of that in the past, but they always fade or end, and every time it happens I pull back a little more.

Now I’m at this stage where I want connection so badly, but I’m exhausted by the effort it takes to try, and terrified that if people really saw me, they’d just quietly drift away like everyone else.
