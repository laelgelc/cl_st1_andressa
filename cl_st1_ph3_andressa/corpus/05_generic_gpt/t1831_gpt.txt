Lately I’ve been realizing how alone I feel even when I’m technically “around people.” I go to work, I make small talk, I laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like I’m acting out a version of myself that no one actually knows.

I miss having someone who checks in just because they want to, not because they need something. My phone only really lights up for group chats or logistics. I scroll through old conversations with people I used to be close to and wonder at what point I became… optional.

What’s weird is that I’m not completely isolated. I have coworkers, acquaintances, family. But it feels like I’m standing behind glass. I want to say, “Can someone please see me properly?” and at the same time I’m terrified that if they did, they wouldn’t like what they saw.
