Lately I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I go to work, come home, scroll through my phone, repeat. I have people I talk to, but no one I really talk to. Conversations stay on the surface: work, news, memes. No one asks how I actually am, and I’m not sure I’d know how to answer if they did. I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I’m exhausted before I even try. It’s strange how heavy it feels to carry something that’s basically just emptiness.
