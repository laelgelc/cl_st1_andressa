I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can be surrounded by coworkers, family, even friends, and still feel like I’m behind glass. I listen, I nod, I make jokes, but everything feels like an act I learned years ago and forgot the meaning of. I don’t really open up because I’m scared it’ll push people away, but not opening up keeps them at a distance anyway. It’s like I’m slowly fading out of my own life while everyone else keeps moving.
