I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time.

My life looks “fine” from the outside. I have coworkers I chat with, a family group chat that pings sometimes, a couple of old friends I send memes to. But there’s no one I can text at 2 a.m. and say, “I feel like I’m falling apart, can you just listen?” And what scares me is how normal this has started to feel.

I keep catching myself editing everything I say so I don’t sound “too much” or “too needy.” I’ll type out long messages and then delete them because I don’t want to burden anyone. Then I feel even more distant because the version of me people see is so filtered it barely feels like me.

I miss casual closeness. Just existing around someone without having to perform. Sitting on a couch, scrolling our phones, saying nothing, but still feeling like I belong somewhere.

Instead, I go through my day, do what I’m supposed to do, come home, and the silence feels heavier than it should.
