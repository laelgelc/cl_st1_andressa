I’ve started to realize that most of my “connections” are just people I orbit around, not people I’m actually close to. I can chat at work, I can be “friendly,” but no one really knows me, and I don’t really know them. When something good or bad happens, I open my phone and then just…close it again, because there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging first.

It’s weird how busy my life looks from the outside—meetings, errands, social media—but it all feels hollow. I’m tired of being the extra in everyone else’s story. I want someone who actually notices when I disappear for a bit, but most of the time I could vanish and I’m not sure anyone would even ask where I went.
