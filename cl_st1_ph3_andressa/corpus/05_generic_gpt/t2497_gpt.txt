I’m starting to feel like I’ve slipped out of everyone’s peripheral vision. People still reply to my messages, but it’s always delayed, surface-level, like they’re just checking a box. I scroll through group chats I’m technically part of and realize no one’s actually talking to me, just around me. I try to initiate plans and get “sometime” or “we’ll see.” It’s getting harder to remember the last time someone reached out first. I keep wondering if I’m boring, or just… forgettable. And then I feel pathetic for caring this much.
