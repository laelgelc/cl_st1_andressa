It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re in a soundproof box. I have friends, a job, conversations every day, but everything feels…surface-level. I watch other people slip into easy laughter or deep talks and I’m there, smiling, nodding, feeling like an actor reading lines. I crave someone who actually sees me, but I’m terrified that if I show who I am, they’ll just quietly drift away.
