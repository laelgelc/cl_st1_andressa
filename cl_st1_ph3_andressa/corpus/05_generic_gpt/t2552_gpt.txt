I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but it never goes beyond surface-level small talk. I go home, sit in my room, and the silence feels heavier than it should. I scroll through social media and see people laughing with friends, going out, having inside jokes. I can’t remember the last time someone texted me first just to ask how I’m doing, and I’m scared they never will.
