I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about TV shows or work drama. But there’s this invisible wall when it comes to anything real. I feel like everyone else got a manual on how to form close friendships and relationships, and I missed that class.

I’ll have a “good social day” and then go home and feel this weird emptiness, like none of it really counted. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, would anyone notice for more than a week?” Not in a dramatic way, just… honestly.

I try to reach out sometimes, but I overthink every message and usually delete it. I’m scared of being annoying, of being the one who “needs too much.” So I stay quiet, then feel hurt that no one checks in.

It’s a strange feeling to be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m not actually *with* anyone.
