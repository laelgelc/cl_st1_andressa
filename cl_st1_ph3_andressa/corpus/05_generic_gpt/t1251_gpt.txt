I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else do it. People know me, but it doesn’t feel like anyone really *knows* me. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but when something actually hurts, I realize I have no idea who I’d call.

It’s weird because from the outside I probably look “fine.” I show up, I smile, I make small talk. Inside, it feels like I’m fading out of focus. I’ve started talking less because it seems like when I do talk, the conversation moves on quickly, like I was just a pause.

Nights are the worst. That’s when it hits me how quiet everything is, and how used to the quiet I’ve become.
