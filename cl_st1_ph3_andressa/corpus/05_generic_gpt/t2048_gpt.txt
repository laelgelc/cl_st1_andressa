I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, joke, be “fine” on the surface, but it all feels like acting. When conversations end, I’m left with this weird emptiness, like nothing really landed on either side.

I have friends on paper, but I don’t feel close to anyone. I don’t tell them when I’m struggling because I don’t want to be “too much,” and then I resent that no one really sees me. It’s a loop I can’t seem to break: I hide, then feel invisible.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, watch random videos, anything to avoid noticing how quiet it is. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would actually notice quickly?” and I genuinely don’t know the answer.
