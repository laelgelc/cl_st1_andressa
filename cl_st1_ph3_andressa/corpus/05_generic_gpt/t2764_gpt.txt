I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people every day—at work, on the train, in my building—but it all feels like walking through a movie set where everyone else has a script and I’m just an extra.

Conversations stay on the surface: weather, deadlines, “how was your weekend?” I answer, they nod, and it’s like nothing actually lands. I keep trying to open up in small ways, but it feels like people either don’t notice or don’t know what to do with it, so I pull back again.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. I come home, scroll, watch something, go to bed. Repeat. I miss feeling known, like someone actually sees the specific way I am, not just the polite version I present. I’m starting to wonder if I’ve forgotten how to connect, or if there was ever really a time I knew how.
