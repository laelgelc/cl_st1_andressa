I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I talk to coworkers, classmates, family, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of being a person. I can joke around, make small talk, but as soon as things get slightly deeper, my mind goes blank. I don’t know what to say that isn’t awkward or oversharing, so I just stay quiet.

Nights are the worst. My phone is always silent unless I message first, and even then conversations die quickly. I scroll through chats with people I used to be close to and can’t figure out how I let it all fade. I keep wondering if I’m actually this forgettable, or if I just slowly pushed everyone away without noticing.
