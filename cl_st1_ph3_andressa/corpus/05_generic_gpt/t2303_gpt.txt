I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I only know how to perform being “okay” around them.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about shows or work or the weather. But when the conversation ends, it’s like the connection was never real. No one texts just to see how I’m doing. If I disappeared, I think I’d just quietly fall out of everyone’s routine.

What hurts is that I’m not physically isolated. I’m surrounded by people every day, yet it feels like I’m behind glass. I watch everyone else move in little groups: inside jokes, shared photos, weekend plans. I don’t even know how to insert myself without feeling like I’m forcing my way in.

I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off about me that others can sense, some invisible sign that says “not worth the effort.”
