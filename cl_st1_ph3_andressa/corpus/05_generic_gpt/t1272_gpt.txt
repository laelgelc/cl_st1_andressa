I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, act “normal,” but it all feels like I’m playing a character I don’t even like. When conversations end, there’s this heavy silence that feels louder than anything that was just said.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family—but I still feel completely separate from everyone, like I’m behind glass watching life happen. I want to be known, but the idea of opening up feels exhausting and pointless, like I’ll just end up misunderstood or ignored.

Most days I scroll through my phone pretending I’m busy, when really I’m just trying not to notice how nobody’s actually reaching out.
