I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I can text at 2am when my brain won’t shut up. I overthink every message, every pause in conversation, every “seen” with no reply. It makes me pull back, which just makes me lonelier.

Weekends are the worst. I’ll scroll through my phone pretending I’m “busy,” when in reality no one is asking me to go anywhere. I watch other people build friendships almost effortlessly and I can’t figure out what I’m missing. It feels like I’m standing behind glass, watching life happen without me.
