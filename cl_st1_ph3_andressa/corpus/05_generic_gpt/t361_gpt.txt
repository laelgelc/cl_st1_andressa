I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I talk to people every day at work, I answer messages, I even laugh at the right times, but it all feels like acting. No one actually knows what my days look like once I’m home—just me, my phone, and a lot of silence. I keep trying to “put myself out there” but it’s like I’m on mute. People seem to have their circles already. I’m always the extra, never the person someone thinks of first.
