I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m standing behind glass. I can see everyone living, joking, making plans, and I’m there, technically included, but not really *in* it. When I try to talk about anything deeper than surface-level stuff, I either shut down or over-explain until it gets awkward.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, re-reading old conversations, trying to figure out when I became this… extra person in everyone’s life. Not important enough to be missed, but familiar enough to be kept around.

I keep thinking maybe I’m just fundamentally uninteresting or draining. People seem to like me in small doses, but no one sticks. I want someone who actually sees me and doesn’t slowly fade away, but I’m starting to feel like that might not happen.
