Lately I feel like I’m living parallel to everyone else instead of with them. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but it all feels like background noise to this heavy quiet in my chest. I have people I talk to, but I don’t feel known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface, and I’m terrified that if I showed what’s underneath, they’d just drift away.

What makes it worse is how ordinary my life looks from the outside. No big tragedy, no dramatic reason. Just this constant sense of being out of sync, like I’m slightly transparent in every room I enter. I’m tired of hearing “you’re not alone” when I’ve never felt more invisible.
