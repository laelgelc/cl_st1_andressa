I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people. I can talk, joke, even seem social, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. When conversations end, there’s this weird emptiness, like nothing really landed. I watch other people build friendships that move forward, while mine just stall at small talk. I’m old enough that this shouldn’t feel so confusing, but it does, and it’s getting harder to ignore.
