I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call if something really bad happened. I watch everyone else slide into friendships so easily while I overthink every message, every silence. I’m tired of being “the quiet one” who’s always on the edge of groups. It’s like I’m present, but no one would notice if I just stopped showing up.
