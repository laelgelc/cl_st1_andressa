I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. Online chats. Group threads. But when it comes to being honest about how I feel, I freeze. I either overshare and feel embarrassed afterward, or I stay on the surface and walk away feeling invisible.

I watch other people slip into friendships so easily—inside jokes, casual hangouts, “let’s grab coffee” that actually happens. My interactions feel like rehearsed lines in a play I don’t understand. I’m always worried I’m being too much, or not enough.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, read other people’s lives, then put my phone down and it’s just…quiet. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone asked how I really am. I’m lonely, but also weirdly afraid of being seen too closely.
