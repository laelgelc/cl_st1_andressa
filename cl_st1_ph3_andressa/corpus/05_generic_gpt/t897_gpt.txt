I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like a background character in my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, family, a couple of acquaintances—but no one who actually knows me. Conversations never go beyond surface level. I’m “easy to talk to” because I mostly just listen and nod. When I do try to open up, people either change the subject or give me quick advice and move on.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk on here, watch videos, anything to fill the silence. I keep thinking I should reach out more, but I’m tired of feeling like I’m forcing myself into other people’s already-full lives.

What scares me most is how normal it’s starting to feel. The empty weekends, the unread messages, the way no one really notices if I disappear for a while. It’s like I’m slowly fading into the background and I don’t know how to reverse it.
