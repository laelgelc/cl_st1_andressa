I’m starting to wonder if I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I see friends’ group photos and realize I’m always the one taking the picture, never in it. When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like an outsider observing a language I used to speak but can’t anymore.

I come home to silence, scroll through chats that have slowly died, and debate whether to send a “hey, how are you?” that might be ignored. It’s weird how you can go days without anyone saying your name out loud.

I keep thinking it’s my fault—that I’m too quiet, too boring, too… something. But I don’t even know where to begin fixing it.
