Lately I feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s peripheral vision. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me or would notice if I disappeared for a while. I keep trying to reach out, suggest plans, be “less awkward,” but it’s like pushing on a locked door. I go days without a real conversation, then start wondering if maybe I’m the problem. It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel this invisible.
