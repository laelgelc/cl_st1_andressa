I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is happening in a soundproof room.

I go to work, I talk to people, I answer emails, I sit in meetings. On paper I’m “social” enough. But I walk home and it’s like none of those interactions actually land anywhere. It’s all surface-level noise that just evaporates the second I’m alone again. No one really knows me and I’m starting to feel like maybe there isn’t much to know.

I’ve had friends before, or at least people I did things with. Over the years, everyone slowly drifted into their own lives—relationships, kids, moving away. I can list a bunch of reasons: I didn’t reach out enough, I cancelled plans too often, I convinced myself they were just being polite. It’s like I watched my own social life die in slow motion and didn’t really fight it.

Now I’m at this weird point where I don’t even know how to be close to people anymore. When someone is friendly, my brain immediately goes, “They’ll get bored of you eventually.” So I keep everything shallow, which then becomes a self-fulfilling prophecy. I don’t give anyone a chance to actually know me, and then I feel abandoned when they inevitably drift away.

Nights are the worst. I do the usual distractions—scrolling, watching random videos, background TV just for noise. But as soon as I turn everything off, it’s just me and that heavy feeling in my chest. It’s not even sadness exactly. It’s more like this dull ache of realizing that if something good or bad happened to me, I wouldn’t know who to tell first. There’s no “person.”

I’ve tried the usual advice: hobbies, going to events, online communities. I can talk about shared interests, but it never crosses that invisible line into actual connection. I leave feeling more alone than when I arrived, like I’m just orbiting other people’s lives without ever being part of them.

I’m not sure what I’m expecting by posting this. I think I just needed to put into words that this is where I am: surrounded by people, but somehow completely on the outside of everything.
