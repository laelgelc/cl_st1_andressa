I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances I can joke with, coworkers I can small-talk with, but nobody I feel safe being fully honest with. Whenever I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or being a burden, so I pull back and act “fine” again.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, and then convince myself they don’t really want to hear from me. It’s like I’m watching my own life from the outside, seeing everyone else form friendships and relationships while I stay on the sidelines, pretending it doesn’t bother me as much as it actually does.
