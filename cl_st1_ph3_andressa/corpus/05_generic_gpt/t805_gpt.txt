I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s hitting harder lately. I go through entire days where the only words I say out loud are “thank you” to a cashier. My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from some app I don’t care about. 

I’ve tried joining group chats, Discord servers, even a couple local meetups, but I always feel like an outsider hovering on the edge of conversations. People seem to already have their circles, their inside jokes, their shared history. I’m just… extra. 

The weirdest part is how normal I look from the outside. Job, hobbies, small talk skills. But once I’m home and the door closes, it’s just silence and the feeling that no one really knows me.
