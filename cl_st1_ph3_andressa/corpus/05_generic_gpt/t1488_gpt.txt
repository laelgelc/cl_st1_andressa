I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely alone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels…surface-level. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure I’d know how to let them even if they tried.

I watch other people make plans, share inside jokes, post group photos, and it feels like I’m always on the outside looking in. When I go home, there’s no one to message, no one who’d notice if I disappeared for a few days. I keep wondering if I’m the problem—too quiet, too awkward, too something.  

It’s like I’m fading into the background of my own life.
