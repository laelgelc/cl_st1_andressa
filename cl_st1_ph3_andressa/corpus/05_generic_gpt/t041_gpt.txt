I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have friends, technically, but most interactions feel like I’m watching myself from the outside, saying the right things, laughing at the right moments, then going home and feeling like nothing really happened.

I keep trying to open up a little more, but the moment I do, I feel exposed and stupid, like I’ve overshared and made things awkward. So I retreat again, then complain to myself that nobody really knows me.

It’s weird how you can be in group chats, meetings, even parties, and still feel like a ghost. Like if I stopped showing up, people would notice briefly, then just… rearrange around the empty space.
