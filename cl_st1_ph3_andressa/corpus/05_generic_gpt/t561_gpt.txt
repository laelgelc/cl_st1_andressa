I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I talk to at work or online, but no one who really knows me. I scroll through chats and it’s just dead conversations, old memes, surface-level stuff. I’m always the one who double-texts, who suggests plans, who gets ignored or postponed.

What bothers me is how invisible it makes me feel. I can go days without anyone messaging first. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or being “too much,” so I shut down again. I miss feeling chosen by someone, not just tolerated or conveniently available when they’re bored.
