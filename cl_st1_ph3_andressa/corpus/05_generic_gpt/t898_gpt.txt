I’m starting to feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life.

I go through the motions—work, groceries, scrolling my phone in bed—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else live a really quiet, empty version of my life. I have people I “know,” but I don’t have anyone I can text without overthinking every word, or anyone who’d notice if I disappeared for a while.

I see friend groups posting photos, coworkers planning things without me, couples arguing over trivial stuff, and I’m weirdly jealous that they have someone to argue with at all. When something happens—good or bad—I catch myself automatically thinking, “Who would I even tell?” and the answer is usually no one.

The worst part is how normal it’s starting to feel. Like I’m getting used to the idea that this is just how my life is going to be.
