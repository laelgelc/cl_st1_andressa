I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one I can text without overthinking every word. I watch friendships happen around me like I’m behind glass. I’m scared I’ve missed the window where people form their “real” connections and now everyone’s already taken. I keep wondering if it’s my personality, my face, my energy—something fundamentally off that people sense and quietly avoid.
