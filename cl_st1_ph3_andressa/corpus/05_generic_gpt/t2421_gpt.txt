I’m surrounded by people but feel like a ghost. Friends, coworkers, family – conversations stay on the surface, like we’re all reading from a script. I try to open up and either get jokes, advice I didn’t ask for, or awkward silence. Then I feel stupid for trying. I scroll, I lurk, I “like” things, but it never feels like connection. It’s like everyone else got a manual for being human and I missed that day.
