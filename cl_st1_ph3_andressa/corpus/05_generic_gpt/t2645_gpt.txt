I’m starting to wonder if I’ve just aged out of being someone people want around. Friends drifted away, relationships never stuck, and now most of my interactions are surface-level: work small talk, quick texts, nothing that feels like it actually touches me. I spend whole weekends without hearing my name spoken out loud. I try to initiate plans, but people are “busy” or forget to follow up. It’s weird how you can be known by many and still not feel known by anyone.
