I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” lately. Like I’m somehow both not enough for people to notice and simultaneously too awkward or intense for anyone to actually want around.

I have friends on paper. Group chats, occasional hangouts, people who’d probably say they care. But when I’m actually with them I feel like a background character, like the extra who’s just there to fill space in the scene. I listen, nod, laugh in the right places, then go home and realize no one really knows anything about me beyond the surface.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone trying to think of someone I could message without feeling like I’m bothering them. I usually give up and just sit with the silence, overthinking every past interaction and wondering if I somehow pushed everyone away without noticing.

It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re on the outside of the glass, watching everyone else live.
