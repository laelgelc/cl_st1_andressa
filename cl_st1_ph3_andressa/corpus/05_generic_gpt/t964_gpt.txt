I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s a weird thing to admit. My days look full on paper: work, errands, scrolling, the usual. But there’s no one I feel comfortable texting just to say, “Today sucked.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but it all feels like talking through glass.

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel out of sync, like everyone else got the social script and I missed rehearsal. I overthink every word, replay conversations afterward, and convince myself I was awkward or annoying. Then I pull back, which just makes the distance worse.

What really gets to me is not having “my person” or even “my people.” Just walking around with this quiet, constant ache, and nowhere for it to land.
