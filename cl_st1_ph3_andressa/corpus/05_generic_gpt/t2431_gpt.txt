I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, I reply to group chats, I “like” things on social media, but it all feels like I’m just maintaining the illusion that I’m connected to anyone.

What hits me hardest is that no one really knows me in detail. People know surface facts—what I do, what shows I watch—but not the stuff that keeps me up at night or the things I actually care about. I’ve tried opening up a few times and it either got brushed off or the conversation just drifted away.

So now I default to being “fine” because it’s easier than risking that awkward silence. But it’s starting to feel like I’m disappearing into my own life, like I’m just background noise in everyone else’s story.
