I’ve been feeling like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work or TV shows, but no one I’d call if I was really falling apart. I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I feel close enough to reach out to without it feeling weird or needy.

Weekends are the worst. I watch other people make plans, post pictures, talk about “the group,” and I’m just… not in anyone’s group. I try to initiate sometimes, but when plans fall through or people respond hours or days later, it just reinforces this idea that I’m optional.

What scares me is how normal it’s starting to feel. The silence, the empty evenings, the pretending I’m fine with it when I’m really not. I’m starting to wonder if this is just how my life is going to be.
