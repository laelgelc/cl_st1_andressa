I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I feel truly close to. I scroll through my messages and there’s no one I can text without overthinking it first. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate because I don’t know who would genuinely care. I keep busy so I don’t have to sit with it, but it hits at night: this quiet sense that I could disappear and not disrupt anyone’s life much.
