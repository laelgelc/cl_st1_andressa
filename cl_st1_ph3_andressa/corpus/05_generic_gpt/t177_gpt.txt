I’m surrounded by people but still feel weirdly invisible. Friends, coworkers, family—I interact with them, laugh, make small talk, but it all feels like I’m playing a role someone else wrote. I don’t really open up because I’m scared it’ll be “too much” or they’ll pull away. Then I feel even more disconnected and blame myself for it. It’s like I’m watching my own life from the outside, not really in it.
