I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about work, random news, small talk stuff, but when it comes to anything deeper, my brain just shuts down. I watch other people fall into friendships so easily, make plans, send memes, have group chats. I’m always the extra, the “oh yeah, you can come too” person. 

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone pretending I’m busy, but no one’s really talking to me. I feel like I’m fading into the background of my own life.
