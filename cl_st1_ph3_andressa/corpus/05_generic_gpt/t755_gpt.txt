I’ve started to realize that I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. When conversations end, there’s this heavy silence that hits me, like the room suddenly gets bigger and emptier.

I see other people making plans, having group chats, inside jokes, partners, and I feel like I’m standing behind glass watching a world I can’t enter. I try to reach out sometimes, but I overthink every message and usually end up deleting it.

What scares me most is how normal this loneliness has started to feel. Like I’m getting used to it, and the idea of actually being known by someone feels more uncomfortable than being alone.
