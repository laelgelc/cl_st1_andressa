I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s a bit terrifying to admit. My days are basically work, commute, internet, sleep. I talk to coworkers about tasks and weather, but nobody really knows me. I scroll through chats I’m barely part of, debating whether to send a message, then overthinking and closing the app. Weekends are the worst; it feels like everyone else has somewhere to be. I’m not sure when I became this background character in my own life, but I don’t know how to step forward again.
