I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, co‑workers, people I joke with, but nobody who really knows me. I’ll have a conversation, seem fine, and then go home and feel this weird emptiness, like I just played a character for a few hours.

I scroll through chats and see so many “we should hang out sometime” messages that never turned into plans. I’m tired of always being the one who reaches out, so I stopped, and it’s just…quiet. No one really notices when I disappear for a bit. I could delete my accounts and it would probably take weeks for anyone to ask where I went.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. Like I’m slowly getting used to being invisible.
