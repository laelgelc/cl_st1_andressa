I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have acquaintances, chats at work, some online friends, but no one I can message at 2 a.m. when my brain won’t shut up. I scroll through old conversations and see this slow fading of contact, like everyone quietly walked away while I wasn’t paying attention. It’s embarrassing how often I rehearse imaginary conversations just to feel less invisible for a few minutes.
