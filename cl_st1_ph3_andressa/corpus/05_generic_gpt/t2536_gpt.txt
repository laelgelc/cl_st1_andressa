I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else’s routine from behind glass. I have people I talk to, but it’s all surface-level: jokes, small talk, memes. No one really knows what’s going on with me, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling needy or annoying.

What hurts most is realizing that if I disappeared from social media or stopped replying, it might take days or weeks before anyone noticed. I keep wanting to reach out, to say “I’m not okay,” but the words get stuck. So I just stay quiet, hoping the feeling passes, but it’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like who I am now.
