I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I exist on the edge of everyone else’s lives. People are “friendly” with me, but no one chooses me. I’m the backup plan, the extra chair at the table, the one they text when their first options are busy.

I try to reach out, to start conversations, but they fizzle out unless I keep pushing. It makes me feel needy and annoying, so I pull back, and then the silence just proves what I was afraid of.

What hurts most is that I can be surrounded by people and still feel invisible. I laugh at the right times, join in, but it’s like I’m acting a part. When I go home, it’s just this heavy quiet, and I realize no one really knows me.
