I’ve started to notice how quiet my life actually is. Not the peaceful kind of quiet, just… absence. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. Days blur together and the only real conversations I have are about tasks, deadlines, or small talk about the weather.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers, family chats, a couple of old friends I message every now and then. But I don’t feel known by anyone. I can’t remember the last time someone asked me how I really was and actually wanted the full answer, not just “I’m fine.”

What’s weird is that I’ve kind of forgotten how to be close to people. When I do get invited somewhere, I feel out of place, like I’m watching everyone else from behind glass. I don’t know how to move past surface-level stuff without feeling like I’m oversharing or being too much.

So I stay home, tell myself I’m just tired, and repeat.
