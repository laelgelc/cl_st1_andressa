I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have coworkers, classmates, acquaintances, but no one who actually knows me. When I do talk to someone, I feel like I’m performing a version of myself that I don’t even like. Then I go home, scroll endlessly, and realize no one has messaged me unless they need something. It’s weird how busy my life looks from the outside, yet I can go days without a real conversation that leaves me feeling anything but empty.
