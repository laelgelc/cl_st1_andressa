I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I was and actually wanted a real answer.

I’m in my late twenties and it feels like everyone else has their “people” already. Group chats, inside jokes, plans every weekend. I go home to silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen. I try to reach out sometimes, but I always feel like an afterthought, like I’m intruding on relationships that are already solid.

What scares me is how normal this emptiness is starting to feel. Like I’m slowly getting used to being invisible.
