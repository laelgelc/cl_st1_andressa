Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone without anything dramatic actually happening. My friends still exist, my family still checks in, but I feel weirdly invisible even when I’m around them. I’ll be in a group chat, typing and deleting the same sentence ten times because I’m convinced no one really cares what I say. When I do send something, the conversation just skips over it, and I end up rereading that silence like it’s proof that I don’t matter. I miss feeling naturally included instead of like I’m forcing myself into spaces where I’m only tolerated. I don’t know how to fix it without sounding desperate, so I just stay quiet and watch everyone else live.
