I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I see others forming friendships, making plans, sharing inside jokes, and I feel like I’m watching life through glass. I have acquaintances, people I talk to, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m., no one who really knows me. I overthink every interaction, replay conversations, assume I was too quiet or too weird. Then I retreat, which just makes the loneliness feel heavier and more permanent.
