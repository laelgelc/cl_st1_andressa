I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but everything feels like background noise. I can talk about work, memes, surface-level stuff, but the second it gets personal my mind goes blank. It’s like there’s a thick glass wall between me and everyone else.

I scroll through messages trying to figure out who I could text without it feeling forced, and usually end up putting my phone down. When I do reach out, I overanalyze every word and then disappear again out of embarrassment. 

What scares me most is how normal this loneliness is beginning to feel, like I’m slowly getting used to being invisible.
