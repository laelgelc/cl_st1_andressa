I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I’m “fine” on the surface—work, small talk, answering “yeah, busy” when people ask how I am. But as soon as I get home and close the door, it feels like I just disappear from the world.

I scroll through chats and see conversations that fizzled out months ago, and I can’t tell if I let them die or if they just slowly stopped breathing on their own. I keep thinking I should message someone, then I overthink every word until it feels easier to say nothing.

The weirdest part is being surrounded by people and still feeling like I’m pressed up against a window, watching everyone else live. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked what’s going on with me.
