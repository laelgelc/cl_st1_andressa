I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life. I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep, repeat. I have people I talk to, but it all feels shallow, like we’re just exchanging lines from a script. I don’t really open up anymore because I’m scared it’ll push people away or they’ll just not care. I miss feeling genuinely known by someone. Lately I catch myself talking out loud just to hear a human voice, and then feeling stupid about it.
