Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m fading into the background of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I don’t feel genuinely connected to any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, “how was your weekend?” I answer, they answer, and it all feels like reading from a script. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling like I’m oversharing or being a burden.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, but I never know who wouldn’t find it weird. I end up just staring at chats from people I used to be close to, wondering when exactly the distance started.

I’m not sure if I’ve isolated myself or if people just quietly drifted away over time. Maybe both. It feels like I’m stuck watching everyone else live in color while I’m stuck in grayscale.
