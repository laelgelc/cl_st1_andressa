I keep realizing that most of my “relationships” are just background noise. Coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with online—none of them really know me. I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching life instead of living it. I’ve tried opening up, but conversations never go deeper than surface-level stuff, and I end up feeling like I overshared or scared them off. Nights are the worst; everything gets quiet and it hits me how replaceable I probably am in everyone’s life but my own.
