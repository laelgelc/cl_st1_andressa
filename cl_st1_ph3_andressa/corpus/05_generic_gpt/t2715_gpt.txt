I’m starting to realize I’m not “busy and independent,” I’m just alone all the time.

My phone only lights up for spam or work emails. Group chats moved on without me years ago; I never left, I just quietly stopped being noticed. I used to initiate plans constantly, but it’s exhausting to be the only one asking, “Want to hang out?” and getting “maybe” or silence.

I talk to cashiers longer than necessary because that’s the only real-time interaction I get some days. I rehearse stories in my head that no one will hear. Even online, I feel like I’m shouting into a crowded room where everyone’s already paired off.

It’s a strange mix of invisibility and embarrassment, like I somehow failed a test everyone else passed.
