Lately I feel like I’m drifting through everyone else’s life as an extra. I have people around me, but no one really with me. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, TV. I’m craving that feeling of being known, of someone remembering the small details about me without being reminded. I overthink every message I send and usually end up deleting it, convinced I’m bothering them. Even when I’m in a room full of people, I feel like I’m behind glass, watching myself pretend to belong. It’s exhausting, and I don’t know how to fix it.
