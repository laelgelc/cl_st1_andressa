I’m starting to realize how much of my life is lived as a background character. I have acquaintances, people who’ll chat at work or send memes, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if everything fell apart. I’m always the one listening, nodding, laughing at the right spots, but when I try to share something real, the conversation somehow slides away. It makes me feel like I’m only useful when I’m convenient or entertaining. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if I’m just fundamentally easy to forget.
