I’ve started to realize I can go entire days without anyone saying my name out loud.

I have coworkers, classmates, people I chat with, but it all feels like background noise. Surface-level jokes, small talk, then everyone goes back to their real lives. I walk home and it’s like I vanish the moment I’m out of sight.

What messes with me most is that I don’t look lonely from the outside. I can be in a group and still feel like I’m pressed up against glass, watching everyone else actually connect while I just… participate.

I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off about me that people sense and quietly avoid. It’s like being homesick for a place I’ve never actually had.
