Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m physically with people. I have friends, but the conversations feel shallow, like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. I hesitate to reach out because I don’t want to bother anyone or seem needy, so I wait for messages that rarely come. Nights are the worst; I scroll, stare at the ceiling, replay awkward moments. I keep wondering if I’m actually hard to love or if I’ve just convinced myself that I am.
