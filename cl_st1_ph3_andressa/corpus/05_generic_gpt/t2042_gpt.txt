I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack jokes, even seem “normal” in group settings, but it always feels like I’m acting out a script. Once I’m alone again, it’s like none of it counted. No one checks in, no one really knows me, and I don’t know how to let them.

I scroll through chats and see long gaps, half-finished conversations, messages I never sent because I didn’t want to “bother” anyone. I tell myself people are busy, but it feels more like I’m optional. I’m surrounded by people living full lives, and I’m just hovering at the edges, watching, not really part of anything.
