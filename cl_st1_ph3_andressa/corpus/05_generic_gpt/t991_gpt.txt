I’m starting to feel like I don’t exist anywhere outside my own head.

I have people around me — coworkers, a couple of acquaintances, family that texts sometimes — but none of it feels like connection. It’s like I’m acting out a version of myself that’s acceptable and easy to be around, while the real version never gets to speak. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can feel myself reaching for something deeper and then pulling back because I don’t want to be “too much.”

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I watch things, I lurk here, and I keep thinking: if I disappeared, who would actually notice in a way that changes their day? I’m not suicidal, just… weirdly ghost-like. I miss a kind of closeness I’m not even sure I’ve ever had, and it’s embarrassing how badly I want it.
