I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels paper-thin. I can be funny, helpful, “nice,” but it’s like I’m playing a character whose lines I memorized years ago. When conversations end, there’s this heavy silence that feels louder than anything anyone said.

I keep wondering if I missed some crucial stage where everyone else learned how to turn small talk into real closeness. I want to be known, but I’m terrified that if I show who I actually am, people will quietly back away. So I stay on the surface and then hate how empty it feels.
