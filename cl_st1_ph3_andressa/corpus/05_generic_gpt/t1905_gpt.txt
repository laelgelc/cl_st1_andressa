I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly fading out of everyone’s peripheral vision.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I don’t feel actually *seen* by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, random memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I was and actually wanted a real answer.

What bothers me most is how invisible this all is from the outside. I go to work, I respond in group chats, I smile when I’m supposed to. No one would guess that most nights I just sit in my room, scrolling and hoping someone will message first.

I’m not even sure what I’m looking for anymore. Just some proof that I exist in someone’s mind when I’m not physically in front of them.
