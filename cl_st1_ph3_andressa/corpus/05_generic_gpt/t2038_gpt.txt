It’s weird how you can be “known” by people and still feel like a ghost. I have friends, family, coworkers, and yet most days I feel like I’m just performing a version of myself they expect. Conversations stay on safe, surface-level topics. I crave someone I can be completely honest with, but I’m scared if I show the real me they’ll back away. So I stay half-visible, half-hidden, and the loneliness just keeps stretching out.
