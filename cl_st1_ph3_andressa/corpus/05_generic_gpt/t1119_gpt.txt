I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family I talk to, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being myself. I can’t remember the last time someone really knew what was going on in my head. I keep thinking I must be the problem, because everyone else seems to find their people somehow, and I’m just…here, orbiting around everyone.
