I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. I’m always the “nice, quiet” person in the background, easy to overlook, easy to forget. I scroll through my contacts and feel this weird mix of embarrassment and emptiness, like I don’t really exist in anyone’s life in a solid way.
