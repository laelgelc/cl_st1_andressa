Lately I’ve been realizing that I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I go to work, make small talk, send the right emojis in group chats, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that everyone else understands better than I do.

What bothers me most is the gap between how my life looks and how it feels. On paper I’m “fine” – job, roof over my head, a few acquaintances. But I don’t have anyone I can text when something small but important happens. I edit myself constantly because I’m scared that if people saw the real version of me – the insecure, awkward, quiet parts – they’d slowly drift away.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through social media, watching people casually exist in each other’s lives, and it feels like I’m behind a glass wall, knocking, but no one hears it.
