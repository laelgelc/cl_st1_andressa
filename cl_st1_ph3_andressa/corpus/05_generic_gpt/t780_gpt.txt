I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with. But it all feels like I’m acting in a play where everyone else got the script in advance and I’m improvising badly. I leave social situations feeling more drained and hollow than before, replaying every sentence I said and cringing at all of it.

I scroll through chats and see long gaps, half-finished conversations, messages left on read. I tell myself people are busy, but it’s hard not to interpret silence as “you’re not worth the effort.” I rarely reach out first because I’m terrified of being annoying, but then I end up isolated and tell myself it’s my fault for not trying.

The worst part is I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing. “Lonely” feels too big, too heavy, and also somehow not enough to capture it.
