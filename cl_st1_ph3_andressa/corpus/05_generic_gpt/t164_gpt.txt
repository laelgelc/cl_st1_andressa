I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but it all feels like background noise. Conversations stay on the surface, and when I try to say something real, it’s like the moment just slides past and no one notices. I go home, scroll, watch shows, sleep, repeat. Days blur together.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel. I could disappear from group chats, skip gatherings, and I’m pretty sure no one would really ask why. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong or if this is just how adulthood quietly swallows people.
