I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed at the same time.

On paper, my life looks “fine.” I have coworkers, some old friends I text occasionally, family chats that light up on holidays. But most of the time it feels like I’m just orbiting around everyone else’s real lives, never actually part of anything.

I can go whole days without anyone asking how I’m really doing. When I do try to open up, I get nervous and start downplaying everything, making jokes or changing the subject so I don’t seem “too much.” Then I walk away feeling even more disconnected, because I didn’t actually let anyone see me.

I scroll social media way more than I should, not because I enjoy it, but because it gives me this illusion of being around people. Then I close the app and the silence in my room feels louder than before.

There’s this constant ache of wanting someone to notice I’m not okay, but also being terrified of what would happen if they actually did. So I just keep going, doing the same routines, trying not to think too hard about how empty it all feels.
