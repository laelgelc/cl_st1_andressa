I’ve hit this weird point where I’m constantly surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one who actually knows me. I’ll be in a room full of conversation and feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live.

I keep trying to make connections, but everything stays surface-level. I’m scared of oversharing and scaring people off, but holding back just makes me feel more invisible. Nights are the worst—scrolling, lurking, wanting to reach out, not wanting to be “too much.” It’s like I’m stuck between wanting to be seen and wanting to disappear.
