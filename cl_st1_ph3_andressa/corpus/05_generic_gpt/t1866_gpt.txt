I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about work, weather, random internet stuff, but the moment it’s supposed to get “real,” my brain just shuts down. I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I stay on the surface and walk away feeling invisible.

Most of my “social life” is scrolling through chats I’m too scared to start, watching other people’s group photos and inside jokes. When someone does message me, I overthink every word until I convince myself I’m annoying them.

I keep wondering if this is just how it’s going to be from now on: physically surrounded by people, mentally stuck behind glass, watching everyone else live their lives like I’m not really part of it.
