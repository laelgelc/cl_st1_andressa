I’m starting to realize I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to and make small talk with, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script I only half remember. The conversations end and I’m left replaying every sentence, wondering if I sounded weird or boring. Then I pull back a little more the next time, just to avoid that feeling.

Most nights it’s just me, my phone, endless scrolling, and this heavy, quiet feeling. I’ll see people posting about group chats, inside jokes, weekend plans, and I feel like I’m watching life through a window I can’t open.

I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually wanted to get close to me. There’s this fear that if they saw how empty my life really is, they’d just slowly drift away like everyone else has. So I stay on the edges and pretend it doesn’t bother me as much as it does.
