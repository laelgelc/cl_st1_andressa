I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but it all feels like I’m acting in a play. When I go home, it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives and friendships, and it feels like I’m behind a glass wall.

I don’t really open up in real life because I’m afraid of being “too much,” so I stay on the surface and then resent how shallow everything feels. I tell myself I’m used to being alone, but it still hits hard when I realize there’s no one who’d really notice if I disappeared for a while.
