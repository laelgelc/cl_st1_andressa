I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels like background noise to this constant sense of being alone.

I technically “know” people: coworkers, a few old classmates, family I text on holidays. But I don’t feel known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface, like we’re all scared to admit how empty we really feel. I keep trying to be easygoing and “fine” so I don’t scare anyone off, and then I end up feeling invisible anyway.

Nights are the worst. I’ll sit on my bed with YouTube or a podcast playing just so there’s some kind of voice in the room. I’ll think about reaching out to someone, but I get stuck wondering if they’d just find it annoying or awkward. So I don’t.

I’m not sure what I’m looking for by posting this. Maybe I just needed to say out loud that I’m lonely.
