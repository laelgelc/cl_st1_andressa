I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. When I do hang out with others, I feel like I’m performing a version of myself that’s acceptable, not actually being me. Then I go home and replay every conversation, convinced I sounded weird or annoying. I keep telling myself I’m “busy,” but mostly I’m just tired of feeling like an outsider in every room I walk into.
