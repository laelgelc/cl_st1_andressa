I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from everyone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I say “hey” to, but no one I can text when something small or stupid happens. I scroll through my contacts and realize there isn’t a single person I feel comfortable bothering.

I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but when I do try, conversations die quickly or feel forced. I’m tired of being the “extra” person in group plans, the one who gets invited out of politeness, not because I’m actually wanted there.

What messes with my head most is how normal I look from the outside. Job, hobbies, small talk skills. But then I come home, close the door, and it’s just this heavy silence that feels like it’s seeping into everything.
