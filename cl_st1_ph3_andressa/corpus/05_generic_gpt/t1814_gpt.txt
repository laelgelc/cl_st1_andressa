I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too visible at the same time. Invisible because no one really checks in, too visible because I’m constantly aware of how alone I look. I go through entire days where the only words I say out loud are “thank you” to a cashier or “excuse me” when I’m in someone’s way.

I have people I “know” but not people I can actually call. I scroll through my contacts and realize I’m just holding onto old numbers from past lives that don’t exist anymore. Everyone seems to have their own little circle, and I’m always orbiting outside of it.

I try to distract myself with hobbies, shows, work, anything, but there’s this heavy silence that comes back the moment everything stops. Nights are the worst. That’s when it really hits how much I’m just… here, by myself, and no one would really notice if I disappeared for a while.
