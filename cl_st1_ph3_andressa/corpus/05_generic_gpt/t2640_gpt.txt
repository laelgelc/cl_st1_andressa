I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to be around them.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about weather, TV, work drama. But the second the conversation could turn into something real, I freeze up or steer it back to something shallow. Then I go home and complain to myself that nobody really knows me.

It’s weird how you can be “liked” and still feel invisible. People invite me to things sometimes, but I feel like a background character filling space in group photos. If I stopped showing up, I’m not sure anyone’s routine would really change.

I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I don’t even know what that means in practice. I’m tired of hearing my own voice echo in my head all day and then realizing no one else hears it.
