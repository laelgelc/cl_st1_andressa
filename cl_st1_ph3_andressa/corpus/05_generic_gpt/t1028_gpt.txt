I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing while still being physically here. I have people around me—coworkers, some old friends I text occasionally, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. No one actually knows what my days look like or what I’m thinking about, and I’m not sure anyone really wants to.  

I keep catching myself editing my personality down to the “least inconvenient” version so I don’t scare people off. Then I feel fake and even more disconnected. I miss the feeling of being someone’s first choice, the person they think of when something happens.  

Mostly I just sit with this heavy, quiet ache and wonder if this is just how adulthood is supposed to feel, or if I somehow missed the part where everyone else figured out how to belong.
