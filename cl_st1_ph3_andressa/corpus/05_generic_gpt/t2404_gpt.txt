I’ve been feeling like I’m living in a parallel world to everyone around me.

On paper I’m “fine” — job, roof over my head, a couple of people I could technically text — but it doesn’t feel like I’m actually connected to anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, memes, random small talk. I can’t remember the last time I felt like someone really knew me, or even wanted to.

I try to reach out sometimes, but I always end up feeling like I’m intruding or being “too much.” People are busy, messages get left on read, plans fall through. After a while I start pre-rejecting myself so I don’t have to watch it happen again. So I stay quiet, which just makes everything worse.

The weird part is I’m not completely socially inept. I can be funny, I can hold a conversation. But as soon as the interaction ends, it’s like I disappear from their world. No one checks in. No one notices the gaps.

I’m starting to wonder if this is just how my life is going to be: always around people, never really with them.
