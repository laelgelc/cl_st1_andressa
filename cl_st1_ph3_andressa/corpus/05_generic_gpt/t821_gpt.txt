I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like acting. When I’m home, it’s just silence and my own thoughts looping. I’ll scroll through chats and see “seen” on messages I tried to make sound casual, then feel stupid for caring so much. I keep imagining someone asking how I really am, and my mind just goes blank. I’m scared that I’ve become someone who only exists in the gaps of other people’s lives, never really in the center of anything.
