I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, pretend I’m fine, but as soon as the conversation needs any depth, it’s like I hit a wall. I freeze up, get awkward, and then replay everything later in my head, convinced I sounded weird or needy.

Most of my “social life” is group chats where I’m mostly a lurker. People meet up without me and I tell myself it’s because I’m busy, but the truth is I’m not really being invited. When I try to initiate, plans just kind of fizzle out.

I keep wondering what’s wrong with me that everyone else seems to find their people and I’m just… orbiting around the edges. I’m tired of my own company but also scared that if people really got to know me, they’d confirm what I already suspect: that I’m not someone worth sticking around for.
