I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m not really “in” anyone’s life. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m., and no one who would call me. Conversations stay surface-level, and whenever I try to open up, it feels like I’m oversharing or making things awkward.

What hurts most is realizing how forgettable I seem. Group plans happen and I find out after the fact. Messages get left on read. When I’m actually with people, I feel like an extra in the background of their stories, not someone who matters.

I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if this is just how adulthood quietly isolates you until you stop expecting more.
