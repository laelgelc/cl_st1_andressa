I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, eat, scroll, sleep—but none of it really involves anyone else. I have acquaintances, people I chat with about surface-level stuff, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong.  

What bothers me most is how invisible it feels. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m pressed up against a glass wall, watching everyone else connect while I just…don’t. I overthink every message I send, then convince myself I’m annoying and delete it.  

It’s like I’m stuck between wanting to be seen and being terrified that if people really saw me, they’d just quietly step away.
