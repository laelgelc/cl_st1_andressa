I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. Conversations feel like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. I censor what I say so I don’t seem “too much,” and then I end up feeling invisible anyway.

What bothers me most is how repetitive it’s become: wake up, work, scroll, sleep. I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I’m exhausted from trying and coming home feeling even more alone than before.
