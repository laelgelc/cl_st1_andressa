I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have people around me—coworkers, family, a few old friends—but the connection feels thin, like plastic wrap between us. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. No one really asks how I am, and when they do, I hear myself say “I’m fine” before I can stop it.

What scares me is how used to this I’m becoming. I scroll, I watch shows, I sleep, and days blend together. I try to reach out sometimes, but I overthink every message and usually delete it. I don’t even know what I’m asking for anymore—just that I’m tired of feeling like a background character in my own life.
