I don’t really know how to say this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers I chat with, family I text sometimes, a couple of old friends I send memes to. But it all feels like I’m playing a role in a play everyone else has already lost interest in. The conversations never go deeper than surface-level stuff. I don’t think anyone actually knows what my days feel like, and I don’t know how to tell them without sounding needy or weird.

I come home, scroll, watch something, lie in bed, repeat. I keep thinking, “I should reach out, I should make plans,” but then I imagine the awkwardness, the small talk, the possibility of being brushed off, and I just…don’t. It’s like I’m stuck between wanting connection so badly it hurts and being terrified of trying for it.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. Like I’m getting used to being lonely, and that maybe this is just how my life is going to be.
