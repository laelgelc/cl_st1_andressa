Lately I feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s peripheral vision. I have people around me, but I don’t really feel *with* them. Conversations skim the surface—work, weather, memes—but nothing that feels like it touches who I actually am. When I do try to open up, I either overthink every word or feel like I’m dumping too much and scare people off.

I watch friendships online and in real life and can’t figure out how they happen. For other people, connection seems natural, almost automatic. For me it feels like a performance I’m constantly failing at. I go home, replay every interaction, and somehow always end up more convinced that I’m just… extra, unnecessary. Like if I stopped reaching out, most people wouldn’t really notice for long.
