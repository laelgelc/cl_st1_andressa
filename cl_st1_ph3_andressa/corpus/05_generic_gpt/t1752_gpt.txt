I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I’ll go through entire days barely speaking to anyone beyond “thanks” at a checkout. Friends drifted, family’s busy, and I’m stuck replaying old conversations in my head just to feel connected. I scroll through chats I’m too scared to revive, worried I was always just the expendable one. It’s strange how loud silence can get when you realize nobody’s really noticing you’re quiet.
