Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m stuck behind glass while everyone else is living in the real world.

I talk to people every day – coworkers, cashiers, neighbors – but it all feels like background noise. I can do small talk, I can even make people laugh sometimes, but as soon as the interaction ends, it’s like I disappear again. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.

What makes it worse is that I can’t even fully blame other people. When someone gets a bit closer, I panic. I worry I’ll be too much, or not enough, or that they’ll eventually see how empty I feel and quietly back away. So I keep things surface-level, then feel lonely because nothing is deeper.

Nights are the hardest. I lie there scrolling, watching other people’s group chats, relationships, inside jokes. I’m “included” in some of it, but I still feel like an extra in my own life.
