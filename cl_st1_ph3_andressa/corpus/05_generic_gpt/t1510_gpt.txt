I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from behind glass.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of old friends I still text—but it all feels thin, like I’m just playing a role. I can do small talk, I can crack jokes, but the second I’m alone again the silence feels like it swallows the room.

What scares me is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped showing up, I’m not sure anyone’s day would really change. People would probably notice eventually, but more in a “huh, haven’t seen them in a while” way than anything else.

I miss feeling known. Not just “how’s work?” but the kind of connection where someone actually sees you, remembers the small details, cares enough to ask follow-up questions. I don’t even know how to start building that anymore, or if I just missed my window.
