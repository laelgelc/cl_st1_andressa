I feel like I’ve somehow slipped through the cracks of everyone’s life. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels paper-thin. No one really knows me beyond the surface stuff. I try to reach out, but I always end up feeling like I’m intruding, like I’m the “extra” person in every group. Nights are the worst—scrolling through my phone, seeing people hanging out, while I’m just lying there with this heavy, physical ache in my chest. It’s embarrassing how used to this emptiness I’m becoming.
