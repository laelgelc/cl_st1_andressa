I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever did. I have acquaintances, people who’ll chat about work or hobbies, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and there’s just… no one it feels appropriate to bother.

What makes it worse is that I used to think this was temporary. “Once I get to college,” then “once I start my job,” then “once I move,” I’d finally find my people. Those milestones came and went, and my life is still mostly me, my phone, and background noise.

I’ve become hyper-aware of how replaceable I am. If I disappeared from most people’s lives, their routines wouldn’t change. Some days I feel invisible in a crowded room, and other days I avoid the room altogether because it just highlights how alone I am.
