I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I don’t feel actually *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, “How was your weekend?” I give the same rehearsed answers every time because it feels like no one really wants the real ones.

I watch other people form inside jokes, share memories, casually lean on each other like it’s the most natural thing in the world. I’m always one step outside that circle, close enough to see it, never part of it.

What scares me most is how used to it I’m becoming. The silence after I turn off my phone doesn’t shock me anymore. It just feels like the baseline.
