I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately I feel like a ghost in my own life. I see people, I interact, I say the right things, but it all feels like I’m behind glass. Friends are more like old contacts I rotate through so I don’t seem weird, not people I actually feel close to. 

I miss having “a person” — someone who checks in without needing a reason, who actually notices if I disappear for a bit. My phone is either painfully silent or full of surface-level chats that go nowhere. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong, or if I’ve just quietly slipped into being forgettable.
