I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t make sense on paper. I have coworkers I talk to, a couple of old friends I text, family chats that ping all day. But I still go to bed every night with this hollow feeling, like nobody really knows me and I’m just playing a role in every interaction.

I’m always the one listening, asking questions, keeping things light. When I try to open up, I feel awkward and exposed, like I’m oversharing or making things weird. So I back off, crack a joke, change the subject. Then later I get upset that no one sees what’s actually going on with me.

Weekends are the worst. I scroll through my phone trying to think of someone I could message, and then talk myself out of it because I don’t want to seem needy. So I just sit there, refreshing feeds, feeling more and more invisible.
