I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I scroll through contacts and there’s nobody I feel comfortable messaging just to say, “I’m not okay.” Weekends are the worst—too much quiet, too much time to think. I keep wondering if I’m the common denominator, if something about me just makes real closeness impossible.
