I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about weather, TV, work. But nobody really knows me, and I don’t really know them either. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging first.  

It’s weird because from the outside I probably look fine. I laugh, I participate, I show up. Then I go home and it’s just silence and this heavy feeling that I’m completely unnecessary to anyone’s life.
