I’m starting to wonder if I’ve just slipped through the cracks of everyone’s life, including my own. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure I know how to let them. I overthink every message I send, then end up saying nothing and watching the conversation move on without me. Weekends are the worst; I scroll, lurk, listen to other people’s lives. It feels like I’m permanently stuck on the outside, pressing my face against the glass.
