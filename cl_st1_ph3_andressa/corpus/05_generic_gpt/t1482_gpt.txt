I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, eat, scroll, sleep—but there’s no real connection anywhere. I have people I talk to, but no one I can actually be honest with without feeling like I’m burdening them or oversharing.

What messes with my head is that from the outside it probably looks like I’m “fine.” I show up, I smile, I make small talk. Inside, it feels like I’m floating in some soundproof box where I can see everyone else living, but they can’t really see me.

I want to reach out more, but I’m terrified of being rejected or just…ignored. So I stay quiet, then hate myself for staying quiet, and the cycle keeps repeating. It’s starting to feel like this is just how things are now.
