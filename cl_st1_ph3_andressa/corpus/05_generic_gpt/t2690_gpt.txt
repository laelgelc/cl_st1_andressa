I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I talk to people every day—coworkers, neighbors, cashiers—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure I remember how to let anyone in anymore. I watch other people make plans, share in-jokes, post pictures with friends, and it’s like I’m behind glass. I keep wondering if I missed some critical point where everyone else learned how to connect and I just…didn’t.
