I’ve started to realize that most of my “social life” exists in my head. I replay old conversations, imagine future ones, and somehow that feels more real than the actual people in my life. When I’m with others, I feel like I’m watching myself from the outside, trying to act normal, trying to be “enough,” and it’s exhausting.

I’m not completely isolated. I have coworkers, a few acquaintances, family chats. But everything feels surface-level, like I’m a background character in their lives. I don’t know how to move from “person they know” to “person they actually care about and think of on purpose.”

Sometimes I go days without getting a message that isn’t about work or logistics. I tell myself it doesn’t matter, that I’m used to it, but it still stings every time I unlock my phone and there’s nothing there.
