I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m lonely in a way that feels like it’s hollowing me out.

My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from an app. I scroll through chats and it’s just me double-texting people from months ago, trying to revive dead conversations. When I do get invited somewhere, I feel so out of practice socially that I either overtalk or go completely quiet, then replay every interaction later like a cringe highlight reel.

I have acquaintances, classmates, coworkers—but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I keep thinking I’m too much for people or not enough, somehow both at once. I’m tired of being the “nice, quiet one” everyone forgets to actually know.
