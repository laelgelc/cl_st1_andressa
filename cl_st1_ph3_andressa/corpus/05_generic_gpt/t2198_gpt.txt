I feel like I’m stuck in this weird in-between where I’m not completely isolated, but I still feel completely alone. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can exchange small talk with, but no one I can actually be real with. I overthink every interaction, wondering if I’m being too much or not enough. I watch other people form friendships so effortlessly and it just reminds me that no one really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone ever will.
