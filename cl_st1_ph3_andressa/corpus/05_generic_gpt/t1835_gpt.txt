I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

Most days I go to work, come home, eat something in front of a screen, and then just…exist until I’m tired enough to sleep. I talk to people, but it’s all surface-level: “How was your weekend?” “Busy, you?” and then back to pretending we’re all fine.

I have contacts, not friends. People I could text, but never do, because I’m convinced I’m an obligation, not a choice. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m dumping something heavy into a room that was supposed to stay light and casual.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. The quiet, the lack of messages, the empty evenings. It’s like I’m slowly adjusting to being invisible, and I don’t know how to reverse it.
