I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have friends on paper, group chats, coworkers who laugh at my jokes, but I feel like a background character in my own life. When I try to open up, I get awkward or make a joke and change the subject. Then I go home and replay every interaction, convinced I’m boring or too much or just… off.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, watch other people’s lives unfold, and it feels like I’m pressing my face against a window. I keep wondering if this is just how adulthood is, or if I somehow missed a crucial step everyone else got.
