I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life. I go through the motions—work, commute, eat, sleep—but there’s no real connection anywhere. I have acquaintances who’ll chat about surface stuff, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m., or even 8 p.m. honestly. When something good or bad happens, my first instinct is to keep it to myself because I don’t want to “bother” anyone.  

What scares me is how normal this is beginning to feel. I’m getting used to not being asked how I’m doing, and I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely did.
