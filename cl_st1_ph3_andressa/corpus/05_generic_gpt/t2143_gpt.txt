I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I’m surrounded by people—at work, online, even family—but it all feels like static, like none of it really reaches me. I try to start conversations, but they fizzle out, or I end up overthinking every word I say. I watch other people make plans, form inside jokes, drift into group chats I’m never invited to. I keep telling myself I’m fine alone, but lately it just feels like I’m disappearing in plain sight.
