It’s strange how you can have people around you and still feel like you’re behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels…surface-level. No one actually knows me, and I don’t really know how to let them. I overthink every message, every silence, and end up withdrawing so I don’t seem needy. Then I feel even more isolated and resentful, like I did this to myself but also didn’t have a real choice. I miss the feeling of being someone’s first call, or even second. Lately it feels like I could disappear and it would just be a minor inconvenience for a few people, at most.
