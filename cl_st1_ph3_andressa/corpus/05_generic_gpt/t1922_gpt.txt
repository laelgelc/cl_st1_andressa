I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. I can make small talk, crack jokes, even seem confident, but the moment I’m alone it’s like everything just collapses into this heavy silence.

I can’t remember the last time I felt genuinely understood by someone. Not just listened to, but actually seen. I keep thinking it’s my fault—like I’m too guarded, too weird, or too boring for anyone to really want to know. When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I hold back and stay stuck in surface-level stuff.

It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like a permanent state I’m quietly adjusting to.
