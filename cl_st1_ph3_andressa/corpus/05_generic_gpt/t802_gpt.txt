I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, even when they’re right in front of me. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script someone else wrote. I can be funny, polite, helpful, but once I’m alone again, it’s like none of it counted.

I scroll through my messages and there’s nothing there unless I started it. If I stop reaching out, everything just goes silent. It makes me wonder if anyone actually thinks about me when I’m not useful or convenient.

I don’t want constant attention, just the feeling that I exist in someone’s mind. That if I disappeared from their life, they’d notice for more than a day.
