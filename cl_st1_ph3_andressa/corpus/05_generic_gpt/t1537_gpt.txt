I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family I text sometimes—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface. I crack jokes, ask questions about their lives, and somehow the topic never comes back to me. When it does, I panic and deflect, then later feel invisible and resentful that no one digs deeper.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, but I convince myself I’d be annoying or that they’re just being polite when they respond. So I wait for others to reach out, and when they don’t, it just confirms the story in my head that I don’t really matter to anyone.

I miss the feeling of being someone’s first choice. Of having a person I can send a stupid meme to without overthinking it. I’m tired of being “included” but not connected, surrounded but lonely, and I don’t really know how to break out of this pattern without feeling desperate or pathetic.
