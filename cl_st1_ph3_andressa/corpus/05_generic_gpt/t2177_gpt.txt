I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely, and it feels like it’s changing who I am. I go days without a real conversation, just small talk with coworkers and then back to my room. I scroll through chats I’m barely part of, watching people make plans I’m never invited to. When I do see people, I feel weirdly out of sync, like I’m speaking a different language. I overthink every message I send and usually end up deleting it. I’m not even sure what I want anymore—friends, a relationship, just someone who actually notices I’m here? Right now it feels like I could disappear and nothing in my life would look different.
