I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack jokes, be “fine” at work or with acquaintances, but it all feels like acting. When I get home, it’s just silence. No messages, no plans, no one who would notice if I disappeared for a few days besides maybe work.

I scroll through chats that died months ago and feel weirdly embarrassed to reach out, like I missed the acceptable window. I keep thinking I should “just join something,” but even imagining showing up alone somewhere makes me tired. It’s like I’m watching life from behind glass.
