I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to talk at them. I can make small talk, crack a joke, but it never turns into anything deeper. People seem to like me “fine,” but no one chooses me. I’m always the optional extra, never the default invitation.

What messes with my head is that my life looks normal from the outside. Job, hobbies, some acquaintances. But no one really knows me. I’m scared that if I disappeared, it would just be minor inconvenience for a couple of people, not an actual loss.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just needed to admit out loud that I’m lonely in a way that feels permanent.
