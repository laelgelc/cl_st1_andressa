I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really match my life on paper. I have coworkers I get along with, a couple of friends I see sometimes, family I text. But I still feel like I’m standing behind a glass wall watching everyone else actually live.

Conversations feel shallow, like we’re all reading from a script. “How’s work?” “How was your weekend?” I answer, they answer, and then it just…ends. I keep hoping someone will ask a follow-up question, or notice when I trail off, but they don’t. I’m guilty of doing the same to them too.

I think what hurts most is feeling forgettable. People cancel plans and I act like it’s fine, but it sticks with me for days. I don’t know how to bridge the gap between being “around people” and actually feeling known. It’s starting to feel like maybe I’m just not someone others think about when I’m not right in front of them.
