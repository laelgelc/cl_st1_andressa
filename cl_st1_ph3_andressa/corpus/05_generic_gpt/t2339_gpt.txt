I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work and online, but no one who really knows me. I scroll through my phone and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging just to say, “I’m not okay.” I overthink every interaction, worrying I’m being too much or not enough, so I pull back and end up even more isolated. It’s this weird mix of wanting connection desperately and being terrified of it at the same time.
