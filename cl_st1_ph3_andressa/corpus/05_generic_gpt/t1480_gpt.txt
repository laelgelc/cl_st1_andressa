I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it. I talk to people every day—at work, online, even in group chats—but it still feels like there’s a glass wall between me and everyone else.

I miss feeling known. Not just “how’s it going” known, but the kind where someone notices when your voice sounds off and actually cares why. I’m tired of being “easy to talk to” but never the person anyone thinks of first.

Nights are the worst. I keep my phone nearby, pretending someone might reach out, but it’s mostly silent. I don’t even know what I’d say if they did. I just know I’m tired of feeling invisible in a room full of people.
