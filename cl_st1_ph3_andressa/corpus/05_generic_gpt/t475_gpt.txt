I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely. My phone almost never lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. I scroll through chats and see mostly one-sided conversations or dead threads from years ago. I keep telling myself I prefer staying in, but the truth is no one’s asking me to go out anyway.

I’ve tried joining groups, talking to coworkers, but I always feel like an extra—someone people are polite to, not someone they think about later. Even when I’m around others, I feel like I’m watching life happen through glass. Then I go home, lie in bed, and wonder if anyone would really notice if I just stopped trying.
