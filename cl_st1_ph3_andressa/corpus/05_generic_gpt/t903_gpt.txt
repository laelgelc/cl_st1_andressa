I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me in a technical sense — coworkers, family chats, a couple of acquaintances — but I don’t feel *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted the full, messy answer.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. I used to try: sending messages first, suggesting plans, oversharing a bit in hopes someone would meet me there. Now I catch myself pulling back, replying slower, telling people I’m “busy” when I’m just lying on my bed scrolling because I don’t see the point.

I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off about me that other people sense and avoid, and I just can’t see it.
