I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, errands—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else do it. I have people around me, technically: coworkers, acquaintances, family chats. But none of it feels like connection. It’s like I’m always a few inches behind a glass wall, watching everyone else actually participate.

I used to think loneliness was just “being alone,” but I’m starting to realize it’s more like being unseen. I can be in a crowded room and still feel like if I disappeared, it would take a while for anyone to notice. I don’t even know how to fix it anymore. Reaching out feels awkward and forced, and staying quiet just makes the silence louder. It’s exhausting.
