I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but it all feels like background noise. I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live. I overthink every message I send, then end up saying nothing. When I do talk, I feel boring and forgettable. Days pass where the only real conversation I have is with myself in my head.
