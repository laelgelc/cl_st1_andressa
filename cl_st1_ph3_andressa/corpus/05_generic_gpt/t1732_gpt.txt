I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but it feels like I’ve slowly vanished from everyone’s life while still being physically here. My phone barely lights up unless it’s some notification from an app I don’t care about. When I try to reach out, conversations die after a few messages, like people are doing me a favor by replying at all.

I go through entire weekends without speaking out loud to another person. It’s making me question whether I’m just fundamentally unlikeable or forgettable. I see people forming friendships, making plans, posting pictures together, and I can’t remember the last time someone actually chose me for anything. It’s like I’m watching life from the outside, pressed up against the glass.
