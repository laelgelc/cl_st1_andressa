I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like I’m playing a role. I can be “pleasant,” “helpful,” even “funny,” but the second I imagine saying how I really feel, I freeze. It’s like there’s a glass wall between me and everyone else. I can see them, hear them, but nothing real gets through.

Most days I go home, sit in silence, scroll through feeds full of people doing things in groups. I don’t even know if I’m jealous or just numb. I tell myself I’m “tired” or “busy,” but the truth is I’m scared of being seen as needy or weird. So I stay quiet, then complain in my head that nobody really knows me.

What bothers me most is how normal this has started to feel. The empty apartment, the one-sided conversations, the background noise of TV just so it’s not totally silent. I keep wondering if this is just how my life is going to be.
