I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but there’s no real connection anywhere. I have “people I know,” but not people I can actually call when I’m falling apart at 2 a.m. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. No one asks how I really am, and I don’t know how to answer even if they did.

What messes with my head is that I look “fine” from the outside. I show up, I crack jokes, I respond to messages eventually. But I go home to silence, eat alone, sleep alone, and wake up with this heavy feeling that no one would really notice if I slowly just stopped reaching out.
