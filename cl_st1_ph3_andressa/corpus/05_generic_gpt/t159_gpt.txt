I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. I talk to people, but it’s all surface-level: “How was your weekend?” “Busy.” Smiles, nods, repeat. No one really knows me, and I’m not even sure I remember how to let them.

The weird part is I’m surrounded by people constantly, but I still feel completely disconnected. Group chats are muted, invitations slowed and then stopped. I keep thinking if I disappeared, the only sign would be my unread notifications quietly expiring.
