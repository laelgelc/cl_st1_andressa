I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else do it. I have people I talk to, but it’s all surface-level: “How’s work?” “How was your weekend?” I don’t remember the last time I was truly honest with anyone in person.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel in group settings. I can be standing there, listening, nodding, laughing at the right times, and still feel like no one would notice if I left. I try to initiate plans, but they fall through or get postponed indefinitely, and eventually I stop asking.

The loneliness feels less like an emotion and more like a permanent atmosphere I move through. Even on days that are “fine,” it’s always there, just under everything.
