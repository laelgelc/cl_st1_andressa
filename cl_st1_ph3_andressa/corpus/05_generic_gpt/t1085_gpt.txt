I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. I go to work, make small talk, laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like acting. When I get home, there’s no one who actually knows what my day was like, or even really wants to. I try to message people, but conversations die out quickly, like I’m forcing something that isn’t there. I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off about me that others can sense. It’s like everyone else got the manual for connection and I missed that day.
