I’m starting to feel like I’ve slipped out of everyone’s peripheral vision. People still text me sometimes, but it’s all surface-level stuff—memes, quick updates, nothing that feels like they actually want *me*, just my reactions. I used to have a small group I felt anchored to, and now everyone’s “busy,” building lives that don’t really have a place for me in them. I don’t know if I’m boring, forgettable, or just not trying hard enough. It’s weird how you can be “known” online and still feel completely unrecognized in real life.
