Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m physically around people. Conversations feel like I’m just performing a script: nod, smile, ask the right questions, then go home and collapse into this heavy silence. I scroll through chats and realize most of my messages are me reaching out first, then overthinking every slow reply. 

I keep telling myself I should “just join things” or “put myself out there,” but I’m exhausted from always being the one trying. I miss feeling known, not just recognized. Like someone noticing when I’m quieter than usual, or remembering small details without me repeating them. 

It’s strange how life can be so full of noise and still feel this empty inside.
