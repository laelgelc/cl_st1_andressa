I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately.

My days are technically “busy”: work, commute, chores, scrolling, sleep. But there’s no real connection in any of it. I talk to coworkers, I answer emails, I say “have a good one” to cashiers, but it all feels like static. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.

What messes with my head is that, from the outside, I probably look fine. I show up, I crack jokes, I reply in group chats sometimes. Then I go home and realize I don’t actually have anyone I can text just to say, “Today sucked, can I vent?”

I keep thinking I should reach out more, join something, be “braver.” But there’s this heavy, tired part of me that whispers it won’t matter, that I’ll still feel like this even in a room full of people.
