I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s lives without anyone noticing. I have acquaintances, a job, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me. I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I’d feel comfortable calling if I broke down at 2 a.m.—not even at 8 p.m., honestly. I keep telling myself I’m “busy” or “independent,” but most nights it’s just me, my phone, and a silence that feels heavier than it should. I’m not sure when being alone turned into feeling this invisible.
