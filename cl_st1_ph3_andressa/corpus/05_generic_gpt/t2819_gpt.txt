I’m starting to feel like I’ve slipped out of everyone’s life without anyone really noticing. I have people I talk to, but everything feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. I scroll through chats and see conversations that just faded out, and I can’t tell if I stopped trying or if they did first. I keep thinking I should reach out, but then I convince myself I’m just bothering them. Even when I’m in a room full of people, I feel like a ghost—visible, but not really seen. It’s exhausting pretending this doesn’t hurt as much as it does.
