I’m starting to feel like I exist in the negative space of other people’s lives. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me or would notice if I disappeared for a while. I watch people my age getting married, having kids, going on trips with lifelong friends, and I can’t even think of one person I could call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong.  

What scares me most is how used to it I’m becoming—how normal it feels to have no one to text, no one to miss, and no one missing me.
