I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, and it’s hitting harder as I get older.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call in the middle of the night. When something good happens, I hesitate because I don’t know who to share it with. When something bad happens, I just go quiet and wait it out alone.

I overthink every interaction. After I talk to someone, I replay the conversation in my head and pick apart every word, convinced I sounded weird or annoying. Then I pull back, answer slower, cancel plans, and people eventually stop asking. It’s like I’m both starving for connection and the one locking the door.

Weekends are the worst. I scroll through group photos and “catching up with the best people” posts, and it feels like I’m watching life from behind glass. I want to be included, but I’m also terrified they’ll see the real me and decide I’m not worth the effort.

I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe just proof that I said it out loud somewhere.
