I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m slowly fading out of my own life.

On paper, I’m “fine.” I have a job, a place to live, people I text sometimes. But I go whole days where I don’t have a single real conversation. Not small talk at work, not “how was your weekend?” with a neighbor. I mean the kind of conversation where you feel seen. I can’t remember the last time I had that.

I watch other people so easily slip into friendships and relationships, make inside jokes, plan trips, call each other just to talk. For me, everything feels like I’m intruding, like I’m always one message away from being annoying. So I hold back. Then I feel even more alone, and the cycle just keeps going.

Nights are the worst. The silence gets so loud. I scroll, I lurk, I read other people’s lives, and somehow it just makes me feel more like a ghost. I’m here, but I don’t really feel like I’m part of anything.
