I’m starting to realize that I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, I can joke, I can do small talk at work or in group chats, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that everyone else seems to understand better than I do.

I watch other people move so easily from acquaintance to friend, to “let’s hang out,” to “I was thinking about you.” For me, it just stops at “we should hang out sometime,” and then nothing. I don’t know if I’m forgettable, off-putting, or just never in the right place at the right time.

The worst part is when something happens—good or bad—and I instinctively reach for my phone, scroll through my contacts, and realize there isn’t a single person I feel comfortable messaging first. Not because they’re bad people, but because I’m not sure I’m anyone’s “person.” I feel like background noise in my own life.
