I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

On paper, I’m “fine.” I have coworkers, a couple of old friends I text occasionally, family group chats that light up with memes. But none of it feels like it actually touches me. Conversations feel like I’m reading from a script: “How was your weekend?” “Got any plans?” I answer, they answer, and then there’s this heavy silence underneath everything that never really goes away.

What’s weird is that I’m not completely isolated. I can be sitting in a room full of people and still feel like I’m pressed up against a glass wall, watching everyone else actually participate in life while I just…exist nearby. I don’t know how to cross that distance.

I’ve started to dread evenings. The quiet of my apartment feels louder than any noise. I scroll, I watch videos, I lurk on subs like this, but when I finally put my phone down, it’s just me and this hollow feeling in my chest that I don’t really know what to do with.
