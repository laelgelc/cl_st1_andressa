I feel like I’m living next to people instead of with them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about shows or work drama, but no one who actually knows me. The kind of knowing where you can just send a text that says “today sucked” and they get it without an explanation.

Most days I’m surrounded by noise but feel completely disconnected. I watch other people make plans, share inside jokes, talk about “the group,” and I’m always the extra, never the default invite. I overthink every interaction, replaying conversations later and convincing myself I said something weird.

I keep wondering if there was a point where I missed the turnoff to “normal life” with close friends, relationships, all of that. Now it feels like I’m on this side road by myself, and I don’t know how to merge back.
