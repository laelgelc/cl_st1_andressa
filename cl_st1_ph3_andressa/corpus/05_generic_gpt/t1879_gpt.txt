I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but it all feels muted. Friends drifted away, or maybe I did. Conversations stay surface-level; I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and waited for the answer. I’m around people often enough, but I still feel weirdly invisible, like if I disappeared, the only difference would be fewer unread messages.
