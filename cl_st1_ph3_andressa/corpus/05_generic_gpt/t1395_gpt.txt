I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I talk to people every day—coworkers, neighbors, even family—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling needy or weird. I see friends forming deeper bonds, moving in together, getting married, and I’m still the “plus one” to my own life. I’m tired of pretending this doesn’t bother me as much as it does.
