I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. Invisible because it feels like no one really knows me, exposed because I’m constantly aware of how alone I am.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family I text sometimes—but it all feels like background noise. We talk about work, memes, random stuff, but nothing that makes me feel understood. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else actually live.

I’ve tried joining group chats, servers, hobbies, whatever, but I always end up drifting to the edge. I don’t know if I’m boring, awkward, or just not “clickable.” I overthink every message, then don’t send it, then hate myself for being quiet.

The hardest part is wanting someone to check in without being asked. Just a “hey, how are you really?” that isn’t small talk. I’m tired of being the one who reaches out, initiates, fills the silence. Some days I go the whole day barely speaking out loud and it feels like I’m slowly fading from my own life.
