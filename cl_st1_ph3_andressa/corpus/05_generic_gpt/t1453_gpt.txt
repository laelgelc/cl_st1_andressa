I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s creeping into everything. I go through the motions—work, chores, scrolling—but I feel like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it. Friends drifted away slowly, no big fight, just fewer messages until it was quiet. I try to reach out sometimes, but small talk feels exhausting and I worry I sound needy or weird. Nights are the worst; my phone is silent and I catch myself rehearsing conversations I’ll never have. It’s embarrassing how invisible I feel.
