It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re behind glass. I have friends, a job, conversations every day, but everything feels… shallow. I don’t know how to move from “we chat” to “we actually know each other.” I’m always scared I’ll overshare or be too much, so I stay on safe topics and then resent that no one really sees me. Nights are the worst—phone in hand, no one to text who’d actually understand, just scrolling and feeling more and more disconnected.
