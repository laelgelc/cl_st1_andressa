I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own.

My phone barely lights up unless it’s spam or work. I scroll through chats and see long stretches of silence, broken by the occasional “we should catch up soon” that never turns into anything concrete. I’ve stopped trying to initiate plans because I’m tired of being the one who reaches out and then watches things fizzle.

What makes it worse is that on paper nothing is “wrong.” I have a job, a place to live, people who would probably say they care. But the day-to-day reality is coming home to an empty space, eating alone, and realizing no one would really notice if I just stopped showing up online.

I’m not even sure what I’m asking for here. Maybe I just needed to say it somewhere.
