I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “around” people. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about surface stuff, but no one I feel truly close to. I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I’d feel comfortable calling if I was really falling apart.

What hurts most is that I used to have close friends, but life changes, moves, relationships, and I just… didn’t keep up. Now I’m out of practice. I overthink every message I send, then delete it. I’m scared people will find me clingy or boring, so I stay quiet, and then resent myself for being invisible.
