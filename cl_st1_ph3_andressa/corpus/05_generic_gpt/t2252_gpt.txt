I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I joke with or work with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. When I scroll through my contacts, there’s no name that feels “safe.” 

I’m tired of pretending I’m fine with it. I watch other people make plans, talk about group chats, inside jokes, and I feel like I’m standing outside a window looking in. I don’t even know what I’m doing wrong, or if it’s just who I am. It feels like I’m slowly disappearing.
