I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but no one I can text when something good or bad happens. My phone is either silent or full of group chats where I’m just a background character.

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like an outsider observing everyone else already having their own history and inside jokes. I don’t know how to move from “we talk sometimes” to “we’re close.” I overthink every message, every pause, every time someone doesn’t reply quickly.

Nights are the worst. I lie in bed scrolling, watching other people’s lives and friendships play out online, and it just emphasizes how replaceable I feel. It’s like I’m present in the world but not really in anyone’s life.
