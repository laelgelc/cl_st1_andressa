I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but there’s no real connection anywhere. Friends drifted away, or maybe I did. I don’t even know when it happened; I just noticed one day that no one really knows me anymore, not even family. I try small talk, but it all feels shallow and forced. Nights are the worst, when the silence is loud and there’s no one to message because it would feel random and awkward.
