I’m starting to think I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one I can text without overthinking every word. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one who feels “right” to message. 

I’m not exactly isolated—I leave the house, I talk to people—but it all feels like background noise. I come home, shut the door, and the silence feels heavier than it should. It’s strange how you can be “known” by many and still feel completely unseen.
