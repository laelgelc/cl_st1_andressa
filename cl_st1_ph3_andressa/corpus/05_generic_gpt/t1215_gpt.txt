I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family—but it all feels thin, like I’m separated from everyone by a sheet of glass. Conversations stay on small talk, and when they’re over I’m left with this heavy silence that feels louder than any noise.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel. I can spend an entire weekend without getting a single message unless I’m the one who reaches out first. I try to be there for others, listen, remember details, but it rarely seems to go both ways. It’s starting to make me question whether I’m actually important to anyone, or just conveniently there when needed.
