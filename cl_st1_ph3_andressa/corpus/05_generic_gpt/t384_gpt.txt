I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from behind glass. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling my phone—but it all feels detached, like I’m not really in it. I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m., no one who really knows what’s going on in my head. When I do see people, I feel out of sync, like everyone else got a manual on how to connect and I missed it. I’m tired of my own company but also exhausted by the idea of trying to be “on” for others. It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like the default setting.
