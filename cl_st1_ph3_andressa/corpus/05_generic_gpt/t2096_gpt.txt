I’m starting to realize I don’t really “exist” in other people’s lives. I’m the person who’s always available, always replying, always initiating, but if I stop texting first, everything just goes silent. People say they care, but they never actually show up. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect. It’s like I’m optional background noise in my own life, and I’m so tired of feeling that replaceable.
