I’ve hit that weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of people I’d call “friends,” but everything feels shallow. We talk about work, shows, memes, but never anything that makes me feel actually known. When I try to open up, I either get hit with advice I didn’t ask for or the conversation just… shifts away. It makes me feel like I’m too much when I’m honest, and too fake when I’m not.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch people post pictures with their “people” and it feels like I’m watching life happen from the outside. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would actually notice beyond a passing ‘that’s sad’?”

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I guess I just wanted to put this somewhere instead of letting it sit in my head.
