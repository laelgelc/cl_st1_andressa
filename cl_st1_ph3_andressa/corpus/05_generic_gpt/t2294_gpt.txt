I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t seem to match my life on paper. I have coworkers I talk to, a couple of friends I see every now and then, family group chats that buzz all day. But I still feel like I’m on the outside of everything, watching through glass.

When people ask how I’m doing, I default to “I’m good, just busy.” I don’t know how to say, “I feel like no one really knows me,” without sounding dramatic or needy. I’ve tried opening up a bit in the past and it usually gets brushed off with jokes or quick advice, and then I feel stupid for even trying.

I think what I miss is that feeling of being someone’s person. Not romantically necessarily, just… someone’s first call, not their backup plan when others are busy. I’m tired of always being the one who initiates plans, the one who listens, the one who says, “No worries, I understand,” and then goes home to silence.

It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like the way my life is just going to be.
