I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m a ghost in my own life. I go to work, do what I’m supposed to do, make small talk about weather and TV shows, and then come home to silence. Not peaceful silence, more like the kind that reminds you nobody is expecting you.

I have acquaintances, people who’d say hi if they saw me, but no one who actually knows what’s going on in my head. I scroll through my phone at night, trying to think of someone I could message without it feeling weird or forced, and I usually end up putting it down again.

I’m tired of being “fine” on the outside while feeling completely disconnected inside. It’s like I’m standing behind glass watching everyone else live in color, and I’m stuck in grayscale, not sure how to step through.
