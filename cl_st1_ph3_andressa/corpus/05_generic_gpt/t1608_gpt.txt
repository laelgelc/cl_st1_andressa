I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, I can be polite and even funny sometimes, but it all feels like I’m performing a version of myself that doesn’t really exist.

My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. Group chats move on without me. When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like a guest actor in other people’s lives, not someone who really belongs there. I usually leave early and nobody seems to notice.

What bothers me most is how used to it I’m becoming. I fill the silence with podcasts, YouTube, background noise, anything so I don’t have to sit with the fact that no one really knows me. I keep wondering if I missed some critical point where everyone else learned how to be close, and I just…didn’t.
