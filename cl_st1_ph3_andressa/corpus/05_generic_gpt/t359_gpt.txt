I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about shows or work. But as soon as it gets even slightly personal, I freeze. I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or stay surface-level and feel invisible. Then I replay every conversation for hours, convinced I came off weird or annoying.

Most of my “social life” is scrolling, watching other people’s friendships and relationships like a movie I’m not in. I tell myself I’m just introverted, but it feels more like I’m locked behind glass. I want to text people, ask to hang out, but the fear of being a burden is so strong that I just don’t.

Days blur together: work, commute, phone, sleep. I’m surrounded by people but feel like a ghost. I don’t know how to break out of this without making everything even more awkward.
