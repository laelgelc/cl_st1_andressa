I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my building, but I don’t feel connected to anyone. Conversations feel scripted—weather, work, “how was your weekend”—and then we retreat back into our bubbles. I keep hoping someone will notice that I’m not actually okay, but I’m also terrified of coming across as needy or weird if I say anything. So I keep everything surface-level and then hate myself later for not opening up. It’s this loop of wanting closeness, sabotaging it, and then sitting alone with that heaviness.
