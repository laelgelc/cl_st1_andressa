I’ve been feeling this weird combination of invisible and too visible at the same time.

Invisible, because no one really checks in. I can go days without a personal message unless I start the conversation, and even then it feels like I’m interrupting people’s “real” lives. Too visible, because when I’m around others I’m painfully aware of every word I say, every awkward pause, every sign that I don’t quite fit.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. But no one I’d call at 2 a.m., or even 9 p.m., honestly. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of those names belong to older versions of me, from schools, jobs, phases that don’t exist anymore.

It’s like I’m standing in a crowded room behind soundproof glass. I can see everyone laughing, connecting, moving on. I’m here, but not really part of it.
