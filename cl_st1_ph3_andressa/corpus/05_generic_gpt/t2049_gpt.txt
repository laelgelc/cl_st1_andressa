I’m starting to feel like I’ve aged out of connection. Friends drifted away, relationships ended, and I never really replaced any of it. My days are just work, screens, and silence. I try small talk with coworkers, but it never goes deeper, like there’s this invisible wall around me. I watch other people laugh, plan trips, share inside jokes, and I’m just… there, orbiting on the outside. I’m not even sure when I stopped trying, or if I ever really knew how.
