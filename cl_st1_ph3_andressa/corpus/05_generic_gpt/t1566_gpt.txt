I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting through my own life as a background character.

On paper, I’m “fine” – job, roof over my head, a couple of acquaintances. But most days I go without having a real conversation with anyone. I can go an entire weekend without my phone lighting up unless it’s a spam call or some notification I don’t care about.

I’m tired of pretending I’m introverted-by-choice when the truth is I just don’t know how to be wanted in anyone’s life. I overthink every message I send, then usually delete it. I replay old interactions and convince myself I was awkward or annoying, and that’s why people drifted away.

What bothers me most is how normal this has started to feel. Eating alone, scrolling alone, going to sleep with no one really knowing how my day went. It’s like I’m slowly disappearing, and no one is close enough to notice.
