I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2am, or even 8pm, honestly. I scroll through my contacts and it’s like looking at a list of people I’m not really part of.

What messes with me is how invisible this feels. From the outside I look functional: job, hobbies, small talk. Inside it feels like I’m watching my own life from the audience. I try to initiate plans and either get polite excuses or one-off hangouts that never become anything.

I keep wondering if I missed some critical stage where everyone else learned how to move from “friendly” to “actually close,” and now I’m just permanently stuck on the outside.
