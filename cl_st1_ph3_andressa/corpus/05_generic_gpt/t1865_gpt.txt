I’ve been feeling like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing.

My phone barely lights up anymore unless it’s some automated notification. I scroll through old chats and see this pattern where I’m always the one reaching out, asking how people are, trying to keep things going. When I stop, everything just goes silent. It’s like my existence depends on me constantly reminding people I’m still here.

I do have acquaintances and coworkers, but it all feels surface-level. We talk about work, shows, random stuff, but nothing that makes me feel actually known. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect while I’m just… present but not included.

What messes with me most is that I’m not even sure how to fix it. I’m not great at opening up in person, and when I try, I feel awkward and exposed. Then I retreat again and the cycle keeps going.
