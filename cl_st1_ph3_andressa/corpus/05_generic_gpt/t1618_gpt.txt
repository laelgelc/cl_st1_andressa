I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “connection.” I can make small talk, crack a joke, seem fine. Then I go home and replay every word, convinced I sounded weird or needy. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people who’d say hi if they saw me, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. Even when I’m with others, I feel like I’m behind glass watching myself interact. It’s exhausting pretending I’m okay with always being on the outside.
