I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, and it’s hitting harder as I get older. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work or shows, but it all feels surface-level. When I’m with them, I feel like I’m acting out a version of myself that’s “acceptable,” and when I go home I’m just…empty.

I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging without overthinking it. If something good or bad happens, I hesitate because I don’t want to be “too much” or bother anyone. So I end up saying nothing, and then complain to myself that no one really knows me.

What scares me is how normalized this is becoming. I’m getting used to the silence, to the background ache of it, like it’s just part of who I am now.
