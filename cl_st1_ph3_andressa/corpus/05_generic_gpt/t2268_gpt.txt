I feel like I’m slowly dissolving into the background of my own life. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but it all feels like acting. Small talk, polite laughs, then back to the silence of my apartment. 

What bothers me most is how forgettable I seem. People don’t remember things I’ve told them, don’t follow up, don’t notice when I disappear from group chats. I try to reach out, but it feels one-sided, like I’m begging for scraps of attention. 

I’m not even sure what I’m doing wrong. I’m not awful-looking, I’m not rude, I listen to others. Yet somehow, I never move past “acquaintance.” It’s like everyone else got a manual for connection that I missed.
