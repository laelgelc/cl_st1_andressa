I’m starting to feel like I don’t exist anywhere outside my own head. I go through the motions—work, commute, small talk—but none of it feels like real connection. I have acquaintances, people who’d say “hey” if they saw me, but no one who actually knows what my days are like, or how heavy everything feels.

I keep trying to “put myself out there,” but it always feels like I’m intruding on groups that are already formed, friendships that already have history. I end up quiet, then blame myself for not being more interesting, more funny, more something.

What scares me most is how used to this I’m becoming. The silence after I turn off my phone doesn’t feel unusual anymore. It just feels like the default.
