Lately I feel like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, say the right things, laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like I’m acting. When I get home, it’s just silence. I scroll through chats I’m too scared to message because I don’t want to seem needy or annoying. I keep thinking people already have their circles, their “person,” and I’m just this extra character no one really notices.
