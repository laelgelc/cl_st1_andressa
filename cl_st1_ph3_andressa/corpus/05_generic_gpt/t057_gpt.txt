I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, joke, make small talk at work, but it all feels like I’m playing a character. When I go home, there’s no one to message, no one who’d notice if I disappeared for a few days. I scroll, lurk, watch everyone else’s lives happen. I’m not exactly miserable, just… empty. Like I’m stuck on the outside of a glass wall, watching everyone else be real with each other.
