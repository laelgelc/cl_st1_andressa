I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I watch other people slip so easily into friendships and relationships, and I feel like I’m behind a glass wall, mimicking the motions but never really in it.  

I keep telling myself I’m “used to it,” but it still hits hard when a whole weekend passes and my phone doesn’t light up once unless I start the conversation.
