I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing.

My phone barely buzzes anymore unless it’s a notification from an app. I scroll through old conversations and see this pattern: me trying to keep things going, asking how people are, suggesting we meet up, and then their replies slowly get shorter, further apart, and then nothing. I don’t even feel angry, just… replaced and quietly pushed to the edge.

What makes it worse is that I’m not completely isolated in a physical sense. I go to work, I say “hey” to coworkers, I make small talk. But it’s all surface-level. No one actually knows what’s going on with me, and I don’t know how to bridge that gap without sounding needy or weird.

It’s like I’m surrounded by people but still somehow stuck in my own little bubble, watching life happen through a window.
