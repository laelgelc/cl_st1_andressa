I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, eat, sleep—but nothing really touches me. I have people around me, technically: coworkers, family I text sometimes, a couple of old friends I “should” reach out to. But I feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live.

What’s weird is that I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing. “I’m lonely” feels too simple, and also too heavy. I’m not sure when it got this bad. I think I slowly stopped trying, and now the silence feels safer than the possibility of being ignored or misunderstood.

I keep checking my phone like something might magically change, but it’s mostly notifications that don’t matter. The days blur together, and I can’t tell if I’m isolating myself or if I was just quietly left behind.
