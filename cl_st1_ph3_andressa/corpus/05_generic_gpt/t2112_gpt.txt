I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately.

I’m surrounded by people on paper—coworkers, neighbors, family chats—but none of it feels like real connection. Conversations stay on the surface: “How’s work?” “Busy.” “You?” Then silence. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted the messy, honest answer.

I’ve tried putting myself out there—meetups, group chats, hobbies—but I always end up on the edge of the circle, smiling, nodding, laughing at the right times, and going home feeling emptier than when I left. It’s like I’m visible enough to be included, but not enough to be known.

What scares me most is how normal this has started to feel. I go through the motions, pretend it’s fine, then lie in bed scrolling until I’m too tired to think about how isolated I actually am.

I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe just to say it somewhere.
