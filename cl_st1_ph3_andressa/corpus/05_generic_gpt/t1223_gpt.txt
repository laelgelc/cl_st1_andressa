I don’t really know how to talk about this in real life, so I’m dumping it here instead.

Lately it feels like I’m surrounded by people but not actually connected to anyone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I “chat” with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really bad happened. I’m always the one who listens, gives advice, cracks a joke, but when I go home it’s just…quiet. Not peaceful quiet, just empty.

I keep replaying old friendships in my head, wondering where I went wrong. People drifted away, got partners, had kids, moved, and I somehow stayed in the same place. I try to reach out, but conversations die quickly and I end up feeling like I’m forcing something that isn’t there anymore.

What makes it worse is feeling like I’m the only one stuck in this limbo while everyone else is “moving on with their lives.” Social media doesn’t help; it just highlights what I don’t have. I’m tired of my own company, but also weirdly scared of letting anyone close because I assume they’ll eventually leave too.

I don’t really have a point to this. I just needed to put into words that I’m lonely in a way that feels like it’s sinking into my bones.
