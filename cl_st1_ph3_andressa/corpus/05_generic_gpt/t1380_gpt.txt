I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really match my life on paper. I have coworkers I chat with, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family group chats that ping all day. But I still feel like I’m existing behind glass, watching everything instead of being part of it.

I’m tired of being “the listener,” the one people vent to and then disappear on. I know a lot about other people’s lives, but no one really knows me. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I pull back and go back to “I’m fine.”

Nights are the worst. I scroll through social media, seeing people hanging out, getting married, having kids, and it feels like I somehow missed a memo everyone else got. I want simple things—someone to text good news to, someone who actually notices when I go quiet.

I’m not even sure what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe I just needed to put it somewhere that isn’t my own head for once.
