I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I have friends on paper, group chats that ping all day, coworkers who say I’m “easy to talk to,” but I still go home feeling like I’m not really known by anyone. 

Whenever conversations get close to anything real, I panic and steer it back to jokes or small talk. Then I resent that no one sees the real me, even though I’m the one hiding. It’s like I’m behind glass: visible, but untouchable. 

I keep wondering if this is just my life now—surrounded by people, but somehow not actually with them.
