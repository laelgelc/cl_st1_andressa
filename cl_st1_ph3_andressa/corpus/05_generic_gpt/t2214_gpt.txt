I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like a ghost.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with about shows or memes, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what my life looks like when I go home, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling like I’m being “too much.”

I’ve tried to reach out more, send the first text, suggest plans, but I always feel like I’m intruding on other people’s real lives. Like they already have their group, their person, their routines, and I’m this extra piece that doesn’t quite fit anywhere. When plans fall through or messages go unanswered, it hits way harder than it should.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. The silence after I close my door. Eating alone, scrolling until my eyes hurt, watching other people’s friendships and relationships through a screen like a spectator.

I keep wondering if there was some point in my life where I missed a turn and everyone else kept going. Now I’m stuck on this side road that loops back into itself, and I can’t tell if there’s actually a way back to where everyone else seems to be, or if this is just it.
