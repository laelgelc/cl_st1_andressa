I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even some old friends I still text sometimes—but it all feels thin, like plastic wrap between me and everyone else. Conversations stay on safe topics. I laugh, I respond, I “seem fine,” and then I go home and it’s just…silence. Not peaceful silence, more like an echo chamber where every small insecurity gets amplified.

What bothers me most is that no one really knows me. Not the version of me that overthinks every interaction, or the one that wants to say “I’m not okay” but can’t find the right moment. I’m scared that if I disappeared, people would notice in the way you notice a missing background character, not someone important to the story.

I’m tired of feeling like an extra in my own existence.
