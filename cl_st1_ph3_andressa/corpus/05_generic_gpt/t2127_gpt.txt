I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, small talk, scrolling—but it all feels hollow. I have acquaintances, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. When I’m around others, I feel like I’m performing a version of myself that’s easier to digest. Then I get home and the silence hits harder because I realize no one really knows me, and I’m not sure I remember who that is either.
