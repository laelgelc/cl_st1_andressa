I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I have friends on paper, chats that light up, group photos I’m tagged in, but it all feels like I’m watching someone else’s life. I can hang out, laugh, make small talk, then come home and feel like I never really existed in the room. I’m tired of being “included” but never really seen. It’s like I’m permanently on the outside of my own relationships.
