I don’t even know if what I feel counts as “lonely” anymore or if it’s just the default setting of my life.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers, family group chat, a couple of old friends I send memes to. But I don’t feel actually *seen* by any of them. Every interaction feels like small talk on top of small talk. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I was and actually wanted the real answer.

What messes with my head is how performative everything is. I can look “fine” online—liking posts, commenting, replying with emojis—while feeling like I’m standing behind glass screaming. I’ll type out long messages, delete them, and go back to “lol yeah” because I don’t want to be “too much” for anyone.

I’ve reached a point where I don’t even try to initiate plans anymore. I got tired of being the one who suggests things and then gets vague “we should totally do that sometime” replies that never turn into anything. It’s like I’m always an optional extra in other people’s lives.

I’m not sure what I’m hoping for by writing this. Maybe I just wanted to put into words that I exist, even if it doesn’t really feel like it most days.
