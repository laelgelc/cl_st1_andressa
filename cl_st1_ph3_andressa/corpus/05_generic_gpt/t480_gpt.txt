I’ve hit that point where I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I have acquaintances, coworkers, family chats, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if everything fell apart. I keep conversations light because whenever I try to go deeper, it feels like I’m oversharing or boring them. So I end up retreating, then feeling even more disconnected. It’s like I’m slowly disappearing from other people’s lives while still being physically present.
