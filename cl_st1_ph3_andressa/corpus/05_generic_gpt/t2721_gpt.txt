I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to be close to people.

On paper, I’m “fine” — job, roof over my head, a couple of acquaintances I can message about surface-level stuff. But I don’t feel known by anyone. I’ll be in a group chat, participating, and still feel like I’m standing outside a window watching everyone else interact.

When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I freeze and default to jokes and small talk. Then I go home and replay every sentence in my head, convinced I sounded weird or needy. So I pull back, which just makes the distance worse.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, maybe type something and delete it. I’m surrounded by ways to “connect,” yet I can go days without a real conversation that goes past “How’s it going?”
