I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I talk to people every day at work, answer emails, sit in meetings, but it all feels like noise around a hollow center. Friends are “busy,” family assumes I’m fine because I don’t say otherwise. I scroll, I lurk, I like posts, but it never turns into anything real. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted to stay for the answer.
