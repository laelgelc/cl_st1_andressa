I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but almost no real interaction. Friends drifted away slowly, not from any big fight, just less and less contact until it felt weird to reach out. Family is busy with their own stuff, and I don’t want to be the one always saying “hey, can we talk?” I keep wondering if this is just how adulthood is supposed to be now, or if I somehow missed a turn.
