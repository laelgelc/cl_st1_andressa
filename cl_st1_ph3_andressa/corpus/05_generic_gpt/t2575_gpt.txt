I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay” around them.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with. But I don’t feel like anyone actually knows me, and I don’t really know how to let them. Whenever conversations start to get personal, I feel this wall go up in my chest and I default back to small talk or sarcasm.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk on here, I watch other people’s lives through a screen and feel like I’m standing outside a house looking in through the window. I want someone to text, someone who’d notice if I vanished for a few days, but I’ve let most of my old friendships die because I couldn’t keep up the “I’m fine” act.

I’m not isolated in the obvious way. I just feel permanently out of place, like I’m always the extra person in every room, even when I’m supposed to belong there.
