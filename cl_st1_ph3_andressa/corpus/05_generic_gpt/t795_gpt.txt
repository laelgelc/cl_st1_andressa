Lately I feel like I’m watching my own life from behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people to nod at, but no one I can text when something small or stupid happens. I scroll through chats that used to be active and it’s just me sending the last message months ago. I try to be friendly, make plans, but it feels like I’m always the extra, never the person someone thinks of first. It’s exhausting pretending I’m okay with this.
