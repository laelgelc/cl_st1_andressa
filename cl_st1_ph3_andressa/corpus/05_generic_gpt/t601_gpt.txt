I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but I don’t feel *with* any of them. Conversations feel like I’m reading from a script. I say the right things, laugh at the right times, but it all feels like I’m watching myself from the outside.

What really gets to me is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped reaching out, I’m not sure who would notice beyond a “hey, you okay?” text once, maybe twice, and then silence. I scroll through my phone sometimes looking for someone I can message without overthinking it and realize there isn’t anyone.

It’s weird how you can be “liked” and still feel completely unwanted.
