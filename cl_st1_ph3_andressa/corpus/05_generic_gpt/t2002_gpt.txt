I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I go to work, I smile, I make small talk, and then I come home to silence that feels heavier every day. Friends are busy, relationships never really stick, and I’m honestly not sure if it’s me pushing people away or them just drifting off. I scroll through chats I’m too scared to start, overthinking every word. It’s weird how you can be “known” by people and still feel completely unseen.
