I’ve started to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about work, news, random stuff, but as soon as it gets even slightly personal, I freeze. It’s like there’s this invisible wall between me and everyone else, and I don’t even know when I built it.

I watch other people fall into easy conversations, inside jokes, shared histories. Meanwhile, I’m always the extra: invited sometimes, remembered rarely, forgotten often. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or boring or just… off.

What scares me most is how normal the loneliness is starting to feel. I go through the motions—messages, scrolling, pretending to be busy—while this quiet emptiness sits in the background, and I honestly don’t know what to do with it anymore.
