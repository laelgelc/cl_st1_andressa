I’m surrounded by people but feel like a ghost. Friends, coworkers, family—they’re there, yet I still feel completely disconnected. Conversations stay on the surface; I can’t seem to bridge that gap to something real. I overthink every interaction, then retreat and feel even lonelier. I crave someone who actually “gets” me, but I don’t even know how to show who I am anymore. It’s like I’m fading in plain sight.
