I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too visible at the same time. Invisible because no one really checks in, too visible because I’m hyper-aware of how alone I look when I’m out by myself. I scroll through chats that went dead months ago, hover over the message box, then close it because I don’t want to seem needy or awkward.

I’m surrounded by people at work, online, in public, but it all feels like background noise. Surface-level small talk, nothing deeper. I can’t tell if I’ve forgotten how to connect or if people just don’t want to.

Nights are the worst. It’s like everything slows down enough that the emptiness gets louder. I keep thinking, “Is this it? Is this just how my life is going to be?”
