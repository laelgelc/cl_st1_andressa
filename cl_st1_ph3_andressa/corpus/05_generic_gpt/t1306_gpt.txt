I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “with” people. Conversations feel like I’m acting out a role I learned a long time ago, and I’m terrified someone will notice how empty it feels inside. I scroll through chats and realize most of my messages are me checking in on others; when I stop initiating, everything just goes silent. It makes me wonder if I’ve ever actually been important to anyone, or if I’ve just been a convenient background character people forget once they leave the scene.
