I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but no one who really knows me. The older I get, the more embarrassing it feels to admit how lonely I am, like it’s some kind of personal failure.

I scroll through chats and see conversations that just… faded. I never know if I was too much, too distant, or just forgettable. I overthink every message, so sometimes I just don’t send anything at all, then blame myself when things die out.

Nights are the worst. It’s quiet, and I keep thinking how nobody is expecting a text from me, nobody is wondering how my day went. It makes everything feel kind of hollow.
