I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing. My phone is mostly silent unless it’s a notification from an app, not a person. I watch my friends move on, get partners, new friend groups, new routines, and I’m still here, stuck in the same loop. I try to reach out, but conversations dry up quickly or feel forced. I’m presentable enough in public, but when I come home it’s just this heavy, dull emptiness that doesn’t really go away.
