I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like noise around a vacuum. I go through the motions of being “fine,” but when I get home it’s just silence and the feeling that no one really knows me.

When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel ashamed afterward, or I shut down and stick to small talk. Both ways leave me feeling more isolated. I watch other people form friendships that seem to grow naturally, and I feel like I missed some crucial class everyone else attended.

It’s not that I want a huge circle. I just want one or two people I can message without overthinking, who’d actually notice if I disappeared for a while.
