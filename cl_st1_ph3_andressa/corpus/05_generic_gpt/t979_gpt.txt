I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like acting. I leave conversations feeling more drained and more alone, like I’ve just proven I don’t really belong anywhere. I overthink every message I send, then end up saying nothing. Weekends are the worst—so much empty time, but no one I feel close enough to reach out to without feeling like a burden.
