Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m living behind glass.

I see people everywhere – at work, on the bus, in my building – but it all feels like I’m watching a movie I’m not in. When I do talk to people, it’s small talk that evaporates as soon as the conversation ends. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to feel like I don’t know myself outside of being “the quiet one.”

What bothers me most is the contrast. Online, I can joke around in group chats and people seem to like me. In person, I freeze. My mind goes blank, I second-guess everything I say, and then I replay it all later, cringing at how awkward I must have seemed. So I pull back, and then feel even more disconnected.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, but I don’t want to be “annoying” or “needy.” So I just lie there with this heavy feeling in my chest, wondering if anyone would actually notice if I just quietly drifted out of their lives.
