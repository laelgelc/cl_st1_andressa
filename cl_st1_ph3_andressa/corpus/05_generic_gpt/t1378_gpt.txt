I’m starting to feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life. I have acquaintances, people I joke with at work, but no one who actually knows me. My phone only lights up for notifications, never for someone asking how I am. When weekends come, I lie about being “busy” because admitting I have no plans feels pathetic. I scroll, I watch shows, I sleep, then repeat. It’s like existing on mute while everyone else is on speaker.
