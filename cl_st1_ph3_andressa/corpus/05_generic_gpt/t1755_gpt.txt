I keep realizing that most of my “social life” is just background noise. I have coworkers, classmates, people I chat with online, but no one I’d actually call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I’m always the one people vent to, but when I try to open up, the conversation somehow drifts back to them.  

I feel weirdly invisible, like I exist on the edge of everyone else’s story. I go home, put on some show, scroll through feeds, and pretend this is normal. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing.
