Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m physically around people. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. I watch others make plans, share inside jokes, and I’m just… nearby, never really included. I try to reach out, but messages get left on read or answered hours later with one-word replies. It makes me question if I’m actually hard to like, or if I’ve already missed my chance to build real connections.
