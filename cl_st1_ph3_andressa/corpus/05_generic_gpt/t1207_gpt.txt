I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, only how to perform “being okay” around them.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online. But I don’t have anyone I’d feel comfortable calling at 2 a.m. if something was wrong. I’m always the one who listens, who gives advice, who says “no worries, I understand,” but when it comes to sharing my own stuff, I freeze up. It feels like I’m an extra in everyone else’s movie.

The worst part is how invisible the loneliness is. From the outside, my life looks fine: job, hobbies, small talk. Inside, everything feels muted and far away, like I’m watching my own life through glass and can’t find the door to step through.
