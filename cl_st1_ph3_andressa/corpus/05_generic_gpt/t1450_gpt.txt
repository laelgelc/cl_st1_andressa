I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately.

I have people around me—coworkers, classmates, family—but I don’t feel *with* any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes, “how was your weekend?” I answer, they nod, and it’s like we’re all reading from a script. I keep waiting for someone to go off-script, to ask something real, but no one does. And I’m too scared to be the one who does it.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels. From the outside, I probably look “fine.” I show up, I laugh at the right moments, I send the “lol that’s funny” texts. Inside, it’s just this heavy, empty feeling that follows me everywhere. I’ll be in a group chat with 10 people and still feel like I’m standing on the outside of a window, watching them all hang out inside.

I’ve tried joining clubs, going to events, saying yes more. I meet people, we talk once or twice, and then it fades. No one really sticks. I start to wonder if there’s something off-putting about me that I just can’t see.

Nights are the worst. That’s when everything slows down and I realize there’s no one I can message who would genuinely *get* me, not just reply out of politeness or boredom. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move forward. Mine just feels…paused.

I don’t even know exactly what I’m asking for here. I think I just needed to say it somewhere.
