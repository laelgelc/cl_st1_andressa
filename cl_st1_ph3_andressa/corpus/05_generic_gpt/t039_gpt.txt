I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m around people often enough—work, family, the usual—but it all feels like acting. I know the lines, the jokes, the “I’m fine, you?” routine, but it never goes past surface level.  

I keep thinking I must be doing something wrong, because everyone else seems to find their people, their group chats, their “let’s hang out” texts. I go home to silence. If my phone buzzes, it’s spam or some app notification.  

I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I am. I’ve been lonely for so long it’s starting to feel like my personality instead of a phase.
