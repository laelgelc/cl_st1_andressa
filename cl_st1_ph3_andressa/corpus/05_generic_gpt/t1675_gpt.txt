I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people at work, I reply to messages, I even crack jokes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who’s better adjusted than I am. When the day ends, no one really checks in, and I don’t feel like I can reach out without seeming needy or dramatic.

What bothers me most is how interchangeable I feel. If I disappeared from group chats or stopped showing up to things, I’m not sure anyone’s routine would actually change. I have acquaintances, but not that one person who’d notice subtle shifts in my mood or ask how I’m really doing.

It’s strange how heavy it can feel to realize you’re basically background noise in your own life.
