I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a few people I message sometimes, but it all feels surface level. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.

When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I hold back and the conversation dies. I watch other people slip into friendships so easily, like it’s breathing, and I’m stuck analyzing every word I say.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, listen to podcasts just to fill the silence. It’s like I’m living next to life instead of in it.
