I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m technically “not alone” — I have coworkers, some acquaintances, a family that checks in occasionally — but I feel like a ghost around all of them. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, “how was your weekend?” I answer, they answer, and then it just…ends. No one really knows me and I’m starting to feel like there’s nothing to know.

I used to think I was just shy or introverted, but lately it feels more like I’m fundamentally out of sync with everyone. I watch friendships forming around me, group chats buzzing, people making plans, and I’m somehow always the person who gets left out without anyone actively excluding me. It’s like I’m easy to forget.

I go home, scroll through my phone, and realize there’s no one I can message without feeling like I’m bothering them. So I don’t. And the silence just stretches.
