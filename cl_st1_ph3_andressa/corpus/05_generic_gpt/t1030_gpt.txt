I’m starting to realize that I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. Not in the casual “how’s it going” way—I can fake that—but in the deeper “this person really sees me” way. I’ll have conversations, laugh at the right moments, say the right things, and still walk away feeling like a ghost that briefly borrowed a body.

Most of my “social life” is group chats I barely participate in and scrolling through other people’s photos, trying not to notice how often I’m just not there. I get invited less and less, and part of me understands why: I’ve become the quiet one who always says “maybe” and usually means “no.”

What makes it worse is that I can’t even tell if I want people around or if I just hate the feeling of being left out. When I’m alone, I ache for company. When I’m with people, I feel out of place, like I’m watching myself from the outside.

It’s like I’m stuck between craving closeness and being terrified of it.
