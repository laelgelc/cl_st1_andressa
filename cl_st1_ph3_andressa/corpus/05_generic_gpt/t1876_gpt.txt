I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like it’s changing who I am.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. I can talk about work, memes, random stuff, but the second I try to say something real, my brain freezes. I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I stay quiet and feel invisible.

I miss having someone who actually knows the ongoing story of my life. Someone I don’t have to “catch up” with every time. I’m tired of being the person who’s always “fine” while feeling like I could disappear from most people’s lives and nothing would really change.
