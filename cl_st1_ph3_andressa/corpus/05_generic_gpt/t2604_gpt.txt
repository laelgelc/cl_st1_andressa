I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I feel truly close to. I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I’d feel comfortable calling if something actually went wrong. I keep conversations shallow because I’m scared of being too much, but then I feel invisible. It’s like I’m always on the outside of the glass, watching everyone else live their lives together.
