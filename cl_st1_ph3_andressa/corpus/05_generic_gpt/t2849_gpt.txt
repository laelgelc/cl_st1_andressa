I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I see everyone around me forming these easy, natural friendships and I feel like I’m behind glass, watching. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. 

When I’m with others I feel out of place; when I’m alone the silence feels heavy. I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying hard enough, but whenever I do reach out it feels forced and awkward. I replay conversations afterward, cringing at every small mistake. 

It’s like being homesick for a place I’ve never actually been.
