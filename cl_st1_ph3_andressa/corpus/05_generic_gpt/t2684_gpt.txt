I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my apartment building, but it all feels like static. Conversations are small talk, nothing sticks, and when I go home it’s like I vanish. I try to reach out, but I always feel like I’m interrupting someone else’s real life, like I’m an extra in their story.  

What really scares me is how normal the loneliness is starting to feel. I go days without a genuine conversation and barely notice until something small hits me—seeing a group laughing together, or hearing “How are you?” and realizing I don’t have an honest answer that anyone actually wants to hear.
