I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people most days—coworkers, classmates, random small talk—but it all feels like noise instead of connection. I can joke around, seem “fine,” but when I go home it’s like I vanish from everyone’s mind, including my own.

What bothers me is that I don’t even know how to fix it. I’ve tried messaging old friends, joining group chats, going to events. Conversations never seem to move past the surface. People are “busy,” or they just stop replying, and I’m left rereading the last message wondering what I did wrong.

It’s starting to feel like there’s something fundamentally off about me that other people can sense before I even open my mouth. I don’t know how you’re supposed to keep trying when it always ends the same way.
