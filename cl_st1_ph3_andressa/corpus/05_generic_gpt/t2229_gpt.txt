I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, joke, even seem social, but it feels like I’m acting out a script. When the conversation ends, there’s this heavy silence that hits harder than before. I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel I can message without it seeming weird or needy. I keep thinking people already have their circles, their “person,” and I’m just this extra background character no one would really miss.
