I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is happening behind glass.

On paper I’m “fine.” I have a job, a place to live, people I can technically text. But it all feels so thin. Conversations are mostly logistics or memes. When something actually hurts, I stare at my phone trying to think of who I could send it to, and I just…don’t. Either I feel like I’d be bothering them, or I’m scared they’ll respond with something surface-level and I’ll feel even more alone.

I go through whole days without anyone saying my name out loud. Sometimes I realize I haven’t heard my own voice except for “thank you” to a cashier. I scroll through social media and see people posting group photos and inside jokes, and I can’t even imagine what it’s like to be someone others naturally think of and invite.

What messes with me is that I know I contribute to this. I turn down plans because I’m tired or anxious, then sit at home wishing I were included in something. I want connection so badly, but when it’s right in front of me I freeze, like I’ve forgotten how to be a person around other people.
