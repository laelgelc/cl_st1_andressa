I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but no one I’d feel okay calling at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. I overthink every message I send and then usually delete it. I watch other people’s lives move forward—relationships, friendships, plans—while mine just loops between work, screens, and silence. It feels like I’m disappearing in plain sight.
