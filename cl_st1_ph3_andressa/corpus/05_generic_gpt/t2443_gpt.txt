I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work or shows, but no one I feel safe being fully honest with. When something good happens, I don’t know who to tell. When something bad happens, I just go quiet and scroll my phone. I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but every time I reach out, it feels forced, like I’m auditioning for a role I never get.
