I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about shows or work, but it all feels like small talk on top of a huge empty space. I go home and there’s no one who really knows what my day was like, or who I can message without feeling like I’m bothering them.

I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel close enough to call. I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but when I do reach out, conversations fade quickly and I feel like I’m forcing something that isn’t there. I miss feeling chosen, important, like someone’s first option instead of an afterthought.
