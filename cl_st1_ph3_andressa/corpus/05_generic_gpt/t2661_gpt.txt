I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to be close to anyone. I have acquaintances, people I joke with, group chats I barely type in, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I keep conversations surface-level because I’m terrified that if I show too much, people will quietly step back. At the same time, I’m exhausted from pretending I’m fine with this “independent” version of myself. It feels like I’m always on the outside of the glass, watching everyone else actually live.
