I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I’m surrounded by people most days—work, errands, the usual—but I don’t feel like I actually exist to anyone in a real way. Conversations stay on the surface. I crack a joke, they laugh, and then it’s like I disappear again.

What messes with me is that I can’t tell if I’ve forgotten how to connect or if I never really knew. I overthink every message I send, then usually delete it and say nothing. I tell myself people are busy, but the silence still feels like a judgment.

Nights are the worst. That’s when the “no one really knows you” thoughts get loud. I keep wondering how other people manage to build those close, stable friendships that don’t feel like they’re about to fade at any moment.
