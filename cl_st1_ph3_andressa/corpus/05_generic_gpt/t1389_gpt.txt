I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold small talk, crack a joke, seem “fine,” but underneath it feels like I’m behind glass. Friends drifted away, or maybe I let them. I answer messages late, then feel hurt when no one insists. I scroll, lurk, watch everyone else’s lives move forward while mine feels paused. I crave someone who really gets me, but when chances appear I freeze or shut down. It’s like I’m both starving for closeness and terrified of it.
