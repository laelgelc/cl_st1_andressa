I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, groceries—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else from a distance. My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from some app, and when I scroll through social media, it’s like everyone else is living in full color while I’m stuck in grayscale.

I used to have friends I’d talk to every day. Now conversations are reduced to “we should catch up sometime” that never actually happens. I’m not even sure when I became this disconnected; it feels like it happened slowly enough that I didn’t notice until everything was quiet.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. The silence, the empty weekends, eating alone. I’m starting to forget what it’s like to feel genuinely seen by another person.
