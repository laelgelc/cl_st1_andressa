I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is something I’m watching instead of living.

I have people around me – coworkers, acquaintances, even family – but I never feel actually *with* them. I can be in a room full of conversation and still feel like I’m behind glass, observing everyone else know when to laugh, what to say, how to just… exist together. I’m always a step off.

I try to reach out, but it feels forced, like I’m playing a part I never learned properly. Small talk drains me, deeper talks never quite happen, and I leave every interaction replaying everything I said, convinced I was awkward or boring.

It’s weird how you can be “known” by people and still feel completely unseen.
