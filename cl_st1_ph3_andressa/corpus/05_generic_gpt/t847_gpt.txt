I’m starting to feel like I exist in the negative space of other people’s lives. I’m around coworkers, classmates, family, but it’s like I’m slightly out of focus for all of them. Conversations skim over me; I’m there, but not really *in* them. 

I keep trying to “put myself out there” like everyone suggests. Join a group, message someone first, suggest plans. Most of it just fizzles out or gets postponed forever. After a while, it’s hard not to take the hint that people’s lives move fine with or without me.

What really gets to me is how constant it is. I can be in a crowded room and still feel this hollow, heavy silence inside, like I’m the only one who can hear it.
