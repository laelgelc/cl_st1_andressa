Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, talk to people, even laugh at jokes, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. When the day ends and I’m back home, it’s just silence. Not peaceful silence, but the kind that reminds you no one is coming over, no one is expecting your message.

What bothers me most is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped showing up, I’m not sure anyone’s routine would change. Friends are “busy,” which I get, but somehow I’m always the one reaching out first, and when I stop, the conversations just vanish. It’s like I’m fading out of people’s lives without them even noticing.
