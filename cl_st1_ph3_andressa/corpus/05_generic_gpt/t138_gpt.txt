I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life and no one noticed when it happened.

I have people around me—coworkers, some old friends online—but everything feels surface-level. We talk about shows, work, memes, but never anything real. When I try to open up, the conversation just quietly dies, like I broke some unspoken rule.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through chats I’m too scared to start because I don’t want to be “too much.” I overthink every message, every silence, every seen-but-not-replied-to.

It’s a weird kind of loneliness: not being completely alone, but feeling like nobody really knows me, and maybe nobody wants to.
