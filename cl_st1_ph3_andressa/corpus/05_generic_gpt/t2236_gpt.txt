I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like it’s rewiring my brain.

I go through entire days barely speaking out loud. When someone finally messages me, I get this rush of anxiety instead of relief, like I’ve forgotten how to be a person. I overthink every word, then end up replying hours later or not at all, which obviously doesn’t help.

I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. Even when I’m around people, it feels like I’m watching from behind glass. They all seem to have shared histories, inside jokes, someone who’d notice if they disappeared for a week.

I’m scared that if I vanished, it would take a long time for anyone to really register it.
