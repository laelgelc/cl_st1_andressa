I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call in the middle of the night. I scroll through old messages and see conversations that just faded out, usually because I didn’t know what to say next. Everyone seems busy, settled, already chosen. I keep wondering if I missed some crucial stage where people learned how to be close to each other.
