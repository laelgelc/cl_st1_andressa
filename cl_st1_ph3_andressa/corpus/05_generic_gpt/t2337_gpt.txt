I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to be around them.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who’s “fine.” I can be funny, helpful, even “social,” but the second I go home, it’s like the volume drops to zero. No messages. No calls. Just silence and the feeling that if I disappeared, it would take a while for anyone to notice.

I’ve tried opening up a bit, but it always feels like too much or not enough. Either I overshare and feel weird, or I keep it surface-level and stay invisible. I’m old enough that I feel like I should have “my people” by now, but I don’t. It’s like everyone else got a manual for how to build real relationships, and I missed that day.
