I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me. The silence when I get home feels louder every day. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives play out and it feels like I’m behind glass. I’m tired of being “the independent one” when that’s just code for “no one checks in.” I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I am.
