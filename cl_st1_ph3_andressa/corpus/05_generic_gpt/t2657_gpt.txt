I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore.

I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but it all feels like I’m playing a role. I can be “pleasant,” “helpful,” even “funny” sometimes, but none of it feels like me. When the interaction ends, I’m just left with this weird emptiness, like I was acting in a play no one watched.

I keep thinking about how long it’s been since someone has really known me—like, the unfiltered version. I’ve edited myself for so long that I’m not sure what the unfiltered version even is. When I try to open up, I get scared I’m being “too much” and pull back.

So I stay on the surface with everyone, then wonder why I feel invisible. It’s like I’m both the one locking the door and the one standing outside, knocking.
