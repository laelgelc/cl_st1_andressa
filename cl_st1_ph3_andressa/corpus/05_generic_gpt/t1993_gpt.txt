Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, I make small talk, people probably think I’m fine. But I go home and it’s just this heavy silence that feels louder every day. I scroll through my phone looking for someone I could message, and realize I don’t actually have anyone I’m close enough to bother. 

I’ve drifted from old friends, and new connections never seem to move past surface level. I’m tired of pretending I’m “busy” when really I’m just alone, hoping someone will notice without me having to say anything. It’s embarrassing how much it hurts to feel this forgettable.
