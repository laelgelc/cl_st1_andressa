I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I’m surrounded by people every day at work, I make small talk, I nod, I laugh at the right times. But when I go home and shut the door, it’s like none of it was real. I don’t have anyone I can text just to say something stupid or unimportant. Everything I share with others feels edited and safe, never the raw stuff.  

I watch friendships around me deepen over years, inside jokes and shared histories, and I realize I’m always the extra. I’m there, but not really woven in. I worry that if I stopped showing up, it would take people a while to notice.
