I’ve been feeling this weird mix of emptiness and exhaustion lately, like I’m constantly surrounded by people and yet completely disconnected from all of them.

I go through the motions: work, small talk, pretending to be “fine.” I laugh at the right times, ask the right questions, and then come home and feel like I’ve just performed a version of myself that isn’t actually me. It’s like I’m present, but not really there.

I don’t think anyone really knows me. Not in a dramatic way, just… in a factual way. The things that actually matter to me, the thoughts that keep me up at night, the fears I have about the future—none of that ever makes it into conversations. I don’t even know how to bring it up without feeling like I’m being too much or too heavy.

What makes it worse is seeing everyone else seem to have their “people” – group chats, weekend plans, inside jokes. I’m always the extra, the afterthought, the person you invite if there’s space. I don’t remember the last time someone reached out first just to check in.

Sometimes I wonder if I’ve just missed the window where people form those deep bonds, and now I’m stuck orbiting around other people’s lives, never really part of them.
