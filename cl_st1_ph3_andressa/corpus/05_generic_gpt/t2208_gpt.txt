I’ve been feeling like I’m living parallel to everyone else instead of with them.

On paper I’m “fine”: I go to work, I answer emails, I nod in meetings, I say “have a good one” to the cashier. But none of it feels like it touches me. It’s like I’m acting out a character who’s vaguely based on me, and everyone else is just far away behind glass.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels. If you looked at my life from the outside, you’d probably never guess how quiet it gets when the door closes. No messages. No calls. Just me refreshing apps out of habit, half-hoping someone remembered I exist, half-dreading any interaction at all.

I’ve tried to “put myself out there” like people say, but the conversations die quickly or stay surface-level. I’m tired of being the person people talk to only when they’re bored, never when they need someone.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. Maybe I just needed to say it somewhere.
