I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. I don’t have that “person” I can message when something small or stupid happens. I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel close enough to bother. I keep telling myself I’m just “busy” or “introverted,” but it feels more like I’ve quietly disappeared from everyone’s life, including my own.
