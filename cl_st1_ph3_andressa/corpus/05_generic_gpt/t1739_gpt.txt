I’m starting to worry that I’ve forgotten how to connect with people. I can chat about work or weather, but anything deeper feels impossible, like there’s a glass wall between me and everyone else. I watch friends move on with partners, kids, group trips, and I’m just… stuck in my room, scrolling, lurking, pretending I’m fine. I don’t really tell anyone how lonely I am because it feels embarrassing, like I’m admitting I’m unlovable. I keep wondering if this is just my life now, and if I’ve already missed my chance to belong anywhere.
