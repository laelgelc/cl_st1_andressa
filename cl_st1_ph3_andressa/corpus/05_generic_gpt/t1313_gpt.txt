I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but I don’t feel *with* any of them. Conversations feel like reading from a script. I say the right things, laugh at the right times, and go home feeling like I never actually showed up.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. I wake up, go through the motions, and the emptiness just feels… normal now. I scroll through social media and see people sharing inside jokes, group photos, even boring everyday stuff, and it hits me that no one really knows mine.

I can’t tell if I’ve isolated myself or if I was just never really “seen” to begin with.
