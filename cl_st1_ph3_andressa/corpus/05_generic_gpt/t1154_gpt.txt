I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s lives without anyone noticing. Days blur into each other: work, commute, stare at my phone, pretend I’m fine. I have people I “talk” to, but no one I can actually be honest with without feeling like a burden. Group chats move on without me, invitations quietly stop coming. I keep wondering whether I’m doing something wrong or if this is just how adulthood quietly swallows people.
