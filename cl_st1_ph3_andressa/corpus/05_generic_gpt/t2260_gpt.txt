I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay” around them. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d feel comfortable calling at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. When I’m with others I feel like I’m watching myself from outside, checking if I’m saying the “right” thing, if I’m being too much or too quiet. It’s exhausting, so I end up isolating, then feeling worse because I’m alone again.  

What scares me most is how replaceable I feel. If I disappeared from my social circles, I think people would notice for a week, maybe two, then just… adjust. I keep wondering if loneliness eventually becomes permanent, like a personality trait you can’t shake.
