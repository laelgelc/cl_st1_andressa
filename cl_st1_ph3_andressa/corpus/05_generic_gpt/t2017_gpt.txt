I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even at family gatherings, but I still feel weirdly invisible. I can make small talk, crack jokes, but it never goes deeper. It’s like there’s a glass wall between me and everyone else.

What gets me the most is the silence when the day ends. No one really checks in, no one I can message without feeling like I’m bothering them. I scroll through my phone pretending I’m “choosing” not to reach out, when really I don’t know who I’d even text.

I’m tired of being “fine” on the outside and hollow on the inside. I don’t know how people build real connections anymore.
