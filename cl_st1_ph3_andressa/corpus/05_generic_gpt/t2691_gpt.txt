I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay.” I go to work, make small talk, crack jokes, and everyone assumes I’m fine. Then I go home and it’s like the world just…shuts off. No messages, no calls, nothing planned. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen in real time while mine feels like a paused screen.

What scares me is how normal it’s starting to feel. I’m getting used to eating alone, walking alone, having no one to tell things to. When something happens—good or bad—my first thought is, “Who would even care to hear this?” And I never really have an answer.
