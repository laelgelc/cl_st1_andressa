I’m starting to realize I’m not “busy and independent,” I’m just… alone all the time.

I go days without saying more than a few sentences out loud. I have coworkers, but once work ends, it’s just silence. I scroll, watch shows, play games, but it all feels like background noise covering up the fact that no one is actually here.

What bothers me most is how forgettable I feel. People seem to like me well enough, but no one thinks of me first. I’m always the one reaching out, suggesting plans, checking in. When I stop, everything just stops.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just wanted to put this somewhere that isn’t my own head.
