I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, and it’s messing with my head.

On paper, I’m “fine” – job, roof over my head, I talk to coworkers, I can make small talk at the store. But it all feels like I’m playing a character who vaguely resembles me. When I get home, it’s like I vanish. No messages, no calls, nothing scheduled. Just the quiet hum of my apartment and the feeling that the world is happening somewhere else, without me.

I scroll through old chats with people I used to be close to and can’t even pinpoint when the distance started. It’s like everything just slowly faded instead of breaking, which somehow hurts more because there’s nothing specific to fix.

I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off-putting about me that I can’t see. I try to initiate conversations, but I overthink every word and then convince myself I’m annoying them. So I back off. Then I complain that no one reaches out.

It feels like I’m stuck in this loop and I don’t really see a way out of it right now.
