I’ve started to realize I feel loneliest when I’m technically “not alone.” I can be in a room full of people, laughing at the right moments, and still feel like I’m behind glass. I’m present, but not really there.  

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I miss having someone who actually knows my history, my weird habits, my fears.  

I keep wondering if it’s something about me that makes connections stall out at the surface like this.
