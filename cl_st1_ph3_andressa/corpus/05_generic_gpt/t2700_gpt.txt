I’m starting to wonder if I’ve somehow aged out of being “reachable.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about weather and TV shows, but nobody who actually knows me. I go home to an empty place, scroll through feeds full of other people’s lives, and feel like I’m watching the world through glass.

I try to initiate plans, but things fizzle. Group chats die when I talk. When I finally get invited somewhere, I feel like an extra, not part of the cast. I keep thinking it’s my fault—too quiet, too awkward, too… something.

What scares me most isn’t being alone right now. It’s the sinking feeling that this might just be how my life is going to be.
