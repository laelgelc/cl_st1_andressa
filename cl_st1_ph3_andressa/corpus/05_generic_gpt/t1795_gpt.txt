Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m physically around people. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role instead of actually connecting. I watch other people joke, touch, share inside references, and I just hover at the edge, invisible. I want to reach out, but I overthink every message, every pause, every silence. It’s exhausting. I’m starting to wonder if I’ve forgotten how to be close to anyone, or if I ever really knew how in the first place.
