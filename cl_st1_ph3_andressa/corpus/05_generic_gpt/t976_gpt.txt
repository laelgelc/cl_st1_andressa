I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of being a real person. When conversations end, there’s this weird emptiness, like nothing actually happened.

I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging just to say “hey, I’m struggling.” Either I don’t want to bother them, or I’m afraid they’ll see me differently. So I keep everything surface-level and then wonder why I feel so disconnected.

What messes with me most is that from the outside, my life probably looks fine. But I go entire weekends without speaking out loud to anyone, and it’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like how my life just is.
