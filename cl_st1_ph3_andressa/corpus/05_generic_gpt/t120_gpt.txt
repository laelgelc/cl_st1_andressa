I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like acting. I can play the role of “friendly person,” but the second I go home, it’s just silence and this heavy feeling that no one really knows me.

I keep trying to “fix” it by joining things, going to events, talking more, but it all stays on the surface. I watch other people slip into easy conversations, inside jokes, group chats, and I’m just hovering at the edge, waiting for a moment that never really comes.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. The empty weekends, the unread messages, the sense that if I disappeared from my usual places, it might take a while before anyone noticed.
