I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but nobody I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. My phone is full of old conversations that just… stopped. I keep rereading them, wondering what I did wrong, or if it was always this fragile.

When I’m around others I feel like I’m acting, saying the “right” things, laughing at the “right” times. Then I go home and the silence feels like it’s pressing on my chest. I tell myself I’m used to it, that I prefer being alone, but that’s a lie. I just don’t know how to be anything else anymore.
