I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I’m “around” others all the time—at work, online, even in group chats—but I still feel like I’m pressed up against the glass of everyone else’s life, watching instead of participating.

I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I’m scared that if I disappeared, people would just assume I was busy, not missing. I try to make conversation, but it feels forced, like I’m playing a role I never learned properly.

What bothers me most is how loneliness makes me weird in subtle ways. I overthink every message I send, then delete it. I decline invitations because I’m out of practice socially, then complain to myself that no one includes me. It’s like I’m sabotaging my own chances and I can’t seem to stop.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for here. Maybe I just needed to say it somewhere.
