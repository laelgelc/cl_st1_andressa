I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately.

Invisible because no one really checks in. I can go days without getting a message that isn’t a notification or some automated email. Exposed because I’m constantly online, scrolling, watching other people’s lives like I’m stuck behind glass. It feels like everyone else is moving in a current and I’m just standing in still water.

I have acquaintances, people I can joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. Sometimes I try to open up a little and it just… lands flat. The conversation changes, or they say “same” and move on. It makes me feel stupid for trying.

What messes with my head is that from the outside I probably look fine. I go to work, I answer emails, I make small talk. I’ve gotten good at sounding “okay” in short bursts. Then I go home, close the door, and it’s just me and the quiet and this heavy feeling that I don’t really matter to anyone in a concrete way.

I’m not even sure what I’m hoping for posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
