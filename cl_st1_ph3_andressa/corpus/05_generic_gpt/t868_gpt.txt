I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I go to work, make small talk, send memes in group chats, and yet it all feels like I’m playing a character who vaguely resembles me.

When I try to open up, I either downplay everything with jokes or feel like I’m oversharing and making people uncomfortable. So I end up keeping it surface-level, then later feel resentful that no one really knows me. It’s a loop I can’t seem to break.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through social media and see people hanging out, traveling, getting married, having kids. I’m happy for them, but it also makes me feel like I somehow missed a train everyone else caught years ago.

I’m not completely isolated, but I don’t feel emotionally “held” by anyone. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing. I’m not sure there’s a neat way out of this.
