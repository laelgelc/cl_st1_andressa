I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I go through the motions—work, small talk, errands—but it all feels like I’m acting out a life instead of living one. I have people around me, technically, but I don’t feel known by anyone. Conversations skim the surface: weather, TV, complaints about work. Nothing that makes me feel like I actually exist in someone’s mind when I’m not physically there.

I keep thinking it’s my fault—that I’m too quiet, too awkward, or just fundamentally uninteresting. I try to open up sometimes, but it lands with a thud and I retreat again. It’s like I’m standing behind glass: I can see everyone, hear them laugh, but there’s this barrier I can’t figure out how to break.
