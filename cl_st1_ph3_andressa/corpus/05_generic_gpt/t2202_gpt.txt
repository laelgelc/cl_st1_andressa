I’ve reached this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about shows or work, but nobody I feel truly close to. I’m always the one listening, nodding, making jokes, but I never really say what’s actually going on with me.  

When I go home, it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives through a screen. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would actually notice beyond a passing ‘that’s sad’?”  

I don’t even know how to fix it anymore. I just feel like I’m slowly fading into the background of my own life.
