I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing. My phone is mostly silent unless I’m the one reaching out first, and even then the conversations feel shallow, like we’re all just going through the motions. I’m around people at work/school, but it’s like I’m behind glass. I laugh at the right times, say the right things, but I don’t feel connected to anyone.

What bothers me most is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped showing up, I honestly don’t know who would actually miss me beyond a passing “oh, that’s weird.” I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong or if I’m just not the kind of person people form real bonds with.
