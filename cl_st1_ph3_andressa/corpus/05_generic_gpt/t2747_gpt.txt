I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go to work, come home, scroll on my phone, and it’s like I’m just filling time between sleeps. I have acquaintances, but no one I’d call if something actually went wrong. I keep conversations light because I’m scared if I open up, it’ll make things weird and they’ll drift away. At the same time, not opening up makes everything feel shallow and fake. I’m stuck in this loop where I want connection but don’t really believe anyone would choose me if they saw the full picture.
