I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family—but everything feels surface-level, like we’re all reading from a script. I can make small talk, crack jokes, even seem “normal,” but no one actually knows what my days feel like or what I’m afraid of. I don’t even know how to bridge that gap anymore. Whenever I try to open up, it feels forced or awkward, so I retreat. Then I’m left wondering if it’s me, or if real connection is just rarer than I thought.
