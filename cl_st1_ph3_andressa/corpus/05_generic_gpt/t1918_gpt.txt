I’m starting to realize I’m not “busy and independent,” I’m just alone all the time.

My phone barely lights up unless it’s spam or a notification from an app. I can go entire weekends without speaking out loud, except to say “thank you” to a cashier. I try to initiate plans, but people are always “so busy” or they cancel last minute. After a while, I stopped asking.

What messes with my head is that I seem “fine” on the outside. I can joke with coworkers, I’m polite, I function. Then I go home, close the door, and it’s just this heavy quiet that feels like it’s pressing on my chest.
