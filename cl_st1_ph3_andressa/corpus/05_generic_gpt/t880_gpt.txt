I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. Like I’m somehow both not enough to matter and too much work for anyone to actually want to deal with.

I have people around me technically—coworkers, acquaintances, some family—but every interaction feels like small talk on top of small talk. I can’t remember the last time I said what I really thought or felt without editing it down first so I didn’t sound “needy” or “dramatic.”

When I’m alone, I scroll and lurk and watch other people’s lives play out, and it feels like there’s this glass wall between me and everyone else. I can see what connection is supposed to look like, but I can’t seem to reach it without feeling fake.

What scares me most is how normal this has started to feel. Like I’m slowly adjusting to the idea that no one will ever really know me, and I’m not sure what that does to a person over time.
