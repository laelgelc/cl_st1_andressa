I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can be surrounded by coworkers, classmates, even family, and still feel like I’m behind glass. I say the right small-talk things, laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like I’m playing a character whose lines I memorized years ago.

I scroll through chats and see conversations that just… stopped. Not with a fight, just with me running out of things to say and letting it die. Then I complain in my head that no one reaches out, but when they do, I feel this weird pressure and sometimes ignore it until it’s awkward.

Nights are the worst. Everything is quiet and I suddenly notice how long it’s been since someone asked how I really am and actually wanted the full answer. I don’t even know what I’d say if they did. It’s like I’ve forgotten how to be known.
