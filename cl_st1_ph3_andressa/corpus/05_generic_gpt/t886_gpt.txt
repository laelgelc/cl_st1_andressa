Lately it feels like my life is happening behind glass. I go to work, I answer messages, I smile when I’m supposed to, but I don’t feel actually *with* anyone. I’m surrounded by people and still feel like I’m on mute.

I’ve tried joining group chats, going to meetups, even forcing myself to start conversations, but everything stays surface-level. It’s like there’s a barrier where small talk ends and real connection should start, and I can’t seem to cross it. I watch other people slip into friendships so easily and I’m just… there, orbiting around them.

What makes it worse is knowing I’m not completely unlikable. People say I’m “nice,” “easy to talk to,” but no one really chooses me. No one checks in first. When my phone is silent, it feels like proof that I’m optional in everyone’s life but my own.
