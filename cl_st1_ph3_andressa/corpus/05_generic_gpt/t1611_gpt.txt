I’ve been feeling this weird mixture of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my own family group chats, but it all feels like background noise. I can talk, joke, participate, but it never feels like anyone really sees me. It’s like I’m acting out a version of myself that people can tolerate, while the actual me just sits in the back of my mind, collecting dust.

What scares me is how easy it’s become to withdraw. I cancel plans, leave messages on read, then feel hurt that no one pushes harder to include me. I know that’s not fair, but it’s how it feels. I scroll through social media and see everyone forming bonds, inside jokes, shared histories. Meanwhile, my days blur together, and when something good or bad happens, I hesitate because I’m not even sure who I’d tell first.
