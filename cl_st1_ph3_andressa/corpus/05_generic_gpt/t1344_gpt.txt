I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. Friends, coworkers, family—they’re there, yet I don’t feel truly known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward. It’s weird being “liked” but not really seen. Sometimes I wonder if I’m the one keeping the distance or if no one actually wants to cross it.
