Lately I feel like I’m disappearing in slow motion. I go through the motions at work, make small talk, answer emails, but it all feels like I’m acting a part someone else wrote. When I get home, it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people living lives that seem full of noise and connection, and I can’t remember the last time someone genuinely checked in on me.

I’m not completely isolated; I have “people,” technically. But everything feels surface-level, like we’re all afraid to go deeper. I miss feeling known. I’m starting to wonder if I ever actually did.
