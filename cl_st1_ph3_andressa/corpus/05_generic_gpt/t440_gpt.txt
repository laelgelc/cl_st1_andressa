I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like rust. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances—but no one really close. Conversations stay on the surface, like we’re all afraid to touch anything real. I miss being known, having someone who remembers small details about me without being reminded. I keep wondering if I’m the problem, or if everyone is just too tired to care anymore.
