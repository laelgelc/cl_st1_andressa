I don’t really know where to put this, so I’m dumping it here.

I’m at that weird point where my life looks “fine” from the outside but feels completely empty from the inside. I go to work, I’m polite, I respond to messages, I even crack jokes sometimes. People say I’m “easy to talk to,” but no one actually talks to me beyond surface-level stuff. It’s like I’m a background character in my own life.

I have people I could text, but I don’t feel like I actually belong to anyone. There’s no person who’d notice if I disappeared for a few days, except maybe out of convenience or habit. When my phone lights up, it’s almost never someone reaching out just to talk; it’s logistics, memes, or group chats where I’m basically invisible.

What makes it worse is that I can function. I go to work. I do my tasks. I laugh at the right times. So no one assumes anything is wrong, and I don’t know how to bring it up without sounding dramatic or needy. “Hey, I feel like a ghost in my own life” doesn’t exactly slide into casual conversation.

Sometimes I sit in my room at night and feel this heavy, physical ache in my chest. Not from anything specific, just from the realization that there’s no one who really knows me. Not the curated version, not the “I’m fine” version. Just me. And I’m starting to wonder if that’s just how it’s going to be.
