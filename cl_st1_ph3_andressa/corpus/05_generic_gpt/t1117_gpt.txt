I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m fading out of everyone’s life in slow motion.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family—but I feel like a background character in all of their stories. I’m always the one who listens, remembers birthdays, checks in when someone is quiet. But when I go silent, it takes a long time for anyone to notice, if they notice at all.

Most of my “social life” is group chats where my messages die with no replies, or people only respond when they need something. I’ll open my phone, scroll through conversations, and realize that if I stopped texting, my phone would probably just… stop.

I’ve also started to feel weirdly out of practice at being around people. When I do get invited somewhere, I overthink every word, every pause, and go home replaying it all like a failure reel.

I’m not sure what I’m looking for here. I just wanted to put this somewhere outside my own head for once.
