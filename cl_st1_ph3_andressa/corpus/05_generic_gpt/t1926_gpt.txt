Lately I feel like I’m drifting through my own life as a background character. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my family group chat, but it all feels like noise I’m not really part of. I can go days without anyone asking how I actually am. When I try to open up, the conversation quickly shifts back to small talk. I’m starting to wonder if I’m just fundamentally hard to connect with, or if I somehow missed the point where everyone else learned how.
