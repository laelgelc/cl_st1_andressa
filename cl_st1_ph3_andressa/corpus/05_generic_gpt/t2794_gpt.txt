I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, be “fine,” but it never goes further than that. Friends drifted, relationships ended, and I never replaced them. My phone is mostly silent unless it’s a notification from an app. I keep thinking I’m the common denominator. It’s like I’m watching my own life from the outside, but there’s no one to turn to and say, “Do you see this too?”
