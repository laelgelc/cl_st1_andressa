I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

I have people around me—coworkers, a couple of acquaintances I see every now and then—but I don’t feel *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else actually live.

I miss having someone I can text without overthinking every word. Someone who’d notice if I went quiet for a few days. I keep telling myself I should “put myself out there,” but I’m tired of being the one who initiates everything and still ends up going home alone.

The worst part is how it’s changing me. I’m becoming quieter, more withdrawn, and then I hate myself for it. It feels like the longer this goes on, the harder it is to imagine it ever being different.
