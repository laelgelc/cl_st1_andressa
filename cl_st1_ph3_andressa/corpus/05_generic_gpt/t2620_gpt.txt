I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, I can be “polite,” but it all feels like I’m playing a character and everyone else got the real script.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I message sometimes, but no one I’d call if something genuinely bad happened. I scroll through my contacts and feel this weird mix of embarrassment and emptiness, like I’d be bothering them or they wouldn’t care.

What makes it worse is how invisible it feels. From the outside, my life probably looks fine. Inside, it’s like I’m always a step removed, watching everyone else actually live while I’m just filling time.
