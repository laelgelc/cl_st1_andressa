Lately I’ve been realizing how alone I feel even when I’m technically “around people.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me or checks in unprompted. I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel comfortable messaging without overthinking it. I worry I’m becoming harder to connect with the longer this goes on. I miss having someone who actually wants to hear the boring details of my day and doesn’t just tolerate them.
