I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I’m surrounded by people at work and online, but no one really knows me beyond the surface-level stuff. I’m always the one listening, nodding, saying “I’m fine” when I’m not. I miss having someone who checks in unprompted, who remembers the small things I say. It’s weird how I can go days without a real conversation and no one notices. I’m starting to wonder if I’m just easy to forget.
