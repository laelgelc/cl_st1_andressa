I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have coworkers, family, people I message sometimes, but it all feels shallow, like I’m an extra in everyone else’s life. I can’t remember the last time someone texted first just to ask how I’m doing, without needing something. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or being dramatic, so I shut down again. It’s exhausting pretending I’m fine when I mostly just feel forgotten.
