It’s strange how I can be surrounded by people and still feel like a ghost. I have acquaintances, coworkers, the usual “how’s it going?” interactions, but it all feels like acting. I laugh at the right moments, ask the right questions, but inside I’m just… not there.

I keep thinking it’s my fault. I cancel plans, then feel hurt that no one insists. I don’t reach out, then resent that my phone stays silent. I want deeper connections, but the idea of being fully seen terrifies me, so I stay in this shallow, safe distance.

Nights are the worst. That moment when you put your phone down and realize no one is expecting you, no one is checking in. It makes me wonder if I’ve somehow missed the window where people form their “real” friendships and now I’m just orbiting other people’s lives, close enough to see them, too far to belong.
