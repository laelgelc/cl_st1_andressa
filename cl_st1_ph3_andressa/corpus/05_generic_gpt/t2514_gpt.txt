I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. When I scroll through my contacts, there’s no one I feel comfortable bothering.

I’ve tried being more social—going to events, saying yes to invitations—but it always feels like I’m standing just outside the circle. I can talk about surface stuff, but when the conversation gets deeper, I freeze or deflect. Later I replay everything I said and convince myself I sounded weird or boring.

What really messes with me is that people describe me as “nice” or “easy to talk to,” but it never seems to go beyond that. No one chooses me as their close friend. I’m always the extra, the backup plan, the person they’re “glad to see” but never think of first.

It’s like I’m visible enough to be polite to, but invisible when it actually counts.
