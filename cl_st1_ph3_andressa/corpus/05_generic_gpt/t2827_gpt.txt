I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m on mute. Friends, coworkers, family—I’m there, I say things, I laugh at the right moments, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being present. I don’t think anyone really knows me beyond the surface-level version I’ve built to be “easy.”  

I want to tell someone I’m lonely, but it feels embarrassing, like admitting a failure at something everyone else seems to do without thinking.
