I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d actually call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. When I’m with others, I feel like I’m playing a role—smiling, making jokes, nodding along—while my real thoughts stay locked up because I’m scared they’d be “too much.”  

Nights are the worst. The silence makes everything louder in my head, and I start wondering if anyone would really notice if I just quietly drifted out of their lives.
