I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore, and it’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like my default state.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. Conversations stay at the surface: work, memes, “how was your weekend.” I can be funny and polite, but the second it gets even slightly personal, I freeze up or change the subject. Then I go home and wonder why no one is close to me.

I watch other people form friendships so easily—inside jokes, group photos, casual hangouts—and it feels like I’m standing behind glass, watching a world I somehow missed the instructions for.

I keep thinking, “If I were more interesting / attractive / confident, this would be different.” But I’m tired of analyzing myself like a broken machine. I just wish I knew how to let someone in without feeling like I’m bothering them or faking everything.
