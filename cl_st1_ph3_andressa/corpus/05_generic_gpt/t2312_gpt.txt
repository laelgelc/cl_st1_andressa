I’m starting to realize I’m not just “busy” or “introverted,” I’m actually lonely. The kind of lonely where you scroll through your contacts and realize there’s no one you feel comfortable texting first. I go to work, make small talk, laugh at the right times, then come home and it’s like I vanish. No one checks in. Days pass where my phone doesn’t light up unless it’s a notification from an app.

I’ve had friends before, but we drifted, and I never replaced them. Now I’m at this weird age where everyone seems to already have their people, their group chats, their plans. I feel like an extra in everyone else’s life, like I’m always on the outside of the glass, watching other people just… belong.
