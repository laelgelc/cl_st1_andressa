I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, and it scares me more than I want to admit.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like acting. I can be “on” for a while, but as soon as I’m alone again it’s like I never existed to anyone. No one really checks in, no one really knows what’s going on with me. I don’t even know who I’d call in an emergency, not emotionally anyway.

I keep trying small things—messaging first, suggesting plans, joining group chats—but it always fizzles out. I replay conversations afterward, wondering what I said wrong, why I can’t just be “normal.” It feels like I’m on the outside of a glass wall, watching everyone else live in a world I somehow missed the instructions for.
