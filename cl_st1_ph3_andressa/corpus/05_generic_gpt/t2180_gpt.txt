I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I talk to people every day at work, I reply in group chats, but none of it feels real. It’s all surface-level stuff: “How was your weekend?” “Did you see that show?” No one actually knows what’s going on with me, and I don’t really know how to let them. When I get home, the silence feels heavy, like it’s pressing on my chest. I keep wondering if anyone would actually notice if I slowly drifted out of their lives.
