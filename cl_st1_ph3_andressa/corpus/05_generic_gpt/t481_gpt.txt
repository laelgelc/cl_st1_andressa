I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I only know how to perform being “okay” around them.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but I don’t feel known by anyone. When conversations end, it’s like I disappear from their minds. I’m always the one who reaches out, and when I stop, everything just goes quiet.

I’m in my 30s and it feels like everyone else has moved on to the next stage of life—partners, kids, friend groups that have solidified over years. I’m still here, orbiting around other people’s lives without really belonging anywhere.

What scares me most is how used to this I’m getting. The silence after my phone lights up and then goes dark again. The empty weekends. The feeling that if I just stopped trying altogether, not much would actually change.
