I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and realize most names are people I’d feel awkward messaging out of the blue. Weekends stretch out, and even when I keep busy, there’s this heaviness. It’s like I’m always “around people,” but never actually with anyone.
