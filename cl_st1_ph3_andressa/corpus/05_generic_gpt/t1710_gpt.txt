I don’t really know how to connect with people anymore. I’m surrounded by coworkers, neighbors, random small talk, but it all feels like noise around a vacuum. I go home, cook for one, scroll through feeds where everyone seems to have “their people,” and I can’t even think of someone I could text without it feeling awkward or forced. I’ve lost touch with old friends, and new ones never move past acquaintance level. I’m starting to feel less like a person and more like a background extra in everyone else’s life.
