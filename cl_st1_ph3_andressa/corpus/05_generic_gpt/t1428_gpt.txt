I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to exist around other people. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I watch other people slip so easily into friendships, group chats, weekend plans, and I’m just… on the outside, observing. It’s weird how you can be surrounded by noise and still feel like your absence wouldn’t change anything for anyone.
