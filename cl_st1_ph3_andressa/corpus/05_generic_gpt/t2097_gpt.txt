I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I go through the motions—work, small talk, scrolling—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else live my life. I have people around me, technically, but I don’t feel known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface, and I’m scared that if I ever went deeper, I’d just make things awkward or get brushed off.

Nights are the worst. That’s when everything gets loud in my head. I replay old messages I never got replies to, plans that fell through, times I tried to reach out and felt like I was bothering people. I keep thinking I’m somehow “too much” and “not enough” at the same time. I don’t really know what to do with that feeling.
