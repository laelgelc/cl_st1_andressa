I’m starting to wonder if I’ve just… aged out of being someone people choose.

I’m not completely isolated. I have coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family I see on holidays. But none of it feels like that “you’re my person, I want to know everything about you” kind of connection. It’s all surface-level, polite, scheduled.

I try to reach out first, suggest plans, ask questions. People respond, but rarely with the same energy. I feel like background noise in other people’s lives. The person you invite if there’s room, not the one you build the plan around.

What scares me most is how normal it’s starting to feel. Like I’m slowly accepting that this might just be it.
