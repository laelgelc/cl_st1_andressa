I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I go through the motions at work, make small talk about weather, shows, whatever, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who’s loosely based on me. When I get home, it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen, but nothing touches me. I don’t even know who I’d message if something good or bad happened.

It’s not like I’m completely isolated. I have “people”: coworkers, acquaintances, old classmates I still follow. But I don’t feel known by anyone. Conversations never get past surface level. When they do, I panic internally and steer them back to safe topics because I’m scared if I’m honest about how lonely I am, I’ll make it awkward and they’ll pull away.

What bothers me most is how out of sync I feel with my age. Everyone seems to be moving into serious relationships, marriages, kids, solid friend groups. I’m stuck in this weird limbo where I’m technically “fine” but emotionally just… not part of anything. Even when I’m invited somewhere, I feel like a guest star in other people’s stories, not someone who actually belongs.

I’ve started to notice that I talk less and less in group settings because I assume what I say doesn’t really matter. Then I leave and feel invisible and stupid for staying quiet. It’s like I’m slowly fading into the background of my own life.

I don’t really know what I want from posting this. I think I just needed to put it somewhere that isn’t my own head.
