I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives, like I’m just background noise in every room I’m in.

I have “people” around me—coworkers, some old friends I still text occasionally, family chats—but it all feels thin and distant. Conversations never go past small talk, and when they do, I always feel like I’ve overshared or made things awkward. Then I replay it for days and pull back even more.

What really scares me is how used to this I’m becoming. I go entire weekends without speaking out loud except to a cashier. I scroll, I watch shows, I lie in bed, and the silence just kind of settles into my bones. When something good or bad happens, my first thought is, “Who would I even tell?” and I come up blank.

It’s like I’m here, but not really in anyone’s life in a real way.
