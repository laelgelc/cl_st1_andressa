I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay” around them. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one who really knows me. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel close enough to message without it feeling weird.

Weekends are the worst. I watch other people’s stories of group trips, dinners, random hangouts, and I’m just in my room, pretending I chose this. I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but when I do try, conversations stay shallow and fade. It feels like I’m always almost part of things, but never actually in them.
