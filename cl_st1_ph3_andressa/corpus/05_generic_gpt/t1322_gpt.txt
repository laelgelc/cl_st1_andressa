I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly dissolving into the background of my own life.

On paper, I’m not “alone.” I have coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family I see on holidays. But it all feels strangely hollow, like I’m interacting through glass. I go through the motions—small talk at work, “how are you?” messages, group chats full of memes—but I can’t shake the feeling that if I stopped responding, most people wouldn’t really notice for long.

What’s been bothering me the most is how hard it is to show my real self to anyone. I always feel like I’m performing a quieter, more acceptable version of me. I censor the things I actually want to say because I’m scared it’ll be “too much” or “weird” or just not worth anyone’s time. Then I end up feeling invisible and misunderstood, but it’s partly my own doing, which makes it worse.

I’ve tried to be more social. I’ve joined a couple of clubs, gone to meetups, forced myself to say yes to invitations. I can get through those events fine, make surface-level conversation, even make people laugh sometimes. But I come home afterwards feeling even more empty, like I’ve just confirmed that I can be “pleasant” but still not actually connect.

I miss the feeling of being known. Not just “oh, you like that TV show” known, but the kind of knowing where someone can tell from your voice that something’s off, where you don’t have to explain your entire backstory for them to understand why certain things hit you harder. I don’t have anyone like that right now. I don’t think anyone is keeping track of me in that way.

There’s also this weird shame about being lonely as an adult. It feels like something I should’ve “figured out” by now, like it’s a personal failing. When people talk about their “support system,” I just stay quiet because I don’t really have one, and admitting that out loud feels like confessing I’m defective.

Most days I just go to work, come home, scroll endlessly, and then lie in bed with this heavy feeling in my chest that doesn’t really go away. I don’t know if I’m looking for advice or just a place to say this out loud where it won’t sound dramatic. I just feel really, really alone, even when I’m surrounded by people.
