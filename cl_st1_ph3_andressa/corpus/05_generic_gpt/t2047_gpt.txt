I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people at work, but it’s all small talk and surface-level jokes. Nobody knows anything real about me, and I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be known.

I try to reach out sometimes, but I always second-guess myself: “Am I being annoying? Too needy? Too weird?” So I end up deleting messages before I send them. Then I scroll through social media and see everyone else laughing in group photos, and it just makes the silence in my own life feel louder.

Nights are the worst. I lie in bed replaying old conversations, wondering when exactly I drifted so far away from everyone, and why it feels so hard to come back.
