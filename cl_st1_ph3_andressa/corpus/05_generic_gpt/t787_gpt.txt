I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m sealed behind glass.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with. But it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me beyond the surface stuff, and I’m not sure anyone wants to. Every time I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I pull back and keep things light and shallow.

The weird part is that I’m not completely socially inept. I can hold conversations, I can be “funny,” but it’s like I’m playing a character. When I go home, I just crash into this heavy silence and it hits me how replaceable I am in everyone’s life.

I keep thinking about how long it’s been since someone asked me how I really am and actually wanted the real answer. I don’t even know what I’d say anymore.
