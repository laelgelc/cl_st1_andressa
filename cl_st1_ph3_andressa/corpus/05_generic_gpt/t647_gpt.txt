I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing from my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, a few acquaintances—but no one who really knows me. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to go deeper, it feels awkward, like I’m oversharing. Nights are the worst; it’s just me, my thoughts, and this heaviness. I keep wondering if I’m somehow fundamentally unlikeable and that’s why nothing sticks.
