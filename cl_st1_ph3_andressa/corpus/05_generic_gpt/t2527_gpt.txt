I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I go to work, make small talk, send the usual “lol” and emojis in group chats, but it all feels like I’m just playing a role someone else wrote for me.

I miss having conversations that go past surface-level stuff. I miss feeling known. Even when I try to open up a little, it feels like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I pull back and pretend I’m fine again.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I watch random videos, I stare at the ceiling. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would actually notice in a real way?” And I genuinely don’t know the answer.
