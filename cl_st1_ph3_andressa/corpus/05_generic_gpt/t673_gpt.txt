I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own.

On paper, I’m “fine.” I have a job, I answer messages, I show up when I’m supposed to. But it all feels like I’m acting out a version of myself that other people remember, not who I actually am now. I go through whole days where no one says my name out loud. I’m there, but I could just as easily not be.

I do have people around me—coworkers, a couple of old friends, family chats—but everything stays on the surface. Jokes, memes, “how’s work?” stuff. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I’m really doing and actually wanted an honest answer. And I’m not innocent in that either; I’ve gotten so used to saying “I’m good” that I’m not sure I’d know how to explain what’s going on even if someone did ask.

The worst part is feeling lonely even when I’m physically with others. I’ll be sitting in a group and feel this wall between me and everyone else, like there’s a glass sheet I can’t break through. I watch everyone connect and laugh and I’m just… present, but not part of it.

I don’t know what I’m looking for writing this. Maybe just proof that I still exist somewhere outside my own head.
