I’ve been feeling like a ghost lately. I go through my day doing all the normal things—work, errands, small talk—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else live my life. I have acquaintances, people who’d say hi if they saw me, but no one I’d feel comfortable calling at 2 a.m. when everything feels too heavy. 

I’m tired of pretending I’m fine when I’m not. I scroll through social media and see everyone in groups, with partners, with families, and it makes my own silence feel louder. I don’t even know how to bridge the gap anymore. I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be genuinely known by someone.
