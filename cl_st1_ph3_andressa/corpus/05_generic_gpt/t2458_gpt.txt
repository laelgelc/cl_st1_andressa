I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

On paper I’m “fine”: I have coworkers, a few acquaintances, people I can text about logistics. But no one really knows me. Most of my interactions feel like small talk on top of small talk, like I’m performing a version of myself that people find easier to deal with.

What gets to me most is the silence after I share something real. I’ll open up a little, and the conversation just… drifts back to safe topics, or they change the subject. It makes me feel stupid for trying, like I misread the room again.

I see other people casually talking about “my best friend” or “we did this together” and I can’t even imagine what that kind of steady closeness feels like anymore. I miss being someone’s first choice, or even second.

Most nights I just scroll, lurk, and hope reading other people’s posts makes this feel less like a personal failure, and more like something that just is.
