I’m starting to wonder if I’m just fundamentally “un-clickable” with other people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I don’t really feel safe enough to let them. I watch other people slip into friendships so effortlessly—inside jokes, group chats, weekend plans—and I’m just hovering at the edges, invited sometimes, but never really *missed* if I’m not there.

I try to initiate conversations, suggest meetups, but it always feels forced, like I’m auditioning for a role I never get. Then I come home, scroll through my phone, and realize there’s no one I can message without feeling like I’m bothering them.
