I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. Every time I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing, so I pull back and make a joke or change the subject. Then I go home and replay every conversation in my head, convinced I sounded weird or needy.

Weekends are the worst. I scroll through my phone, trying to think of someone I could message, and I either talk myself out of it or get no response. It’s like I’m standing behind glass, watching everyone else live their lives together while I’m stuck narrating mine to myself.
