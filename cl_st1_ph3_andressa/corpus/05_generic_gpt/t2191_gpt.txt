Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, talk to people, make jokes, and somehow still come home feeling like I don’t actually exist to anyone once I walk out the door. 

My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. I try to reach out, but conversations die quickly and I end up rereading old messages just to feel like I used to matter more. 

I’m not completely isolated, but everything feels surface-level, like I’m an extra in everyone else’s story. I miss having someone who really knows me, who notices when I’m quieter than usual. 

It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people all day and still feel like you could disappear and nothing in the world would really change.
