I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can chat, joke around, even seem social, but it all feels like acting. When the conversation ends, nothing sticks. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure I know myself outside of this constant feeling of being on the outside looking in. I scroll through chats that are all surface-level, watch other people make plans I’m not part of, and tell myself I’m fine. But the silence after the small talk feels heavier every day.
