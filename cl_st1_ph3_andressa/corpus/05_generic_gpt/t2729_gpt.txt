I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s lives, including my own.

I have people around me – coworkers, acquaintances, even family – but I feel completely disconnected. Conversations are shallow, scripted. I can laugh and make small talk, but the second I’m alone, it’s like the bottom drops out and I realize none of it actually meant anything. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to think maybe that’s my fault, but I don’t even know where to start fixing it.

I watch other people form friendships so effortlessly. They text each other, make plans, share stupid memes. I stare at my phone and realize the only notifications I get are from apps, not people. When I do reach out, I overthink every word and usually end up deleting the message.

What scares me most is how normal this emptiness is starting to feel, like I’m getting used to being invisible.
