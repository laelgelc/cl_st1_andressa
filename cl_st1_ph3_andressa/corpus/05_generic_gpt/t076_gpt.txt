I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever did.

I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers I joke with. On paper it looks like I’m not alone. But then a whole week goes by and no one messages first. If I stop initiating, everything just…goes quiet. It makes me feel like a background character in my own life.

When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel comfortable sending it to without overthinking. I worry I’m “too much” if I open up, or “too boring” if I don’t. So I end up saying nothing and just lying in bed, phone in hand, waiting for a notification that never comes.

I’m not completely isolated, but I feel emotionally homeless. Like there’s no person who would notice right away if I disappeared for a while. And it’s getting harder to convince myself that this is just a phase that will magically change on its own.
