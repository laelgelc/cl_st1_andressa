I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m acting a part instead of being myself. I can’t remember the last time I felt genuinely understood by someone, or like my presence actually mattered to them.

I keep thinking I’m the common denominator. Maybe I’m too quiet, too awkward, too “much” when I do open up. So I end up withdrawing, then feeling even lonelier. It’s like I’m watching my own life from behind glass, and I don’t know how to step through it.
