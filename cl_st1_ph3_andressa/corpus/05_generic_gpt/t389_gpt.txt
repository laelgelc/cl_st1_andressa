I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but no one I’d actually call if something really went wrong. I watch people casually mention “my best friend” or “we did this together” and it hits me that I don’t have that person. I’m tired of being the extra, the backup plan, the one people like, but never seem to choose first.
