I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely, and it’s creeping into everything. I go entire weekends without speaking out loud to anyone. Messages pile up in my head that I never send because I convince myself I’m bothering people. When I finally do reach out, the replies are slow, dry, or just never come.

I’m surrounded by people at work, but it all feels surface-level. Jokes, small talk, nothing that makes me feel known. I scroll social media and see group photos, inside jokes, plans I’m never part of. I’m tired of pretending I’m fine with my own company when it’s starting to feel less like “peace” and more like being invisible.
