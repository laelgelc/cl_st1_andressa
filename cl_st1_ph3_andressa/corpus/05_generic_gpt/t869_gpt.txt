I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m disappearing from my own life.

On paper I’m “fine.” I have a job, I answer emails, I say “good, you?” when people ask how I am. I scroll through group chats I’m technically part of, but by the time I think of something to say, the conversation has already moved on. Or I just convince myself no one cares what I add anyway.

I’m surrounded by people all the time, but I don’t feel connected to any of them. I have “work friends” who I can joke with about meetings, but I don’t think any of them actually know me. I don’t even know if I remember how to let someone know me. When I try, it feels forced, like I’m acting out a version of myself I think might be acceptable.

Nights are the worst. It’s like everything goes quiet at once and I’m left with this heavy, empty feeling. I’ll open social media and see everyone doing things together, even if I know it’s probably not as perfect as it looks. It still stings. I keep thinking, “Why can’t I just be in someone’s background photo for once?”

I’ve reached this weird point where I’m not even sure what I’m looking for. A close friend? A relationship? Just someone who notices when I’m not there? I don’t know. I just know that I’m tired of pretending I’m okay with being invisible when I’m really not.
