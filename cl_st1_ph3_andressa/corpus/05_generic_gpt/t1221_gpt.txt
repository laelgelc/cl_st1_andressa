Lately I feel like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep, repeat. I talk to people, but it’s all surface-level stuff—weather, TV shows, “how was your weekend?” No one really knows me and I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be known. I’m surrounded by noise but it’s quiet in the parts that matter. I’m not even sure what I’m hoping for anymore.
