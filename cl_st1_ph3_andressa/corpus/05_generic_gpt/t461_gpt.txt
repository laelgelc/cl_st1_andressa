I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. Like I’m standing in the middle of a crowded room with a spotlight on me, but somehow no one actually sees me.

I have people in my life, technically. Coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family I call on holidays. But most of my interactions feel like small talk on repeat. Weather, work, memes. Nothing that touches the stuff that actually keeps me up at night. I try to steer conversations deeper and it usually just… dies. People change the subject, or laugh it off, or go back to their phones.

What really messes with me is how needy I feel whenever I want more. I’ll type out a long message, then delete half of it because it sounds “too much.” I don’t want to scare people away or seem dramatic. So I shrink everything down into something casual and light, and then feel even more disconnected when they respond like it’s no big deal.

I miss feeling known. Not just liked, not just tolerated, but actually understood. I’m starting to forget what that feels like, or if I ever really had it in the first place.
