I’m surrounded by people but feel like I don’t actually exist to anyone. Friends only reach out when they need something, family assumes I’m “fine” because I don’t complain. I try to open up and the conversation drifts back to them, like my words just dissolve mid-air. I scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one double-texting. It’s starting to feel easier to stay silent than to risk being reminded how little space I occupy in anyone’s life.
