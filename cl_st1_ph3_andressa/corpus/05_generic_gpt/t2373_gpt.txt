I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. Invisible in the sense that no one really notices if I’m there or not, but exposed because I’m painfully aware of how alone I am all the time.

I have acquaintances, people I can chat with about work or random stuff, but it never goes deeper. I don’t have that person I can text when something small but important happens, or when I’m just having a rough day. I scroll through my phone and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable reaching out to without overthinking it.

What bothers me most is how routine it’s become. Wake up, work, come home, distract myself with the internet or TV, go to bed. Repeat. It’s like my life is happening in the background and I’m just watching it from a distance, not really part of anything.
