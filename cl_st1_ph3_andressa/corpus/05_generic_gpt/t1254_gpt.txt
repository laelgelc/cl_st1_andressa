I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I’m surrounded by people at work and online, but it all feels like noise instead of connection. I can go days without anyone asking how I really am. When I try to open up, conversations slide back to small talk, like there’s an invisible barrier I can’t cross. I’m tired of pretending I’m fine and cracking jokes so people stay comfortable. It’s weird how you can be “known” by many and still feel completely unseen.
