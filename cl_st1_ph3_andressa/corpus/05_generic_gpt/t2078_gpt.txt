I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but it all feels like we’re just trading lines in a script. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure I’d even know what to say if they tried.

I scroll through chats and see conversations that just… stopped. Not with a fight, just with silence that never ended. I replay old interactions in my head and wonder if I was too quiet, too weird, too boring.

It’s a strange mix of wanting someone to notice I’m not okay, and being terrified they might actually ask. I keep thinking this is just a phase, but it’s starting to feel more like a personality.
