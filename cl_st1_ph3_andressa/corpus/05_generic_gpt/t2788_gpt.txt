I’m starting to realize that I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like it’s changing who I am. I go entire days without speaking out loud to anyone. When my phone buzzes, it’s usually an automated notification, not a person. Group chats I’m in have moved on without me; when I try to jump in, my messages just float there, seen but not really answered.

What messes with my head is that I’m not totally isolated. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people who’d say hi if they saw me. But it all feels surface-level, like I’m permanently stuck in small talk while everyone else has real connections somewhere I can’t reach.
