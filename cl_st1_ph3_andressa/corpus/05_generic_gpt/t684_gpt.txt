I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. Not in a dramatic “no one likes me” way, but in this weird, quiet, numb way where everything feels slightly out of sync.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people who’d say hi if they saw me, but no one I can text just to say, “I’m having a bad day, can you talk?” I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one who feels like an option. It’s like being surrounded by people and still feeling like I’m behind glass.

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like an outsider observing a conversation that’s already been going on for years. I overthink every word I say, replay it later, and cringe. Then I pull back, which just makes the distance worse. It’s this loop I can’t seem to break.

The worst part is nights. The silence feels heavier then. I keep the TV or podcasts on just to have voices in the background, but it’s not the same as someone actually knowing me. I’m not even sure who “me” is in relation to other people anymore.

I don’t know how you’re supposed to go from this to actually belonging somewhere.
