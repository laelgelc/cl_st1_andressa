I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with. But there’s no one I can text when something small but important happens, or when I’m just sitting in my room and the silence gets heavy. I scroll through my contacts and it feels like walking through a museum of people who used to know me, or almost knew me, but not quite.

I keep thinking it’s my fault: I cancel plans, I overthink messages, I assume I’m annoying. Then I resent people for not trying harder, even though they don’t know what’s going on in my head.

It’s a weird mix of wanting to be seen and being terrified of actually letting anyone see me. So I stay on the surface with everyone and then wonder why I feel invisible.
