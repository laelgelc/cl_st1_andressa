I’m starting to realize I’m not just “a bit introverted” but actually really lonely, and it’s unsettling how quietly it crept up on me.

My days are basically work, commute, screens, sleep. I talk to coworkers about tasks, weather, random jokes, but nobody really knows what’s going on with me. I don’t think anyone would notice if I stopped trying to be “pleasant” and just went silent.

I have acquaintances I can text about TV shows or memes, but I don’t have that one person I can message like, “Today sucked, can I just vent?” I scroll through chats and see long gaps, old conversations that just faded out. I don’t even know how to restart them without it feeling weird or desperate.

Nights and weekends are the worst. I keep myself busy with podcasts, games, YouTube, just to drown out that heavy feeling. I tell myself I’m “resting,” but it feels more like hiding.

I’m not even sure what I’m looking for posting this. I just needed to say out loud that I’m lonely, because pretending I’m fine is starting to feel like lying.
