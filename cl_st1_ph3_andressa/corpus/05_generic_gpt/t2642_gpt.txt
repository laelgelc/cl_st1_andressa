Lately I feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life. I go to work, come home, scroll on my phone, repeat. I have people around me, technically, but the conversations never seem to go past surface level. I’m always the one asking questions, listening, laughing at the right times, and then I realize no one has actually asked how I’m doing in weeks.

It’s a strange kind of loneliness where you’re surrounded by noise but still feel like you’re in a soundproof box. I keep wondering if I’m the problem, or if everyone is just too wrapped up in their own worlds to notice I’m fading into the background.
