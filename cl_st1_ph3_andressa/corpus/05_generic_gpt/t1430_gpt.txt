I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, grocery store—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else from far away. I have acquaintances, people I can chat with about TV shows or weather, but no one I can text at 2 a.m. and say, “I’m not okay.”

What gets to me is how invisible this feels. From the outside, I probably look fine: I show up, I smile, I do my job. Inside, there’s this constant ache, like a room that’s too quiet. I keep wondering if this is just how it’s going to be from now on, and that thought scares me more than I want to admit.
