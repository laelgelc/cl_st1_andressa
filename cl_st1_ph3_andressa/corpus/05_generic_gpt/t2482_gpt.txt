Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m living in parallel to everyone else instead of with them. I go to work, I make small talk, I smile in the right places, but it all feels like acting. When the day ends and I come home, it’s just silence. Not peaceful silence, more like an echo chamber where every thought bounces back louder.

I have people I “know,” but I don’t feel known. Conversations stay on the surface. I hesitate to share anything real because I’m scared of being too much, or not enough, or just…awkward. So I hold back, and then I feel even more disconnected.

What bothers me most is how invisible it feels. You can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re pressed up against the glass, watching everyone else live their lives while you’re stuck on the outside, unsure how to step in.
