I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with online, but no one I’d actually call when something really hurts. I scroll through chats and realize every conversation would die if I stopped replying. I keep trying to be “easygoing” and not a burden, and somehow that’s turned into being forgettable. I don’t know how to cross that line from “person people know” to “person someone would actually miss.”
