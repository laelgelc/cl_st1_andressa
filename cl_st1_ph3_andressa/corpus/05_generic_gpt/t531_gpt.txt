I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people every day—work, errands, random small talk—but none of it feels like it actually touches me. It’s like I’m acting out a version of myself that’s acceptable and easy to digest, while the real version just sits in the back of my mind, watching.

I don’t really know how to fix it. I try to reach out sometimes, but my messages feel forced and awkward, like I’m intruding. Then I overthink and stop trying. Nights are the worst—everything is quiet and there’s nothing to distract me from the feeling that if I disappeared, my absence would just be a temporary inconvenience, not a real loss for anyone.
