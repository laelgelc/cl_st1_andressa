I don’t know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like a ghost in my own life. I see people at work, I make small talk, I even laugh sometimes, but it all feels like acting. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if there’s even a “real me” left under all the pretending.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives pass by, but I never feel like I’m part of anything. I crave connection so badly it’s almost physical, but when I get the chance, I freeze or push people away. Then I sit alone and replay everything, asking myself what’s wrong with me.
