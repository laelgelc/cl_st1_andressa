I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life without anyone noticing. Friends are “busy,” messages get left on read, and I’ve stopped trying to suggest plans because the silence afterward hurts more than the loneliness I already feel. I go through my day on autopilot—work, errands, scrolling online—but it all feels like filler between long stretches of being alone with my thoughts. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing. I just know “fine” isn’t true, but it’s easier than explaining this emptiness.
