I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I don’t exist to any of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d actually call if something really bad happened. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or being dramatic, so I pull back and keep it surface-level.  

Most nights I just scroll or watch something until I’m tired enough to sleep, because the quiet feels heavier than it should. I keep thinking maybe I missed some crucial stage where people learn to form real bonds. I’m old enough that it feels embarrassing to admit I don’t really have close friends. It’s like living next to life instead of in it.
