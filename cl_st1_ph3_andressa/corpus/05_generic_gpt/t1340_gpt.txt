I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know what “having friends” feels like as an adult.

I have people I chat with at work, we joke around, share memes, but it all evaporates at 5 p.m. No one texts. No one asks to hang out. Weekends are just… quiet. I scroll, I watch shows, I pretend I’m “recharging,” but it’s more like I’m just waiting for something to happen.

I’ve tried joining clubs, going to meetups, even messaging old classmates. Conversations never seem to move past small talk. I feel like I’m always on the outside of some invisible wall where everyone else just “clicks” and I don’t.

The worst part is how it messes with my sense of self. I start wondering if maybe I’m just fundamentally unlikeable, and that thought sits with me more than any person does.
