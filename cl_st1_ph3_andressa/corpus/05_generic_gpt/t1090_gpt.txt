I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of the conversations died months ago, usually with my message left on read or “haha” as the last reply.

What hurts is how invisible it feels. I show up, I’m polite, I do my work, and then I go home to silence. It’s like my life only exists in the gaps between other people’s plans.
