I’m starting to realize that I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, pretend I’m fine, but underneath it feels like I’m watching my own life from behind glass. Friends drifted away over the years, and I didn’t fight it, partly out of fear and partly out of exhaustion. Now most days are just work, phone, bed. I scroll through chats I never start, type messages and delete them because I’m convinced I’m annoying. I miss feeling known. Not just “hey, how are you?” but someone who remembers what I said last week, who notices when I disappear. Lately I’m scared this is just how things will be.
