I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it.

I have people around me—coworkers, some acquaintances, family I text occasionally—but I don’t feel actually *known* by anyone. Conversations feel like small talk on repeat: work, weather, memes, “how was your weekend?” I answer, they answer, and then we both go back to our separate bubbles. No one asks follow-up questions that go deeper, and I’m too afraid of being “too much” to push it.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I watch shows, I tell myself I’ll reach out to someone, then convince myself they’re busy and I’d be annoying. The silence becomes this heavy thing that sits on my chest.

It’s strange how you can be “fine” on paper and still feel like you could disappear and it wouldn’t change much for anyone.
