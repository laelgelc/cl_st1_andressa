I’m starting to realize that I feel lonelier around people than I do by myself.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, a family that checks in occasionally. I can make small talk, crack a joke, seem “fine.” But it all feels like I’m playing a character who knows the right lines but doesn’t actually belong in the scene.

What bothers me most is the lack of emotional closeness. Nobody really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling needy or weird. I overthink every message I send, then convince myself I’m bothering them and delete it.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through chats I never start, stare at group photos I’m not in, and wonder if anyone ever thinks of me first, unprompted. It’s like I exist on the edge of everyone’s life, technically “there,” but not actually missed if I disappear.
