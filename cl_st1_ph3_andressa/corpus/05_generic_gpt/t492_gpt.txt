Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. Repeat. I talk to people every day, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if I even know myself anymore.

I used to have friends I’d text without overthinking. Now I stare at my phone and delete drafts because I don’t want to be “too much” or “too needy.” I wait for messages that don’t come and pretend it doesn’t bother me, but it does. It makes me feel pathetic.

What scares me is how normal this has started to feel. The silence, the empty weekends, the pretending I prefer being alone. I keep thinking I’m one small step away from completely giving up on trying to connect with anyone, and I’m not sure what happens after that.
