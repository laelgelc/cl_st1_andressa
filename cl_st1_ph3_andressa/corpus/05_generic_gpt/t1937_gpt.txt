I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but no one I can message without overthinking every word. I replay conversations for hours, convinced I sounded weird or boring, and then I pull back even more. Weekends are the worst—too much time, not enough distractions. I watch other people’s lives through screens and feel like I’m behind glass, close enough to see everything, but too far to touch any of it.
