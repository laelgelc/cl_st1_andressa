Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m physically with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels surface-level. I don’t really have that one person I can text when something good or bad happens. I scroll through my phone and realize there’s no one I’d message without overthinking it. I try to be friendly, but I always feel like an extra in other people’s lives, never a main character. It’s exhausting pretending I’m fine when I mostly just feel invisible.
