I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anything dramatic happening. No big fights, no clear “we’re not friends anymore” moment—just slower replies, canceled plans, group chats that go on fine without me. I keep wondering if I did something wrong or if I’m just…forgettable.  

Most days I go to work, come home, scroll, and realize I haven’t used my voice for hours. I’m surrounded by people online, but it still feels like I’m in a soundproof room where no one actually hears me.
