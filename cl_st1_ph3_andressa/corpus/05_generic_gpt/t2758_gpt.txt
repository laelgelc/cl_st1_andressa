I’ve been feeling weirdly invisible lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, I chat, I laugh at the right times, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script someone else wrote. When the day ends, no one really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.

I scroll through my phone and realize most of my conversations are just old group chats that died months ago. I keep typing messages and deleting them because I don’t want to seem needy or annoying. It’s like I’m always “almost” close to people, but never actually close.

Nights are the worst. That quiet moment before sleep where it hits me how easily I could disappear from most people’s lives.
