I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. The days blur together and the only real difference is what I reheat for dinner.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers, family chats, a couple of old friends I message every now and then. But I don’t feel known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface—weather, memes, complaints about work—and I can’t remember the last time I said something real and didn’t instantly regret oversharing.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. I used to crave connection so badly it hurt. Now it’s more like a dull ache that’s always there, just part of the background noise. I keep thinking I should do something about it, reach out more, join something, whatever. But most days I just don’t.
