I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. I watch other people slip so easily into friendships and relationships, and I feel like I’m behind a glass wall, knocking, but no one hears it.

I’ve tried joining groups, talking more, saying yes to invitations, but I always end up feeling like the extra chair at the table. Present, but not needed. I overthink every message I send, then feel stupid and needy and go quiet again.

What scares me most is how normal the emptiness is beginning to feel. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling, sleep—and days blur together without anyone really seeing me, and without me knowing how to change that.
