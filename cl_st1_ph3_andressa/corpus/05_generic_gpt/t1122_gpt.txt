I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. When I’m with others I feel like I’m acting out a version of myself they’ll tolerate, and when I’m alone I don’t even know who the “real” me is. I keep waiting for someone to notice I’m not okay, but I’m too good at pretending that I am.
