I don’t really know how to talk about this in my “real” life, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m surrounded by people but I feel like a ghost. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of people I’d technically call friends, but it all feels surface-level. We talk about work, TV shows, random news, but no one actually knows me. And I’m starting to realize I don’t really let them, either.

When I try to open up, I get this tight feeling in my chest, like I’m about to say something wrong or overshare and make it weird. So I pull back, make a joke, change the subject. Then later, I’m mad at myself for not saying what I really meant. It’s like I’m building my own cage and then complaining that I’m trapped.

Nights are the worst. The silence gets heavy and my brain replays every awkward moment from the last decade. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives through a screen, and it just emphasizes how disconnected I feel.

I’m not even sure what I want from posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
