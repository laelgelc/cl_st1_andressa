I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like a ghost in my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, some acquaintances—but no one who actually knows me. Conversations never go deeper than surface-level stuff, and when I try to open up, it feels like I’m oversharing or making things awkward. So I stop. Then I get frustrated that no one understands me, even though I’m the one staying silent.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, but I stare at the chat list and realize there’s no one I feel comfortable bothering. I end up putting the phone down and just lying there, listening to my thoughts echo back at me like I’m talking into an empty room.
