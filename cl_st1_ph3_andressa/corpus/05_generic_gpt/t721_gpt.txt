I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold small talk, crack a joke, seem “fine,” but it all feels like I’m acting out a script I learned a long time ago and never updated. Friends have drifted away, and I don’t blame them, but it leaves this weird echo behind. I scroll through chats I’m too embarrassed to revive, and new conversations die after a few messages. I keep wondering if I missed some crucial life class where everyone else learned how to be… wanted.
