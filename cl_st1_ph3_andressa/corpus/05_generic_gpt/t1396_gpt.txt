I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. I talk to people every day, but nothing feels like it sticks. Conversations are surface-level, like we’re all reading from a script. I miss feeling known, not just recognized. Friends drifted away, relationships ended, and I never really replaced any of it. I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I’m exhausted, and I’m not sure anyone’s actually waiting on the other side.
