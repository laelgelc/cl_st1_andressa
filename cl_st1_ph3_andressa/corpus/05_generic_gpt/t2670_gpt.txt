I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate before messaging anyone because I’m never sure who would genuinely care, or if I’m just bothering them.

I keep replaying old conversations, wondering if I was too quiet, too awkward, too much. The more I overthink, the more I withdraw, and the more I withdraw, the stranger it feels to reach out. It’s like I’m slowly fading out of other people’s lives and nobody really notices, and I can’t tell if I’m choosing it or if it’s just happening to me.
