I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “okay.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it never feels like anyone really knows me. When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel ashamed afterward, or I shut down and make jokes. I watch other people make plans, have group chats, share inside jokes, and I’m always just… adjacent. Present, but not included. It’s like I’m a background character in my own life, and I’m scared this is just how it’s going to be.
