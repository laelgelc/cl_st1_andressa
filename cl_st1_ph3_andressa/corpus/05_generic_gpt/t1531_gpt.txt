I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can small-talk with, but no one I can message when something good or bad happens. My phone only lights up for spam or work emails. I scroll through chats that used to be active and see the slow fade: fewer replies, longer gaps, then nothing.

I’m not completely isolated, but I feel invisible in the spaces I’m in. I laugh at the right times, say the right things, and then go home and sit in silence, replaying every interaction and wondering what I did wrong years ago that led me here.
