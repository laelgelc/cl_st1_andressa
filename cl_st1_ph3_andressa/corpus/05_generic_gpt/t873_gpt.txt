I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really make sense on paper. I have coworkers I chat with, family I text sometimes, a couple of old friends I still send memes to. But it all feels…surface level. Like I’m constantly in a room full of people with my hands in my pockets, watching myself from the corner.

I catch myself rehearsing conversations in my head before I send a simple message, then deleting it because it suddenly feels “too much.” I don’t want to bother anyone, or come off as needy, or get that slow, polite reply that basically says, “I don’t have space for you right now.”

I miss having someone who actually knows my day-to-day life, not just the version I filter through small talk. Someone I don’t have to perform for or keep “interesting.” Lately it feels like if I disappeared, people would notice for a bit, then just…adjust.

I’m not even sure what I’m asking for. I just needed to put this somewhere that isn’t the Notes app on my phone.
