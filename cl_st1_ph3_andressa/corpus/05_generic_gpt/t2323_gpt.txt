I keep realizing how replaceable I am to everyone in my life. People seem happy to talk to me when I’m right in front of them, but as soon as we’re apart, I’m basically invisible. Messages go unanswered for days, plans get “forgotten,” and I’m always the one reaching out first.  

What messes with my head is that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. It’s like I’m watching everyone else live their lives through a window. I’m not sure if I’m the problem or just… unnecessary.
