I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, neighbors, family chats—but no one who really knows me beyond surface-level stuff. I go through entire days without anyone asking how I genuinely am, and I’m not sure if it’s because I hide too well or because no one’s actually looking. I try to initiate conversations, but they fade quickly, like I’m easy to forget. It’s strange how loud my thoughts get when there’s no one to share them with.
