I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family I text sometimes—but I don’t feel known by any of them. Most of my conversations are about surface-level stuff: work, weather, memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked me a genuinely curious question about my life and actually waited for the answer.

I keep trying to “put myself out there,” but it feels like I’m always the one initiating, always the one following up. When I stop, everything just goes quiet. It makes me wonder if my presence is more of a convenience than a value to anyone.

I’m not even sure what I’m looking for writing this. Maybe I just needed to put it somewhere that isn’t my own head for once.
