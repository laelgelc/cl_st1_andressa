I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people every day, but it feels like I’m just passing through scenes in someone else’s life. Conversations stay on the surface—work, weather, memes—and then I go home and it’s just silence and the hum of my computer. I keep my phone close, hoping for messages that rarely come. When I do reach out, I overthink every word and usually delete it. It’s like I’ve forgotten how to actually connect, but I still miss it constantly.
