Lately I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life. I talk to people every day—coworkers, neighbors, cashiers—but none of it feels real. It’s all small talk and surface-level stuff. I go home, close the door, and it’s just silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen. I’m not completely isolated, but I feel completely unseen, like I could vanish and nothing would actually change.
