I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. When the interaction ends, I go home and it’s just silence. I keep my phone nearby, hoping for a message that never comes unless I’m needed for something practical.

I try to initiate plans, but people are “busy,” and after enough polite rejections I stop asking. Then I feel guilty for not trying harder, like the loneliness is my own fault. I scroll through social media and see everyone surrounded by friends, partners, families. I know it’s curated, but it still stings.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel, like I’m slowly getting used to being invisible.
