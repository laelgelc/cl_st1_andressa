I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but it all feels like acting. I say the right things, laugh at the right moments, and then go home feeling like I was never really there.  

I overthink every message I send, then end up deleting it and disappearing. I want someone I can be boring around, someone I don’t have to perform for, but I’m terrified they’ll see the “real” me and lose interest.  

It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely invisible.
