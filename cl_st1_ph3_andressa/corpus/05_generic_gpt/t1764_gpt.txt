I’ve been feeling like a ghost lately. I go to work, say the right small talk phrases, smile when I’m supposed to, and then come home to silence that feels heavier every day. I have people around me, technically, but I don’t feel known by anyone. It’s like I’m constantly on the outside of a glass wall, watching everyone else connect while I press my hands against it.

I’m also starting to worry this is just who I am now—someone who can’t quite bridge the gap. I overthink every message I send, then delete it and stay quiet. Weekends stretch out in front of me and I pretend I like the “downtime,” but mostly I just sit with this ache in my chest, wondering if this is all there is.
