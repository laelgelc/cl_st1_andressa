I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I see people everywhere – coworkers chatting, couples on the street, groups of friends posting photos – and I feel like I’m watching it all through thick glass. I’m there, but I’m not really *in* it. Even when I’m physically around others, I feel like an extra in someone else’s movie.

I don’t really have anyone I’d call “my person.” I have acquaintances, people I can joke with at work or school, but no one I can text when I’m having a bad day. I scroll through my phone sometimes just to prove to myself that there’s no one to message, like I’m confirming the emptiness.

What makes it worse is this sense that it’s partly my fault. I overthink every interaction, replay conversations, convince myself I sounded weird or boring. Then I withdraw. Then I feel even more lonely. It’s like a loop I can’t get out of.

Nights are the hardest. Everything is quiet, and it hits me how replaceable I feel. Like if I disappeared, maybe a couple people would notice briefly, then things would just go back to normal for them.

I don’t even know exactly what I’m looking for writing this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
