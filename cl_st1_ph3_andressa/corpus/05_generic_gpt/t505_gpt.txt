I’ve been feeling like I’m fading into the background of my own life. I talk to people every day at work, but it’s all surface-level: deadlines, weather, “how was your weekend?” No one actually knows how I’m doing, and I don’t really know how to let them.

I used to have close friends, but we slowly drifted apart. Now I hesitate to text anyone because I’m convinced I’m an obligation. I scroll through chats, type something, delete it.

Nights are the worst. It’s quiet, and that’s when it hits me how replaceable I seem, even to myself.
