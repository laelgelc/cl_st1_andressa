I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of old friends I still text sometimes—but everything feels thin, like plastic wrap over real connection. Conversations stay on safe topics: work, shows, memes. Nobody asks how I actually am, and when they do, I give the “I’m fine” script because I don’t know how to explain this heavy emptiness without sounding dramatic.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk on here, watch other people’s lives through a screen, then go to bed with this ache in my chest. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would really notice beyond a passing ‘that’s sad’?”  

It’s a strange thing to be constantly “reachable” and still feel completely unreachable.
