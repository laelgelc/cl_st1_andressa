I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically surrounded by people. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but I don’t feel like anyone really knows me, and I’m not sure I know how to let them.

Every time I think about opening up, I hear this voice in my head saying, “They don’t actually care,” or “You’ll just make things weird.” So I stay on the surface: jokes, small talk, the usual. Then I go home and it hits me how empty it all feels.

I keep seeing other people form friendships so naturally, like it’s effortless. For me it feels like trying to speak a language I never learned. I want to be part of something, but I’m always stuck on the outside, watching through the glass, wondering what’s wrong with me.
