I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like acting. I go home and there’s this heavy silence that almost feels louder than noise. I keep my phone close, but it rarely lights up unless it’s a notification from some app I don’t care about.  

I’ve tried joining groups, talking more, being “open,” but conversations never seem to move past the surface. Everyone else seems to already have their people. I feel like I’m always arriving late to a party that’s already in full swing, standing in the doorway, not sure where to go.
