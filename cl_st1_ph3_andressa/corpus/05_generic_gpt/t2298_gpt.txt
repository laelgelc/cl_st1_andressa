I’m starting to realize how invisible I feel in my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but no one who actually knows what’s going on in my head. I censor everything because I’m scared of being “too much” or a burden, and then I end up feeling fake and disconnected. I miss the feeling of being someone’s first choice, of having a person I can message without overthinking every word. Lately it just feels like I’m background noise.
