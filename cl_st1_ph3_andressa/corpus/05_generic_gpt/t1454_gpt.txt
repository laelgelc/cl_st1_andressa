I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life without sounding dramatic, so I’m dumping it here instead. I’m surrounded by others pretty often — work, family chats, random social stuff — but I still feel weirdly separate, like there’s an invisible wall between me and everyone else.

Conversations stay on the surface. I listen, I nod, I make jokes, but it never feels like anyone actually knows me. I try to open up a bit and it either gets brushed off or turned into small talk again. After a while, I just stop trying.

It’s a strange mix of wanting to be seen and being terrified that if I really was, people would quietly step away.
