I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what my days look like, what I’m worried about, what I’m hoping for. I keep conversations light because I’m scared if I open up, I’ll come off as needy or weird. Then I go home and replay every interaction, convinced I said the wrong thing. It’s this strange mix of craving closeness and feeling like I’m fundamentally not built for it.
