I’m starting to realize I don’t really have a “person.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d actually call if something truly bad or even really good happened. Group chats move without me, plans happen around me, and I’m just… there, orbiting.

What makes it worse is that I’m not completely isolated. I see people, talk to them, even laugh, but it still feels like I’m on the outside of a window looking in. I keep wondering if it’s my personality, my timing, or something fundamentally unlikable about me that I just can’t see.
