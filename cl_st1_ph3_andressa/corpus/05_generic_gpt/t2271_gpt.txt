I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call if I was really falling apart. I watch other people make plans, share inside jokes, talk about their weekends, and I feel like I’m behind glass. I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but I’m also exhausted from always being the one who reaches out first.
