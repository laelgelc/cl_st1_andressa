I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really broke inside me. I scroll through chats and see conversations that just… fizzled out, usually because I didn’t know how to keep them going without feeling like I was bothering someone.

I watch other people form friendships so naturally, like it’s breathing, and I feel like I missed some crucial class on “being a person.” I’m not completely isolated, but I feel emotionally homeless, if that makes sense. Like I’m always visiting other people’s lives and never really belonging in any of them.
