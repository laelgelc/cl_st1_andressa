I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did.

I have “people” in my life—coworkers, acquaintances, a couple of old friends I text every few weeks—but it all feels like background noise. We talk about work, memes, random stuff, but nothing that makes me feel seen. I can be in a group chat, or at a gathering, and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else actually be there.

When I try to open up, I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or I hold back so much that the conversation stays shallow. I don’t know where the middle is. I keep thinking there’s something fundamentally off about me that other people can sense.

Most nights I just scroll, lurk, and listen to other people’s lives. Then I shut everything off and it’s just me and this heavy, empty feeling that I don’t really know how to describe to anyone offline.
