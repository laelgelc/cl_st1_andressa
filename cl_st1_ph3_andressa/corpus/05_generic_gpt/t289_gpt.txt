I’m starting to feel like I’m becoming transparent in my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, some acquaintances, family I text sometimes—but I don’t feel *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: weather, work, memes. No one asks how I really am, and when they do, I don’t know how to answer without sounding dramatic or needy.

I scroll through chats and see so many half-dead conversations: “We should hang out sometime!” followed by silence. I keep wondering if I’m the common denominator, if there’s something about me that pushes people away without me realizing it.

What scares me most is how used to this I’m becoming. Nights alone don’t hurt as sharply as they used to; they just feel… normal. I go through my day, do what I’m supposed to do, come home, distract myself with screens, and repeat. It’s like my life is technically functioning, but emotionally on mute.
