I don’t really know how to talk about this in real life, so I’m dumping it here.

Lately I feel like I’m living next to other people instead of with them. I go to work, I make small talk, I answer “how’s it going?” with “not bad, you?” and then I come home and it’s like all the noise shuts off and I remember how empty everything actually feels.

I have “people” in my life, technically. Coworkers, some old friends in a group chat that’s mostly memes, family I see on holidays. But I don’t feel known by anyone. I can’t remember the last time someone asked me a question that went deeper than surface level and actually waited for the answer.

I’ve also started noticing how much I censor myself. I don’t say when I’m lonely because it feels embarrassing, like admitting I somehow failed at something everyone else figured out in high school. When I do try to be honest, I either get brushed off with “you just need to get out more” or people get uncomfortable and change the subject.

So I end up scrolling, lurking, watching other people’s lives go by. I see people casually mentioning “my friends and I” like it’s the most normal thing in the world, and it just feels… foreign. I don’t even know what I’d talk about if someone actually wanted to hang out with me regularly.

I’m not sure what I’m hoping for posting this. I guess I just wanted to say it somewhere, even if it doesn’t change anything.
