I’ve been feeling like a ghost lately. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. Nobody really knows me beyond “reliable coworker” or “the quiet one.” I try to start conversations but they die out fast, like people are being polite more than actually interested. Group chats move on without me, invitations don’t come, and when I do get included, I feel like an extra in someone else’s movie.

What makes it worse is how hard it is to admit this in real life. Saying “I’m lonely” feels pathetic, like I’m confessing a personal failure. So I just keep it to myself and pretend I’m fine. Some days I honestly can’t tell if I’m introverted or just used to being left out. It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like who I am now.
