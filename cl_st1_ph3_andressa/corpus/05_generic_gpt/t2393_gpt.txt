It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re on mute.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like I’m playing a role. I don’t really let anyone see the parts of me that feel heavy or awkward, and I’m scared that if I did, they’d quietly step back. So I stay on the surface and then come home and feel this huge emptiness.

I try to keep busy, scroll, watch shows, but the silence hits hardest at night. I start wondering if I somehow missed the “how to connect” lesson everyone else got. I’m tired of hearing “just put yourself out there” when I already feel like I’m out there, just invisible.
