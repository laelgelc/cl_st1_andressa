I’ve been feeling this strange kind of loneliness where it’s not about being physically alone, but about feeling like no one really “gets” me. I have people around—coworkers, acquaintances, even some friends—but everything feels surface-level. I can laugh and talk with them, then go home and feel like I don’t actually exist in anyone’s mind once I’m out of sight.

I also struggle to initiate deeper conversations because I’m scared I’ll come off as needy or weird. So I keep it light, then feel invisible. It’s like I’m always half-present, never fully known. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing. I’m not sure when I started feeling this detached, but it’s starting to feel permanent.
