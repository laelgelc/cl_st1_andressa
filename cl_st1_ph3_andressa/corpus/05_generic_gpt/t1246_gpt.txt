I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, eat, scroll, sleep—but none of it feels connected to anyone. I’ll go days without using my voice for anything besides “thank you” at a checkout or “have a good one” on a work call.

I have people on social media, people I used to be close to, but I don’t know how to bridge the gap anymore. I type messages and delete them because I’m convinced I’m bothering them, or that I won’t know what to say if they actually respond. It’s like the longer I stay distant, the heavier it becomes to try.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. The silence, the empty weekends, the sense that if I disappeared, the world around me wouldn’t really change.
