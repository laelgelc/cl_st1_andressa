I’ve been feeling like I’m on the outside of a glass box lately. I can see people’s lives happening, I can hear the laughter, the casual “how was your weekend?” chats at work, but I never feel like I’m actually part of it. I’m there physically, I respond when spoken to, but it all feels like I’m playing a background character in my own life.

What bothers me most is how invisible this feels from the outside. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people who’d probably say they like me. But no one really knows me. No one texts first. No one checks in unless I initiate, and even then the conversations die quickly, like they’re being polite rather than interested.

I’ve tried hobbies, meetups, talking more, talking less. It all ends the same: me walking home alone, replaying every interaction and wondering what exactly is wrong with me that I can’t seem to move past “friendly stranger” in anyone’s life.
