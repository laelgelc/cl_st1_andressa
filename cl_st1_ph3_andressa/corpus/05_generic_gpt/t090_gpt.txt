I’m starting to realize I’m not actually “busy,” I’m just filling time so I don’t have to notice how alone I am. I scroll, work, watch stuff, but there’s no one who really knows me day-to-day. Friends drifted, relationships never stuck, and now I don’t even know how to reach out without feeling like I’m bothering people. It’s weird how you can be surrounded by noise and still feel like you’re disappearing in plain sight.
