I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like it’s changing who I am.

I go entire days without speaking out loud to another person. At work it’s surface-level small talk, then I come home, scroll, eat, sleep. I keep thinking I should reach out to people, but I feel like I’ve forgotten how to be someone others would want to talk to.

I used to have friends. Now it’s mostly old group chats that are dead, people I only see through their life updates on Instagram. I don’t feel hated, just… unnecessary.

The worst part is how invisible it feels. If I disappeared from social media and stopped answering messages, I honestly don’t know who would notice, or how long it would take.
