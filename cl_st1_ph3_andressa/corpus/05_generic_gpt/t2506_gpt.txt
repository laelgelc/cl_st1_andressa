I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, and it’s hitting harder as I get older. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work or hobbies, but no one I feel truly close to. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there’s just…no one I feel comfortable reaching out to.

I keep replaying old conversations, wondering if I said something weird, if that’s why people drift away. I overthink texts so much that I sometimes never send them, then feel hurt when no one checks in. It’s like I’m both starving for connection and terrified of it.

Most days I keep myself busy so I don’t notice the emptiness. But at night, when everything’s quiet, the loneliness feels physical, like a weight in my chest that I can’t explain to anyone in my life.
