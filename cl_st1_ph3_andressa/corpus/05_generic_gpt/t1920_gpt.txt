Lately it feels like my life is just background noise. I go to work, do what I’m supposed to do, make small talk about weather and TV shows, then come home to silence. It’s not that I have nobody in my life, it’s that nobody really knows me. Conversations never go past the surface. I try to open up sometimes, but I can see people get uncomfortable, like I’m saying too much.

I scroll through social media and see people hanging out, making memories, and I’m just… watching. I don’t even know how to ask someone to hang out without feeling like I’m bothering them. I keep thinking I must be the common denominator. If everyone else seems to find their people, maybe the problem actually is me.
