I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing. My phone barely lights up anymore unless it’s a notification from some app I don’t care about. I’ll scroll through chats and see my own name over and over as the last person who sent a message. Then I get embarrassed and stop reaching out.

I’m around people sometimes—work, errands, family—but it all feels like acting. I laugh at the right moments, ask how others are doing, and no one seems to realize I’m just filling silence. When I get home, it’s like I take off a costume and there’s just…nothing.

What messes with my head most is wondering if this is my fault. Maybe I’m too quiet, too weird, too “much.” I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually asked how I’m doing.
