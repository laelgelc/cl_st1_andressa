I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold a conversation, crack a joke, do all the surface-level stuff, but it’s like I’m playing a role instead of being myself. When I’m with others I feel invisible, and when I’m alone I feel like I don’t exist at all. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move on. Mine feels paused, like I’m waiting for something that never shows up.
