I’m surrounded by people but feel like I’m behind glass. Friends, coworkers, family—I can talk to them, but nothing feels like it really connects. I censor myself because I’m scared of being “too much,” and the more I hold back, the more distant I feel. Nights are the worst; I scroll, lurk, listen to podcasts just to fill the silence. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing.
