I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I’m technically “around people” a lot — at work, on the commute, even in group chats — but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: tasks, memes, small talk about weekends. I can’t remember the last time someone asked me a real question and waited for the real answer.

What makes it worse is that I’m starting to forget how to open up. When I try, it feels forced or awkward, like I’m performing vulnerability instead of actually sharing anything. I worry people will think I’m too intense or needy, so I end up pulling back and then resenting the distance I created.

Nights are the hardest. I scroll, I lurk, I read about other people’s lives, and it just highlights how disconnected I feel from my own. It’s like I’m standing behind glass, watching everyone else participate in something I can’t quite reach.
