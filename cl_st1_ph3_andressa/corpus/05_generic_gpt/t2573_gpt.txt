I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but nobody I’d call if something really went wrong. When I scroll through my contacts, there isn’t a single name that feels “safe.”  

I overthink every message I send, then convince myself I’m annoying and delete it. I cancel plans because I’m tired, then feel even worse sitting at home. It feels like I’m watching my own life from behind glass—present, but not really in it.  

I keep wondering if this is just how it’s going to be from now on.
