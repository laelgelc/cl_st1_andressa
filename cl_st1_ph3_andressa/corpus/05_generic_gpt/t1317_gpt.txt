I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I nod to, but no one who actually knows me. I go whole weekends without speaking out loud, then feel weird hearing my own voice again on Monday. 

When I try to reach out, I second-guess every message, delete it, and tell myself they’re too busy anyway. I scroll through chats full of dead conversations and old group photos where I already felt like the extra. 

It’s not that I want constant company. I just want to feel missed by someone.
