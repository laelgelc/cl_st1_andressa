I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, pretend I’m fine, but the second a conversation needs any depth, I freeze. It feels like everyone else got some manual on “how to be a person” and I missed that day.

I’m surrounded by people at work, online, in public, but it all feels like I’m watching life through glass. I go home, sit in my room, and the silence is so loud it almost has a weight. I try to distract myself with YouTube, games, scrolling, but when I stop, the emptiness just rushes back in.

What makes it worse is that I’m starting to feel awkward even with the few people I used to be close to. I overthink every message, every pause, every time they take a bit longer to reply. I’m tired of hearing “just put yourself out there” when I don’t even feel like I have a self to put anywhere.
