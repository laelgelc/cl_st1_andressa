I’m starting to feel like I exist in the gaps between other people’s lives. Friends text in group chats I’m not in, make plans I hear about afterward. I’m not completely isolated—I have coworkers, acquaintances—but everything feels surface-level, like I’m a background character in their stories. I miss feeling chosen, missed, checked on without having to initiate. Lately I’m wondering if this is just how adulthood is going to feel from now on.
