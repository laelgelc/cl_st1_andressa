I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but it never feels like anyone really sees me. Conversations stay on the surface, and I’m scared to push deeper because I don’t want to seem needy or weird. I scroll through social media and it looks like everyone else has a “person,” or at least a group. I keep wondering what’s wrong with me that I can’t seem to connect past small talk.
