I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like I’m playing a role. When the day ends, no one really knows me and I’m not sure I’d know how to let them. I scroll through chats and social media, hovering over names, but I never hit send because I assume I’m bothering them. The silence becomes this heavy proof that I’m forgettable, and the more time passes, the harder it is to imagine that changing.
