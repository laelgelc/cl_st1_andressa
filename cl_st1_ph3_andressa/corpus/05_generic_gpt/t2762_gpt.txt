I’ve been feeling like I’m fading into the background of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family I see sometimes—but I don’t feel *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, the weather, random small talk. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really was and actually wanted to hear the answer.

I try to reach out, but it always feels like I’m intruding, like I’m the “extra” person in every group. I’ll send a message, get a polite reply, and then it just dies. I start wondering if I’m just fundamentally forgettable.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, hoping for a notification that never comes, then put it down and just lie there, feeling this heavy, quiet emptiness that I don’t know what to do with.
