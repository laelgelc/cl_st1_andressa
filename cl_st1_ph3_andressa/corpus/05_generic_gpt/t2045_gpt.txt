I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people. I can talk, joke, be “fine” on the surface, but it all feels like I’m playing a character. When conversations end, I’m left with this weird emptiness, like nothing real happened. I scroll through chats and it’s all small talk and memes. No one really knows me, but I’m also terrified of letting them. It’s like I’m invisible and exposed at the same time.
