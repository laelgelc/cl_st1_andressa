I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, cook, sleep—but I don’t really talk to anyone beyond “hey” and “thanks.” Even when I’m around people, I feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect while I’m stuck on mute.

I’ve tried joining group chats and hobbies, but I always end up feeling like the extra person no one would really miss if I stopped showing up. It’s getting harder to remember what it feels like to be genuinely known by someone, not just recognized. I miss being on someone’s mind without having to initiate every conversation.
