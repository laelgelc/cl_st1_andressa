I’m starting to realize I don’t really *exist* in other people’s lives. I’m the person who’s “nice to see” but never the one anyone thinks to invite first. Conversations feel like I’m interviewing people about their lives while no one asks about mine. I try to reach out, but it feels one-sided, like I’m begging for scraps of attention. Even when I’m physically with others, there’s this quiet sense that I could vanish and nothing would actually change.
