I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people most days—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but it all feels like background noise. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted to hear the messy answer.

I’ve tried to open up a few times, but it either gets brushed off with jokes or people get uncomfortable and change the subject. That makes me shut down even more. I scroll through social media and see everyone hanging out, getting married, having kids, and I feel like I’m stuck behind glass, watching life happen to other people.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel, like loneliness is just the default setting now.
