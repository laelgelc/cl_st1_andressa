I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one who really knows me. I scroll through chats and it’s all surface-level stuff that could disappear tomorrow and nothing would change. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate because I don’t know who to tell. I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but I’m also exhausted from always being the one reaching out.
