I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have friends, technically, but everything feels surface-level, like I’m a background character in my own life. I listen to everyone else’s problems and stories, then go home and sit with this heavy, silent feeling that no one really knows me.

I try to share more, but the words feel wrong coming out, like I’m oversharing or being dramatic. So I pull back again. Then I watch other people form those easy, natural bonds and I feel like I’m behind glass, observing instead of participating.

It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re disappearing.
