I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one who really knows me. I watch other people make plans, send memes to their “best friends,” talk about group chats, and it feels like I’m behind a glass wall. I try to reach out sometimes, but the conversations stay shallow and fade quickly. It’s like I’m forgettable, even in places where I show up every day.
