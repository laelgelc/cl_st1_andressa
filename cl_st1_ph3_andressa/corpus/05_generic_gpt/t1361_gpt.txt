I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life in slow motion. Friends don’t really reach out anymore, and when I try, the conversations feel forced or short, like they’re just being polite. I go through entire days where the only words I say out loud are “thank you” at a checkout. I keep wondering if I did something wrong, or if I’m just fundamentally forgettable. It’s exhausting pretending it doesn’t bother me.
