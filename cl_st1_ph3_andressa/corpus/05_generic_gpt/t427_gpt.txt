I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing while still being physically here. I have people around me—coworkers, family I text sometimes—but it all feels like background noise. I can go days without anyone asking how I really am, and I’m not sure anyone would notice if I stopped trying to initiate every conversation.

The weird part is I’m not totally isolated. I show up, I smile, I make small talk. But there’s this constant sense that I’m on the outside of a glass wall, watching everyone else connect while I just mimic the motions. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely wanted to know me. I’m not sure I remember how to be known.
