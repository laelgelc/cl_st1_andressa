I’m starting to realize I don’t really have anyone who knows me in the way I wish someone would. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work and online, but everything feels surface-level. When something genuinely good or bad happens, I hesitate before messaging anyone because I don’t know who actually cares, or who wouldn’t just see it as an obligation to respond.

I scroll through old conversations and see how many just slowly faded out. No big fight, no reason—just fewer messages until nothing. It makes me wonder if I’m forgettable, or if I just don’t know how to maintain connections. I keep thinking it’s my fault somehow, but I don’t know what I’m doing wrong, and I’m tired of pretending it doesn’t bother me.
