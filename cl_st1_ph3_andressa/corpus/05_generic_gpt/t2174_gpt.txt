I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. Not in a “I’m shy” kind of way, but in a “I feel like an alien in a human suit” kind of way.

I have acquaintances. People at work who say hi, a group chat that lights up sometimes, a couple of old friends I talk to every few weeks. On paper, it looks like I’m not alone. But I go through entire days without feeling like anyone actually sees me, or even wants to.

When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing, being dramatic, or just boring. When I stay quiet, I feel fake and invisible. It’s like there’s no version of me that fits. I watch other people slip into conversations so easily, making inside jokes, planning things together, and I’m just… there. Present, but not really part of it.

I keep thinking maybe I missed some crucial stage where everyone else learned how to be close to each other, and I just didn’t. And now I’m too old, too awkward, and too tired to figure it out from scratch.
