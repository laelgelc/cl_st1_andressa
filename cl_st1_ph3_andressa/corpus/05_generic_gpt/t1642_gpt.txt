I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like a background character in my own life.

I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers I joke with. On paper, it looks like I’m “not alone.” But no one actually checks in on me unless I initiate. If I stop messaging people, my phone just goes silent. I’ve tested it. It’s like I disappear and nothing changes for anyone.

I see people posting photos with “my people,” “my best friend,” “my person,” and I can’t think of a single person who would say that about me. I’m always the extra, the plus-one, the convenient listener.

What scares me most is how used to it I’m getting. I go through entire weekends without speaking out loud, just scrolling, watching other people’s lives. Then Monday comes, I put on the “I’m fine, just tired” mask, and no one notices the difference.
