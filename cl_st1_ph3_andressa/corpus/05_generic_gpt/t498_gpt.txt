Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, make small talk, answer emails, come home, and it’s like none of it really touches me. I have people around me, but I don’t feel known by any of them. When I try to open up, I end up making a joke or changing the subject because I’m scared of being “too much.” Then I go back to my room, scroll on my phone, and wonder if anyone would actually notice if I just quietly disappeared from their routines.
