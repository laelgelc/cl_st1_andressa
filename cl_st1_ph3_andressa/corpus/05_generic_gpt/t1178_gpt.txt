I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being myself. When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I pull back again. Then I watch everyone else forming friendships, relationships, plans, and I’m just… there. Present, but not really included. It’s like living behind glass, watching a life I can’t quite reach.
