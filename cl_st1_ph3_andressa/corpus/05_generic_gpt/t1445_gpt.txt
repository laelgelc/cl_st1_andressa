I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely in a way that sticks to everything. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with online, but nobody I’d call if I were really falling apart. Conversations never seem to get past the surface. I’m either “too much” when I’m honest or “too quiet” when I hold back. 

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move forward. Mine feels like it’s happening behind glass. I don’t even know what I’m hoping for anymore, just that this can’t be all there is.
