I’ve started to notice that I can go entire days without speaking out loud to another person, and it’s messing with my sense of what’s “normal.” I have coworkers, classmates, people I say “hi” to, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what’s going on with me, and I don’t feel like I have the right to take up space by telling them.

I scroll through chats where I used to be active and see how the conversations moved on without me. I wasn’t pushed out; I just slowly stopped replying, then realized no one really chased after me. That hurt more than an actual rejection.

What’s weird is I don’t even know what I’d say if someone did want to talk deeply. I feel out of practice at being known. It’s like my personality has gone quiet from disuse, and I’m not sure how to turn the volume back up.
