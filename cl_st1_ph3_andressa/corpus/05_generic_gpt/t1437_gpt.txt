I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel like I don’t actually exist to any of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. I keep conversations light because whenever I try to open up, it feels like I’m oversharing or making things awkward. Then I replay it in my head and feel stupid. I want someone who genuinely cares, but I’m also terrified of being a burden. So I stay quiet and the distance just keeps growing.
