I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to be around them.

I have coworkers, classmates, people I chat with online. I can joke, be “on,” even seem social. But the second the interaction ends, it’s like I don’t exist to anyone. No messages, no invites, no “hey, how are you?” unless I initiate, and even then it feels forced and shallow.

What hurts most is that I’m not completely isolated; I’m just emotionally alone. I can sit in a room full of people and feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live in a world I somehow missed the instructions for.

I keep wondering what’s wrong with me. Am I boring? Too quiet? Too weird? I replay conversations, looking for the moment I made myself forgettable.

It’s exhausting wanting closeness and constantly feeling like an optional extra in everyone’s life.
