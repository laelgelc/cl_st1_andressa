I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives. Messages go unanswered, plans never quite materialize, and I’m always the one reaching out first. I have acquaintances, but no one who really knows me. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect. I miss being someone’s first choice instead of an afterthought, or worse, not a thought at all.
