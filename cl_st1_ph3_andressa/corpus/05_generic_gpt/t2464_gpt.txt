I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m just… adjacent to everyone else’s life. I have people around me – coworkers, acquaintances, family chats – but I don’t feel like I’m actually *in* anyone’s life. I’m the person people update, not the person they invite.

I keep trying to initiate things: messages, “we should hang out,” suggesting plans. People respond, but it’s always vague, or something comes up. After a while you start wondering if you’re misreading every interaction.

The weirdest part is how exhausting it is to keep pretending I’m fine. I’m not falling apart, I just feel like I’m fading into the background, and no one would really notice if I stopped trying.
