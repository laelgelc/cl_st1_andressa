I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of people I can text, but it all feels surface-level. I don’t know how to move conversations beyond “how’s it going” without feeling like I’m oversharing or being a burden.

What really gets to me is not having anyone who actually knows me. No one I can message in the middle of the day just to say something random, or call when I’m not okay. I keep thinking it’s my fault somehow—like I missed some crucial stage where everyone else learned how to build real friendships and I just…didn’t.
