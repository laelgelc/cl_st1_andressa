I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “around” people. I go to work, I smile, I make small talk, but it all feels like I’m playing a role that doesn’t belong to me. The second I’m home and the door closes, it’s like the world just forgets I exist. No messages, no calls, no plans. Just silence.

I’ve tried to reach out more, but it always feels one-sided, like I’m forcing my way into other people’s lives. I’ll send a message and stare at my phone, then overthink every minute that goes by without a reply. Eventually I convince myself I’m being annoying and pull back again.

What makes it worse is that I can’t even explain this properly to anyone in my life. I’m “fine” on the surface, functional enough that nobody asks questions. But underneath, it feels like I’m slowly disappearing, and I’m scared that if I really vanished, it wouldn’t make much difference to anyone.
