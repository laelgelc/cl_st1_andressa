I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, family chats, the occasional meetup—but everything feels…thin. Like I’m behind glass, watching myself talk and nod and smile at the right moments. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without sounding dramatic or needy.

I miss having “my person,” someone I can message about nothing and everything. Now conversations are scheduled, polite, and surface-level. I scroll through old chats with people I used to be close to and can’t figure out when exactly the distance happened, just that it did.

Most days I tell myself it’s fine. But it doesn’t really feel fine.
