I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I nod to, but no one who really knows me. I go through entire days without anyone saying my name. When I get home, the silence feels heavy, like the walls are absorbing whatever is left of me.

I scroll through chats that went dead months ago, hover over the text box, then close it because I don’t want to be “that person” who only reaches out when they’re lonely. I tell myself I’m just busy, but the truth is I’m scared I’ve become boring, or too much, or both.

The worst part is how invisible this all is. From the outside, I look fine. I laugh at the right times, meet deadlines, make small talk. Inside, it feels like I’m fading into the background of my own life, and no one’s noticed.
