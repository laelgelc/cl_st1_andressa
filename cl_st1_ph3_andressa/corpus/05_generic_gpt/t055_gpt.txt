I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m in a soundproof box. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even family nearby, but every interaction feels like small talk on repeat. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling like I’m oversharing or being dramatic.

I scroll through chats I never start, type messages and delete them. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would actually notice beyond a passing ‘that’s sad’?” It’s like I’m fading into the background of my own life, and I don’t know how to step back into it.
