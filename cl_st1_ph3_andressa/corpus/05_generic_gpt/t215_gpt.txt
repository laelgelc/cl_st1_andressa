I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one who really knows me. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there isn’t a single person I feel comfortable texting without overthinking it.

I watch other people make plans so effortlessly, like it’s breathing, and I’m stuck analyzing every word I say. I worry I come off as awkward or needy, so I default to silence. Then the silence becomes evidence that I’m forgettable, and I pull back even more. It feels like I’m slowly fading into the background of my own life.
