I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with, but when the day ends it’s like someone cuts the sound and I’m left in this heavy silence. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives move forward. Everyone seems to have a group chat, a person to text, a plan for the weekend. I have unread notifications from apps, not from humans. I’m so tired of pretending this doesn’t bother me.
