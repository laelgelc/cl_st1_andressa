I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, seem “fine,” but it never goes past that surface level. I leave every interaction feeling like I performed a version of myself instead of actually being there.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, wanting to message someone, and then convince myself they’re busy or don’t really care. So I say nothing, and the silence just gets heavier.

What hurts most is that I’m not technically alone. There are people around me, but I still feel like I’m standing behind glass, watching everyone else live their lives while I’m stuck on the outside.
