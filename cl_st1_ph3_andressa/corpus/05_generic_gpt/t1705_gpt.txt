It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re pressed up against a window looking in.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. I don’t feel “known” by anyone. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate before messaging anyone because I’m never sure who actually wants to hear from me, or if I’m just bothering them.

I keep thinking it’s my fault—that I’m too quiet, too awkward, too “much” when I do open up. I replay conversations afterward, cringing at every little thing I said, and then I retreat even more. It’s like I’m slowly erasing myself to avoid embarrassing moments.

I’m not even sure what I’m hoping for posting this. Maybe I just needed to admit out loud that I’m lonely instead of pretending I’m fine all the time.
