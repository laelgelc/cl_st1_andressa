I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life, so I’m dumping it here.

I’m surrounded by others most days – coworkers, family texts, group chats that never shut up – but I feel like a ghost walking through all of it. I can make small talk, crack a joke, pretend I’m fine, but as soon as I’m alone again it’s like the silence is physically loud.

I keep thinking there must be something fundamentally off about me. Other people seem to slide into friendships and relationships like it’s the most natural thing in the world. For me, every interaction feels like an audition I’m failing. I replay conversations later, picking apart every word, convinced I sounded weird or desperate.

What hurts most is that people see me as “independent” or “low maintenance.” They don’t see that I’m just scared of being a burden, so I never reach out first. Then I sit there wondering why no one ever really reaches back.
