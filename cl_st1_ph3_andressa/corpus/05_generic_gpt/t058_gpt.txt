I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or if I ever really did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke around with, but it all feels like I’m playing a character. As soon as I’m alone again, it’s like I vanish from everyone’s mind, and honestly, from my own. I don’t get invited anywhere, and when I try to reach out, conversations die quickly. It makes me wonder if I’m just… boring, or draining, or somehow giving off a “don’t bother” vibe without meaning to.

What makes it worse is that I can remember times in my life when I did have real friends, late-night talks, stupid inside jokes. Now I scroll through old messages that just stopped one day and can’t even pinpoint what I did wrong.

I keep thinking I should “put myself out there,” but I’m tired of being the one who tries, only to feel like background noise in other people’s lives.
