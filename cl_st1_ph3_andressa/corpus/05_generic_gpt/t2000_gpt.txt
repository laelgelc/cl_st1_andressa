I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life.

Most days are work, commute, screen, sleep. I talk to coworkers about tasks and deadlines but never anything real. When I get home, the silence is so loud it almost feels physical. I put on YouTube or podcasts just to hear another voice, but it makes me feel even more like I’m watching life instead of living it.

Friends drifted away slowly—different cities, relationships, kids. I don’t even know when I became the person no one thinks to invite. I scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one who double-texted last.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel, like I’m getting used to being invisible.
