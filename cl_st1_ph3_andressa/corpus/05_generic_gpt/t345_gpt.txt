I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like it’s changing who I am. I go days without using my voice for more than “thank you” at a cashier. Group chats are muted; when I unmute them, I still don’t know what to say. I watch people share inside jokes and casual plans and it feels like I’m standing behind glass, knocking, but no one hears.

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel so out of practice at being around people that I end up quiet and awkward, then hate myself on the way home. I miss being known by someone—having a person who notices when I disappear for a bit. Right now it feels like if I vanished, it would mostly just make things simpler for everyone else.
