I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about work, hobbies, the weather, whatever. But the second the conversation is supposed to go deeper, my brain just kind of… stalls. I either overshare and feel ashamed later, or stay surface-level and feel invisible.

Most of my “social life” is group chats that are basically just memes and occasional “how’s everyone doing?” that no one really answers honestly. When someone does invite me out, I feel like an extra in a movie, standing slightly outside the circle, laughing half a second too late.

What messes with me is that from the outside my life looks fine. I have a job, a place to live, people I can technically text. But I don’t feel known by anyone. I don’t feel chosen. It’s like I’m always the backup plan, never the person someone thinks of first.

I don’t even know what I’m looking for posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
