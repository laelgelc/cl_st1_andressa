I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure anyone wants to. I scroll through chats I used to have with friends and see this slow fade-out, like we all just quietly decided to stop trying. I don’t know when I became “optional” in other people’s lives. I keep thinking if I were more interesting, more funny, more something, maybe I’d matter. Most days I just go home, sit with my phone, and pretend I’m busy so I don’t have to face how empty everything feels.
