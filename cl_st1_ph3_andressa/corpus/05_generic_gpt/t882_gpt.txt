I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me or checks in unprompted. When I’m around others, I can be funny, helpful, “easy to be with,” but when I go home it’s just silence and the feeling that I’m completely optional.  

I see people planning trips with friends, texting constantly, having group chats. My phone is mostly quiet. I’m scared that if I disappeared from social media and stopped initiating, everything would just…stop, and nothing would really change for anyone but me.
