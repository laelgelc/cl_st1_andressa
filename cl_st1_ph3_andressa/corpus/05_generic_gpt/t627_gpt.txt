I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m lonely in a way that feels like it’s rewiring my brain.

I can go days without anyone saying my name out loud. I scroll through chats and see conversations I used to be part of, and now I’m just this silent observer watching other people’s lives move forward without me. I keep typing messages and deleting them because I feel like I’m bothering people just by existing.

In public, I feel like a ghost. I’m surrounded by bodies but not really *with* anyone. I overthink every interaction: Did I talk too much? Too little? Was I weird? Then I end up avoiding people to escape that anxiety, which just makes everything worse.

I don’t even know what I’m craving anymore—friends, connection, or just proof that I’m still real to someone.
