I’ve been feeling this weird combination of invisible and exposed lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, small talk—but it feels like I’m just a background character in everyone else’s life. People know my name, but no one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s partly my fault.

I’ve noticed I censor myself a lot. I don’t say what I’m really thinking because I’m afraid it’ll come off as too intense, too needy, too “much.” So I stay on the surface, and then I resent that everything stays on the surface.

Nights are the worst. I’ll scroll through my phone, not even looking for anything specific, just hoping something will make me feel connected. Instead I end up closing everything and lying there, feeling this heavy, hollow kind of ache, like I’m missing a language everyone else seems to speak fluently.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for by posting this. Maybe I just wanted to put it somewhere outside my own head for once.
