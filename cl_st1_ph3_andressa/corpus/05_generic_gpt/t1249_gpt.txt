Lately I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels muted, like I’m behind a pane of glass watching everyone else actually live.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers, acquaintances, family chats. But I don’t feel known by any of them. Conversations stay on the surface: weather, work complaints, memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted to hear the answer.

I’m at this weird point where I crave connection so badly, but I’m exhausted by every attempt. I overthink every message, every silence, every “seen” with no reply. Then I feel pathetic for caring so much.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. The quiet evenings, the empty weekends, the constant sense that if I disappeared, life would go on exactly the same for everyone else.
