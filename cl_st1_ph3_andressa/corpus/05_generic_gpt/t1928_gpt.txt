I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life without sounding dramatic, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m not “alone” in the obvious sense. I have coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family chats that light up on holidays. From the outside, it probably looks like I’m doing fine. But I feel like a background character in my own life.

Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes, “how was your weekend.” No one really asks follow-up questions, and I’ve realized I’ve stopped offering real answers anyway. It feels pointless to open up when I can see people mentally checking out mid-sentence.

What really messes with me is that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I watch everyone else pair off, form tighter circles, make plans that don’t include me. I go home, sit in the quiet, and wonder if anyone would actually notice if I just slowly drifted away.
