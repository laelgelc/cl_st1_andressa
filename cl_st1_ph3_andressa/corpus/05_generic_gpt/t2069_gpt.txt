I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing.

I have acquaintances, people I can chat with at work or online, but no one I’d call when something actually matters. I watch other people make plans, talk about “the group,” and I’m just… orbiting around it. Close enough to see it, never actually part of it.

I’ve tried joining clubs, messaging first, suggesting meetups. People are polite, sometimes even enthusiastic, but it never seems to stick. After a while I start wondering if I’m doing something wrong just by existing the way I do.

The worst part is how normalized it’s become. I go whole weekends without speaking out loud to anyone. I scroll, I lurk, I consume other people’s lives like a spectator. Then Sunday night hits and I realize I’ve barely been a person in my own.
