I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I can joke with or talk about work or random stuff, but it never goes past that. No one really knows me, and I don’t really know anyone.  

I scroll through chats and it’s just dead. If I disappeared, I’m not sure who would notice quickly enough for it to matter. I see people hanging out on social media and I feel like I’m watching life through a window I can’t open.  

I’ve also forgotten how to initiate anything without feeling like I’m bothering people. So I stay quiet, then feel lonely, then get more awkward. It’s like a loop I can’t break.
