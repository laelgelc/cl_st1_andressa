I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, joke around, even seem “social,” but it all feels like acting. As soon as I’m alone again, it’s like I vanish from everyone’s mind, and honestly from my own.

I watch other people form friendships so naturally—group chats, plans, inside jokes—and I’m always the person who’s “nice to have around” but never the one anyone thinks to invite first. I tell myself I’m used to it, but it still stings every time I see photos of things I wasn’t even aware were happening.

I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off about me that other people can sense right away, some invisible sign that says “don’t get too close.”
