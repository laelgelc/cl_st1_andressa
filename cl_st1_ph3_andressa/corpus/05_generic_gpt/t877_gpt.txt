I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m slowly dissolving from everyone’s life.

On paper, I’m “fine.” I go to work, I answer messages, I show up when I’m supposed to. But everything feels like I’m playing a role someone else wrote. I’m there, but not really there. People talk to me, but it’s like they’re talking at a placeholder, not an actual person.

I have “friends,” but they mostly talk in group chats that I’m technically in but never really part of. If I stop messaging, it takes days before anyone notices, if they notice at all. I’m always the one asking “how are you?” and getting short answers, then nothing back.

I keep thinking if I disappeared, the first reaction would be confusion about who’s missing, not concern. Like I’ve become background noise in my own life.
