I’m starting to feel like a ghost in my own life. I talk to people every day—coworkers, cashiers, neighbors—but none of it feels real. It’s all small talk, like I’m playing a character who knows the right lines but never gets a real scene. I go home, scroll through feeds full of other people’s connections, then sit in silence. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually asked how I’m doing and meant it.
