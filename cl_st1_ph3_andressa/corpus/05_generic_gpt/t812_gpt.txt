Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone without anything dramatic actually happening. I have people in my life, but every conversation feels like small talk on repeat. I listen to others’ problems, crack a few jokes, and somehow that convinces them I’m “doing fine.” I don’t know how to say, “I’m lonely,” without sounding needy or childish.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives through a screen and feel like a ghost. I’m present, but not really part of anything. I keep thinking if I were more interesting, more something, I’d connect with people better. Instead I just keep shrinking my world down to my room, my phone, my thoughts.
