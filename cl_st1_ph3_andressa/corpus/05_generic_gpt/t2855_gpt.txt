I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people at work, I make small talk, I smile in the right places, but it all feels like I’m acting out a version of myself that no one actually knows.

I scroll through my phone at night, staring at conversations that faded months ago and group chats I was quietly left out of. I tell myself I could reach out, but the thought of sending a “hey, how have you been?” feels like begging for attention I’m not sure anyone wants to give.

What gets to me most is how ordinary I look from the outside. I’m functioning. I’m not in crisis. I get things done. But there’s this constant ache of not really belonging anywhere, like I’m always a guest in other people’s lives, never someone whose absence would actually change anything.

Sometimes I wonder if this is just how it’s going to be.
