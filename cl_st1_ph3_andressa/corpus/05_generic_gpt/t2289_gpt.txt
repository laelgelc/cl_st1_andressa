I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m surrounded by people most days – coworkers, neighbors, random small talk – but none of it feels like it actually touches me. It’s like I’m playing a character that everyone accepts, but no one knows the person underneath.

I keep thinking about how long it’s been since someone really asked me how I’m doing and meant it, or remembered a small detail I mentioned weeks ago. I miss being known, not just recognized.

I try to initiate conversations, suggest plans, but it often fizzles out or stays surface-level. After a while it starts to feel like I’m the only one trying, which makes me pull back even more. Then I wonder if I’m the problem, or if I’ve just somehow drifted off to the edge of everyone’s life without noticing when it happened.
