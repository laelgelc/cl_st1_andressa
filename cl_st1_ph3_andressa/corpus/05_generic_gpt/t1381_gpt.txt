I’m in that weird place where I’m surrounded by people but still feel completely alone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about movies or work, but it never goes past surface level. I go home, shut the door, and it’s like I vanish. My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from an app. 

What really gets me is feeling like I’m always the one reaching out, initiating, asking to hang out, and when I stop, everything just…stops. It makes me wonder if anyone actually thinks of me when I’m not right in front of them. 

I’ve started talking less, even when I’m with others, because I feel like I’m just background noise. I miss feeling important to someone, like my absence would actually be noticed.
