I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing. My phone is mostly silent unless it’s a notification from some app, never an actual person. I watch other people make plans, post photos, talk about “the group,” and I’m just… not part of anything. I try to reach out sometimes, but I feel like I’m forcing myself into spaces where I’m not really wanted. It’s embarrassing how much I replay old conversations, wondering what I did wrong, or if I was ever actually included in the first place.
