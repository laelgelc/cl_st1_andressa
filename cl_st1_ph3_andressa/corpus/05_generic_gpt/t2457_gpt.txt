I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is just… background noise.

I have people around me: coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family chats that light up my phone. But none of it feels like it actually reaches me. I’ll be in a group conversation and feel like I’m watching myself talk from a distance, saying the right things so no one notices how empty it feels.

What really scares me is how replaceable I feel. Like if I stopped reaching out, most people would just slowly forget I existed. Not out of cruelty, just because I’m not really woven into anyone’s life in a solid way. I’m the “oh yeah, we should hang out sometime” person that never quite makes it onto the calendar.

I miss feeling known. Not just listened to, but understood. The kind of connection where you don’t have to translate yourself or downplay things.

Lately I catch myself thinking that if this is all there is, I’m not sure what I’m supposed to be aiming for.
