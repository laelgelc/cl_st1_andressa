Lately I’ve been realizing that I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, I can be “polite,” but it all feels like I’m acting out a script instead of being a real person. When conversations start to get deeper, I panic and either overshare something weird or shut down completely. Then I replay everything later and cringe, which makes me avoid people even more.

Online it’s a bit easier, but even then I feel like everyone already has their own circles, their own inside jokes. I’m just some extra account in the comments. I’m not totally isolated in a dramatic way, I just feel permanently “adjacent” to everyone else’s lives. Close enough to see them, never close enough to feel like I belong.
