I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, some old friends I text occasionally, family who check in—but none of it feels like actual connection. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted the full answer.

I’ve tried joining groups, going to events, being “social,” but I always end up on the edge of things, like I’m watching everyone else from behind glass. I leave feeling more alone than when I arrived.

What scares me is how normal this is starting to feel. Like loneliness is just the background noise of my life now, and I’m not sure how to turn it down.
