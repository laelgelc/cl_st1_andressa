I’m starting to realize that I’m not “alone” in the obvious ways, but I still feel completely disconnected.

I have coworkers, I answer messages, I even go out sometimes. On paper it looks like I’m functioning. But every interaction feels like I’m playing a role I learned by watching other people, not something that comes naturally. I can talk about work, weather, memes, but when the conversation shifts to anything real, I freeze up inside.

I don’t really have anyone who knows what my day actually feels like, or what I’m scared of, or what I’m hoping for. I keep everything filtered because I’m afraid if I show too much of myself, people will quietly back away. So I stay “pleasant” and “easygoing” and then go home and feel this huge, dull ache like I’m missing some basic human instruction manual everyone else got.
