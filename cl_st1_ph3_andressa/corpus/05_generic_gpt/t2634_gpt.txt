I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk, joke, even seem social, but it feels like I’m acting out a script I never really learned. When I’m around others, I feel like an outsider watching myself from the corner of the room. When I’m alone, it’s like everything goes silent at once. I scroll, I distract myself, but there’s this heavy, dull ache that just sits there.
