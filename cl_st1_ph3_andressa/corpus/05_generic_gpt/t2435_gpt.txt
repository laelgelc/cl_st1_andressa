I’ve started to realize I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely alone. I have coworkers, a few acquaintances, family I talk to sometimes, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a version of myself that people can tolerate but not really know.

I crave the kind of connection where you can message someone randomly, say “today sucked,” and they just get it. Instead, I hesitate before sending anything, worried I’m being annoying or too much. So I stay quiet, then feel forgotten, and the cycle keeps repeating.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, watch other people’s lives happen, and it’s like I’m behind glass. I don’t even know if I’ve forgotten how to connect or if I ever really learned in the first place.
