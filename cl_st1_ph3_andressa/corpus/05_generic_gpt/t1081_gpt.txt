I don’t even know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family texts—but I still feel like I’m on mute in a room full of noise.

I keep trying to start conversations, send messages first, suggest plans, but everything feels one-sided and forced. When people cancel or leave me on read, I tell myself it’s not personal, but it still hits like proof that I’m forgettable.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, watch other people’s friendships and relationships play out online, and it just emphasizes how replaceable I seem. I’m tired of being “the nice one,” “the reliable one,” and still somehow the one nobody really knows.
