I’m starting to feel like I exist in the negative space of other people’s lives. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me or would notice if I disappeared for a while. I keep trying to be more social, but it feels like I’m always orbiting other people’s circles instead of being part of them.  

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel. I go days without a real conversation and somehow I’m getting used to it, like this is just what my life is now.
