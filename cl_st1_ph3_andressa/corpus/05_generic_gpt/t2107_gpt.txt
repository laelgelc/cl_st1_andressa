I’ve been feeling like I’m slowly fading out of everyone’s periphery. Friends don’t really reach out anymore unless I message first, and even then the conversations feel forced, like we’re all just going through the motions. I go through entire weekends barely speaking out loud, just scrolling, lurking, existing in the background.  

What messes with my head is that from the outside my life looks “fine”: job, roof, no major crises. But the quiet is heavy. I miss being someone’s first choice, or even second. Lately I’m starting to wonder if maybe I’m just easier to forget than I thought.
