I’m starting to wonder if I’ve forgotten how to be close to people.

I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers I joke with, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script I memorized a long time ago. When the interaction ends, it’s like someone turns the sound off and I’m back in my head again, replaying everything I said and finding new ways to feel stupid.

What I really miss is the feeling of being known. Not just “oh, that’s your favorite show” known, but the kind of knowing where someone can tell you’re not okay from a single text, where you don’t have to explain your whole life story every time you’re a bit off. I don’t have that. I’m not sure I ever really did.

I keep thinking it’s my fault. I cancel plans, I answer messages late, I tell myself I’m “tired” when really I’m scared of being awkward or rejected. Then I complain that no one is close to me. It’s like I’m building my own prison brick by brick and then crying about the walls.

I don’t even know what I want from posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head for a minute.
