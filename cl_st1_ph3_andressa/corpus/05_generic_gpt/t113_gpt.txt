Lately I feel like I’m existing next to people instead of with them. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels surface-level. I go home and there’s no one who really knows me, no one I can text without feeling like I’m bothering them. I keep wondering if I’m doing something wrong or if I’m just fundamentally forgettable. The days blur together and I’m not sure who would even notice if I quietly disappeared.
