I’m starting to feel like I don’t exist outside of other people’s convenience. Friends text when they’re bored, not because they miss me. If I don’t reach out, days pass in silence. I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep. Repeat. I try joining group chats, hobbies, whatever, but I always end up on the edge of things, half-invisible. People say “you should put yourself out there,” but I’m so tired of being the one who cares more than anyone else.
