I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. I’m surrounded by people at work, I answer messages, I laugh at jokes, but it all feels like I’m playing a character who’s loosely based on me. No one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I’m starting to doubt anyone ever will.

I try to open up sometimes, but it either feels like I’m oversharing or like I’m boring people. Then I replay every conversation in my head and convince myself I sounded needy or awkward. So I pull back again, and the distance just grows.

It’s not that I don’t have anyone around. It’s that the connections feel thin, like they’d snap if I leaned on them even a little. And I’m tired of feeling like a background extra in my own life.
