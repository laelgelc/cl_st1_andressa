I’ve hit this weird stage where I’m surrounded by people but feel completely disconnected from all of them. I go to work, make small talk, send memes in group chats, but it all feels like I’m playing a character. Nobody actually knows me beyond the “I’m fine, just tired” script.

When I get home, the silence is so loud it’s almost physical. I’ll put on YouTube or a podcast just to have voices in the background, but it doesn’t really help. I miss feeling chosen, like someone is genuinely glad I exist and wants to hear the boring details of my day.  

I don’t even know how to fix it anymore. I’m starting to forget what it felt like to really belong.
