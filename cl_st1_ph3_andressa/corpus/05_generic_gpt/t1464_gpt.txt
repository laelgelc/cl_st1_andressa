I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I chat with at work or online, but no one who really knows me. When something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and just…stop. There’s no one I feel comfortable bothering. I keep thinking it’s my fault—like I missed some class where everyone else learned how to be close to each other, and now I’m permanently behind.
