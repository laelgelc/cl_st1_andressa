I’m starting to realize that I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, seem “normal” for a bit, but it never goes deeper. It’s like I’m playing a character I don’t even like, just to avoid awkward silence.

Most days are a loop: work/school, home, phone, sleep. I scroll through group chats I’m in where the conversation moved on without me a long time ago. I hover over the text box, type something, delete it, because it feels like I’m forcing myself into a space where I’m not really wanted.

What messes with me is that I’m not technically alone. There are people around me. But I feel invisible next to them, like I’m behind a glass wall watching everyone else live. When I do try to open up, I either overshare and regret it, or I downplay everything and end up feeling more isolated.

I don’t even know what I’m looking for anymore: a friend, a partner, just someone who actually notices when I’m quiet. Lately it feels like if I disappeared from everyone’s life, it would mostly just be… convenient for them.
