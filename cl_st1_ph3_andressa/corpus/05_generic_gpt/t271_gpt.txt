Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, make small talk, answer emails, smile when I’m supposed to, but I don’t feel actually connected to anyone. When I get home, it’s just silence. I scroll through my phone, see people hanging out, getting married, having kids, and I feel like I got left behind without noticing when it happened.

I have acquaintances, but no one I could call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I don’t even know how to move friendships past that shallow level anymore. I worry that I’ve become boring or emotionally dull, like I forgot how to be close to people. It’s a strange mix of wanting to be seen and wanting to disappear.
