I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, a couple of old friends I text with every now and then—but I don’t feel *known* by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, memes, “how was your weekend.” I can’t remember the last time someone asked me how I really am and actually wanted the full answer.

Most evenings I just go home, scroll on my phone, maybe watch something, then go to bed. It’s like my life is just filler scenes between other people’s important moments. I try to reach out sometimes, but I always feel like I’m interrupting, like I’m an extra in their movie.

I miss feeling close to someone. I miss being able to message a person without overthinking whether I’m being annoying. I miss having someone who would notice if I went quiet for a while.
