Lately I’ve been realizing how lonely I feel even when I’m technically not “alone.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels surface-level, like I’m playing a role and no one actually knows me. I’ll be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect in a way I somehow missed learning.

I try to reach out, make plans, but either people are busy or it fizzles out after a couple of messages. Then I start overthinking every interaction: Did I talk too much? Not enough? Am I boring? Annoying?

Nights are the worst. The silence feels heavy, and I catch myself scrolling just to feel some kind of presence, but it never really fills the gap.
