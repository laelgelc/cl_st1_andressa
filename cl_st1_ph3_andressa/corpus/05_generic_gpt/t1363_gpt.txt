I’m starting to realize I don’t actually talk to anyone anymore, I just sort of orbit people. I show up to work, nod, make small jokes, and then go home to silence. My phone barely lights up unless it’s a notification from an app. When it does buzz, I get this weird rush of hope, then it’s spam or some random update.

I keep thinking I should reach out first, but I’m tired of feeling like I’m forcing myself into other people’s lives. I watch friendships happen around me like I’m behind glass. I don’t know when I became this background character. I miss feeling chosen, or even just noticed without having to earn it.
