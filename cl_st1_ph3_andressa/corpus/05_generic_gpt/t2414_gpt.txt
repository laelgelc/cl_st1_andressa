I feel like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but there’s no real connection anywhere. Friends drifted away over the years, and I never really replaced them. When I try to reach out, conversations feel forced, like I’m auditioning for a role I don’t remember learning. I overthink every message I send, then end up saying nothing. Most days, the silence in my apartment feels louder than any noise outside.
