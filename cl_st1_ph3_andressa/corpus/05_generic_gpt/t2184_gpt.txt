I don’t really know how to talk about this in real life, so I’m dumping it here.

Lately I feel like I’m living in a parallel universe where I can see everyone else connecting but can’t quite touch it myself. I have people around me – coworkers, a few acquaintances, family chats – but it all feels like background noise. I can go days without anyone asking me something that isn’t purely practical or surface-level. I answer, they answer, and then it’s like the conversation just dissolves.

What’s messing with my head is that from the outside I probably look “fine.” I show up where I’m supposed to, I make small jokes, I respond to messages. But I feel like a placeholder version of myself, like I’m standing in for the real person who never actually shows up.

I miss feeling known. Not just “oh you like this show” known, but the kind of knowing where someone notices you’re quieter than usual and actually cares why. I don’t have that. I’m the one people vent to, or ask for help, but when I try to open up even a little, it either gets brushed off or turned back around.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for here. I just wanted to say out loud that I’m lonely in a way that doesn’t make sense to the people around me. It’s like being homesick for a place I’ve never actually been.
