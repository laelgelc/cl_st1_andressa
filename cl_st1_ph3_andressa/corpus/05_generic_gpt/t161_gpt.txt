I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, pretend I’m fine, but it all feels like I’m reading from a script I barely remember writing. When conversations end, there’s this heavy silence afterward that feels louder than anything that was said.

Most of my “social life” is just scrolling, watching other people live out friendships and relationships like it’s the most natural thing in the world. I keep thinking there must have been a moment where I fell behind, but I can’t pinpoint when it happened. I just slowly stopped getting invited, stopped reaching out, and now it’s like I don’t exist outside of work and my room.

I’m surrounded by people every day, but it feels like I’m behind glass. I can see everything, but I can’t touch any of it.
