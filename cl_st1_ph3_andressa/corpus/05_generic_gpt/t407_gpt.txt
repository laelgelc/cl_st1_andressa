I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about shows or work, but no one I feel genuinely close to. I go home, make dinner, scroll my phone, and realize there’s no one I could text and say “today sucked, can we talk?” without it feeling like I’m burdening them.

I keep replaying old friendships in my head, wondering what I did wrong or when exactly the distance started. It’s like everyone else got the memo on how to maintain relationships and I missed that class. The silence in my apartment feels louder lately, and I’m not sure what to do with it anymore.
