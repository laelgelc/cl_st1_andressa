I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but no one I feel comfortable calling when everything feels heavy. I watch others make plans, share inside jokes, drift into their own little circles, and I’m always just… nearby, never really in it.

I’ve tried joining groups, messaging old friends, making small talk, but it all feels forced, like I’m playing a version of myself that’s easier to be around. When I get home, the silence feels louder because I used up all my “social energy” on conversations that didn’t mean anything. It makes me wonder if I’m the problem, or if this is just how adulthood quietly isolates people.
