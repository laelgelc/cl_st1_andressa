I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but nobody I’d call if something really bad happened. I keep conversations shallow because I’m scared if people see the real me, they’ll leave, but then I end up feeling invisible anyway. Weekends are the worst—just me, my phone, and that sinking feeling when no one messages first. It’s like I’m here, but not really part of anything.
