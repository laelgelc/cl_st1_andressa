Lately it feels like my life is happening behind glass. I go to work, make small talk, answer emails, and everyone assumes I’m fine because I can function. But I come home to silence and it’s like all the noise of the day just drops off a cliff. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives unfold in photos and comments, and I can’t figure out how they all seem to have these tight circles of friends and group chats. I’m not totally isolated, but everything feels surface-level, like I’m always the extra, never the person someone thinks of first. I miss feeling known. I don’t even know how to fix it anymore; I’m tired of being the one who always reaches out.
