I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in a way that feels like a personality trait now. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I feel like I’m behind glass. I can talk, joke, participate, but it never feels like anyone really sees me.  

I miss having someone who checks in unprompted, someone I don’t have to perform for. I overthink every message I send, then end up saying nothing. Days pass where the only real conversation I have is with myself in my head, and it’s exhausting.
