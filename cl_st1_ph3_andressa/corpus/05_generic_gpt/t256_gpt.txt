I feel like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else’s boring documentary. Friends drifted away, or maybe I did. I’m never the first person anyone thinks to invite, just an optional extra. Conversations stay shallow, and when I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward. It’s like I’m here physically, but socially and emotionally, I’m a ghost.
