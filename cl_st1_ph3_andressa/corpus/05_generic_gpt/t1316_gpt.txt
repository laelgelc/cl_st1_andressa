I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately.

I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but it’s like everything is happening behind glass. I’m “busy” all the time and somehow still incredibly alone. I have acquaintances, people I can joke with, but no one I feel safe being fully honest with. When something good happens, I don’t know who to text. When something bad happens, I just… don’t tell anyone.

What messes with my head is that I don’t look lonely from the outside. I show up, I smile, I make small talk. People tell me I seem “put together” or “independent.” Inside, I feel like I’m screaming into a void. I crave real connection so badly that it’s almost embarrassing. I want someone to actually notice when I disappear for a bit, to ask how I’m doing and really want to know.

I try to reach out sometimes, but conversations fade, plans get canceled, group chats go silent. After a while it starts to feel like I’m forcing myself into people’s lives where I’m not really wanted. So I retreat, then feel even more isolated, and the cycle repeats.

Mostly I just feel tired. Tired of pretending I’m fine, tired of feeling like an extra in everyone else’s story.
