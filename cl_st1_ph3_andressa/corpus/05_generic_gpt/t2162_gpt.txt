I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I go through my days doing all the normal things—work, errands, small talk—but it’s like I’m on mute. People see the surface version of me, but no one really knows what’s going on underneath.

What makes it worse is that I’m not completely isolated. I have acquaintances, a few people I can message, but I don’t feel emotionally close to anyone. I hesitate to reach out because I don’t want to seem needy or dramatic, so I just keep everything to myself and pretend I’m fine.

Nights are the hardest. When everything’s quiet, it hits me how long it’s been since I felt genuinely understood or truly connected to someone. It’s starting to feel less like a phase and more like the default.
