Lately it feels like my life is happening behind glass.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I can chat with about work or TV shows, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and realize most of them are people I haven’t spoken to in months, or people who only reach out when they need something.

I try to initiate plans, and sometimes people say yes, but it all feels surface-level. We meet, we talk, we laugh a bit, then everyone goes home to their “real” lives, and I go back to my apartment that’s too quiet. I’m there, physically, but I don’t feel like I actually exist in anyone’s mind once I’m out of sight.

What bothers me most is how invisible it makes me feel. I can go days without a message that isn’t work-related. When I’m in a group, I feel like background noise. And I honestly don’t know how to bridge the gap between “friendly” and actually feeling known.
