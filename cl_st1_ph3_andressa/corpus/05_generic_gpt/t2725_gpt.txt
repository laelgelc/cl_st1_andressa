I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, group chats, people I “should” be able to reach out to, but whenever I think about messaging them I convince myself I’d just be annoying. Days blur together: work, phone, sleep. I talk to almost no one outside of small talk. I miss being genuinely known by someone, but I also feel like I’ve forgotten how to let people in. It’s like I’m stuck watching my own life from the outside.
