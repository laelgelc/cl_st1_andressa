I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did. I have acquaintances, people I message sometimes, coworkers I chat with, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m not sure I’ve given anyone the chance.

When I do try to open up, I overthink every word, then replay the conversation later and cringe at myself. So I end up retreating again, waiting for someone else to make the effort, which they rarely do. I can go days without a real-time conversation that isn’t about work or logistics.

What messes with my head is that from the outside I probably look “fine.” But I feel like a ghost hovering around my own life, watching everyone else have these easy, natural connections that I can’t seem to access.
