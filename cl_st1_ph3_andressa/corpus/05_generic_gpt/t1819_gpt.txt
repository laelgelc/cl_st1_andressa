I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “around” people. I go to work, I chat with coworkers about projects and weekend plans, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that sort of looks like me. When the day ends, I go home and it’s just silence. Not peaceful silence, more like the kind that reminds you nobody’s really there.

What messes with my head is that I’m not completely isolated. I have a few people I could text, but I never feel like I’m anyone’s first choice. I’m the “if no one else is free” person. I scroll through chats where I used to talk to people more, and now it’s just me sending a message every few months, trying to revive something that’s clearly fading.

I miss having someone who actually knows what’s going on in my life without me having to summarize months of events. Someone who notices when I disappear for a bit. Lately I’ve started talking less because I feel like I’m always “too much” or “too boring,” and the less I talk, the less people ask. It’s like I’m slowly erasing myself from my own life and no one’s really noticing.
