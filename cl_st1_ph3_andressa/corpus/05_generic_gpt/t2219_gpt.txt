I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but nothing feels connected to anyone. Friends drifted away over the years, and I never really replaced them. I keep telling myself I’ll reach out, join something, start conversations, but when the moment comes, I freeze or convince myself they’re just being polite.

I’m surrounded by people all the time, yet I can’t shake the feeling that if I disappeared, it would take a while for anyone to really notice. I miss being known without having to explain myself from scratch every time.
