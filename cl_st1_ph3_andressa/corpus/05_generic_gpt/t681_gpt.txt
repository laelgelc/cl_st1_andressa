I don’t really know how to talk about this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’ve slowly disappeared from my own life.

I have people around me in the technical sense — coworkers, acquaintances, family chats — but I don’t feel *with* any of them. Conversations are surface-level: work, weather, memes. I can go days without anyone asking how I actually am, and I’ve realized I don’t really ask others either because I’m scared they’ll give me a real answer and I won’t know what to do with it.

I scroll through social media and see people posting group photos, inside jokes, “remember when we…” stories. I don’t have a “we.” I have a collection of almost-friendships that never quite deepen, like I’m always the extra chair at someone else’s table.

What scares me is how normal it’s starting to feel. I’ve started to lose the urge to reach out because the silence afterward feels worse than not trying at all. I’m not sure when being alone turned into feeling like I’m not even visible.
