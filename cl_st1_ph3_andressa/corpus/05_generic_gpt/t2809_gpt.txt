I’ve been realizing lately that I can be surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like I’m playing a role rather than actually being known.

What gets to me most is how small my life feels outside of obligations. When the workday ends, there’s no one to text just to share something random, no one who’d notice if I went quiet for a few days. I scroll through my phone and realize there’s no one I feel close enough to reach out to without overthinking it.

I’m tired of being “the reliable one” or “the quiet one” without anyone seeing what’s underneath. I miss having deep conversations where time disappears, or just sitting in silence with someone and not feeling like I have to perform.

Lately I catch myself wondering if maybe I’ve already missed the window where people form those lasting bonds, and now I’m just orbiting other people’s lives without really being part of them.
