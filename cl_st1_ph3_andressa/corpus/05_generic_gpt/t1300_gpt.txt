I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s peripheral vision. People still reply to my messages, but it’s always delayed, surface-level, like I’m a chore they’re getting to. I scroll through group chats where I used to be active and now I’m just… observing. I don’t think anyone would notice if I stopped talking altogether, except maybe when they need something. I try to initiate plans, conversations, anything, and when it falls flat I end up feeling embarrassed for even trying. It’s like I’m present but not really there.
