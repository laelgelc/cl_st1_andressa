I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if I even know how to be known anymore.  

I try to start conversations, but they stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. The moment I think about saying something real, it feels awkward and I shut down. Then I go home, scroll, lurk, and watch other people’s lives move forward.  

It’s like I’m stuck behind glass, watching myself be “fine” while quietly realizing I’m not.
