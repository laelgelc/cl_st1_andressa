I’ve started to realize that most of my “social life” is just background noise. Group chats that I never type in, Discord servers where I’m always on mute, scrolling through feeds full of people I barely talk to. I’m surrounded by constant connection and still feel completely separate from everyone.

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like an extra—physically there, but not really part of anything. Conversations move too fast, inside jokes I don’t know, people pairing off while I’m checking my phone to look busy.

What bothers me most is how invisible it all is. From the outside, I probably look fine. But I go home, close the door, and it’s just…quiet.
