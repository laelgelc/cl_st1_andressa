I’ve started to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely. My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from some app. I scroll through group photos of people I used to know and feel like I’ve slipped out of everyone’s lives without them noticing.

I try to initiate conversations, but they fizzle out after a few messages. It’s like I’m easy to ignore. I go to work, come home, put something on in the background, and just exist in this weird quiet.

What scares me most is how normal it’s starting to feel. The longer I’m alone, the harder it is to remember what it was like to be genuinely seen and wanted.
