I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I go through the motions—work, small talk, errands—but nothing ever feels like it connects. Friends are “busy,” group chats are quiet when I speak, and when I finally get invited somewhere, I feel like a guest star in everyone else’s lives. I try to initiate plans and feel annoying, so I stop. Then the silence feels like proof that I don’t really matter to anyone. It’s exhausting pretending this doesn’t hurt as much as it does.
