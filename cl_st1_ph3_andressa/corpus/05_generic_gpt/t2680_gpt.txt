I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with. Online friends I game with sometimes. But when it comes to actually saying “I’m lonely” or “can we talk for a bit,” my brain shuts down. I either overshare and feel ashamed afterward, or I stay on the surface and go home feeling weirdly invisible.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, maybe type something and delete it. I’ll see people casually mentioning hanging out, calling a friend, having someone who checks in. I can’t remember the last time my phone lit up with a message that wasn’t a notification or something work-related.

What messes with me is that I look “normal” from the outside. People assume I’m fine because I can hold a conversation. But when the day ends, it’s just me, my thoughts, and this heavy quiet that feels like it’s getting thicker every year.
