I’ve been feeling this weird, heavy kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really match my life on paper. I have coworkers I chat with, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family I see on holidays. But I still feel like I’m watching my own life from the outside.

Conversations feel shallow, like we’re all reading from scripts: work, weather, memes, “we should hang out sometime.” I can’t remember the last time I said exactly what I was thinking without editing it down into something lighter, less “too much.” I’m constantly afraid that if people really saw how empty and disconnected I feel, they’d quietly step back.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone just to see proof that I exist in someone’s messages, even if it’s just a thumbs-up reaction from days ago. I’ll type something vulnerable and then delete it, because I don’t want to be the person who “makes things weird.”

I don’t know if I’m lonely because I can’t connect, or if I can’t connect because I’m lonely. It just feels like I’m missing whatever everyone else seems to have figured out.
