I’ve hit this weird point where I’m never technically “alone,” but I feel completely disconnected from everyone. I go to work, make small talk, answer messages, scroll through group chats, and somehow it all feels like I’m watching someone else’s life from the outside.

I can’t remember the last time I said what I was actually thinking instead of what sounded acceptable or light. I keep conversations shallow because I’m scared that if I show the real stuff—how empty and replaceable I feel—people will quietly back away.

It’s confusing because nothing is dramatically wrong. It’s just this constant, low-level ache of not really belonging anywhere, of knowing that if I disappeared from most people’s lives, their routines would stay exactly the same.
