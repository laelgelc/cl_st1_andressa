I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really bad happened. I scroll through my phone and there’s no one I feel comfortable reaching out to without overthinking it.

Most of my day is spent in my own head. I replay conversations, analyze every word I said, and convince myself I was awkward or annoying. Then I pull back, which just makes the distance worse. Weekends are the hardest—everyone seems busy with their own lives, and I’m just… background noise. I’m not sure if I’ve isolated myself or if I was never really included in the first place.
