I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life in slow motion. Messages get left on read, plans “fall through,” and after a while I stopped suggesting anything because the silence afterward hurts more than the loneliness itself. I go through entire days where my voice is only used for work or small talk. No one really knows what my life looks like anymore, and I’m not sure anyone cares to ask. It’s like being a background character in my own story.
