I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore, or maybe I never did.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but nobody who really knows me. I’ll have a decent conversation with someone, then go home and replay it in my head, convinced I said something weird and they secretly don’t like me. So I pull back, answer slower, stop initiating. Then I’m lonely again and blame myself.

Weekends are the worst. I scroll through chats I never start, stare at my phone like it’s going to ring out of nowhere. I tell myself I’m “resting” or “introverting” but it feels more like hiding. Even when I’m in a group, I feel like an extra in the background of everyone else’s lives.

What messes with me is that I’m not even sure what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing. “I’m lonely” feels too big and too small at the same time.
