It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel like you’re behind glass.

I have friends, technically. Group chats, occasional hangouts, people who’d say they care. But the connection feels…thin. Surface-level. I listen, I joke, I nod, and then I go home and it’s like I was never really there.

I keep thinking it’s my fault for not opening up more, but when I do, conversations die or get redirected. So I end up sharing less each time. I scroll, I lurk, I watch everyone else’s “real lives” pass by.

I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own story.
