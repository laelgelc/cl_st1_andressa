I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold small talk, crack a joke, seem “fine,” but it all feels like acting. When conversations end, there’s this heavy silence that hits harder than before. My phone barely lights up unless it’s spam or work, and I keep telling myself I like being alone, but it’s starting to feel less like a choice and more like a default.

I watch other people form friendships so naturally, like they’re speaking a language I never learned. I overthink every message I send, then end up not sending anything. I’m scared I’ve missed some window in life where you’re supposed to build your circle, and now I’m just orbiting everyone else.
