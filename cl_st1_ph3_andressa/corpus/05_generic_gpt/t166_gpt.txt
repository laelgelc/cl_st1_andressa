I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind a glass wall. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but no one I’d actually call if something really bad or really good happened. I’m always “nice,” “easy to talk to,” but somehow that never turns into anything deeper.

I keep replaying old friendships that faded and wondering what I did wrong, or if I was just never that important to them. It feels like everyone else is moving on with partners, families, tight friend groups, and I’m stuck in this holding pattern of small talk and silence.

Most nights I just scroll, hoping some notification will make me feel less invisible. It usually doesn’t. The worst part is how normal I look from the outside, like nothing is missing at all.
