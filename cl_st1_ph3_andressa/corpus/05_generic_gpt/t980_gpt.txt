I’m starting to feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels like background noise with no one actually there. I have acquaintances, people I can chat with about shows or memes, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. 

What bothers me most is how hard it is to admit this out loud. Loneliness feels embarrassing, like it’s proof that I’m somehow defective. I watch other people slide so easily into friendships and relationships, and I can’t figure out what invisible step I keep missing. 

I’m tired of pretending I’m fine when every quiet moment just reminds me how alone I am.
