I keep realizing how replaceable I am to everyone in my life. People seem happy enough to talk to me when I’m right in front of them, but the second I’m not physically there, I just… disappear from their minds. No texts, no invites, no “how are you?” unless I initiate. I’ve tried being more social, more interesting, more “low maintenance,” but it doesn’t change anything. I’m starting to feel like a background character in my own life, watching everyone else connect while I fade into static.
