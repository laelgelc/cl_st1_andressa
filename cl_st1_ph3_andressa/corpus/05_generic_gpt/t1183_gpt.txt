I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels like acting. I have acquaintances, people I can talk to about TV shows or work drama, but no one I feel truly close to. I miss the feeling of being known without having to explain everything from scratch. 

What makes it worse is that I’m starting to forget how to connect. Small talk feels exhausting, and deeper conversations feel impossible to initiate. So I just stay quiet and let the days blur together.
