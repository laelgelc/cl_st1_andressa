I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, groceries—but there’s no real sense of being known by anyone. I have acquaintances, people I can joke with, but no one who actually checks in or notices when I disappear for a few days.

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel in group settings. I can be sitting at a table with people I’ve known for years and still feel like I’m on the outside looking in. I try to contribute, but my words land flat or get talked over. Then I go home and replay every interaction, wondering if I’m just fundamentally uninteresting or easy to forget.
