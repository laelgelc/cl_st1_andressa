I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I keep conversations surface-level because I’m scared if people see the real me, they’ll leave. At the same time, the shallow stuff feels exhausting. It’s like I’m always half-present, half-watching myself from the outside, wondering why everyone else seems to know how to be human except me.
