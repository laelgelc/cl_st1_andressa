I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel completely disconnected. I go to work, make small talk, send memes in group chats, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that kind of looks like me. When I get home, the silence is so loud it almost feels physical. I scroll, I watch shows, I lurk here, but I don’t really *talk* to anyone about anything real.  

What scares me most is how used to it I’m becoming. Like I’m slowly forgetting how to be close to someone, or how to let them be close to me. It’s like I’m fading out of my own life in slow motion.
