I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too exposed at the same time. I’m around people most days—coworkers, neighbors, random small talk—but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s my fault or just how life is now.

I used to have close friends. We’d stay up late talking about everything and nothing. Now our conversations are mostly “we should catch up soon” texts that never turn into anything. I watch their lives move forward through Instagram stories while mine feels like it’s stuck in buffering.

I try to put myself out there—join things, say yes to invites—but I always end up feeling like the extra person in the group, the one who could disappear and the dynamic wouldn’t change. It’s exhausting pretending it doesn’t bother me when it really, really does.
