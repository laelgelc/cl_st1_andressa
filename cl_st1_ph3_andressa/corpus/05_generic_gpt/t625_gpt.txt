I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk at work, I can joke around in group chats, but it all feels like I’m playing a character that people sort of like, while the real me is sitting in the back, untouched.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone, staring at conversations that fizzled out months ago, wondering if I was the one who let them die. I keep telling myself I’m “busy,” but the truth is I’m just scared of being the one who cares more.

I miss being someone’s first choice. Or at least feeling like I mattered enough that my absence would be noticed.
