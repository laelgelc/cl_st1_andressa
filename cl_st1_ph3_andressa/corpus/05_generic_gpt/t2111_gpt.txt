I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but nobody I’d call if something truly bad happened. I scroll through my contacts and there’s no one I feel close enough to disturb.

I’ve tried joining groups, going to events, chatting online, but everything feels like acting. I can be funny, attentive, “interesting,” yet it never moves past surface level. People seem to have their circles already, and I’m the extra piece that doesn’t fit into the puzzle.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. I fill the silence with podcasts, YouTube, anything, so I don’t have to hear my own thoughts. I don’t know if I’m lonely because I’m different, or if I’ve just slowly forgotten how to be with other people.
