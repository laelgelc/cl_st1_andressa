I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I watch other people slip so easily into friendships and group chats and weekend plans, and I feel like I’m behind glass, watching my own life from the outside. I second-guess every message I send, every invite, until I talk myself out of it. Then I sit alone and pretend I chose this.
