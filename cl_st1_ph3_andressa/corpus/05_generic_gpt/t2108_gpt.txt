Lately I feel like I’m living in this weird in-between space where I’m technically “not alone,” but I still feel completely disconnected. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I message sometimes, but it all feels surface-level. I go home, sit in my room, and it’s like the silence gets heavier every day. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen through screens, and mine feels like it’s on pause. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I am. I’m starting to forget what real closeness is supposed to feel like.
