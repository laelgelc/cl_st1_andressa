I keep realizing how replaceable I am in other people’s lives.

Friends hang out and I see it later on their stories. I’m “invited next time,” but next time never comes. Group chats go quiet for me, but I can see them typing to each other. I’m not even mad, just… sidelined.

I’ve tried joining clubs, talking more at work, saying yes to plans. I show up, people are polite, we laugh, and then it never turns into anything deeper. They go back to their circles; I go back home.

What messes with me most is that I’m not isolated physically. I’m surrounded by people all the time, yet it feels like I’m watching life through glass. I exist in the background of everyone else’s story, and I don’t know how to step into my own.
