It’s weird how I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of old friends I text occasionally, but it all feels shallow, like we’re just exchanging updates, not actually connecting. I miss feeling known. I overthink every interaction, worry I’m boring or too much, and end up retreating. Then I sit alone, scrolling, wondering if anyone is quietly feeling this invisible too.
