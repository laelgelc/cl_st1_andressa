I’m starting to realize how much of my life happens in silence. I go through entire days where the only words I say out loud are “thank you” to a cashier or “excuse me” when I’m in someone’s way. I have people online I chat with, but when I close the apps, it’s just me and the walls again.

What messes with my head is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped messaging, I don’t think anyone’s routine would really change. I’m “nice,” “easy to talk to,” but no one actually reaches out first. I’m tired of being the supporting character in everyone else’s life while feeling like an extra in my own.
