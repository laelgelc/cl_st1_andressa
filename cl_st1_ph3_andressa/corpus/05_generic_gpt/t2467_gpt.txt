I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about work, random facts, even make people laugh sometimes, but anything deeper feels like there’s a glass wall between me and everyone else. I watch people fall into friendships so naturally, like it’s breathing, and for me it’s this awkward, conscious effort that never quite lands.

I overthink every message I send, then usually delete it. I replay conversations for days, picking apart every pause and weird phrasing. The result is I just… stop trying. Then I sit alone and wonder why no one reaches out, even though I’ve made myself almost impossible to reach.
