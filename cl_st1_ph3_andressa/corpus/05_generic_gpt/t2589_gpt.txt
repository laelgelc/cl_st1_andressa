I’m starting to realize I’m not just “a bit introverted,” I’m actually lonely in a way that’s starting to feel like a personality trait.

My days are weirdly full and completely empty at the same time. I go to work, I answer emails, I make small talk about weather and TV shows, and then I come home and it’s like I vanish. My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from an app. When I scroll through my contacts, I see names but no one I feel like I can text without overthinking it.

What bothers me most is how invisible it feels. I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m standing behind glass. I laugh at the right moments, say the right things, and no one notices that my mind is just looping on, “You don’t really belong here.”

I’ve also started lying when people ask what I’m doing on the weekend. I say “meeting friends” or “going out” when really I’m just trying to make the hours pass without thinking too hard about any of this.
