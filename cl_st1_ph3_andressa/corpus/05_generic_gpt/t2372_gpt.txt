I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing. Conversations feel like I’m an extra in the background, nodding along while everyone else actually connects. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but no one I could call at 2 a.m. if I completely fell apart. I keep trying to be “easy to be around,” not a burden, and somehow that just makes me more invisible. It’s like I’m always nearby, but never really *with* anyone.
