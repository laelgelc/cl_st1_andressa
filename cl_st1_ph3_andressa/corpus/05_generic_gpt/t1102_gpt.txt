I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I go through my day surrounded by people, but it all feels like background noise, like I’m watching someone else’s life from behind glass. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about weather, shows, “how was your weekend,” but nobody I can text when something really hits me at 2 a.m.

What scares me is how used to it I’m getting. I don’t even try to reach out much anymore because I’m tired of feeling like the one who cares more. I scroll through social media and see everyone else’s group photos and inside jokes, and it just highlights how replaceable I must be. I don’t know if it’s me pushing people away or if I was never really “in” to begin with.
