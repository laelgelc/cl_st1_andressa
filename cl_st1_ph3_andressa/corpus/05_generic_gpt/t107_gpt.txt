I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was really wrong. I’m always the one who checks in first, and when I stop, everything just goes quiet.  

I watch other people make plans around me, not with me. It’s like I’m physically present but socially invisible, and I’m scared this is just how it’s going to be.
