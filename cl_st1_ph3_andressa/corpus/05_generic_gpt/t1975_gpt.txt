I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like my life is happening behind glass.

I have people around me — coworkers, family, the occasional friend I see every few weeks — but I still feel completely disconnected. I can do the small talk, the “how’s work?” and “what are you watching on Netflix?” stuff, but anything deeper just dies in my throat. I watch myself from the outside, nodding and smiling, and inside I’m just…numb and tired.

What scares me is how normal this has started to feel. I wake up, go through the day, scroll on my phone, and then lie in bed with this heavy feeling in my chest that I can’t put into words when anyone asks what’s wrong. So I say “I’m just tired” and change the subject.

I miss feeling genuinely close to someone, like I could text them anything without rehearsing it ten times. I miss being understood without having to over-explain. I’m surrounded by noise all day, but it’s like no one can actually hear me.
