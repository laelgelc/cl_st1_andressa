I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life without sounding dramatic, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m surrounded by people but feel like a ghost. I have acquaintances, coworkers, family chats, but there’s no one I feel truly close to. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. No one asks how I really am, and honestly, I don’t think I’d know how to answer if they did.

I watch other people slip into friendships so easily, like it’s breathing. For me it’s this awkward performance where I’m constantly monitoring myself: Am I talking too much? Too little? Do they actually like me or are they just being polite? By the time I get home I’m exhausted from pretending I’m fine.

Nights are the worst. The silence feels heavy, like it’s pressing on my chest. I keep my phone nearby, hoping someone will message first. It almost never lights up.
