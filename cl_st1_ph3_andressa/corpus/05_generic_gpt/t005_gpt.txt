I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m fading out of my own life.

I have people around me technically—coworkers, some acquaintances, family I text occasionally—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, memes. I can be in a room full of people and feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else connect while I’m just… there.

I’ve tried joining clubs, going to events, forcing myself to be “social,” but it all feels like I’m acting out a version of myself that people might tolerate, not the real one. When I do try to open up a bit more, it either gets brushed off or I feel like I’ve overshared and scared them away.

It’s like I’m craving intimacy and dreading it at the same time. I keep wondering if this is just how my life is going to be.
