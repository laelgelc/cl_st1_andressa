It’s weird how I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of “friends,” but everything feels surface-level and scripted. I listen to people talk about their weekends, their relationships, their plans, and I just nod along, adding little comments, but no one really asks about me in a way that feels genuine.  

I’ve started to notice how quiet my phone is when I don’t initiate. Days can go by without a single message unless it’s about work or logistics. I’m tired of being the one who reaches out, the one who suggests meeting up.  

Sometimes I wonder if I’m just fundamentally forgettable, like background noise people only notice when it’s gone.
