I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my family group chats, but I still feel like I’m on the outside looking in. I try to start conversations and they fizzle out. When I stop reaching out, no one seems to notice. Nights are the worst — scrolling, watching other people’s lives, feeling like I’m missing some basic skill everyone else learned. I’m tired of my only real company being my own thoughts.
