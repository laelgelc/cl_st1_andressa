I’ve been feeling this weird hollow space around me lately, like everyone else is moving through life with invisible strings connecting them to people, and mine just…never got tied.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about TV shows or the weather. But it never goes deeper. No one really knows me, not in the way where you can just show up at their place unannounced and it’s fine, or text them something unfiltered and not worry you’re being “too much.” I edit myself constantly because I’m scared that if I show the real version, they’ll decide I’m not worth the effort.

I scroll through group photos on social media and feel like I’m looking at a world I somehow missed the instructions for. People talk about “my best friend,” “my group,” “my person.” I don’t have any of that. I’m always the extra, the temporary, the one they like but don’t think to invite.

What really gets to me is the silence at night. No messages, no calls, no one checking in. If I disappeared from the internet for a week, I’m honestly not sure who would notice, or how long it would take.

I don’t even know what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe just to put it somewhere outside my own head for once.
