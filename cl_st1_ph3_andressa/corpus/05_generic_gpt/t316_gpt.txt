I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. Like I take up space physically, but socially I’m basically not there.

I have people around me — coworkers, acquaintances, even some old friends I still text sometimes — but it all feels like background noise. Conversations stay on the surface. I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually wanted the full answer, not just “I’m fine.”

When I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward. Then I replay it later and cringe, and it just makes me want to shut down more. It’s like I’m stuck between wanting connection so badly and being terrified of being “too much” for anyone.

Nights are the worst. That’s when it hits that no one is really “for” me. No one to message something small and stupid to, no one who’d notice if I went quiet for a while. I keep wondering if this is just how it’s going to be.
