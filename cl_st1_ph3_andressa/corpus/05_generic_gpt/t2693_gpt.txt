I’m starting to feel like I’ve become invisible in my own life. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling on my phone—but I can’t remember the last time someone asked how I really am and actually waited for the answer. I have acquaintances, group chats, people who’ll “like” things, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. I miss feeling known. It’s like I’m surrounded by noise but living in complete silence inside.
