I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, small jokes. No one asks deeper questions, and I’m starting to forget how to answer them anyway.

I scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one reaching out. When I stop, everything goes silent. It’s like I’m testing my own existence by seeing how long it takes for someone to notice I’ve gone quiet. So far, the answer is: they don’t. I’m tired of pretending this is enough, but I don’t know what else to do with this emptiness.
