I’m starting to realize I’m not sure what “connection” even feels like anymore. I talk to people at work, I respond in group chats, I nod and smile in all the right places, but it’s like I’m acting out a script. No one really knows me, and I don’t even know how to let them. Every time I think about opening up, I imagine the awkward silence afterward and just… don’t. So I stay in my head instead.
