I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, seem “normal” for a while, but it all feels like acting. As soon as the conversation ends, there’s this heavy silence that feels louder than anything. I watch other people slip so easily into friendships and group chats and late-night calls, and I feel like I’m behind some invisible glass. I want to be known, but I’m terrified that if I show who I really am, there’ll just be more distance, not less.
