I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about weather, TV, deadlines. But no one I can text “I’m not okay” without rehearsing it ten times and then deleting it. I scroll through old conversations just to remember what it felt like when someone was actually interested in me.

Weekends are the worst. I lie about being “busy” when people ask how things are, because saying “I did nothing and spoke to no one” sounds like a personal failure. I’m tired of my own company, but also terrified of being seen and then rejected.

It feels like I’m slowly fading into the background of my own life.
