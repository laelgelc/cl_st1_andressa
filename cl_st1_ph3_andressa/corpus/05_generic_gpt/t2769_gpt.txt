I don’t even know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m disappearing in slow motion.

I talk to people every day – coworkers, family group chats, the usual small talk – but nothing ever feels like it lands. It’s like I’m always playing a role and nobody actually knows the person underneath. I try to share a bit more and it either gets brushed off or misunderstood, so I retreat again.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives through screens and feel like a ghost pressing my face against the glass. I keep thinking, “If I vanished tomorrow, who would really notice beyond a brief inconvenience?” And I honestly don’t know the answer.
