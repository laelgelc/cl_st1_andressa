I’ve been feeling like a ghost in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, errands—but it all feels like I’m watching someone else on a screen. I don’t really talk to anyone outside of surface-level stuff. Even when I’m around people, I feel weirdly separate, like there’s a glass wall between us.

I keep thinking I should “just reach out,” but I don’t know who to reach out to anymore. Old friends drifted away, new connections never really stick. I’m scared that if I’m honest about how lonely I am, I’ll sound needy or pathetic, so I keep it to myself and act “fine.”

Nights are the worst. The silence feels heavy, and it hits me how forgettable I probably am to most people.
