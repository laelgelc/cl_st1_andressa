I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and “too much” at the same time. Like I take up space, but not the kind anyone actually notices. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, family chats—but it all feels like background noise instead of connection.

I keep wondering if there’s something fundamentally off about me that other people can sense. Conversations feel like interviews I’m failing. I replay everything I say, convinced I sounded awkward, annoying, or boring. Then I pull back, which obviously doesn’t help, but in the moment it feels safer than risking more rejection.

What messes with my head is how normal my life looks from the outside. Job, roof over my head, “fine” when people ask how I am. But I come home and it’s just silence and my own thoughts echoing back at me. The days blur together. I’m not sure when “being alone a lot” turned into “I don’t feel truly known by anyone,” but that’s where I am.
