I’m starting to feel like I’ve “aged out” of making friends.

I’m in my 30s, work remotely, and most of my old friends have moved away, coupled up, or just drifted. We still “like” each other’s stuff, but that’s about it. I can go days where the only voice I hear is my own.

I try to join things—meetups, hobby groups—but everyone already seems to have their circles. I’m pleasant enough to chat with, but it never goes beyond surface-level small talk. No one texts later. No one says, “We should hang out.”

What really gets to me is the silence at night. It’s not dramatic, just this steady, dull awareness that no one really knows me right now.
