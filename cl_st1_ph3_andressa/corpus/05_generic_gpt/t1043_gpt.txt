I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but no one I feel safe being fully honest with. Whenever I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward, so I pull back and keep it surface-level.

The weird part is I’m surrounded by people most days, yet I still feel like I’m watching life from behind glass. I see friends posting group photos, trips, inside jokes, and I’m always the one reacting, never included. I’m not sure if I’m the problem or just forgettable, but it’s getting harder to pretend it doesn’t bother me.
