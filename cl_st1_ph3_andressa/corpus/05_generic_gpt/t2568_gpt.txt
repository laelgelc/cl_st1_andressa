I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, seem “normal,” but it all feels like I’m playing a role I never learned properly. I watch other people slide into friendships so easily, like it’s an instinct, and I’m stuck overthinking every text, every silence. I have contacts in my phone but no one I feel safe bothering. Even when I’m around others, I feel like an extra in a movie, not part of the cast.
