I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something was wrong. When I’m with others I can play the part, laugh, be “fine,” but the second I’m alone it feels like I drop off a cliff emotionally.

I see people my age forming tight friend groups, moving in together, getting married, having kids. I’m just… orbiting around all of that, watching. I keep wondering if there was some stage in life where everyone else learned how to build lasting connections and I just missed that class.
