I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people. I can talk, joke, be “fine” on the surface, but it feels like I’m acting out a version of myself I don’t believe in. When the conversation ends, it’s like I disappear again. I watch other people form friendships so naturally while I overthink every message I send. I’m scared that if anyone saw the real, quiet, awkward me, they’d just slowly drift away like everyone else has.
