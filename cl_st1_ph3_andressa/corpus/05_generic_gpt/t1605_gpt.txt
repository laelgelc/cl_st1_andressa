I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m surrounded by people on paper – coworkers, family chats, a couple of old friends I text every now and then – but I feel completely disconnected. Conversations feel like small talk on autopilot. I say the right things, laugh at the right moments, and the whole time it feels like I’m watching myself from outside.

The weirdest part is how invisible the loneliness is. From the outside my life looks “fine”: job, roof over my head, I’m not totally isolated. But nobody actually knows what’s going on in my head. I don’t even remember the last time I was fully honest with someone about how I’m really doing.

I keep thinking about how easy it would be to disappear from everyone’s daily life. Not dramatically, just… slowly stop reaching out, and see how long it takes anyone to notice.
