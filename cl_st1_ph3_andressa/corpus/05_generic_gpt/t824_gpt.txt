I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too visible at the same time. Like, people see me, but they don’t actually *see* me. I show up to work, I make small talk, I answer “I’m fine” automatically, and then I go home and it’s like I fall off the edge of the world.

I have acquaintances, not friends. People I can joke with about shows or complain about the weather, but no one I’d feel comfortable texting at 2 a.m. when my chest is tight and my thoughts won’t shut up. I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I can reach out to without feeling like I’m bothering them or crossing some line that doesn’t really exist but feels very real.

What messes with me is that from the outside, my life probably looks okay. I have a job, a place to live, I’m not completely socially inept. But there’s this constant quiet in my life that doesn’t feel peaceful, just empty. Even when I’m around people, I feel like there’s a glass wall between us.

Sometimes I wonder if this is just how it’s going to be—going through the motions, surrounded by people yet still somehow on my own.
