I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about work or random stuff, but no one I can text when something good or bad happens. My phone is just silent unless it’s a notification from some app.

When I do get invited somewhere, I feel like an outsider watching everyone else already have their own history, inside jokes, shared memories. I’m there physically, but it’s like I’m behind glass. I don’t know how to move from small talk to something real without feeling like I’m being weird or needy.

I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but when I do try, it feels forced and awkward. Then I overthink every word I said and end up pulling back again, which just makes the whole thing worse.
