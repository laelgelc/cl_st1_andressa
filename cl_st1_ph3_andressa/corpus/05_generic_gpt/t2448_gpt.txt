I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have coworkers, a few acquaintances, group chats that light up sometimes, but I still feel like I’m behind glass watching everyone else live. When something good or bad happens, there’s no one I instinctively want to tell. I overthink every message, every silence, until I talk myself out of reaching out at all. Then I sit with this heavy, quiet feeling and wonder if this is just how my life is going to be.
