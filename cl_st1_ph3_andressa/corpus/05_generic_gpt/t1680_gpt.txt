I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. I go home and there’s just this heavy silence that almost feels physical. I scroll, I lurk, I “like” things, but I don’t really exist to anyone in a way that matters.

I’m scared that the longer this goes on, the weirder I’ll seem if I try to reach out. It feels like everyone else already formed their circles and I missed the deadline. So I keep drifting, pretending I’m fine, and I don’t know what would actually change this.
