I’m starting to feel like I’ve aged out of connection.

I’m not “old,” but it seems like everyone my age is busy with partners, kids, careers, or friend groups that are already solid. I’m just… extra. I hang out on the edges of things: group chats I barely talk in, work events where I smile and nod, family gatherings where no one really asks how I’m doing beyond small talk.

I crave the kind of friendship where you can text someone nothing and they still get it. Instead, I second-guess every message, every invite, every silence. It’s exhausting watching life happen around you, like pressing your face against the glass of a house you’re not invited into.
