I’ve hit this weird point where I’m surrounded by people but still feel like I don’t actually exist to anyone. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it never goes deeper. No one checks in first. If I stopped reaching out, I’m not sure anyone would notice for a long time. I’m tired of being “easy to talk to” but never really known. It feels like I’m always the background character in everyone else’s story.
