I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but it all feels like acting. I can play the role of “normal person” for a while, then I go home and it’s just silence. The moment I close my door, it’s like I stop existing for everyone else.

I’ve tried to reach out, but conversations die quickly. People are “busy,” or they respond hours or days later with short replies that don’t go anywhere. I scroll through chats where I’m always the one who sent the last message. It makes me feel like I’m forcing myself into people’s lives.

What gets to me is how invisible this all is. From the outside, I probably look fine. I show up, I smile, I get things done. But there’s this constant heaviness in my chest, like I’m watching life through glass.

I don’t even know what I’m asking for. I just needed to say it somewhere.
