I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and realize I’m mostly there as a convenience to others. I’m “fun” or “helpful,” but not essential. It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely unanchored.
