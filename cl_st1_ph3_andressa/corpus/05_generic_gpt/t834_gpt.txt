Lately I feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life, including my own. I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling—but nothing feels connected. I have people around me, but conversations stay on the surface, like we’re all afraid to go deeper. I miss feeling known. Even when I try to reach out, I overthink every word and usually delete the message. It’s exhausting to feel this alone while technically not being alone at all.
