I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have coworkers, classmates, acquaintances, but it all feels like background noise. I can joke, make small talk, even seem “social,” yet the second I’m alone it’s like none of it counted. No one texts first. If I stop reaching out, everything just…stops.

What scares me is how used to it I’m getting. Nights blur into each other: scrolling, watching something, pretending I’m just “tired” instead of lonely. I overthink every interaction, wondering if I said something weird, if that’s why people keep me at arm’s length.

I don’t even know what I’m looking for anymore—friend, partner, just someone who’d actually notice if I disappeared for a while.
