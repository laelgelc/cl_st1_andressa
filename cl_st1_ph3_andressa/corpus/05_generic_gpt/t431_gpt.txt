I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like I’m living next to my own life instead of inside it.

On paper I’m “fine.” I have a job, a place to live, people I can technically text. But I move through my days like I’m underwater. Conversations are surface-level: work, memes, “how was your weekend?” I can talk, joke, function, but I don’t feel known by anyone. Not really.

I’ll have these moments where I’m surrounded by people and suddenly it hits me how replaceable I am to all of them. If I vanished, they’d notice for a bit, then just… adjust. There’s no one who sees me as their person, the one they think of first. I’m always the extra, the backup plan, the “if no one else can make it.”

I try to reach out, but I overthink every message. I worry I’m being annoying, too needy, too quiet, too something. So I pull back, then feel even more isolated, then hate myself for that too. It’s like I’m both starving for connection and terrified of it at the same time.

Nights are the worst. That’s when the silence gets loud and I start wondering if this is just how my life is going to feel.
