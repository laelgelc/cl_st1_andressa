I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with about work, shows, random stuff. But it all feels like I’m playing a role I memorized years ago and never updated. I can talk, joke, even seem “normal,” but the second I get home, there’s this heavy silence that feels almost physical.

I scroll through chats and see long gaps, half-finished conversations, people I used to be close to but somehow let drift away. I don’t even know when it happened. I keep wanting someone to notice I’m not okay, but I also ignore messages because I feel too drained to reply. Then I hate myself for that and feel even more alone.

It’s like I’m stuck behind glass, watching everyone else do life together while I pretend I’m fine with being on my own. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone actually asked how I’m really doing.
