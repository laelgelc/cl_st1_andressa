I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call if something really went wrong. When I’m with others I feel like I’m performing a version of myself they might tolerate, but not actually like. Then I go home, overthink every interaction, and convince myself I sounded weird or annoying. I scroll through chats and see only dead conversations I didn’t know how to keep alive. It feels like everyone else got a manual I somehow missed.
