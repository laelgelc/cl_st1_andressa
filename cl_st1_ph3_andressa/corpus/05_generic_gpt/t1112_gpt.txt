I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone really noticing. I have acquaintances, group chats, coworkers, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something went wrong. Most days I don’t speak out loud beyond “thanks” at a checkout. When I’m invited somewhere, I feel like a last-minute filler, not someone anyone is genuinely excited to see.  

What messes with me is how replaceable I feel. If I disappeared from social media, I don’t know who would actually reach out and ask if I’m okay, or even realize I’m gone.
