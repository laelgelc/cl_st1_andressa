I’m starting to realize I can feel lonely even when I’m not technically “alone.” I have coworkers, a few acquaintances, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me beyond the surface stuff, and I’m not sure anyone wants to.

I keep thinking it’s my fault for not being “interesting” enough or for always overthinking every interaction. I replay conversations later and cringe at tiny things I said, then I withdraw more. It’s like I’m watching life from behind glass: I can see everyone else connecting, laughing, making plans, but I can’t seem to step through.

I miss feeling genuinely seen. I’m not even sure anymore what I’d say if someone actually asked how I was, and meant it.
