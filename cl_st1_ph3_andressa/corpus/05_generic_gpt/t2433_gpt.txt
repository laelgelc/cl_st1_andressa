I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of everyone’s life without anyone noticing. Messages get left on read, plans “never quite work out,” and I’ve stopped being the person people think to invite. I go through whole days where my only real conversations are with cashiers or coworkers making small talk. I’m not totally alone on paper, but I feel emotionally untethered, like I could disappear and it would just be a minor inconvenience for a few people at most. I miss feeling chosen, not just tolerated.
