I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life without sounding dramatic, so I’m dumping it here.

My life looks “fine” from the outside. I have a job, I answer messages, I show up when I’m supposed to. But I feel like I’m acting out a version of myself that other people expect, while the real me is somewhere behind glass, watching.

I have acquaintances, not friends. People I can joke with, but no one I can text and say, “Today sucked and I don’t know why.” I’m always the extra person in a group, never the one someone thinks of first. If I disappeared, I’m honestly not sure whose daily life would actually change.

What scares me is how used to it I’m getting. The silence after I turn off my phone doesn’t even feel strange anymore. It just feels… normal.
