I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about shows or work, but no one I feel safe being fully honest with. Whenever I try, I hear myself oversharing or making jokes to soften everything, and it feels fake.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives, and it’s like pressing on a bruise. Everyone seems to have “their person,” or at least a group. I have…me, my phone, and a quiet apartment.

I’m not completely isolated, yet I feel invisible. If I disappeared, some people would notice, but I don’t think anyone would really miss *me*—just the role I fill. That thought has been sitting in my chest a lot lately.
