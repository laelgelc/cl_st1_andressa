Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. I talk to people every day, but it all feels like background noise. No one really knows me beyond surface-level facts, and I’m starting to wonder if that’s my fault or just how things are now.

I’ve lost touch with most of my old friends. We didn’t fight or anything; we just slowly stopped reaching out. Now it feels weird to message them after so long, like I’d be intruding on their “real” lives. I see their group photos and inside jokes online and feel like a ghost who forgot to move on.

What scares me most is how used to this I’m becoming. The silence, the empty weekends, eating every meal alone. It’s starting to feel normal, and I’m not sure if that’s better or worse.
