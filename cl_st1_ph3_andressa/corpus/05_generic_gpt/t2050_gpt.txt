I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely. I go through days without anyone saying my name out loud. Group chats are muted, not because they’re blowing up, but because they’re dead. I scroll, lurk, consume other people’s lives, but no one really knows mine. I keep thinking I should reach out, then feel weird and don’t. It’s like I’m slowly fading into the background and no one’s noticing, including me.
