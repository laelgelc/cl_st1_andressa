I’m starting to realize I’m not “busy and independent,” I’m just alone all the time and getting used to it in a way that feels dangerous.

Most of my days are a loop: work, phone, sleep. I talk to coworkers, but it’s surface-level stuff—jokes, deadlines, “how was your weekend?” where nobody really wants a real answer. I’ve drifted from old friends, and I don’t even know when it happened exactly. Just a lot of “we should catch up soon” that never turns into anything.

What scares me is how quiet everything feels when I come home. I can go days without hearing my own voice except for ordering coffee or saying “thank you” to a delivery driver. I keep thinking about how, if I disappeared, it would probably be my job that noticed first, not an actual person.
