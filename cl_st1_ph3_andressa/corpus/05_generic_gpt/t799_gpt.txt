I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels like background noise. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate over my contacts list and then just put my phone down. I’m scared I’m getting used to this quiet, like it’s becoming my default setting. I miss feeling genuinely known, but I’m not sure I remember what that feels like.
