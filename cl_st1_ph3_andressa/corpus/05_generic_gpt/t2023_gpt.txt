I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and too visible at the same time.

Invisible, because no one really checks in on me unless I reach out first. Too visible, because whenever I’m around people I feel hyper-aware of how awkward and out of place I am. It’s like I’m a guest star in everyone else’s life and an extra in my own.

Most of my interactions are surface-level: work small talk, a few memes in group chats, the occasional “we should hang out sometime” that never becomes an actual plan. I can go days without hearing my name spoken out loud by someone who genuinely wants to talk to me, not just needs something.

I think what’s messing with me the most is the lack of emotional intimacy. I don’t have that person I can message at 2 a.m. with something stupid or something serious and know they’ll get it. When something good or bad happens, I hesitate before sharing because I’m not sure who would even care beyond a polite response.

I try to put myself out there, but it often feels like I’m forcing my way into spaces where I’m not really wanted, just tolerated. Then I retreat, which only makes the loneliness worse.

It’s a strange feeling to be surrounded by people online and still feel like there’s no one who really knows me. Or maybe they do know me, and that’s why they keep their distance. I honestly don’t know anymore.
