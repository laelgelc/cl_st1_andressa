I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “with” people. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role I never auditioned for. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people who’d say hi if they saw me, but no one I can text when something small but important happens. I scroll through my phone at night, staring at names I don’t feel close enough to message. It’s weird how I can be so desperate to be seen and simultaneously terrified of being really known. Most days, it just feels like I’m background noise in my own life.
