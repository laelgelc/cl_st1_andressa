I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold small talk, I can be “pleasant,” but it never seems to go deeper. I watch other people slide into friendships so naturally, like there’s an invisible script they all got and I somehow missed it.  

Most days I go to work, come home, scroll, sleep. Messages are mostly group chats where I’m optional. I keep thinking I should try harder, but I’m exhausted by the thought of another conversation that ends with “we should hang out sometime” and never goes anywhere.
