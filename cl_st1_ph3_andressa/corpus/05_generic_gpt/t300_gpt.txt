I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to be around them.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I chat with about weather, shows, work stuff. On paper I’m not “alone.” But when something good or bad happens, I scroll through my contacts and there isn’t a single person I feel comfortable texting without overthinking it to death.

I’ll replay every interaction afterward, convinced I sounded weird or boring. Then I pull back a little more, which of course makes me even more distant. It’s like I’m watching my own life through glass—close enough to see everyone, too far to touch anything.

I miss the feeling of being known. Not “how’s it going?” known, but “I noticed you’re quieter today, what’s wrong?” known.

Right now it feels like if I disappeared, people would notice the absence, but not really miss *me*.
