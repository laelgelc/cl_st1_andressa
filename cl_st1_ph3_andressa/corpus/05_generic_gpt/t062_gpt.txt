I’m starting to realize I’m not just “busy” or “introverted,” I’m actually lonely. I go entire weekends without speaking out loud to anyone except cashiers. My phone only lights up with spam or work emails. When something funny or awful happens, I instinctively reach for my phone, then remember there’s no one to send it to. I keep telling myself I prefer being alone, but it feels more like I’ve forgotten how to be with people. It’s like I’m slowly disappearing from everyone’s life, including my own.
