I’m starting to feel like a ghost who’s still paying rent.

My days are basically work, commute, screen, sleep. I talk to coworkers, but it’s all surface-level: deadlines, weather, “got any plans this weekend?” (I never do.) When I get home, the silence feels heavy, like the walls are listening.

I scroll through chats I’m too embarrassed to revive because it’s been months. I watch people post about hangouts and inside jokes and realize I’m never part of anyone’s “we,” only my own “I.”

What scares me most isn’t being alone now, but how normal it’s starting to feel. Like I’m slowly forgetting how to be with people, and they’re forgetting I exist.
