I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to connect with people. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with online, but it all feels paper-thin. I go home and there’s no one who really knows me, no one I can message without overthinking it first. I watch other people’s group photos and inside jokes from the outside, like a ghost scrolling through evidence of a world I’m not part of. Some days I’m not even sure what I’d say if someone actually asked how I really am.
