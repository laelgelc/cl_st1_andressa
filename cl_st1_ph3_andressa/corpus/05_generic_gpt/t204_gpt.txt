I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight. I go through my day doing all the normal things—work, errands, small talk—but it all feels like acting. People know my name, but no one really knows me. I’ll get home, unlock the door, and the silence hits like a wall. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen. I’m surrounded by noise and still feel like I’m on the outside of everything, pressed up against the glass.
