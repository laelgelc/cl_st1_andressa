I’m starting to feel like I’ve forgotten how to be around people.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but no one who really knows me. Conversations stay on the surface: work, weather, TV shows. I try to open up a little and somehow it always feels like I’ve said “too much” or “the wrong thing,” so I retreat again.

Nights are the worst. I scroll through my phone hoping someone will message first, then feel pathetic when no one does. I tell myself to reach out, but I’m tired of being the one who initiates everything and still feeling like an afterthought.

What scares me most is how normal this is beginning to feel. Like isolation is becoming my default setting, and I’m not sure how to reverse it.
