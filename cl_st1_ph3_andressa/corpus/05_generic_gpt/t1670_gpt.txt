I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately.

I go through the motions—work, errands, scrolling through my phone—but there’s this constant sense that I’m not really connected to anyone. I have “people” around me: coworkers, acquaintances, family I text every now and then. But I don’t feel known by any of them. It’s like I’m always slightly out of sync, a beat behind every conversation.

I’ve tried joining groups, going to events, making small talk. I can get through the surface-level stuff, but it never goes deeper. I leave feeling more drained and somehow even more alone, like I just confirmed that there isn’t actually a place where I fit.

What bothers me most is how invisible the loneliness is. From the outside, my life probably looks fine. Inside, it feels like I’m slowly fading into the background and no one would really notice if I did.
