It’s weird how you can be surrounded by people and still feel completely disconnected. I talk to coworkers, classmates, family, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being myself. I keep conversations light because I’m scared that if I open up, I’ll either be ignored or make things awkward.

Most of my “social life” is scrolling through feeds, watching other people share moments with friends, partners, families. I’m happy for them, but it also feels like I’m watching life from the outside. I can go days without a single message that isn’t about work or logistics. When my phone lights up, it’s never from someone just wanting to talk to me.

I can’t tell if I’m lonely because I’ve pulled away, or if I pulled away because I was lonely. It feels like I’ve forgotten how to connect, and I’m not sure anyone notices that I’m drifting further into the background.
