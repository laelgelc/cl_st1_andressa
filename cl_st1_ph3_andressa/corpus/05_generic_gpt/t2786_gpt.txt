I’m starting to realize I’m not just “introverted,” I’m actually lonely. My phone barely buzzes unless it’s a notification from an app. I scroll through chats and see long gaps, old conversations that just faded out. I keep telling myself I’m busy, but the truth is there’s no one really checking in on me, and I don’t know how to change that.

I’m tired of always being the one who reaches out first. It makes me feel unwanted, like I’m forcing myself into people’s lives. So I just stop, and the silence proves my point.
