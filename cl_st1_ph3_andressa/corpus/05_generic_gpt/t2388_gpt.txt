I don’t even know how to describe this feeling anymore. It’s like being surrounded by people but still somehow stuck behind glass. Friends, coworkers, family – they all exist, but I feel like an extra in my own life. Conversations stay on the surface, and I don’t know how to bridge that gap to something real without seeming needy or weird.

I scroll through chats, wondering who I could message without it feeling forced. Usually I just lock my phone and tell myself “maybe later.” The more time passes, the harder it feels to reach out. I’m scared I’ve forgotten how to actually connect, or maybe I never really knew how in the first place.
