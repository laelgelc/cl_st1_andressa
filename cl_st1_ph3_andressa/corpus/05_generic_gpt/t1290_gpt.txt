I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can make small talk, crack a joke, but everything feels like I’m acting out a script. When I go home, there’s no one to text, no one who’d notice if I disappeared for a few days. I see other people’s group chats, trips, casual hangouts, and it just highlights how replaceable I am. I’m not even sure if I want friends or if I just want proof that I’m not invisible.
