I’m starting to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind glass. Friends, coworkers, family – they’re there, but it’s like I’m always slightly out of sync, watching myself interact instead of actually connecting.

I try to open up, but the words feel clumsy or “too much,” so I default to being the listener, the one who nods and says, “I’m fine.” Then I go home and replay every conversation, convinced I was awkward or boring and that everyone secretly just tolerates me.

What makes it worse is that I’m not sure how to fix it. I want deeper connections, but I don’t know how to get from here to there without feeling needy or weird. So I just keep existing in this in-between space, hoping something changes, but not really expecting it to.
