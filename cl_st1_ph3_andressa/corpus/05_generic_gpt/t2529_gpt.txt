It’s strange how I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m on the outside of a glass wall. I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of old friends I message occasionally, but I don’t feel truly known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface. I censor myself because I’m scared that if I show the “real” me, people will quietly drift away. Most evenings are just me, my phone, and the same apps I scroll through on autopilot. I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be genuinely close to someone.
