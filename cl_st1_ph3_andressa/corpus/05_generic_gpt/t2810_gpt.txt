I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of people’s lives without anyone noticing. Friends are “busy,” messages get left on read, and I’ve stopped being the person anyone thinks to invite. I go days without a real conversation, just small talk with coworkers and scrolling through feeds where everyone else seems connected. I keep wondering if I did something wrong, or if I was never that important to begin with. It’s like I’m present, but not really there for anyone.
