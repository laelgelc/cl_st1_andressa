I don’t really know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but I feel like a ghost in my own life.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but I don’t feel *with* any of them. Conversations never go past surface level. I crack jokes, I nod in the right places, and then I go home and feel like I’ve just performed a role all day. No one actually knows what’s going on in my head.

What scares me is how used to it I’m becoming. I used to crave deep connections and late-night talks; now I just scroll on my phone until I’m tired enough to sleep. When someone cancels plans, I’m half relieved because it means I don’t have to pretend.

I keep thinking there must be something wrong with me that other people can sense, like an invisible sign that says “don’t get too close.” And I don’t know how to change that.
