Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from outside a window. I go to work, I’m polite, I crack a few jokes, people seem to like me well enough. But the second I’m home and the door closes, it’s just this heavy silence that feels louder than any noise.

I don’t really have “people.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, old classmates I occasionally text, but no one I can message at 2am when my brain won’t shut up. I scroll through chats that died months ago and wonder what exactly I did wrong.

I keep telling myself I’m “used to it,” but I’m not. I’m just better at pretending it doesn’t bother me. Then nights like this happen and it hits all over again how empty everything feels.
