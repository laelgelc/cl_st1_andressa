I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform being “fine.” I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I joke with, but when I go home it’s like I vanish. No one checks in, my phone stays silent unless I’m the one reaching out first. 

I see other people slipping so easily into friendships, group chats, plans. I’m there on the edges, invited sometimes, but it feels like I’m a guest actor in everyone else’s lives, never part of the main cast. 

I don’t even know what I’d say if someone asked how I really am. I’m lonely, but also embarrassed that I’m this lonely as an adult.
