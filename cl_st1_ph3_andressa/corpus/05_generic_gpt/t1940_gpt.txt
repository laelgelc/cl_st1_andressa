Lately I feel like I’m drifting through my own life as a ghost. I go to work, I talk to people, I even laugh at the right times, but none of it feels like it actually reaches me. When I get home and close the door, it’s like the whole world just… disappears, and I’m left with this heavy quiet that feels louder than any noise.

I have contacts, not friends. People I can text about practical things, but no one I feel I can message just to say, “I’m not okay.” I scroll through chats and social media, trying to figure out who I could talk to, and realize I’m basically an extra in everyone else’s story.

What makes it worse is how embarrassing it feels to admit this in real life. I’m at that age where everyone seems to have partners, kids, tight friend groups. I’m just here, watching my own life from the outside, not sure how to step in.
