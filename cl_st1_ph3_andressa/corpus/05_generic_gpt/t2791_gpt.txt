I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, commute, groceries—without really talking to anyone beyond small talk. Friends drifted away, or maybe I did, I’m not even sure anymore. I scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one who stopped replying last, but no one followed up either.  

What gets me most is how invisible I feel in public spaces. Surrounded by people, but no one knows me. I don’t even know what I’d say if someone genuinely asked how I’m doing.
