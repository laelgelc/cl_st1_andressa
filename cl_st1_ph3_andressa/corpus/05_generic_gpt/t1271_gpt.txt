Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m watching my own life from the outside. I go to work, I come home, I scroll, I sleep. I talk to people every day, but it all feels like small talk on top of a huge empty space. My friends are busy with partners, kids, “real lives,” and I’m stuck in this loop of almost-connection that never goes any deeper. I try to reach out, but I always feel like I’m intruding or asking for more than people want to give. It’s weird how you can be surrounded by noise and still feel like you don’t really exist to anyone.
