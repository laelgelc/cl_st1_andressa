I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, and it’s hitting harder as I get older. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with about surface stuff, but no one who really knows me. I go entire weekends without my phone lighting up unless it’s spam or some app notification.

What bothers me is how invisible it all is. From the outside, I probably look fine: I show up, I smile, I make small talk. Inside, it feels like I’m watching life from behind glass. I try to initiate plans sometimes, but people are “busy,” or we hang out once and it never turns into anything deeper.

I’m not even sure what I’m asking for. I just needed to put this somewhere, because keeping it all in my head makes it feel heavier, like it’s slowly hardening into the way things will always be.
