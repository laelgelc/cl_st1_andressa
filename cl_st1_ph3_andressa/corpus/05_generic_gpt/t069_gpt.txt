I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, in group chats, even in my own family, but I still feel like I’m behind glass. I listen to everyone else, but when it’s my turn to talk, I freeze or make a joke and move on. No one really knows what’s going on with me, and I’m not sure I remember how to let them.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, I lurk, I watch other people’s lives happen. I keep thinking, “If I disappeared, who would actually notice and reach out?” I don’t know the answer, and that scares me more than I want to admit.
