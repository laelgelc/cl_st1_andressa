I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I chat with, but it all feels like I’m acting out a script. I can talk about work, weather, random hobbies, but as soon as it gets even slightly personal, I freeze or make a joke and change the subject.

What scares me is how normal this has started to feel. I go through entire days without anyone really knowing what’s going on in my head. When something good or bad happens, my first thought is still to keep it to myself because “no one would really care.”

I keep thinking maybe I missed some crucial stage where people learned how to build real friendships, and now I’m just pretending, hoping no one notices.
