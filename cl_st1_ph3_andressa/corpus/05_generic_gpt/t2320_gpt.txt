I don’t really know how to put this into words without sounding dramatic, but lately it feels like I’m just slowly fading out of everyone’s life, including my own.

I have people around me, technically. Coworkers, some old friends in group chats, family I see on holidays. But I don’t feel *with* any of them. Conversations are small talk or memes, surface-level stuff. I keep trying to bring up how I actually feel, but I either get brushed off with “same lol” or people change the subject. After a while I just stopped trying.

What’s messing with me most is this sense that I’m forgettable. Like if I stopped replying, it would take a long time for anyone to really notice. Sometimes I test it—wait to see who messages first. Days go by. Nothing. Then I cave and reach out so I don’t have to face the answer.

I also feel weirdly out of sync with everyone. People my age are moving in with partners, getting married, having kids, posting photos of trips with “the best friends ever.” I’m sitting in my room scrolling, eating alone, pretending I’m fine when people ask what I’ve been up to. I lie and say “been busy” when the truth is I just didn’t have anyone to do anything with.

The worst part is I’m starting to feel numb. I used to be sad about it; now it’s just this dull ache that’s always there. I go through the motions—work, home, phone, sleep—hoping something will change, but not really believing it will.
