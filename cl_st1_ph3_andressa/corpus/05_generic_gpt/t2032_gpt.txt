I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can talk about work, the weather, memes, whatever, but the second it’s supposed to get real, my brain just… freezes. I either overshare and feel stupid afterward, or stay on the surface and walk away feeling invisible.

Most of my “social life” is scrolling, lurking, maybe commenting once in a while and then overthinking it for hours. I have contacts, but not many actual friends. The people I used to be close to have their own lives now, and I feel like an old tab they forgot to close.

It’s weird being surrounded by noise and still feeling like no one would notice if I just stopped showing up.
