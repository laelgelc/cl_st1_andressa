I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

My days look “normal” from the outside: work, small talk, maybe a gym session, then home. But there’s no one who actually knows what my days feel like. I have people to message, but not people to call. Group chats, but no one I’d ask to meet when I’m really not okay.

I scroll through social media and see everyone at dinners, weddings, trips. I’m happy for them, but it also feels like I missed some crucial step where people formed their circles and I just…didn’t. I’m always the peripheral friend, the extra invite, never the first person anyone thinks of.

What scares me most is how used to this I’m becoming, like loneliness is just the baseline I operate from now.
