I’m surrounded by people but feel like a ghost. Friends, coworkers, family—they’re there, yet I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to open up, I feel like I’m oversharing or making things awkward. I scroll through social media and see everyone else forming real connections, inside jokes, shared histories. I keep wondering if there’s just something fundamentally off about me that others can sense.
