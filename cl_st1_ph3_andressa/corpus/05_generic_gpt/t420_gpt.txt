I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.” I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call at 2 a.m. if something really went wrong. I scroll through my contacts and it’s like looking at a list of people I’m on “good terms” with but not really close to.

I keep thinking it’s my fault for not trying harder, but whenever I do try, conversations feel forced and I end up overanalyzing everything I say. Then I retreat again and tell myself I’m just “introverted,” when it really feels more like I’m invisible.
