Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m physically around people. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. Friends are “busy,” and I get it, but the silence between messages stretches longer and longer until it feels like I’m the only one still checking my phone. I keep replaying old memories where I used to feel included and wondering what changed, or if it was always like this and I just didn’t notice. It’s weird how you can be known by many people and still feel unseen.
