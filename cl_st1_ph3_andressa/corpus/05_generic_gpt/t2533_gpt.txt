I don’t really know how to explain this to people in my life without sounding dramatic, so I’m dumping it here instead.

I’m surrounded by people, technically. Coworkers, acquaintances, family chats buzzing on my phone. But I feel like I’m behind glass. I can see everyone, hear the conversations, but I can’t get through. I say the right things, nod at the right times, laugh when I’m supposed to, and still go home feeling like I was just acting.

What messes with my head is that no one would guess I’m lonely. I’m “busy,” I show up to things, I respond to messages. But there’s no one I can text just to say, “Today sucked and I don’t know why,” without feeling like I’m being a burden or oversharing.

I miss having someone who actually knows me, not just the version I present to keep everything smooth and unawkward. I’ve forgotten what it feels like to be chosen, to be the person someone thinks of first instead of the backup plan.

Most days I just go through the motions and hope the emptiness doesn’t show on my face.
