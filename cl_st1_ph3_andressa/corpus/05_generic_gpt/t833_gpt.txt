I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, people I joke with or work with, but no one who really knows me. Whenever conversations get even slightly personal, I pull back or change the subject. Then I go home and wonder why I feel so invisible. I scroll through chats, see “online” next to people’s names, and still feel like I’m on the other side of a glass wall. I’m not sure if I’ve isolated myself or if I was just never really “in” to begin with.
