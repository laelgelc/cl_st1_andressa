I’m starting to feel like I’m fading out of other people’s lives without anyone noticing.

I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can joke with, but no one I can actually call when I’m not okay. I scroll through my contacts and realize there isn’t a single person I’d feel comfortable texting, “Can we talk?” without worrying I’m being a burden.

Most of my days are a loop: work, home, phone, sleep. I watch other people make plans in group chats I’m not in, post pictures with “my people,” “my tribe,” and I can’t even imagine what that feels like. It’s not dramatic loneliness, just this quiet, constant ache that never really goes away.
