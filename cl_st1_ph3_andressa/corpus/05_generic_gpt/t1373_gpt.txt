I’ve been feeling like I’m drifting further away from everyone, even when I’m technically “around” people. I have coworkers, acquaintances, people I text sometimes, but I don’t feel actually known by anyone. Conversations stay on the surface—work, memes, random stuff—but no one really asks how I am, and I don’t know how to push past that without feeling needy or weird.

I keep replaying old friendships that faded. Some of it was my fault—I shut down when I’m stressed and don’t reach out. Then too much time passes and it feels awkward to message, like I have to explain the silence, so I just…don’t. And then I sit here convinced everyone else has moved on and I’m the only one stuck.

What makes it worse is that I’m not even sure how to fix it. I sign up for things, go to events, try to be friendly, but it never seems to go beyond “nice to see you here.” I leave feeling more alone than if I’d just stayed home.

It’s starting to feel like I’m invisible in my own life, like I’m watching it happen from the outside instead of living it with anyone.
