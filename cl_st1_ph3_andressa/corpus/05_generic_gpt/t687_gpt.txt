I’ve been feeling this weird mix of invisible and exposed lately. I’m surrounded by people at work, online, even in my own family group chats, but I still feel like I’m watching life through a window. I try to join conversations, but it always feels like I’m a beat off, like everyone else has this shared language I never learned.

What really messes with me is that I don’t look lonely from the outside. I laugh at the right times, I show up, I respond to messages. But when I come home, it’s just silence and this heavy feeling in my chest that I can’t shake. I keep wondering if this is just how things are going to be from now on.
