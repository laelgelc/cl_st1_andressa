I’m starting to wonder if I’ve just aged out of being someone people choose.

Most of my “friends” are old group chats that went silent, or people who say “we should catch up soon” and never suggest a time. I scroll through my contacts and realize there’s no one I can message without feeling like I’m intruding.

I’m not completely isolated: coworkers, small talk, the usual. But it all feels like background noise. No one really knows what’s going on with me, and I don’t know how to bridge that gap without sounding desperate.

Nights are the worst. I keep replaying past conversations, wondering when exactly I became optional.
