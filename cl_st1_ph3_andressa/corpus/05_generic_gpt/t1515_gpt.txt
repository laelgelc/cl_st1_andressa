Lately I feel like I’m drifting further away from everyone without anything dramatic actually happening. Friends are “busy,” texts get shorter, calls don’t happen. I scroll through chats and realize I’m always the one who reached out last. I’m not isolated on paper—I have coworkers, acquaintances, family—but I still feel weirdly invisible. It’s like I’m in a room full of people behind soundproof glass, watching everyone else actually live.
