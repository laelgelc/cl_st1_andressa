I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, groceries, scrolling on my phone—but it all feels disconnected, like I’m watching someone else live. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I can chat with, but no one I feel truly close to. I don’t know who I’d call if something really bad happened, or even something really good. 

What bothers me most is how invisible I feel. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like if I disappeared, the overall shape of things wouldn’t really change.
