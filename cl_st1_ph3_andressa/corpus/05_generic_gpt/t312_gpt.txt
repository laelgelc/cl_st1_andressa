I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I have acquaintances, coworkers, people I joke with, but no one I’d call if something really bad happened. I watch everyone else forming friendships, making plans, moving in together, and I’m just… orbiting around them. I overthink every message I send, then delete it and stay silent. When I do see people, I feel like I’m performing a version of myself that’s easier to like. Then I go home, sit in the quiet, and wonder if anyone noticed I was gone.
