I’m starting to realize how invisible I feel most of the time. I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but it’s like I’m acting a role instead of actually being there. Conversations stay on the surface, and whenever I try to open up, I either get talked over or brushed aside. I go home, scroll endlessly, and watch other people’s lives move forward while mine just loops. I’m not sure if it’s me pushing people away or if I’m just forgettable.
