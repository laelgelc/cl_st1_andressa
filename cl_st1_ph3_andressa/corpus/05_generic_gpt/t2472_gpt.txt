I’ve started to realize I’m not just “alone,” I’m actually lonely, and it feels like it’s eating away at me in slow motion. I go through entire days where the only words I say out loud are “thank you” to a cashier or “have a good one” to a delivery person. Friends drifted away, or maybe I did, and now I don’t even know how to reach out without feeling like I’m bothering them.  

I scroll through chats, type messages, then delete them. I keep thinking someone will notice I’ve gone quiet, but the silence just keeps proving that no one’s really listening in the first place.
