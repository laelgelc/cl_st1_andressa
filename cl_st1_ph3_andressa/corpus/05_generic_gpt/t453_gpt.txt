I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, grocery store, scrolling on my phone—but none of it feels connected to anyone. I have people I “know,” but no one I can text without overthinking it first. I replay conversations afterward, convinced I sounded weird or boring, and then I retreat even more.

Nights are the worst. I lie in bed with my phone in my hand, wanting to reach out, but the silence on my notifications just makes me feel pathetic. It’s strange how you can be surrounded by people online and still feel like you’re standing behind glass, watching everyone else live. I’m starting to forget what it feels like to be genuinely wanted in someone’s life.
