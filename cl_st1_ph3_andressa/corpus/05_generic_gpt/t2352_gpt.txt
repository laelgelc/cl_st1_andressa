I’ve started to realize that most of my life happens in my head. I go to work, say the necessary things, smile when I’m supposed to, then come home and just… disappear into my room. No one really checks in, and I don’t really reach out either. It feels like I’ve forgotten how.

I scroll through chats and see conversations that died months ago. I hover over names, think about sending a message, then convince myself they’re too busy or don’t really care. It’s not like anyone is cruel to me; it’s more like I’m just not on anyone’s radar.

What makes it worse is that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m behind a glass wall. I watch everyone else connect so easily, while I overthink every word. I miss feeling genuinely known by someone, but I’m starting to wonder if that was ever really real or just something I imagined.
