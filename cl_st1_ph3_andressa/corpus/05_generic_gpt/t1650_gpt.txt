I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people. I can talk, joke, make small talk, but it all feels like acting. I leave every interaction replaying what I said, convinced I sounded weird or boring. Friends drift away and I never fight it, because I assume they’re relieved. Even when I’m in a group, I feel like a ghost hovering at the edge. I’m not sure if I’m lonely or if I’ve just become used to this numb distance.
