I’m starting to feel like I’m disappearing in plain sight.

I have people around me—coworkers, acquaintances, even family—but everything feels so surface-level. We talk about work, shows, random news, but no one really *sees* me. I don’t think I help either; I’ve forgotten how to open up without feeling like I’m oversharing or being a burden.

Nights are the worst. I scroll, lurk, maybe type something and delete it. I watch other people joke around in group chats I’m technically in, but I’m always the one reacting with an emoji instead of saying anything real. It’s like I’m present but not actually part of it.

I’m not sure if I’m lonely because I’m different, or if I’ve just built walls so high that even I don’t know how to climb them anymore. I just know I’m tired of feeling like a background character in my own life.
