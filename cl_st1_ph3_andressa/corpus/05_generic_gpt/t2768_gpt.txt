I’ve started to realize that I can be surrounded by people and still feel like I’m on mute.

I have coworkers, acquaintances, even a couple of people I’d technically call friends, but everything feels surface-level. We talk about work, shows, random memes, but no one really knows what’s going on in my head, and I don’t know how to let them in without feeling like I’m oversharing or being a burden.

When I get home, the silence hits hard. I scroll, I watch videos, I lurk on here, but it’s like I’m just trying to fill space until I’m tired enough to sleep. I keep thinking I must be doing something wrong socially, because other people seem to find their “person” or their group eventually.

I’m not sure what I’m hoping for by posting this. Maybe just proof that I exist somewhere outside my own head.
