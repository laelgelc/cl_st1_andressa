I’ve been feeling like a background character in my own life lately. I go through the motions—work, chores, scrolling—but there’s no real point where I feel seen or needed. Friends drifted away slowly, nothing dramatic, just fewer messages until it turned into silence. I don’t know when I became the person people forget to invite.

What bothers me most is how quiet everything is when I get home. No one to tell small, pointless stories to. I catch myself rehearsing conversations in my head that never actually happen. It’s embarrassing how much I look forward to random notifications on my phone, just to feel like I exist to someone, somewhere.
