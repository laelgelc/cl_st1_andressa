I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people, I just know how to perform “being okay.”

On paper, I’m not isolated: I have coworkers, some acquaintances, a family chat that pings all day. But I still feel like I’m watching life through glass. I show up, make small talk, laugh at the right moments, then go home and feel like I never really existed in anyone’s mind.

I keep thinking about how there’s no one I can text “I’m not doing well” without feeling like I’m burdening them or being dramatic. I end up deleting messages before I send them. Even when I’m with people, I’m already anticipating going back to my room, to the silence, to scrolling until I’m too tired to think.

It’s a weird mix of wanting to be seen and being terrified that if anyone actually saw me, they’d quietly step away.
