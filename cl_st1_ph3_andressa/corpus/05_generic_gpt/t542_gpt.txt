I’m starting to realize I don’t actually know how to connect with people anymore. I can hold a conversation, crack a joke, be “fine” on the surface, but it all feels like acting. When I’m around others, I feel like I’m behind glass—close enough to see everything, but never really part of it.

I scroll through chats and social media and see people making plans, inside jokes, group photos. I’m technically “in” some of those groups, but I’m not really *in* them. If I disappeared, I’m not sure anyone’s day-to-day life would change.

I miss the feeling of being known without having to explain myself from scratch. I miss being someone’s first choice to text, not an afterthought. It’s like I’m living next to my own life instead of in it.
