I feel like I’m drifting further from everyone, even when I’m technically “around” people. Conversations feel scripted, like I’m playing a role I never learned properly. Friends are busy, relationships fade, and I’m never the person anyone thinks to call first. I scroll through chats and see long gaps, half-finished conversations, unanswered messages I’m too embarrassed to bump. It’s like I’m slowly becoming invisible while still having to show up and pretend I’m fine.
