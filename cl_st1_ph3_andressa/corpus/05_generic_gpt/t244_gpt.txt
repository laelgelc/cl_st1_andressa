I’ve been feeling this weird, hollow kind of loneliness lately that doesn’t really fit into the usual “make more friends” advice. I technically have people around me—coworkers, a couple of old friends I text sometimes, family chats—but it all feels like background noise. I’m there, but not really seen.

What gets to me most is how replaceable I feel. If I stopped initiating, most conversations would just quietly die. I’m scared to test it fully, because I’m pretty sure I already know the answer.

I miss having someone who checks in just because they thought of me, not because I reminded them I exist. I miss long, unhurried conversations where I don’t have to perform or filter myself. Lately it feels like I’m drifting further away from that possibility, and I don’t really know what to do with that feeling.
