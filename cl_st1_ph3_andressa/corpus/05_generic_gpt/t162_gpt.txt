Lately I’ve been feeling like I’m living behind glass. I go to work, make small talk, send memes in group chats, but it all feels like I’m playing a role instead of actually being there. People say I seem “fine” or “put together,” and I don’t know how to explain that I feel completely disconnected most of the time. I’ll scroll through my phone wanting to message someone, then convince myself I’d just be annoying. Even when I’m with friends, I feel like an extra in their lives, not someone who really matters. Nights are the worst—everything goes quiet and it hits me how replaceable I feel, like if I disappeared, it would just be a minor inconvenience at best.
