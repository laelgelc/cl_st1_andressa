I keep realizing that most of my conversations are either transactional or scheduled. Work meetings, quick “how are you” texts, family check-ins where everyone is half-distracted. I don’t really have anyone I can just message at 2 a.m. because my brain won’t shut up. 

I’ll go days without saying more than a few sentences out loud, then suddenly feel this weird panic when I hear my own voice. I try joining group chats, Discord servers, whatever, but I always feel like an outsider who joined too late and missed all the bonding. 

What scares me most is how normal it’s starting to feel. Like I’m slowly forgetting what it’s like to be known by someone.
