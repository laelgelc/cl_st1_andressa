I’m starting to feel like I exist in the negative space of other people’s lives. I’m “around,” but not really *in* anything. Friends have their own circles now—partners, kids, group chats I’m not in. We still talk sometimes, but I feel like a guest appearance in their story, never a main character.

What messes with me most is how normal everything looks from the outside. I go to work, answer emails, make small talk. People would probably say I seem fine. Then I come home, close the door, and it’s just silence and the sense that nobody would notice if I quietly disappeared from their routines.

I keep wondering if it’s something about me—my personality, my timing, my face, my energy—that makes me forgettable.
