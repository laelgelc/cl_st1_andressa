A 23-year-old man shares that he separated from his partner on June 29, 2025, after nearly seven years together. He reflects on how the past few months have changed him, in both good and bad ways, and wishes he could go back and do things differently. Although he has tried to move on when he felt ready, he still reminisces, feels unhealed, and holds some hope they might get back together. He doesn’t talk to many people, so writing this post feels relieving for him.
