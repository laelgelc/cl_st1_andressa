The 21-year-old writer describes having unconsciously chosen isolation over many years by not maintaining or developing friendships, which led to shallow, unfulfilling relationships and a damaged self-image. They now feel empty rather than actively miserable and believe others are better off without them, framing their withdrawal as a kind of “service” or martyrdom despite knowing this thinking is irrational.
