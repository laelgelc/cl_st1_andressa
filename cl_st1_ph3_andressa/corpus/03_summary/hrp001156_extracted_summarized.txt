A 24-year-old man describes feeling very lonely and discouraged about dating. He’s been on Hinge for three weeks with no matches or likes, and a friend told him maybe online dating isn’t for him, adding a “😅” that made the comment hurt even more. Now he feels both mentally and physically exhausted, unsure whether to keep trying to find love or give up. He longs to be held, reassured that he’s a good and lovable person, and to have just one person who truly understands him. The ongoing lonely days and nights are wearing him down, and he feels like a bother, though he doesn’t want to be. He feels out of breath from it all and hopes things will get better soon.
