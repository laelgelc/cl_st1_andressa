The poster is a 31-year-old woman in Bangalore working as a well-paid software developer from a middle-class family. Despite actively trying to be social—having long calls with friends, attending house parties, going to piano classes, and shopping with acquaintances—she feels a deep lack of a stable, close, family-like connection and worries friends will eventually move on to marriage and kids. She is unsure if she even wants marriage, feels like a “soft soul” who can’t handle this recurring phase of loneliness and anxiety about the future, and wonders if overthinking is making things worse. Her anticipatory anxiety about an empty life is consuming her passions, ambitions, and personality, leaving her feeling weak, frustrated, and useless for not working toward her goals.
