The post describes how prolonged solitude slowly turns from peaceful to painful. At first, being alone brings clarity and calm, but over time the silence becomes suffocating and mentally harsh. Loneliness quietly undermines self-worth, makes reaching out feel scary, and leads to feeling unseen and unheard. Gradually, the person feels hardened, guarded, and deeply tired, as if their sense of self is being worn away until they can no longer remember what it’s like to be truly understood.
