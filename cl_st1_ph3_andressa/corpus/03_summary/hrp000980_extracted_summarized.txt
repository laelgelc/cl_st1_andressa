The poster has lived 20 years without ever having a best friend or a romantic relationship. Others are often surprised to hear this, which makes them feel like they must be doing something wrong. They feel like they’re always a third or fourth choice to people, never someone’s first priority or the person others are excited to see. Although they sometimes feel genuinely okay being alone and waiting for the right person, the long wait has worn them down, and they’re starting to give up on the hope of finding someone who will choose them first.
