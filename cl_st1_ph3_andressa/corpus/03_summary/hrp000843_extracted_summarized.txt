A 20-year-old man says he just lost his best friend after making a hurtful joke about her. He realized he went too far as soon as he said it, but it still deeply hurt her. This made him see a pattern: he believes he has no friends because he is the problem. He feels he doesn’t know when to stop joking, tries to match others’ humor to be liked, and ends up coming across as an asshole and fake. He concludes that being a “smartass” is all he knows, blames himself for being alone, and doesn’t know what to do next.
