The poster describes a life filled with lies, bullying, discrimination, rejection, and pain from other people. They decided to live in isolation and feel it was the best choice because it protects them from further hurt and allows them to set their own rules. However, this long-term isolation has made them emotionally numb and indifferent toward others. They believe it’s better for them and for the rest of humanity to stay separate, limiting interactions to what’s absolutely necessary and having no friends to avoid lies, pain, and “beasts.”
