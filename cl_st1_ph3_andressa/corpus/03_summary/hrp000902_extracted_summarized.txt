Linus, a 25-year-old man from Belgium, is very shy and has a stutter, which makes it hard for him to make friends or find a girlfriend. He also has chronic kidney disease, and since his diagnosis he has closed himself off and felt very alone, thinking he was the only one with this condition. He works in a nursing home and can talk with people there in a “different talking voice,” but once work is over he feels alone again. Outside of work he sometimes goes to the gym, reads books, or plays video games, but still finds it hard to make friends and hopes this will change.
