Kenneth is a 22-year-old from Singapore who has felt alone his whole life. He was bullied in school as a child and teenager, had few friends, and has never been in a relationship. Although the bullying stopped in college, the loneliness remained and grew alongside strong feelings of lust that he describes as a “monster” that now consumes him.

The main things that have stayed with him are music, Five Nights at Freddy’s, and himself. In 2021 he attempted to end his life and was admitted to a psychiatric ward, where medication and treatment helped stabilize him for a while, but his struggles keep returning. He also describes his parents as toxic and not understanding.
