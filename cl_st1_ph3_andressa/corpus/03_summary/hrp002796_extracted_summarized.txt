The poster describes an idea for a dark, morbid board game using their real-life friends (and themselves) as characters. Each “friend” would be represented with real stats like age, height, weight, substance abuse, income, and family status. These pieces are placed on a bingo-like board where one axis is age and the other is causes of death (common and uncommon). The objective of the game is to guess the order and cause of death for each person, including the poster.
