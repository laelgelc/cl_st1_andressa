They’re on a week-long work trip with meetings and training that they intellectually appreciate, but their social anxiety is making it very hard to benefit from the experience. They feel this is why they have no friends and don’t go out in their free time, because they can’t handle these social situations. They’re also dreading an upcoming awards dinner with a dance floor at the end of the week.
