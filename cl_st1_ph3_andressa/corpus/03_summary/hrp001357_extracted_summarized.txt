The poster has always disliked the holidays because their family treated them like a cook and waiter for over 16 years, making them serve and clean while sitting alone in the kitchen and taking them for granted. They were pressured into buying expensive last-minute gifts that were then dismissed, while receiving little in return, and had an ex who threw tantrums about attending family events. They’ve now moved away from their toxic family to another state, aren’t celebrating or putting up a tree, and feel there’s no point in the holidays—but at the same time, they’re surprised to find they miss the chaos and haven’t yet found a sense of calm in their new, lonely situation.
