The poster is a 35-year-old man from Kolkata who lost both parents in the last 10 years and is struggling to cope. He works in a state government job and is financially middle class but feels no energy to live or carry on. He lives with his bachelor uncle in a two-storied house; the uncle is friendly, but the home still feels very empty and lonely with just the two of them. Although his father was abusive, his death has still deeply affected and suddenly torn him emotionally. He is asking others how they have dealt with similar losses.
