A 22-year-old man went out to enjoy his day but ended up feeling very lonely when he saw everyone else with friends or at least one companion while he was alone. He tried to join some groups, but they rejected him, saying they didn’t need a new member, which he says he respects. What really hurts him is that he has always been alone, has never had real-life friends to go out with, and always spends his time by himself, leaving him feeling like he’s not allowed to have friends.
