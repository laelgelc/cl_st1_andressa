26-year-old man describes feeling socially capable but drained by socializing due to low self-esteem and social anxiety. He says he tries too hard to keep others happy without feeling it’s reciprocated, which is exhausting. He went through five years of college without making real friends, only acquaintances, and now works fully remote, so he hasn’t formed friendships there either. He lives with his girlfriend, whom he describes as great, but has no social life beyond her and barely interacts with anyone else, even online. He feels he may have missed out on life but also finds this isolated lifestyle easier and less painful, and he’s unsure if this is normal, sharing in case others relate.
