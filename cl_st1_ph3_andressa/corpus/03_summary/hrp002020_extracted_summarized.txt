The poster has worked hard to change their physical appearance—losing weight, improving fashion, hair, and grooming—to make more friends, but it hasn’t improved their social life. They feel their looks may attract people initially, but their personality drives them away. They have ADHD and autism and say that even when they try to mask, their true self comes out and seems to repel others. Others often label them as “annoying,” “draining,” “too much,” and “weird.” They feel stuck in a cycle where no one stays in their life and wish they were neurotypical.
