A 37-year-old woman feels she “should” be socializing, finding a partner, and building a shared domestic life, but whenever she’s actually with friends or on dates she quickly wants to be alone. She prefers the comfort and freedom of solitude, without worrying about her appearance, behavior, or eating. She’s unsure what this means—whether it’s loneliness, confusion, or lack of self-acceptance—and is asking where to go from here.
