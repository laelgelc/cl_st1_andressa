31-year-old man says he has been romantically lonely his whole life and has been trying hard, especially since last year, to change that but keeps failing. With Christmas approaching, he feels especially sad about being alone, seeing it as a romantic time with decorations, snow, and cuddling by the fireplace. He longs for something as simple as a hug with a girl under a Christmas tree at least once in his life and wonders if that’s asking too much, ending with a wistful reference to needing Santa Claus.
