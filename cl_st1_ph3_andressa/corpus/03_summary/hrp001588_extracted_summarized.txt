A 19-year-old guy describes himself as naive, inexperienced, and sheltered, and is now curious about relationships. He wants to find the right girl, someone he can talk to about anything and share intimacy with. He admits he’s probably addicted to pornography, which he feels is trying to emulate intimacy. He feels conflicted: part of him thinks he’s missing out by not being in a relationship, while another part believes relationships aren’t everything in life. He asks if he is really missing out.
