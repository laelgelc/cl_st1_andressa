A 24-year-old man describes feeling more lonely than ever over the past couple of years and says he’s at his breaking point. He stays up very late to distract himself from negative thoughts, but it’s getting harder each day. He hides his emotions from his mother and only gets emotional when she’s not around. He says relationships haven’t worked out, feels lost and numb, and says life doesn’t seem to mean much to him anymore, and he doesn’t know what to do.
