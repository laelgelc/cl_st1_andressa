An 18-year-old woman with BPD describes having worsening episodes and isolating more. She feels lonely even around friends, believing they’re closer to each other than to her and that she’ll never have “her person.” She worries that love might never come, feels dismissed when people tell her she’s young and should just wait, and feels like she’s not allowed to openly yearn for connection. She strongly wants a boyfriend or friend who genuinely likes and understands her, and wonders what’s wrong with her, saying she doesn’t want to be alone and just wants to feel normal.
