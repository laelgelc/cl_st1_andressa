20F college student who was always very quiet and “weird” is now trying hard to be more outgoing and start conversations, but feels people still don’t like her. She’s on casual talking terms with classmates but not close enough for texting or hanging out, and one girl only messages her about homework. This makes her feel rejected and regret trying to be outgoing, because rejection hurts more than choosing to be alone. She’s also been trying to befriend a shy guy she sits next to, but he seems irritated and very quiet, so she’s thinking of giving up, though he hasn’t moved seats, which gives her a bit of hope. She wonders if she’s pestering people, if other quiet people just want to be left alone, and if she’s simply annoying.
