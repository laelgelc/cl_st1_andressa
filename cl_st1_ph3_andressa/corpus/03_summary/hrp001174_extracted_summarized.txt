The poster reflects on loneliness and pain, saying people often see being alone as bad, but they believe pain is temporary and can make you stronger. Drawing from their experience as a historical fencer getting repeatedly hit and bruised, they say it’s okay to hurt and to take time alone to heal. They emphasize that emotional struggles—grief, regret, sadness, insecurity, heartbreak, creative failure, and losing loved ones to cancer—are part of a difficult but meaningful journey “through” pain, not out of it. They encourage others to keep fighting, believe in their own value, see life as a difficult but worthwhile blessing, and not give up.
