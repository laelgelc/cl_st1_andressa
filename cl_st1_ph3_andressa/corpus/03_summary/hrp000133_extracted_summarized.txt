The writer appears cheerful on the outside but feels lonely and out of place inside. In college, they relied on romantic relationships as a coping mechanism for childhood trauma and didn’t invest in friendships, so after breakups they were left without close friends. They now have only casual or not-very-close friends, including one good friend they can’t be fully vulnerable with. They also have a relatively new boyfriend (5 months) but don’t feel ready to lean on him for everything and don’t want to depend on him just because they lack other support. With postgrad now finished, they feel hopeless and fear they’ll have to live without real, close friends forever.
