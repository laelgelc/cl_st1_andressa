The writer feels deeply lonely and unwanted, longing for someone to approach them and genuinely want to get to know them. They constantly fantasize about finding a partner while seeing others happily in relationships, which makes their own isolation feel worse. They fear they’ll always be the friend who never finds love, and that dreams of marriage, children, and a happy life will never come true for them. They wish they were different and had whatever it is that others want, but at the core, they simply want to feel wanted.
