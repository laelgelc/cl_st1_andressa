The poster describes how people praise them as kind, fun, and a good friend when they’re healthy, but disappear whenever their serious illness flares up—when they’re hospitalized, too weak to get out of bed, or need emergency help. They feel that others’ loving words are empty because their actions show abandonment. This repeated pattern leaves the poster feeling like they’re performing on a stage, forced to mask their pain and carry on, while everyone else walks away and they remain alone in the spotlight.
