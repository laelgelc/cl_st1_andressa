The writer reflects on a past relationship that was intense, hurtful, and ultimately broke them, requiring years of healing. They recently saw their ex’s online profile and discovered the ex is now married and living the kind of life they once imagined together. This brings up lingering pain and some jealousy, even though the writer feels they are over the ex. Now older and having received help, the writer has found someone new who makes them feel loved without the past toxicity. By expressing these feelings, they decide it’s time to fully let go of the past and feel at peace, seeing life as a journey that is just beginning for them.
