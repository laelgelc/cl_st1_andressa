The poster is feeling very lonely and wants someone to talk to and play a simple two-person “creative response game” they read about. In the game, Person A gives Person B a keyword, and Person B must answer all of Person A’s questions using that keyword somehow. Person A wins if Person B fails to use the keyword in an answer; Person B wins if Person A runs out of questions. The poster gives an example using the keyword “cake” and invites others to ask any kind of questions in the game.
