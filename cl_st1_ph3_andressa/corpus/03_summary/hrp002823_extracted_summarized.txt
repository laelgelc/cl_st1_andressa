The poster, referencing ideas from the book *The Art of Gathering*, proposes an ambitious and slightly unconventional agenda for a meetup. The goals are to: finalize a complete restructuring of their organization, have each member create and share three quick logo sketches, identify at least one example website with desired features and design, and have everyone share three key insights from their own experience that every meetup organizer should know.
