The writer matched with someone on a dating app where both were looking for friends, and they’ve been chatting for about a month. She feels safe with him, shares interests like fitness, and thinks about him often, but is scared of getting attached because it leads to emotional pain and overthinking. She imagines moments with him, then feels hurt by her own thoughts and fears he will lose interest or leave. Despite having a busy life with hobbies, workouts, school, cooking, and YouTube, she can’t stop thinking about him and wishes she could be happy alone without this anxiety about attachment.
