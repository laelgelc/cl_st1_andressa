A 54-year-old introverted man is trying to overcome his loneliness and rebuild social skills after not having friends since the 1990s. He doesn’t drink, so he goes to different coffee shops with his computer and sets small goals, like saying at least one nice thing to a stranger and gradually working up to a full conversation. He feels a bit silly about it but wants to change, remembering that he used to be very social. He also considers Reddit and other social media as valid forms of social interaction, especially when in-person connection is hard.
