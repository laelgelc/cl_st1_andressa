The poster is a 25-year-old woman who feels she has ruined her life. From ages 18 to 23 she intensely hated herself, believed she was ugly and boring, and avoided socializing, mostly just working and going home. She had two relationships with men who treated her poorly and stayed in them too long, leaving her now with no one. She often cries at night, wishing she could go back and live those years differently instead of hiding away out of insecurity. She feels she wasted important formative years when it was easier to make friends, and now at 25, as people around her are getting married or busy with work, she feels extremely lonely and believes making friends is almost impossible.
