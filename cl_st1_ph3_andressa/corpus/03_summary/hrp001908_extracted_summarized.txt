The poster describes a monotonous, repetitive life: they go to work, only talk to 2–3 people there, then go to the gym where they speak to no one. They have only about two friends left and rarely see even one of them; when they do, they just get drunk and wander their rundown town late at night, which now counts as a “fulfilling” weekend. Compared to a year ago, when they had more going on, they feel that since turning 18 their life has become like reliving the same day over and over.
