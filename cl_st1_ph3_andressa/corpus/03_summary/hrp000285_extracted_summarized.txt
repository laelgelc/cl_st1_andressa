A 19-year-old furry describes feeling devastated after seeing TikTok videos of other furries, including teens as young as 15, already racing in regional motorsport events. Racing has been their lifelong dream, but they’ve never even sat in a racecar and don’t have the money to start. This made them feel like they’ve wasted their life so far and that the window to pursue racing is gone. The realization has hit so hard that they’ve stayed in their room since Friday, feel like giving up their love for cars, and now feel deep sadness whenever they think about them. They also see themselves as very unattractive and unhealthy, believing this would put them at an even greater disadvantage, and they’re left wishing they had done “just something” with their life.
