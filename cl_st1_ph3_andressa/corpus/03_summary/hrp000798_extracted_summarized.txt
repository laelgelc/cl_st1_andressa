A 17-year-old male says he’s never felt lonelier. He wakes up every day feeling like he isn’t anyone’s favorite person, which really hurts him. About a month ago he deleted all his social media, and when he re-downloaded Snapchat to check if he’d missed anything, he found no messages—not even a “hi” from the friends he thought were closest. He feels that a month is a long time for no one to reach out and says he just needs someone in his life.
