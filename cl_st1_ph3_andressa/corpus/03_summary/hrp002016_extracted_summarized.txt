17-year-old girl feels like she’s been socially rejected since first grade. She sees everyone around her—classmates, people at school, and family members—having friends and meaningful connections, while she has none. At school, people ignore her, avoid eye contact, and don’t want her in group projects; she feels even teachers don’t tolerate her. At home she has no plans or messages. She deeply wants someone to interact and do things with, but feels so consistently unwanted that she’s starting to think she shouldn’t even bother trying anymore.
