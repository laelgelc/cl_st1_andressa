A 32-year-old married woman with three biological children and one stepchild says she has friends and spends time with them when possible, but still feels empty and alone. Her husband works full-time five days a week and spends most of his days off with his friends, so she only sees him about one day a week. She feels she “should be grateful” but that doesn’t remove the loneliness, and she asks what others do to fill the lonely, empty gap.
