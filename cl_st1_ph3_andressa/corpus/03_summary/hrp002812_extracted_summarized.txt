Max, 27, has felt lonely for most of his life, often without realizing it because it felt natural. He now recognizes how deeply loneliness drains his energy and affects his well-being. This led him to start researching loneliness and its impact, hoping to help himself and others who feel isolated and to do something good. He invites others to share their experiences in the comments or via direct messages and would love to chat, aiming to make the world less lonely together.
