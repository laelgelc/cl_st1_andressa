The poster has long been used to doing everything alone—sitting, eating, going out—and even used to choose solitude over spending time with friends, which led those friends to gradually leave them out. They’ve never been in a relationship and didn’t previously see this as a problem. Now, for the first time, they’re feeling loneliness and realize it’s not about lacking a boyfriend or friends in general, but about not having anyone to talk to, especially since they haven’t spoken to another person in a while.
