The poster hasn’t had friends for a long time, so they’ve been using Reddit and other social media since age 11 to share their thoughts and questions. They feel that on Reddit, almost every post attracts criticism, often harsh, because strangers don’t care about their feelings, and they see this as a consequence of living among billions of people who only value those they know. They say being attacked by many people online feels awful, especially when they’re just being vulnerable and misunderstood, and they’re exhausted from constantly arguing. They wish they could share their thoughts with a real person without having to defend themselves all the time.
