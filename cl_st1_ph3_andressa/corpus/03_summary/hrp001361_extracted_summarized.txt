The poster talks about how many people feel lonely even with friends, family, or social media, especially when they lack someone they can talk to in the moment without feeling like a burden. They created a free, ad-free voice-based AI called “Will Reply” that listens, remembers what matters to the user over time, and makes conversations feel more familiar. They’ve been using it themselves and feel the continuity helps. They share the link and ask for honest feedback from anyone who tries it.
