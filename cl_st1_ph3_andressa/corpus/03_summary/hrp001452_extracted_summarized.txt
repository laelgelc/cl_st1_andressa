A 21-year-old man shares that he lost his father in 3rd grade and was raised only by his mother. He recently completed a BSc in Visual Communication from what he describes as a very bad college and is now in a difficult financial situation, feeling he has no skills to get a job. Four months ago he went through a breakup, has no friends, and feels lonely, scared about his career, and under financial pressure. He says he has no hope, feels like he is “living dead,” and believes dying seems better than living.
