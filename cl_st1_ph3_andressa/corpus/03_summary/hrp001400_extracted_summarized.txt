A 17-year-old describes feeling increasingly isolated over the past year as classmates and friends have started ignoring and avoiding them, even making jokes at their expense in front of others. This has led to crying in private, feeling that no one understands, listens, or can be trusted. They’ve tried to talk to their parents but haven’t found a good time, and spending time with their goldfish hasn’t eased the loneliness. Their motivation, interest in hobbies, and studies are suffering. They pray for just one true friend who will understand, support, and stay with them in difficult times, but they doubt they will ever find such a friend and are sharing this painful part of their life.
