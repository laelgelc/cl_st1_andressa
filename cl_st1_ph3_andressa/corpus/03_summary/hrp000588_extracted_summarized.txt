A 19-year-old man feels like a lifelong “loser” and says he’s faced discrimination from people around him, including his family, which has damaged his self-worth and made him overthink. He’s been mostly alone, struggles with communication, and hasn’t had success making friends online through games. He wants someone to be there for him and for him to be there for them, hoping to find a genuine connection with someone who feels the same way, ideally a girl.
