A 23-year-old woman with a two-year-old feels deeply lonely despite being married and around people. Her husband (24) works six days a week, so she is alone most days and only gets childcare from his family when she’s cleaning clients’ houses; her own family doesn’t help with babysitting. She’s exhausted from being the only one who cleans and manages the house and from having only a few hours a day with her husband, with no deep conversations.

She wants to build a shared community or shared land with friends/family to reduce financial and emotional stress, and she doesn’t care about owning the land herself—she just wants mutual support. Her husband refuses shared ownership and wants to be sole owner with full control. She’s starting to wonder if their star signs (Taurus and Aquarius) reflect real differences between them. She feels unable to pretend she’s happy, believes he may be repressing emotions to function in capitalist society, and is asking what she should do.
