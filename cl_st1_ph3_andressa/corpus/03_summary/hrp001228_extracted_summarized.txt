The poster feels hurt and confused because men treat her very differently than they treat her friends. Her friends get crushes who act decent, do nice things, buy gifts, and invite them places, even when the friends aren’t interested in dating. In contrast, when men show interest in her and she clearly says no but offers friendship, they often react badly: excluding her from the social circle, ignoring her, or even becoming hostile, insulting her, and smearing her to others. She doesn’t string them along and is considered conventionally attractive, so she doesn’t understand why she gets this negative treatment while her friends can “simply exist” without the same social fallout. This pattern makes her feel less than, isolated, and like she’s always the one treated differently, seeing only the “ugly side” of people.
