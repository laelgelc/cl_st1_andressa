A 46-year-old person, living only with their 74-year-old father, has no partner or children and just one friend. After past negative experiences and “sick” people who put them down, they cut those people out and now struggle with trust, making it hard to meet new people and hang out. They feel extremely lonely and often wonder how others manage to have families, support, and friends. They’ve considered church as a way to connect but find it boring and feel people there may have agendas. They’re reaching out and trying to figure out ways to socialize.
