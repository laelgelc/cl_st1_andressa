A 47-year-old divorced woman, separated from her ex-husband for 11 years after a 15-year marriage and three sons, realizes he may have been the love of her life and that she may never have that kind of relationship again. They rarely speak except about their sons, but a recent text exchange felt like old times and made her cry because she misses her best friend and hasn’t felt the same since the divorce. He left her for another woman, and she had expected to remarry by now, but that hasn’t happened. She feels scared, alone, and isolated, with all her friends married and busy with their own families, leaving her with no one to talk to.
