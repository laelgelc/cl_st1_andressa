A 43-year-old man says he has worked hard, is financially stable, in good shape, and “looks great on paper,” but has no real friends, isn’t close to his family, and feels emotionally numb and indifferent to everything. He says he was never mean, just kept to himself and drifted away from people. He’s considering buying remote land, living alone with some dogs, and isolating for the rest of his life, and asks if others feel the same or if he’s an anomaly.
