A 39-year-old man, new to Reddit, feels deeply lonely and depressed. He feels “seen but not heard,” uses jokes to cope, but inside feels lost and increasingly suicidal, especially at work. He has no real friends, is afraid to open up, and struggles with what to say or do socially. As he gets older, he feels he’s missed his chance to form friendships and genuine connections, and is only “surviving” rather than living. His life is mostly just working all day, and past experiences make him feel people only approach him for money or to sell him something. He wonders if it’s hopeless or if there’s still a chance to find real friendship and connection.
