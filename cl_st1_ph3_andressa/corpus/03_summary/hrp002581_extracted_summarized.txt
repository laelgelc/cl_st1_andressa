An 18-year-old woman feels like an outcast because she has no friends and rarely sees other women who are loners like her, only men. In high school she was the only girl who sat alone at lunch, went weeks without speaking, and had no friends from grade 10 to graduation. Now in her first year of university, she wants to make these years count and have friends, but fears it will be a repeat of high school. She finds being alone easier but feels embarrassed by it and is seeking comfort from other girls who share a similar experience.
