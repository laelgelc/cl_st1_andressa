The writer describes having a very public, humiliating fallout with a group of popular girls in a batch of 200 students. Since then, those girls have been warning others to “beware” of them, which has damaged their reputation. They’ve tried to talk to people and make connections, but no one seems interested in being friends. Far from home, they feel very lonely and see making new friends as impossible because everyone is already in established groups that don’t accept newcomers. They feel frustrated and question what they did to deserve this.
