The poster is a 27-year-old man who feels he may have missed his chance at love. He’s had a few mostly online relationships when he was younger, but doesn’t really count them because they didn’t involve much. He’s always been fixated on love and having a partner to care for and spoil, and doesn’t know how to stop thinking this way even though he believes he should be stable first. He feels it may never happen again, which has made his depression worse than it’s been in years. He says that without love he sees no meaning in life and is terrified of getting older, fearing that finding a relationship will only become harder or even impossible.
