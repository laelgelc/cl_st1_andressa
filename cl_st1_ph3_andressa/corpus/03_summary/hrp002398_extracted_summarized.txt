The poster has had one close online friend since 2013/2014 and has no other friends, either online or in real life, largely due to severe social anxiety and difficulty talking to people. Recently, this friend seems uninterested: the poster always has to message first, the friend can go weeks without initiating contact, and now mostly responds with one-word replies like “yup,” unlike their past conversations.
