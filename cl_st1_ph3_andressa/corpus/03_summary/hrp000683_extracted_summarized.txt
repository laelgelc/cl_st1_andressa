The 19-year-old female poster, originally from Greece and now in Switzerland, describes a life of ongoing bullying, severe trauma, and loss. Her parents died when she was 15, leaving her homeless and sometimes sleeping on the street. She began working in a red-light club at 15, where she started heavy daily drinking and using drugs, especially cocaine, which she still uses. A close friend from the club, Lena, who cared for her, was shot and killed by men when the poster was 16. After two years she moved to Switzerland, but her situation remains the same: she works in a sex club, drinks until blacking out every day, uses drugs, deeply misses her parents, feels that life is terrible, and increasingly thinks about ending her life to have a “better life in heaven,” saying she doesn’t know what to do anymore.
