The poster says that after deleting dating apps, their feelings of loneliness largely disappeared. They feel that swiping emphasizes how small and insignificant one can feel in a huge “sea” of people. Without the apps, they have more time to be productive and build enjoyable routines like reading, drinking tea, walking their dogs, rock climbing, and going to the gym. They suspect social media might have a similar negative effect on loneliness as dating apps.
