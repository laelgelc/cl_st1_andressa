The writer describes a classroom activity where students had to form pairs. They expected to partner with their seatmate, but the seatmate left to form a trio with others, leaving the writer alone and embarrassed. The teacher said trios weren’t allowed, but the group ignored it. The writer, feeling watched and upset, told the teacher they would work alone. Eventually, another pair invited them to join, forming a trio, but the initial rejection still felt very embarrassing and disheartening.
