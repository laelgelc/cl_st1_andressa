A 24-year-old man describes losing his small, close friend group he used to game and play DND with, after his severe depression and anxiety pushed them away, which he blames on himself. He realizes his hobbies (gaming, guitar) were only enjoyable because they were ways to spend time with those friends, and without them he feels like he has no real interests or hobbies of his own. He says his only real “interest” was making his friends laugh. Now he feels completely alone and empty, wants to rebuild some kind of life or hobbies, but his anxiety makes it very hard to imagine just joining new activities or groups. He’s asking for suggestions on where to start despite feeling terrified.
