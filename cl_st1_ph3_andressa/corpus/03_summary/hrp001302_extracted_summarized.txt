A 22-year-old man in Cuba feels very alone and has hit a dead end trying to connect with the few people around him through social media. He isn’t looking for advice, just to share his feelings. He feels people have become hypervigilant, defensive, and quick to judge, always searching for hidden motives, which makes social interaction exhausting and pushes him to withdraw. He also feels that society now sees people more as members of collectives than as individuals, with inherited labels that erase individuality, making it harder and harder to build bridges with others.
