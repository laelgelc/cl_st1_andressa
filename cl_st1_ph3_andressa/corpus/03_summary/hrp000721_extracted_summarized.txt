A 22-year-old who recently moved to Bengaluru for work is facing their first Diwali away from home, without family or old friends. As the festive atmosphere starts, they’re feeling the loneliness and don’t want to spend the night alone or on their phone. They’re looking to connect with others in Bengaluru who are also staying back and interested in hanging out—whether it’s clubbing, cafés, food, long drives, conversations, music, or a small Diwali celebration. They emphasize wanting normal, positive company and invite people to DM or comment to make the city feel more like home.
