A 30-year-old man in Delhi NCR describes how his marriage, which lasted only 17 days after a December 2023 wedding, ended in divorce and false accusations from his ex-wife. The legal battle and alimony wiped out all his savings, investments, and PF, leaving him in heavy debt that takes about half his salary and may take a decade to repay. Most friends pulled away during this time, and some colleagues who know about his situation now mock his failed marriage despite him asking them to stop; he stays silent out of fear of losing his job.

He lives with his parents and elder brother but says his family is not emotionally close and they don’t talk about feelings. His phone is silent except for work, and although he has over 500 contacts, he feels there is no one he can call to open up to. He has tried meditation, exercise, and prayer but can’t maintain any of these habits and instead spends hours doom-scrolling. He feels emotionally numb, questions the point of his job and life, and says the loneliness has become unbearable.
