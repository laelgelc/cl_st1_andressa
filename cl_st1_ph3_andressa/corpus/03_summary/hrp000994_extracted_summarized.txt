The writer feels their life has always been full of terror, pain, and mental starvation, but their relatives minimized it and others don’t know how to handle it. They feel ashamed to talk about their issues, believe almost no one truly understands, and think the world is hell and their life a “shit show.” They are not angry at their relatives, whom they believe acted from love, but are angry at the creator of the world for allowing suffering instead of peace and kindness.
