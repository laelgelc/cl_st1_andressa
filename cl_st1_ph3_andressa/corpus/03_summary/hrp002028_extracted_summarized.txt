The poster is a 20-year-old college sophomore who struggles with depression but is strongly supported by a close-knit group of six friends they live with in a small apartment. This lively, always-together household keeps them from staying depressed for long, and they recognize how rare and special this bond is. Considering studying abroad made them realize their time living together is temporary; in two years they’ll all graduate and move away. This thought terrifies them, as they fear coming home to an empty place, feeling unbearably lonely, and possibly spiraling without this unique friend group. They feel both very lucky to have this now and very scared for when it ends.
