21-year-old woman with no in-person friends, only a few online ones and just her dad as family, describes feeling lonely most of her life and having only short-lived friend groups. She prefers online friendships and lacks the energy for real-life ones. She thought she’d found a close friend who related to her loneliness and promised to prioritize her, but he distanced himself once he reconnected with other friends. She longs for one person who truly prioritizes and cares about her as much as she cares about them, feels she always cares more than others do, and likes being supportive but wants someone who won’t ditch her. She feels very sad and lonely, is shy, has few interests beyond playing random Roblox games, and doesn’t know how to make friends; she mainly wanted to get these feelings off her chest.
