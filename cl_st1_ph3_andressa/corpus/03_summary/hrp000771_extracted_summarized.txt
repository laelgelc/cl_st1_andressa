The poster reflects on how being alone when younger felt heavy, but says that after being widowed at 39, with no friends, no nearby family, and no social media, they experienced a much deeper, uncontrollable kind of aloneness. They encourage others to embrace their current independence and “clean slate” while they still have agency, suggesting that what feels like sad loneliness now might actually be a form of “wicked cool” independence, especially compared to the profound aloneness that can come after major loss.
