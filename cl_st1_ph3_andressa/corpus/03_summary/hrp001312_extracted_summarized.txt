A 29-year-old woman is on a mostly two-week break from work and is trying to use the time productively—doing chores, reading, and walking in the park—but everything feels empty. She feels intensely lonely and has a strong, urgent urge to swipe on dating apps and get attention from any guy, just to have someone. She knows this isn’t a healthy or effective solution and believes she should learn to tolerate being alone, but at the moment it feels impossible.
