The poster describes a project they made called OneSky, where you write a thought on a virtual paper lantern and release it to be received by a random person somewhere in the world at 9am or 9pm. There is no chat, profile, or way to reply—just a single, anonymous connection between two strangers. They share that they wrote “tomorrow will be better” and felt helped simply by sending it. They provide the link (thatonesky.com) and suggest it for anyone up late who needs to get something off their chest.
