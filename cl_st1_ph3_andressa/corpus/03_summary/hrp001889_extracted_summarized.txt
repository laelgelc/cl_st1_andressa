The poster has felt lonely since high school, where they mostly stayed in their room, barely talked to family, had only acquaintances and one real friend. After moving to Sydney to study abroad, their loneliness has worsened: they live, eat, and study alone, struggle to make friends in class, and feel extremely socially awkward. Despite joining many events to try to change things, they now have no close connections or physical affection, and feel they have already wasted their teenage years in loneliness and are doomed to waste their 20s the same way.
