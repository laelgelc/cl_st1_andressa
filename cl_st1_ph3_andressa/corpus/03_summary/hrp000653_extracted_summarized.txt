The poster has created a first draft of an app called LIFT, designed to support people in emotional distress (e.g., loneliness, health issues, addiction, trauma, domestic abuse). The app is free and anonymous: users can sign up and post about their issues, while volunteers sign up and specify the areas in which they’re willing to help. The goal is to match people in need with volunteers for emotional connection and practical support. The poster doesn’t share the URL but asks the community for feedback on the idea.
