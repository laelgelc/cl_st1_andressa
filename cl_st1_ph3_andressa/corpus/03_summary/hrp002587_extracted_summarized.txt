An 18-year-old woman describes having been very depressed for four years, during which she isolated herself and had no friends. She’s now starting to improve her life and has recently gained one friend, but that friend prefers staying in, while she loves going out, walking, and being around people. She feels more alive with in-person social interaction but has no one to go out with and believes her social skills are very poor. She asks if others relate and suggests chatting and sharing ways to become more social.
