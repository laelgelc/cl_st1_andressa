The writer spent nearly a year regularly participating in a community, investing emotionally and believing they mattered there. After a conflict with one person that made them feel disrespected and unsafe, they chose to step back. In the weeks and months after they stopped showing up, no one from the community checked in or asked about their absence. This lack of response makes them feel deeply lonely and unimportant, as if their presence never really mattered. They’re not angry, but feel sad and very alone, hurt that their leaving seemed to matter only to them.
