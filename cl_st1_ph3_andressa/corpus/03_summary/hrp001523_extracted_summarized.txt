The poster argues that loneliness can be worsened by a victim mindset and self-pity, which bring no benefits and repel connection. They stress that self-pity is not the same as having a disability or illness; it’s a separate character trait anyone can have or avoid. Using an example of a miserable billionaire and a grateful homeless person, they highlight that attitude matters more than circumstances. They encourage taking responsibility, working with whatever limitations you have, and holding yourself accountable for your own happiness, while still being kind to yourself.
