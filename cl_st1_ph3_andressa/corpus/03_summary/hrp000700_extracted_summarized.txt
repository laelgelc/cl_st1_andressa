The poster is a 22-year-old (almost 23) who feels extremely lonely, bitter, and resentful. They believe life is unfair because people from their school who behaved badly now have relationships, children, and even houses, while they have no friends, girlfriend, children, job, money, or home of their own. They feel they were sensible and respectful in school and therefore “deserve” success and a girlfriend more than others, including a younger cousin who has a “hot girlfriend.” They are very angry, feel they have no future, and fear that when their supportive father retires or dies, they will be left jobless, cashless, and homeless.
