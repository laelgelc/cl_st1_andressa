23-year-old man in East Asia feels lonely and struggles to form friendships or relationships with women. He has mild ASD and believes many women around him are highly neurotic, have different political views, and often lower intelligence than he wants, especially those he finds attractive. He insists he is not a woman-hater and is straight, but these beliefs make it hard for him to talk happily with women and find a high-intelligence partner. He feels lonely because of this and asks whether he should hide his real thoughts and feelings in order to get a girlfriend.
