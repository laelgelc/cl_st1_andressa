A 30-year-old woman describes feeling deeply hurt and abandoned after her best friend cut her off without resolution, posting about their pain online but refusing to talk to her. She feels they were both wrong but that she’s the only one taking accountability, and now the person who used to comfort her is the source of her pain. Nights feel very quiet and lonely, and she’s been coping by listening to soft, emotional music like “The Mountain Is You,” “Rivers and Roads,” and “Exile.” She asks for recommendations for similar soft songs that either give her hope or let her cry more.
