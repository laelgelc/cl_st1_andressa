A 47-year-old single man says he has no friends and tries to talk to women around his age while out running errands. Recently, at a new Trader Joe’s, he chatted with a much younger cashier who seemed interested in him, and later she greeted him very warmly when he returned. He also gets along well with his 23-year-old beard trimmer and her coworkers, who complimented his beard. These experiences make him feel that he connects better with significantly younger people, and he’s wondering if it would really be a bad thing if he ended up in a successful relationship with a much younger woman.
