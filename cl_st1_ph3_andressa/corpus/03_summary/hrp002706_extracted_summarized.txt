The poster feels very comfortable being alone and has long felt guilty about this because people around them don’t seem to feel the same. They have friends but get anxious and overwhelmed when friends reach out, worrying about “socializing correctly” and quickly wanting to be alone again, even with people they like. The COVID-19 lockdown made them realize even more how relieved they feel staying at home without contacting others, and they feel they might be something “more extreme” than just introverted. Discovering the r/loner community makes them feel understood and less alone in these feelings.
