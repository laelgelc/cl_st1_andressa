A 21-year-old man is frustrated and hurt by being told he’s “young” and has “plenty of time” when it comes to relationships. He feels left behind as friends from high school get engaged while he hasn’t had a meaningful connection with a woman since he was 17. He believes his difficult childhood hasn’t helped but wonders if he still deserves love and worries that having a family might not be in his future. He’s struggling to stay patient and asks if anyone has felt the same way but eventually found a real relationship.
