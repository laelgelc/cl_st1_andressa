The poster speaks to people who feel lonely, unappreciated, and distant from others, saying they stopped sharing their problems because it seemed nobody cared and felt alone even around people. They encourage readers to question their negative self-beliefs, insist they are not failures, and say that unrewarded hard work doesn’t mean giving up but changing priorities. The poster shares that giving love to the wrong person taught them not to place all hope in others but to focus on self-improvement and their environment. They note many people are fake and will betray you, but emphasize that only you define who you are, regardless of age, race, or gender. They conclude that life will always be hard, but living on your own terms will help you see light at the end of the tunnel, affirming that the reader is amazing, strong, and more than their current struggles.
