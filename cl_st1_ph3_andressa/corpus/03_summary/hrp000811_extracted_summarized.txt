The poster is a younger brother who feels depressed and lonely watching his older brother frequently go out, drive to different friend groups, and have fun. While the older brother is always texting and laughing with others, the poster mainly only texts him and has no real friends outside of school. They try to convince themself they’re better off alone but don’t believe it, feel unhappy staying home with their parents, and feel lost about what to do or how to cope with these feelings.
