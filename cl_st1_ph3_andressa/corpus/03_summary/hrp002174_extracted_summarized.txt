The poster is a 21-year-old man who appears to be doing well externally—finishing a bachelor’s degree, close with family, travels with them, has a job and side projects, and is applying for a master’s. Internally, he feels like he’s just existing and “killing time,” with scattered goals and little daily accomplishment. He describes himself as not hardworking, barely getting by in school, having few friends and doing little with them, and not participating in campus life, parties, dating, or social activities. Despite having cool colleagues and a good boss, he doesn’t engage or take initiative. Because of his background and upbringing, he feels a deep sense of not belonging and sees himself as an outwardly empty, personality-less “NPC,” disconnected from the person he feels he is inside.
