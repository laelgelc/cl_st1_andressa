The poster reflects that most people’s lives seem to turn out either very good (good careers, marriage, etc.) or very bad (jail, homelessness, death after bad choices), with little in between. They feel stuck in a kind of “purgatory” in the middle: they don’t have a good career, have never dated, don’t travel or do anything spectacular, but also haven’t done anything terrible or criminal. They describe themselves as just existing, like a “grey blob” without notable successes or serious failures.
