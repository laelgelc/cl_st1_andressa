The writer has been alone for a year and expects to stay that way, feeling worn out and emotionally at rock bottom after a difficult life and many heartbreaks. Their ex wished to see them happy with someone else and has moved on, while their own attempts at new relationships have failed and left them mentally exhausted. Seeing other people happy makes them question whether they can keep going alone. They’ve recently started to develop feelings for someone who responds to their compliments, goes out with them, and is recovering from a breakup, but they’re unsure if they’re just a friend or something more. Overall, they feel tired of merely surviving since childhood.
