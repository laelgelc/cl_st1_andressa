The writer feels deeply hurt and rejected by family members, especially someone whose children they love and see as their emotional anchor. After being in jail and having health problems and deaths around them, they feel they’ve still tried to be there for everyone, but are now being ignored and treated as if they don’t exist, including by a little girl who looked through them and avoided speaking. They say they still love the person hurting them but warn that if things aren’t resolved by the end of the year, they will cut that person off, though they will never reject the children.
