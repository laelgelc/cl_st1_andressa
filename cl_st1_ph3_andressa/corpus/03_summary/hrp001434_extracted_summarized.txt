The poster is frustrated with being told that “being alone is good” and that they should just love themselves. They say they do understand the importance of self‑love, but they don’t want to be alone and find it unfulfilling and boring. They strongly yearn to share their life with someone, to connect deeply, experience human touch, be understood, and give and receive love in a way they can’t provide themselves.
