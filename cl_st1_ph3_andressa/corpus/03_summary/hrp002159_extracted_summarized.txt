The poster was an unplanned child born to older parents, with brothers much older and already living their own lives, so they were never close to them. They grew up mature for their age, learned a lot from their parents, and were very social, often feeling like the youngest and even envied for it. Now, suddenly, they find themselves the oldest in their social circles, feeling like “the lost old guy” who wants to help others but instead feels like they’re just in the way.
