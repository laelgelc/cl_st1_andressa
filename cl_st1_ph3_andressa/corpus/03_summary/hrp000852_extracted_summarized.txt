A 21-year-old who just finished college and started an IT job 150 km from home describes moving into a small 150 sq ft rented place with few amenities but enjoying the silence and personal space. They contrast this with college life, which was fun but lacked privacy, and with family life, where responsibilities felt constantly “in the face.” Now they like having the choice: go to the office for fun and human interaction, then return home for personal time and breathing space.
