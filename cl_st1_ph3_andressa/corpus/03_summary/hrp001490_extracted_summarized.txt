The poster reflects on the paradox that social media, originally promoted as a cure for alienation, has instead coincided with people feeling as lonely and isolated as ever. They describe users as “sad seekers of human connection” who are increasingly isolated in their own islands, tribes, and cliques. They argue that technology only masks, rather than solves, basic human problems, and suggest that understanding loneliness requires looking beyond social media to deeper, more elusive psychological or universal causes.
