The poster is a college student who feels it’s very hard to meet people in the post-COVID environment. In classes and on campus, they see students avoiding each other, often absorbed in their phones, and feel that trying to talk to someone—especially girls—makes you seem like a weirdo. Their social circle fell apart during COVID, and they had hoped college would help them rebuild connections, but instead they find that people only interact with those they already know, leaving the poster unsure how to meet anyone outside of social media.
