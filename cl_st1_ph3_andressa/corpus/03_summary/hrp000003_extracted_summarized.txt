An 18-year-old high school student shares that although they have friends at school, they never hang out with anyone outside of school. They feel it’s awkward to ask people to hang out and say their strict, fearful parents are a major reason they don’t socialize or get invited anywhere, since they’re not allowed to go out and their parents think teens only do dangerous or “dumb” things. Seeing others on Snapchat spending time with friends or partying, especially on weekends, makes them feel lonely, left out, and like they don’t belong. This has started to really hurt lately, and they worry they’ll feel this way for the rest of their life. They ask if anyone else has the same problem.
