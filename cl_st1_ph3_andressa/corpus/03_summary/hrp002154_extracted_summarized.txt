A 45-year-old man who has never been in a relationship is working on losing weight, building confidence, and using dating apps but has had little success, even for a simple coffee date. He previously convinced himself he liked being alone because he had no other option, but after a brief experience with someone in an open relationship, he got a glimpse of what a relationship could be like and has felt depressed since. He plans to keep looking for love and, in the meantime, is creating a bucket list of relationship experiences he’s never had, such as holding hands while driving, kissing on New Year’s Eve, cuddling on the couch, making breakfast in bed, cooking a romantic dinner, traveling with someone, and sharing an impromptu slow dance.
