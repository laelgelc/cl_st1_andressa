The poster describes finally getting their tool app approved after a 3‑month App Store review delay due to its use of Screen Time for an auto check‑in feature. The app has no backend server and keeps all data and device usage private within Apple’s local sandbox. To pass review, they added a feature to block distracting apps during bedtime to improve sleep. They are also planning a feature that schedules a message to an emergency contact before doing something dangerous, which can be canceled once the user is safe, inspired by a Reddit comment about setting a timed text before risky activities.
