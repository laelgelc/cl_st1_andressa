The poster has experienced deep loneliness and is working on something to help with social and emotional isolation. They want to better understand how others experience loneliness, what helps, and what makes it worse, especially in terms of words and comfort. They’re inviting people who are currently lonely or have been lonely to talk with them—either over coffee in Lyon, France, or via a ~20-minute call. They emphasize that it’s confidential, not commercial, no personal info is required, and their goal is to understand common patterns and make the other person feel genuinely heard.
