I’m 18 and still in high school, and lately this has been weighing on me a lot.

I technically have friends at school. I talk to people in class, we joke around, I’m not a complete loner or anything. But once the final bell rings, that’s it. I never hang out with anyone outside of school. No movies, no mall, no just walking around or grabbing food. It’s like my social life only exists between 8am and 3pm.

A big part of it is my parents. They’re super strict and honestly kind of paranoid. They think teenagers only go out to do dangerous or “dumb” things, so I’m basically never allowed to go anywhere. Because of that, people don’t really invite me to things anymore. I feel awkward even trying to ask someone to hang out, because I’m already expecting to have to say no or make an excuse.

On Snapchat I see everyone posting stories of them at parties, sleepovers, just… being together. Weekends are the worst. I’ll be in my room scrolling through all these snaps and I just feel so left out, like there’s this whole part of life I’m not part of. It makes me feel like I don’t belong anywhere.

It’s started to really hurt lately, and I’m scared that this is just how my life is going to be forever. Does anyone else deal with something like this?
