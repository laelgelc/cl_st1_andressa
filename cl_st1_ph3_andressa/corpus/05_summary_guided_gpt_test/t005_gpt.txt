I feel so alone lately, and it hits the hardest on my birthday and holidays. Every year it’s the same: only my immediate family even acknowledges my birthday. My friends don’t reach out, don’t celebrate, nothing… unless I’m the one who starts the conversation.

I always try to make their birthdays special, send messages, remember dates, put in effort. But when it’s my turn, it’s like I don’t exist. It hurt so much that I just stopped talking to them. I cry every year on my birthday because it feels like proof that I don’t matter to anyone.

I’m scared that when my family is gone, I’ll have absolutely no one. I feel invisible, like I could disappear and nothing would change. I’ve even said out loud that I’m depressed, and it still feels like nobody really hears me. I’m so tired. I don’t know how much longer I can keep trying when it feels this one-sided and pointless.
