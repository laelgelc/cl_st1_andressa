An 18-year-old high school student feels lonely because, although they have friends at school, they never hang out with anyone outside of school. They feel awkward about asking people to hang out and have very strict, fearful parents who don’t allow them to go out or make many social plans, believing teens only get into trouble. As a result, they aren’t invited to things, feel like they don’t belong anywhere, and spend weekends alone while seeing others on Snapchat out with friends or partying. This has recently started to really hurt them, and they’re worried they’ll feel this way for the rest of their life, asking if anyone else has the same problem.
