I’m starting to realize I’m not just “alone a lot,” I’m actually lonely in a way that’s starting to feel like it’s rewiring my brain.

I go through entire days without anyone saying my name out loud. My phone lights up, but it’s notifications from apps, not people. When someone does message me, I stare at it for too long, overthinking every possible reply until it feels safer not to answer at all. Then I feel even more disconnected.

I have acquaintances, but no one who really knows me. I can be in a room full of people and still feel like I’m behind glass, watching everyone else live in a world I somehow wasn’t invited into. I don’t even know what I’d talk about if someone actually wanted to listen.

What scares me most is how normal this is starting to feel, like I’m slowly accepting that this is just how my life is going to be.
